# Directive 29/2006/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
Intensifying the prevention and control of avian influenza and human type-A (H5N1) influenza
With this Directive, the Prime Minister requests the Ministry of Health, the National Steering Committee for human influenza pandemic prevention and control and concerned agencies to perform several tasks in order to prevent and control avian influenza and human type-A (H5N1) influenza from re-spreading.- (Summary)
