# Chỉ thị 10/2005/CT-BBCVT

DIRECTIVE
On ensuring and raising the quality of telecommunications services
The Minister of Post and Telematics requests telecommunications and Internet enterprises, and functional agencies of the Ministry of Post and Telematics to perform several tasks so as to ensure and raise the quality of telecommunications services.- (Summary)
