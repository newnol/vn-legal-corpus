# Decision 963/QD-TTg

THE PRIME MINISTER
Decision No.963/QD-TTg of October 4, 2000 adding new members to the National Committee for International Economic Cooperation

- Mr. Hoang Ngoc Nhat, Vice Minister of Public Security
- Mr. Truong Khanh Chau, Lieutenant General-Vice Minister of Defense.- (Summary)
