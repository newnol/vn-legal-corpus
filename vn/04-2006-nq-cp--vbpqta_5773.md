# Resolution 04/2006/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
On the Government’s March 2006 regular meeting
On March 28 and 29, the Government held its March 2006 regular meeting, where it discussed:
- An additional report on the fulfillment of the 2005 tasks and the implementation of the 2006 plan, presented by the Minister of Planning and Investment, an additional report on the 2005 state budget and the implementation of the 2006 state budget tasks and a report on the settlement of the 2004 state budget, presented by the Minister of Finance;
- A scheme on learning and teaching foreign languages in the national education system in the 2006-2015 period, presented by the Minister of Education and Training;
- A draft decree guiding the implementation of the Bidding Law, presented by the Minister of Planning and Investment;
- The draft Ordinance on Conclusion and Implementation of International Agreements, presented by the Minister of Foreign Affairs;
- A report on socio-economic situation and production, business, service and investment results in March and the first quarter of 2006, presented by the Minister of Planning and Investment, and a report on trade situation in March 2006, presented by the Minister of Trade.- (Summary)
