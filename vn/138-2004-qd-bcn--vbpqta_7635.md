# Quyết định 138/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 138/2004/QD-BCN of November 22, 2004 transforming the Industrial Mineral and Mining Mechanical Company into the Mineral and Mechanical Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, the Mineral and Mechanical Joint-Stock Company (MIMECO) is headquartered at No. 2 Dang Thai Than street, Hoan Kiem district, Hanoi.
Its charter capital is VND 3.2 billion, 28% of which to be held by the State and 72% sold to its laborers.- (Summary)
