# Decision 295/2006/QĐ-UBTDTT

DECISION
Promulgating the Physical Training and Sports Committee’s 2006 legal document-making program
According to this Decision, in 2006, the Committee for Physical Training and Sports shall assume the prime responsibility for, and coordinate with other ministries in, elaborating 27 legal documents.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
