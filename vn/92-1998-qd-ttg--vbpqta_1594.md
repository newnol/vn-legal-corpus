# Quyết định 92/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.92/1998/QD-TTg of May 11, 1998 on the establishment of the State Audit Agency in the western South Vietnam, under the Central State Audit Agency.
The State Audit Agency in the western South Vietnam has the legal person status, its own seal and accounts; its operating fund is allocated by the State budget and included in the general budget of the Central State Audit Agency; its head office is located in Can Tho city of Can Tho province.- (Summary)
