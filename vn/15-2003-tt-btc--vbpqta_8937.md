# Thông tư 15/2003/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 15/2003/TT-BTC of March 7, 2003 providing guidance on charges for keeping bicycles, motorcycles and cars temporarily seized due to violations of law provisions on traffic order and safety
Under the guidance in this Circular, payers of charges for keeping transport means are transport means operators who violate law provisions on traffic order and safety and have their transport means temporarily seized.
This Circular guides the charge rates, charge-collection vouchers and management and use of the collected charge amounts.- (Summary)
