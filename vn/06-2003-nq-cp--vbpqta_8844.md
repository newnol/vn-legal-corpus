# Resolution 06/2003/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION No. 06/2003/NQ-CP of May 2, 2003 on the Government’s April 2003 regular meeting
On April 28, 2003, the Government held its April 2003 regular meeting, discussing and resolving the following issues:
- It considered the additional report on the implementation of the 2002 plan as well as plans of April and the first 4 months of 2003, the additional report on the implementation of the 2002 State budget and the performance of the 2003 State budget tasks.
- It considered the draft strategy on development of Vietnam’s legal system till 2010. The existing system of legal documents has not yet been on par with practical quantitative and qualitative requirements; not comprehensive, not synchronous and not yet met the practical requirements; many burning fields of life have still been left ungoverned by law; institutions to ensure law enforcement remain inadequate and weak; mechanism for elaborating and amending laws are still rigid, showing many mishaps. Therefore, it is necessary to elaborate a strategy on development of Vietnam’s legal system, ensuring the prompt and full institutionalization of the Party’s guidelines and policies on developing a socialist-oriented market economy and building a law-governed Vietnamese state; to bring into full play the internal strengths and take initiative in international economic integration. The development of the legal system must stem from Vietnam’s reality and at the same time selectively assimilate international experiences.
- It considered and gave opinions on the Bill amending and supplementing a number of articles of the Cooperative Law and the Bill amending and supplementing a number of articles of the Law on Complaints and Denunciations.
- It considered the report on the work of inspection and settlement of citizens’ complaints and denunciations in the first quarter of 2003.- (Summary)
