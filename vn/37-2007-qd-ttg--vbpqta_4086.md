# Decision 37/2007/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Approving the Agreement between the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the Government of the Republic of Turkey on visa exemption for holders of diplomatic, official and special passports, concluded in Hanoi on January 26, 2007
The Prime Minister assigns the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to carry out foreign-relation procedures for such approval and notify relevant agencies of the effective date of the Agreement.- (Summary)
