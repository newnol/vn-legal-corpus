# Thông tư 65/2003/TT-BQP

CIRCULAR No. 65/2003/TT-BQP of June 5, 2003 guiding the implementation of a number of articles of the Government’s Decree No. 41/2002/ND-CP of April 11, 2002 on policies towards laborers redundant from the restructuring of State enterprises in the army
This Circular guides in detail the scope, subjects and policies towards laborers redundant from the restructuring of State enterprises in the army.- (Summary)
