# Quyết định 179/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.179/1998/QD-TTg of September 19, 1998 on the establishment of the Finance and Accountancy Department of the State Securities Commission
To establish the Finance and Accountancy Department of the State Securities Commission. The specific tasks of the Finance and Accountancy Department shall be defined by the Chairman of the State Securities Commission.- (Summary)
