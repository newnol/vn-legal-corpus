# Quyết định 03/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 03/2004/QD-BCN of January 6, 2004 merging Hoi An Garment Enterprise into Hoa Tho Textile and Garment Company
The Ministry of Industry decided to merge Hoi An Garment Enterprise under the People's Committee of Quang Nam province into Hoa Tho Textile and Garment Company, a member of Vietnam Textile and Garment Corporation.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
