# Circular 74/1999/TT-BTC

Circular No. 74/1999/TT-BTC of June 14, 1999 stipulating work-trip allowances for commune officials
Entitled to the work-trip allowances are officials of the commune-level Party organizations, administrations and such mass organizations as the Fatherland Front, the Women�s Union, the Peasants� Association, the War Veterans� Association and the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union as well as the specialized personnel of these organizations, who are sent on working trips.
The Circular also specifies conditions for such entitlement as well as the allowance payments for commune officials.- (Summary)
