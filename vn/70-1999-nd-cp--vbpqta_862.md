# Nghị định 70/1999/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 70/1999/ND-CP of August 11, 1999 readjusting administrative boundaries for the splitting and establishment of wards and townships of Pleiku city and Mang Yang district, Gia Lai province
To establish Tay Son ward of Pleiku city on the basis of a land area of 154.33 ha and a population of 10,112 of Hoa Lu ward.
To establish Ia Kring ward of Pleiku city on the basis of a land area of 669.72 ha and a population of 10,270 of Dien Hong ward.
To establish Kon Dong township of Mang Yang district on the basis of a land area of 1,800 ha and a population of 3,081 of Kon Dong commune.- (Summary)
