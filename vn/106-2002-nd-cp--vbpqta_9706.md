# Nghị định 106/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 106/2002/ND-CP of December 20, 2002 on the adjustment of administrative boundaries for establishing Hai An urban district and its wards, expanding and establishing wards of Le Chan urban district and renaming An Hai rural district, Hai Phong city, as An Duong rural district
To establish Hai An urban district on the basis of the whole land area of 8,838.97 ha and the population of 69,862 of Dang Lam, Dang Hai, Dong Hai, Nam Hai, and Trang Cat communes of An Hai rural district and Cat Bi ward of Ngo Quyen urban district. To establish Dang Lam, Dang Hai, Dong Hai, Nam Hai and Trang Cat wards of Hai An urban district.
To merge Vinh Niem and Du Hang Kenh communes of An Hai rural district with their status quo land areas and population into Le Chan urban district, and establish Vinh Niem and Du Hang Kenh wards of Le Chan urban district.
Le Chan urban district shall have a natural land area of 1,231.02 ha and a population of 179,168, comprising 14 dependent administrative units being An Bien, An Duong, Cat Dai, Du Hang, Dong Hai, Hang Kenh, Ho Nam, Lam Son, Me Linh, Niem Nghia, Trai Cau, Tran Nguyen Han, Vinh Niem and Du Hang Kenh wards.
Ngo Quyen urban district shall have a natural land area of 1,096.78 ha and a population of 155,253, comprising 13 dependent administrative units being Cau Dat, Cau Tre, Dang Giang, Dong Khe, Dong Quoc Binh, Gia Vien, Lach Tray, Lac Vien, Le Loi, Luong Khanh Thien, May Chai, May To and Van My wards.
To rename An Hai rural district as An Duong rural district. This rural district shall have a natural land area of 9,831.96 ha and a population of 134.137, comprising 16 dependent administrative units being An Dong, An Hoa, An Hong, An Hung, Bac Son, Dai Ban, Dang Cuong, Dong Thai, Hong Phong, Hong Thai, Le Loi, Le Thien, Nam Son, Quoc Tuan and Tan Tien communes and An Duong district capital.- (Summary)
