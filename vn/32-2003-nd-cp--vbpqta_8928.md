# Nghị định 32/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 32/2003/ND-CP of April 1, 2003 adjusting the administrative boundaries for the expansion of Phu Tho provincial capital, and the establishment of communes and wardS of Phu Tho provincial capital and Doan Hung district, Phu Tho province
To transfer the whole land area and population of Phu Ho township of Phu Ninh district, the whole land area and population of Ha Thach commune of Lam Thao district, and part of the land area and population of Thanh Ha commune, Thanh Ba district, to Phu Tho provincial capital for management.
To dissolve Phu Ho district township for the establishment of Phu Ho commune; to establish Thanh Vinh commune and Truong Thinh ward of Phu Tho provincial capital.
After the adjustment of boundaries, Phu Tho provincial capital shall have a land area of 6,328.65 ha and a population of 62,863 with 10 dependent administrative units, including 4 wards and 6 communes. Phu Ninh district shall be left with a land area of 16,820.6 ha and a population of 104,780 with 20 dependent administrative units, including Phong Chau district township and 19 communes. Lam Thao district shall be reduced to a land area of 12,101.66 ha and a population of 114,030 with 16 dependent administrative units, including Lam Thao district township and 15 communes. Thanh Ba district shall be sized down with a land area of 17,566.09 ha and a population of 109,168 with 26 dependent administrative units, including Thanh Ba district township and 25 communes.
To establish Ca Dinh commune of Doan Hung district on the basis of a land area of 1,417 ha and a population of 2,749.- (Summary)
