# Directive 04/2006/CT-BYT

DIRECTIVE
On the work of flood and storm prevention and combat, and search and rescue in 2006
The Minister of Health requests directors of provincial/municipal Health Services and directors of units under the Ministry to perform several tasks in implementation of the Prime Minister’s Directive No. 13/2006/CT-TTg of March 31, 2006, on the work of flood and storm prevention and combat, and search and rescue in 2006.- (Summary)
