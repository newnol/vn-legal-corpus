# Quyết định 58/2004/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 58/2004/QD-BTC of July 6, 2004 prescribing the financial management of capital-construction investment capital from profit portions divided from Vietsovpetro Joint-Venture Enterprise
To annul the Finance Ministry's Circular No. 49/TC/NSNN of July 30, 1997 guiding the financial management of profit portions divided from Vietsovpetro Joint-Venture Enterprise.
As from 2004, after-tax profit portions divided to the Vietnamese party from Vietsovpetro Joint-Venture Enterprise and oil and gas volumes shared to Vietnam under the product-sharing contracts, must be remitted into the State budget (Chapter 121, category 03, clause 02, item 027, sub-item 03 of the current State budget index) according to law provisions.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
