# Quyết định 01/2005/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 01/2005/QD-TTg of January 5, 2005 transforming Binh Tay Food & Agro Materials of the Southern Food Corporation into one-member limited liability company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Binh Tay Food & Agro Materials Co. Limited (BITAFAMCO) is headquartered at No. 406 Tran Van Kieu road, Ward 7, District 6, Ho Chi Minh city, and has a charter capital of VND 38,536,520,541.- (Summary)
