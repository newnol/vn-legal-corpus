# Quyết định 44/2005/QĐ-UBTDTT

DECISION No. 44/2005/QD-UBTDTT of January 13, 2005 promulgating the Regulation on medical work for sport team members
The Regulation issued together with this Decision provides for health check-up and care, prevention and treatment of injuries and use of medicines for sport team members in the training and competition.
