# Directive 01/2008/CT-BYT 

DIRECTIVE
Stepping up the application of Good Pharmacy Practices (GPP) principles and standards to drug retailers
The Minister of Health requests directors of departments, the Office, the Inspectorate, provincial-level Health Services and concerned units to perform several tasks in order to step up the application of GPP principles and standards to drug retailers according to roadmaps, contributing to rational, safe and efficient use of drugs and raising the quality of pharmaceutical services for people.- (Summary)
