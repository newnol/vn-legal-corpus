# Quyết định 25/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 25/2002/QD-TTg of February 1, 2002 on a number of regimes and policies towards officials working at medical establishments set up under the Ordinance on Handling of Administrative Violations of July 6, 1995
This Document prescribes the levels of occupational allowances and a number of other allowances for officials and employees working at medical establishments which have been set up under the Ordinance on Handling of Administrative Violations of July 6, 1995.- (Summary)
