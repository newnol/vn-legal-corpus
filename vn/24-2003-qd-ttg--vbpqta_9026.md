# Quyết định 24/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 24/2003/QD-TTg of February 12, 2003 establishing the External Affairs Service of Quang Tri province
To establish the External Affairs Service under the People’s Committee of Quang Tri province on the basis of reorganizing the Foreign Relations Section and the Board for Border Issues under the provincial People’s Committee.- (Summary)
