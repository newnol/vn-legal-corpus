# Quyết định 29/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.29/1999/QD-TTg of February 27, 1999 on assigning the task of doctorate training to the Industrial Machinery and Tool Institute
To assign the task of doctorate training to the Industrial Machinery and Tool Institute. The Ministry of Education and Training shall assume the prime responsibility and coordinate with the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment to define areas with doctorate training for the Industrial Machinery and Tool Institute.- (Summary)
