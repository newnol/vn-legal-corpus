# Nghị quyết 14/2003/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION No. 14/2003/NQ-CP of December 9, 2003 on the Government's November 2003 regular meeting
On December 2, 2003, the Government held its November 2003 regular meeting, discussing and resolving the following issues:
- The Government discussed a draft decree on a number of measures to ensure public order, presented by the Ministry of Public Security.
It assigned the Ministry of Public Security to assume the prime responsibility for, and coordinate with the Ministry of Justice, the Government Office and concerned agencies in, gathering comments of concerned persons and agencies, finalizing and submitting the draft decree to the Prime Minister for decision.
- The Government heard the Minister of Industry presenting a bill on electricity. The Government concluded that the electricity bill must institutionalize the Party's and State's lines and policies on electricity development and management of electricity activities, confirming the undertaking to diversify forms of ownership and management in electricity generation, distribution and business; ensure the State's uniform management over electricity activities, creating legal grounds for promoting the right to production and business autonomy of electricity units, and meeting the requirements for international economic integration.
The Government assigned the Ministry of Industry to assume the prime responsibility for, and coordinate with concerned agencies in, gathering comments of cabinet members and finalizing the bill for submission to the National Assembly Standing Committee.
- The Government discussed a draft ordinance amending and supplementing a number of articles of the Ordinance on Income Tax on High-Income Earners, presented by the Ministry of Finance. It assigned the Ministry of Finance to assume the prime responsibility for, and coordinate with concerned agencies in, gathering comments of cabinet members and finalizing this draft ordinance before submitting it to the Prime Minister for decision on the submission thereof to the National Assembly Standing Committee.
- The Government also considered a report on the implementation of the socio-economic development plan in November and the first eleven months of 2003, presented by the Ministry of Planning and Investment.- (Summary)
