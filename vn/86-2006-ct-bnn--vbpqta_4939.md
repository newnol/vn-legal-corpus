# Directive 86/2006/CT-BNN

DIRECTIVE
Managing the change of forest use purposes
The Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development requests directors of provincial/municipal Agriculture and Rural Development Services, heads of provincial/municipal Ranger Sub-Departments, and concerned departments to perform several tasks in order to rectify the change of forest use purposes.- (Summary)
