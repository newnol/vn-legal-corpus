# Quyết định 113/2007/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Approving the list of communes having attained the objectives of the Program on socio-economic development in communes in ethnic minority, mountainous, border, deep-lying and remote areas during 1999-2005; and adding communes, hamlets and villages to be covered by Program 135, phase II, and coastal and island communes to be covered by the national target program on poverty reduction during 2006-2010
