# Directive 07/2007/CT-BGTVT

DIRECTIVE
On further raising the quality of the training and testing of, and grant of driver licenses to, drivers
The Minister of Transport requests concerned agencies and units to perform several tasks in order to raise the quality of the training and testing of, and grant of driver licenses to, drivers.- (Summary)
