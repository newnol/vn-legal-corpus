# Quyết định 31/2006/QĐ-BYT

DECISION
Extending the effective duration of the Health Minister’s Decision No. 17/2006/QD-BYT of May 19, 2006, which promulgates the Provisional Regulation on the import of medicines without registration numbers
Under this Decision, which shall take effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” Decision No. 17/2006/QD-BYT shall be effective until the end of December 31, 2006.- (Summary)
