# Chỉ thị 26/2005/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On enhancing petrol and oil conservation
With this Directive, the Prime Minister requests ministries, branches, localities and enterprises to use petrol and oil economically and efficiently, so as to save foreign currencies and lessen the burden to the State budget.- (Summary)
