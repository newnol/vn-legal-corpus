# Directive 04/2004/CT-BYT

DIRECTIVE No. 04/2004/CT-BYT of April 12, 2004 enhancing the work of fire prevention and fighting in the medical sector
In order to put the work of fire prevention and fighting into order with efficiency, the Health Minister instructs the heads of the units in the medical sector to enhance dissemination, propagation and education of legal documents and internal regulations on fire prevention and fighting for officials and employees in order to raise their sense of responsibility in managing and protecting the State's properties and residents' life and assets, ensuring safety for agencies and units. The Minister also instructs the units to coordinate with other forces in enhancing the work of fire prevention and fighting.- (Sumamry)
