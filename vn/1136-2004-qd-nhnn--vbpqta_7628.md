# Quyết định 1136/2004/QĐ-NHNN

DECISION No. 1136/2004/QD-NHNN of September 9, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on organization and operation of the State Bank's Transaction Office
The Transaction Office is a unit under the State Bank, functioning to assist the Governor in conducting several operations of the central bank.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
