# Quyết định 62/2003/QĐ-BKH

DECISION No. 62/2003/QD-BKH of January 27, 2003 promulgating the periodical statistical reporting regime applicable to State enterprises
Issued together with this Decision was the periodical statistical reporting regime applicable to State enterprises.
This reporting regime aims to supply information to the State statistical system for synthesis of economic statistical norms.
Reports include monthly reports, annual reports and particular and seasonal reports.
The reporting contents cover the general information on enterprises (information for identification of enterprises, labor and income, development investment capital,...) and specialized information on enterprises (import and export activities, agricultural, forestry and aquatic resource activities, industrial activities, construction and installation activities,...).
The report-receiving units shall be the provincial/municipal Statistics Departments of the localities where enterprises and their immediate superior managing bodies are headquartered.- (Summary)
