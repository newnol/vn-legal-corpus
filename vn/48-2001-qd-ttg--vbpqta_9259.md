# Quyết định 48/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 48/2001/QD-TTg of April 4, 2001 assigning the task of master training to the Institute of Cryptography
To assign the task of master training to the Institute of Cryptography under the Government Committee on Cipher.
The Ministry of Education and Training shall consider and prescribe master- training majors for the Institute of Cryptography.- (Summary)
