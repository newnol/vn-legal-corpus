# Quyết định 101/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 101/2004/QD-BCN of September 27, 2004 transforming Dong Nai Paper Company into Dong Nai Paper Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Dong Nai Paper Joint-Stock Company (COGIDO) is headquartered in Bien Hoa 1 Industrial Park, Bien Hoa city, Dong Nai province.
Its charter capital is VND 23 billion, 51% of which to be held by the State and 49% sold to its laborers.- (Summary)
