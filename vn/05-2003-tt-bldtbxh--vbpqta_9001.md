# Thông tư 05/2003/TT-BLĐTBXH

CIRCULAR No. 05/2003/TT-BLDTBXH of February 18, 2003 guiding the adjustment of levels of monthly allowances and subsidies for people with meritorious services to the Revolution
Levels of monthly allowances and subsidies for people with meritorious services to the Revolution shall be adjusted to increase by 38.1% of the total preferential allowance and subsidy funds. This adjustment aims to contribute to improving the life of people with meritorious services and overcome irrationality in levels of preferential subsidies for people with meritorious services.
This Circular guides the principles, subjects and levels as well as procedures and dossiers of allowance and subsidy adjustment.- (Summary)
