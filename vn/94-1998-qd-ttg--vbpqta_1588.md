# Quyết định 94/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.94/1998/QD-TTg of May 15, 1998 on the establishment of provincial Sports and Physical Training Services of Dong Nai, Quang Nam and Tra Vinh provinces.
To establish the provincial Sports and Physical Training Services of Dong Nai, Quang Nam and Tra Vinh provinces on the basis of transferring the function of State management over sports and physical training activities from the provincial Services of Culture, Information and Sports.- (Summary)
