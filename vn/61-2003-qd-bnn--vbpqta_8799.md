# Quyết định 61/2003/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 61/2003/QD-BNN of May 7, 2003 promulgating the system of printed forms used in the inspection, examination and sanctioning of administrative violations by specialized plant protection and quarantine inspectorate
Issued together with this Decision are the following 16 forms:
1. Decision on inspecting and examining the observance of the legislation on plant protection and quarantine;
2. Record on inspecting and examining the observance of the legislation on plant protection and quarantine;
3. Record on administrative violations in the field of plant protection and quarantine;
4. Decision on sanctioning administrative violations in the field of plant protection and quarantine;
5. Decision on temporary seizure of material evidences and/or means of administrative violations in the field of plant protection and quarantine;
6. Record on temporary seizure of material evidences and/or means of administrative violations in the field of plant protection and quarantine;
7. Record on confiscation of material evidences and/or means of administrative violations in the field of plant protection and quarantine;
8. Record on hand-over and reception of material evidences and/or means of administrative violations in the field of plant protection and quarantine;
9. Record on destruction of material evidences and/or means of administrative violations in the field of plant protection and quarantine;
10. Decision on coercive enforcement of the Decision on sanctioning administrative violations in the field of plant protection and quarantine;
11. Record on coercive enforcement of the Decision on sanctioning administrative violations in the field of plant protection and quarantine;
12. Decision on transferring dossiers of administrative violations in the field of plant protection and quarantine to the agency(ies) conducting criminal procedures;
13. Decision on settling complaints about the Decision on sanctioning administrative violations in the field of plant protection and quarantine;
14. Working minutes;
15. Record on sampling plant protection drugs for quality control in service of inspection and examination work.
16. Appendices to records.
This Decision replaces Decision No. 331/NN-BVTV of March 7, 1997 promulgating the forms of sanctioning administrative violations in the field of plant protection and quarantine.- (Summary)
