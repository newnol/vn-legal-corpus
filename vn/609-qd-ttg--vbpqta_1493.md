# Decision 609/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 609/QD-TTg of July 16, 1998, replacing members of the Vietnam's National Committee for the "International Decade of Natural Calamities Reduction"
The Committee is composed of a Chairman, who is Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development, Le Huy Ngo; 6 Vice Chairmen and 17 members who are representatives of different ministries and branches.- (Summary)
