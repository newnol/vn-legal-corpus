# Quyết định 11/2001/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 11/2001/QD-TTg of January 17, 2001 on the establishment of the Central Judicial Medicine Institute under the Health Ministry
To establish the Central Judicial Medicine Institute under the Health Ministry on the basis of the Central Medico-Judicial Expertise Organization.
The Central Judicial Medicine Institute is a public-service unit, having the function of conducting research into and application of legal medicine and medico-judicial, participating in medico-judicial training and providing medico-judicial direction throughout the country.- (Summary)
