# Quyết định 51/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 51/2004/QD-BCN of June 17, 2004 amending Clauses 1 and 3, Article 1 of the Industry Minister's Decision No. 227/2003/QD-BCN of December 22, 2003 on transformation of Geology and Mineral Resources Company into Geology and Mineral Resources Joint-Stock Company
To amend Clauses 1 and 3, Article 1 of the Industry Minister's Decision No. 227/2003/QD-BCN of December 22, 2003 on transformation of Geology and Mineral Resources Company into Geology and Mineral Resources Joint-Stock Company regarding the charter capital structure (the State's share percentage: 56.89%; shares to be sold to the company's laborers: 30.60% and shares to be sold to outsiders: 12.51%) and the preferences for the company's laborers (preferential shares to be sold to the company's laborers amount to 9,908 valued at VND 297,240,000).
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
