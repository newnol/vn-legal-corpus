# Quyết định 56/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 56/2002/QD-TTg of April 26, 2002 ratifying the Agreement between the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the Government of the Republic of Argentina on cooperation in using nuclear power for peaceful purposes
To ratify the Agreement signed in Hanoi on November 19, 2001 between the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the Government of the Republic of Argentina on cooperation in using nuclear power for peaceful purposes.
The Ministry for Foreign Affairs shall carry out external procedures for the ratification and notify Vietnam�s agencies of the effective date of the agreement.- (Summary)
