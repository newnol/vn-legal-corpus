# Decree 14/2002/ND-CP

DECREE No. 14/2002/ND-CP of January 31, 2002 dividing Kon Plong district of Kon Tum province into Kon Plong and Kon Ray districts; renaming Kon Plong district township as Dak Rve district township
Kon Plong district shall have a land area of 136,160 ha and a population of 17,967, including 6 communes.
Kon Ray district shall have a land area of 88,660 ha and a population of 20,992, including Dak Rve district township and 5 communes.- (Summary)
