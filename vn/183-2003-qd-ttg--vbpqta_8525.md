# Quyết định 183/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 183/2003/QD-TTg of September 8, 2003 on the establishment of the Industrial Parks Management Board of Hung Yen province
To establish the Industrial Park Management Board under the People's Committee of Hung Yen province to manage industrial parks in the territory of Hung Yen province. It has the tasks and powers for the management of industrial parks defined in Article 27 and its assisting apparatus organized according to Article 28 of the Regulation promulgated together with Decree No. 36/CP of April 24, 1997.
The Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
