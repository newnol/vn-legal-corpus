# Decree 32/2007/NĐ-CP

DECREE
Providing for the levels of preferential subsidies and allowances for people with meritorious services to the revolution
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces Decree No. 147/2005/ND-CP of November 30, 2005.
The preferential subsidies and allowances specified in this Decree apply as from January 1, 2007.- (Summary)
