# Quyết định 23/2002/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION No. 23/2002/QD-BGDDT of April 25, 2002 amending Article 7 of the Regulation on complementary primary education graduation examinations, issued together with Decision No. 45/2000/QD-BGDDT of October 18, 2000
The amended contents are the stipulations on exam subjects; exam contents; duration for each exam subject; and duration of break between exam subjects.- (Summary)
