# Decision 1122/1997/QD-TTg

DECISION No.1122/1997/QD-TTg of December 24, 1997 on the tasks, powers and organizational structure of the National Anti-AIDS Committee and of organizations in charge of AIDS prevention and fight of different branches and levels.
The National Anti-AIDS Committee shall assist the Prime Minister to direct and carry out the work of HIV/AIDS prevention and fight. The Committee shall have the following tasks and powers:
- To work out strategy, lines, policies and plans on HIV/AIDS prevention and fight.
- To coordinate with a number of concerned ministries in making the annual budget estimate for HIV/AIDS prevention and fight and submit it to the Government for ratification and in allocating the ratified fund for the ministries, branches and localities.
- To guide, direct, inspect and urge the implementation of the work of HIV/AIDS prevention and fight in the whole country.
- To act as the main body for international cooperation and coordination and management of the country's, branches' and localities' bilateral and multilateral cooperation on HIV/AIDS prevention and fight.
- To coordinate with the concerned ministries and branches in examining, inspecting and settling complaints and denunciations and handling violations of the legislation on HIV/AIDS prevention and fight.
- To annually and periodically make reviews and assessment of the HIV/AIDS prevention and fight results.
To organize the implementation of the work of AIDS prevention and fight in different branches and levels as follows: the ministries, the ministerial-level agencies, the agencies attached to the Government, the mass organizations and social organizations with their activities involved in AIDS prevention and fight shall set up anti-AIDS steering boards; provinces and cities directly under the Central Government shall set up anti-AIDS committees; districts, provincial towns and cities shall set up anti-AIDS steering committees. The presidents or vice-presidents of the communal People's Committees shall take charge of the work of AIDS prevention and fight in communes and wards.- (Summary)
