# Nghị định 85/2005/NĐ-CP

DECREE
On the establishment of communes of Nui Thanh and Dien Ban districts, Quang Nam province
This Decree provides for the establishment of Tam My Dong, Tam My Tay, Tam Anh Bac and Tam Anh Nam communes of Nui Thanh district and Dien Nam Bac, Dien Nam Trung, Dien Nam Dong, Dien Thang Bac, Dien Thang Trung and Dien Thang Nam communes of Dien Ban district, Quang Nam province.
This it takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
