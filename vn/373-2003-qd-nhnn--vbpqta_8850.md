# Quyết định 373/2003/QĐ-NHNN

DECISION No. 373/2003/QD-NHNN of April 22, 2003 amending and supplementing a number of articles of Decision No. 440/2001/QD-NHNN of April 17, 2001 on loans provided to laborers going abroad to work for given periods of time
This Decision amends and supplements the following articles of Decision No. 440/2001/QD-NHNN of April 17, 2001 on loans provided to laborers going abroad to work for given periods of time:
- To amend and supplement Article 6 on loan security;
- To amend and supplement Article 7 on loan provision procedures.- (Summary)
