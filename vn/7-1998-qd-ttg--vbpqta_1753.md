# Decision 7/1998/QD-TTg

DECISION No.7/1998/QD-TTg of January 16, 1998 on the establishment of the State Steering Committee for the project for afforestation on five (5) million hectares during the 1998-2010 period.
The State Steering Committee shall comprise Deputy Prime Minister Nguyen Cong Tan, as its Chairman, the Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development as its Standing Vice-Chairman and other members including leading officials of the Ministry of Planning and Investment, the Commission for Ethnicities and Mountainous Areas, the Ministry of Finance, the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment, the Ministry of Defense, the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs, the State Bank of Vietnam, the General Land Administration and representatives of leading officials of the National Assembly's Council for Nationalities, the Vietnam Peasants' Association and the Central Committee of Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union.- (Summary)
