# Decision 192/2003/QD-BBCVT

DECISION No. 192/2003/QD-BBCVT of December 22, 2003 approving the planning on allocation of frequency channels for ground-like television of VHF/UHF bands till 2010
The Minister of Post and Telematics approved the planning on allocation of frequency channels for ground-like television of VHF/UHF band till 2010 aiming to prescribe the frequency bands used for ground television and allocate frequency channels for ground-like television stations, thus meeting the requirements for reasonable use of frequency for transmission of national as well as local television programs till 2010.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
