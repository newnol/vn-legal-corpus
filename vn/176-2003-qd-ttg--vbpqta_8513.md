# Quyết định 176/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 176/2003/QD-TTg of September 1, 2003 on transforming the Inland Waterway Cargo Transport and Handling Company to experimentally operate after the parent company-affiliate company model
This Decision approves the scheme on transforming the Inland Waterway Cargo Transport and Handling Company to experimentally operate after the parent company-affiliate company model.
The parent company (the Inland Waterway Cargo Transport and Handling Company) is an independent-accounting State enterprise.
Affiliate companies are joint-stock companies or two-plus member limited liability companies with the parent company holding dominant shares or dominant contributed capital.- (Summary)
