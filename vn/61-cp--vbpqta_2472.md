# Decree 61/CP

Decree No.61-CP of June 7, 1997 on the establishment of Yen Thinh township, Yen Mo district, Ninh Binh province.
To establish Yen Thinh township of Yen Mo district, Ninh Binh province, with a land area of 56.2 hectares, a population of 1,347 of Yen Phu commune plus a land area of 99.32 hectares and a population of 2,776 of Khanh Thinh commune.- (Summary)
