# Decision 96/2004/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 96/2004/QD-BTC of December 8, 2004 on management of the budget investment capital allocated to Vietnam Petroleum Corporation from the sources of profits distributed from Vietsovpetro Joint-Venture and product-sharing contracts (PSC)
Under this Decision, as from 2004, Vietnam Petroleum Corporation shall have to remit into the State budget the after-tax profits distributed from Vietsovpetro Petroleum Joint-Venture and the proceeds from product-sharing contracts in strict accordance with the State’s regulations.
