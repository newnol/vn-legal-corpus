# Thông tư 14/2002/TT-BLĐTBXH

CIRCULAR No. 14/2002/TT-BLDTBXH of September 11, 2002 providing guidance on procedures and dossiers of application for certification of fallen heroes for cases left after the revolutionary and resistance war periods
This Circular guides the remaining cases entitled to be considered for compiling dossiers of application for recognition of fallen heroes as well as the procedures, dossiers, competence and responsibility for compiling such dossiers.- (Summary)
