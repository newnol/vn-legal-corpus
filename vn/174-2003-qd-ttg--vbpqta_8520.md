# Quyết định 174/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 174/2003/QD-TTg of August 20, 2003 establishing Ho Chi Minh City Banking University
To establish Ho Chi Minh City Banking University on the basis of the Ho Chi Minh city-based branch of the Banking Institute.
Ho Chi Minh City Banking University is attached to the State Bank of Vietnam, has the legal person status. It is tasked to:
- Train university-degree human resources in the monetary, credit and banking domain;
- Conduct scientific research in service of socio-economic development.- (Summary)
