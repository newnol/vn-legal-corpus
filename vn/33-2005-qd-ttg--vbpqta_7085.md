# Decision 33/2005/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 33/2005/QD-TTg of February 15, 2005 promulgating the Provisional Regulation on conditions, criteria, order and procedures for conferment of Ho Chi Minh awards and State awards on science and technology in 2005
According to this Decision, the conferment of Ho Chi Minh awards and State awards on science and technology in 2005 must be completed before April 10, 2005 for the grassroots level; must be made between April 11, 2005 and May 15, 2005 for the ministerial level, and between May 16, 2005 and July 11, 2005 for the national level.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
