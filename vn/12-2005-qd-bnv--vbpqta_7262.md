# Quyết định 12/2005/QĐ-BNV

DECISION No. 12/2005/QD-BNV of January 5, 2005 permitting the establishment of the Association for Counterfeits Combat and Intellectual Property Right Protection of Foreign-Invested Enterprises in Vietnam
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, the Association for Counterfeits Combat and Intellectual Property Right Protection of Foreign-Invested Enterprises in Vietnam shall be organized and operate according to its Charter and be subject to the State management by the Ministry of Planning and Investment.- (Summary)
