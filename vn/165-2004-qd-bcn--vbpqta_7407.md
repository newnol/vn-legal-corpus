# Quyết định 165/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 165/2004/QD-BCN of December 8, 2004 transforming the PetroVietnam Construction Investment Consultancy Company into PetroVietnam Investment Consultancy and Engineering Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, PetroVietnam Investment Consultancy and Engineering Joint-Stock Company (PV Engineering) is headquartered at No. 201 Dien Bien Phu road, Ward 15, Binh Thanh district, Ho Chi Minh city.
Its charter capital is VND 25 billion, 51% of which to be held by the State, 20% sold to its laborers and 29% to outsiders.- (Summary)
