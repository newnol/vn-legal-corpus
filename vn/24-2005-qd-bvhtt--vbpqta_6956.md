# Quyết định 24/2005/QĐ-BVHTT

DECISION
On ranking a national relic
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” the historical relic of the Justice Ministry’s headquarters (1949-1950) in Minh Thanh commune, Son Duong district, Tuyen Quang province, is now a national relic.- (Summary)
