# Decree 159/2006/NĐ-CP

DECREE
On the implementation of the monthly pension regime for armymen who personally joined the anti-America resistance war from April 30, 1975 backwards and have been demobilized
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”
The pension regime provided for in this Decree applies from January 1, 2007.- (Summary)
