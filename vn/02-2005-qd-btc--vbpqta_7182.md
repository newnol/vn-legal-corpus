# Quyết định 02/2005/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 02/2005/QD-BTC of January 12, 2005 canceling the fee for textile and garment quotas for export to the EU and Canadian markets
This Decision cancels the fee for textile and garment quotas for export to the EU and Canadian markets, prescribed in the Finance Minister’s Decision No. 16/2004/QD-BTC of February 12, 2004 and Decision No. 118/2002/QD-BTC of September 25, 2002.
