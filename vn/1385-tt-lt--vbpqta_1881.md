# Thông tư liên tịch 1385/TT-LT

INTER-MINISTERIAL CIRCULAR No.1385/TT-LT of October 11, 1997 guiding the supply and management of uniforms to be worn by civil judgement enforcement officials.
The Circular provides guidances for the provisions on objects, criteria, and time-limits for the use of uniforms; the drawing up of estimates, and the distribution and management of such uniforms.- (Summary)
