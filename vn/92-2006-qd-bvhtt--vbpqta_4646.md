# Quyết định 92/2006/QĐ-BVHTT

DECISION
On ranking a national relic
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” the historical relic of the place where President Ho Chi Minh lived and worked in Khau Ty hill in 1947 in Diem Mac commune, Dinh Hoa district, Thai Nguyen province is ranked as a national relic.- (Summary)
