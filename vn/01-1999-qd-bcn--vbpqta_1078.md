# Decision 01/1999/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No.01/1999/QD-BCN of January 11, 1999 on renaming the Power Surveying and Designing Company 1 into the Power Engineering Consulting Company 1
To rename the Power Surveying and Designing Company 1 into the Power Engineering Consulting Company 1, or PECC 1 for short.
The Power Engineering Consulting Company 1 is an independent economic cost-accounting unit attached to the Vietnam Electricity Corporation and headquartered in Thanh Xuan ward, Thanh Xuan district, Hanoi.
The Company’s business lines include:
- Providing consultancy and services on the construction of power stations, power grids and other industrial and civil projects.
- Producing and testing electric posts of different types
- Building, repairing small-scale hydro power plants
- Building power grids of 35 KV or under.- (Summary)
