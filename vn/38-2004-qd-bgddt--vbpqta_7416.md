# Decision 38/2004/QD-BGDDT

DECISION No. 38/2004/QD-BGDDT of December 2, 2004 promulgating the provisional Regulation on the examination of universities’ quality
The Regulation issued together with this Decision applies to universities, institutes and colleges in the Vietnamese territory.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
