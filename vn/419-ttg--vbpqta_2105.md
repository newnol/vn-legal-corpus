# Decision 419/TTg

Decision No.419-TTg of June 19, 1996 of the Prime Minister on the additional appointment of 12 part-time members of the National Committee for Clean Water Supply and Environmental Hygiene:
- Ngo The Dan, Vice Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development,
- Tao Huu Phung, Vice Minister of Finance,
- Nguyen Thi Hang, Vice Minister of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs,
- Nguyen Tan Van, Vice Minister of Construction,
- Dang Vu Minh, Director of the National Center for Natural Sciences and Technology,
- Nguyen Ngoc Sinh, Director of the Environment Department under the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment,
- Tran Di, Director of the Vietnam Geology Department under the Ministry of Industry,
- Trinh Van Thu, Deputy General Director of the General Department of Meteorology and Hydrology,
- Nguyen Van Da, Deputy Chief of the General Logistics Department under the Ministry of Defense,
- Tran Quang Minh, Deputy General Director of the General Logistics Department under the Ministry of the Interior,
- Nguyen Ngoc Nhat, Director of the Department of Infrastructure under the Ministry of Planning and Investment,
- Hoang Dinh Hoi, Director of the Hygiene and Epidemics Prevention Department under the Ministry of Health.- (Summary)
