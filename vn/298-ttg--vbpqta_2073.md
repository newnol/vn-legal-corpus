# Directive 298/TTg

DIRECTIVE No.298-TTg OF MAY 11, 1996 OF THE PRIME MINISTER ON REVIEWING AND EVALUATING THE 5-YEAR ENFORCEMENT OF THE LAW ON THE PROTECTION, CARE AND EDUCATION OF THE CHILD
This work must be completed by the end of the third quarter of 1996, with emphasis being laid on reviewing the guidance activities of the People�s Committees and the responsible Ministries and branches, especially on the issues concerning the mechanism, policies, organization of implementation and coordination between agencies, branches, mass organizations and localities. The evaluation results shall serve as basis to work out measures for continually directing the implementation of this Law and at the same time, making concrete proposals on undertakings and policies regarding child protection, care and education, including amendments and supplements to the Articles of the Law and other relevant legal documents.- (Summary)
