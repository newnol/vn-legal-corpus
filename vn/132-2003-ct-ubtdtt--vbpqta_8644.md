# Chỉ thị 132/2003/CT-UBTDTT

DIRECTIVE No. 132/2003/CT-UBTDTT of June 11, 2003 enhancing the work of investment and construction management
This Directive, issued by the Minister-Director of the Committee for Physical Training and Sports, aims to enhance the work of investment and construction management in service of the 22nd SEA Games and the 2nd ASEAN Para Games.- (Summary)
