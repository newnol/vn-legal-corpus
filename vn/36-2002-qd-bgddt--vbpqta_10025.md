# Quyết định 36/2002/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION No. 36/2002/QD-BGDDT of August 30, 2002 promulgating the provisional Regulation on educational targets and plans for complementary junior secondary education
To issue together with this Decision the provisional Regulation on the educational targets and plans for complementary junior secondary education.
Complementary junior secondary education aims to help learners consolidate and develop the results of primary education, reach the educational level equivalent to junior secondary education, be able to work and better do their current jobs, seek jobs or continue job-training courses and educational courses of higher level.- (Summary)
