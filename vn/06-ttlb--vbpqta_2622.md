# Joint circular 06/TTLB

Inter-ministerial Circular No. 06/TTLB of February 28, 1997 guiding the regimes of allowances applicable to employees and officials doing special jobs and occupations in enterprises.
This Circular provides guidance on the compulsory food rations, flight safety allowances, allowances for divers, allowances for employees and officials of enterprises who reside and work in areas deficient in fresh water.- (Summary)
