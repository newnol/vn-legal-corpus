# Decision 97/2001/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 97/2001/QD-TTg of June 26, 2001 supplementing the allowance regimes for officials and employees of the health service
This Decision prescribes the supplementation of the allowance regimes applicable to medical official and employees who directly participate in eliminating extremely dangerous epidemics, including cholera, bubonic plague, malaria and HIV/AIDS; the addition of allowances of special professions for medical officials and employees working in establishments which provide medical examination and treatment as well as services for HIV/AIDS patients.- (Summary)
