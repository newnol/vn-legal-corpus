DECISION No. 70/2001/QD-TTg of May 3, 2001 transforming Vietnam Fumigation Company, a State enterprise, into a joint stock company
To transform Vietnam Fumigation Company, which is a State enterprise under the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development, into Vietnam International Inspection Fumigation Joint-Stock Company - VFC.
VFC�s headquarter: No. 29 Ton Duc Thang street, Ben Nghe ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh city.
VFC�s charter capital: VND 12,000,000,000, in which the State�s equities represent 20%, equities sold to the laborers of the enterprise, 60%, and equities sold to other subjects outside the enterprise, 20%.
VFC shall be engaged in the following business lines:
- Production and processing of assorted plant protection drugs and fumigation supplies;
- Provision of fumigation and pest-, and other insect-killing services;
- Import of supplies and chemicals in service of plant protection and plant quarantine and fumigation.
