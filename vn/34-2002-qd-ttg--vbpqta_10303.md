# Decision 34/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 34/2002/QD-TTg of February 20, 2002 on the use and management of the Funeral House at No. 5, Tran Thanh Tong street, Hanoi
The Funeral House at 5 Tran Thanh Tong street, Hanoi shall be used for:
- Organizing funerals according to the national mourning ceremonies;
- Organizing funerals according to State funeral ceremonies;
- Organizing funerals for the subjects specified in this Decision.
This Funeral House shall be managed by the Ministry of Defense.- (Summary)
