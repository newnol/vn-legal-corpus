# Nghị định 05/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 05/2002/ND-CP of January 11, 2002 on the establishment of Yen Bai city of Yen Bai province
To establish Yen Bai city on the basis of the whole natural land area of 5,802 hectares and the population of 81,234 of Yen Bai provincial town. Yen Bai city shall have 11 attached administrative units, being wards of Nguyen Phuc, Hong Ha, Nguyen Thai Hoc, Yen Ninh, Dong Tam, Yen Thinh and Minh Tan and communes of Tuy Loc, Nam Cuong, Tan Thinh and Minh Bao.
Yen Bai province shall comprise nine administrative units being Yen Bai city, Nghia Lo provincial town and rural districts of Van Yen, Van Chan, Tram Tau, Tran Yen, Luc Yen, Mu Cang Chai and Yen Binh.-
