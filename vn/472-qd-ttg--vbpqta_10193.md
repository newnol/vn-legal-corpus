# Decision 472/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 472/QD-TTg of June 18, 2002 approving the Project on population settlement and socio-economic development in Da River population relocation area, Hoa Binh province, the 2002-2006 period
The Project on population settlement and socio-economic development in Da River population relocation area, Hoa Binh province, the 2002-2006 period is an overall project which aims to continue implementing and completing the objectives of population settlement and socio-economic development in Da River population relocation area.
On the basis of this overall project, the People�s Committee of Hoa Binh province shall have to direct the continued study, formulation and approval of component projects in line with the practical demands and the current regulations on investment and construction management.- (Summary)
