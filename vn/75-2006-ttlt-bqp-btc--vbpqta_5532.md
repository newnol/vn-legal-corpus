# Joint circular 75/2006/TTLT/BQP-BTC 

JOINT CIRCULAR
On funds for the purchase of the Quan Doi Nhan Dan (People’s Army) daily, applicable to agencies engaged in defense work at ministries, ministerial-level agencies and Government-attached agencies; commune, ward and township Military Commands; and Military Commands at agencies, organizations and militia and self-defense units
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
