# Circular 50/TT-PC

the Ministry of Communications and Transport
Circular No.50-TT/PC of March 1st, 1997 guiding the implementation of Decree No.39-CP of July 5, 1996 of the Government on ensuring railway traffic order and safety.
The Circular details some provisions regarding special-purpose railways; the safety protection area of railway bridges; competence to grant permits for the construction and repair of railway projects; competence to ratify and issue timetables of train services; the prescribed capacity of the carriage; and other regulations.- (Summary)
