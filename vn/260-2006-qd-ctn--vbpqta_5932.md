# Quyết định 260/2006/QĐ-CTN

DECISION
Permitting the relinquishment of Vietnamese nationality
Under this Decision, which takes effect after its signing, the Prime Minister permits 87 citizens residing in the Republic of Korea, 666 in the Federal Republic of Germany; 28 in the Republic of Austria; 8 in the Republic of Poland; 1 in the Kingdom of Sweden; 11 in the Kingdom of Denmark; 2 in the Czech Republic; 39 in the People’s Democratic Republic of Laos; and 1 in the Republic of Bulgaria, to relinquish the Vietnamese nationality.- (Summary)
