# Decision 45/2002/QD-BGDDT

DECISION No. 45/2002/QD-BGDDT of October 29, 2002 promulgating the program on Marxist-Leninist philosophy study subject for majors of social sciences, humanities, natural sciences and techniques; program on Marxist-Leninist political economy study subject for economics-business administration majors; and program on Marxist-Leninist political economy study subject for majors other than economics-business administration majors in universities
To issue together with this Decision the programs on Marxist-Leninist philosophy study subject for majors of social sciences, humanities, natural sciences and techniques; program on Marxist-Leninist political economy study subject for economics-business administration majors; and program on Marxist-Leninist political economy study subjects for majors other than economics-business administration majors in universities.
The programs of Marxist-Leninist philosophy and Marxist-Leninist political economy study subjects for economics-business administration majors, and Marxist-Leninist political economy study subject for majors other than economics-business administration majors in universities shall replace the programs on Marxist-Leninist philosophy study subject and Marxist-Leninist political economy study subject in universities and colleges, issued together with Decision No.2054/QD-CT-HS of August 31, 1991 of the Minister of Education and Training, and be implemented as from the 2002-2003 academic year.- (Summary)
