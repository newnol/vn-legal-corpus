# Nghị quyết 49/2005/QH11

RESOLUTION
On the 2006 Law- and Ordinance-making program
THE NATIONAL ASSEMBLY OF THE SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF VIETNAM
Pursuant to the 1992 Constitution of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam, which was amended and supplemented under Resolution No. 51/2001/QH10 of December 25, 2001, of the XIth National Assembly, the 10th session;
Pursuant to the Law on Organization of the National Assembly and the Law on Promulgation of Legal Documents;
Having considered the National Assembly Standing Committee's Report No. 418/UBTVQH11 of October 18, 2005, on the 2006 tentative law-and ordinance-making program and opinions of National Assembly deputies;

Article 3.-
1. To assign the National Assembly Standing Committee to direct the implementation of the 2006 law- and ordinance-making program.
2. The agencies that draft and submit bills, ordinances or resolutions shall work out specific plans on organization of the implementation thereof, ensuring the quality and drafting schedule of bills, ordinances, and resolutions as well as the timely promulgation of guiding documents.
3. The National Assembly's Nationality Council and other Committees shall take initiative in coordinating with drafting agencies and other concerned agencies in raising the quality of bills and draft ordinances, reporting on the appraisal thereof and assisting the National Assembly Standing Committee in gathering comments on and adjusting bills, ordinances and resolutions; to enhance supervision over the promulgation of documents guiding the implementation of laws, ordinances and resolutions.
4. The Government shall work out measures to intensify law dissemination, propagation and introduction among all agencies, organizations, and individuals for uniform understanding and implementation.
This Resolution was passed on November 11, 2005, by the XIth National Assembly of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam at its 6th session
