# Quyết định 127/2005/QĐ-NHNN

DECISION No. 127/2005/QD-NHNN of February 3, 2005 amending and supplementing a number of articles of the Regulation on the provision of loans by credit institutions for their customers, issued together with the State Bank Governor’s Decision No. 1627/2001/QD-NHNN of December 31, 2001
This Decision amends and supplements Clause 2 of Article 2, Clauses 4, 5 and 6 of Article 3, and Clause 2 of Article 13; amends Articles 21 and 22, and Clause 1 of Article 23; and replaces the term “legal persons” in Articles 7 and 10 with the word “organizations”, of above-said Decision No. 1627/2001/QD-NHNN.
