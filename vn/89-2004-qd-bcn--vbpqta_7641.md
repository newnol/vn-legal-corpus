# Quyết định 89/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 89/2004/QD-BCN of September 13, 2004 transforming Hanoi Mechanical Company into Hanoi Mechanical Limited Company
Under this Decision, Hanoi Mechanical Limited Company (HAMECO. Ltd.) is headquartered at No. 74 Nguyen Trai road, Thanh Xuan district, Hanoi city.
Its charter capital is VND 52,684,560,000.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
