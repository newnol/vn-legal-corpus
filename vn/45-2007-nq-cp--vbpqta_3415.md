# Nghị quyết 45/2007/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
On the plan on the implementation of the National Assembly’s Resolution on the structure and organization of the Government of the XII th Legislature
On August 5, 2007, the XII th cabinet held its first meeting where it adopted a plan on the implementation of the National Assembly’s Resolution on the structure of the Government of the XII th Legislature and discussed socio-economic situation in the first seven months of 2007 and some key tasks in the near future.- (Summary)
