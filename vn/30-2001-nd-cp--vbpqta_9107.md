# Nghị định 30/2001/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 30/2001/ND-CP of June 21, 2001 adjusting the administrative boundaries of Dak Nong and Dak Mil districts for the establishment of Dak Song district, Dak Lak province
To establish Dak Song district on the basis of the entire land area and population of Dak Rung and Truong Xuan communes of Dak Nong district and Dak Mol, Thuan Hanh and Dak Song communes of Dak Mil district.
Dak Song district shall have a land area of 80,811 ha and a population of 28,380.- (Summary)
