# Quyết định 29/2005/QĐ-BGD

DECISION
Promulgating framework tertiary-education programs of social science disciplines of university degree
This Decision promulgates 10 framework tertiary-education programs of social science disciplines of university degree, which shall be used in universities, institutes and faculties.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO".- (Summary)
