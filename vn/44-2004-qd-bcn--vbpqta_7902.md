# Quyết định 44/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 44/2004/QD-BCN of June 8, 2004 transforming Bien Hoa Wool Factory of Vietnam Wool Company into Bien Hoa Spinning Knitting Joint-Stock Company
To transform Bien Hoa Wool Factory of Vietnam Wool Company, an independent cost-accounting member enterprise of Vietnam Textile and Garment Corporation, into Bien Hoa Spinning Knitting Joint-Stock Company (COTYLEN).
Its head-office: Road 2, Bien Hoa 1 Industrial Park, Bien Hoa city, Dong Nai province.
Its charter capital is VND 9.7 billion, with 20% of which being held by the State, 41.02% sold to its laborers, and 38.98% sold to outsiders.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
