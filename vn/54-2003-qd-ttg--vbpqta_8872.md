# Quyết định 54/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 54/2003/QD-TTg of April 15, 2003 approving the master plan for reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Gia Lai province in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises with 100% of their charter capital being held by the State: 4 public-utility enterprises and 12 business enterprises to be kept intact; 15 forestry farms to be reorganized according to the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 187/1999/QD-TTg of September 16, 1999 on renewing the organization and management mechanisms of State-run forestry farms; and 1 enterprise to have its sections equitized.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding over 51% of their charter capital when shares are sold for the first time: 2 in 2003 and 3 in 2004.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding shares at low levels or holding no shares: 5 in 2003, 2 in 2004 and 1 in 2005.
Enterprises to be dissolved or bankrupted: 2 in 2003 and 3 in 2004.- (Summary)
