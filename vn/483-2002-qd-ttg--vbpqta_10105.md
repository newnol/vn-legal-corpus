# Decision 483/2002/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 483/QD-TTg of June 20, 2002 transforming the Southern Fabrics-Fiber-Garment Company into a joint-stock company
To transform the Southern Fabrics-Fiber-Garment Company, which is under the Ministry of Trade, into Nam Tien Joint-Stock Company.
Its head office: 80 Truong Dinh street, ward 9, district 3, Ho Chi Minh City.
Its charter capital: VND 11.7 billion (Eleven billion and seven hundred million Vietnam dong).
Equitization form: Selling part of the value of the existing State capital at the enterprise, of which the State equities represent 20% of the charter capital, equities sold to laborers inside the enterprise, 60%, and equities sold to the outsiders, 20%.- (Summary)
