# Decision 252/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 252/2003/QD-BCN of December 31, 2003 establishing the Garment and Textile Technical-Economic Secondary School under Vietnam Textile and Garment Corporation
To establish the Garment and Textile Technical-Economic Secondary School on the basis of Nam Dinh Textile-Garment Job-Training School under Vietnam Textile and Garment Corporation.
Its head-office: 6 Hoang Dieu road, Nang Tinh ward, Nam Dinh city, Nam Dinh province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces Decision No. 934/CNn-TCLD of July 25, 1968 of the Minister of Light Industry (now the Ministry of Industry).- (Summary)
