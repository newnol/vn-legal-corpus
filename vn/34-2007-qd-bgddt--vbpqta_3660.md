# Decision 34/2007/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION
Amending and supplementing a number of articles of the Regulation on lower secondary and upper secondary education graduation examinations, issued together with the Education and Training Minister’s Decision No. 17/2002/QD-BGD&DT of April 5, 2002, which was amended and supplemented under Decision No. 13/2004/QD-BGD&DT of April 28, 2004, Decision No. 06/2005/QD-BGD&DT of February 17, 2005, Decision No. 19/2006/QD-BGDDT of May 11, 2006, and Decision No. 11/2007/QD-BGDDT of April 27, 2007
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
