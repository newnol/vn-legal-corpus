# Nghị định 95/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 95/2003/ND-CP of August 20, 2003 establishing communes and wards in Song Hinh and Phu Hoa districts and Tuy Hoa provincial capital, Phu Yen province
To establish Ealy commune of Song Hinh district on the basis of part of the land area and population of Eabar commune.
To divide Hoa Quang commune of Phu Hoa district into Southern Hoa Quang and Northern Hoa Quang communes.
To establish ward 9 of Tuy Hoa provincial capital on the basis of part of the land area and population of Binh Kien commune.- (Summary)
