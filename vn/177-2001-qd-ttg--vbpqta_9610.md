# Quyết định 177/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 177/2001/QD-TTg of November 9, 2001 on the establishment of the Managing Board of the national target program on hunger elimination, poverty alleviation and employment in the 2001-2005 period
The Management Board of the national target program on hunger elimination, poverty alleviation and employment in the 2001-2005 period shall be composed of a Deputy Prime Minister as its Chairman, the Minister of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs as its Vice Chairman and representatives of the concerned ministries and branches as its members.
