# Quyết định 99/2004/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 99/2004/QD-BTC of December 17, 2004 supplementing the list of projects eligible for the State’s development investment credit loans
With this Decision, the Finance Minister adds new investment projects on cement production to the list of projects eligible for the State’s development investment credit loans, issued together with the Finance Minister’s Decision No. 54/2004/QD-BTC of June 16, 2004. The loan amount provided to each of these projects for purchase of home-made equipment must not exceed 30% of their total investment capital.
The deadline for investors having the above-said projects and the Development Assistance Fund to sign credit contracts is December 31, 2005.
