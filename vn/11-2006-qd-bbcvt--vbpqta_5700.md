# Decision 11/2006/QĐ-BBCVT

DECISION
On the adjustment of Inmarsat service charges
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” the maximum (ceiling) rates of Inmarsat services charged on customers shall be those specified in Decision No. 839/2001/QD-TCBD of October 5, 2001, of the General Department of Post (now the Ministry of Post and Telematics).- (Summary)
