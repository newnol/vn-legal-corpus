# Circular 12/2006/TT-BLĐTBXH

CIRCULAR
Guiding the adjustment of wages and wage-based allowances for employees of enterprises under the Government’s Decree No. 94/2006/ND-CP of September 7, 2006
This Circular shall take effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and annul Circular No. 25/TT-BLDTBXH of October 4, 2005, guiding the adjustment of wages and wage-based allowances under the Government’s Decree No. 118/2005/ND-CP of September 15, 2005.- (Summary)
