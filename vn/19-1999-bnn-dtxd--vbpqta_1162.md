# Circular 19/1999/BNN-DTXD

CIRCULAR No.19/1999/BNN-DTXD of January 26, 1999 amending and supplementing Circular No.08/NN/DTXD/TT of September 20, 1997 on guidances for the implementation of the Regulation on Bidding for investment projects of the units attached to the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development
The Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development issued Circular No.08/NN/DTXD/TT on September 20, 1997 guiding the implementation of the Regulation on Bidding issued together with Decree No.43/CP of July 16, 1996 and Decree No.93/CP of August 23, 1997 of the Government.
To meet the requirements of the management and organization of bids for the Ministry-run investment projects in the new situation, pending a new Bidding Regulation issued by the Government to replace the old one, the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development provides temporary amendments and supplements to a number of points of Circular No.08/NN/DTXD/TT.- (Summary)
