# Quyết định 289/TTg

DECISION No.289-TTg of May 3, 1997 ratifying the agreement on the establishment of the International Vaccine Institute in Seoul, the Republic of Korea.
To ratify "the Agreement on the Establishment of the International Vaccine Institute" in Seoul, the Republic of Korea, which was signed by the Ambassador, Head of Vietnamese Mission at the United Nations in New York on October 28, 1996.
The Ministry for Foreign Affairs shall carry out diplomatic procedures for the ratification and notify the concerned agencies of the effective date of the above-said agreement.- (Summary)
