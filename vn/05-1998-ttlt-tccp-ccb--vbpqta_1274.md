# Thông tư liên tịch 05/1998/TTLT/TCCP-CCB

Joint Circular No. 05/1998/TTLT-TCCP-CCB of September 30, 1998 on the organization and operation of the latter’s chapters in administrative and non-business agencies and State enterprises
The War Veterans’ Association organizations in the administrative and non-business agencies and State enterprises shall be placed under the direct and comprehensive leadership of the Party Committees and the State management by agency heads.
This Circular provides detailed guidance on the organizational structure, tasks and operations of War Veterans’ Association organizations in the administrative and non-business agencies and State enterprises.- (Summary)
