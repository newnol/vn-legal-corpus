# Chỉ thị 32/2004/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE No. 32/2004/CT-TTg of September 17, 2004 on a number of measures to prevent flash floods in mountainous provinces
With this Directive, the Prime Minister requests the concerned ministries, branches and localities to carry out a number of activities to prevent and minimize damage caused by flash floods in mountainous provinces.- (Summary)
