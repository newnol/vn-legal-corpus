# Decision 08/2006/QĐ-BVHTT

DECISION
On ranking a national relic
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” the historical relic of the headquarters of the Ministry of National Education (1951-1954) in Yen Nguyen commune, Chiem Hoa district, Tuyen Quang province, is ranked a national relic.- (Summary)
