# Quyết định 174/1998/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 174/1998/QD-TTg of September 15, 1998 on the establishment of the Vietnam Industrial Construction Corporation
To split Electricity Construction and Installation Companies No. 1, 2, 3 and 4 attached to the Vietnam Electricity Corporation; the Chemical Construction and Installation Company attached to the Vietnam Chemical Corporation; the Construction, Installa-tion and Production Company attached to the Vietnam Steel Corporation; and other industrial construction and installation companies from their parent corporations for joining the to be established Vietnam Industrial Construction Corporation.
To ratify the project on the establishment of the Vietnam Industrial Construction Corporation which shall operate according to the model prescribed in Decision No. 90-TTg of March 7, 1994 of the Government and on the basis of reorganizing construction and installation companies of the above-said industries. To authorize the Minister of Industry to organize, on the basis of the current provisions of law, the evaluation of the project and sign Decision on the establishment of this Corporation.- (Summary)
