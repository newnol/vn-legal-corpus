# Nghị định 83/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 83/2002/ND-CP of October 22, 2002 on the establishment of communes and wards of Chau Duc and Xuyen Moc districts, Ba Ria provincial town and Vung Tau city of Ba Ria � Vung Tau province
To establish ward 12 of Vung Tau city on the basis of a land area of 3,429.8 ha and a population of 15,826 of ward 11. Ward 11�s land area shall be reduced to 1,069.2 ha with a population of 10,001.
To establish Long Huong ward of Ba Ria provincial town on the basis of a land area of 1,485.1 ha and a population of 6,767 of Long Huong commune.
To establish Kim Dinh ward of Ba Ria provincial town on the basis of the remaining land area of 1,804.9 ha and a population of 7,356 of Long Huong commune.
To wholly transfer Lien Hiep 2 hamlet of Xa Bang commune with a land area of 941 ha and a population of 2,744, and Tan Giao hamlet of Ngai Giao district township with a land area of 627 ha and a population of 875, to Lang Lon commune. After the adjustment of the administrative boundaries, Lang Lon commune shall have a land area of 6,760 ha and a population of 13,670; Xa Bang commune shall have a land area of 3,785 ha and a population of 12,325; Ngai Giao district township shall have a land area of 2,490.6 ha and a population of 14,823.
To establish Cu Bi commune on the basis of a land area of 4,671 ha and a population of 8,642 of Lang Lon commune.
To establish Tan Lam commune of Xuyen Moc district on the basis of a land area of 8,557.995 ha and a population of 5,593 of Bau Lam commune. Bau Lam commune�s land area shall be reduced to 3,519.165 ha with a population of 8,970.- (Summary)
