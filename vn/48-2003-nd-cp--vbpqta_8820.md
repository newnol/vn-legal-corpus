# Nghị định 48/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 48/2003/ND-CP of May 12, 2003 on the establishment of communes, wards and townships of Chau Thanh A, O Mon and Phung Hiep rural districts and Vi Thanh provincial town, Can Tho province
To establish Mot Ngan township, the district capital of Chau Thanh A rural district, on the basis of a part of the natural land area and population of Tan Thuan and Nhon Nghia A communes. Mot Ngan township shall have a natural land area of 730 ha and a population of 6,756. Tan Thuan commune shall be left with a natural land area of 1,578 ha and a population of 7,627, and Nhon Nghia A commune with a natural land area of 1,543 ha and a population of 11,301.
To establish Xuan Thang commune of O Mon rural district on the basis of a natural land area of 1,207.7 ha and a population of 7,645 of Thoi Lai commune. Thoi Lai commune shall be left with a natural land area of 2,608.53 ha and a population of 11, 157.
To establish Tan Thanh commune of Phung Hiep rural district on the basis of a natural land area of 1,481.1 ha and a population of 6,924 of Dai Thanh commune, which is now reduced to a natural land area of 2,696.32 ha and a population of 13,486.
To establish ward VII of Vi Thanh provincial town on the basis of a natural land area of 616 ha and a population of 6,625 of Hoa Luu commune, which is now left with a natural land area of 1,711 ha and a population of 7,122.- (Summary)
