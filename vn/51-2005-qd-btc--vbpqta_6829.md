# Quyết định 51/2005/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
On the interest rate for the 2005 Government bonds, drive III, issued directly via State treasuries
Under this Decision, as from August 1, 2005, the interest rate of 8.6%/year shall apply to the 2005 Government bonds, drive III, issued directly via State treasuries for investment in a number of key irrigation and traffic projects.
This Decision takes effect after its signing.- (Summary)
