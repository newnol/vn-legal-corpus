# Chỉ thị 11/2006/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On job-training and employment supports for laborers in areas where agricultural land is used for other purposes
With this Directive, the Prime Minister assigns several tasks to ministries, branches and provincial/municipal People’s Committees in order to create jobs, raise incomes and improve living conditions of people in areas where agricultural land is used for other purposes.- (Summary)
