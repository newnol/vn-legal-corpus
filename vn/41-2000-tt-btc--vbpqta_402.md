# Thông tư 41/2000/TT-BTC

THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE
CIRCULAR No. 41/2000/TT-BTC of May 19, 2000 guiding the financial management of the national target programs as well as other target programs allowed for the application of the national target program mechanism
This Circular guides the implementation of the regulations on the assignment and registration of budget estimate norms; on the allocation of capital and funding; expenditure reports and final settlement; on funding for agencies managing national target programs and other target programs allowed for the application of national target program mechanism.- (Summary)
