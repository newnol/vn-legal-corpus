# Quyết định 249/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 249/2003/QD-TTg of November 20, 2003 on the establishment of the External Affairs Service of Dong Nai province
To establish the External Affairs Service of Dong Nai province on the basis of reorganizing the External Affairs Bureau under the Office of the People's Council and People's Committee of Dong Nai province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
