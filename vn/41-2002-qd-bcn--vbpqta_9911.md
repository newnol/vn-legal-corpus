# Decision 41/2002/QD-BCN

DECISION No. 41/2002/QD-BCN of October 4, 2002 promulgating the Regulation on the inspection and pre-acceptance test of the volume of encallowed soil and rock as well as the output of coal exploited at open-cast mines
The Regulation on the inspection and pre-acceptance test of the volume of encallowed soil and rock as well as the output of coal exploited at open-cast mines, issued together with this Decision, applies to enterprises which exploit coal by open-cast mining method or which are being in the process of capital construction in preparation for open-cast mining.
The inspection and pre-acceptance test of the volume of encallowed soil and rock as well as the output of coal exploited at open-cast mines must be determined by measuring method. The measurement figures shall be the official figures used for the calculation of exploited coal output as well as encallowed soil and rock volume.
