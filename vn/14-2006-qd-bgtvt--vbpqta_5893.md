# Decision 14/2006/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION
Promulgating branch standard 22 TCN 302-06: “Motor vehicles operating on roads - City buses - Technical requirements”
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.” Particularly, Section 4.9.1 of the standard shall apply 24 months after the effective date of this Decision.
For motor vehicles which had been granted quality certificates before the effective date of this Decision, their manufacture or assembly may be prolonged up to 12 months.- (Summary)
