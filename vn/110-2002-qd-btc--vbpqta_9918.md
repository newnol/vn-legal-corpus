# Quyết định 110/2002/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 110/2002/QD-BTC of September 18, 2002 amending and supplementing the regime of issuance, management and use of goods sale invoices
This Circular amends and supplements the regime of issuance, management and use of goods sale invoices, issued together with Decision No. 885/1998/QD-BTC of July 16, 1998 of the Minister of Finance. Concretely, it amends and supplements Article 10 (on the procedures for invoice purchase) and Article 22 (on the tax offices� refusal to sell invoices to, or decision to suspend the use of invoices by, organizations and/or individuals violating the regulations on use of invoices).- (Summary)
