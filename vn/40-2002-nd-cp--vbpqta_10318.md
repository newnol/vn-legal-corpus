# Decree 40/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 40/2002/ND-CP of April 10, 2002 establishing communes of Quynh Luu, Thanh Chuong and Nghi Loc districts, Nghe An province
To establish Tan Thang commune of Quynh Luu district with a land area of 7,018 ha and a population of 3,019.
To establish Thanh Duc commune of Thanh Chuong district with a land area of 17,000.76 ha and a population of 4,870.
To divide Nghi Cong commune of Nghi Loc district into Nghi Cong Bac and Nghi Cong Nam communes. Nghi Cong Bac shall have a land area of 1,500 ha and a population of 4,822. Nghi Cong Nam commune shall have a land area of 1,807 ha and a population of 5,718.- (Summary)
