# Joint circular 105/2002/TTLT/BTC-BYT

To supplement Point 1, Section II of Part B – Specific spending contents of the Project on malaria prevention and combat:
"1.12. Expenses for transportation of drugs, chemicals and medical devices to communes, villages and hamlets in service of malaria prevention and combat."- (Summary)
