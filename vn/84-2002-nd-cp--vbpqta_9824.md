# Nghị định 84/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 84/2002/ND-CP of October 22, 2002 establishing Thai Binh and Thinh Lang wards of Hoa Binh provincial capital, Hoa Binh province
To establish Thai Binh ward on the basis of the whole land area of 1,198.89 ha and population of 5,242 of Thai Binh commune.
To establish Thinh Lang ward on the basis of the whole land area of 288.05 ha and population of 4,534 of Thinh Lang commune.- (Summary)
