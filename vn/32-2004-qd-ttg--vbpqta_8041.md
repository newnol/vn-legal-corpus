# Quyết định 32/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 32/2004/QD-TTg of March 9, 2004 renaming Dak Lak province's Industrial Parks Management Board Dak Nong province's Industrial Parks Management Board
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Dak Lak province's Industrial Parks Manage-ment Board is renamed Dak Nong province's Industrial Parks Management Board.- (Sumamry)
