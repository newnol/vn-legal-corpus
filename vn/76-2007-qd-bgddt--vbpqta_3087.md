# Decision 76/2007/QD-BGDDT 

DECISION
Promulgating the Regulation on process and cycle of examining the educational quality in universities, colleges and professional secondary schools
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces Chapters I, III, IV, V and VI of the Provisional Regulation on examination of the quality of universities promulgated together with Decision No. 38/2004/QD-BGDDT of December 2, 2004; and Decision No. 27/2006/QD-BGDDT of June 27, 2006.- (Summary)
