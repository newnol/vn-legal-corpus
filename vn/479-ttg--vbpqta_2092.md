# Decision 479/TTg

Decision No.479-TTg of July 24, 1996 of the Prime Minister to upgrade the Intermediate Mining Techniques School into the Mining Techniques College.
The Mining Techniques College is placed directly under the Ministry of Industry and subject to the branch management of the Ministry of Education and Training.- (Summary)
