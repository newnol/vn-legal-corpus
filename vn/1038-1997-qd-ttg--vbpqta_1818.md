# Quyết định 1038/1997/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.1038/1997/QD-TTg of December 5, 1997 on the establishment of the Center for Securities and Stock Market Studies and Professional Fostering, which is attached to the State Securities Committee.
The Center is a non-business establishment that has revenues and the legal person status.
The Center is tasked to: conduct scientific research on securities and stock market; supply scientific information on securities and stock market; organize professional fostering courses on securities management and business; enter cooperation with training and scientific research organizations inside and outside the country in the field of securities management and business.- (Summary)
