# Decree 36/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 36/2002/ND-CP of April 5, 2002 adjusting the administrative boundaries for the establishment of communes and a district township of the districts of Phuoc Long, Bu Dang, Binh Long and Dong Phu, Binh Phuoc province
To establish Phu Nghia commune of Phuoc Long district with a land area of 14,501 ha and a population of 7,455.
To establish Phuoc Son commune of Bu Dang district with a land area of 10,300 ha and a population of 4,841.
To establish Minh Thang commune of Binh Long district with a land area of 3,600.4 ha and a population of 3,634.
To establish Thuan Phu commune of Dong Phu district with a land area of 8,735 ha and a population of 10,253.
To establish Dong Tien commune of Dong Phu district with a land area of 5,210 ha and a population of 9,686.
To establish Tan Tien commune of Dong Phu district with a land area of 6,740 ha and a population of 7,431.
To establish Tan Phu district township of Dong Phu district with a land area of 3,102 ha and a population of 5,631.- (Summary)
