# Quyết định 21/2002/QĐ-BTS

DECISION No. 21/2002/QD-BTS of December 31, 2002 promulgating the Regulation on protection of State secrets at the Aquatic Resources Ministry's office
The Regulation, issued together with this Decision, prescribes the management, use and protection of the Aquatic Resources Ministry's State secrets, including:
- List of State secrets in the aquatic resources sector, approved by the Prime Minister. Secret documents sent to the Ministry by central and local agencies.
- Information on the Ministry's undertakings, policies and executive measures which are under discussion and processing.
- Hand-written comments on daily work by the Ministry's leaders on reports, official dispatches and documents under the Ministry's management.- (Summary)
