# Thông tư liên tịch 16/2000/TTLT/BLĐTBXH-BTC-BKHĐT

THE MINISTRY OF LABOR, WAR INVALIDS AND SOCIAL AFFAIRS - THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE - THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING AND INVESTMENT
JOINT CIRCULAR No. 16/2000/TTLT-BLDTBXH-BTC-BKHDT of July 5, 2000 guiding the supplements and amendments to a number of points of Joint Circular No. 08/1999/TTLT-BLDTBXH-BTC-BKHDT of March 15, 1999, which guides the settlement of risk-struck projects on borrowing capital from the National Employment Support Fund (National Employment Fund)
This Circular amends and supplements Point 3, Section I; Point 1, Section II; Item c, Point 2, Section II; 2 nd em rule of Item c, Point 4, Section II; Item e, Point 4, Section II; it adds Point 5 to Section II of Circular No. 08/1999/TTLT-BLDTBXH-BTC-BKHDT of March 15, 1999.- (Summary)
