# Joint circular 16/1999/TTLT/LDTBXH-TWDTNCSHCM

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 16/1999/TTLT-LDTBXH-TWDTNCSHCM of July 6, 1999 guiding the implementation of policies for youth volunteers who have fulfilled their tasks in the resistance wars
The said Joint Circular guides the implementation of the Prime Minister’s Decision No.104/1999/QD-TTg of April 14, 1999 on a number of policies toward the youth volunteers, who have fulfilled their tasks in the resistance wars.-
