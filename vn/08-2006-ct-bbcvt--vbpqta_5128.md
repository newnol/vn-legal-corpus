# Directive 08/2006/CT-BBCVT

DIRECTIVE
On measures to accelerate the development of fixed telephone service
The Minister of Post and Telematics requests the Ministry’s attached agencies and units, provincial/municipal Post and Telematics Services and telecommunications enterprises to perform several tasks in order to accelerate the development of fixed telephone service.- (Summary)
