# Quyết định 53/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 53/2004/QD-BCN of June 21, 2004 transforming Southern Battery Company into Dry Cell and Storage Battery Joint-Stock Company
To transform Southern Battery Company into Dry Cell and Storage Battery Joint-Stock Company (PINACO).
Its head-office: 321 Tran Hung Dao street, Co Giang ward, district 1, Ho Chi Minh city.
Its charter capital is VND 102,630 million, with 51% of which being held by the State, 39.15% sold to its laborers, and 9.85% to outsiders.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
