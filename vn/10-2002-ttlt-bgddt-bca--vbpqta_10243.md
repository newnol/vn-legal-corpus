# Thông tư liên tịch 10/2002/TTLT/BGDĐT-BCA

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 10/2002/TTLT/BGDDT-BCA of March 22, 2002 on the protection of security and order in schools and educational establishments
It is the responsibility of the educational service to ensure security and order in schools and educational establishments; the police office shall have to coordinate with and provide advice as well as guidance to them.
This Circular aims to enhance the mobilization of officials, employees, teachers,... as well as pupils, students, learners,... to effectively participate in the activities of preventing and suppressing crimes, so as to ensure security and order in schools and educational establishments.- (Summary)
