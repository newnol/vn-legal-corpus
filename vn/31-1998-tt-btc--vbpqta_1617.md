# Circular 31/1998/TT-BTC

Circular No.31/1998/TT-BTC of March 17, 1998 amending Circular No.47/TC/TCT of July 18, 1997 on the collection, payment and management of the business licensing fee.
To amend Point 1, Section II of Circular No.47/TC/TCT as follows: the uniformly applicable business licensing fee is now VND 30,000/license (thirty thousand VND).- (Summary)
