# Quyết định 48/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 48/2004/QD-BCN of June 15, 2004 amending Clause 1 and Clause 3, Article 1 of the Industry Minister's Decision No. 205/2003/QD-BCN of December 3, 2003 on transformation of Metallurgy Construction and Installation Factory under the Southern Steel Company into the Southern Investment and Construction Joint-Stock Company
To amend Clause 1 and Clause 3, Article 1 of Decision No. 205/2003/QD-BCN of December 3, 2003 on transformation of Metallurgy Construction and Installation Factory under the Southern Steel Company into the Southern Investment and Construction Joint-Stock Company regarding the charter capital structure (the State's share percentage: 20%; shares to be sold to the company's laborers: 18.34%; and shares to be sold to outsiders: 61.66%) and the preferences for the company's laborers (preferential shares to be sold to the company's laborers is 9,472, valued at VND 284,160,000).
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
