# Quyết định 105/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.105/1998/QD-TTg of June 9, 1998 allowing the Vietnam Red Cross Society to establish a fund in support of victims of agent orange used during the Vietnam war
To allow the Vietnam Red Cross Society to set up the fund in support of the victims of agent orange used during the Vietnam war with a view to mobilizing various domestic and foreign sources of humanitarian aids to assist those affected by agent orange used during the Vietnam war.- (Summary)
