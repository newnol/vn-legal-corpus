# Thông tư 87/1997/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No.87/1997/TT-BTC of December 3, 1997 providing provisional guidance for the management and use of the Study Promotion Fund.
The Vietnam Study Promotion Fund is set up by the Vietnam Study Promotion Society on the principle of voluntariness and operating not for profit in order to render financial support for educational and training activities, stimulate the emulation of good learning and good teaching in schools. The Vietnam Study Promotion Fund is a social fund set up in accordance with Article 115 of the Civil Code.
This Circular provides provisional guidance on the revenue sources, the management and use of the Fund.- (Summary)
