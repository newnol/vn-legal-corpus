# Joint circular 04/TTLB

Inter-ministerial Circular No. 04/TTLB of January 27, 1997 guiding the readjustment of the wage and allowance levels for officials and employees in the administrative and non-business sector; pensioners; people who have retired due to poor health; commune and ward officials and a number of beneficiaries of social entitlement policies in 1997
This Circular guides the implementation of Decree No.06-CP of January 21, 1997 of the Government, more concretely: the subjects and scope of regulation, the methods for readjustment of wage and allowance levels; the order and procedures for making reports, the approval and allocation of increased wage and allowance funds in 1997.- (Summary)
