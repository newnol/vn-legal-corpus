# Quyết định 226/2004/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION No. 226/2004/QD-BGTVT of February 3, 2004 supplementing the Communications and Transport Minister’s Decision No. 4381/2001/QD-BGTVT of December 19, 2001
This Decision adds the registration codes for special-use motorcycles in Dien Bien, Dak Nong and Hau Giang provinces to Appendix 2 to Decision No. 4381/2001/QD-BGTVT of December 19, 2001 promulgating the Regulation on granting of registration numbers and number plates for special-use motorcycles joining in road traffic and takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
