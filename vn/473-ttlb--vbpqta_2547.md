# Joint circular 473/TTLB

Inter-ministerial Circular No. 473/TTLB of March 10, 1997 providing guidance on the policies towards the militia and self-defense forces and funds for their activities.
This Circular provides guidance on the regime on cost-of-living, allowances, subsidies, uniform allowances; sickness, accident and death allowances; preferential regimes for the personnel of militia and self-defense forces, and also guides the regulations on financial sources to cover these spendings.- (Summary)
