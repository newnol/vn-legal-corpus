# Chương trình 02/2005/CT-BGD

DIRECTIVE No. 02/2005/CT-BGDDT of January 31, 2005 on enhancing education about environmental protection
With this Directive, the Minister of Education and Training requests education-managing authorities and educational establishments to raise their awareness about and well perform environmental protection education.- (Summary)
