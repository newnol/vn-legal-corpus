# Resolution 12/2006/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
On the Government’s May 2006 regular meeting
On May 31, 2006, the Government held its May regular meeting, where it heard:
- The Planning and Investment Minister presenting a draft decree guiding the implementation of the Investment Law and a draft decree guiding investment in the forms of build-operate-transfer (BOT), build-transfer-operate (BTO) and build-transfer (BT) contracts;
- The Public Security Minister presenting a draft decree on entry and exit by Vietnamese citizens; and the Minister-Director of the Government Office reporting cabinet members’ opinions on the draft.
The Government also examined the Agriculture and Rural Development Ministry’s report on national program of action for coordinated prevention and control of avian influenza and human influenza in the 2006-2010 period; the Planning and Investment Ministry’s report on socio-economic, production, business, service and investment situation in May and the first five months of the year; and the Trade Ministry’s report on domestic market and export situation in the same period.- (Summary)
