# Decree 62/2000/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 62/2000/ND-CP of October 30 2000 on the establishment of communes in Don Duong, Duc Trong and Bao Lam districts, Lam Dong province
To establish communes in Don Duong, Duc Trong and Bao Lam districts, Lam Dong province as follows:
- To establish Da Ron commune in Don Duong district on the basis of a land area of 3,349.3 ha and a population of 9,133 of Thach My township.
- To establish Tan Thanh commune in Duc Trong district on the basis of a land area of 2,270 ha and a population of 5,326 of Tan Hoi commune.
