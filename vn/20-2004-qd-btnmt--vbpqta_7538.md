# Decision 20/2004/QD-BTNMT

DECISION No. 20/2004/QD-BTNMT of October 4, 2004 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Environmental Protection Sub-Department in the Central Vietnam and Central Highlands region
The Environmental Protection Sub-Department in the Central Vietnam and Central Highlands region shall assist the director of the Environmental Protection Department in performing the State management over inter-provincial and inter-regional environmental protection in the Central Vietnam and Central Highlands region, including 11 provinces and cities, namely Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh, Phu Yen, Khanh Hoa, Kon Tum, Gia Lai, Dac Lac, Dac Nong, Lam Dong and Da Nang.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
