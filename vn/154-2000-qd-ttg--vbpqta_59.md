# Decision 154/2000/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 154/2000/QD-TTg of December 31, 2000 assigning the task of elaborating the project on State salary policy reforms
The Prime Minister assigns the tasks of elaborating the project on State salary policy reform to the ministries, departments and branches being members of the Steering Committee for State Salary according to the responsibility assignment table, issued together with this Decision.- (Summary)
