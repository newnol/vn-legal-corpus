# Quyết định 111/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 111/2004/QD-BCN of October 13, 2004 transforming Yen Vien Engineering Factory of Electric Equipment Manufacturing Company into Power Engineering Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, the Power Engineering Joint-Stock Company, abbreviated to PEC, shall have a charter capital of VND 20,000,000,000; of which the State’s equities represent 51%, equities sold to laborers in the factory, 44% and equities sold to other subjects, 5%.
Its head office is located at 150 Ha Huy Tap, Yen Vien township, Gia Lam district, Hanoi.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
