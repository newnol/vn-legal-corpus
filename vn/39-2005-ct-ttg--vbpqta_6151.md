# Chỉ thị 39/2005/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On the exercise of rights and performance of obligations of the state owner towards state companies provided for in the Government’s Decree No. 132/2005/ND-CP of October 20, 2005
With this Directive, the Prime Minister instructs ministries, ministerial-level agencies, Government-attached agencies, provincial/municipal People’s Committees, and managing boards of state companies to perform several tasks in order to enhance enterprises’ autonomy, self-responsibility, competitiveness and production and business efficiency.- (Summary)
