# Chỉ thị 29/2003/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE No. 29/2003/CT-TTg of December 23, 2003 on rectifying the management of construc-tion and investment with State capital source
This Directive points out a number of tasks to be performed immediately by the ministries, branches, localities and Corporations 91 in order to overcome weaknesses and loopholes in the management of construction and investment with State capital.- (Summary)
