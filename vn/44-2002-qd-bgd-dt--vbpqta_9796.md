# Quyết định 44/2002/QĐ-BGD&ĐT

DECISION No. 44/2002/QD-BGDDT of October 24, 2002 promulgating the list of postgraduate training majors
To issue together with this Decision the list of postgraduate training majors. The Department for Postgraduate Training shall have to guide the tertiary-training institutions in determining their assigned training majors according to the majors prescribed in this list.- (Summary)
