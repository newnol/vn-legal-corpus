# Quyết định 63/2007/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION
Promulgating the Regulation on on-road transportation of extra-long and extra-heavy cargoes and operation of track vehicles damaging road surfaces, and oversized and overloaded vehicles in excess of limit road sizes
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces Decision No. 824/2002/QD-BGTVT of March 25, 2002, Decision No. 2112/2003/QD-BGTVT of July 21, 2003, and Circular No. 21/2001/TT-BGTVT of December 10, 2001.- (Summary)
