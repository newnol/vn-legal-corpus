# Decision 14/2006/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION
Promulgating the Regulation on organization and operation of inspectorates in higher education establishments and professional secondary schools
The Regulation promulgated together with this Decision provides for the organization, tasks, powers, responsibilities and operation of inspectorates in higher education establishments and professional secondary schools.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
