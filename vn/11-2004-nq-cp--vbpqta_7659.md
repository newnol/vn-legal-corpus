# Nghị quyết 11/2004/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION No. 11/2004/NQ-CP of September 6, 2004 on the Government's August 2004 regular meeting
On August 30 and 31, 2004, the Government held its August regular meeting, where:
- It heard the Finance Minister's reports on draft decrees on methods of determining land prices and land price brackets; on collection of land-use levies; and on compensation, support and resettlement upon the State's withdrawal of land; the Natural Resources and Environment Minister's report on a draft decree on administrative sanctioning in the land field;
- It heard the Planning and Investment Minister's report on the 2005 socio-economic development plan; the Finance Minister's report on the 2004 State budget implementation evaluation and the 2005 State budget estimates. It examined reports on the production, export, import, export and socio-economic situation in August and the first 8 months of 2004, presented by the Minister of Planning and Investment; and heard the Trade Minister's report on the trade situation in August;
- It heard the Justice Minister's report on the draft 2005 legislative program; and the Government Office Minister-Director's report on the cabinet members' comments on the program;
- It heard the Trade Minister's report on the commercial bill (amended); and the Government Office Minister-Director's report on the cabinet members' comments on the bill;
- It heard the Education and Training Minister's report on the education situation;
Also at the meeting, Deputy Prime Minister Pham Gia Khiem briefed the report of the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs, the Ministry of Education and Training and the Nationality Committee on the results of implementation of Program 135, the hunger eradication and poverty reduction program, and the program on solidification of schools and classrooms.- (Summary)
