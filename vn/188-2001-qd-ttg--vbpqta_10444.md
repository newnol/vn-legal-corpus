# Quyết định 188/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 188/2001/QD-TTg of December 11, 2001 allowing Loong Sap and Chieng Khuong border-gates, Son La province, to apply border-gate economic zone policies
Loong Sap (Pa Hang) border-gate economic zone encompasses Loong Sap commune of Moc Chau district.
Chieng Khuong border-gate economic zone encompasses Chieng Khuong commune of Song Ma district.
Loong Sap and Chieng Khuong border-gate economic zones shall be allowed to apply the Prime Minister�s Decision No. 53/2001/QD-TTg of April 19, 2001 on policies towards border-gate economic zones.- (Summary)
