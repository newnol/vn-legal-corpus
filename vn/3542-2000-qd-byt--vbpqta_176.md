# Decision 3542/2000/QD-BYT

THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH
Decision No. 3542/2000/QD-BYT of October 9, 2000 promulgating the Regulation on hygiene and safety for draught beer and low-alcoholic content wine
The Regulation issued together with this Decision sets forth the compulsory requirements on quality, hygiene and safety for draught beer and low-alcoholic content wine, imported or produced for consumption on the Vietnamese market, including the requirements on raw materials and production conditions, physical and chemical criteria, micro-biological criteria and packing safety.- (Summary)
