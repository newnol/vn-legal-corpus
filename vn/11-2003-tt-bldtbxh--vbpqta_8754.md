# Circular 11/2003/TT-BLDTBXH

CIRCULAR No. 11/2003/TT-BLDTBXH of May 22, 2003 guiding amendments and supplements to Circular No. 11/2002/TT-BLDTBXH of June 12, 2002 of the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs on the implementation of a number of articles of the Government’s Decree No. 41/2002/ND-CP of April 11, 2003 on policies towards laborers redundant due to the restructuring of State enterprises
This Circular amends and supplements Points 2, 3 and 6 of Section I, Point 1 of Section II, Points 1 and 2 of Section IV, and 10 forms of Circular No. 11/2002/TT-BLDTBXH.- (Summary)
