# Decision 85/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 85/2001/QD-TTg of June 1, 2001 ranking Ba Mun Nature Conservation Zone, Quang Ninh province, as Bai Tu Long National Garden
To rank Ba Mun Nature Conservation Zone in Van Don island district, Quang Ninh province, as Bai Tu Long National Garden
To assign Quang Ninh People�s Committee to directly manage Bai Tu Long National Garden. The Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development shall assume the prime responsibility and, together with the Ministry of Aquatic Resources, assist Quang Ninh People�s Committee in completing the investment project on building and developing Bai Tu Long National Garden and the investment project on building and developing a buffer zone for Bai Tu Long National Garden for submission to competent authorities for approval, then deploying the implementation thereof according to current regulations.- (Summary)
