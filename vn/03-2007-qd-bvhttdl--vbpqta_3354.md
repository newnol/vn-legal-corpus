# Decision 03/2007/QĐ-BVHTTDL

DECISION
On ranking a national relic
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” the historical relic of the site of banknote-printing factory in Chi Ne plantation (1946-1947) in Co Nghia commune, Lac Thuy district, Hoa Binh province, is ranked as a national relic.- (Summary)
