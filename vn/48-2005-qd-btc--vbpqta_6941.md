# Quyết định 48/2005/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
Amending the preferential import tax rates of a number of medicaments in the preferential import tariffs
THE MINISTER OF FINANCE
Pursuant to the Tariffs according to the List of import tax-liable commodity groups, issued together with Resolution No. 63/NQ-UBTVQH10 of October 10, 1998, of the Standing Committee of the Xth National Assembly, which was amended and supplemented under Resolution No. 399/2003/NQ-UBTVQH11 of June 19, 2003, of the Standing Committee of the XIth National Assembly;
Pursuant to the Government's Decree No. 77/2003/ND-CP of July 1, 2003, defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Ministry of Finance;
Pursuant to Article 1 of the Government's Decree No. 94/1998/ND-CP of November 17, 1998, detailing the implementation of Law No. 04/1998/QH10 of May 20, 1998 Amending and Supplementing a Number of Articles of the Export Tax and Import Tax Law;
At the proposal of the director of the Tax Policy Department,
DECIDES:
Article 1.- To amend the preferential import tax rates of a number of medicaments under Heading No. 3004 of the Preferential Import Tariffs (including Tables I and II), issued together with the Finance Minister's Decision No. 110/2003/QD-BTC of July 25, 2003, into new ones specified in the list of amended import tax rates of a number of commodity items of the Preferential Import Tariffs (Appendices I and II), issued together with this Decision.
For commodity items not specified in Table II, the import tax rates specified in Table I shall apply.
Article 2.- This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."
For the Minister of Finance Vice Minister TRUONG CHI TRUNG
