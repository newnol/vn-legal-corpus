# Decision 10/2006/QĐ-BTS

DECISION
Promulgating the Regulation on registration of fishing vessels and crewmembers
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”
The Regulation promulgated together with this Decision replaces the provisions on registration of fishing vessels and crewmembers in the Regulation on registry of fishing ships and registration of fishing vessels and crewmembers, issued together with Decision No. 494/2001/QD-BTS of June 15, 2001.- (Summary)
