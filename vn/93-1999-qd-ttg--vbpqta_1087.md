# Decision 93/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.93/1999/QD-TTg of April 5, 1999 on the allowance regime for dike controllers who directly take part in handling incidents when flood or storm warnings are announced
The Decision stipulates the allowance levels and sources for dike controllers, who directly take part in handling incidents when flood or storm warnings are made.- (Summary)
