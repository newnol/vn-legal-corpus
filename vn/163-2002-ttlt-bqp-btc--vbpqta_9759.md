# Joint circular 163/2002/TTLT/BQP-BTC

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 163/2002/TTLT/BQP-BTC of November 8, 2002 guiding the implementation of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 116/2002/QD-TTg of September 17, 2002 on the allowance regime for managing officials of detention camps and custody houses under the Defense Ministry
This Joint Circular provides guidance on subjects and levels of the allowances, as well as methods of calculating the allowance for payment.- (Summary)
