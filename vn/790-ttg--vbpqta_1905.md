# Decision 790/TTg

DECISION No.790-TTg of September 24, 1997 on the establishment of Lac Hong people-funded university.
It is a tertiary education institution subject to the State management by the Ministry of Education and Training. Its system of academic diplomas and certificates belongs to the national system of academic diplomas and certificates.
The head office of the university is located in Dong Nai province.- (Summary)
