# Joint circular 147/1998/TTLT/TCCP-TC-LĐTBXH-GDĐT

Joint Circular No.147/1998/TTLT-TCCP-TC-LDTBXH-GDDT of March 5, 1998 guiding the implementation of Decision No.973/1997/QD-TTg of November 17, 1997 of the Prime Minister on the preferential allowances for teachers who are directly teaching in the State-run public schools.
This Circular specifies provisions on the conditions for being entitled to preferential allowances, the allowance levels and calculation method; the mode of payment of allowances.-
Circular No.29/1998/TT-BTC of March 11, 1998 of the Ministry of Finance guiding the financial management regime so as to implement the Regulation on the National Reserve Management issued together with Decree No.10-CP of February 24, 1996 of the Government.
This Circular guides the provisions on the drafting of budget estimates, the allocation of the approved budget estimates for the national reserve activities; the sale and purchase of national reserve goods; the management of fees for sale, purchase and preservation of national reserve goods; the allocation of capital construction investment funds; the import and/or export of reserve goods.- (Summary)
