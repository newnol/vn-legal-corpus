# Quyết định 172/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 172/2004/QD-TTg of September 29, 2004 approving the plan on reorganization and orientations for development of schools under the Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment
The plan envisages the reorganization of existing schools under the Ministry in the 2004-2005 period and several orientations for their development in the 2010-2015 period.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
