# Chỉ thị 03/2004/CT-BGTVT

DIRECTIVE No. 03/2004/CT-BGTVT of February 3, 2004 on reorganizing the financial management, book-keeping and settlement of investment capital of completed projects by the project management units and investors
In order to overcome weaknesses and handle violations in the financial management, book-keeping and settlement of project investment capital by investors strictly according to the State’s regulations, the Minister of Communications and Transport requests investors, the State management specialized departments and corporations under the Ministry to enhance the management of the observance of the regime of financial management, book-keeping and settlement of investment capital of completed projects by their dependent units.- (Summary)
