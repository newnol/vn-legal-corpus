# Joint circular 18/1999/TTLT/BGDDT-BTCCBCP

THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION AND TRAINING - THE GOVERNMENT COMMISSION FOR ORGANIZATION AND PERSONNEL
Joint Circular No. 18/1999/TTLT/BGDDT-BTCCBCP of May 5, 1999 guiding the implementation of the contents of examinations for the recruitment of pre-school and general-education school teachers
This Circular provides detailed guidance for regulations on the contents of recruitment examinations; the list of materials for examinations; the time of organizing examinations; the membership of the examination boards for the recruitment of pre-school and general-education school teachers.- (Summary)
