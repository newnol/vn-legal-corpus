# Nghị định 27/1998/NĐ-CP

DECREE No.27/1998/ND-CP of May 9, 1998 on the establishment of townships of Tram Tau and Mu Cang Chai districts, Yen Bai province.
To establish Tram Tau township, the capital of Tram Tau district, on the basis of a land area of 372.5 hectares and a population of 2,863 of Hat Luu commune.
To establish Mu Cang Chai township, the capital of Mu Cang Chai district, on the basis of a land area of 407 hectares and a population of 1,132 of Mo De commune; and a land area of 335 hectares and a population of 905 of Kim Noi commune.- (Summary)
