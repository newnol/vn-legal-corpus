# Decision 288/2006/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Approving the Economic Cooperation Agreement between the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the Government of Slovakia, which was concluded in Hanoi, Vietnam, on October 16, 2006
The Prime Minister assigns the Trade Ministry to guide the implementation of the Agreement and the Foreign Affairs Ministry to complete external-relation procedures and notify Vietnamese relevant agencies of the effective date of the Agreement.- (Summary)
