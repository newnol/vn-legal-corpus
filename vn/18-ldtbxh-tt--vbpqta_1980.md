# Circular 18/LĐTBXH-TT

CIRCULAR No.18-LDTBXH/TT of June 27, 1997 guiding the implementation of the subsidy regime for armymen and people’s police, who suffer from occupational diseases as prescribed by Decree No.28-CP of April 29, 1995 of the Government.
The Circular provides detailed guidances on the contents, objects and conditions for the application of the monthly subsidy regime and the organization of implementation thereof.- (Summary)
