# Quyết định 23/2004/QĐ-BKHCN

DECISION No. 23/2004/QD-BKHCN of August 25, 2004 promulgating Vietnamese standards
Promulgated together with this Decision are the following two Vietnamese standards:
- TCVN 6213: 2004: Bottled/packaged natural mineral waters;
- TCVN 6096: 2004: Bottled/packaged drinking waters.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
