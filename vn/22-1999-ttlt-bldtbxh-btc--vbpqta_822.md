# Thông tư liên tịch 22/1999/TTLT/BLĐTBXH-BTC

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 22/1999/TTLT-BLDTBXH-BTC of September 18, 1999 guiding the final account settlement of wages according to the actual average wages which is twice or three times higher than the common average wages of enterprises assigned with the wages price unit
Subject to this Circular are State enterprises assigned with the wage price unit as stipulated in Circular No.13/LDTBXH-TT of April 10, 1997 of the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs which guides the implementation of the Government’s Decree No.28/CP of March 28, 1997 on the renewal of the management of wages and incomes in State enterprises.- (Summary)
