# Quyết định 90/2000/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 90/2000/QD-TTg of August 4, 2000 on the conversion of Da Nang Plastic Company into a joint-stock company
To ratify the plan on equitization of the State-run Da Nang Plastic Company at the proposal of the President of the People�s Committee of Da Nang city in Official Dispatch No. 1611/TT-UB of July 12, 2000.
To convert Da Nang Plastic Company under the People�s Committee of Da Nang city into a joint-stock company under its international transaction name: Da Nang Plastic Joint Stock Company (abbreviated to DaNang Plast).
Its charter capital: VND 15,965,200,000, of which equities sold to the laborers inside the enterprise represent 70%, those sold to the outsiders: 30%.
Head office: 371 Tran Cao Van, Thanh Khe district, Da Nang city.
Its business lines:
- Producing consumer plastic products; packings of various kinds; hard PVC compound water pipes;
- Direct export and import;
- Wholesaling and retailing plastic products, supplies, materials and additives;
- Conducting other business lines in compliance with law provisions.- (Summary)
