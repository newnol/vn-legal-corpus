# Quyết định 187/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 187/2001/QD-TTg of December 7, 2001 allowing Tay Trang and Ma Lu Thang border-gates, Lai Chau province, to apply border-gate economic zone policies
Tay Trang border-gate economic zone comprises Na U, Pa Thom and Sam Mun communes of Dien Bien district.
Ma Lu Thang border-gate economic zone comprises Ma Li Pho and Muong So communes of Phong Tho district and Huoi Luong commune of Sin Ho district.
Tay Trang and Ma Lu Thang border-gate economic zones shall be allowed to apply the Prime Minister�s Decision No. 53/2001/QD-TTg of April 19, 2001 on policies towards border-gate economic zones.- (Summary)
