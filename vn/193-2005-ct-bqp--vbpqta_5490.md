# Chỉ thị 193/2005/CT-BQP

DIRECTIVE
On the implementation of regimes and policies towards a number of subjects who directly participated in the anti-American war but have not yet enjoyed Party and State preferential regimes and policies
With this Directive, the Minister of Defense directs concerned units to implement preferential regimes and policies towards eligible subjects and report the implementation results to the Ministry according to regulations.- (Summary)
