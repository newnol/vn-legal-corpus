# Nghị định 15/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 15/2002/ND-CP of January 31, 2002 adjusting the administrative boundaries of Tuy Hoa provincial capital for the establishment of Phu Hoa district and Tuy Hoa provincial capital, Phu Yen province
To establish Phu Hoa district on the basis of a land area of 26,324 ha and a population of 98,044 of Tuy Hoa provincial capital. Phu Hoa district shall have 7 communes.
After the adjustment of administrative boundaries, Tuy Hoa district township shall have a land area of 6,436 ha and a population of 96,904, including 8 wards and 3 communes.- (Summary)
