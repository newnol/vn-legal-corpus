# Quyết định 192/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 192/1999/QD-TTg of September 20, 1999 on the dissolution of the Steering Board for National Information Technology Program
To dissolve the Steering Board for National Information Technology Program, which was established under the Prime Minister�s Decision No. 212/TTg of May 6, 1994 and Decision No. 154/TTg of March 11, 1995.
The Steering Board for National Information Technology Program shall transfer the task of State management over information technology to the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment.- (Summary)
