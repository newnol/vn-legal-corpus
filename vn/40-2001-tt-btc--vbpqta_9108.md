# Thông tư 40/2001/TT-BTC

Circular No. 40/2001/TT-BTC of June 5, 2001 guiding the regime of management, allocation and payment of foreign currency State budget expenditures through the Central State Treasury
The Finance Ministry issued this Circular to guide the regime of management and allocation of a number of foreign currency expenditures of the estimating units, which belong to the central budget at the Central State Treasury.

- Expense for repayment of foreign debts;
- Providing aid to international organizations and foreign countries under the Government�s decisions;
- Funding for overseas Vietnamese representative offices;
- Contributions to specific international organizations (ADB, WB, IMF,�);
