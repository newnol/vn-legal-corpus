# Nghị định 40/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 40/2003/ND-CP of April 21, 2003 establishing communes of Minh Hoa and Tuyen Hoa districts, Quang Binh province
To establish Trong Hoa commune of Minh Hoa district on the basis of a land area of 18,712 ha and a population of 2,492 of Dan Hoa commune, which now has a land area of 17,650 ha and a population of 2,419.
To establish Thanh Thach commune of Tuyen Hoa district on the basis of a land area of 3,200 ha and a population of 2,576 of Thanh Hoa commune, which now has a land area of 13,228 ha and a population of 5,341.
To establish Nam Hoa commune of Tuyen Hoa district on the basis of a land area of 2,365 ha and a population of 2,320 of Thach Hoa commune, which now has a land area of 5,209 ha and a population of 5,659.- (Summary)
