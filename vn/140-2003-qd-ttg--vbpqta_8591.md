# Quyết định 140/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 140/2003/QD-TTg of July 11, 2003 establishing Phu Xuan People-Founded University
Phu Xuan People-Founded University is a tertiary educational establishment, operating under the Regulation on People-Founded Universities, issued together with the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 86/2000/QD-TTg of July 18, 2000.
It is headquartered in Hue city, Thua Thien - Hue province.- (Summary)
