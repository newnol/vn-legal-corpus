# Decision 34/2006/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION
Annulling Joint Resolution No. 22/2006/NQLT-BGDDT-HKHVN of May 12, 2006, of the Ministry of Education and Training and the Vietnam Study Promotion Society on coordination in implementing the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 112/2005/QD-TTg of May 18, 2005, approving the scheme on building a learning society in the 2005-2010 period
This Decision shall take effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
