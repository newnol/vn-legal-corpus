# Decision 9/1998/QD-TTg

THE PRIME MINISTER
DECISION No.9/1998/QD-TTg of January 22, 1998 assigning the task of post-graduate training to the Foodstuff Industry Institute.
To assign the task of post-graduate training to the Foodstuff Industry Institute under the Ministry of Industry. The Ministry of Education and Training shall coordinate with the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment in defining the post-graduate training majors for the Foodstuff Industry Institute.- (Summary)
