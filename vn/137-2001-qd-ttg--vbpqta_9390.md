# Quyết định 137/2001/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 137/2001/QD-TTg of September 17, 2001 setting up the Executive Board of the project on computerizing the State administrative management in the 2001-2005 period
The Executive Board of the project on computerizing State administrative management in the 2001-2005 period shall be composed of the Government Office�s Vice Director Vu Dinh Thuan as its head and representatives of the concerned ministries and branches as its members.
The Executive Board of the project on computerizing the State administrative management in the 2001-2005 period is tasked to:
- Organize the implementation of the project on computerizing the State administrative management in the 2001-2005 period;
- Coordinate and guide the ministries, the ministerial-level agencies, the agencies attached to the Government and the People�s Committees of the provinces and centrally-run cities in elaborating and implementing the project on computerizing their State administrative management in the 2001-2005 period;
- Propose policies and apply necessary measures to ensure the efficient implementation of the project on computerizing the State administrative management in the 2001-2005 period; coordinate with the competent agencies in urging and inspecting the implementation of the project on computerizing the State administrative management in the 2001-2005 period by the ministries, branches and localities.- (Summary)
