# Decision 1778/2004/QD-BTM

DECISION No. 1778/2004/QD-BTM of November 30, 2004 approving the supplemented 2004 national key trade promotion program
With this Decision, which takes effect 15 addition of the days after its publication in the Official Gazette, the Trade Minister approves the addition of the scheme "National E-Commerce Gate - ECVN" to the 2004 national key trade promotion program.
The scheme shall be carried out from 2004 to 2006 with a total fund of VND 17 billion wholly supported by the State.
This Decision replaces the Trade Ministry's Decision No. 1675/2004/QD-BTM of November 12, 2004.- (Summary)
