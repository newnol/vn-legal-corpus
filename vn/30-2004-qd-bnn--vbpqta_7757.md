# Quyết định 30/2004/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 30/2004/QD-BNN of July 30, 2004 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational apparatus of the Northern Plant Protection Center
The Northern Plant Protection Center is a plant protection non-business unit under the Plant Protection Department, having the legal person status, its own seal, operation funding and bank accounts.
The Center is headquartered in Trung Trac commune, My Van district, Hung Yen province, and operates in the provinces from Ninh Binh northward.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
