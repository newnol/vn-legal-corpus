# Quyết định 69/2002/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 69/2002/QD-BNN of August 5, 2002 establishing the National Key Laboratory for Plant Cell Biotechnology under the Institute of Agricultural Genetics
The National Key Laboratory for Plant Cell Biotechnology is a non-business biotechnological unit under the Institute of Agricultural Genetics.
It is headquartered in Co Nhue commune, Tu Liem district, Hanoi.
It is entitled to have a seal and open its own account at the State Treasury.- (Summary)
