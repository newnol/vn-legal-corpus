# Thông tư 69/2004/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 69/2004/TT-BTC of July 9, 2004 guiding the management and use of funding for free supply of several types of newspapers and magazines to ethnic minority regions and mountainous areas
This Circular replaces the Finance Ministry's Circular No. 28/2002/TT-BTC of March 26, 2002 guiding the management and use of funding for free supply of several types of newspapers and magazines to ethnic minority regions and mountainous areas. It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
