# Decision 46/2000/QD-BGDDT

THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION AND TRAINING
DECISION No. 46/2000/QD-BGDDT of October 18, 2000 promulgating a short-term program on training of defense education teachers of general secondary and intermediate vocational training schools
The short-term program on training of defense education teachers for general secondary and intermediate vocational training schools aims to train graduates of teachers� training colleges and teachers who concurrently teach the defense education subject at general secondary and intermediate vocational training schools so that after completing the program they can teach the defense education subject or take up the work of management and direction of this subject at provincial/municipal Education and Training Services.- (Summary)
