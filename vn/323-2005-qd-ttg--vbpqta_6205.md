# Decision 323/2005/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
On extending the effective duration of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 46/2001/QD-TTg of April 4, 2001, on the management of goods export and import in the 2001-2005 period
Under this Decision, which takes effect as from January 1, 2006, the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 46/2001/QD-TTg shall be effective till the end of April 30, 2006.- (Summary)
