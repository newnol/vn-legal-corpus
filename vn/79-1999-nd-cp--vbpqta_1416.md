# Decree 79/1999/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 79/1999/ND-CP of August 24, 1999 on the adjustment of administrative boundaries of communes in Lac Duong, Lam Ha and Bao Lam districts, Lam Dong province
To establish Dung KNo commune of Lac Duong district on the basis of a land area of 16,500 ha and a population of 1,146 of Da Long commune.
To establish Da Knang commune of Lam Ha district on the basis of a land area of 5,673 ha and a population of 2,565 of Phi Lieng commune.
To establish Tan Lac commune of Bao Lam district on the basis of a land area of 2,442.15 ha and a population of 3,247 of Loc Thanh commune.- (Summary)
