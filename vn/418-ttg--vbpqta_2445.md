# Decision 418/TTg

THE PRIME MINISTER
Decision No.418-TTg of June 14, 1997 on the attire regime applicable to civil judgment execution officials and employees.
The officials and employees working at the Department for Management of Civil Judgment Execution under the Ministry of Justice, and officials and employees (except for judgment executors) working at local civil judgment execution agencies shall be provided with attires.
The principles for the provision and use of attires, the term for use of attires are provided for in this Decision.-
