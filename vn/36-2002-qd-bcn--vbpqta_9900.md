# Quyết định 36/2002/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 36/2002/QD-BCN of September 13, 2002 amending Clause 1, Article 1 of the Industry Minister�s Decision No. 29/2002/QD-BCN of June 27, 2002 which transforms Duc Giang Metal Processing Enterprise under Hanoi Metal Company into Thang Long Steel Joint-Stock Company
The charter capital of Thang Long Steel Joint-Stock Company is VND 8,323,900,000 (eight billion, three hundred and twenty three million, and nine hundred thousand Vietnam dong), of which the State equities represent 34.2%, equities sold to the laborers inside the enterprise, 20%, and equities sold to the outsiders, 45.8%.- (Summary)
