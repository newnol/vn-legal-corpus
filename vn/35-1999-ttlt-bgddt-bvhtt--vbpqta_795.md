# Thông tư liên tịch 35/1999/TTLT/BGDĐT-BVHTT

JOINT-CIRCULAR No. 35/1999/TTLT-BGDDT-BVHTT of September 15, 1999 on the publication and distribution of reference books to be used by general education pupils of all grades
The Ministry of Education and Training and the Ministry of Culture and Information issue this Joint-Circular to implement the provisions of Education Law and Publishing Law, and step by step re-establish order in the publication and distribution of reference books for general education pupils of all grades.- (Summary)
