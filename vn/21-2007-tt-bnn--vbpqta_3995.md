# Circular 21/2007/TT-BNN

CIRCULAR
Guiding the implementation of a number of contents of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 193/2006/QD-TTg of August 24, 2006, which approved the Program on population settlement in natural disaster- and exceptional difficulty-stricken areas, border areas, islands, free migrant areas, important and very important areas of protection forests, and strictly protected zones of special-use forests in the 2006-2010 period and orientations towards 2015
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
