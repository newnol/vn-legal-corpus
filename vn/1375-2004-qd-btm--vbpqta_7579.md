# Quyết định 1375/2004/QĐ-BTM

DECISION No. 1375/2004/QD-BTM of September 27, 2004 amending and supplementing the Trade Minister's Decision No. 1273/2004/QD-BTM of September 7, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on coloring commodity gasoline and oil
Under this Decision, the Trade Minister's Decision No. 1273/2004/QD-BTM of September 7, 2004 takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and applies as from December 1, 2004.- (Summary)
