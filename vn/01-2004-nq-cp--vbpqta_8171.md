# Nghị quyết 01/2004/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION No. 01/2004/NQ-CP of January 12, 2004 on a number of major solutions to the performance of the 2004 plan and State budget, which require concentrated direction and administration
Under this Resolution, the Government has pinpointed a number of major solutions to the improvement of investment and production/business environment; expansion of investment scale and raising of investment efficiency; administration of financial and monetary policies; acceleration of export and international economic integration; social domain; and administrative reform program.- (Summary)
