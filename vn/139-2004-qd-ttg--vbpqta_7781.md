# Quyết định 139/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 139/2004/QD-TTg of August 4, 2004 setting up the Defense Education Councils of military-zone level
The Defense Education Councils shall be set up at Military Zones 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 7 and 9, and the Capital Military Zone to assist the military zones' commanders in considering and settling defense education affairs and decide on their orientations, tasks, work programs and plans.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
