# Decision 48/2007/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Ratifying the Agreement between the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the Government of the Republic of Mozambique on mutual investment promotion and protection
The Prime Minister ratifies the Agreement on mutual investment promotion and protection signed on January 16, 2007, and assigns the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to carry out ratification procedures and notify the effective date of the Agreement to concerned agencies.- (Summary)
