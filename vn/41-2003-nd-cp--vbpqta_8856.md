# Nghị định 41/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 41/2003/ND-CP of April 22, 2003 on the establishment of communes of Cai Nuoc and U Minh rural districts, Ca Mau province
To establish Tan Hai commune of Cai Nuoc rural district on the basis of a natural land area of 4,233.56 ha and a population of 9,532 of Phu Tan commune. Phu Tan commune is now left with a natural land area of 5,682.47 ha and a population of 15,130.
To establish Khanh Hoi commune of U Minh rural district on the basis of a natural land area of 3,360.80 ha and a population of 9,780 of Khanh Lam commune. Khanh Lam commune is narrowed to a natural land area of 11,230.87 ha and a population of 11,096.- (Summary)
