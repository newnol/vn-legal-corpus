# Decision 385/TTg

Decision No.385-TTg of June 6, 1997 on the annual labor-employment surveys (the 1997-2000 period).
To conduct the sample annual labor-employment surveys (the 1997-2000 period) in rural and urban areas throughout the country. The surveys shall focus on: The basic demographic features, educational professional levels of the labor force, the employment situation, the employment structure of the labor force; the unemployment situation; structure of the unemployed labor; the underemployment situation and structure of the underemployed labor; impacts of labor and employment policies and solutions; issues of concern regarding policies and solutions so as to reduce the unemployment rate in urban areas and underemployment in rural areas.- (Summary)
