# Chỉ thị 20/2004/CT-BGTVT

DIRECTIVE No. 20/2004/CT-BGTVT of September 1, 2004 on implementation of the Regulation on monitoring and evaluation of operation efficiency of State enterprises
Under this Directive, the Transport Minister requests the directors of the specialized State management departments, the heads of non-business units, the chairmen of managing boards of State corporations and the directors of independent State companies under the Ministry to immediately perform a number of tasks in order to monitor and evaluate the operation efficiency of State enterprises under the Ministry.- (Summary)
