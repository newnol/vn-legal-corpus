# Decision 19/2008/QD-TTg 

DECISION
Approving land categories imposed with agricultural land use levy applicable to Tra Vinh province
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces Point 6, Section II of the detailed classification of land categories imposed with agricultural land use levy applicable to provinces and centrally run cities (attached to the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 326/TTg of May 18, 1996).- (Summary)
