# Quyết định 67/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 67/2002/QD-TTg of May 28, 2002 promulgating the Working Regulation of the Managing Board of the national target program on hunger elimination, poverty alleviation and employment in the 2001-2005 period
Issued together with this Decision is the Working Regulation of the Managing Board of the national target program on hunger elimination, poverty alleviation and employment in the 2001-2005 period.
The program�s Managing Board shall have to assist the Prime Minister in elaborating and directing the implementation of the national target program on hunger elimination, poverty alleviation and employment in the 2001-2005 period, more concretely:
- To elaborate plans and contents of operation of the program, the annual plans, mechanisms, policies and measures to implement the program; to estimate and anticipate the annual funding allocation.
- To coordinate with ministries and branches in directing localities to integrate activities of programs, projects and policies relating to hunger elimination, poverty alleviation and employment.
- To periodically report to the Prime Minister on the tempo and results of implementation of the program.
