# Quyết định 92/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 92/2002/QD-TTg of July 12, 2002 upgrading Chu Yang Sin Nature Conservation Zone, Dak Lak province, into Chu Yang Sin National Park
To upgrade Chu Yang Sin Nature Conservation Zone in Dak Lak province into a national park belonging to the system of Vietnam�s special-use forests.
Chu Yang Sin national park is located in communes of Yang Mao, Chu Dram, Cu Vui, Hoa Phong, Hoa Le, Hoa Son and Khue Ngoc Dien of Krong Bong rural district and communes of Yang Cao, Bong Krang, Krong No and Dak Phoi of Lak rural district, Dak Lak province.
It has the following objectives and tasks:
- To preserve the model ecological system of high-mountain forests in the Central Highlands, to preserve wild flora and fauna species, especially endemic and rare and precious species.
- To conduct scientific research and environmental education, to develop ecological tourism, thus contributing to local socio-economic development.
- To protect Srepok river and Mekong river headwater forests, to regulate and supply water resources for agricultural production.
The People�s Committee of Dak Lak province shall directly manage Chu Yang Sin National Park.- (Summary)
