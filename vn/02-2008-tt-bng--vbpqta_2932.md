# Circular 02/2008/TT-BNG 

CIRCULAR
Guiding the grant, renewal, modification and supplementation of diplomatic passports and official passports at home and abroad under the Government’s Decree No. 136/2007/ND-CP of August 17, 2007, on entry and exit of Vietnamese citizens
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces the Foreign Affair Ministry’s Circular No. 04/2000/TT-BNG of November 8, 2000, and Circular No. 06/2002/TT-BNG of September 3, 2002.- (Summary)
