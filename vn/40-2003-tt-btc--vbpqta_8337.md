# Thông tư 40/2003/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 40/2003/TT-BTC of May 5, 2003 guiding the implementation of customs seniority allowance regime
Customs officials and employees who have worked for full 5 years (full 60 months) in the customs service shall be entitled to customs seniority allowance.
This Circular guides the subjects entitled to allowances, allowance levels, modes of allowance payment, and seniority calculation time.- (Summary)
