# Decision 05/2004/QD-BNN

DECISION No. 05/2004/QD-BNN of March 16, 2004 promulgating branch standards
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, the following 4 branch standards are promulgated:
- 10 TCN 590-2004: Cereals and pulses - Milled rice - Sensory evaluation of cooked rice by scorecard;
- 10 TCN 591-2004: Cereals and pulses - Determination of tryptophan content by spectrophotometric method;
- 10 TCN 592-2004: Cereals and pulses - Paddy - Specification and test method;
- 10 TCN 593-2004: Cereals and pulses - The method for determination of protein-nitrogen and non protein-nitrogen contents.- (Summary)
