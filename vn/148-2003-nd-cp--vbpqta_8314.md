# Nghị định 148/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 148/2003/ND-CP of December 2, 2003 establishing Sop Cop rural district and adjusting the administrative boundaries of Thuan Chau and Quynh Nhai rural districts, Son La province
To establish Sop Cop rural district on the basis of a land area of 146,841 ha and a population of 31,467 of Song Ma rural district. Sop Cop rural district shall consist of 8 dependent administrative units.
Song Ma rural district shall be left with a land area of 163,125 ha, a population of 109,849 and 19 dependent administrative units.
To transfer a land area of 25,911 ha and population of 26,659 of Thuan Chau rural district to Quynh Nhai rural district. After the adjustment of their administrative boundaries, Quynh Nhai rural district shall have a land area of 104,907 ha, a population of 57,593, and 13 dependent administrative units while these figures for Thuan Chau rural district shall be 151,649, 125,520, and 29 respectively.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
