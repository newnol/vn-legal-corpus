# Quyết định 72/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 72/2003/QD-BCN of May 6, 2003 establishing Vietnam Plastic Company
To establish Vietnam Plastic Company on the basis of reorganizing Van Don Plastic Company, a dependent unit of Vietnam Plastic Corporation and Vietnam Plastic Corporation's Office.
Vietnam Plastic Company is a State enterprise under the Ministry of Industry.
Its charter capital: VND 95,531 million.
Its head-office: 92-94 Ly Tu Trong street, Ben Thanh ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City.
Its branch: 39 Ngo Quyen street, Hoan Kiem district, Hanoi.
It is engaged in the following principal production and business lines:
- Producing plastic products, packings, accessories and components in service of industry, agriculture, fishery, communications and transport, construction, post and telecommunications and civil sector; manufacturing plastic molds and accessories.
- Exporting raw materials, production supplies and consumer goods.
- Providing services on investment consultancy, research, training and transfer of new technologies.
- Dealing in fairs, exhibitions, information, advertisements, supermarkets, hotels and office leasing.- (Summary)
