# Directive 02/2006/CT-BYT

DIRECTIVE
On further stepping up the implementation of medicine-market stabilization solutions in 2006
With this Directive, the Minister of Health instructs the units under and attached to the Ministry, provincial/municipal Health Services and pharmaceuticals-producing, -trading, -importing or -exporting companies to perform several tasks in order to stabilize human-use preventive and curative medicine market.- (Summary)
