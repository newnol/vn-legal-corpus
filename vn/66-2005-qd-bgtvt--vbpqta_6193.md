# Decision 66/2005/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION
On professional criteria and certificates of seafarers and minimum safe manning of Vietnamese seagoing ships
This Decision takes effect as from January 1, 2006, and replaces the Transport Minister’s Decision No. 1387/1998/QD-BGTVT of June 3, 1998, and Decision No. 1581/1999/QD-BGTVT of June 30, 1999.
Professional certificates already granted or renewed under the above-mentioned Decisions are still valid.- (Summary)
