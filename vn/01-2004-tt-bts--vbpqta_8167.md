# Thông tư 01/2004/TT-BTS

CIRCULAR No. 01/2004/TT-BTS of January 15, 2004 guiding the implementation of Clause 1, Article 1 of the Government's Decree No. 80/2002/ND-CP of October 15, 2002 which amends and supplements a number of articles of Decree No. 72/1998/ND-CP of September 15, 1998 on ensuring safety for fishermen and fishing means while operating on the sea
This Circular guides a number of specific points regarding the organization of registry of fishing ships and registration of fishing ships and crewmen.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, annulling all previous regulations contrary hereto.- (Summary)
