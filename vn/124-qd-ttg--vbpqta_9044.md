# Decision 124/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 124/QD-TTg of January 28, 2003 on investment in construction of the National Economics University’s Training Center Building
To build the National Economics University’s Training Center Building in order to meet the immediate training demands of the University; in the long term, this establishment shall serve as a center to administer the whole University as well as institutes and research, training and international cooperation centers.
Investor of the project on construction of the Training Center Building: The National Economics University.
Total investment capital: VND 518.1 billion, including the State budget capital, capital mobilized by the University itself and other lawful capital sources.
The Training Center Building shall be built on the land premise being managed and used by the National Economics University at 207 Giai Phong street, Hanoi with an area of around 3.67 ha.- (Summary)
