# Quyết định 21/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 21/1999/QD-TTg of February 13, 1999 on the establishment of the Steering Board for the setting up and management of the Gratitude Fund
To establish the centrally-run Steering Board for the setting up and management of the Gratitude Fund with Mrs. Nguyen Thi Hang, Minister of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs as its head, and three deputy heads and 11 members being representatives of the relevant agencies and organizations.- (Summary)
