# Thông tư 72/2005/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR
Guiding the formulation of the Regulation on financial management applicable to state companies operating after the parent company-subsidiary company model
Subject to this Circular are State corporations and their independent cost-accounting member companies, independent State companies operating after the parent company-subsidiary company model, provided for in the Government's Decree No. 153/2004/ND-CP of August 9, 2004.
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary
