# Quyết định 55/2004/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 55/2004/QD-BNN of November 1, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on the licensing of activities carried out within the protected irrigation works
The Regulation prescribes the competence and procedures for licensing activities carried out within the protected irrigation works.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
