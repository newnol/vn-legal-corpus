# Nghị định 06/1999/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 06/1999/ND-CP of February 10, 1999 amending Clause 1, Article 7 of Decree No.79-CP of November 6, 1993 of the Government detailing the implementation of the Publication Law
To amend Clause 1, Article 7, Decree No.79/CP of November 6, 1993 of the Government as follows:
The Publishing House directly attached to the Government Commission for Religions is tasked to publish bibles, sutras and holy books of religious organizations.- (Summary)
