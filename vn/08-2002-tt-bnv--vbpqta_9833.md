# Thông tư 08/2002/TT-BNV

CIRCULAR No. 08/2002/TT-BNV of October 16, 2002 guiding the implementation of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 149/2000/QD-TTg of December 28, 2000 on a number of preferential policies towards members of voluntary young intellectuals’ teams participating in rural and mountainous development
Subjects entitled to the preferential policies include:
Voluntary young intellectuals’ teams participating in rural and mountainous development, organized by the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union’s Central Committee;
- Voluntary young medical workers’ teams participating in rural and mountainous development, organized by the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union’s Central Committee;
- Voluntary young intellectuals’ teams participating in rural and mountainous development under other projects decided by the Prime Minister.
The Circular prescribes the preferential policies towards members of voluntary teams during their task performance and after their task accomplishment; and the conditions for enjoying the preferential policies.- (Summary)
