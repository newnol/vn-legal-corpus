# Quyết định 96/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 96/2003/QD-TTg of May 13, 2003 establishing Hai Duong province's Industrial Parks Management Board
To establish Hai Duong province's Industrial Parks Management Board, which is under the People's Committee of Hai Duong province and shall manage industrial parks in the province.
Hai Duong province's Industrial Parks Management Board shall have to perform the tasks and exercise the powers regarding the management of industrial parks as prescribed in Article 27 of the Regulation on Industrial Parks, Export-Processing Zones and Hi-Tech Parks, issued together with the Government's Decree No. 36/CP of April 24, 1997.- (Summary)
