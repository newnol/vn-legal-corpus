# Quyết định 35/2005/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 35/2005/QD-TTg of February 22, 2005 establishing Khanh Binh border economic zone in An Phu district, An Giang province
Khanh Binh border economic zone, established under this Decision, covers 5 communes of Khanh Binh, Khanh An, Quoc Thai, Nhon Hoi and Phu Huu of An Phu district.
It shall enjoy policies applicable to border economic zones, prescribed in the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 53/2001/QD-TTg of April 19, 2001 (except for back investment from the State budget revenues).
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
