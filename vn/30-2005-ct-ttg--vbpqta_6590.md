# Chỉ thị 30/2005/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On enhancing the management of cattle and poultry slaughtering for assurance of food safety and hygiene
With this Directive, the Prime Minister instructs provincial/municipal People's Committees, the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development, the Ministry of Health and concerned ministries and branches to perform several tasks in order to enhance the management of cattle and poultry slaughtering for assurance of food safety and hygiene.- (Summary)
