# Decision 33/2006/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION
Annulling decisions, circulars and directives
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and annuls the Transport Minister’s Decision No. 06/2005/QD-BGTVT of January 4, 2005, Decision No. 337/1998/QD-BGTVT of March 4, 1998, Decision No. 1781/CGD-KHDT-TCKT of July 17, 1997, Circular No. 167/1998/TT-BGTVT of June 18, 1998, Directive No. 23/2002/CT-BGTVT of December 3, 2002, and Directive No. 423/1998/CT-BGTVT of December 22, 1998.- (Summary)
