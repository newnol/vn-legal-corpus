# Decree 45/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 45/2002/ND-CP of April 12, 2002 establishing wards of Thai Binh provincial capital and Thanh Ne district township of Kien Xuong district, Thai Binh province
To establish Tien Phong ward of Thai Binh provincial capital with a land area of 310.2 ha and a population of 8,349.
To establish Tran Lam ward of Thai Binh provincial capital with a land area of 330 ha and a population of 13,750.
To establish Thanh Ne district township of Kien Xuong district with a land area of 681.54 ha and a population of 11,500.- (Summary)
