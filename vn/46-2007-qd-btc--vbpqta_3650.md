# Decision 46/2007/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
Amending and supplementing Article 2 and annulling Article 3 of the Finance Minister’s Decision No. 77/2006/QD-BTC of December 29, 2006, which promulgates the list of goods and their import tax rates for application of tariff quotas
This Decision takes effect and applies to imports customs declarations registered with customs offices 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
