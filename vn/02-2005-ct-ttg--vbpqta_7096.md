# Chỉ thị 02/2005/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE No. 02/2005/CT-TTg of February 7, 2005 on some measures to speed up the implementation of, and to basically complete, the program on the building of population clusters and lines and dwelling houses in flooded areas in the Mekong River delta in 2005
Ussing this Directive, the Prime Minister requests the concerned ministries, branches and the People’s Committees of provinces and cities in the Mekong River delta to speed up the implementation of the program on the building of population clusters and lines in flooded areas in the Mekong River delta in order to basically complete the program by the end of 2005.- (Summary)
