# Quyết định 43/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 43/2003/QD-BCN of April 7, 2003 merging the Rare Earth Company into Minerals Development Company No. III under Vietnam Minerals Corporation
To merge the Rare Earth Company into Minerals Development Company No. III under Vietnam Minerals Corporation.
The Managing Board of Vietnam Minerals Corporation shall organize the merger strictly according to the State's current regulations.- (Summary)
