# Decision 06/2007/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION
Promulgating Vietnam civil aviation security program
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces the Vietnam Civil Aviation Administration Director’s Decisions No. 1539/CAAV of August 5, 1996; No. 1921/CAAV of September 9, 1996; No. 07/1999/QD-CHK of March 29, 1999; and No. 15/1999/QD-CHK of May 22, 1999; the Transport Minister’s Decisions No. 04/2004/QD-BGTVT of March 19, 2004; No. 20/2004/QD-BGTVT of October 28, 2004; No. 46/2005/QD-BGTVT of September 23, 2005; and No. 62/2005/QD-BGTVT of November 30, 2005; and the Vietnam Civil Aviation Administration Director’s Circular No. 65/1999/TT-CHK of December 27, 1999.- (Summary)
