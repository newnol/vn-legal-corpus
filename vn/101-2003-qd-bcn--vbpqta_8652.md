# Quyết định 101/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 101/2003/QD-BCN of June 20, 2003 approving the Charter on organization and operation of Thang Long Shoes Company
To approve together with this Decision the Charter on organization and operation of Thang Long Shoes Company.
Thang Long Shoes Company is an independent-accounting State enterprise under the Ministry of Industry, which is set up, invested and managed by the State in the capacity as its owner.
It is specialized in producing and trading in assorted footwear and other products made of leather and leatherette; deals in other production and business lines as prescribed by law and performs other tasks assigned by the Ministry of Industry.
Its head office: 411 Tam Trinh street, Hoang Van Thu ward, Hai Ba Trung district, Hanoi.- (Summary)
