# Quyết định 180/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 180/2001/QD-TTg of November 16, 2001 on the appointment and dismissal of deputy general directors, chief accountants, and directors of member units, of corporations with 100% State capital
To assign the chairpersons of the Management Boards of Corporations with 100% State capital to decide the appointment, dismissal, commendation and discipline of the corporations� deputy general directors and chief accountants; and to approve the appointment, dismissal, commendation and discipline of member units� directors for the general directors to issue decisions thereon later.- (Summary)
