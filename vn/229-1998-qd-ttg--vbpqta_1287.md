# Decision 229/1998/QD-TTg

Decision No. 229/1998/QD-TTg of November 27, 1998 to supplement Article 2 of Decision No. 782-TTg of October 24, 1996 on the restructure of scientific and technological research and development institutions
To add the following subjects to those entitled to the State�s development investment under Article 2 of Decision No.782-TTg:
1. The Standard- Measurement and Quality Control agencies of national importance;
2. The scientific and technological information and archives agencies of national importance.
3. Key laboratories of universities which have performed the State�s scientific and technological priority programs and projects.
4. The specialized research institutes under the corporations.
The development investment for the newly-added subjects as defined in this Decision is stipulated as follows:
- The State shall concentrate its development investment for the agencies mentioned in Points 1, 2 and 3 above.
- The State shall provide initial support in term of development investment for the specialized research institutes mentioned in Point 4 above.- (Summary)
