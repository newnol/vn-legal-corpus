# Decision 1117/TTg

DECISION No.1117-TTg of December 23, 1997 to add a new member to the Steering Committee for implementation of the project on the construction of the historical relic of South Vietnam Resistance War Headquarters.
To add Mr. Tran Huu Phuoc, former assistant to the Minister of Culture and Information and former Director of the Representative Office of the Ministry of Culture and Information in Ho Chi Minh City, to the membership of the Steering Committee for implementation of the project on the construction of the historical relic of South Vietnam Resistance War Headquarters.- (Summary)
