# Quyết định 65/2005/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
On ceasing the issuance of Government bonds - phase III of 2005 - for mobilization of capital for traffic and irrigation works via state treasuries
With this Decision, which takes effect after its signing, the Finance Minister decides to cease the issuance (retail) of Government bonds - phase III of 2005 - for mobilization of capital for traffic and irrigation works via state treasuries as from 16:30 hrs. of September 30, 2005.- (Summary)
