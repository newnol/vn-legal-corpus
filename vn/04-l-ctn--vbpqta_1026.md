# Order 04/L-CTN

ORDER No. 04/L-CTN OF MAY 8, 1999 PROMULGATING THE ORDINANCE ON THE ISSUANCE OF GOVERNMENT BONDS FOR NATIONAL CONSTRUCTION AND THE ORDINANCE ON THE PROTECTION OF CONSUMERS’ INTERESTS
THE STATE PRESIDENT OF THE SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF VIETNAM
Pursuant to Articles 103 and 106 of the 1992 Constitution of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam;
Pursuant to Article 78 of the Law on Organization of the National Assembly;
Pursuant to Article 51 of the Law on the Promulgation of Legal Documents,
PROMULGATES:
1. The Ordinance on the Issuance of Government Bonds for National Construction;
2. The Ordinance on the Protection of Consumers’ Interests,
Which were passed on April 27, 1999 by the Standing Committee of the Xth National Assembly of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam.
State President
of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam
TRAN DUC LUONG
