# Quyết định 208/2003/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 208/2003/QD-TTg of October 10, 2003 renaming the Finance-Pricing Services the Finance Services under the People's Committees of the provinces and centrally-run cities
According to this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, the Finance-Pricing Services are renamed the Finance Services under the People's Committees of the provinces and centrally-run cities.- (Summary)
