# Nghị định 83-CP

THE GOVERNMENT
Decree No.83-CP of December 17, 1996 of the Government on the establishment of DaKrong district of Quang Tri province
To establish DaKrong district of Quang Tri province on the basis of 10 communes of Huong Hoa district (Mo O, Huong Hiep, DaKrong, Ta Long, Huc Nghi, Ta Rut, A Vao, A Ngo, A Bung, Ba Nang) and 3 communes of Trieu Phong district (Hai Phuc, Ba Long, Trieu Nguyen).
DaKrong district has a land area of 118,337 ha, a population of 24,680 and 13 commune-level administrative units .- (Summary)
