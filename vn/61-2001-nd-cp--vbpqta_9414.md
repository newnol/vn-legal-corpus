# Nghị định 61/2001/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 61/2001/ND-CP of September 7, 2001 prescribing the retirement age of laborers being coal pit miners
The retirement age of coal pit miners is 50, provided that they have paid social insurance for full 20 years, and have been working as coal pit miners for at least 15 years.
In cases where coal pit miners reach the prescribed retirement age but fail to satisfy the social insurance duration requirement, if being in good health, they may continue their jobs until they satisfy the retirement conditions, but not beyond the age of 55.- (Summary)
