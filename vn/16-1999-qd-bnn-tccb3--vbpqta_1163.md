# Decision 16/1999/QD-BNN-TCCB3

DECISION No.16/1999/QD-BNN-TCCB3 of January 19, 1999 on the establishment of the Irrigation Informatics Research and Application Section attached to the Southern Irrigation Science Institute
To establish the Irrigation Informatics Research and Application Section attached to the Southern Irrigation Science Institute, which is tasked to conduct scientific study and technological development of mathematics models and information technology in the irrigation field.- (Summary)
