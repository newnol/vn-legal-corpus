# Directive 13/2006/CT-BKHCN

DIRECTIVE
Enhancing the management of radiation safety and radioactive sources security
The Minister of Science and Technology instructs the Agency for Radiation and Nuclear Safety and Control, scientific and technological management agencies under ministries, branches and provincial/municipal Services of Science and Technology, and radiation establishments to perform several tasks to meet the requirements of enhancing the management of radiation safety and radioactive sources security and realizing the strategy on peaceful utilization of nuclear energy up to 2020.- (Summary)
