# Thông tư 83/2001/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 83/2001/TT-BTC of October 4, 2001 guiding the handling and re-arrangement of State-owned houses and land in Ho Chi Minh City
The Circular guides in detail the scope and subjects of application, the principles for handling and rearranging State-owned houses and land in Ho Chi Minh City; the order for revising and inspecting for rearrangement of State-owned houses and land as well as the competence to decide on the handling plans; the modes of house sale, land use right or leased land use right transfer; management and use of the proceeds from the house sale, land use right or leased land use right transfer.- (Summary)
