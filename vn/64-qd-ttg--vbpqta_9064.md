# Decision 64/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 64/QD-TTg of January 14, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Thua Thien-Hue province till 2005
Under the master plan, 26 enterprises, including 10 public-utility enterprises, 10 business enterprises and 6 forestry farms, shall continue to have 100% of their charter capital held by the State and to be legal entities.
Roadmap for the reorganization of enterprises shall be as follows:
- In 2003:
To be equitized, with the State holding under 50% of their charter capital or holding no equities: 11 enterprises.
To be equitized, with the State holding under 50% of their charter capital or holding no equities: 1 enterprise’s unit.
To be assigned to labor collective: 1 enterprise.
To be merged: 3 enterprises.
To be transferred to become member of a State corporation: 1 enterprise.
- In 2004:
To be equitized, with the State holding over 50% of their charter capital: 2 enterprises.
To be equitized, with the State holding under 50% of their charter capital or holding no equities: 7 enterprises.
To be merged: 1 enterprise.
- In 2005:
To be equitized, with the State holding over 50% of their charter capital: 3 enterprises.
To be equitized, with the State holding under 50% of their charter capital or holding no equities: 1 enterprise.
To be merged: 2 enterprises.- (Summary)
