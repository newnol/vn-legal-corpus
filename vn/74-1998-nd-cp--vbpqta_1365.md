# Nghị định 74/1998/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 74/1998/ND-CP of September 24, 1998 on the establishment of Lai Cach township of Cam Giang district, Hai Duong province
To establish Lai Cach district township of Cam Giang district, Hai Duong province on the basis of the entire land area of 753.66 hectares and the entire population of 11,693 of Lai Cach commune.- (Summary)
