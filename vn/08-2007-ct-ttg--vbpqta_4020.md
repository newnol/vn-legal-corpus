# Directive 08/2007/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On enhancing the flood and storm prevention and combat, rescue, salvage and alleviation of damage caused by natural disasters in 2007
The Prime Minister requests ministries, branches and localities to well perform several tasks in flood and storm prevention and combat as well as rescue and salvage activities in order to minimize damage caused by natural disasters in 2007.- (Summary)
