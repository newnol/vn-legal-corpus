# Quyết định 93/2001/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 93/2001/QD-TTg of June 13, 2001 on "the Month of Action for Drug Prevention and Combat" and "the Day for Drug Prevention and Combat by the Entire Population"
The Prime Minister has decided to take June every year as "the Month of Action for Drug Prevention and Combat" and June 26 as "the Day for Drug Prevention and Combat by the Entire Population".- (Summary)
