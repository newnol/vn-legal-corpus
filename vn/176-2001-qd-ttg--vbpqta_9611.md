# Quyết định 176/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 176/2001/QD-TTg of November 9, 2001 on the Post and Telecommunications State Inspectorate
The Post and Telecommunications State Inspectorate shall have the function of State inspection and specialized inspection over post and telecommunications, Internet, radio frequency and other fields falling under the State management competence of the General Department of Post and Telecommunications nationwide.
