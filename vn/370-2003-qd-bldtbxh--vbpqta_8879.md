# Quyết định 370/2003/QĐ-BLĐTBXH

DECISION No. 370/2003/QD-BLDTBXH of April 3, 2003 promulgating the Regulation on appointment, recognition, re-appointment, resignation and relief from office, of job-training schools’ headmasters and deputy headmasters as well as job-training centers’ directors
Issued together with this Decision is the Regulation on appointment, recognition, re-appointment, resignation and relief from office, of job-training schools’ headmasters and deputy headmasters as well as job-training centers’ directors.
This Regulation prescribes the criteria, conditions, order and procedures for the appointment, recognition, re-appointment, resignation and relief from office, of headmasters and deputy headmasters of public, semi-public, people-founded or private job-training schools as well as directors of public, semi-public, people-founded or private job-training centers.
This Regulation shall not apply to foreign-invested job-training schools.- (Summary)
