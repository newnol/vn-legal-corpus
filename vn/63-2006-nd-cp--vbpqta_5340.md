# Decree 63/2006/NĐ-CP

DECREE
On organization and operation of People’s Public Security Inspectorates
People’s Public Security Inspectorates are state inspectorates organized systematically in people’s public security forces, functioning to conduct administrative and specialized inspection under the state management by the Ministry of Public Security.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
