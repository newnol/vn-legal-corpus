# Decision 06/2007/QĐ-BVHTTDL

DECISION
On ranking a national relic
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” the historical relic of the worshipping house for ancestors of An Hai village and Marquis Thoai Ngoc in An Hai Tay ward, Son Tra district, Da Nang city, is ranked as a national relic.- (Summary)
