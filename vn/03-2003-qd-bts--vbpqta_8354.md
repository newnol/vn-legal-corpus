# Decision 03/2003/QD-BTS

DECISION No. 03/2003/QD-BTS of April 21, 2003 amending the list of legal documents which cease to be effective
To take the Aquatic Resources Minister's Decision No. 552/TS-QD of November 11, 1997, which promulgates criteria of the aquatic resource sector workers' grades, out of the list of legal documents which cease to be effective (issued together with the Aquatic Resources Minister's Decision No. 01/2003/QD-BTS of February 13, 2003).- (Summary)
