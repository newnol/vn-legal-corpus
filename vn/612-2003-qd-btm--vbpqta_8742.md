# Quyết định 612/2003/QĐ-BTM

DECISION No. 612/2003/QD-BTM of May 23, 2003 on transferring independent cost-accounting member units under the Machine and Spare Part Corporation to the Trade Ministry
To transfer the following 10 independent cost-accounting member units under the Machine and Spare Part Corporation to the Trade Ministry in order to implement equitization plans:
- Hanoi Equipment and Spare Part Company.
- Hai Phong Equipment and Spare Part Company.
- Da Nang Equipment and Spare Part Company.
- Ho Chi Minh city Equipment and Spare Part Company.
- Hanoi Machine Import-Export Company.
- Ho Chi Minh city Machine Import-Export Company.
- Spare Part Company.
- Hanoi Equipment Company.
- Hanoi Supplies and Technical Services Company.
- Machinco Package Printing Factory.- (Summary)
