# Directive 88/2006/CT-BNN

DIRECTIVE
Intensifying the application of information technology in the ranger force
The Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development requests the ranger force to perform several tasks in order to intensify the application of information technology to state management of forest protection.- (Summary)
