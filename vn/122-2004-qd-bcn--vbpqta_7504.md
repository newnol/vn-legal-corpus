# Quyết định 122/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 122/2004/QD-BCN of November 2, 2004 on setting up the Secondary School of Industry and Construction attached to the Ministry of Industry
The Secondary School of Industry and Construction, SSIC, is set up under the Ministry of Industry on the basis of the Mechanics and Construction Job-Training School in Phuong Dong commune, Uong Bi town, Quang Ninh province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
