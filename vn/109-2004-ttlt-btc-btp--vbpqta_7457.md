# Joint circular 109/2004/TTLT/BTC-BTP

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 109/2004/TTLT/BTC-BTP of November 17, 2004 guiding the management and use of funds for the examination of legal documents
This Joint Circular applies to the Justice Ministry's Department for Examination of Legal Documents, legal organizations of ministries, ministerial-level agencies and Government-attached agencies; provincial-level Justice Services and district-level Justice Bureaus.
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
