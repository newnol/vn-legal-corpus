# Directive 24/2006/CT-BNN

DIRECTIVE
Enhancing the execution of the program on “3 reductions and 3 increases” (reduction of seeds for sowing, fertilizer and insecticide; increase of productivity, quality and economic efficiency)
With this Directive, the Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development instructs the performance of several tasks in order to effectively execute the program on “3 reductions and 3 increases” to suit conditions of localities nationwide.- (Summary)
