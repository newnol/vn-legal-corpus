# Quyết định 20/2003/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 20/2003/QD-BTC of February 17, 2003 amending the import tax rates of a number of goods items under Heading No. 2710 of the Preferential Import Tariff
This Decision amends the preferential import tax rates of a number of goods items under Heading No. 2710 (petroleum oils and oils obtained from bituminous minerals, other than crude; preparations not elsewhere specified or included, containing by weight 70% or more of petroleum oils or of oils obtained from bituminous minerals, these oils being the basic constituents of the preparations; waste oils) prescribed in Decision No. 08/2003/QD-BTC of January 23, 2003 of the Finance Minister.
This Decision takes effect and applies to import goods declarations already submitted to customs offices from February 18, 2003 on.- (Summary)
