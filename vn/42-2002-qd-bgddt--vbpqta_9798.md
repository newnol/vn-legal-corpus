# Decision 42/2002/QD-BGDDT

DECISION No. 42/2002/QD-BGDDT of October 21, 2002 promulgating the Regulation on assessment of study results of pupils and students of universities, colleges and intermediate vocational schools
To issue together with this Decision the Regulation on assessment of study results of pupils and students of universities, colleges and intermediate schools, who are on formal training and study.
This Regulation prescribes the assessment contents and grounds, mark scale, mark frame, the classification of results and the process for assessment of study results of pupils and students on formal training and study in universities, colleges and intermediate vocational schools.- (Summary)
