# Quyết định 149/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 149/2004/QD-TTg of August 16, 2004 transforming Saigon Shipbuilding Industry Company under Vietnam Shipbuilding Industry Corporation into a State-owned one-member limited liability company
Under this Decision, Saigon Shipbuilding Industry Company, an independent cost-accounting unit under Vietnam Shipbuilding Industry Corporation, shall be transformed into a State- owned one-member limited liability company.
The company's international transaction name is Saigon Shipbuilding Industry Company Limited, abbreviated to SSIC.
Its charter capital is VND 101,291,195,719.
It is headquartered at 1027 Pham The Hien street, Ward 5, District 8, Ho Chi Minh city.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
