# Decision 33/2002/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 33/2002/QD-BTC of March 14, 2002 amending the import tax rates for a number of commodities under Headings No. 2709 and 2710 of the Preferential Import Tariff
To amend the preferential import tax rates for a number of commodities under Headings No. 2709 and 2710 prescribed in Decision No. 10/2002/QD-BTC of February 1, 2002 of the Minister of Finance.
This Decision takes effect and applies to import goods declarations already submitted to the customs offices as from March 15, 2002.- (Summary)
