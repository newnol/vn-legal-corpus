# Quyết định 90/2004/QĐ-BNV

DECISION No. 90/2004/QD-BNV of December 28, 2004 approving the Charter of Vjietnama Esperanto Associo
Under this Decision, Vjietnama Esperanto Asocio, VEA, is a social organization, which rallies Vietnamese esperantidists in order to popularize and encourage the use of Esperanto. VEA is subject to the State management by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
