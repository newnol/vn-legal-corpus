# Quyết định 48/2000/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION No. 48/2000/QD-BGDDT of November 13, 2000 promulgating the Regulation on professional evaluation and classification of primary school teachers
The professional evaluation and classification of primary school teachers are the task of the education administration levels, aiming to manage, encourage and help primary school teachers to well perform the teaching and education tasks, thus contributing to raising primary education efficiency and quality.
The Regulation specifies the evaluation grounds, classification criteria as well as the organization of professional evaluation and classification of primary school teachers.- (Summary)
