# Nghị định 42/1998/NĐ-CP

DECREE No.42/1998/ND-CP of June 19, 1998 on the establishment of a district township of Gia Luong district, Bac Ninh province.
To establish Thua township of Gia Luong district on the basis of the total land area of 691 ha and population of 9,176 people of Pha Lang commune of the same district.- (Summary)
