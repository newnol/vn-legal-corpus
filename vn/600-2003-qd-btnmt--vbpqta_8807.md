# Quyết định 600/2003/QĐ-BTNMT

DECISION No. 600/2003/QD-BTNMT of May 8, 2003 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Department for Management of Water Resources
The Department for Management of Water Resources is an organization under the Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment, which functions to assist the Minister in performing the task of State management over water resources, including rain water, surface water, underground water and sea water in Vietnam’s territory and sea area.
The tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Department are specified in this Decision.- (Summary)
