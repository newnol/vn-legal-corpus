# Thông tư 36/2005/TT-BLĐTBXH

CIRCULAR No. 36/2005/TT-BLDTBXH of December 26, 2005, guiding the implementation of a number of articles of the Government's Decree No. 30/2002/ND-CP of March 26, 2002, and Decree No. 120/2003/ND-CP of October 20, 2003, which provides and guides the implementation of a number of articles of the Ordinance on Elderly People
This Circular replaces Circular No. 16/2002/TT-BLDTBXH of December 9, 2002, and takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
