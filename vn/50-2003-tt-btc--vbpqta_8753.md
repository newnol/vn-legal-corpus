# Thông tư 50/2003/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 50/2003/TT-BTC of May 22, 2003 guiding non-business units with revenues to elaborate the Regulations on internal spendings according to the provisions in the Government’s Decree No. 10/2002/ND-CP of January 16, 2002
Subject to the application of this Circular are non-business units with revenues, which are given financial autonomy by competent authorities under the Government’s Decree No. 10/2002/ND-CP of January 16, 2002.
The Regulation on internal spendings includes the stipulations on spending regime, criteria and norms to be uniformly applied in the units, ensuring that the units shall fulfill the assigned political tasks, conduct regular activities in compatibility with their specific activities, efficiently use funds and enhance the management work.
The Regulation on internal spendings shall be discussed in a democratic and public manner in units and commented by the units’ trade union organizations.- (Summary)
