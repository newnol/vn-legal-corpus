# Quyết định 859/1997/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.859/1997/QD-TTg of October 14, 1997 assigning the task of post-graduate training to Hue university.
To assign the task of post-graduate training to Hue university. The Ministry of Education and Training shall, together with the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment, determine specialized branches for post-graduate training at Hue university.- (Summary)
