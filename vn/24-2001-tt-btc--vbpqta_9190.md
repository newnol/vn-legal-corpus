# Thông tư 24/2001/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 24/2001/TT-BTC of April 16, 2001 guiding the use of fines collected from the sanctioning of administrative violations in the field of traffic order and safety
The whole amount of fine money collected from the sanctioning of administrative violations in land road, urban, railway and waterway traffic order and safety shall be retained at local budgets for management.
The Circular also guides in detail the allocation, management and use of fines; the elaboration, execution and final settlement of funding proceeded from fines.- (Summary)
