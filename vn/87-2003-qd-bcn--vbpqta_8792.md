# Quyết định 87/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 87/2003/QD-BCN of May 13, 2003 on reorganizing Da Nang Construction and Producing Concrete Block Company into one-member limited liability company under Electricity Construction and Installation Company 3
To reorganize Danang Construction and Producing Concrete Block Company (a joint venture between Electricity Construction and Installation Company 3 and Central Vietnam Construction Corporation) into one-member limited liability company under the Electricity Construction and Installation Company 3.
Danang Construction and Producing Concrete Block Company is an affiliate company which has the legal person status, its own seal and bank accounts.
The company is headquartered in Hoa Khanh industrial park, Lien Chieu district, Danang city.
The company’s charter capital at the time of establishment is VND 7,050,007,352.
The company is engaged in the following production and business lines:
- Producing and trading in assorted building materials, concrete block, terrazzo, daramic tiles and concrete products;
- Building and/or installing electricity grid works and systems, industrial and civil works as well as traffic and irrigation works;
- Conducting tests for determining building materials’ properties and evaluating of construction and traffic work quality;
- Providing investment and construction consultancy, including elaboration and appraisal of investment projects, exploration and surveys, designs of planning and works, and project management consultancy.
- Dealing in development of urban houses and technical infrastructures, industrial parks, export-processing zones, high-tech parks and new economic zones; trading in various commodities for interior and exterior decoration; real estate service provision.
- Other business lines suitable to the company’s capability and as permitted by law.- (Summary)
