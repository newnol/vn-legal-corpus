# Nghị định 31/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 31/2003/ND-CP of April 1, 2003 adjusting the administrative boundaries for the establishment of communes of Thanh Tri district, Soc Trang province
To establish Vinh Thanh commune on the basis of a land area of 2,562.47 ha and a population of 4,795 from Vinh Loi commune, which shall be reduced to a land area of 2,263.74 ha and a population of 5,346.
To establish Long Binh commune on the basis of a land area of 2,963 ha and a population of 7,812 from Tan Long commune, which shall be sized down with a land area of 3,312.04 ha and a population of 6,278.
To establish My Binh commune on the basis of a land area of 2,107 ha and a population of 4,925 from My Quoi commune, which now has a land area of 2,924.99 ha and a population of 8,014.- (Summary)
