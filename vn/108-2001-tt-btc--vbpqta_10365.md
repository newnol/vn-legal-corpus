# Thông tư 108/2001/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 108/2001/TT-BTC of December 31, 2001 guiding the accounting applicable to entrusted import/export business activities
Subject to the application of this Circular are production and/or business establishments of all economic sectors, which are engaged in entrusted import and/or export.
The Circular guides in detail the accounting applicable to import-entrusting units and units entrusted the import; and those applicable to export-entrusting units and units entrusted the export.
This Circular takes effect as from January 1, 2002.-
