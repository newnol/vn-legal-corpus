# Quyết định 03/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 03/2004/QD-TTg of January 7, 2004 approving the orientations for the planning on training and fostering officials and employees of communes, wards and townships till 2010
The orientations for the planning on training and fostering officials and employees of communes, wards and townships till 2010 points to the objectives, requirements, subjects and orientations of the planning on training and fostering grassroots officials and employees till 2010. It also covers the training and fostering contents, programs, forms and establishments.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
