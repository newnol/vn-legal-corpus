# Decision 01/1999/QD-BNN

DECISION No. 01/1999/QD-BNN of January 4, 1999 on the establishment of Irrigation Construction and Technology-Transfer Company attached to the Irrigation Science Institute
To establish the Irrigation Construction and Technology-Transfer Company attached to the Irrigation Science Institute.
The Company is a State-run enterprise and has a statutory capital of 5,400 million VN dong.
The Company�s business lines:
- Investment consultancy; technology transfer; production of construction materials; construction of irrigation projects and mini- hydro-electric power stations; protection of river banks and sea shores; water supply and drainage; construction of communication projects, civil projects, rural infrastructure and a number of other relevant projects.
- Participation in the study and building of study and demonstration models in service of scientific research;
- Participation in personnel training and fostering, international cooperation and relevant fields.- (Summary)
