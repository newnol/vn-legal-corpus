# Chỉ thị 07/2003/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE No. 07/2003/CT-TTg of April 3, 2003 on organizing the issuance of the 2003 education public bonds
In order to gain the set target of capital mobilization through the issuance of education public bonds, the Prime Minister instructs:
On the occasion of the 28th anniversary of the South Vietnam liberation and national reunification, to launch a campaign of mobilization for education public bond purchase throughout the country in the spirit that the entire people care for the cause of educational development. The Ministry of Finance shall quickly complete preparatory work so as to begin issuing the public bonds as from May 5, 2003.
During the time of issuing public bonds, all levels, branches, agencies, units and localities shall have to clearly identify that mobilizing for and actively responding to education public bond purchase constitute a key and extraordinary task, considering the active participation in the purchase of assigned public bonds an important content in the evaluation of thrift practice.
The result of education public bond issuance and the management and use of capital mobilized from public bond issuance must be publicly announced on the mass media.
The Prime Minister also gave direction on activities of propagating and campaigning for public bond purchase.- (Summary)
