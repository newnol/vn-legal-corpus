# Joint circular 10/2006/TTLT-BLĐTBXH-BYT

JOINT CIRCULAR
Amending and supplementing Clause 2, Section II of Joint Circular No. 10/1999/TTLT-BLDTBXH-BYT of March 17, 1999, of the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs and the Ministry of Health, guiding the implementation of the regime of allowances in kind for laborers working under dangerous and hazardous conditions
This Joint Circular shall take effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
