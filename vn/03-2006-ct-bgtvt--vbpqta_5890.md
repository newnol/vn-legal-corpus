# Chỉ thị 03/2006/CT-BGTVT

DIRECTIVE
Enhancing the realization of policies in support of people with disabilities in the transport sector
With this Directive, the Minister of Transport instructs concerned departments, units and enterprises to perform several tasks in order to enhance the implementation of the State’s guidelines and policies towards people with disabilities in the transport sector.- (Summary)
