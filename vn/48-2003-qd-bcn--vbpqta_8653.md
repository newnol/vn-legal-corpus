# Decision 48/2003/QD-BCN

DECISION No. 48/2003/QD-BCN of April 8, 2003 establishing Power Construction Company 3.1 (a State affiliate company) under Power Construction Company 3 on the basis of consolidating the Power Mechanization Construction Enterprise and the Mechanization Construction Team
To establish Power Construction Company 3.1 (a State affiliate company) under Power Construction Company 3 (a State parent company) on the basis of consolidating the Power Mechanization Construction Enterprise and the Mechanization Construction Team.
Power Construction Company 3.1 is engaged in the following production/business lines:
- Building and installing electric works and electric networks as well as transformer stations of up to 500 kV and electric source works;
- Building and installing industrial and civil works; communications, irrigation, telecommunications, cultural and sport works as well as airports and ports;
- Manufacturing electric equipment and accessories;
- Exploiting and trading in sand, stone, gravel and other types of construction materials;
- Transporting goods;
- Dealing in housing and urban technical infrastructure development, industrial parks, export-processing zones, hi-tech parks and new economic zones;
- Importing and exporting electric supplies and equipment, construction materials, transport equipment and means as well as construction vehicles and machinery.
Its charter capital: VND 11,117,745,115 (eleven billion, one hundred and seventeen million, seven hundred and forty five thousand, one hundred and fifteen Vietnam dong).- (Summary)
