# Decision 105/2002/QD-BNN

DECISION No. 105/2002/QD-BNN of November 19, 2002 promulgating the operation process of the system of Nhue river irrigation works
Issued together with this Decision was the operation process of the system of Nhue river irrigation works.
The system of Nhue river irrigation works is an inter-provincial system covering Hanoi, Ha Tay and Ha Nam, functioning to:
- Irrigate 81,148 ha of cultivated land and drain 107,530 ha in the basin areas;
- To prevent and fight floods for Red and Day rivers;
- To ensure waterway traffic and water for other use demands.
This process governs the management of exploitation operation and use of the works in the whole system.
All the concerned organizations and individuals benefiting from the system of Nhue river irrigation works shall have to follow this process.- (Summary)
