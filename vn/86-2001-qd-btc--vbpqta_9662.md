# Quyết định 86/2001/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 86/2001/QD-BTC of September 12, 2001 issuing the Regulation on the management and use of public-labor workday funds
To issue together with this Decision the Regulation on the management and use of public-labor workday funds.
Public-labor workday funds shall be managed and used by the People�s Committees of the provinces and centrally-run cities, the People�s Committees of the rural districts, urban districts, provincial capitals and cities, and the People�s Committees of communes, wards and townships for the construction and repair of projects in service of the community�s common interests according to law provisions.
The Regulation prescribes the principles for the management and use of the funds; the elaboration of plans on and allocation of fund source; the contents of fund management and use; and responsibilities of the agencies managing and using public-labor workday funds.- (Summary)
