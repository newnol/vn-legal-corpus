# Nghị định 22/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 22/2004/ND-CP of January 12, 2004 on consolidating the apparatuses performing religious work under the People's Committees of various levels
This Decree prescribes principles for the consolidation of the apparatuses performing religious work under the People's Committees of various levels; criteria and model of organizations performing religious work in the provinces and centrally-run cities; and the competence to set up such apparatuses under the People's Committees of various levels.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
