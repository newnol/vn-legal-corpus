# Quyết định 150/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 150/2004/QD-BCN of December 1, 2004 transforming the Investment, Trading and Service Company into Investment, Trading and Service Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, the Investment, Trading and Service Joint-Stock Company (ITASCO) is headquartered at No. 10 Ho Xuan Huong street, Hong Bang district, Hai Phong city.
Its charter capital is VND 20 billion, 57% of which to be held by the State, 40.61% sold to its laborers and 2.39% to outsiders.- (Summary)
