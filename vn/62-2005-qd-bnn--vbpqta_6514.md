# Quyết định 62/2005/QĐ-BNN

DECISION
Promulgating the Regulation on criteria for classification of special-use forests
THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND RURAL DEVELOPMENT
Pursuant to the Government's Decree No. 86/2003/ND-CP of July 18, 2003, defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development;
Pursuant to the 2004 Law on Forest Protection and Development;
At the proposal of the director of the Forestry Department,
DECIDES:
Article 1.- To promulgate together with this Decision the Regulation on the criteria for classification of special-use forests.
Article 2.- This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."
Article 3.- The director of the Office, the director of the Forestry Department, the director of the Science and Technology Department, the director of the Legal Department, the director of the Ranger Department, directors of provincial/municipal Services of Agriculture and Rural Development, heads of provincial/municipal Ranger Sub-Departments, and heads of concerned units shall have to implement this Decision.
