# Quyết định 103/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 103/2002/QD-TTg of July 30, 2002 upgrading Chu Mom Ray Nature Conservation Zone into Chu Mom Ray National Park, Kon Tum province
To upgrade Chu Mom Ray Nature Conservation Zone into a national park belonging to the system of Vietnam�s special-use forests.
Objectives and tasks of Chu Mom Ray National Park are:
- To conserve the bio-diversity as well as rare and precious flora and fauna species, including 187 endangered species, to protect primitive vegetational cover and important habitats for animals and plants.
- To contribute to maintaining and protecting the ecological environment balance, supporting the forest protection, increasing the forest coverage in headwater protection areas for Ya Ly hydro-electric power plant as well as rivers, reservoirs and dams in the region, thus ensuring environmental security and eco-natural sustainable development in the locality.
- To build material and technical bases in service of training and scientific research as well as the conservation and development of natural resources of Northern Central Highlands.
- To participate in the international cooperation program on the Indochinese peninsula on natural resource conservation and environment at the Vietnam-Laos-Cambodia border T-junction.
- To prepare material bases and other conditions for ecological tourist development, thus contributing to step by step stabilizing and improving local people�s life.
The People�s Committee of Kon Tum province shall directly manage Chu Mom Ray Natural Park.- (Summary)
