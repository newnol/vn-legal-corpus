# Quyết định 70/2002/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 70/2002/QD-BTC of June 4, 2002 amending fee levels for periodical technical expertise according to the safety standards, not extending the permits for liquefied gas bottles
To amend fee levels for periodical technical expertise according to the safety standards, not extending the permits for liquefied gas bottles, prescribed in Clause 2, Article 1 of the Finance Minister�s Decision No. 58/2000/QD-BTC of April 21, 2000 promulgating the Table of fees for technical expertise and fees for granting of permits for use of machinery, equipment, supplies and substances subject to strict safety requirements, as follows:
- For standard liquefied gas bottles: VND 12,000/expertise/bottle;
- For non-standard liquefied gas bottles: VND 20,000/expertise/bottle.
This Decision takes effect as from June 15, 2002.- (Summary)
