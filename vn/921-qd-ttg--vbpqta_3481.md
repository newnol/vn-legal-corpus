# Quyết định 921/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Terminating activities of the National Steering Council for Compilation of Vietnam’s Encyclopedia
From August 31, 2007, activities of the National Steering Council Compilation of Vietnam’s Encyclopedia, set up under the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 112/1998/QD-TTg of June 30, 1998, will terminate.
This Decision takes effect on the date of its signing.- (Summary)
