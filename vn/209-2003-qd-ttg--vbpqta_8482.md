# Quyết định 209/2003/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 209/2003/QD-TTg of October 10, 2003 establishing the External Affairs Service of Quang Binh province
To establish the External Affairs Service under the People's Committee of Quang Binh province on the basis of reorganizing the External Relations Section and the Frontier Issues Board under the People's Committee of Quang Binh province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
