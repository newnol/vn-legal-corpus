# Decision 19/2005/QD-BNV

DECISION No. 19/2005/QD-BNV of January 25, 2005 approving the Charter of Vietnam Association of Port-Waterway-Offshore Engineering
This Decision approves the Charter of Vietnam Association of Port-Waterway-Offshore Engineering (VAPO), which was adopted at the first congress on September 18, 2004.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
