# Quyết định 146/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 146/2004/QD-BCN of November 24, 2004 on transforming Saigon-Soc Trang Beer Factory into Saigon-Soc Trang Beer Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Saigon-Soc Trang Beer Joint-Stock Company, SASOBECO, has a charter capital of VND 55,000,000,000, of which the State's equities represent 51%, equities sold to laborers of the factory, 10.06% and equities sold to other subjects, 38.94%.
Its head-office is located at 16 National Highway 1, district 2, Soc Trang provincial capital, Soc Trang province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
