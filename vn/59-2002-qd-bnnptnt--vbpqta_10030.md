# Quyết định 59/2002/QĐ-BNNPTNT

DECISION No. 59/2002/QD-BNNPTNT of July 3, 2002 stipulating the designed water levels for dyke lines of the Red river and Thai Binh river systems
Ha Noi dykes (of special grade) must be capable of coping with floods at the designed water level of 13.4 m in Hanoi. The dyke lines of grades I, II and III must be capable of coping with floods at the designed water level of 13.1 m in Hanoi.
The director of the Department for Flood and Storm Prevention and Combat and Dyke Manage-ment shall base himself/herself on this Decision to prescribe the designed water levels at key spots of each dyke line of Red river and Thai Binh river systems.- (Summary)
