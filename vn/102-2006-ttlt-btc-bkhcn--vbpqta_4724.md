# Joint circular 102/2006/TTLT/BTC-BKHCN

JOINT CIRCULAR
Guiding the management of finance of the program on supports for development of intellectual assets of enterprises
This Joint Circular guides the management of finance of the program on supports for development of intellectual assets of enterprises under the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 68/2005/QD-TTg of April 4, 2005, and Decision No. 36/2006/QD-TTg of February 8, 2006.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
