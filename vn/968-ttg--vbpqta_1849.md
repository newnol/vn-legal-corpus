# Quyết định 968/TTg

Decision No.968-TTg of November 14, 1997 on the replacement of the Chairman and the Vice Chairman of the State Academic Title Council.
To appoint Minister of Education and Training, Nguyen Minh Hien to the post of Chairman of the State Academic Title Council in replacement of Mr. Tran Hong Quan; and appoint Vice Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, Chu Hao to the post of Vice Chairman of the State Academic Title Council in replacement of Mr. Chu Tuan Nha.- (Summary)
