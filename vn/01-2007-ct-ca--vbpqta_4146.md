# Directive 01/2007/CT-CA

DIRECTIVE
On the performance of key tasks of the people’s court sector in 2007
With this Directive, the President of the Supreme People’s Court requests heads of units under the Supreme People’s Court, chief judges of people’s courts and military courts of all levels to organize the performance of tasks of the people’s court sector in 2007.- (Summary)
