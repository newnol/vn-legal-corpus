# Decree 53/CP

Decree No.53-CP of May 28, 1997 on the establishment of Yen Lac township of Yen Lac district, Vinh Phuc province.
To establish Yen Lac township of Yen Lac district on the basis of the whole land area of 644 hectares and a population of 11,968 from Minh Tan commune.-
