# Quyết định 268/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 268/QD-TTg of March 12, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Hai Duong province in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises which continue to have 100% of their charter capital held by the State and be legal entities: 1 public-utility enterprise and 6 business enterprises.
To be equitized, with the State holding over 50% of charter capital in 2004: 1 enterprise.
To be equitized, with the State holding at least 51% of charter capital when shares are sold for the first time: In 2003: 4 enterprises and in 2004: 4 enterprises.
To be equitized, with the State holding under 50% of charter capital or holding no shares: In 2003: 5 enterprises; in 2004: 11 enterprises.
To be transformed from State enterprise into non-business unit with revenues in 2003: 1 enterprise.
To be dissolved in 2003: 2 enterprises.
To be assigned to laborer’s collective in 2003: 1 enterprise.
To be contractually leased in 2003: 1 enterprise.
To be merged in 2003: 2 enterprises.
To consolidate 12 irrigation work exploitation enterprises in 2003.- (Summary)
