# Quyết định 21/2000/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 21/2000/QD-BCN of April 5, 2000 on the transfer of the Mining Technique College, a member unit of the Vietnam Coal Corporation, to the Ministry of Industry
To transfer in status quo the Mining Technique College, a member unit of the Vietnam Coal Corporation, to the Ministry of Industry.
The College is headquartered in Yen Tho commune, Dong Trieu district, Quang Ninh province.- (Summary)
