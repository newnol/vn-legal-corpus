# Decision 185/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 185/QD-TTg of February 19, 2003 approving the list of part-time members of the Committee for Population, Families and Children
The Prime Minister has approved the list of part-time members of the Committee for Population, Families and Children, comprising:
- A vice-minister of Health
- A vice-minister of Justice
- A vice-minister of Public Security
- A vice-minister for Foreign Affairs
- A vice-minister of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs
- A vice-minister of Education and Training
- A vice-director of the National Center of Social Sciences and Humanities
- A deputy-commander in charge of political affairs of the Border Guard Command, the Ministry of Defense
- Representatives of the Women’s Union, Peasants’ Association, Youth Union and War Veterans’ Association.- (Summary)
