# Quyết định 1805/2003/QĐ-BTM

DECISION No. 1805/2003/QD-BTM of December 24, 2003 transforming Petrolimex Construction Consultancy Company under Vietnam Oil and Gas Corporation into Petrolimex Engineering Joint-Stock Company
To transform Petrolimex Construction Consultancy Company under Vietnam Oil and Gas Corporation into Petrolimex Engineering Joint-Stock Company (PEC).
Its transaction office: 149 Truong Chinh road, Thanh Xuan district, Hanoi.
Its charter capital is VND 3.6 billion, with 51% of which being held by the State and 49% sold to its laborers.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, annulling all previous documents contrary hereto.- (Summary)
