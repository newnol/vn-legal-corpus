# Quyết định 42/2005/QĐ-BVHTT

DECISION
On ranking a national relic
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO," the historical relic of frontline command post of the General Department of Logistics, High Command 559 and High Command 500 in Huong Do commune, Huong Khe district, Ha Tinh province, is ranked a national relic.- (Summary)
