# Quyết định 29/2002/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 29/2002/QD-BCN of June 27, 2002 transforming Duc Giang Metal Processing Enterprise under Hanoi Metal Company into Thang Long Steel Joint-Stock Company.
To transform Duc Giang Metal Processing Enterprise under Hanoi Metal Company into Thang Long Steel Joint-Stock Company.
Its head office: Duc Giang township, Gia Lam district, Hanoi.
Its charter capital: VND 8,000,000,000 (eight billion Vietnam dong), of which the State equities represent 36.4%, equities sold to the laborers inside the enterprise, 17.5%, and equities sold to the outsiders, 46.1%.
Thang Long Steel Joint-Stock Company is engaged in the following production and business lines:
- Processing, rolling, drawing and structuring steel products;
- Dealing in iron-steel and construction commodity items, and consumer goods;
- Importing and exporting metal, electric and mechanical, and consumer goods items;
- Providing office, warehouse and yard, workshop, and loading services.- (Summary)
