# Nghị quyết 08/2003/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION No. 08/2003/NQ-CP of July 4, 2003 on the Government's June 2003 regular meeting
On June 30 and July 1, the Government held its June 2003 regular meeting, discussing and resolving the following issues:
- It passed the report on reviewing the Government's direction and administration in the first 6 months and the report on the implementation of socio-economic plans in June and the first 6 months as well as a number of implementation solutions in the last 6 months.
- It gave opinions on the draft Ordinance on the State Audit; the draft decree detailing and guiding the implementation of a number of articles of the Population Ordinance; the Inspection Bill; and the draft decree on exercising the rights of the owners of State enterprises.- (Summary)
