# Quyết định 64/2007/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION
Promulgating tertiary education framework programs for collegial-degree technology disciplines
The ten collegial-degree technology disciplines under tertiary education framework programs promulgated together with this Decision include automation technology, mechantronic technology, printing technology, automotive engineering technology, construction engineering technology, electrical and electronics engineering technology, garment technology, mechanical engineering technology, electrical engineering technology, and heating and refrigeration technology.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
