# Thông tư liên tịch 104/2002/TTLT/BQP-BTC

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 104/2002/TTLT-BQP-BTC of August 1, 2002 guiding the implementation of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 47/2002/QD-TTg of April 11, 2002 on the regimes towards armymen and defense workers and employees who participated in the anti-French resistance war and were demobilized (released from active service or abandoned their jobs) as from December 31, 1960 backwards
This Circular guides the subjects of and conditions for application; the to-be enjoyed regimes; the procedures and dossiers for approval of the regimes applicable to armymen and defense workers and employees who participated in the anti-French resistance war and were demobilized (released from active service or abandoned their jobs) as from December 31, 1960 backwards.- (Summary)
