# Circular 5/1998/TT/BNN-BVTV

CIRCULAR No.5/1998/TT/BNN-BVTV of May 6, 1998 guiding the implementation of Directive No.9/1998/CT-TTg of February 18, 1998 of the Prime Minister on urgent measures to kill rats for crop protection
This Circular specifies activities to be carried out: the propaganda; the organization of training courses; the inspection and survey of cultivation fields; the launching of rat killing campaigns; the research, survey and application of rat killing measures; the encouragement of the cat raising by people; the protection and development of rat's natural enemies. The Circular also guides the tasks of the concerned agencies in carrying out the above-said activities.- (Summary)
