# Nghị định 96/2001/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 96/2001/ND-CP of December 13, 2001 dividing Quang Hoa rural district of Cao Bang province into Phuc Hoa and Quang Uyen rural districts
Phuc Hoa rural district shall have a natural land area of 25,010 hectares and a population of 21,235, comprising 9 administrative units being the communes of Hoa Thuan, My Hung, Hong Dai, Cach Linh, Trieu Au, Dai Son, Tien Thanh and Luong Thien, and the district capital of Ta Lung.
Quang Uyen rural district shall have a natural land area of 38,331 hectares and a population of 43,081, comprising 17 administrative units being the communes of Binh Lang, Cai Bo, Chi Thao, Doai Khon, Doc Lap, Hong Dinh, Hanh Phuc, Hong Quang, Hoang Hai, Phuc Sen, Phi Hai, Quoc Phong, Quang Hung, Quoc Dan, Tu Do and Ngoc Dong, and the district capital of Quang Uyen.- (Summary)
