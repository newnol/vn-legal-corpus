# Quyết định 14/2001/QĐ-BGDĐT

Decision No. 14/2001/QD-BGDDT of May 3, 2001 promulgating the Regulation on physical and health education at schools
Physical and health education at schools is a compulsory education activity, aiming to educate, protect, improve the health of, and physically develop pupils and students, thus contributing to the formation of and fostering their personality, meeting the requirements of all-round education for pupils and students.
The Regulation on physical and health education at schools, issued together with this Decision, prescribes physical and health education at schools under the national education system.- (Summary)
