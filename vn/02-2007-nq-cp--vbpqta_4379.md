# Resolution 02/2007/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
Annulling the Government’s Decree No. 81/2000/ND-CP of December 29, 2000, which details and guides the implementation of a number of articles of the Ordinance on Public Labor Obligations
With this Resolution, the Government decides to annul Decree No. 81/2000/ND-CP and its guiding documents as from January 1, 2007.- (Summary)
