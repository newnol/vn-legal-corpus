# Circular 48/1999/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 48/1999/TT-BTC of May 6, 1999 guiding the use of fines from sanctioning administrative violations in the field of traffic order and safety
The total fine amount collected from sanctioning administrative violations in the field of traffic order and safety shall be retained at the local budgets for management and use. The Circular also guides the allocation and use of this money.-
