# Quyết định 369/2003/QĐ-BLĐTBXH

DECISION No. 369/2003/QD-BLDTBXH of April 2, 2003 prolonging the location allowance level reservation duration
This Decision prescribes the prolongation of the location allowance level reservation duration until the reform of salary policies, the amendment of salary grades and tables as well as salary allowance regimes are carried out for communes, wards, townships and units, which are subject to the reduction of location allowance levels prescribed at Point 2, Section B, Part III (Appendix No. 2) of Joint Circular No. 03/2001/TTLT/BLDTBXH-BTC-UBDTMN of January 18, 2001 of the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs, the Ministry of Finance and the Committee for Ethnicities and Mountainous Areas guiding the implementation of the location allowance regime.- (Summary)
