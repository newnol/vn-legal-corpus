# Circular 07/2002/TT-BKHCN

CIRCULAR No. 07/2002/TT-BKHCN of November 15, 2002 guiding the implementation of the Prime Minister?s Decision No. 72/2002/QD-TTg of June 10, 2002 on the uniform use of the Vietnamese character code under Standard TCVN 6909:2001 in the electronic information exchange among the Party?s and State?s organizations
The following applications must be converted into the character code TCVN 6909:2001:
- The applications in office computerization.
- The Vietnamese information exchange through computer networks, including Internet and intranets of the Party?s and State?s organizations.
- On-line or off-line transactions and services using electronic information between the Party?s or State?s organizations and people.
- Websites containing information in Vietnamese language.
The conversion of electronic dossiers and data of the Party?s and State?s organizations, which have been archived in other codes, into the code TCVN 6909:2001, shall be effected step by step, depending on the urgency therefor, as well as each organization?s service scale, scope and demand.- (Summary)
