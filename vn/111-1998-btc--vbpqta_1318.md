# Circular 111/1998/BTC

CIRCULAR No. 111/1998/BTC of August 3, 1998 guiding the levels of expenses for creating electronic information pertaining the information technology
Subject to this Circular are administrative and non-business units using the State budget's funds to create electronic information on computer networks of the ministries, branches and localities.
Application scope: This Circular shall apply only to the payment of remuneration to employees for their extra working time to create electronic information on computer networks or those hired from outside.
This Circular guides in details the levels of remuneration for data input, photo scanning, creation of websites and creation of electronic information in foreign language(s).- (Summary)
