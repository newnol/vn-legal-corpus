# Decision 1050/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 1050/QD-TTg of October 23, 2000 appointing members of the Steering Committee for Enterprise Renewal and Development
To appoint the following persons to be members of the Steering Committee for Enterprise Renewal and Development:
- Standing Deputy Prime Minister Nguyen Tan Dung, as its head,
- Mr. Nguyen Minh Thong, former standing Vice Director of the Central Board for Enterprise Management Renewal, as its standing deputy-head,
- A representative of the Central Economics Commission leadership, as its member,
- A representative of the Vietnam Labor Confederation leadership, as its member,
- Representatives of leaderships of the Ministry of Planning and Investment, the Ministry of Finance, the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs and the Government�s Commission for Organization and Personnel, as its members,
- Mr. Nguyen Van Huy - former Vice Director of the Central Board for Enterprise Management Renewal, as its full-time member,
- Mr. Pham Viet Muon - former Vice Director of the Central Board for Enterprise Management Renewal, as its full-time member.- (Summary)
