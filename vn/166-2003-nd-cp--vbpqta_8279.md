# Nghị định 166/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 166/2003/ND-CP of December 24, 2003 on the establishment of communes and wards of Vinh Loi, Phuoc Long, Hong Dan, Gia Rai and Dong Hai rural districts and Bac Lieu provincial capital, Bac Lieu province
To establish Nha Mat ward of Bac Lieu provincial capital on the basis of a land area of 2,439 ha and a population of 9,237 of Hiep Thanh commune.
To establish Vinh Hau A commune of Vinh Loi rural district on the basis of a land area of 5,150 ha and a population of 7,416 of Vinh Hau commune.
To divide Phong Thanh Nam commune of Phuoc Long rural district into Phong Thanh Tay A and Phong Thanh Tay B communes, which shall have a land area and a population of 5,664 ha and 9,436; and 5,907 ha and 9,192, respectively.
To establish Vinh Loc A commune of Hong Dan rural district on the basis of a land area of 4,237.10 ha and a population of 9,314 of Vinh Loc commune.
To establish Phong Thanh A commune of Gia Rai rural district on the basis of a land area of 3,800 ha and a population of 9,271 of Phong Thanh commune.
To establish Dinh Thanh A commune of Dong Hai rural district on the basis of a land area of 2,986.50 ha and a population of 9,410 of Dinh Thanh commune.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
