# Circular 12/2000/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 12/2000/TT-BTC of February 2, 2000 guiding reward mechanism for overfulfilment of Value Added Tax collection cost estimates in 1999
Value Added Tax (VAT) on home-made commodities considered for reward includes:
- Amount of VAT on home-made commodities
- Outstanding amount of turnover tax carried forward from 1998
This Circular guides principles and bases for reward consideration, reward levels, use and final account settlement of reward money.- (Summary)
