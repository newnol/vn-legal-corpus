# Nghị quyết 58/2007/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
Approving adjustments to the land use planning up to 2010 and the 5-year (2006-2010) land use plan of Bac Kan province
The People’s Committee of Bac Kan province shall, based on the criteria approved under this Resolution, organize the implementation of the land use planning and plan in association with socio-economic development objectives as well as defense, security and eco-environmental protection tasks.- (Summary)
