# Decision 80/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.80/1998/QD-TTg of April 9, 1998 on the establishment of the Management Board of the National Target Program for Hunger Elimination and Poverty Alleviation.
The newly set-up board is composed of Deputy Prime Minister Nguyen Cong Tan as the program's manager, two deputy managers and four members who are representatives of the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs, the Ministry of Planning and Investment, the Ministry of Finance and the Commission for Ethnic Minorities and Mountainous Areas.- (Summary)
