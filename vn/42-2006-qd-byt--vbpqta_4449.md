# Decision 42/2006/QĐ-BYT

DECISION
Extending the effective duration of the Health Minister’s Decision No. 17/2006/QD-BYT of May 19, 2006, which promulgates the Provisional Regulation on import of finished-product drugs without registration numbers
The Minister of Health decides to extend the effective duration of Decision No. 17/2006/QD-BYT to the effective date of the Prime Minister’s decision guiding the implementation of the Pharmacy Law regarding the import of drugs without registration numbers.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
