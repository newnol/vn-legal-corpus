# Directive 05/2008/CT-TTg 

DIRECTIVE
Raising efficiency of the use of work time by state cadres, civil servants and public employees
The Prime Minister instructs heads of administrative agencies, heads of state non-business units, cadres, civil servants, public employees, and concerned ministries and agencies to perform several tasks in order to restore discipline and raise efficiency of the use of work time by cadres, civil servants and public employees.- (Summary)
