# Quyết định 42/2004/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 42/2004/QD-BNN of September 1, 2004 promulgating the "Vietnamese Golden Rice" Prize and the Regulation on the registration, consideration for selection and awarding of the "Vietnamese Golden Rice" Prize
The "Vietnamese Golden Rice" Prize shall be awarded to products being goods, services and scientific and technological works in the field of agriculture and rural development, which are of high socio-economic value and bring benefits to agriculture and rural development.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
