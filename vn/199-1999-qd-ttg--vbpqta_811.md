# Decision 199/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.199/1999/QD-TTg of October 6, 1999 stipulating the remuneration regime for anti-drug police force
The above-said Decision stipulates in detail the remuneration levels and remuneration fund for anti-drug police force attached to the Ministry of Public Security.- (Summary)
