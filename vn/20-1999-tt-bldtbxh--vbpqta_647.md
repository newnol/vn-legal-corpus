# Circular 20/1999/TT-BLĐTBXH

Circular No. 20/1999/TT-BLDTBXH of September 8, 1999 guiding the implementation of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 121/1999/QD-TTg of May 8, 1999 on wages and incomes of Vietnam Electricity Corporation
Subject to this Circular are workers and employees of Vietnam Electricity Corporation, including its member units in the production sector, directly controlling, servicing and supporting the electricity production and business.
The Circular also guides in detail the regimes related to wages and incomes of the above-mentioned subjects.- (Summary)
