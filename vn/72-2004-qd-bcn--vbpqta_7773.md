# Quyết định 72/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 72/2004/QD-BCN of August 5, 2004 transforming the Casting Company No. 1 into a dependent cost-accounting member of the Motors and Agricultural Machines Corporation
The Casting Company No. 1, an independent cost-accounting member of the Motors and Agricultural Machines Corporation, is transformed into a dependent cost-accounting member.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
