# Quyết định 112/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 112/2004/QD-BCN of October 14, 2004 approving the Charter on organization and operation of Viet Tien Garment Company
This Decision approves the Charter on organization and operation of Viet Tien Garment Company, an independent cost-accounting member company of Vietnam Textile and Garment Corporation, which is experimentally organized and operates after the parent company-subsidiary company model.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
