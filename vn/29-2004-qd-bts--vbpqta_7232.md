# Quyết định 29/2004/QĐ-BTS

DECISION No. 29/2004/QD-BTS of December 31, 2004 defining the functions, tasks and organizational structure of Vietnam Institute of Fisheries Economics and Planning
Under this Decision, Vietnam Institute of Fisheries Economics and Planning, VIFEP, is a non-business economic unit, which functions to research into, and advise on, fisheries management policies and development plannings in service of the Fisheries Ministry’s State management tasks.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces the Fisheries Ministry’s Decision No. 311/TS-QD of May 7, 1984.- (Summary)
