# Quyết định 118/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 118/2004/QD-BCN of November 2, 2004 transforming Hanoi Electrical Engineering Company into Hanoi Electrical Engineering Limited Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Hanoi Electrical Engineering Limited Company (CTAMAD Co. Ltd.) is headquartered at Km 12, National Road 32, Phu Dien commune, Tu Liem district, Hanoi city, and has a charter capital of VND 154,186 million.- (Summary)
