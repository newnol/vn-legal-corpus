# Quyết định 48/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 48/2003/QD-TTg of April 8, 2003 suspending the enforcement of Joint Circular No. 03/2001/TTLT-BKHDT-BCN-BTM of May 18, 2001 of the Ministry of Planning and Investment, the Ministry of Industry and the Ministry of Trade
To suspend the enforcement of Joint Circular No. 03/2001/TTLT-BKHDT-BCN-BTM of May 18, 2001 of the Ministry of Planning and Investment, the Ministry of Industry and the Ministry of Trade guiding the import of car components in CKD1 form.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after it is published on the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
