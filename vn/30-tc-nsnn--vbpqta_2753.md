# Circular 30/TC-NSNN

CIRCULAR No. 30-TC/NSNN of April 5, 1994 of the Ministry of Finance on guidelines on a number of financial and budgetary matters based on Decision No. 60-TTg of February 8, 1994 of the Prime Minister.
- Responsibilities for expenditures from the local budget.
- Constitution of sources of revenues for the local budget.
- Guidelines for revenues which exceed the projected budget granted by the Central Government to the local government.- (Summary)
