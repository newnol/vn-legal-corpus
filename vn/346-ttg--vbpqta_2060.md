# Decision 346/TTg

Decision No.346-TTg of May 28, 1996 of the Prime Minister on the tasks and powers of the Agriculture and Forestry Promotion Department under the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development
The Agriculture and Forestry Promotion Department assists the Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development in performing the function of State management over cultivation (except afforestration), and husbandry and working out the Government�s policy on agricultural and forestry promotion.- (Summary)
