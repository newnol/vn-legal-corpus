# Decision 55/2006/QĐ-BVHTT

DECISION
On ranking a national relic
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” the historical relic of Ngoa Van pagoda in Binh Khe commune, Dong Trieu district, Quang Ninh province, is ranked as a national relic.- (Summary)
