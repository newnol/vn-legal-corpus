# Decision 203/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 203/1999/QD-TTg of October 14, 1999 on the establishment of the University for Fire Prevention and Fighting
To establish the University for Fire Prevention and Fighting on the basis of the College for Fire Prevention and Fighting under the Ministry of Public Security.
The University for Fire Prevention and Fighting is attached to the Ministry of Public Security and subject to the State management by the Ministry of Education and Training over its educational and training activities.
The University for Fire Prevention and Fighting is tasked to train fire fighters of university and lower levels; foster the professional skills in fire prevention and fighting; conduct scientific research in the domain of fire prevention and fighting to meet the socio-economic development demand.- (Summary)
