# Decision 60/2000/QD-TTg

THE PRIME MINISTER
DECISION No. 60/2000/QD-TTg of May 26, 2000 assigning the task of master- degree training to the Theatrical and Cinematographic University, the Industrial Fine Art University and the Institute for International Relations
To assign the task of master-degree training to the Hanoi Theatrical and Cinematographic University under the Ministry of Culture and Information, the Industrial Fine Arts University under the Ministry of Education and Training and the Institute for International Relations under the Ministry for Foreign Affairs.
The Ministry of Education and Training shall consider and stipulate specialized branches of master-degree training for the above-mentioned two universities and institute.- (Summary)
