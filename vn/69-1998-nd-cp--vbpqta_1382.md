# Nghị định 69/1998/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 69/1998/ND-CP of September 3, 1998 on the establishment of wards and communes in Kon Tum provincial capital and Dak Ha district, Kon Tum province
To establish Le Loi ward with a land area of 395 ha and a population of 3,456 on the basis of rearranging a land area of 215 ha and a population of 2,390 people of Chu H Reng commune, a land area of 155 ha and a population of 898 of Doan Ket commune, and a land area of 25 ha and a population of 168 of Hoa Binh commune.
To readjust a land area of 274 ha and a population of 4,056 of Vinh Quang commune for Quang Trung ward and establish Duy Tan ward on the basis of a land area of 545.5 ha and a population of 3,436 of Quang Trung ward.
To establish Dak Mar commune with a land area of 4,658.5 ha and a population of 3,799 on the basis of rearranging a natural land area of 4,123.5 ha and 2,567 people of Dak H Ring commune together with 535 ha of land area 1,232 people of Ha Mon commune.- (Summary)
