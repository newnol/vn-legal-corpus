# Joint Resolution 05/2006/NQLT/CP-UBTWMTTQVN

JOINT RESOLUTION
Promulgating the Regulation on Vietnam Fatherland Front’s supervision of cadres, civil servants and Party members at population quarters
The Regulation promulgated together with this Decision applies to several communes, wards and townships; hamlets, villages, population quarters, population groups and population clusters in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh cities and Ninh Binh, Quang Binh and Tien Giang provinces.
This Joint Resolution takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
