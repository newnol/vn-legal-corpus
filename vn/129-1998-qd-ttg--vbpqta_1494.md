# Quyết định 129/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 129/1998/QD-TTg of July 13, 1998 on the establishment of Ha Nam Teachers' Training College
To set up Ha Nam Teachers' Training College on the basis of the Ha Nam Teachers' Training Intermediate School.
The Ha Nam Teachers' Training College shall be placed under the People's Committee of Ha Nam province and subject to State management by the Ministry of Education and Training over the work of education and training. The College is tasked to train and foster teachers with college or lower degrees.- (Summary)
