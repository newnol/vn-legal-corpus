# Decision 47/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.47/1999/QD-TTg of March 20, 1999 on the transfer of a number of schools attached to the Vietnam Coal Corporation to the Ministry of Industry
To transfer the Economics Intermediate School, the Mechanical and Construction Vocational Training School and the Electro-Mechanical Vocational Training School from the Vietnam Coal Corporation to the Ministry of Industry for the direct management thereof.- (Summary)
