# Quyết định 57/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.57/1998/QD-TTg of March 5, 1998 on supplements to Decision No.160-TTg of March 15, 1996 of the Prime Minister on allowances for juridical expert witnesses.
Persons who directly assist the expert witnesses during the expert testimony period shall be entitled to an allowance equal to 70% of that enjoyed by the expert witnesses.
Persons assigned by competent agencies to be present in the expert testimony period shall be entitled to an allowance equal to 50% of that enjoyed by the expert witnesses.- (Summary)
