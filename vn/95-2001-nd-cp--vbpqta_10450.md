# Nghị định 95/2001/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 95/2001/ND-CP of December 12, 2001 dividing Ky Son rural district of Hoa Binh province into Cao Phong and Ky Son rural districts
Cao Phong rural district shall have a natural land area of 25,437 hectares and a population of 38,414, comprising 13 administrative units being the communes of Yen Thuong, Yen Lap, Dung Phong, Nam Phong, Tay Phong, Tan Phong, Dong Phong, Xuan Phong, Thu Phong, Bac Phong, Binh Thanh and Thung Nai, and the district capital of Cao Phong.
Ky Son rural district shall have a natural land area of 20,203.76 hectares and a population of 32,979, comprising 10 administrative units being the communes of Dan Hoa, Mong Hoa, Phuc Tien, Dan Ha, Phu Minh, Hop Thinh, Hop Thanh, Trung Minh and Doc Lap, and the district capital of Ky Son.- (Summary)
