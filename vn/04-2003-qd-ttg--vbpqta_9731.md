# Quyết định 04/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 04/2003/QD-TTg of January 6, 2003 establishing Hung Yen Technical Teachers� Training University
To establish Hung Yen Technical Teachers� Training University on the basis of Technical Teachers� Training College No. I.
Hung Yen Technical Teachers� Training University is attached to the Ministry of Education and Training. It has legal person status, its own seal and accounts. Its office is headquartered in Khoai Chau district, Hung Yen province.
It is tasked:
- To train job-training teachers of university and college degrees;
- To train engineers and practicing technicians of lower degrees;
- To provide professional fostering;
- To conduct scientific research and apply technical-technological advances in service of socio-economic development.- (Summary)
