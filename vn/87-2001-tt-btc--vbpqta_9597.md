# Thông tư 87/2001/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 87/2001/TT-BTC of October 30, 2001 guiding the contents and levels of the expenditures for the elaboration of the frame program for tertiary- and vocational secondary-training majors, as well as the compilation of programs and teaching materials of assorted study subjects
The expenditure contents and levels prescribed in this Circular shall apply to the elaboration of the frame program for tertiary- and vocational secondary-training majors, as well as the compilation of programs and teaching materials of assorted study subjects with funding allocated by the State budget.- (Summary)
