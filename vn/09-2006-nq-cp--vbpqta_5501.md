# Resolution 09/2006/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
Adjusting the land use planning up to 2010 and five-year (2006-2010) land use plan of Bac Ninh province
The Government approves adjustments to the land use planning up to 2010 and the five-year (2006-2010) land use plan of Bac Ninh province and assigns the president of the People’s Committee of Bac Ninh province, the Minister of Natural Resources and Environment and heads of concerned ministries and branches to implement this Resolution.- (Summary)
