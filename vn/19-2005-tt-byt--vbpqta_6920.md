# Thông tư 19/2005/TT-BYT

CIRCULAR
Guiding the order, procedures and competence for the grant of Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) certificates for vaccines and medical biologicals
Under this Circular, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO," Vietnam-based establishments producing vaccines and/or medical biologicals that fully meet the conditions set in the "Vietnam's guidance for Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) for vaccines and medical biologicals," issued together with the Health Minister's Decision No. 5405/2002/QD-BYT of December 31, 2002, shall be granted GMP certificates.- (Summary)
