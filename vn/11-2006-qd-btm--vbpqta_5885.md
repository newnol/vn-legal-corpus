# Quyết định 11/2006/QĐ-BTM

DECISION
Establishing the Council for evaluation of the national trade promotion program in the 2006-2010 period
The Council is tasked to consider, assess and evaluate trade promotion schemes of program-managing units and adjust and include them into annual national trade promotion programs to be approved by the Trade Minister under the Prime Minister’s authorization.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
