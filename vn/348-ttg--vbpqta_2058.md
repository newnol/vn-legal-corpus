# Decision 348/TTg

Decision No.348-TTg of May 28, 1996 of the Prime Minister on the functions and tasks of the Veterinary Department under the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development
The Veterinary Department assists the Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development in performing the function of State management over the animal disease prevention and combat, animal quarantine and slaughter and veterinary drugs throughout the country.- (Summary)
