# Directive 30/2006/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On a number of urgent measures to prevent and eradicate brown rice leaf-hopper, rice grassy stunt disease and rice ragged stunt disease
The Prime Minister sets down several measures to be applied immediately by concerned ministries and localities in order to promptly prevent and combat the epidemics and protect rice production in the Mekong River delta.- (Summary)
