# Thông tư liên tịch 91/1998/TTLT/BTC-BQP

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 91/1998/TTLT-BTC-BQP of June 29, 1998 guiding the drafting, implementation and settlement of the State budget in the field of national defense
This Circular specifically guides the stipulations on the assignment of tasks of making the State budget expenditures in the national defense; the drafting of annual State budget estimates of the Ministry of Defense; the Ministry of Defense's budget implementation; the accounting and settlement of the Ministry of Defense's budget.- (Summary)
