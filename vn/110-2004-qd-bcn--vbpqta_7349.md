# Quyết định 110/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 110/2004/QD-BCN of October 13, 2004 amending Clause 1, Article 1 of the Industry Minister’s Decision No. 85/2004/QD-BCN of September 1, 2004 transforming Da Nang Steel Structure Manufacturing Factory into Da Nang Steel Structure Manufacturing Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, the joint-stock company shall have a charter capital of VND 7,500,000,000; of which the State equities represent 51.54%, equities sold to laborers in the factory, 42.06% and equities sold to other subjects, 6.4%.
