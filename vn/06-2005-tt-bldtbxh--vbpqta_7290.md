# Thông tư 06/2005/TT-BLĐTBXH

CIRCULAR No. 06/2005/TT-BLDTBXH of January 5, 2005 guiding the method of formulating labor norms in State companies under the Government’s Decree No. 206/2004/ND-CP of December 14, 2004
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces Circular No. 14/LDTBXH-TT of April 10, 1997.
The provisions of this Circular apply as from January 1, 2005.- (Summary)
