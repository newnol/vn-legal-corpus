# Quyết định số 40/2005/QD-BTC On release of the List of legal documents issued by the Ministry of Finance that had lapsed, abrogated or replaced (&lt;font color="red"&gt;Content Attached&lt;/font&gt;)

MINISTRY OF FINANCE No. 40/2005/QD-BTC SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF VIETNAM Independence - Freedom - Happiness ­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­ Hanoi dated 06 July 2005
MINISTRY OF FINANCE
No. 40/2005/QD-BTC
SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF VIETNAM
Independence - Freedom - Happiness ­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­­
Hanoi dated 06 July 2005
MINISTER OF FINANCE
- Pursuant to the Law on promulgation of legal documents dated 12 November 1996 and the Law on amendment of and addition to a number of articles of the law on promulgation of legal documents dated 6 December 2002;
- Pursuant to the Decree No. 101/CP dated 3 September 1997 of the Government providing detail provisions for implementation of several articles of the Law on promulgation of legal documents;
- Pursuant to the Decree No. 77/2003/ND-CP dated 01 July 2003 of the Government providing the functions, tasks, power and organizational structure of the Ministry of Finance;
Considering the proposal of the Legal Departments,
DECIDED:
Article 1 . Release the List of Legal documents issued by the Ministry of Finance that had lapsed from 31 December 2004, including 233 documents (Attachment).
Article 2 . This Decision shall be in full force 15 days from the date of publication in the Official Gazette.
Article 3 . Ministries, ministerial-level agencies, government agencies, people committees of centrally-run provinces, cities and organizations, unit under the Ministry of Finance shall take responsibility to implement this Decision./.
