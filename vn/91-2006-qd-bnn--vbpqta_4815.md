# Quyết định 91/2006/QĐ-BNN

DECISION
Defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Inspectorate of the Plant Protection Department
The Inspectorate of the Plant Protection Department functions to conduct administrative and specialized inspection within the scope of state management by the Plant Protection Department according to the provisions of law.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
