# Joint circular 15/2007/TTLT-BTC-BNNPTNT 

JOINT CIRCULAR
Guiding the management and use of state budget funds for the Program on plant varieties, animal breeds and forest tree saplings
This Joint Circular guides the management and use of state budget funds for the Program on plant varieties, animal breeds and forest tree saplings in the 2006-2010 period under the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 17/2006/QD-TTg of January 20, 2006.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces Joint Circular No. 101/2005/TTLT-BTC-BNNPTNT of December 20, 2001.- (Summary)
