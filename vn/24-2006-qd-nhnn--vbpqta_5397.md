# Decision 24/2006/QD-NHNN 

DECISION
Promulgating the Regulation on the grant and withdrawal of establishment and operation permits of people’s credit funds; the opening and termination of operation of transaction offices, branches and representative offices, and transaction bureaus and points of people’s credit funds; division, splitting, consolidation and merger of people’s credit funds; and liquidation of people’s credit funds under the State Bank’s supervision
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces Decision No. 615/2003/QD-NHNN of June 16, 2003.- (Summary)
