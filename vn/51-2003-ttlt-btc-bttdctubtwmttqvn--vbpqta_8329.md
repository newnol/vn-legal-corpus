# Thông tư liên tịch 51/2003/TTLT/BTC-BTTĐCTUBTWMTTQVN

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 51/2003/TTLT/BTC-BTTDCTUBTWMTTQVN of May 23, 2003 guiding the funding for direction and operation of the People's Inspection Boards of communes, wards and townships
The State budget shall allocate a funding amount for the Fatherland Front Central Committee to organize and direct the inspection work.
Funding for organization and operation of the People's Inspection Boards of communes, wards and townships shall be submitted by the provincial-level People's Committees to the People's Councils of the same level for the latter to apportion them for the commune budgets, so that the commune-level People's Committees shall allocate adequate funding for the operation of the People's Inspection Boards and assign the commune-level Fatherland Front Committees to manage such funding.- (Summary)
