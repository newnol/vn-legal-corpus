# Decision 1518/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 1518/QD-TTg of November 30, 2001 transforming Medical Instrument Factory No. II into a joint-stock company
To transform the State-run Medical Instrument Factory No. II under Vietnam Medical Equipment Corporation, the Ministry of Health, into Mechanical Instrument Joint-Stock Company.
Its head office: Mo Che ward, Song Cong town, Thai Nguyen province.
Its charter capital is VND 21 billion, of which the State equities represent 0%, equities sold to the laborers inside the enterprise, 80%, and equities sold to outsiders, 20%.
Mechanical Instrument Joint-Stock Company shall be engaged in production and business lines according to law provisions.- (Summary)
