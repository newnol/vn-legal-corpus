# Decision 270/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 270/QD-TTg of March 12, 2003 on disciplining collectives and individuals jointly responsible for U Minh forest fires
To discipline in form of reprimand the following collectives and individuals jointly responsible for U Minh forest fires in Kien Giang and Ca Mau provinces:
- The leadership of the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development.
- Mr. Truong Quoc Tuan, President of the People’s Committee of Kien Giang province.
- Mr. Nguyen Van Tan, Vice-President of the People’s Committee of Kien Giang province.
- Mr. Vo Thanh Binh, President of the People’s Committee of Ca Mau province.
- Mr. Nguyen Quoc Viet, Vice-President of the People’s Committee of Ca Mau province.- (Summary)
