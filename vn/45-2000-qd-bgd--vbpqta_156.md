# Decision 45/2000/QD-BGD&DT

THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION AND TRAINING
DECISION No. 45/2000/QD-BGD&DT of October 18, 2000 promulgating the Regulation on complementary primary education graduation examinations
Complementary primary education graduation examinations are the State�s appreciation of learning and practicing results of learners as well as efforts of teachers and local education establishments according to the training objectives after they have completed the complementary primary education program. The complementary primary education graduation examination results shall serve as basis for granting primary education graduation diplomas (complementary education system) and considering the enrolment or sitting for examinations for promotion to the first grade of complementary junior secondary schools.
The Regulation on complementary primary education graduation examinations, issued together with this Decision, comprises 8 chapters with 32 articles, specifies the conditions and dossiers for sitting at the examinations; examination dates, subjects and contents; graduation conditions and grading; questions and the council for question compilation; organization and directing of examinations; approval of examinations results and keeping of dossiers; commendation and disciplines.
The complementary primary education graduation examination subjects include Vietnamese language and mathematics. Learners qualified to sit for the graduation examinations shall be classified into 3 levels: excellent, good and fair (clearly stated in graduation diplomas). Primary education graduation diplomas (complementary education system) are national diplomas granted only once by the Ministry of Education and Training.- (Summary)
