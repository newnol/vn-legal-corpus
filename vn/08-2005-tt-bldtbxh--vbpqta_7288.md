# Thông tư 08/2005/TT-BLĐTBXH

CIRCULAR No. 08/2005/TT-BLDTBXH of January 5, 2005 guiding the implementation of the Government’s Decree No. 207/2004/ND-CP of December 14, 2004 on the salary, bonus and responsibility regimes for Managing Boards’ members, general directors and directors of State companies
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
The provisions of this Circular apply as from January 1, 2005.- (Summary)
