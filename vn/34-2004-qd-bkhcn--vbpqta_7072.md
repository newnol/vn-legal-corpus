# Quyết định 34/2004/QĐ-BKHCN

DECISION No. 34/2004/QD-BKHCN of October 29, 2004 promulgating Vietnamese standards
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, the following 6 Vietnamese standards are promulgated:
- TCVN 7373: 2004: Soils quality - Index values of total nitrogen content in the soils of Vietnam;
- TCVN 7374: 2004: Soils quality - Index values of total phosphorous content in the soils of Vietnam;
- TCVN 7375: 2004: Soils quality - Index values of total potassium content in the soils of Vietnam;
- TCVN 7376: 2004: Soils quality - Index values of total organic carbon content in the soils of Vietnam;
- TCVN 7377: 2004: Soils quality - pH index values in the soils of Vietnam;
- TCVN 7378: 2004: Vibration and shock - Vibration of buildings - Limits of vibration levels and method for evaluation.- (Summary)
