# Quyết định 06/2005/QĐ-BTNMT

DECISION
Defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Remote Sensing Center
Under this Decision, the Remote Sensing Center is a non-business organization under the Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment and has the functions of supervising natural resources and environment by remote sensing technology and researching into the application and transfer of remote sensing technology in the natural resources and environment domain.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
