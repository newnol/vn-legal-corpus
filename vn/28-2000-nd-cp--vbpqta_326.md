# Decree 28/2000/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 28/2000/ND-CP of August 4, 2000 on the establishment of townships in Thot Not, O Mon and Phung Hiep districts, Can Tho province
To establish Thanh An township in Thot Not district on the basis of a land area of 185.51 ha and a population of 3,000 of Thanh Thang commune; a land area of 1,595.1 ha and a population of 9,157 of Thanh An commune.
To establish Thoi Lai township in O Mon district on the basis of a land area of 946.51 ha and a population of 10,183 of Thoi Lai commune.
To establish Cay Duong township in Phung Hiep district on the basis of a land area of 1,512 ha and a population of 7,981 of Hiep Hung commune.
To establish Kinh Cung township in Phung Hiep district on the basis of a land area of 1,300 ha and a population of 10,288 of Hoa An commune.- (Summary)
