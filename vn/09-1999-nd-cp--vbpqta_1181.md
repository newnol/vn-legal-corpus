# Nghị định 09/1999/NĐ-CP

DECREE No.09/1999/ND-CP of March 1st, 1999 to establish Long Xuyen city of An Giang province
To establish Long Xuyen city of An Giang province on the basis of the total land area of 10,687 ha and a population of 245,149 of Long Xuyen town. Long Xuyen city shall comprise 10 administrative units being My Long, My Binh, My Xuyen, My Phuoc, Binh Duc wards and My Hoa, My Thoi, My Thanh, My Khanh and My Hoa Hung communes.- (Summary)
