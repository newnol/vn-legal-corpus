# Quyết định 1050/2004/QĐ-NHNN

DECISION No. 1050/2004/QD-NHNN of August 23, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on organization and operation of the State Bank's representative office in Ho Chi Minh city
The State Bank's representative office is headquartered at 17 Ben Chuong Duong street, District 1, Ho Chi Minh city.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces Decision No. 410/1998/QD-NHNN9 of December 3, 1998.- (Summary)
