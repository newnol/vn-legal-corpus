# Thông tư 24/1997/TT-BGD

CIRCULAR No.24/1997/TT-BGD & DT of December 29, 1997 guiding a number of articles of the Regulation on national exams to select brilliant general education pupils.
This Circular provides guidance on: the selection of teams to participate in the examination, the exam subjects, the exam date and timetable, the exam program, the organization and directing of exams, the regulations on examination participation and invigilation, the marking of examination papers, the granting of certificates.- (Summary)
