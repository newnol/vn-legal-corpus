# Quyết định 30/2003/QĐ-BNV

DECISION No. 30/2003/QD-BNV of June 12, 2003 approving the Charter of Vietnam Plastics Association
To approve the Charter of Vietnam Plastics Association already adopted by its IIIrd Congress on December 7, 2002.
Vietnam Plastics Association (abbreviated to VPA) is a non-governmental voluntary organization embracing units engaged in the fields of scientific research as well as plastics and rubber production and trading.
The Association's objectives are to align and cooperate with and support one another economically and technically in the research, production, trading, service and raising products' value; represent and protect the legitimate interests of its members; contribute to creating more jobs and improving laborers' living standards.
The Association is a member of the ASEAN Federation of Plastics Industries (AFPI) and the Asian Plastics Forum (APF).
It has the legal person status and is headquartered in Ho Chi Minh city. It has representative offices at home and abroad.
It operates nationwide, is subject to the Industry Ministry's management in plastics research, production, trading and service activities.- (Summary)
