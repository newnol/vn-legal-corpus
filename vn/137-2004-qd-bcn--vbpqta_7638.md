# Quyết định 137/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 137/2004/QD-BCN of November 22, 2004 transforming Dong Nam Textile Company into Dong Nam Textile Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Dong Nam Textile Joint-Stock Company (DONATEXCO) is headquartered at No. 18/3 Au Co street, Tan Thanh ward, Tan Phu district, Ho Chi Minh city.
Its charter capital is VND 48 billion, 51% of which to be held by the State, 42.51% sold to its laborers and 6.49% to outsiders.- (Summary)
