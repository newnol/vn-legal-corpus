# Decision 09/2002/QD-BKHCN

DECISION No. 09/2002/QD-BKHCN OF NOVEMBER 22, 2002 PROMULGATING VIETNAMESE STANDARDS
THE MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY
Pursuant to the Government?s Decree No. 22/CP of May 22, 1993 on the tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment (now the Ministry of Science and Technology);
Pursuant to the Ordinance on Goods Quality of December 24, 1999;
At the proposal of the General Director of Standardization, Metrology and Quality Control,
DECIDES:
Article 1.- To promulgate the following 8 Vietnamese standards:
1. TCVN 7159 : 2002 (ISO 7851 : 1983) Fertilizers and soil improvement substances ? Classification.
2. TCVN 7160 : 2002 (ISO 2131 : 1972) Surface activators ? Simple classification.
3. TCVN 6722 : 2002 (ISO 14123-2 : 1998) Machine safety ? Reduction of impacts on health caused by dangerous substances discharged from machines ? Part 2 Methodology for Machine safety ? guiding the inspection process.
4. TCVN 7011-4 : 2002 (ISO 230 ? 4 : 1996) Metal cutting machines ? Rules on inspecting machine tools ? Part 4 Process of inspecting digitally controlled machine tools.
5. TCVN 7012 : 2002 (ISO 1708 : 1989) Metal-cutting machines ? Conditions for pre-acceptance test of common lathes ? Inspecting the precision.
6. TCVN 7013-1 : 2002 (ISO 2773 ? 1 : 1973) Metal-cutting machines ? Conditions for inspecting column drilling machines ? Inspecting the precision ? Part 2 Geometrical inspection.
7. TCVN 7013-2 : 2002 (ISO 2773 ? 2 : 1973) Metal-cutting machines ? Conditions for inspecting column drilling machines ? Inspecting the precision ? Part 2 Actual inspection.
8. TCVN 7014 : 2002 (ISO 13853 : 1998) Machine safety ? Safe distance for preventing people?s feet from reaching dangerous areas.
Article 2.- This Decision takes implementation effect 15 days after its signing.
For the Minister of Science and Technology Vice Minister BUI MANH HAI
