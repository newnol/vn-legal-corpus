# Quyết định 123/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 123/2004/QD-BCN of November 4, 2004 transforming the Nam Tobacco Raw Materials Company into Hoa Viet Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Hoa Viet Joint-Stock Company, abbreviated to HOA VIET JSC., shall have a charter capital of VND 133,000,000,000; of which the State's equities represent 51%, equities sold to laborers of the company, 15.82%, equities preferentially sold to tobacco growers, 0.5% and equities auctioned to other subjects, 32.68%.
Its head-office is located in Long Binh ward, Bien Hoa city, Dong Nai province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
