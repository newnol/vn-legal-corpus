# Decree 64/1999/ND-CP

Decree No. 64/1999/ND-CP of August 2, 1999 on the establishment of a number of wards of Long Xuyen town, An Giang province
To establish My Thanh ward on the basis of the whole land area of 1,389.82 hectares and a population of 24,881 of My Thanh commune.
To establish My Thoi ward on the basis of the whole land area of 2,000.31 hectares and a population of 19,875 of My Thoi commune.
To establish Binh Khanh ward on the basis of a land area of 628.80 hectares and a population of 24,820 of Binh Duc ward.
To establish My Quy ward on the basis of a land area of 472.22 hectares and a population of 9,983 of My Phuoc ward.- (Summary)
