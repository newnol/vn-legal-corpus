# Decision 17/2004/QD-BGTVT

DECISION No. 17/2004/QD-BGTVT of September 30, 2004 promulgating the Regu-lation on speed of, and distance between, motorized vehicles operating on roads
The Regulation prescribes the speed of, and distance between, two motorized vehicles operating on roads.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces Decision No. 4596/2001/QD-BGTVT of December 28, 2001.- (Summary)
