# Quyết định 28/2003/QĐ-BNV

DECISION No. 28/2003/QD-BNV of June 11, 2003 promulgating the plan on training and fostering of State management lecturers in the 2003-2005 period
Issued together with this Decision was the plan on training and fostering of State management lecturers in the 2003-2005 period.
This plan is part of the plan on training and fostering officials and public employees in the 2001-2005 period, already approved by the Prime Minister. It aims to build up a contingent of State management lecturers with professional qualifications and pedagogic methods, who meet the current requirements of training and fostering State employees, thus contributing to successfully performing the master program on State administrative reform.
Subject to this plan are lecturers teaching the State management study subject, who are on the payrolls of Ho Chi Minh National Political Institute, the National Institute of Public Administration, the provincial-level political schools as well as ministries' and branches' establishments for training of State employees.- (Summary)
