# Decision 124/1999/QD/BNN/TCCB

DECISION No. 124/1999/QD/BNN/TCCB of September 3, 1999 on the establishment of the standing office of the national target program on rural clean water and environmental hygiene
The standing office of the national target program on rural clean water and environmental hygiene is located at the Center for daily-life water and rural environmental hygiene. The standing office has its own seal and is entitled to open bank account(s) at the State Treasury.
The standing office of the national target program on rural clean water and environmental hygiene is tasked to assist the Management Board of the national target program on rural clean water and environmental hygiene.- (Summary)
