# Thông tư 30/2004/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 30/2004/TT-BTC of April 7, 2004 guiding the regime of collection, payment, management and use of charge for exploitation and use of archived documents
This Circular applies to the collection, payment, management and use of charge for exploitation and use of State-managed documents archived at the National Archival Center under the State Clerical and Archival Department and provincial/municipal Archival Centers set up by competent State agencies.
Charge payers are organizations and individuals that exploit and use documents archived at the archival centers mentioned above.
Such charge is a revenue belonging to the State budget. 90% of the collected charge amount shall be retained at the charge-collecting units and the remaining 10% shall be remitted into the State budget.
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, annulling all previous regulations on exploitation and use of archived documents contrary hereto.- (Summary)
