# Thông tư liên tịch 89/2001/TTLT/BTC-BCA

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 89/2001/TTLT-BTC-BCA of November 8, 2001 guiding the payment, use and final settlement of enterprise income tax for public-utility State enterprises under the Ministry of Public Security
This Circular guides the tax registration, declaration, payment and final settlement, as well as the use of enterprise income tax amount in compatibility with production and business specifications of public-utility State enterprises under the Ministry of Public Security.- (Summary)
