# Quyết định 1133/2004/QĐ-NHNN

DECISION No. 1133/2004/QD-NHNN of September 9, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on organization and operation of the Organization and Personnel Department
The Organization and Personnel Department is a unit under the State Bank, functioning to assist the Governor and the Party Caucus Commission of the State Bank in the organizational and personnel work, personnel management, training and salary of the State Bank and the entire sector according to law provisions.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
