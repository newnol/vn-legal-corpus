# Quyết định 131/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 131/2003/QD-TTg of July 1, 2003 adjusting Clause 3, Article 4 of Decision No. 168/2001/QD-TTg of October 30, 2001 on the long-term orientations, 2001-2005 five-year plan and basic solutions for socio-economic develop-ment in the Central Highlands
To cancel the "provision of 4 meters of ordinary fabrics/person/year to hungry households and poor households as well as village patriarchs and hamlet chiefs meeting with difficulties and families with meritorious services to the country" prescribed in Clause 3, Article 4 of Decision No. 168/2001/QD-TTg.
The Committee for Nationalities shall assume the prime responsibility and coordinate with the concerned ministries and branches in proposing practical and efficient forms of support in replacement of the cancelled support forms.- (Summary)
