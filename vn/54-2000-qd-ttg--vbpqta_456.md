# Decision 54/2000/QĐ-TTg

THE PRIME MINISTER
Decision No. 54/2000/QD-TTg of May 19, 2000 on the establishment of the Steering Committee for the compilation of the book "History of the Vietnamese Government from 1945 up to now"
To establish the Steering Committee for the compilation of the book "History of the Vietnamese Government from 1945 up to now." The Committee shall be tasked to approve the blueprints, direct the study, compilation and pre-acceptance test of the project.
The Committee is composed of Deputy Prime Minister Pham Gia Khiem, as its head; Mr. Do Quang Trung, Minister-Chairman of the Government Commission for Organization and Personnel, as its deputy-head and 9 members being representatives of concerned units.- (Summary)
