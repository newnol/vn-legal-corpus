# Quyết định 128/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 128/1999/QD-TTg of May 28, 1999 on the establishment of Construction College No. 1
To establish Construction College No. 1 on the basis of Construction Intermediate School No. 1.
Construction College No. 1 is tasked to train and foster cadres with college or lower degrees; and conduct scientific research in service of socio-economic development.
Construction College No.1 is attached to the Ministry of Construction and subject to the State management over its education and training by the Ministry of Education and Training.- (Summary)
