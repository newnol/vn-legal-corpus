# Quyết định 153/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 153/2004/QD-BCN of December 2, 2004 transforming the Mineral Import-Export Company into Mineral Import-Export Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, the Mineral Import-Export Joint-Stock Company (MIMEXCO) is headquartered at No. 6 Pham Ngu Lao street, Phan Chu Trinh ward, Hoan Kiem district, Hanoi.
Its charter capital is VND 3 billion, 40% of which to be held by the State, 44.78% sold to its laborers and 15.22% to outsiders.- (Summary)
