# Decision 68/2005/QD-BNV 

DECISION No. 68/2005/QD-BNV of July 11, 2005, approving the Charter of Vietnam Beer-Alcohol-Beverage Association
Vietnam Beer-Alcohol-Beverage Association (VBA) is a voluntary organization of Vietnamese enterprises, engaged in the production, service provision, processing and supply of special-use materials and equipment in the beer-alcohol-beverage industry.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
