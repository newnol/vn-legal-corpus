# Quyết định 05/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 05/2002/QD-TTg of January 4, 2002 on organizing the 2002 general survey of economic, administrative and non-business establishments
To organize the 2002 general survey of economic, administrative and non-business establishments (the second time) throughout the country; excluding economic establishments belonging to agricultural, forestry and aquatic sectors, which have already had data from the general survey of rural, agricultural and aquatic sectors in 2001.
The survey contents cover the following issues:
- General norms and outlines of economic, administrative and non-business establishments (names, addresses, types of organization, economic sectors,�);
- Norms on the quantity and qualifications of laborers, production and business lines, and results of production and business activities;
- Some norms on information technology foundation and level of application.
The general survey shall last from July 1 to July 30, 2002. The General Department of Statistics shall report the results thereof by October 2002; the official results of the general survey shall be announced in the second quarter of 2003.- (Summary)
