# Decision 348/TTg

Decision No.348-TTg of May 28, 1997 on the new membership of the Vietnam National Committee for the "International Decade for the Reduction of Natural Calamities".
The said Committee is now composed of a Chairman- Mr. Nguyen Cong Tan, Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development; 6 Vice Chairmen and 19 members.- (Summary)
