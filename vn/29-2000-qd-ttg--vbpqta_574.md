# Decision 29/2000/QD-TTg

Decision No.29/2000/QD-TTg of February 28, 2000 on the transformation of Seagull Shipping Company into a joint-stock company
To transform Seagull Shipping Company, a State enterprise, into Seagull Shipping Joint Stock Company (SESCO)
- Head office: 15 Cu Chinh Lan, Hong Bang district, Hai Phong city.
- Charter capital: 15,000,000,000 VND, of which the State�s stocks represent 30% while the remaining 70% shall be sold to shareholders inside and outside the enterprise.
- Business lines:
+ Domestic and overseas sea shipping
+ Agency services for seagoing ships, multi-mode goods delivery and transportation
+ Maritime brokerage
+ Seagoing ship supply
+ Import and export.- (Summary)
