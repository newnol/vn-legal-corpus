# Nghị định 192/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 192/2004/ND-CP of November 23, 2004 establishing communes of Cai Nuoc and Phu Tan districts, Ca Mau province
To establish Thanh Phu commune of Cai Nuoc district on the basis of a land area of 3,111 ha and a population of 13,725 of Luong The Tran commune.
To establish Hoa My commune of Cai Nuoc district on the basis of a land area of 3,860.20 ha and a population of 9,326 of Hung My commune.
To establish Dong Hung commune of Cai Nuoc district on the basis of a land area of 3,283.40 ha and a population of 10,299 of Dong Thoi commune.
To establish Phu Thuan commune of Phu Tan district on the basis of a land area of 3,925 ha and a population of 11,359 of Phu My commune.
To establish Rach Cheo commune of Phu Tan district on the basis of a land area of 4,450 ha and a population of 9,998 of Tan Hung Tay commune.
To establish Khai Quang ward on the basis of the existing Khai Quang commune, with a land area of 1,152.08 ha and a population of 16,624.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
