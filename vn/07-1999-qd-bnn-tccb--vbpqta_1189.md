# Decision 07/1999/QD-BNN-TCCB

DECISION No.07/1999/QD/BNN-TCCB of January 12, 1999 on transforming Construction Company 47, a member of Construction Corporation 4, into an independent enterprise
Construction Company 47 is no longer a member of Construction Corporation 4. Instead, it is now transformed into an independent enterprise of the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development.- (Summary)
