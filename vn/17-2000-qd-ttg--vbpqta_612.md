# Decision 17/2000/QD-TTg

DECISION No.17/2000/QD-TTg of February 3, 2000 on the regime of wages and allowances for officials and employees of the Development Assistance Fund
The Prime Minister issued this Decision to specify the application of the regime of wages and allowances as prescribed in the Government�s Decree No.26/CP of May 23, 1993 provisionally prescribing the new wage regime in State enterprises, to officials and employees of the Development Assistance Fund. The Development Assistance Fund shall be entitled to apply the additional coefficient of the minimum wage provided for in the Government�s Decree No.28/CP of March 28, 1997 on the reform of the management of wages and incomes in State enterprises, provided that the conditions prescribed in this Decision must be satisfied.- (Summary)
