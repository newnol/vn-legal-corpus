# Quyết định 623/2003/QĐ-BTM

DECISION No. 623/2003/QD-BTM of May 27, 2003 promulgating the Regulation on organization and operation of the National Advanced Training Institute
To issue together with this Decision the Regulation on organization and operation of the National Advanced Training Institute.
The National Advanced Training Institute (abbreviated to NATI) is a non-business unit with revenues under the Trade Ministry, having part of expenses for its regular activities covered by itself and operating in the fields of education, training and fostering as well as scientific and technological research on the work of organization, management and professional operations of the trade service throughout the country.- (Summary)
