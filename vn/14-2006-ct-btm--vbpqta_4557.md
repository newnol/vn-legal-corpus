# Directive 14/2006/CT-BTM

DIRECTIVE
On the implementation of the general plan on e-commerce development in the 2006-2010 period
The Minister of Trade requests concerned agencies to perform several tasks in order to organize the implementation of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 222/2005/QD-TTg of September 15, 2005, approving the general plan on e-commerce development in the 2006-2010 period.- (Summary)
