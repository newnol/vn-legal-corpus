# Quyết định 08/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 08/2004/QD-TTg of January 15, 2004 approving the program on renewing the financial management mechanism for State administrative agencies and public-service units in the 2004-2005 period
The program on renewing the financial management mechanism for State administrative agencies and public-service units in the 2004-2005 period, approved together with this Decision, shall be carried out through the following 3 specific schemes:
- Scheme 1: Elaboration and distribution of State budget estimates according to output results;
- Scheme 2: Further perfecting the financial management mechanism for State administrative agencies and public-service units; and
- Scheme 3: Renewing the work of financial inspection and supervision for State administrative agencies and public-service units.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
