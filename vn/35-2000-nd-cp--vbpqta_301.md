# Decree 35/2000/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 35/2000/ND-CP of August 18, 2000 on the establishment of new commune and ward of Muong Lay district and Dien Bien Phu provincial capital, Lai Chau province
To establish new commune and ward under Muong Lay district and Dien Bien Phu provincial capital, Lai Chau province as follows:
- To establish Na Hy commune under Muong Lay district on the basis of a land area of 44,284 ha and a population of 10,441 of Cha Cang commune.
- To establish Tan Thanh ward under Dien Bien Phu provincial capital on the basis of a land area of 102 ha and a population of 8,210 of Muong Thanh ward.- (Summary)
