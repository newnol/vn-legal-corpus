# Nghị định 157/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 157/2003/ND-CP of December 10, 2003 on the adjustment of administrative boundaries for the establishment of communes of Chau Thanh, Cau Ngang, Cang Long and Tra Cu rural districts, Tra Vinh province
To establish Luong Hoa A commune of Chau Thanh rural district on the basis of a land area of 2,348.77 ha and a population of 8,557 of Luong Hoa commune.
To split Hiep My commune of Cau Ngang rural district into Hiep My Dong and Hiep My Tay communes, which shall have a land area and a population of 1,560.21 ha and 7,025; and 1,970.06 ha and 8,097, respectively.
To establish Dai Phuc commune of Cang Long rural district on the basis of a land area of 1,050,675 ha and a population of 5,003 of Dai Phuoc commune.
To establish Nhi Long Phu commune of Cang Long rural district on the basis of a land area of 1,193.13 ha and a population of 7,560 of Nhi Long commune.
To establish Kim Son commune of Tra Cu rural district on the basis of a land area of 2,228.72 ha and a population of 7,874 of Thanh Son commune.
To establish Tan Son commune of Tra Cu rural district on the basis of a land area of 1,521.145 ha and a population of 6,435 of Tap Son commune.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
