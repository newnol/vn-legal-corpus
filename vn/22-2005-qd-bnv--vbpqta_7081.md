# Decision 22/2005/QD-BNV

DECISION No. 22/2005/QD-BNV of February 3, 2005 permitting the establishment of Vietnam Association of Craft Villages
Vietnam Association of Craft Villages, established under this Decision, is organized and operates under the Association’s Charter approved by the Minister of Home Affairs. Its operation shall be subject to the uniform State management by the Ministry of Home Affairs and the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development.
