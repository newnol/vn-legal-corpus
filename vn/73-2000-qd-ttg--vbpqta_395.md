# Quyết định 73/2000/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 73/2000/QD-TTg of June 21, 2000 on the conversion of Dong Nai Roofsheet & Construction Material Company into a joint- stock company
To convert Dong Nai Roofsheet & Construction Material Company under the Vietnam Cement Corporation into Dong Nai Roofsheet & Construction Material Joint-Stock Company (DONAC).
Head office: Street No. 4, Bien Hoa 1 Industrial Park, Bien Hoa city, Dong Nai province.
Charter capital: VN dong 48,389,000,000, of which the State equities represent 25-30%, equities sold to laborers inside the enterprise: 30% and those sold to subjects outside the enterprise: 45-40%.
Business lines:
- Producing and dealing in cement products, roofsheet, construction materials, construction and installation.
- Providing trade services, export and import of supplies and raw materials, construction materials, interior decorations and construction equipment and accessories thereof.- (Summary)
