# Quyết định 140/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 140/2002/QD-TTg of October 18, 2002 assigning the task of doctorate training to the National Institute of Public Administration
To assign the task of doctorate training to the National Institute of Public Administration, the Ministry of the Interior.
The Ministry of Education and Training shall define doctorate-training specialties for the National Institute of Public Administration.- (Summary)
