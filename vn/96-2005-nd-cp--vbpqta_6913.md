# Nghị định 96/2005/NĐ-CP

DECREE
On the establishment of Hoa Binh rural district, Bac Lieu province
This Decree provides for the establishment of Hoa Binh district, Bac Lieu province on the basis of a land area of 41,133 ha and a population of 102,063 of Vinh Loi rural district.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
