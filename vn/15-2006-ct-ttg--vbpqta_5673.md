# Directive 15/2006/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On enhancing the management of capital, property and personnel at state agencies and units
The Prime Minister sets several tasks for ministries, agencies and localities to perform in order to enhance the management of their capital, property and personnel and prevent corruption and waste.- (Summary)
