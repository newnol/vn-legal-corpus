# Nghị định 183/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 183/2004/ND-CP of November 1, 2004 establishing Hung Son township of Lam Thao district, Phu Tho province
To establish Hung Son township on the basis of a land area of 117.40 ha and a population of 3,532 of Hy Cuong commune, a land area of 200.20 ha and a population of 2,754 of Tien Kien commune, and a land area of 152.40 ha and a population of 3,158 of Chu Hoa commune.
Hung Son township shall have a land area of 470 ha and a population of 9,444.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
