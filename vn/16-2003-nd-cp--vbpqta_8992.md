# Nghị định 16/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 16/2003/ND-CP of February 20, 2003 on the establishment of townships of Tan Yen and Viet Yen rural districts, Bac Giang province
To establish Nha Nam township of Tan Yen rural district on the basis of a part of the land area and population of Nha Nam and An Duong communes. Nha Nam township shall have a land area of 127.16 ha and a population of 3,983. Nha Nam commune shall be left with a land area of 350.45 ha and a population of 3,847, and An Duong commune with a land area of 1,151.37 and a population of 6,546.
To establish Nenh township of Viet Yen rural district on the basis of a part of the land area and population of Hoang Ninh and Quang Minh communes. Nenh township shall have a land area of 560.55 ha and a population of 7,132. Hoang Ninh commune shall be reduced to a land area of 680.83 ha and a population of 8,690, and Quang Minh commune to a land area of 550.30 ha and a population of 9,007.- (Summary)
