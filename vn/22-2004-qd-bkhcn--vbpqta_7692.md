# Quyết định 22/2004/QĐ-BKHCN

DECISION No. 22/2004/QD-BKHCN of August 25, 2004 canceling Vietnamese standards
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, the following two Vietnamese standards are canceled:
- TCVN 6213 - 1996: Bottled natural mineral water;
- TCVN 6096 - 1995: Bottled drinking water.- (Summary)
