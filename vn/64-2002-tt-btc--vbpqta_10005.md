# Thông tư 64/2002/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 64/2002/TT-BTC of July 24, 2002 guiding the management of the proceeds from the sale of illicitly imported cigarettes displayed for sale on domestic market
This Circular guides the management and use of the proceeds from the sale of illicitly imported cigarettes which were confiscated while being displayed for sale on domestic market in service of the inspection and control of illicitly imported cigarettes displayed for sale on domestic market.- (Summary)
