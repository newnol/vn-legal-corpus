# Directive 15/2007/CT-BNN

DIRECTIVE
On intensifying the management of slash-and-burn field cultivation
The Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development requests People’s Committees of provinces where exist forests to perform several tasks in order to enhance the management of slash-and-burn field cultivation and protect forests.- (Summary)
