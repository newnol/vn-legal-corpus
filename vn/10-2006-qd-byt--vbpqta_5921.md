# Decision 10/2006/QĐ-BYT

DECISION
Assigning the medicine price management task and establishing the Medicine Price Management Section under the Vietnam Pharmacy Management Department
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” the Minister of Health assigns the Vietnam Pharmacy Management Department to assist her in performing the function of state management of medicine prices strictly according to the provisions of law.- (Summary)
