# Quyết định 249/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 249/QD-TTg of March 22, 2000 replacing and adding members of the Council of Ho Chi Minh Prize and State Prize on science and technology
To appoint Professor, Academician, Doctor of Science Vu Tuyen Hoang, Chairman of the Association of Vietnam Science and Technology Societies, to replace Professor Ha Hoc Trac as Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ho Chi Minh Prize and State Prize on Science and Technology.
To appoint Professor, Doctor Le Huu Nghia, Deputy Director of the Ho Chi Minh National Politics Institute, to replace Professor To Huy Rua as member of the Council.
To add Professor Pham Song, former Minister of Health and Professor, Doctor Ngo The Dan, Vice-Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development, as members of the Council.- (Summary)
