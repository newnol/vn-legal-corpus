# Thông tư 63/2000/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 63/2000/TT-BTC of June 29, 2000 guiding the funding allocation for implementation of the Prime Minister�s Decision No. 20/2000/QD-TTg of February 3, 2000 on support for people who had joined revolutionary activities before the August 1945 Revolution to improve their housing conditions
The Ministry of Finance guides the balance and arrangement of funding source for implementation of Decision No. 20/2000/QD-TTg as well as the modes of allocation and settlement of this funding source as follows:
Funding source shall be included in the 2000, 2001 and 2002 State budget. The provincial/municipal People�s Committees shall use revenues from land use right transfer, land rentals and revenues from sale of State-owned houses to spend on the supports. In the immediate future, in order to spend in time, the Finance Ministry may, at the proposal of the provincial People�s Committees, make advances for the provinces facing with great difficulties which do not have enough housing and land revenues.
For fixed-level supports (VND 20 and 50 million), basing themselves on the provincial People�s Committees� decisions, the provincial/municipal Finance-Pricing Services shall make spending order through the State Treasury in order to pay in lumpsum in cash or through account transfer as requested by the beneficiaries.- (Summary)
