# Circular 24/TC-KBNN

THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE
Circular No.24-TC/KBNN of May 13, 1997 guiding the regime of concentration and management of the State budget revenues via the State Treasury.
This Circular guides the following contents: drafting the revenue estimates; organizing the collection; the tasks and powers of the collecting agency, the State Treasury and the financial agency in organizing the collection of the State budget; the operation fund and the reward and sanction regime.-
