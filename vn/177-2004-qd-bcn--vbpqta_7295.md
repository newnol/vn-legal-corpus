# Quyết định 177/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 177/2004/QD-BCN of December 30, 2004 transforming Hanoi Textile-Garment Company, an independent cost-accounting member company of Vietnam Textile-Garment Corporation, into the one experimentally organized and operating after the parent company-subsidiary company model
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, the parent company (Hanoi Textile-Garment Company - HANOSIMEX) is headquartered at No. 1 Mai Dong street, Hoang Mai district, Hanoi, and has a charter capital of VND 155,188,892,395 by December 31, 2003.- (Summary)
