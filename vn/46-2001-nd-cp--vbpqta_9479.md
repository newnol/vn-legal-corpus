# Nghị định 46/2001/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 46/2001/ND-CP of August 10, 2001 on the adjustment of administrative boundaries of Hoa Thanh district for the expansion of Tay Ninh provincial capital, the establishment of wards therein and Thanh Bac commune in Tan Bien district, Tay Ninh province
To adjust administrative boundaries for the expansion of Tay Ninh provincial capital on the basis of merging into Tay Ninh provincial capital the whole land area and population of Tan Binh, Ninh Son, Ninh Thanh, Thanh Tan and Hiep Ninh communes, and a land area of 139 ha with a population of 7,815 of Hiep Tan commune of Hoa Thanh district.
To establish ward IV of Tay Ninh provincial capital on the basis of a land area of 139 ha and a population of 7,815 of Hiep Tan commune; a land area of 49 ha and a population of 3,408 of Hiep Ninh commune. Ward IV shall have a land area of 188 ha and a population of 11,223.
To establish Hiep Ninh ward of Tay Ninh provincial capital on the basis of the remaining land area of 331 ha and population of 17,728 of Hiep Ninh commune.
After the adjustment of administrative boundaries, Tay Ninh provincial capital shall have a land area of 13,965 ha and a population of 112,393; Hoa Thanh district shall have a land area of 8,316 ha and a population of 129,040; Hiep Tan commune of Hoa Thanh district shall have a land area of 485 ha and a population of 8,635.
To establish Thanh Bac commune of Tan Bien district on the basis of a land area of 9,164 ha and a population of 2,960 of Thanh Binh commune. After the adjustment of administrative boundaries, Thanh Binh shall have a land area of 11,500 ha and a population of 8,404.- (Summary)
