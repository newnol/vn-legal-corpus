# Quyết định 166/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 166/2004/QD-TTg of September 21, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on approval of national measurement standards
The Regulation prescribes the contents of, as well as order and procedures for, approval of national measurement standards.
The Minister of Science and Technology shall have to organize the assessment and evaluation of, and submit to the Prime Minister for approval, national measurement standards in each measurement domain.
The General Department of Standardization, Metrology and Quality Control shall formulate, maintain, preserve and exploit national measurement standards.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
