# Quyết định 49/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 49/2004/QD-BCN of June 15, 2004 amending Clause 1 and Clause 3, Article 1 of the Industry Minister's Decision No. 225/2003/QD-BCN of December 19, 2003 on transformation of Binh Tay Steel Wire Netting Factory under the Southern Steel Company into Binh Tay Wire Netting Joint-Stock Company
To amend Clause 1 and Clause 3, Article 1 of Decision No. 225/2003/QD-BCN of December 3, 2003 on transformation of Binh Tay Steel Wire Netting Factory under the Southern Steel Company into Binh Tay Wire Netting Joint-Stock Company regarding the charter capital structure (the State's share percentage: 51%; shares to be sold to the company's laborers: 12.07%; and shares to be sold to outsiders: 36.93%) and the preferences for the company's laborers (preferential shares to be sold to the company's laborers is 14,027 valued at VND 420,810,000).
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
