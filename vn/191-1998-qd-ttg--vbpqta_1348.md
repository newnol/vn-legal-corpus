# Quyết định 191/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 191/1998/QD-TTg of September 29, 1998 on assigning the task of post-graduate training to the Agriculture and Forestry College attached to the Thai Nguyen University
To assign the task of post-graduate training to the Agriculture and Forestry College attached to the Thai Nguyen University. The Ministry of Education and Training shall coordinate with the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment in defining the post-graduate training specialties of this college.-
