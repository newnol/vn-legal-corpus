# Quyết định 42/2005/QĐ-BNN

DECISION
Defining the functions, tasks and organizational structure of the Institute of Agricultural Economics
Under this Decision, the Institute of Agricultural Economics is a non-business scientific unit attached to the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development and tasked to conduct scientific research and provide information in service of the formulation of strategies and policies on agricultural and rural development.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
