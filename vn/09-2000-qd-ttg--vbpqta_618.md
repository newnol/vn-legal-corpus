# Decision 09/2000/QD-TTg

THE PRIME MINISTER
DECISION No.09/2000/QD-TTg of January 17, 2000 on transforming Thu Duc Trading and Import-Export Company into a joint stock company
To transform Thu Duc Trading and Import-Export Company being a State enterprise into Thu Duc Trading and Import-Export Joint Stock Company (TIMEXCO).
- Head office: 191 Quang Trung street, Hiep Phu ward, district 9, Ho Chi Minh City.
- Charter capital: 14,000,000,000 dong, of which 40% shall be held by the State and the remaining 60% shall be sold in shares to laborers in the company and other subjects outside the company.
- Production and business lines and trades: Producing and trading in export and import goods being processed forest products, farm produce, aquatic and marine products, foodstuffs, packages, fine-art handicrafts, metal, electric mechanical items and machinery, supplies, raw materials, fuels, spare parts, construction materials, transport means; and providing export, import and trading services as well as warehouse and storing yard services.- (Summary)
