# Thông tư 63/2004/TT-BNN

CIRCULAR No. 63/2004/TT-BNN of November 11, 2004 guiding a number of contents of the Government's Decree No. 139/2004/ND-CP of June 25, 2004 on sanctions against administrative violations in the domain of forest management and protection and forest-product management
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
Administrative violations committed before July 21, 2004 (the effective date of Decree No. 139/2004/ND-CP), for which handling decisions have not yet been issued, shall be handled according to the Government's Decree No. 77/CP of November 29, 1996 and Decree No. 17/2002/ND-CP of February 8, 2002.
Administrative violations not defined in Decree No. 139/2004/ND-CP or bearing the legal responsibility lower than that in Decrees No. 77/CP and No. 17/2002/ND-CP shall be handled according to Decree No. 139/2004/ND-CP.- (Summary)
