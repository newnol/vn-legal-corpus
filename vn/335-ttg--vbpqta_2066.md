# Decision 335/TTg

DECISION No.335-TTg OF MAY 23, 1996 OF THE PRIME MINISTER ON THE ISSUANCE OF THE REGULATION ON THE ORGANIZATION AND OPERATION OF THE NATIONAL CENTER FOR SOCIAL SCIENCE AND HUMANITIES
This Regulation includes detailed provisions on the tasks and powers of the Director and Deputy Directors of the Center; The Scientific Council of the Center; The scientific research institutes and units supporting the scientific research; Employees and officials of the Center; Its finance and assets.- (Summary)
