# Decision 34/2000/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 34/2000/QD-TTg of March 10, 2000 on the conversion of Housing Trading and Development Company of Tan Binh district into a joint stock company
To convert the State-run "Housing Trading and Development Company of Tan Binh district" under Sai Gon Real Estate Corporation into Tan Binh Real Estate Joint Stock Company (TAN BINH RES).
- Head office: 205-207-209 Ly Thuong Kiet, ward 6, Tan Binh district, Ho Chi Minh City.
- Charter capital: Vietnam dong 13,000,000,000; of which the State shares represent 40 percent, shares sold to officials and employees inside the company and subjects outside the company account for 60 percent.
- Business lines:
+ Building, repairing and trading in civil houses of all kinds, urban infrastructure works and residential areas (roads, water supply and drainage system, bridges and sewerage).
+ Producing and trading in construction materials.
+ Ground dredging and filling-up, foundation digging and filling.
+ Constructing regular foundations of all kinds.
+ Building and installing construction structures, installing electric and water equipment for works.
+ Finalizing construction, making interior and exterior decorations, and creating works� scenery architecture.
+ Designing, elaborating projects and making cost estimates for civil works.
+ Surveying, designing and making detailed plannings of residential quarters.
+ Organizing real estate consultancy services activities.
+ Organizing management and providing housing services in urban cities: house repair, management of sport yards, elevator management, gas supplying agents, post office agents, rubbish gathering and baby-sitting services.- (Summary)
