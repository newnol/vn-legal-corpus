# Nghị định 70/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 70/2002/ND-CP of July 18, 2002 adjusting the administrative boundaries to expand Tra Vinh provincial capital and establish wards of Tra Vinh provincial capital, Tra Vinh province
To establish ward 8 of Tra Vinh provincial capital with a land area of 310.83 ha and a population of 7,373.
To establish ward 9 of Tra Vinh provincial capital with a land area of 991.26 ha and a population of 7,458.
To adjust the boundaries of Chau Thanh district for the expansion of Tra Vinh provincial capital.
After the expansion, Tra Vinh provincial capital shall have a land area of 6,515.69 ha and a population of 86,102, including 10 administrative units being 9 wards and 1 commune.
After the adjustment of the administrative boundaries, Chau Thanh district shall have a land area of 43,161.21 ha and a population of 134,587, including 13 administrative units being 1 district township and 12 communes.- (Summary)
