# Nghị định 11/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 11/2004/ND-CP of January 8, 2004 on the establishment of communes, wards and townships of Hon Dat, An Bien and Tan Hiep rural districts and Rach Gia provincial capital, Kien Giang province
To establish An Binh ward of Rach Gia provincial capital on the basis of a land area of 479.75 ha and a population of 13,794 of An Hoa ward.
To establish Soc Son township of Hon Dat rural district on the basis of a land area of 2,206.88 ha and a population of 15,082 of Soc Son commune.
To establish Thanh Yen A commune of An Bien rural district on the basis of a land area of 2,388.90 ha and a population of 7,359 of Thanh Yen commune.
To establish Tan An commune of Tan Hiep rural district on the basis of a land area of 3,415.50 ha and a population of 8,234 of Tan Hiep A commune.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
