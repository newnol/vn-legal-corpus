# Decision 40/2006/QĐ-BNN

DECISION
Adjusting and supplementing Decision No. 74/2004/QD-BNN of December 16, 2004, promulgating the list of plant varieties permitted for production and trading
This Decision promulgates the supplemented list of plant varieties permitted for production and trading, annulling Article 2 of Decision No. 74/2004/QD-BNN of December 16, 2004.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
