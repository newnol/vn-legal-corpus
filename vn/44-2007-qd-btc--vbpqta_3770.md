# Decision 44/2007/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
Ceasing the refund of special consumption tax on exported homemade cigarettes bearing foreign trademarks
From July 1, 2007, to cease the refund of special consumption tax on exported homemade cigarettes bearing foreign trademarks under the guidance in Circular No. 28/2006/TT-BTC of April 4, 2006.
For cigarettes exported before July 1, 2007, the special consumption tax refund may be considered and settled in accordance with this Circular.
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
