# Decision 666/TTg

THE PRIME MINISTER
Decision No.666-TTg of August 21, 1997 on the list of members of the Ho Chi Minh and State Science and Technology Awards Council.
The Council shall be headed by Associate Doctor Pham Gia Khiem, Minister of Science, Technology and Environment and composed of three Vice Chairmen and 13 members who are all professors.- (Summary)
