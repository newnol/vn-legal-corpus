# Nghị định 117/1997/NĐ-CP

DECREE No.117/1997/ND-CP of December 22, 1997 on the establishment of Muong Thanh township of Dien Bien district, Lai Chau province.
To establish Muong Thanh township in Dien Bien district on the basis of a natural land area of 356.25 ha and a population of 5,276 of Thanh Xuong commune.- (Summary)
