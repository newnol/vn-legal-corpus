# Chỉ thị 03/2004/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE No. 03/2004/CT-TTg of January 15, 2004 on a number of urgent measures to prevent and combat drought and protect the 2004 Winter-Spring and Summer-Autumn crops
Under this Directive, the Prime Minister instructs the concerned ministries and branches as well as the provincial/municipal People’s Committees to immediately take some measures to prevent and combat drought and overcome its consequences, ensuring the sufficient supply of daily-life water for people and in service of agricultural production and aquaculture.- (Summary)
