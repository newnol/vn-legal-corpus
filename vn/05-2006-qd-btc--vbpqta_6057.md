# Quyết định 05/2006/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
Defining functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Finance Ministry’s Inspectorate
Under this Decision, the Finance Ministry’s Inspectorate is an agency belonging to the system of financial inspectorate and a unit under the Finance Ministry which functions to conduct administrative inspection and specialized inspection within the scope of state management by the Finance Ministry.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
