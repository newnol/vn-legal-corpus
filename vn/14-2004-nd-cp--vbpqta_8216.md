# Nghị định 14/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 14/2004/ND-CP of January 9, 2004 on establishing Thinh Dan ward and renaming Thinh Dan commune of Thai Nguyen city, Thai Nguyen province, Quyet Thang commune
To establish Thinh Dan ward on the basis of a land area of 466.95 ha and a population of 7,866 of Tan Thinh ward; and a land area of 128.96 ha of Thinh Duc commune and 20.27 ha of Thinh Dan commune.
Thinh Dan ward shall have a land area of 616.18 ha and a population of 7,866.
To rename Thinh Dan commune Quyet Thang commune.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
