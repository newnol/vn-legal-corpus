# Decision 138/2002/QD-TTg1

DECISION No. 138/2002/QD-TTg of October 15, 2002 establishing the Odonto-Stomatology University
To establish the Odonto-Stomatology University on the basis of the Odonto-Stomatology Faculty of Hanoi Medical University and Hanoi Odonto-Stomatology Institute.
The Odonto-Stomatology University is attached to the Ministry of Health, has the legal person status, its own seal and accounts.
Its office is headquartered in Hanoi city.
It is tasked to:
- Train officials of university degree in the odonto-stomatology field;
- Conduct scientific research and provide social services in the odonto-stomatology and field.- (Summary)
