# Thông tư 24/2003/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 24/2003/TT-BTC of March 27, 2003 amending a number of points of Circular No. 43/2002/TT-BTC of May 7, 2002 which guides the management and allocation of the State budget capital for the project on planting 5 million hectares of forests
This Circular amends the fourth em rule, Point 1, Section I, Part II - Investment capital for the project on planting protection and special-use forests, of Circular No. 43/2002/TT-BTC.- (Summary)
