# Nghị định 98/2001/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 98/2001/ND-CP of December 24, 2001 splitting Gia Rai rural district of Bac Lieu province into Dong Hai and Gia Rai rural districts
Dong Hai rural district shall have a natural land area of 52,786.36 hectares and a population of 123,440, encompassing the communes of An Trach, An Phuc, Dinh Thanh, Long Dien, Long Dien Tay, Long Dien Dong and Long Dien Dong A, and the district capital of Ganh Hao.
Gia Rai rural district shall have a natural land area of 33,760.06 hectares and a population of 118,899, encompassing the communes of Phong Thanh, Phong Thanh Dong, Phong Thanh Dong A, Phong Thanh Tay, Tan Thanh, Tan Phong and Phong Tan, and the district towns of Ho Phong and Gia Rai.- (Summary)
