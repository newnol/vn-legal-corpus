# Quyết định 25/2002/QĐ-BKHCNMT

DECISION No. 25/2002/QD-BKHCNMT of May 6, 2002 promulgating the Regulation on the organization and operation of NEAD Technology Application and Development Company
Issued together with this Decision is the Regulation on the organization and operation of NEAD Technology Application and Development Company.
NEAD Technology Application and Development Company is a State-run enterprise under Vietnam Atomic Energy Institute, the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment.
Its production, business and service activities cover:
1. The provision of services on:
- Technological transfer consultancy and investment in the fields of nuclear energy and radiation technology;
- Science and technology in the fields of nuclear energy, radiation technology, radiation safety and control, environmental technology, informatics, industrial electricity, and civil electricity;
- Construction and installation of scientific, technical, civil, air conditioning, cooling technique and radiation works.
2. Import and export of:
- Scientific and technical supplies, machinery and equipment in the fields of nuclear energy, radiation safety, bio-technology, health, informatics equipment, telecommunications, civil and industrial electric equipment, transport means, project construction equipment, cooling and air-conditioning equipment, chemicals and chemical equipment in service of test and production, supplies and office equipment, consumer goods and materials, and means of production;
- Experts and technicians;
- Dealing in scientific and technical books and journals written on paper and CD-ROM.
3. Agency for purchasing, selling, consigning and dealing in industrial and civil supplies and goods.- (Summary)
