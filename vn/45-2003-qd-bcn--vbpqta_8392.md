# Quyết định 45/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 45/2003/QD-BCN of April 7, 2003 merging Viet Bac Gemstones and Gold Company into Cao Bang Iron Company under Vietnam Minerals Corporation

The Managing Board of Vietnam Minerals Corporation shall organize the merger strictly according to the State's current regulations.- (Summary)
