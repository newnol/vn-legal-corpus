# Thông tư liên tịch 49/2002/TTLT/BTC-BLĐTBXH

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 49/2002/TTLT-BTC-BLDTBXH of May 28, 2002 guiding the implementation of fundings for work related to martyrs' tombs
The Circular guides the spending levels and sources of fundings for the survey and seeking of martyrs' tombs; the gathering of tombs and building of martyr tombs' crusts; the making of lists of martyrs’ tombs; and the support for martyrs' relatives during their visits to martyrs' graves.- (Summary)
