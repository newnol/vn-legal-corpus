# Quyết định 65/2002/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 65/2002/QD-BTC of May 22, 2002 annulling the provisions on percentage of price difference collection for imported clinker
To annul the provisions on percentage of price difference collection for clinker under Heading No. 2523.10.00 in the Finance Minister�s Decision No. 71/2001/QD-BTC of July 27, 2001 stipulating the percentage of price difference collection for imported clinker.
This Decision takes implementation effect and applies to import goods declarations already submitted to the customs offices 15 days after its signing.- (Summary)
