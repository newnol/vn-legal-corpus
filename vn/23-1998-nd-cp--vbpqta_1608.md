# Nghị định 23/1998/NĐ-CP

DECREE No.23/1998/ND-CP of April 24, 1998 on the establishment of Tien Phong commune of Yen Hung district, Quang Ninh province.
To establish Tien Phong commune on the basis of a land area of 1,117.77 hectares and a population of 1,364 of Lien Vi commune and a land area of 523.23 hectares of Lien Hoa commune.- (Summary)
Resolution No.5/1998/NQ-CP of April 28, 1998 on its April 1998 regular meeting.
On April 26, 1998, the Government held its monthly regular meeting to discuss and adopt the draft decrees detailing the implementation respectively of the Ordinance on Thrift Practice and Wastefulness Combat; and of a number of the Ordinance against Corruption; the draft decree promulgating the Regulation on the promotion of democracy in agencies; and the draft decree on securities and stock market. It assigned the drafting bodies to finalize such draft decrees then submit them to the Prime Minister for signing for promulgation. The Government also adopted the draft report on renaming the Ministry of the Interior as the Ministry of Police which had been submitted to the third session of the Xth National Assembly. - (Summary)
