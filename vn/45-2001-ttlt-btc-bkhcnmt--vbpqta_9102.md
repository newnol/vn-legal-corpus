# Joint circular 45/2001/TTLT/BTC-BKHCNMT

Joint-Circular No. 45/2001/TTLT-BTC-BKHCNMT of June 18, 2001 guiding a number of spending regimes for scientific and technological tasks
This Circular prescribes a number of spending regimes for scientific and technological tasks (subjects and projects) of State-, ministerial-, or provincial/municipal level, which are financed by the State budget.- (Summary)
