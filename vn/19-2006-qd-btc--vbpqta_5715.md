# Decision 19/2006/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
Promulgating administrative and non-business accounting regime
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces the administrative and non-business accounting regime promulgated together with Decision No. 999-TC/QD/CDKT of November 2, 1996, and circulars amending and supplementing this Decision.- (Summary)
