# Decision 48/2008/QD-BNN 

DECISION
Defining the functions, tasks and organizational structure of the Informatics and Statistics Center
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces Decision No. 68/2005/QD-BNN of November 2, 2005, on the functions, tasks and organizational structure of the Informatics and Statistics Center under the former Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development, and Decision No. 17/2003/QD-BTS of August 5, 2003, on the functions, tasks and organizational structure of the Informatics Center under the Ministry of Fisheries.- (Summary)
