# Thông tư liên tịch 01/2005/TTLT/BQP-BTC

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 01/2005/TTLT/BQP-BTC of January 4, 2005 guiding the implementation of regimes and policies toward organizations and individuals mobilized for national defense under the Government’s Decree No. 19/2002/ND-CP of February 19, 2002 on mobilizing the State’s scientific and technological potentials in service of national defense
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
