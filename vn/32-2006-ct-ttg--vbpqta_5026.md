# Directive 32/2006/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On a number of urgent measures to rectify administrative order and discipline in the settlement of people’s and enterprises’ affairs
The Prime Minister sets several tasks to be performed by ministers, heads of ministerial-level agencies and government-attached agencies and presidents of provincial/municipal People’s Committees in order to rectify administrative order and discipline in the settlement of people’s and enterprises’ affairs.- (Summary)
