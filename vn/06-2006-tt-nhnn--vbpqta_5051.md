# Circular 06/2006/TT-NHNN

CIRCULAR
Guiding the implementation of Clause 3, Article 6 of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 270/2005/QD-TTg of October 31, 2005, on mobilization, management and use of postal savings
This Circular shall take effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replace Circular No. 05/1999/TT-NHNN of November 5, 1999, guiding the implementation of Clause 2, Article 5 of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 215/1998/QD-TTg of November 4, 1998.- (Summary)
