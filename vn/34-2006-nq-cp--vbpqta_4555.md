# Nghị quyết 34/2006/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
On the Government’s November 2006 regular meeting
On December 1, 2006, the Government held its November regular meeting where it discussed:
- A draft decree detailing and guiding the implementation of a number of articles of the Ordinance on War Veterans, presented by the Home Affairs Ministry;
- A draft decree on search for and gathering of fallen heroes’ remains, presented by the Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs Ministry;
- A scheme on electricity prices in the 2006-2010 period, presented by the Industry Ministry;
- A report on the audit of final settlement of completed projects under Resolution No. 01/2006/NQ-CP of January 16, 2006, presented by the Finance Ministry;
- A planning on the network of universities and colleges in the 2006-2020 period, presented by the Education and Training Ministry;
- A report on production, business and investment results in November and socio-economic situation in the first 11 months of 2006, presented by the Investment and Planning Ministry; and a report on trade activities in November, presented by the Trade Ministry.- (Summary)
