# Decision 281/2005/QD-BNG

DECISION No. 281/2005/QD-BNG of February 2, 2005 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Institute for International Relations
The Institute for International Relations (IIR) is a non-business organization under the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, operating with the State budget in the domains of the Ministry’s tasks of State management over university and post-graduate training of foreign-relation officials, scientific research into international relations, and management of the Ministry’s scientific research programs.
