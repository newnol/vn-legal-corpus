# Joint circular 395/TTLT/KCM-TCCBCP

Joint circular No. 395/TTLT/KCM-TCCBCP of April 12, 1997 guiding the operational mechanism and relationships of scientific and technological institutes attached to the State corporations.
Subjects governed by this Circular include State scientific and technological research and development institutes attached to the State corporations as defined in Item 2, Article 1 of Decision No.782-TTg of October 24, 1996 of the Prime Minister and those institutes which are placed under the corporations by decision of the Prime Minister (if any).
The Circular provides detailed guidances on the functions, tasks and powers of an institute; its organization and personnel; financial mechanism; issues regarding properties; labor and wage; the elaboration and implementation of plans; the re-organization and dissolution and its relationships.- (Summary)
