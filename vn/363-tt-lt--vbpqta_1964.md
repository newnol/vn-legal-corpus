# Joint circular 363/TT-LT

INTER-MINISTERIAL CIRCULAR No.363-TT/LT of August 7, 1997 of the Ministry of Finance, the Ministry of Health and the Government Commission on Organization and Personnel guiding the implementation of Decision No.924-TTg of December 13, 1996 of the Prime Minister supplementing the regime of special allowances for special occupations applicable to medical personnel.
The Circular provides guidances on the objects and levels of allowance, sources of allowance and the forms of payment thereof applicable to medical personnel performing specialized jobs related to: surgery, pathological anatomy, forensic medicine, psychiatry, leprosy and tuberculosis and some others.- (Summary)
