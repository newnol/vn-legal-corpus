# Nghị định 33/1999/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 33/1999/ND-CP of May 11, 1999 on the establishment of wards and communes under Bac Giang provincial capital and Yen The district, Bac Giang province
To establish Tho Xuong ward under Bac Giang provincial capital on the basis of a land area of 439 hectares and a population of 12,309 of Tho Xuong district.
To establish Xuong Giang commune under Bac Giang provincial capital on the basis of the remaining land area of 250 hectares and population of 6,218 of Tho Xuong district.
To establish Hoang Van Thu ward under Bac Giang provincial capital on the basis of a land area of 52 hectares and a population of 6,469 of Le Loi ward; and a land area of 115 hectares and a population of 2,697 of Dinh Ke commune.
To establish Dong Tien commune of Yen The district on the basis of a land area of 3,700 hectares and a population of 3,116 of Dong Vuong commune.- (Summary)
