# Quyết định 83/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION 83/2001/QD-TTg of May 30, 2001 on the establishment of the Religious Publishing House
To establish Religious Publishing House under the Government Religious Committee, which shall perform the function of publishing assorted prayer-books, religious works, teaching materials and purely religious cultural products of religious organizations, which are permitted to operate by the State, as well as other books serving the propagation and education of religion legislation, guidelines and policies of the Party and the State.- (Summary)
