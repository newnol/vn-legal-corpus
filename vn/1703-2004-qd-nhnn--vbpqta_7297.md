# Quyết định 1703/2004/QĐ-NHNN

DECISION No. 1703/2004/QD-NHNN of December 28, 2004 amending and supplementing the State Bank Governor’s Circular No. 10/2003/TT-NHNN of September 16, 2003 which guides the implementation of the Government’s Decree No. 174/1999/ND-CP of December 9, 1999 on management of gold trading activities and Decree No. 64/2003/ND-CP of June 11, 2003 on amendment and supplementation of Decree No. 174/1999/ND-CP
This Decision amends and supplements Section 1, Chapter II and Section 1, Chapter V of, and annuls Appendices 4, 5 and 8 to, Circular No. 10/2003/TT-NHNN.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
