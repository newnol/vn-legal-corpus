# Decision 111/2000/QD-BNN-TCCB

Decision No. 111/2000/QD-BNN-TCCB of November 3, 2000 on the conversion of the Rubber Professional Technical School into the Rubber Professional Technical Intermediate School
To convert the Rubber Professional Technical School into the Rubber Professional Technical Intermediate School under the Vietnam Rubber Corporation. The Rubber Professional Technical Intermediate School shall be a training public-service unit, have the legal person status and its own seal.
The school is headquartered at Dong Xoai town, Binh Phuoc province.
The school shall have the following principal tasks:
- Training and fostering technical and economic cadres of intermediate level.
- Training, fostering technical workers.
- Participating in scientific research and application of scientific and technical advances relating to the school�s training contents as well as transfer of technical advances in service of the production.
- Cooperating with domestic and foreign training, research, production and business establishments in order to combine training with production.
- Managing the organization, personnel and assets of the school as assigned by the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development and according to the State regulations.-
