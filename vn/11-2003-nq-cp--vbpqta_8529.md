# Nghị quyết 11/2003/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION No. 11/2003/NQ-CP of September 5, 2003 on the Government's August 2003 regular meeting
The Government, in its August 2003 regular meeting, examined the following:
- A report on the 2004 socio-economic development plan and socio-economic situation in August and the first eight months of 2003; a report evaluating the implementation of the 2003 State budget task and the 2004 State budget estimates.
- The scheme on the State management decentralization between the central and local administrations and the Government's draft resolution on the accelerated decentralization of State management between the central and local administrations.
- A bill amending and supplementing a number of articles of the Law on Credit Institutions, the draft Veterinary Ordinance (amended); and the Government's 2004 law- and ordinance-making program.- (Summary)
