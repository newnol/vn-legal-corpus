# Directive 01/2007/CT-BCT 

DIRECTIVE
On tasks in service of the “Year of the Rat” Tet holiday - 2008
The Minister of Industry and Trade requests directors of provincial/municipal Industry or Trade/Trade-Tourism Services, corporations and companies under its management to perform several tasks in order to supply sufficient commodities for people on the occasion of the “Year of the Rat” Tet holiday.- (Summary)
