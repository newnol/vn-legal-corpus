# Quyết định 138/2001/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 138/2001/QD-TTg of September 19, 2001 on the establishment of the State Council for Professor Title
The State Council for Professor Title is composed of 26 professors with Mr. Pham Minh Hac as its chairman.
The State Council for Professor Title is tasked to:
- Assist the Prime Minister in directing and effecting the consideration, recognition and deprivation of professor and associate professor titles;
- Propose regimes and policies towards people recognized as professors or associate professors and those appointed to professor or associate professor ranks.- (Summary)
