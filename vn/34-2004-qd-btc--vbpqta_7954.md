# Quyết định 34/2004/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 34/2004/QD-BTC of April 14, 2004 promulgating regulations on preservation of national reserve goods
Promulgated together with this Decision are the regulations on preservation of national reserve goods, including:
- The regulations on preservation of national reserve paddy;
- The regulations on preservation of national reserve rice;
- The regulations on preservation of national reserve automobiles and motorcycles; and
- The regulations on preservation of national reserve metals.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces the National Reserve Director's Decision No. 03/2000/QD-CDTQG of January 13, 2000 promulgating the regulations on preservation of national reserve goods. Other regulations contrary to this Decision are annulled.- (Summary)
