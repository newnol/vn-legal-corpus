# Quyết định 212/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 212/2004/QD-TTg of December 16, 2004 approving the national action program of law dissemination and education and raising of the sense of law observance for officials and people in communes, wards and townships in the 2005-2010 period
The action program, comprising 4 schemes, aims to create substantial changes in the awareness and sense of respect for and observance of law for officials and people in communes, wards and townships, thus contributing to precluding and limiting law violations, maintaining security and social order and safety.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
