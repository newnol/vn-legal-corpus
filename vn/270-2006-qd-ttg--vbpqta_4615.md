# Quyết định 270/2006/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Establishing Da Nang Architectural University
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” Da Nang Architectural University operates under the Regulation on organization and operation of private universities promulgated together with the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 14/2005/QD-TTg of January 17, 2005.- (Summary)
