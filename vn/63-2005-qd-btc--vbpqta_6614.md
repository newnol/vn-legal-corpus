# Quyết định 63/2005/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
Promulgating the accounting regime applicable to securities investment funds
This accounting regime, comprising the system of accounting documents; the system of accounting accounts; the system of accounting books and applicable accounting forms; and the system of financial statements, applies to securities investment funds nationwide and replaces the one issued together with Official Letter No. 2913/TC/CDKT of November 8, 2004.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
