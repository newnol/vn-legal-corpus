# Quyết định 44/2004/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 44/2004/QD-BTC of April 29, 2004 on interest rate on State development investment credit loans
The interest rate on State development investment credit loans of 6.6%/year shall apply to projects, for which the credit contracts have been signed for the first time, as from the effective date of this Decision and be kept intact during the capital-borrowing term.
For projects for which the contracts had been signed with the Development Assistance Fund before the effective date of this Decision, the provisions inscribed in the signed credit contracts shall apply.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
