# Thông tư 07/2004/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 07/2004/TT-BTC of February 9, 2004 guiding the deduction for setting up, management, use and accounting of the reserve fund for job-loss allowance at credit institutions
Under this Circular, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, credit institutions shall make deductions for setting up, manage, use and account the reserve fund for job-loss allowance under the guidance of the Finance Ministry’s Circular No. 82/2003/TT-BTC of August 14, 2003.
The regulations in this Circular replace those in the Finance Ministry’s Circulars No. 92/2000/TT-BTC of September 14, 2000; No. 98/2000/TT-BTC of October 12, 2000 and No. 97/2000/TT-BTC of October 12, 2000.- (Summary)
