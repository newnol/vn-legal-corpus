# Thông tư 01/2003/TT-BYT

CIRCULAR No. 01/2003/TT-BYT of March 12, 2003 guiding the supplements to Point a, Section 1, Part II of Circular No. 22/2001/TT-BYT of November 15, 2001 on the spending levels for a standard blood unit
According to regulations, a standard blood unit means a volume of 250, 350 or 450 ml of total blood packed in plastic bags containing antithrombin CPD-A1 and having undergone all necessary screening tests in order to ensure blood transfusion safety.
The spending levels for a standard blood unit shall be as follows: VND 260,000 for a blood unit of 250 ml; VND 364,000 for a blood unit of 350 ml and VND 468,000 for a blood unit of 450 ml.
The allowances for blood donors shall be as follows: VND 150,000 for a blood unit of 250 ml; VND 210,000 for a blood unit of 350 ml and VND 270,000 for a blood unit of 450 ml.- (Summary)
