# Quyết định 07/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 07/2004/QD-BCN of January 12, 2004 transforming Hai Duong Pump Manufacturing Company into Hai Duong Pump Manufacturing Joint-Stock Company
To transform Hai Duong Pump Manufacturing Company, an independent cost-accounting member enterprise of the Industrial Machinery and Equipment Corporation, into Hai Duong Pump Manufacturing Joint-Stock Company (HPMC).
Its head-office: No. 37, Ho Chi Minh road, Hai Duong city, Hai Duong province.
Its charter capital is VND 17,143,300,000, with 51% of which being held by the State and 49% sold to its laborers.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
