# Directive 03/2008/CT-BYT 

DIRECTIVE
Enhancing epidemics prevention and control during winter-spring seasons
The Minister of Health instructs directors of provincial-level Health Services, the Department for Preventive Medicine and Environment, the Department for Management of Medical Examination and Treatment, the Food Safety and Hygiene Department, and concerned departments and units to perform several tasks in order to take the initiative in preventing and controlling epidemics during winter-spring seasons.- (Summary)
