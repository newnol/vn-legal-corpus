# Thông tư 109/1998/TT-TCCP

THE GOVERNMENT COMMISSION FOR ORGANIZATION AND PERSONNEL
CIRCULAR No.109/1998/TT-TCCP of May 28, 1998 on the amendments and supplements to Circular No.28/TCCP-DP of March 17, 1995 guiding the management and use of dossiers, maps and landmarks of administrative boundaries at all levels
This Circular guides the amendments to Sections II-3 and II-4 of Circular No.28/TCCP-DP on the preservation and keeping of dossiers and maps of administrative boundaries at all levels.- (Summary)
