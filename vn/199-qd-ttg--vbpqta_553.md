# Quyết định 199/QĐ-TTg

THE PRIME MINISTER
DECISION No. 199/QD-TTg of March 7, 2000 on the establishment of the Steering Committee for elaboration of the Project on renewal of scientific and technological management mechanism
To establish the Steering Committee for elaboration of the Project on renewal of scientific and technological management mechanism with the Minister of Science, Technology and Environment as its Head and 10 representatives from the concerned ministries and branches as its members.
The Steering Committee for elaboration of the Project on renewal of scientific and technological management mechanism is tasked:
- To comprehensively review and evaluate the work of scientific and technological management in the past, draw lessons therefrom to serve as experiences for the work of scientific and technological management;
- On the basis of combining the domestic experiences from scientific and technological management with the study and consultation of international experiences, to elaborate the Project on renewal of scientific and technological management mechanism, and draft relevant legal documents to submit to the Prime Minister before June 1 st , 2000.- (Summary)
