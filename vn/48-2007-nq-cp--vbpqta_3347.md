# Nghị quyết 48/2007/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
Adjusting a number of contents in policies on the sale of state-owned houses to their current tenants under the Government’s Decree No. 61/CP of July 5, 1994, and prices of leasing state-owned houses which are not yet renovated or rebuilt
The Government agrees to adjust a number of contents in policies on the sale of state-owned houses in order to speed up and finish soon the sale of state-owned houses to their current tenants under Decree No. 61/CP.- (Summary)
