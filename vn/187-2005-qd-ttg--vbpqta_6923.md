# Decision 187/2005/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Approving the master plan on control of cross-border drug traffic till 2010
THE PRIME MINISTER
Pursuant to the December 25, 2001 Law on Organization of the Government;
Pursuant to the December 9, 2000 Law on Drug Prevention and Fight;
Pursuant to the Government's Resolution No. 01/2004/NQ-CP of January 12, 20004;
At the proposal of the Minister of Public Security, the Minister of Defense and the Minister of Finance,
DECIDES:
Article 1.- To approve the master plan on control of cross-border drug traffic till 2010, enclosed with this Decision.
Article 2.- This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."
Article 3.- Ministers, heads of ministerial-level agencies, heads of Government-attached agencies, and presidents of provincial/municipal People's Committees shall have to implement this Decision.
