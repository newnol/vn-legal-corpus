# Nghị quyết 14/2004/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION No. 14/2004/NQ-CP of December 9, 2004 on the Government’s November 2004 regular meeting
On December 6, 2004, the Government held its November regular meeting, where:
- It heard the national energy policy scheme, presented by the Industry Minister;
- It considered the draft decree on the procedures for application of measures to coerce execution of administrative sanctioning decisions, presented by the Ministry of Public Security; and the Minister-Director of the Government Office synthesizing the cabinet members’ opinions on the draft decree;
- It heard the Planning and Investment Ministry’s report on the socio-economic situation in November and the first 11 months of 2004; and considered the Trade Ministry’s report on the trade situation.- (Summary)
