# Quyết định 49/2002/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 49/2002/QD-BNN of June 11, 2002 amending and supplementing a number of points of Decision No. 16/2002/QD-BNN of March 12, 2002 of the Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development promulgating the list of plant protection drugs permitted for, or restricted from, use in Vietnam
This Decision amends and supplements the following contents of Decision No. 16/2002/QD-BNN:
- A number of control subjects;
- Names and constituents of active substances of some kinds of drugs;
- Contents of active substances and forms of some kinds of drugs;
- Trade names;
- The purity of active substances;
- Names of units registering plant protection drugs.- (Summary)
