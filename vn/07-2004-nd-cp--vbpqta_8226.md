# Nghị định 07/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 07/2004/ND-CP of January 2, 2004 establishing wards of Dong Hoi provincial capital and renaming communes of Le Thuy rural district, Quang Binh province
To establish Duc Ninh Dong ward of Dong Hoi provincial capital on the basis of a land area of 313.69 ha and a population of 5,037 of Duc Ninh commune.
To establish Bac Nghia ward of Dong Hoi provincial capital on the basis of a land area of 776.10 ha and a population of 6,220 of Nghia Hinh commune.
To rename Ngu Thuy commune of Le Thuy rural district Ngu Thuy Nam commune.
To rename Hai Thuy commune of Le Thuy rural district Ngu Thuy Trung commune.
To rename Ngu Hoa commune of Le Thuy rural district Ngu Thuy Bac commune.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
