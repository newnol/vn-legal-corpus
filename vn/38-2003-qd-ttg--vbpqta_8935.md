# Quyết định 38/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 38/2003/QD-TTg of March 18, 2003 prescribing the criteria and norms for the use of home and mobile phones for official duty by officials in Vietnam People’s Army
Home and mobile phones supplied to officials in Vietnam People’s Army for use in public-duty activities are State property.
This Decision prescribes the subjects to be supplied with phones as well as use norms and funds for the supply of above-said phones.- (Summary)
