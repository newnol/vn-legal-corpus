# Decree 37/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 37/2002/ND-CP of April 8, 2002 establishing Suoi Hoa ward of Bac Ninh provincial capital and Gia Binh district township of Gia Binh district, Bac Ninh province
To establish Suoi Hoa ward of Bac Ninh provincial capital with a land area of 118.5 ha and a population of 5,081.
To establish Gia Binh district township of Gia Binh district with a land area of 436.39 ha and a population of 7,672.- (Summary)
