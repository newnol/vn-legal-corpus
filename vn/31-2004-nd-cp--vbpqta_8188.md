# Nghị định 31/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 31/2004/ND-CP of January 19, 2004 on adjusting the pension levels for pensioners who had retired before April 1993
As from January 1, 2004, the pension levels provided for in Decree No. 03/2003/ND-CP of January 15, 2003 and currently enjoyed by pensioners who retired under Decree No. 218/CP of December 27, 1961, Decree No. 161/CP of October 30, 1964 of the Government, and Decree No. 236/HDBT of September 18, 1985 of the Council of Ministers (now the Government) shall be increased by 7% or 9% (for persons who held leading posts before the retirement).
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
