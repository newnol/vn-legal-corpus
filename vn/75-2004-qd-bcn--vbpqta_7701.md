# Quyết định 75/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 75/2004/QD-BCN of August 9, 2004 establishing the Industrial Technical Secondary School under the Ministry of Industry
The Industrial Technical Secondary School (ITSS) is established on the basis of the Electro-Engineering and Chemicals Job-Training School under the Industry Ministry.
ITSS is headquartered at 202 Tran Nguyen Han road, Bac Giang provincial capital, Bac Giang province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
