# Decision 1529/1999/QD-BYT

DECISION No. 1529/1999/QD-BYT of May 25, 1999 promulgating the "Regulation on functions, tasks and organizational structure of the traditional medicine hospitals under the Health Services of the provinces and centrally-run cities
The traditional medicine hospitals under the Health Services of the provinces and centrally-run cities are non-business medical units attached to the provincial/municipal Health Services and the highest local institution for medical examination and treatment by traditional medicine.
The Regulation issued together with this Decision specifies the position, function, tasks, organizational structure, operating funds and working relationship of the traditional medicine hospitals of the provinces and centrally-run cities.- (Summary)
