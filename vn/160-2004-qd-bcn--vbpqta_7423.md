# Quyết định 160/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 160/2004/QD-BCN of December 6, 2004 on transforming Power Construction Company 3.9 into Power Construction Joint-Stock Company 3.9
Under this Decision, Power Construction Joint-Stock Company 3.9, PCC 3.9, has a charter capital of VND 4,100,000,000, of which the State’s equities represent 25%, equities sold to laborers of the company, 63.64% and equities sold to other subjects 11.36%.
Its head-office is located at No. 6 Hung Vuong street, Loc Tho ward, Nha Trang city, Khanh Hoa province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
