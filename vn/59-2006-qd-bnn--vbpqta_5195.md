# Decision 59/2006/QĐ-BNN

DECISION
Adding several plant protection drugs to the list of those permitted for use in Vietnam
This Decision shall take effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”
The import and export of plant protection drugs mentioned in this Decision shall comply with the provisions on goods import and export of the Government’s Decree No. 12/2006/ND-CP of January 23, 2006.- (Summary)
