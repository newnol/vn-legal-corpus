# Directive 127/TTg

THE PRIME MINISTER
Directive No.127-TTg of March 3, 1997 of the Prime Minister on the preparation for the general census and dwelling house survey on April 1st, 1999
The Prime Minister shall direct the works of the General Department of Statistics, the Ministry of Finance, the Ministry of Defense, the Ministry of the Interior, the Ministry for Foreign Affairs in their preparation for such event.- (Summary)
