# Decree 113/2006/NĐ-CP

DECREE
Establishing Tam Ky city of Quang Nam province
Under this Decree, which shall take effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” Tam Ky city is established on the basis of the whole land area and population of Tam Ky provincial town.- (Summary)
