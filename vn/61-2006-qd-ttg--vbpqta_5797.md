# Quyết định 61/2006/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Ratifying the Agreement on Investment Promotion and Protection between the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the Government of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh
With this Decision, the Prime Minister ratifies the Agreement on Investment Promotion and Protection between the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the Government of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh, which was concluded in Hanoi, Vietnam, on May 18, 2005.- (Summary)
