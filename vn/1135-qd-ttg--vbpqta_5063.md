# Decision 1135/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Replacing the head of the Steering Committee for the campaign “All the people unite to build a cultured life”
Under this Decision, which takes effect after its signing, Minister of Culture and Information Le Doan Hop is appointed as the head of the Steering Committee for the campaign “All the people unite to build a cultured life”, in replacement of Mr. Pham Quang Nghi.- (Summary)
