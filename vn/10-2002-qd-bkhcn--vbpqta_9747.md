# Decision 10/2002/QD-BKHCN

DECISION No. 10/2002/QD-BKHCN OF NOVEMBER 22, 2002 PROMULGATING VIETNAMESE STANDARDS
THE MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY
Pursuant to the Government?s Decree No. 22/CP of May 22, 1993 on the tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment (now the Ministry of Science and Technology);
Pursuant to the December 24, 1999 Ordinance on Goods Quality;
At the proposal of the General Director of Standardization, Metrology and Quality Control,
DECIDES:
Article 1.- To promulgate 28 Vietnamese standards as follows:
1. TCVN 7149-1: 2002 (ISO 385-1 : 1984) Laboratory glass device - Burettes - Part 1: General requirements
2. TCVN 7149-2 : 2002 (ISO 385-2 : 1984) Laboratory glass device - Burettes - Part 2: Burettes for which the waiting time is not specified
3. TCVN 7150-1 : 2002 (ISO 835-1 : 1981) Laboratory glass device ? Graduated pipettes - Part 1: General requirements
4. TCVN 7150-2 : 2002 (ISO 835-2 : 1981) Laboratory glass device - Graduated pipettes - Part 2: Pipettes for which the waiting time is not specified
5. TCVN 7150-3 : 2002 (ISO 835-3 : 1981) Laboratory glass device - Graduated pipettes - Part 3: Pipettes for which the waiting time is 15 seconds
6. TCVN 7150-4: 2002 (ISO 835-4 : 1981) Laboratory glass device - Graduated pipettes - Part 4: Of blowing style
7. TCVN 7151 : 2002 (ISO 648 : 1977) Laboratory glass device - One-grade pipettes
8. TCVN 7152 : 2002 (ISO 7712 : 1983) Laboratory glass device ? Pasteur pipettes for single use only
9. TCVN 7153 : 2002 (ISO 1042 : 1998) Laboratory glass device - Quantification jars
10. TCVN 7154 : 2002 (ISO 3819 : 1985) Laboratory glass device - Beakers
11. TCVN 7155 : 2002 (ISO 718 : 1990) Laboratory glass device - Thermal shock and its strength - Testing method
12. TCVN 7156 : 2002 (ISO 4798 : 1997) Laboratory glass device - Filter funnels
13. TCVN 7157 : 2002 (ISO 4799 : 1978) Laboratory glass device - Condensers
14. TCVN 7158 : 2002 (ISO 4800 : 1998) Laboratory glass device - Extractor funnels and dripping extractor funnels
15. TCVN 4833-1 : 2002 (ISO 3100-1 : 1991) Meat and meat products - Sampling and preparation of testing samples - Part 1: Sampling (Second check- In replacement of TCVN 4833- 1993)
16. TCVN 4833-2 : 2002 (ISO 3100-2 : 1988) Meat and meat products - Sampling and preparation of testing samples - Part 2: Preparation of testing samples for micro biological examination
17. TCVN 4835: 2002 (ISO 2917: 1999) Meat and meat products - Measurement of pH degree- Standard method (first-time check- In replacement of TCVN 4835-1989)
18. TCVN 7135: 2002 (ISO 6391: 1997) Meat and meat products - Quantification of escherichia coli- Colony counting technique at 44 0 C using filtering membrane
19. TCVN 7136: 2002 (ISO 5552: 1997) Meat and meat products - Detection and quantification of enterobacteriaceae without going through the restoration process - The MPN technique and colony counting technique
20. TCVN 7137 : 2002 (ISO 13681 : 1995) Meat and meat products - Quantification of yeast and must- Colony-counting technique
21. TCVN 7138 : 2002 (ISO 13720 : 1995) Meat and meat products Quantification of pseudemonas species
22. TCVN 7139 : 2002 (ISO 13722 : 1996) Meat and meat products - Quantification of brochothrix thermosphacta- Colony-counting technique
23. TCVN 7140 : 2002 (ISO 13496 : 2000) Meat and meat products - Dye detection - Laminal chromatography method
24. TCVN 7141 : 2002 (ISO 13730 : 1996) Meat and meat products - Determination of total phosphorus - Spectrum method
25. TCVN 7142: 2002 (ISO 936: 1998) Meat and meat products - Determination of total ash
26. TCVN 6426 : 2002 (AFQRJOS 19 : 2002) Aviation turbine jet fuel - JET A-1- Technical requirements (first-time check- In replacement of TCVN 6426- 1998)
27. TCVN 7169 : 2002 (ASTM D 1094- 00) Aviation fuel- Water reaction-testing method
28. TCVN 7170 : 2002 (ASTM D 2368- 97) Aviation fuel- Ice point determining method
Article 2.- This Decision takes effect 15 days after its signing.
For the Minister of Science and Technology Vice Minister BUI MANH HAI
