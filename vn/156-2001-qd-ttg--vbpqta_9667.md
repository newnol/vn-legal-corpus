# Quyết định 156/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 156/2001/QD-TTg of October 15, 2001 changing the Standing Vice-Chairman of the National Steering Committee for preparation of the 22 rd SEAGAMES
Mr. Nguyen Danh Thai, Minister-Chairman of the Committee for Physical Training and Sports, shall replace Mr. Ha Quang Du to act as Vice-Chairman of the National Steering Committee for preparation of the 22 rd South East Asian GAMES in Vietnam in 2003.- (Summary)
