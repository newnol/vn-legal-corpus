# Nghị quyết 16/2005/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
On the Government’s November 2005 regular meeting
On December 5, 2005, the Government held its November 2005 regular meeting, where it heard:
- The Education and Training Minister’s report on the preschool education development scheme for the 2006-2015 period;
- The Natural Resources and Environment Minister’s report on the inspection of observance of the 2003 Land Law;
- The Planning and Investment Minister’s report on solutions to reduction of trade deficit in the 2006-2010 period.
The Government also considered and approved the Planning and Investment Minister’s report on production, export, import and investment in November and socio-economic situation in the first 11 months of 2005; and the Trade Minister’s report on trade activities in November 2005 and forecasts for the last month of the year.- (Summary)
