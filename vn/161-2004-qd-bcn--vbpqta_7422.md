# Quyết định 161/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 161/2004/QD-BCN of December 6, 2004 on transforming Khanh Hoa Power Company of Power Company 3 into Khanh Hoa Power Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Khanh Hoa Power Joint-Stock Company, KHPC, has a charter capital of VND 163,221,000,000, of which the State’s equities represent 51%, equities sold to laborers of the enterprise, 36% and equities sold to other subjects, 13%.
Its head-office is located at 11 Ly Thanh Ton street, Nha Trang city, Khanh Hoa province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
