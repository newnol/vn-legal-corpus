# Decree 97/2007/NĐ-CP

DECREE
Providing for the handling of administrative violations and the coercive enforcement of administrative decisions in the customs domain
This Decree takes effect on July 1, 2007, and supersedes Decree No. 138/2004/ND-CP of June 17, 2004.
For administrative violations committed before the effective date of this Decree but sanctioned after this Decree takes effect, their sanctioning must comply with the provisions of this Decree provided that this Decree does not define legal responsibilities or define lesser legal responsibilities.
Other relevant provisions not mentioned in this Decree must comply with the law on handling of administrative violations and relevant provisions of law.- (Summary)
