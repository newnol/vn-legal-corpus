# Decision 144/2000/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 144/2000/QD-TTg of December 14, 2000 on serving allowances
This Decision stipulates subjects being officials and employees, elected or appointed to leading posts in State agencies, political organizations or socio-political organizations, or high-level specialists, who are entitled to enjoy serving allowances; levels of serving allowances.- (Summary)
