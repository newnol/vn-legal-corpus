# Quyết định 37/2004/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 37/2004/QD-BNN of July 30, 2004 establishing Post-Import Plant Quarantine Center II under the Plant Protection Department
Post-Import Plant Quarantine Center II is a post-import plant quarantine non-business unit under the Plant Protection Department, having the legal person status, its own seal, operation funding and bank accounts.
The Center is headquartered in Ho Chi Minh city, and operates in the provinces from Da Nang city southward.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
