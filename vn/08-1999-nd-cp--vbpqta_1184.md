# Nghị định 08/1999/NĐ-CP

DECREE No.08/1999/ND-CP of March 1st, 1999 on the establishment of Dong Le and Dong Luong wards of Dong Ha town, Quang Tri province
To establish Dong Le ward on the basis of the total land area of 919.62 ha and a population of 5.197 of Trieu Le commune.
To establish Dong Luong ward on the basis of the total land area of 1,969.21 ha and a population of 6,432 of Trieu Luong commune.- (Summary)
