# Chỉ thị 09/2005/CT-BBCVT

DIRECTIVE
On the implementation of the Bucharest World Postal Strategy, the 2005-2008 period
In order to implement the Bucharest World Postal Strategy for the 2005-2008 period, the Minister of Post and Telematics requests concerned agencies and units to focus on universal postal services; the quality of services and the efficiency of the postal network; and postal renewal and development.- (Summary)
