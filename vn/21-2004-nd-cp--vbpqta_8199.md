# Nghị định 21/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 21/2004/ND-CP of January 12, 2004 on the establishment of communes of Chau Thanh and Trang Bang rural districts and the adjustment of administrative boundaries of Duong Minh Chau and Tan Chau rural districts, Tay Ninh province.
To establish An Binh commune of Chau Thanh rural district on the basis of a land area of 2,221 ha and a population of 6,485 of Thanh Dien commune.
To establish An Co commune of Chau Thanh rural district on the basis of a land area of 3,673 ha and a population of 9,988 of Hao Duoc commune.
To establish Hung Thuan commune of Trang Bang rural district on the basis of a land area of 1,681 ha and a population of 2,636 of Loc Hung commune; and a land area of 2.606 ha and a population of 6,281 of Don Thuan commune.
To transfer a land area of 7,433 ha and a population of 1,104 of Suoi Da commune of Duong Minh Chau rural district to Tan Thanh commune of Tan Chau rural district.
To transfer a land area of 7,930 ha and a population of 1,775 of Suoi Da commune of Duong Minh Chau rural district to Tan Hoa commune of Tan Chau rural district.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
