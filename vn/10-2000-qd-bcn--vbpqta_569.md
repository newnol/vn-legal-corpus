To establish Ialy Hydro-electric Power Plant (HPP) attached to Vietnam Electricity Corporation.
- Head office: Iamnong commune, ChuPah district, Gia Lai province.
- Charter capital: 8,876,704,000.00 VND.
- Business lines:
+ Management, operation and production of electric power
+ Repair, maintenance, overhaul, modification and upgrading of the Plant’s equipment and projects.- (Summary)
