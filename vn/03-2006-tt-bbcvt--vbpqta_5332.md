# Circular 03/2006/TT-BBCVT

CIRCULAR
Amending and supplementing a number of contents of Circular No. 05/2004/TT-BBCVT of December 16, 2004, which guides the implementation of a number of articles on handling of administrative violations and settlement of complaints and denunciations specified in Chapter IV of the Government’s Decree No. 55/2001/ND-CP on management, provision and use of Internet services
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
