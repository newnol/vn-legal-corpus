# Nghị quyết 09/2002/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION No. 09/2002/NQ-CP of July 24, 2002 on the Government�s July 2002 regular meeting
At its July regular meeting, the Government discussed and resolved the following issues:
- It considered the scheme on establishment of State-run financial investment companies. The establishment of State-run financial investment companies is an important measure to renew the mechanism for management of the State capital at enterprises, renovate the relationship between the State and State enterprises on the principle of clearly determining the right to manage and the right to own capital at enterprises, and ensure that State enterprises actually have to right to business autonomy, taking initiatives in and bearing the responsibility for financial matters by themselves. The Government assigned the Ministry of Finance to continue finalizing the scheme and experiment plan.
- It considered the report on the administration of monetary policies in the first six months of 2002 and orientations for the last six months of 2002 and till 2005; the report on socio-economic situation in July and the first seven months of 2002; and the report on the work of inspection and settlement of complaints and denunciations in the first six months of 2002.- (Summary)
