# Quyết định 52/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 52/2004/QD-BCN of June 18, 2004 renaming Vinapac Joint-Stock Company defined in Article 2 of the Industry Minister's Decision No. 235/2003/QD-BCN of December 26, 2003 on transformation of Vinapac Packing Factory under Thieu Nien Tien Phong (Vanguard Juvenile) Plastic Company into Vinapac Joint-Stock Company
Vinapac Joint-Stock Company, which was defined in Article 2 of the Industry Minister's Decision No. 235/2003/QD-BCN of December 26, 2003 on transformation of Vinapac Packing Factory under Thieu Nien Tien Phong (Vanguard Juvenile) Plastic Company into Vinapac Joint-Stock Company, is now renamed Tien Phong Packing Joint-Stock Company (TIFOPACK) and headquartered at No. 2 An Da, Ngo Quyen district, Hai Phong city.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
