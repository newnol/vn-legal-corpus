It heard the Justice Minister reporting the scheme on "some ideas about amendments and supplements to the 1992 Constitution"; it heard the Minister-Director of the Government�s Commission for Organization and Personnel reporting the scheme on "the basic amendments and supplements to the Law on Organization of the Government, the Law on Organization of the People�s Councils and People�s Committees, and the Law on Election of Deputies to the People�s Councils".
