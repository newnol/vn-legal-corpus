# Quyết định 149/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 149/2004/QD-BCN of December 1, 2004 transforming the Export, Import and International Cooperation Company into VINACOAL Import-Export Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, VINACOAL Import-Export Joint-Stock Company (COALIMEX) is headquartered at No. 47 Quang Trung street, Tran Hung Dao ward, Hoan Kiem district, Hanoi.
Its charter capital is VND 20 billion, 59% of which to be held by the State, 32.2% sold to its laborers and 8.8% to outsiders.- (Summary)
