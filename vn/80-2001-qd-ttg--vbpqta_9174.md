# Quyết định 80/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 80/2001/QD-TTg of May 24, 2001 on handling and rearranging State-owned houses and land in Ho Chi Minh City
The Prime Minister requests the ministers, the heads of ministerial-level agencies and agencies attached to the Government, the heads of central committees of associations and the president of Ho Chi Minh City People�s Committee to direct the State management agencies, public-service units, associations� bodies and State enterprises under their management, which are directly managing and using State-owned houses and land in Ho Chi Minh City to revise and inspect such houses and land, so as to re-locate their offices as well as production and business establishments according to current standards and norms.- (Summary)
