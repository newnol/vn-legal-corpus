# Decision 898/2002/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 898/QD-TTg of October 7, 2002 on investment in Se San 3A hydro-electric power project
The investor of Se San 3A hydro-electric power project is Song Da Corporation under the Ministry of Construction.
Construction location: On Se San river, which runs through Mo Rai commune, Sa Thay rural district, Kon Tum province, and Yakrai commune, Iagrai rural district, Gia Lai province.
Investment form: The investor shall mobilize investment capital, construct and operate the project by itself, and sell electricity to Vietnam Electricity Corporation.
Target of the investment project: To create an electricity source for supply to the national electricity grid with an installation capacity of 96 MW and an annual average electricity output of 475 million kWh.
Total investment level: VND 1,864.597 billion.
Investment capital sources: To mobilize domestic capital and investor�s capital.
Tempo: To operate electricity-generating units in 2006-2007.
To allow Song Da Corporation to appoint domestic main consultants to perform the tasks of conducting surveys, elaborating technical designs and supervising the construction process. For works involving complicated techniques, it shall be allowed to hire foreign specialists and/or consultants on a contractual basis.- (Summary)
