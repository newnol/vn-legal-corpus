# Circular 05/1998/TT-BGD

THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION AND TRAINING
CIRCULAR No.05/1998/TT-BGDDT of February 7, 1998 guiding the assessment and grading of study results and conduct of students in complementary secondary schools and regular education centers.
This Circular specifies the exams and marking of exam papers; grading of study results and conduct; the criteria for commendation and class promotion.- (Summary)
