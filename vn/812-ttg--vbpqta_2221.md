# Decision 812/TTg

DECISION No.812-TTg OF DECEMBER 12, 1995 OF THE PRIME MINISTER on broadening the range of beneficiaries of long-term labor incapacitation allowances, who have retired from their work and who have been benefiting from such allowances if their service seniority is 20 full years or more. The Decision also provided for additional allowances to lonely pensioners and retired workers and State employees, who had served in the armed forces.- (summary)
