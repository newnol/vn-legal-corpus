# Quyết định 164/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 164/2004/QD-BCN of December 8, 2004 transforming Ninh Binh Garment Export Company into Ninh Binh Garment Export Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Ninh Binh Garment Export Joint-Stock Company (NIGACO) is headquartered at No. 9, Dinh Tien Hoang street, Ninh Binh provincial capital, Ninh Binh province.
Its charter capital is VND 3 billion, 40% of which to be held by the State and 60% sold to its laborers.- (Summary)
