# Circular 10/2006/TT-BCN

CIRCULAR
Amending Clause 3, Section II of Circular No. 01/2006/TT-BCN of April 11, 2006, which guides the management of import and export of toxic and hazardous chemicals, narcotic pre-substances and chemicals according to their technical standards, which are subject to the Industry Ministry’s specialized management
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
