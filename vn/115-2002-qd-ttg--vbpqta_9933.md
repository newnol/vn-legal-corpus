# Quyết định 115/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 115/2002/QD-TTg of September 13, 2002 on the establishment of border-gate economic zones in, and application of border-gate economic policies to, Hoanh Mo-Dong Van and Northern Phong Sinh areas of Quang Ninh province
Hoanh Mo-Dong Van border-gate economic zone covers Hoang Mo and Dong Van communes of Binh Lieu rural district.
Northern Phong Sinh border-gate economic zone covers Quang Duc commune of Hai Ha rural district.
The two above-mentioned border-gate economic zones shall enjoy the policies applicable to border-gate economic zones in border areas.- (Summary)
