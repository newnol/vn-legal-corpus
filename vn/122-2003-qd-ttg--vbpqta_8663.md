# Quyết định 122/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 122/2003/QD-TTg of June 12, 2003 on the Government's action program for implementation of the Resolution of the 7th plenum of the Party Central Committee (the IXth Congress) on the nationality work
This program covers the following contents:
1. Organization of the thorough study of the Resolution;
2. Organization of the efficient performance of some following major urgent tasks:
- Developing production, stepping up hunger elimination and poverty alleviation, raising the living standard of ethnic minority people, bringing on substantial changes to the socio-economic appearance of regions inhabited by ethnic minority people and mountainous areas;
- Socio-cultural tasks related to educational development, raising people's intellectual standard, intensifying the training of medical, cultural and information human resources;
- Protecting the environment;
- Consolidating and raising the quality of grassroots political system;
- Enhancing the national defense, ensuring political stability as well as social order and safety in regions inhabited by ethnic minority people and mountainous areas.
3. Reform of the nationality work:
- Elaborating the Law on Nationalities for the Government to submit it to the National Assembly;
- Elaborating, supplementing and perfecting a number of specific policies for regions inhabited by ethnic minority people and mountainous areas;
- Renovating the State management contents and modes of the nationality work;
- Consolidating the organizational system of State management over the nationality work from the central to grassroots levels.- (Summary)
