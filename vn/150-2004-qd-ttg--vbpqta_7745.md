# Quyết định 150/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 150/2004/QD-TTg of August 16, 2004 transforming Ben Kien Shipbuilding Plant under Vietnam Shipbuilding Industry Corporation into a State-owned one-member limited liability company
Under this Decision, Ben Kien Shipbuilding Plant, an independent cost-accounting unit under Vietnam Shipbuilding Industry Corporation, shall be transformed into a State-owned one-member limited liability company.
The company's international transaction name is Ben Kien Shipbuilding Industry Company Ltd., abbreviated to Ben Kien Shin Co, Ltd.
Its charter capital is VND 49,594,562,448.
It is headquartered in An Hong commune, An Duong rural district, Hai Phong city.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
