# Joint circular 119/2002/TTLT/BTC-BYT

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 119/2002/TTLT-BTC-BYT of December 27, 2002 guiding the collection and spending contents and levels of regular spendings of commune health stations
In order to ensure funding for regular activities of commune health stations, contributing to well implementing the task of providing healthcare for people at the grassroots level, this Joint Circular guides in detail the collection items as well as the contents and levels of regular spendings of commune health stations.- (Summary)
