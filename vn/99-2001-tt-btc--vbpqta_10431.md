# Thông tư 99/2001/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 99/2001/TT-BTC of December 5, 2001 guiding the priority given to the allocation of funding for the application and development of information technology
The Circular guides the priority given to the allocation of funding for the application and development of information technology (IT) by the ministries, branches and localities, concretely, the regulations on the elaboration and execution of IT application and development plans; the spending contents, financial mechanism, accounting and final account settlement of the amounts used for IT application and development.- (Summary)
