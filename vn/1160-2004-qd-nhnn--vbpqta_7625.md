# Quyết định 1160/2004/QĐ-NHNN

DECISION No. 1160/2004/QD-NHNN of September 13, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on savings
The Regulation on savings governs activities of receiving and paying Vietnam-dong and foreign-currency savings on the Vietnamese territory between the savings-receiving organizations and depositors.
Savings-receiving organizations include credit institutions set up and operating under the Credit Institution Law and other organizations engaged in banking activities that are licensed by law to receive savings.
Vietnam-dong savings depositors include Vietnamese and foreign individuals lawfully residing and operating in Vietnam, while foreign-currency savings depositors are individual residents.
This Decision takes effect as from January 1, 2005.- (Summary)
