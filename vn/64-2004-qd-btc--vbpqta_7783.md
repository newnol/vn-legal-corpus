# Quyết định 64/2004/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 64/2004/QD-BTC of August 2, 2004 promulgating Deo Ngang tunnel toll rates
Promulgated together with this Decision is the Table of Deo Ngang tunnel toll rates.
The collected tolls shall be managed and used according to the Finance Ministry's Circular No. 109/2002/TT-BTC of December 6, 2002.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
