# Quyết định 125/2004/QĐ-BTM

DICISION No. 125/2004/QD-BTM of February 6, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on the Trade Ministry’s working activities with the press agencies
The Regulation promulgated together with this Decision prescribes the Trade Ministry’s regime of information supply to the press agencies, applicable to the Ministry’s Office and the units attached to the Trade Ministry. The Trade Ministry shall perform the tasks and exercise the powers of a press-managing agency toward the press agencies under its management according to the provisions of the Press Law.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
