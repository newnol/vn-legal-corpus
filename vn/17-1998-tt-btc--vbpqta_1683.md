# Circular 17/1998/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No.17/1998/TT-BTC of February 11, 1998 guiding the implementation of a number of points concerning policies and measures for the management of the 1998 State budget estimates.
This Circular specifically guides provisions on the budget assignment, budget revenues, budget expenditures and thriftiness in expenditure.- (Summary)
