# Quyết định 35/2001/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION No. 35/2001/QD-BGDDT of August 8, 2001 promulgating the Regulation on the criteria for the conferment of the titles of Good Teacher of the Constant Education Centers, Model and Excellent Constant Education Centers
The Regulation issued together with this Decision clearly specifies the criteria and procedures for the consideration and conferment of the titles of Good Teacher of the Constant Education Centers, Model and Excellent Constant Education Centers.- (Summary)
