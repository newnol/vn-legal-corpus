# Quyết định 72/2002/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 72/2002/QD-BTC of June 5, 2002 amending the import tax rates of a number of commodity items under Headings No. 2709 and 2710 in the Preferential Import Tariff
To amend the preferential import tax rates of a number of commodity items under Headings No. 2709 (Petroleum oils and oils obtained from bituminous minerals, crude) and No. 2710 (Petroleum oils and oils obtained from bituminous minerals, other than crude; preparations not elsewhere specified or included, containing by weight 70% or more of petroleum oils or of oils obtained from bituminous minerals, these oils being the basic constituents of the preparations.)
This Decision takes effect and applies to the import goods declarations already submitted to the customs offices as from June 7, 2002.- (Summary)
