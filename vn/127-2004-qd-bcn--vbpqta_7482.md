# Quyết định 127/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 127/2004/QD-BCN of November 10, 2004 transforming Dien Quang Lamp Company into Dien Quang Lamp Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Dien Quang Lamp Joint-Stock Company is headquartered at No. 121-123-125 Ham Nghi street, District 1, Ho Chi Minh city.
Its charter capital is VND 23.5 billion, 51% of which to be held by the State and 49% sold to its laborers.- (Summary)
