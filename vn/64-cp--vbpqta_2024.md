# Nghị định 64-CP

Decree No.64-CP of October 28, 1996 of the Government on the establishment of new wards in Hai Duong provincial town, Hai Hung province:
- To establish Ngoc Chau ward comprising the whole area and population of Ngoc Chau commune
- To establish Thanh Binh ward comprising the whole area and population of Thanh Binh commune
- To establish Hai Tan ward comprising the whole area and population of Hai Tan commune
- To establish Cam Thuong ward comprising the whole area and population of Cam Thuong commune
- To establish Binh Han ward comprising the whole area and population of Binh Han commune
- To establish Le Thanh Nghi ward comprising 140 hectares of land area and a population of 8,100 people of Tran Phu ward.-(Summary)
