# Decision 162/QD-TTg

DECISION No.162/QD-TTg of February 19, 2001 on the transformation of a State enterprise being Construction Company No. 40 under the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development into a joint stock company
To transform a State enterprise being Construction Company No.40 under the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development into Construction Joint Stock Company No.40 or CTXD 40.
The joint stock company�s head office: 292 Cach Mang Thang 8 street, Can Tho city, Can Tho province.
The joint stock company shall have a charter capital of VND 16,176,000,000, of which the State�s shares account for 30%, the proportion of shares to be sold to its laborers: 60%, and share proportion to be sold to entities outside the enterprise shall be 10%.
Construction Joint Stock Company No.40 shall engage in the following business lines and trades:
- Constructing irrigation and water conservancy works, including: embankments, dikes, dams, canals, ditches, reservoirs; hydroelectric power plants; traffic, industrial and civil works;
- Repairing and supplying spare parts, specialized water conservancy equipment; manufacturing and installing sluice-gates and construction steel structures; building and repairing barges;
- Performing construction consultancy works: formulating and evaluating investing projects, survey, designing and making cost estimates of construction irrigation, traffic and civil projects and wrapping materials for grade-3 industrial projects; bidding for selecting managers, procuring construction supplies and equipment and conducting pre-acceptance inspection;
- Dealing in houses.- (Summary)
