# Decision 946/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Establishing the Management Board of Vung Ang Economic Zone, Ha Tinh province
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” the Management Board of Vung Ang Economic Zone, Ha Tinh province, is established on the basis of the Ha Tinh Industrial Parks Management Board.
This Decision replaces Decision No. 160/2002/QD-TTg of November 15, 2002.- (Summary)
