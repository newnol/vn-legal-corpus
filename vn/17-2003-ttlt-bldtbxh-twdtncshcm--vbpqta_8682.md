# Joint circular 17/2003/TTLT/BLDTBXH-TWDTNCSHCM

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 17/2003/TTLT-BLDTBXH-TWDTNCSHCM of June 9, 2003 guiding the implementation of policies towards youth volunteers who fulfilled their tasks in resistance wars
This Circular guides the subjects and conditions for youth volunteers to enjoy preferential regime. It also guides the competence and responsibilities for making dossiers and settling this preferential treatment policy.- (Summary)
