# Decision 278/2006/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Approving the Agreement between the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the Government of Slovakia on visa exemption for holders of diplomatic passports and facilitation of visa issuance for holders of official passports
The Prime Minister requests the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to carry out procedures and notify concerned agencies of the effective date of the Agreement.- (Summary)
