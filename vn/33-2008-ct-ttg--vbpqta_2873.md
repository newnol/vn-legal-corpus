# Directive 33/2008/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On strict implementation of financial policies, conclusions and proposals of audit and inspection agencies
With this Directive, the Prime Minister requests ministers, heads of ministerial-level agencies, heads of government-attached agencies and other central agencies and presidents of provincial/municipal People’s Committees to well perform several tasks in order to strictly implement financial policies, conclusions and proposals of audit and inspection agencies.- (Summary)
