# Decree 05/1998/ND-CP

DECREE No.05/1998/ND-CP of January 9, 1998 on the establishment of Cho township, the district township of Yen Phong district, Bac Ninh province.
To establish Cho district township on the basis of the whole land area of 782 hectares and a population of 11,320 of Ham Son commune.- (Summary)
