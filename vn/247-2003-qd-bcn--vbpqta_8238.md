# Quyết định 247/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 247/2003/QD-BCN of December 31, 2003 transforming Electrical Devices Company No. 1 into Electrical Devices Joint-Stock Company No. 1
To transform Electrical Devices Company No. 1 (an independent-accounting member of the Electrical Technical Equipment Corporation) into Electrical Devices Joint-Stock Company No. 1 (VINAKIP).
Its head-office: Xuan Khanh ward, Son Tay provincial town, Ha Tay province.
Its charter capital is VND 11 billion, with 51.04% of which being held by the State and 48.96% sold to its laborers.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
