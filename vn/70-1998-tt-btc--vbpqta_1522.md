# Thông tư 70/1998/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No.70/1998/TT-BTC of May 21, 1998 guiding the amendments to a number of points stipulated in Circular No.56-TC/NSNN of August 21, 1997 of the Ministry of Finance guiding the implementation of Decision No.100-QD/TW of June 3, 1995 of the Secretariat of the Party Central Committee on the operating funds for the district-level political fostering centers
This Circular guides the amendments to the levels of remunerations paid to the part-time lecturers and the meal allowances for students of political fostering classes at the district-level political fostering centers.- (Summary)
