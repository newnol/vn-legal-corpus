# Quyết định 260/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 260/2003/QD-TTg of December 5, 2003 on the establishment of the International College under Ho Chi Minh City National University
Under this Decision, the International College is a member college of Ho Chi Minh City National University, which shall have the legal person status as well as its own seal and accounts. The International College shall be located in the area planned for Ho Chi Minh City National University in Thu Duc urban district, Ho Chi Minh City, and Di An rural district, Binh Duong province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
