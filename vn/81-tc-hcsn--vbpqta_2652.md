# Circular 81/TC-HCSN

Circular No.81-TC/HCSN of December 23, 1996 of the Ministry of Finance guiding the management and use of advertising revenues for investment in the development of Vietnam�s television.
Revenues from television advertisement services are State budget revenues which are allowed by the Government to be managed and used by the television in service of its development .
This Circular provides detailed guidance on revenues and expenditures; planning; the management and distribution of revenues from television advertisement; accounting regime and cost-profit accounts reporting.- (Summary)
