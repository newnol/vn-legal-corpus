# Thông tư 71/2002/TT-BNN

CIRCULAR No. 71/2002/TT-BNN of August 12, 2002 guiding the implementation of the in-kind allowance regime for laborers working under conditions involving hazardous elements in the agriculture and rural development sector
The Circular guides in detail the allowance subjects, conditions and levels as well as principles for implementing the allowance regime.- (Summary)
