# Thông tư liên tịch 162/2004/TTLT/BQP-BTC

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 162/2004/TTLT/BQP-BTC of December 6, 2004 guiding the spending contents and the management of budgets for management and protection of national borders as well as maintenance of security and social order and safety in border areas
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces Joint Circular No. 3729/2001/BQP-BTC of November 29, 2001 of the Ministry of Defense and the Ministry of Finance.- (Summary)
