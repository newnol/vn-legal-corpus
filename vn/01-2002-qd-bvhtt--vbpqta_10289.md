# Quyết định 01/2002/QĐ-BVHTT

DECISION No. 01/2002/QD-BVHTT of January 2, 2002 promulgating the Regulation on recognizing the Culture Family, Culture Village and Culture Street Quarter titles
The Regulation on recognizing the Culture Family, Culture Village and Culture Street Quarter titles, issued together with this Decision, prescribes the titles, norms and procedures for recognizing the culture families, culture villages and culture street quarters for the families, villages and street quarters, which have recorded many achievements in the campaign: "the entire population unites to build a cultural life in population quarters".- (Summary)
