# Decision 222/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 222/QD-TTg of February 25, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under People’s Committee of Binh Thuan province till 2005
Enterprises where the State holds 100% of their charter capital and their legal person status remains unchanged till 2005: 1 public-utility enterprise and 5 enterprises engaged in business activities.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding more than 50% of their charter capital: 2 in 2003.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding at least 51% of shares when their shares are sold for the first time: 5 in 2003.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding under 50% of their charter capital or holding no shares: 3 in 2003, 4 in 2004 and 2 in 2005.
Enterprise section to be equitized with the State holding under 50% of its charter capital or holding no shares: 1 in 2003.
Enterprise to be dissolved: 1 in 2003.- (Summary)
