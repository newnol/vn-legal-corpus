# Nghị quyết 06/1999/NQ-CP

Resolution No. 06/1999/NQ-CP of June 3, 1999 on the Government�s May regular meeting
The Government held its regular May meeting on May 29, 1999 to discuss and resolve the following issues:
- To adopt the "project on diverging and slowing down floods in the Red River system", presented by the Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development. The Government has assigned the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development to assume the prime responsibility and coordinate with the relevant agencies in gathering and reviewing comments made at the meeting, completing the project and submitting it to the Prime Minister for signing its promulgation in June 1999.
- To adopt the draft "Government�s Decree on the State�s development investment credits" and "Decree on organization and operation of the development support fund", presented by the Minister-Director of the Government�s Office; To assign the Government�s Office to assume the prime responsibility and coordinate with the relevant ministries and branches in receiving comments from the cabinet members, completing the two said draft Decrees and submitting them to the Prime Minister for signing their promulgation in June 1999.
- To adopt the draft Ordinance on Commercial Bills, presented by the State Bank and the draft Ordinance on the Public Labor Obligations, presented by the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs.
Also at this meeting, the Government heard a "report on the implementation of the May plan and the plan in the first 5 months of 1999 as well as on a number of issues related to the direction of implementation of the June plan", presented by the Minister of Planning and Investment.- (Summary)
