# Quyết định 74/2005/QĐ-BVHTT

DECISION
On the classification of a national relic
Under this Decision, the historical relic of Le Phuc family line’s worshiping house in Lien Hoa commune, Yen Hung district, Quang Ninh province, is classified as a national relic.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
