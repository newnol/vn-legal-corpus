# Resolution 29/2007/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
Adjusting the land use planning up to 2010 and the 5-year (2006-2010) land use plan of Dong Nai province
The People’s Committee of Dong Nai province shall, based on this Resolution, organize the implementation of the land use planning and plan in association with socio-economic development objectives as well as defense, security and eco-environmental protection tasks.- (Summary)
