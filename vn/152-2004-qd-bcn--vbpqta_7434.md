# Quyết định 152/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 152/2004/QD-BCN of December 2, 2004 transforming Phuc Yen Shoes Plant of Vietnam Leather and Shoes Company into Phuc Yen Shoes Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Phuc Yen Shoes Joint-Stock Company (PYSHO-CO) is headquartered in Phuc Yen provincial capital, Vinh Phuc province.
Its charter capital is VND 5 billion, 20% of which to be held by the State and 80% sold to its laborers.- (Summary)
