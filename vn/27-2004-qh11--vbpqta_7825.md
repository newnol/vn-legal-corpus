# Nghị quyết 27/2004/QH11

RESOLUTION No. 27/2004/QH11 promulgating the Working Regulation of the National Assembly's Nationality Council and other Committees
The National Assembly (NA)'s Nationality Council and other Committees are the NA's agencies and set up by the NA. They have the tasks and powers prescribed by the Law on Organization of the National Assembly and other law provisions.
The NA's Nationality Council and other Committees are answerable for, and report on, their activities, to the NA, or to the NA Standing Committee in cases where the NA is in recess. They submit to the NA Standing Committee's direction, regulation and operation coordination in the performance of their tasks and powers.
This 4-chapter and 44-article Regulation also prescribes the organization and operation of the NA's Nationality Council and other Committees; their responsibilities and working relations; as well as their assisting apparatuses and operation fundings.
This Regulation replaces the Working Regulation of the Nationality Council and the Working Regulation of the NA's Committees, which were passed on July 7, 1993 by the IXth National Assembly at its 3rd session.- (Summary)
