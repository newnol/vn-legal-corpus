# Quyết định 27/2004/QĐ-BNV

DECISION No. 27/2004/QD-BNV of April 21, 2004 approving the (amended) Charter of Vietnam Nature and Environment Protection Society
To approve the amended Charter of Vietnam Nature and Environment Protection Society, which was adopted on November 6, 2003 by its IVth Congress.
The amended Charter, containing 25 articles arranged in 7 chapters, defines the name, operation aims and objectives, rights and obligations, members, organizational structure, finance and assets of the Society.
Vietnam Nature and Environment Protection Society is a socio-professional organization of persons devoted to or engaged in the domain of nature and environment protection, was established under the Prime Minister's Decision No. 299/CT of November 23, 1988 of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers (now the Prime Minister), operates under its Charter and the State's law and submits to the State management by the Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment and the concerned State management agencies. It is a member of Vietnam Union of Scientific and Technological Associations, and has the legal person status, its own seal and bank accounts, its own mouthpiece and logo.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
