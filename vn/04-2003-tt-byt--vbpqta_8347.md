# Thông tư 04/2003/TT-BYT

CIRCULAR No. 04/2003/TT-BYT of April 24, 2003 guiding the enrolment of students to nursing and medicinal techniques majors of tertiary and collegial degrees of on-the-job training system in the 2003-2004 school year
In the 2003-2004 school year, Ho Chi Minh City Medical and Pharmaceutical University, Hue Medical University and Hanoi Medical University shall train bachelors of general nursing and bachelors of medicinal techniques of tertiary degree of on-the-job training system. Nam Dinh Medical College shall provide on-the-job training of general nursing and obstetric nursing of collegial degree.
This Circular guides in detail subjects and criteria of enrolment, priority policies, dossiers of application for enrolment, organization of examinations and consideration of examination results, school fees and benefits of learners.- (Summary)
