# Quyết định 1273/2004/QĐ-BTM

DECISION No. 1273/2004/QD-BTM of September 7, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on coloring commodity gasoline and oil
Under the Regulation issued together with this Decision, kerosene, assorted non-lead gasoline, and jet fuels must be properly colored before they are sold in the domestic market in order to prevent fraudulent acts and protect consumers' interests.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
