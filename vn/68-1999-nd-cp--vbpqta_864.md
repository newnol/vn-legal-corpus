# Nghị định 68/1999/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 68/1999/ND-CP of August 9, 1999 on the division of Tien Son and Gia Luong districts for the re-establishment of Tien Du, Tu Son, Gia Binh and Luong Tai districts, Bac Ninh province
To divide Tien Son district into Tien Du and Tu Son districts. Tien Du district has a land area of 10,630.03 ha and a population of 125,157, comprising 16 dependent administrative units. Tu Son district has a land area of 6,027.7 ha and a population of 114,825, comprising 11 dependent administrative units.
To divide Gia Luong district into Gia Binh and Luong Tai districts. Gia Binh district has a land area of 10,752.81 ha and a population of 103,092, comprising 13 dependent administrative units. Luong Tai district has a land area of 10,116.15 ha and a population of 101,540, comprising 14 dependent administrative units.- (Summary)
