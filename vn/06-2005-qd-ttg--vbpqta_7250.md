# Quyết định 06/2005/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 06/2005/QD-TTg of January 7, 2005 approving the scheme on experimental equitization of Vietnam Electronics and Informatics Corporation
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Vietnam Electronics and Informatics Joint-Stock Corporation (VEIC) is the parent company where the State holds over 50% of the charter capital.
VEIC is headquartered at No. 15 Tran Hung Dao street, Hoan Kiem district, Hanoi city.- (Summary)
