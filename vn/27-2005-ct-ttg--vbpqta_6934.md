# Chỉ thị 27/2005/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On speeding up the tempo of the program on solidification of schools and classrooms
With this Directive, the Prime Minister instructs the Education and Training Ministry, the Central Steering Committee for the program on solidification of schools and classrooms, the Finance Ministry, the State Treasury, the Planning and Investment Ministry, the Construction Ministry, Vietnam Television Station, the Radio Voice of Vietnam, provincial/municipal People's Committees, and concerned agencies to perform several tasks in order to speed up the tempo of the program on solidification of schools and classrooms.- (Summary)
