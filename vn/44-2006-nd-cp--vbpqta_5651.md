# Decree 44/2006/NĐ-CP

DECREE
On the sanctioning of administrative violations in the domain of railway transport
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces Chapter VI of the Government’s Decree No. 39/CP of July 5, 1996, on ensuring railway traffic order and safety, which was amended and supplemented by Decree No. 76/1998/ND-CP of September 26, 1998.- (Summary)
