# Quyết định 139/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 139/2004/QD-BCN of November 22, 2004 on transforming Construction, Installation and Construction Materials Company No. 5 into Construction Investment Joint-Stock Company No. 5
Under this Decision, Construction Investment Joint-Stock Company No. 5, CISCO.5, has a charter capital of VND 12,500,000,000, of which the State' equities represent 54%, equities sold to laborers of the company, 38.35% and equities sold to other subjects, 7.65%.
Its head-office is located at No. 22 Ly Tu Trong street, Ben Nghe ward, district 1, Ho Chi Minh city.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
