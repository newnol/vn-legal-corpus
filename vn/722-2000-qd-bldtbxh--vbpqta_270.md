# Decision 722/2000/QD-BLDTBXH

THE MINISTRY OF LABOR, WAR INVALIDS AND SOCIAL AFFAIRS
DECISION No. 722/2000/QD-BLDTBXH of August 2, 2000 supplementing and amending the list of personal protection equipment and devices for laborers doing jobs involving dangerous and/or hazardous factors
To amend a number of items in the list of personal protection equipment and devices for laborers doing jobs involving dangerous and/or hazardous factors, issued together with Decision No. 955/1998/QD-BLDTBXH of September 22, 1998.
The list issued together with this Decision specifies types of protection equipment and devices for each job in every field involving dangerous and/or hazardous factors such as: mining, metallurgy-founding; mechanical engineering; cultivation-husbandry-veterinary; chemical production.- (Summary)
