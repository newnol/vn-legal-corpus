# Decision 116/2000/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 116/2000/QD-TTg of October 10, 2000 assigning the task of doctorate training to Mechanical Research Institute of the Ministry of Industry
To assign the task of doctorate training to Mechanical Research Institute - the Ministry of Industry. The Ministry of Education and Training shall assume the prime responsibility and coordinate with the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment and the Ministry of Industry in providing for majors of doctorate training to the Mechanical Research Institute.- (Summary)
