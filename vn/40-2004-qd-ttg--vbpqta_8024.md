# Quyết định 40/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 40/2004/QD-TTg of March 19, 2004 establishing the Vietnam Studies and Development Science Institute of Hanoi National University
To establish the Vietnam Studies and Develop-ment Science Institute of Hanoi National University on the basis of the Center for Vietnam Studies and Cultural Exchange of Hanoi National University.
The Institute functions to conduct inter-branch scientific research, apply sciences and provide post-graduate training on Vietnam studies.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
