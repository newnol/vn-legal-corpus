# Quyết định 145/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 145/2004/QD-BCN of November 23, 2004 on transforming the Installation and Electric Construction Factory of the Installation Company into Installation and Electric Construction Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, the Installation and Electric Construction Joint-Stock Company, IEC, has a charter capital of VND 7,000,000,000, of which equities sold to laborers of the factory represent 86.80% and equities sold to other subjects, 13.20%.
Its head-office is located at Km 9+200 Nguyen Trai avenue, Thanh Xuan district, Hanoi city.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
