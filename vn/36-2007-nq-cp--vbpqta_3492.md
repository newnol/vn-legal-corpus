# Nghị quyết 36/2007/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
On the Government’s July 2007 regular meeting
On July 16, 2007, the Government held its July regular meeting where it discussed a scheme on reforming the policies on wages, social insurance and preferential allowances for people with meritorious services in the 2007-2010 period, presented by the Minister of Home Affairs, and adopted a draft decree guiding the implementation of a number of articles of the Enterprise Law, presented by the Ministry of Planning and Investment, and a draft decree amending and supplementing the Government’s Decree No. 188/2004/ND-CP of November 16, 2004, on methods of determining land prices and land price frames, presented by the Minister of Finance.- (Summary)
