# Thông tư 106/2004/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 106/2004/TT-BTC of November 9, 2004 guiding the use of funds allocated from the central budget for assurance of traffic order and safety
This Circular applies to the Ministry of Public Security and the Ministry of Transport (the National Committee for Traffic Safety).
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces the Finance Ministry's Circular No. 12/2002/TT-BTC of February 4, 2002.- (Summary)
