# Thông tư 06/1999/TT-BTM

CIRCULAR No. 06/1999/TT-BTM OF MARCH 23, 1999 ADJUSTING SECTION C OF CIRCULAR No.09/1998/TT-BTM OF JULY 18, 1998 OF THE MINISTRY OF TRADE
For the projects with certificates of investment preferences granted before or after February 1, 1998 (the effective date of Decree No. 07/1998/ND-CP of January 15, 1998 of the Government):
1. They shall be exempt from import tax on goods imported into Vietnam after February 1, 1998;
2. They shall be subject to import tax on goods already imported into Vietnam before February 1, 1998;
3. The date when the goods are imported into Vietnam shall be the date written in the custom declaration.
This Circular is an inseparable part of Circular No.09/TT-BTM of July 18, 1998 of the Ministry of Trade.
For the Minister of Trade
Vice Minister
MAI VAN DAU
