# Nghị định 148/2005/NĐ-CP

DECREE
On the organization and operation of the Planning and Investment Inspectorate
Under this Decree, the Planning and Investment Inspectorate shall be organized at the Planning and Investment Ministry and provincial/municipal Planning and Investment Services to conduct administrative inspection and specialized inspection in the domain of planning and investment according to the provisions of law.
