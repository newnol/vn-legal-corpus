# Quyết định 15/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 15/2004/QD-TTg of January 29, 2004 setting up Thai Binh province's Industrial Park Management Board
To set up Thai Binh province's Industrial Park Management Board under the People's Committee of Thai Binh province to manage industrial parks in the locality.
Thai Binh province's Industrial Park Management Board shall have to perform tasks and powers prescribed in Article 27 of the Regulation on Industrial Parks, Export-Processing Zones and Hi-Tech Parks, issued together with the Government's Decree No. 36/CP of April 24, 1997. It shall have the legal person status, its own bank accounts and seal.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
