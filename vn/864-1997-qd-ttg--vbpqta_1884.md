# Quyết định 864/1997/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.864/1997/QD-TTg of October 15, 1997 on the establishment of a bureau of the Radio Voice of Vietnam in Can Tho city.
The functions, tasks and organizational structure of the Can Tho bureau of the Radio Voice of Vietnam shall be defined by the General Director of the Radio Voice of Vietnam after consulting the Minister-Chairman of the Government Commission on Organization and Personnel.- (Summary)
