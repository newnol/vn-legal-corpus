# Decision 143/TTg

Decision No.143-TTg of March 10, 1997 of the Prime Minister on renaming a number of Departments under the Ministry of Trade.
The Department of Urban and Rural Market Policies is renamed as the Department of Domestic Trade Policies.
The Department of Mountainous Market Policies is renamed as the Department of Mountainous Trade Development.- (Summary)
