# Decision 85/2000/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 85/2000/QD-TTg of July 18, 2000 on transforming Vien Dong Paper Company into a joint-stock company
To approve the plan on the equitization of Vien Dong paper company of the Vietnam Paper Corporation as proposed in Official Dispatch No. 754/CV-HDQT of June 16, 2000 of the Management Board of the Vietnam Paper Corporation, thereby Vien Dong paper company of the Vietnam Paper Corporation shall be transformed into Vien Dong Paper Joint Stock Company (abbreviated to VIDON).
* Charter capital: 11,600,000,000 VND, in which:
- State�s equity: 0%
- Equities sold to laborers of the enterprise and outsiders: 100% (equities shall be sold first to the enterprise�s laborers without any limitation, the remaining equities shall be sold outside the enterprise).
* Headquarter: 129 Au Co street, ward 13, Tan Binh district, Ho Chi Minh City.
* Business lines:
- Production of and trading in toilet papers, sanitary napkins; tissues and other paper products; supplies and equipment for paper industry;
- Export and import of assorted paper, supplies and equipment for paper industry;
- Provision of services for equipment design, manufacturing, installation, processing and repair, technical consultancy and transfer of paper-producing technology;
- Other business lines prescribed by law.- (Summary)
