# Quyết định 87/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 87/2001/QD-TTg of June 1, 2001 on experimenting the regime of contract assignment of financial revenue and expenditure for activities of Vietnam Television
Vietnam Television is funded by the State budget to cover expenses for the performance of its assigned functions and tasks. All revenues earned from television advertisement and other service activities must be remitted into the State budget according to current regulations.
Vietnam Television shall be allowed to experiment the regime of contract assignment of financial revenue and expenditure in two years (2001-2002) under the principle that revenues earned from its activities, including the amounts of value added tax and enterprise income tax imposed on television advertisements as well as revenues from other services, which must be remitted into the State budget, shall be retained in form of revenue/expenditure entries.- (Summary)
