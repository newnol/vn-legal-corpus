# Quyết định 164/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 164/2003/QD-TTg of August 8, 2003 establishing the Committee for Ethnic and Religious Affairs of Hoa Binh province
To establish the Committee for Ethnic and Religious Affairs under the People's Committee of Hoa Binh province. This Committee is a specialized agency of the provincial People's Committee, which functions to assist the provincial People's Committee in performing the State management over the ethnic and religious affairs.- (Summary)
