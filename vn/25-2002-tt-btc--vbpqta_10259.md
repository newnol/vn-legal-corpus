# Circular 25/2002/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 25/2002/TT-BTC of March 21, 2002 guiding the implementation of the Government�s Decree No. 10/2002/ND-CP of January 16, 2002 on the financial regime applicable to public-service units having income
This Circular applies to public-service units, established by the State and having income generated from the fields of education and training; health care; science, technology and environment; culture and information; physical training and sports; economic non-business and employment services,�
The Circular guides the classification of public-service units having income; financial sources as well as spending contents and norms; the fulfillment of obligations towards the State budget, deduction for setting up funds and use thereof; management and use of capital and assets; elaboration and execution of revenue-expenditure estimates; opening of transaction accounts; as well as inspection and control of revenue-expenditure activities.- (Summary)
