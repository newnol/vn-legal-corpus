# Quyết định 84/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 84/2001/QD-TTg of May 30, 2001 on the establishment of Region I Post and Telecommunications Department under the General Department of Post and Telecommunications
To establish Region I Post and Telecommunications Department under the General Department of Post and Telecommunications to assist the General Director of Post and Telecommunications in performing the function of State management over post and telecommunications activities, as well as radio frequencies in the northern provinces and cities.- (Summary)
