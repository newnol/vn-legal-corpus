# Quyết định 212/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 212/2003/QD-TTg of October 21, 2003 on the list of State secrets classified as top secret in the field of natural resources and environment
The list of State secrets classified as top secret in the field of natural resources and environment comprises the information on water resources, geology and minerals, environment, hydrometeorology and surveying, which are specified in this Decision.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
