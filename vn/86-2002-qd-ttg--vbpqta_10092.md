# Quyết định 86/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 86/2002/QD-TTg of July 4, 2002 establishing the Customs Department of Quang Ngai province
The Customs Department of Quang Ngai province shall be attached to the General Department of Customs.
The Customs Department of Quang Ngai province shall have its own seal and accounts, and its office headquartered in Quang Ngai province.- (Summary)
