# Quyết định 57/2004/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 57/2004/QD-BTC of June 28, 2004 promulgating the list of legal documents, issued by the Finance Ministry up to December 31, 2003, which cease to be effective
This Decision promulgates the list of 140 legal documents, issued by the Finance Ministry up to December 31, 2003, which cease to be effective. It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
