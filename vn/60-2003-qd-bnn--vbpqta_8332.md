# Decision 60/2003/QD-BNN

DECISION No. 60/2003/QD-BNN of May 6, 2003 promulgating the 2003 lists of veterinary drugs allowed to be produced, exported, re-packed, imported, circulated, used or restricted from use in Vietnam
Issued together with this Decision were:
1. List of veterinary drugs allowed to be produced, exported and re-packed.
2. List of drugs and raw materials for production of veterinary drugs, allowed to be imported.
3. List of drugs and raw materials for production of veterinary drugs, restricted from use.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
To annul lists of veterinary drugs issued together with Decision No. 40/2002/QD-BNN of May 27, 2002.- (Summary)
