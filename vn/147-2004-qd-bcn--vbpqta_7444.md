# Quyết định 147/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 147/2004/QD-BCN of November 24, 2004 on transforming Saigon-Can Tho Beer Factory into Saigon-Can Tho Beer Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Saigon-Can Tho Beer Joint-Stock Company, SACABECO, has a charter capital of VND 90,000,000,000, of which the State's equities represent 51%, equities sold to laborers of the factory, 4% and equities sold to other subjects, 45%.
Its head-office is located at Tra Noc industrial park, Tra Noc ward, Binh Thuy district, Can Tho city.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
