# Nghị định 98/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 98/2003/ND-CP of August 25, 2003 establishing Vu Ninh, Kinh Bac and Dai Phuc wards of Bac Ninh provincial capital, Bac Ninh province
To establish the following 3 wards of Bac Ninh provincial capital:
- Vu Ninh ward with a land area of 620.33 ha and a population of 9,737;
- Kinh Bac ward with a land area of 206.02 ha and a population of 6,315;
- Dai Phuc ward with a land area of 460.6 ha and a population of 10,073.- (Summary)
