# Decision 82/2007/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Approving the Maritime Transport Agreement between the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the Government of the United States of America, concluded in Washington on March 15, 2007
The Prime Minister assigns the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to carry out foreign-relation procedures for the approval of the Agreement and notify Vietnamese agencies of the effective date of the Agreement.- (Summary)
