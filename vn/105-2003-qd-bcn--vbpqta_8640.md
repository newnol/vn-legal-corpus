# Quyết định 105/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 105/2003/QD-BCN of June 25, 2003 approving the Charter on organization and operation of Tan Tien Plastic Packaging Company
This Decision approves the Charter on organization and operation Tan Tien Plastic Packaging Company.
Tan Tien Plastic Packaging Company is an independent cost-accounting enterprise under the Ministry of Industry, which is set up, invested and managed by the State in the capacity as its owner.
The Company shall be tasked to produce, trade in, import and export plastic commodities according to the plannings and plans of the Ministry of Industry and market demands: producing, supplying and selling products, importing and exporting materials and raw materials, supplies and goods items turned out by the Company; making investment in capital construction; researching into and applying advanced technologies and techniques; training and fostering managerial and technical staff; dealing in other production and/or business lines according to law provisions and performing other tasks assigned by the Ministry of Industry.
The Company’s head-office: 117/2 Luy Ban Bich, Ward 20, Tan Binh District, Ho Chi Minh City.- (Summary)
