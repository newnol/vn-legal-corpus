# Quyết định 05/2005/QĐ-BTNMT

DECISION
Defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Hanoi Natural Resources and Environment College
The Hanoi Natural Resources and Environment College is an educational non-business organization under the Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment, training and fostering officials of collegial degree and lower degrees in the fields of hydrometeorology, water resources, environment, geodesy, mapping, land management, and information technology; fostering and re-training officials and employees in the natural resources and environment sector; conducting scientific research and experimentation; entering into cooperation in training at home and abroad; and providing scientific and technological services.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
