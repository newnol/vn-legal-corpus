# Quyết định 48/2003/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 48/2003/QD-BNN of March 21, 2003 classifying and prescribing the functions, tasks and organizational structure of the Agriculture Hospital
This Decision classifies Region I Hospital as class II and changes its name into Agriculture Hospital.
The Agriculture Hospital is a medical non-business unit under the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development, which is allowed to use its own seal and open its account at State Treasury.
Tasks of the Agriculture Hospital:
- To perform the work of primary healthcare and provide medical examination and treatment for officials, employees, pupils and students of various agencies and units under the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development in Northern Vietnam, and to be the superior level of hospitals under the Ministry of Agricultural and Rural Development.
- To take part in scientific research and provide treatment for occupational disease sufferers as well as accident victims.
- To organize a specialized injury department in order to render first aid and treatment to victims of traffic accidents on Highway 1A Section, south of Hanoi, as prescribed by the Health Ministry.
- To provide medical examination and treatment for patients with health insurance and people living in communes around the hospital, and step by step to socialize healthcare.- (Summary)
