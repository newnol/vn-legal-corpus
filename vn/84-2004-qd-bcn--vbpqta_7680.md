# Quyết định 84/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 84/2004/QD-BCN of August 31, 2004 transforming Quang Trung Mechanical Engineering Company into Quang Trung Mechanical Engineering State-owned one-member limited liability company
To transform Quang Trung Mechanical Engineering Company, an independent cost-accounting member enterprise of the Industrial Machines and Equipment Corporation, into a State-owned one-member limited liability company.
The company's international transaction name is Quang Trung Mechanical Engineering Limited Company, abbreviated to QTMEC Ltd.
Its charter capital is VND 12,251,990,000.
Its head-office is located at No. 360 Giai Phong Avenue, Thanh Xuan district, Hanoi.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Sumamry)
