# Decision 163/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 163/QD-TTg of February 18, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Bac Lieu province in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises where the State continues to hold 100% of their capital: 2 public-utility enterprises and 7 enterprises engaged in business activities.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding more than 50% of their charter capital: 2 in 2003, 2 in 2004 and 3 in 2005.
Enterprise to be equitized with the State holding under 50% of its charter capital: 1 in 2004.
Enterprise to be sold or business contracted before being equitized: 1 in 2003.
Enterprise to be transformed into non-business unit with revenues: 1 in 2003.
Enterprise to be dissolved or declared bankrupt: 1 in 2003.- (Summary)
