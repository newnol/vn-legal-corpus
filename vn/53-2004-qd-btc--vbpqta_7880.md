# Quyết định 53/2004/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 53/2004/QD-BTC of June 15, 2004 readjusting the preferential import tax rates for a number of iron and steel commodity items
Promulgated together with this Decision is the List and preferential import tax rates of a number of iron and steel commodity items.
For imported commodities on the List of commodities for implementation of the ASEAN countries' Common Effective Preferential Tariffs Agreement, the tax rates prescribed in the Government's Decree No. 78/2003/ND-CP of July 1, 2003 and guided in the Finance Ministry's Circular No. 64/2003/TT-BTC of July 1, 2003 shall apply.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and applies to all import goods declarations submitted to the customs as from July 1, 2004.- (Summary)
