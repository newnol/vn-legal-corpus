# Decision 1621/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 1621/QD-TTg of December 26, 2001 adding investment capital and providing support for the overcoming of natural calamities� consequences in 2001
The Finance Ministry deducted VND 190 billion from the 2001 capital construction capital source (VND 13 billion) and the 2001 reserve source for natural calamities prevention and combat ( VND 177 billion) to add investment capital and financially support the overcoming of consequences caused by storms and floods; handle riverside and coastal landslides; urgently evacuate people from lanslide areas.- (Summary)
