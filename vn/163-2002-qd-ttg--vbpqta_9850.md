# Decision 163/2002/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 163/2002/QD-TTg of November 18, 2002 approving the master plan on arrangement and renovation of State enterprises under the People�s Committee of Son La province in the 2002-2005 period
Under the approved plan, the State enterprises which continue to hold 100% of their charter capital and maintain their legal person status till 2005 include 2 public-utility enterprises, 10 enterprises engaged in business activities and 4 enterprises formed on the basis of consolidation of enterprises.
The plan also sets the 2002-2004 roadmap for arrangement of State enterprises.- (Summary)
