# Chỉ thị 21/2004/CT-BGTVT

DIRECTIVE No. 21/2004/CT-BGTVT of September 8, 2004 on reorganizing the pre-acceptance check of research schemes and the management, use, payment and settlement of funds for scientific and technological research in the transport branch
With this Directive, the Minister of Transport requests the concerned departments, agencies, units and individuals to carry out some tasks to overcome the existing problems in the pre-acceptance check of research schemes and the management, use, payment and settlement of funds for scientific and technological research.- (Summary)
