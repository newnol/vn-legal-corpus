# Quyết định 56/2005/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
Providing for the rates of wharfage for Tan Son fish wharf, Thai Thuy district, Thai Binh province, and the regime of collection, remittance, management and use of the charges
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO." Other matters related to charge collection, remittance, management and use not mentioned in this Decision shall comply with Circular No. 63/2002/TT-BTC of July 24, 2002.- (Summary)
