# Quyết định 18/2002/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION No. 18/2002/QD-BGDDT of April 8, 2002 promulgating the Regulation on graduation examinations for complementary junior secondary education and complementary senior secondary education
The Regulation on graduation examinations for complementary junior secondary education and complementary senior secondary education, issued together with this Decision, prescribes the organization, management and contents of graduation examinations for complementary junior secondary education and complementary senior secondary education; the organization and operation of Examination Steering and Inspection Boards; the management decentralization as well as the work of commendation and discipline in examinations.- (Summary)
