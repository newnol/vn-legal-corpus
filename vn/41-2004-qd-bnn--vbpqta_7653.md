# Quyết định 41/2004/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 41/2004/QD-BNN of August 30, 2004 promulgating the 2004-2005 lists of animal feed and raw materials used for animal feed processing, which are permitted for import into Vietnam
These lists replace the lists of animal feed and raw materials used for animal feed processing, which are permitted for import into Vietnam in the 2002-2005 period.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
