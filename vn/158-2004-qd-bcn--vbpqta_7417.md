# Quyết định 158/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 158/2004/QD-BCN of December 6, 2004 on transforming Power Construction Company 3.1 into Power Construction Joint-Stock Company 3.1
Under this Decision, Power Construction Joint-Stock Company 3.1, PCC 3.1, has a charter capital of VND 5,500,000,000, of which the State’s equities represent 30%, equities sold to laborers of the company, 63.03% and equities sold to other subjects, 6.97%.
Its head-office is located at No. 489 Nguyen Luong Bang avenue, Hoa Hiep ward, Lien Chieu district, Da Nang city.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
