# Decision 23/2004/QD-BTS

DECISION No. 23/2004/QD-BTS of September 22, 2004 establishing the South Research Sub-Institute for Marine Fisheries
The South Research Sub-Institute for Marine Fisheries (SORESIMF), headquartered in Vung Tau city, Ba Ria-Vung Tau province, is attached to and represents the Marine Fisheries Research Institute; directly conducts scientific research into marine fishery technologies in the southern sea areas, from Da Nang city to Kien Giang province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
