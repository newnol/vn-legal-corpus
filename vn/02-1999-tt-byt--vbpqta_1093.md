# Circular 02/1999/TT-BYT

CIRCULAR No.02/1999/TT-BYT of March 12, 1999 guiding the organization of the sale of medicines with freight subsidies in mountainous, island and ethnic minority regions
Entitled to sell freight-subsidized medicines shall be: State enterprises engaged in curative medicines production and trading; limited liability and stock companies engaged in medicine production and trading; medicine trading private enterprises; and private drug stores. These subjects shall enjoy freight subsidies according to the current regulations if they have business registration papers and strictly comply with their business lines in mountainous, island and ethnic minority regions.
The Circular also specifies the areas where the sale of curative medicines with freight subsidies is applied; the list of curative medicines with freight subsidies; and the organization of the sale of curative medicines with freight subsidies.- (Summary)
