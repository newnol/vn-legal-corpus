# Quyết định 36/2003/QĐ-BNV

DECISION No. 36/2003/QD-BNV of June 24, 2003 approving the Charter of Vietnam Electrical Industry Association
To approve the Charter of Vietnam Electrical Industry Association, which was adopted by its second congress on April 5, 2003.
Vietnam Electrical Industry Association is a non-governmental voluntary organization embracing units engaged in the fields of scientific research, manufacture, production, construction and installation, and trading of specialized electrical commodities.
The Association’s objectives are to align and cooperate with and support one another economically and technically in the research, production, trading, service and raising products’ value; represent and protect the legitimate interests of its members; contribute to creating more jobs and improving laborers’ living standards.- (Summary)
