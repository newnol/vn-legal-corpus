# Decision 327/2002/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 327/QD-TTg of May 2, 2002 adjusting the investment project on the construction and commercial operation of technical infrastructure in Tan Dong Hiep industrial park, Binh Duong province
To adjust the investment project on the construction and commercial operation of technical infrastructure in Tan Dong Hiep industrial park, Binh Duong province, prescribed in the Prime Minister�s Decision No. 714/TTg of August 30, 1997 as follows:
Binh Duong Production and Import-Export Company shall transfer Zone B, which occupies a land area of 164.12 ha, to Tu Hai Limited Liability Company for investment therein and commercial operation of the technical infrastructure, with the total investment capital of VND 302.4 billion. The operation duration of the project shall be 50 years after it is licensed for investment.- (Summary)
