# Quyết định 11/2003/QĐ-BGDĐT

Decision No. 11/2003/QD-BGDDT of March 18, 2003 promulgating the Regulation on enrolment of intermediate vocational school students of the formal training system
To issue together with this Decision the Regulation on enrolment of intermediate vocational school students of the formal training system.
This Regulation provides for the powers and responsibilities of schools in the enrolment work; specific provisions on enrolment work; enrolment and convoking of those who have passed the enrolment exams; and reporting and archival regimes.
This Decision replaces the Education and Training Minister’s Decision No. 05/1999/QD-BGDDT of February 23, 1999 and Decision No. 20/2000/QD-BGDDT.- (Summary)
