# Circular 9/TT-KHDT

THE MINISTRY OF INDUSTRY
Circular No.9-TT/KHDT of August 30, 1997 guiding the implementation of the model statute of industrial and construction cooperatives issued together with Decree No.44-CP of April 9, 1997 of the Government.
In furtherance of Article 2 of Decree No.44-CP of April 9, 1997 of the Government, the Ministry of Industry guides the application of the model statute of industrial and construction cooperatives in the provinces, cities, urban/rural districts, to all cooperatives and cooperative members.
This Circular also details a number of articles of the model statute.- ( Summary)
