# Quyết định 05/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 05/2004/QD-BCN of January 7, 2004 on transferring the Power Construction and Installation Intermediate School, a member non-business unit under Vietnam Industrial Construction Corporation, to the Ministry of Industry
Under this Decision, the Power Construction and Installation Intermediate School, a non-business unit under Vietnam Industrial Construction Corporation, shall be transferred in status quo to the Ministry of Industry.
The Power Construction and Installation Intermediate School is a non-business unit with revenues, has the legal person status and its own seals and is allowed to open accounts at the State Bank and Treasury.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces the Industry Minister's Decision No. 817/QD-TCCB of June 6, 1997 and Decision No. 339/QD-TCCB of February 15, 2001.- (Summary)
