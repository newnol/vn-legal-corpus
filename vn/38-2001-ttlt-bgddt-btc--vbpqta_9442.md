# Joint circular 38/2001/TTLT/BGDDT-BTC

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 38/2001/TTLT/BGDDT-BTC of August 22, 2001 additionally guiding Joint-Circular No. 54/1998/TTLT/BGDDT-BTC of August 31, 1998 on the collection, spending and management of school fees at public education and training establishments under the national education system according to the Prime Minister’ Decision No. 70/1998/QD-TTg of March 31, 1998
The Ministry of Education and Training and the Ministry of Finance hereby guides the contents of spendings in support of general management and regulating work under local education and training sector according to Clause 2.4, Article 4 of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 70/1998/QD-TTg of March 31, 1998.- (Summary)
