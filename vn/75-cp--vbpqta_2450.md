# Decree 75/CP

Decree No.75-CP of June 16, 1997 on the establishment of Phu My township of Phu Tan district, An Giang province.
To establish Phu My township of Phu Tan district, An Giang province on the basis of the whole land area of 701 hectares and a population of 27,043 of Phu My commune.-
