# Quyết định 87/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 87/2003/QD-TTg of May 8, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under the Committee for Physical Training and Sports till 2005
The Physical Training and Sports Publishing House shall be kept intact with 100% State capital.
Vietnam Physical Training and Sports Company shall be equitized in 2004 with the State holding more than 50% of its charter capital.- (Summary)
