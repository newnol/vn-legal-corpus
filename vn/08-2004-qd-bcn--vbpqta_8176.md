# Quyết định 08/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 08/2004/QD-BCN of January 13, 2004 promulgating the criteria of the directors of the provincial/municipal Industry Services
Promulgated together with this Decision are the criteria of the directors of the provincial/municipal (or provincial-level) Industry Services, which shall serve as bases for the provincial-level People’s Committees to make consideration and appointment.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
