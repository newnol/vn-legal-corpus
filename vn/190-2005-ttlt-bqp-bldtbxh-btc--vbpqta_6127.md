# Joint circular 190/2005/TTLT/BQP-BLDTBXH-BTC 

JOINT CIRCULAR
Guiding the implementation of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 92/2005/QD-TTg of April 29, 2005, on implementation of regimes towards ethnic minority armymen in Military Zones No. 7 and 9, who participated in the anti-US war and had returned home before January 10, 1982
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
