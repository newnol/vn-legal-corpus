# Quyết định 51/2004/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 51/2004/QD-BNN of October 19, 2004 promulgating the technical process for planting, tendering and exploiting Diem Truc bamboo shoots
Branch standard 04TCN 69-2004: Technical process for planting, tendering and exploiting Diem Truc bamboo shoots ( Dendrocalamus Latiflorus Munro), promulgated together with this Decision, prescribes the planting of Diem Truc bamboo for exploiting bamboo shoots with the minimum productivity of 30 tons/ha/year from the 4th year on.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
