# Decision 396/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 396/QD-TTg of April 10, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Khanh Hoa province in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises where the State continues to hold 100% of their charter capital: 7 public-utility enterprises and 14 business enterprises shall be kept intact; 1 one-member limited liability company and 2 enterprises shall have their sections equitized.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding dominant shares: 4 in 2003, 7 in 2004 and 8 in 2005.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding shares at low rates or holding no shares: 8 in 2003 and 2 in 2004.
Enterprise to be sold: 1 in 2003.- (Summary)
