# Quyết định 42/1999/QĐ-TTg

Decision No.42/1999/QD-TTg of March 10, 1999 on sending officials and employees to communes for a certain period of time as reinforcement for the work of hunger eradication and poverty alleviation there
To dispatch officials and employees of district and provincial levels to communes as reinforcement for the work of hunger eradication and poverty alleviation there. The criteria, tasks and regimes applicable to such officials and employees are specified in this Decision.- (Summary)
