# Quyết định 22/2006/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
Providing for the collection, remittance, management and use of charges and fees for management of aquatic product quality, safety and hygiene and aquatic veterinary work
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces the Finance Minister’s Decision No. 90/2000/QD-BTC of June 1, 2006, Part A (except Clauses 1, 2, 3, 4 and 25) and Part C of Decision No. 20/2000/QD-BTC of February 21, 2000, and Circular No. 116/2000/TT-BTC of December 19, 2000.- (Summary)
