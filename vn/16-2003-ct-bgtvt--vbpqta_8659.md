# Chỉ thị 16/2003/CT-BGTVT

DIRECTIVE No. 16/2003/CT-BGTVT of June 10, 2003 enhancing the formulation, distribution and use of the Communications and Transport newspaper
This Directive aims to continue formulating and developing the Communications and Transport newspaper to perform the political tasks in the new situation; especially it propagates and encourages the entire sector to successfully implement the Resolution of the Party's IXth Congress, contribute to stepping up the cause of national industrialization and modernization, continue implementing the Politburo's Directive on enhancing, renovating and raising the efficiency of the Party's leadership over the work of press and publication.- (Summary)
