# Thông tư 73/2001/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 73/2001/TT-BTC of August 30, 2001 guiding the implementation of the regime of using home-installed telephones and mobile phones for official duties by leaders of administrative agencies, public-service units, political organizations and socio-political organizations
This Circular guides the implementation of the financial management regime applicable to the use of home-installed telephones and mobile phones for official duties by leaders of administrative agencies, public-service units, political organizations and socio-political organizations.- (Summary)
