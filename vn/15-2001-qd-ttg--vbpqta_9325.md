# Quyết định 15/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 15/2001/QD-TTg of February 12, 2001 on the reorganization of Ho Chi Minh City National University
To reorganize Ho Chi Minh City National University on the basis of re-arranging its existing universities, scientific and technological research institutes, faculties, centers and attached units.
The organizational structure of Ho Chi Minh City National University includes:
- The member universities:
+ The University of Natural Sciences;
+ The University of Social Sciences and Humanities;
+ The Polytechnical College.
- The Environment and Natural Resources Institute;
- The faculties and affiliated scientific and technological research centers.
- The Office and a number of functional sections assisting the director of Ho Chi Minh City National University.
- The attached units to serve the training, scientific research and application activities.
Basing himself on the training and scientific and technological research requirements in each period, the Prime Minister shall decide the establishment, addition or readjustment of the number of member universities and scientific and technological research institutes of Ho Chi Minh City National University.
Ho Chi Minh City National University has the legal person status, is organized and operates under the Government�s Decree on the National Universities and the Regulation on organization and operation of the National Universities, promulgated by the Prime Minister.-
