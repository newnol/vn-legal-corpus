# Quyết định 63/2003/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 63/2003/QD-BNN of May 27, 2003 on the official registration, additional registration and exceptional registration of a number of plant protection drugs into the list of those permitted for use in Vietnam
To permit the official registration of 6 types of drug into the list of plant protection drugs permitted for use in Vietnam, including 1 insecticide, 2 fungicides, 1 herbicide and 2 plant growth regulators.
To permit the additional registration of 118 types of drug into the list of plant protection drugs permitted for use in Vietnam, including 58 insecticides, 39 fungicides, 18 herbicides, 1 plant growth regulator, 1 rodenticide and 1 termiticide.
To permit the exceptional registration of 7 types of drug into the list of plant protection drugs permitted for use in Vietnam, including 2 insecticides, 1 fungicide, 1 supporting substances and 2 rodenticides.- (Summary)
