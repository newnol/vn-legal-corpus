# Decision 32/2006/QĐ-BTM

DECISION
Supplementing Appendix No. 9 on rules of origin applicable to iron and steel products in Chapter 72 to the Trade Minister’s Decision No. 1420/2004/QD-BTM of October 4, 2004, promulgating the Regulation on grant of Vietnam’s certificates of origin - form D, for enjoyment of preferences under the Agreement on Common Effective Preferential Tariffs (CEPT) Scheme for establishment of the ASEAN Free Trade Area (AFTA)
This Decision shall take effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
