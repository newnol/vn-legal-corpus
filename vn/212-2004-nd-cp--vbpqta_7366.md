# Decree 212/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 212/2004/ND-CP of December 24, 2004 adjusting administrative boundaries and establishing wards and communes of Vung Tau city and Chau Duc district, Ba Ria-Vung Tau province
In Vung Tau city:
To transfer 127.78 ha of land and 712 people of Ward 1 to Ward 2 for management.
To establish Thang Tam ward on the basis of 251.5 ha of land and 13,410 people of Ward 2.
To transfer 2.02 ha of land of Ward 8 to Ward 10, and 38.15 ha of land of Ward 10 to Ward 8, for management.
To establish Nguyen An Ninh ward on the basis of 411.13 ha of land and 8,217 people of Ward 8.
To establish Rach Dua ward on the basis of 325.77 ha of land and 14,414 people of Ward 10.
In Chau Duc district:
To establish Bau Chinh commune on the basis of 1,134.46 ha of land and 3,544 people of Ngai Giao township, and 849.32 ha of land and 2,985 people of Kim Long commune.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
