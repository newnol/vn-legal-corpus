# Quyết định 16/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 16/2004/QD-TTg of February 5, 2004 on financial support for households with two or more members who cannot take care of themselves due to consequences of toxic chemicals used by the U.S. in Vietnam war
Under this Decision, the levels of financial support of VND 200,000/household/month, VND 300,000/household/month or VND 400,000/household/month shall be rendered to households respectively with two, three, or four or more members, who cannot take care of themselves due to consequences of toxic chemicals used by the U.S. in Vietnam war.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
