Appendix
MAJOR OBJECTIVES AND CONTENTS OF THE KEY STATE-LEVEL SCIENTIFIC AND TECHNOLOGICAL PROGRAM IN THE 2001-2005 FIVE-YEAR PERIOD: "Scientific research in and development of information and communication technologies", Code: KC.01
(Issued together with Decision No. 16/2001/QD-BKHCNMT of June 11, 2001 of the Minister of Science, Technology and Environment)
a/ Objectives:
- To build scientific and technological potentials for use as basis for receiving, adapting, mastering, innovating and developing information and communication technologies.
- To develop oriented basic technologies with a view to supporting the import, transfer and development of technologies in the fields of electronics, information and communication technologies.
- To apply advanced technologies in the fields of electronic, information and communication technologies in service of socio-economic development, security and defense.
b/ Major contents:
- To conduct basic research for use as basis for technologies of manufacturing new electronic components such as nano-microelectronics; nano-optoelectronics; ASIC technology; photonic reception-transmission and switching technologies.
- To research in the wider use of LINUX operating system; to build and use widely on an experimental basis parallel processing systems; simulation technology; to secure information and computer network safety; to identify voices and pictures; multi-media man-machine interface; virtual reality and 3D technology; knowledge technology (AI), CAD, GIS and DVD.
- To develop telecommunications network on the basis of IP technology; the second-generation Internet; satellite information technology; VINASAT satellite end-terminals; digital radio and television broadcasting technology; the third-generation mobile communication up to IMT 2000 standard; to increase the access speed on the basis of photo-electronics integration; to develop software technology.-
