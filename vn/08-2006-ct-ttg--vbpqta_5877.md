# Directive 08/2006/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
Intensifying urgent measures to preclude the illegal destruction, burning and exploitation of forests
With this Directive, the Prime Minister instructs all levels, branches and localities to take urgent measures to rectify shortcomings and weaknesses in forest management and protection, re-establish disciplines and well protect existing natural forest areas.- (Summary)
