# Decision 83/2006/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
On the preferential allowance regime applicable to persons doing hydrometeorological work in the natural resources and environment sector
The preferential allowance regime provided for in this Decision does not apply to those doing hydrometeorological work in units under the Defense Ministry and the Public Security Ministry.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
