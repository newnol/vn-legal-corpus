# Quyết định 24/2005/QĐ-BTS

DECISION
Promulgating the Regulation on working relations between the Fisheries Ministry and societies and associations
This Decision promulgates the Regulation on working relations between the Fisheries Ministry and Vietnam Fishery Association and Vietnam Association for Fisheries Processing and Export.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
