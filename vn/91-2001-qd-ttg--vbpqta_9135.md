# Quyết định 91/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 91/2001/QD-TTg of June 8, 2001 ranking Phu Quoc Conservation Zone in Kien Giang province as Phu Quoc National Garden
To rank Phu Quoc Conservation Zone in Phu Quoc island district, Kien Giang province, as Phu Quoc National Garden.
To assign Kien Giang People�s Committee to directly manage Phu Quoc National Garden.
The objectives and tasks of Phu Quoc National Garden:
- To preserve the ecological system of the tropical offshore island forests, the gene sources of rare and valuable forest animals and plants as well as the natural and unique biotope in Phu Quoc island forest as a particular feature of the forest ecological system in Me Kong river delta;
- To maintain and develop the forest vegetational coverage in order to ensure the protection function the water head forests, thus supplying fresh water in service of daily-life activities of the people and sustainable socio-economic development in Phu Quoc island district;
- To contribute to consolidating defense and strengthening the country�s Southwestern defense line.-(Summary)
