# Decision 312/TTg

DECISION No.312-TTg of May 10, 1997 on the additional allocation of the 1997 rice export quotas to various localities and enterprises.
To increase the 1997 rice export quotas by 1,000,000 tons, of which 700,000 tons shall be allocated to provinces and enterprises, and 300,000 tons shall be kept in reserve for additional export.- (Summary)
