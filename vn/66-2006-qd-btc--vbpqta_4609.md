# Decision 66/2006/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
Amending and supplementing the Finance Minister’s Decision No. 22/2005/QD-BTC of April 11, 2005, which promulgates levels of the fee for granting radio frequency permits and the charge for use of radio frequencies
This Decision takes effect on January 1, 2007.
Other matters which are not amended or supplemented under this Decision still comply with Decision No. 22/2005/QD-BTC.- (Summary)
