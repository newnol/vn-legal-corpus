# Quyết định 193/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 193/2003/QD-TTg of September 17, 2003 supplementing the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under the National Center for Natural Sciences and Technologies in the 2003-2005 period
The New Technology Application and Tourism Corporation (NEWTATCO) shall remain to be a State corporation engaged in business activities under the National Center for Natural Sciences and Technologies (the 2003-2005 period).
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
