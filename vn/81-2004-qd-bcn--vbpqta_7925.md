# Quyết định 81/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 81/2004/QD-BCN of August 20, 2004 transforming the Southern Coal Processing and Trading Company into Southern Coal Joint-Stock Company
To transform the Southern Coal Processing and Trading Company (an independent cost-accounting enterprise of the Vietnam Coal Corporation) into the Southern Coal Joint-Stock Company, abbreviated to SCC.
Its charter capital is VND 4,605,000,000, of which the State's equities represent 77.30% and equities sold to laborers of the company, 22.70%.
Its head-office is located at No. 49, Nam Ky Khoi Nghia avenue, District 1, Ho Chi Minh city.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette .- (Summary)
