# Quyết định 10/2004/QĐ-BNN-KHCN

DECISION No. 10/2004/QD-BNN-KHCN of April 1, 2004 promulgating branch standards in the forest industry domain
This Decision promulgates 3 branch standards in the forest industry domain, which take effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and are mentioned below:
- 04TCN 64 - 2004: Particalboard production - Safety requirements in operation;
- 04TCN 65 - 2004: Fiberboard production - Safety requirements in operation; and
- 04TCN 66 - 2004: Vietnamese woods - Names and characteristics.- (Summary)
