# Decision 110/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 110/QD-TTg of February 4, 2002 transforming the State-run Vietnam Boiler Company into a joint-stock company
To transform the State-run Vietnam Boiler Company under the Agricultural Electro-Engineering and Irrigation Corporation, the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development, into Vietnam Boiler Joint-Stock Company.
Its head office: Quarter 3A, Dong Anh district township, Dong Anh district, Hanoi.
Its charter capital is VND 12 billion, of which the State equities represent 20%, equities sold to the laborers inside the enterprise, 70%, and equities sold to the outsiders, 10%.
Vietnam Boiler Joint-Stock Company is engaged in the production and business lines prescribed by law.- (Summary)
