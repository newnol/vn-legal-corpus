# Nghị định 77/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 77/2002/ND-CP of October 4, 2002 adjusting the administrative boundaries for the expansion of Cao Bang provincial capital, Cao Bang province
To merge De Tham commune of Hoa An district with a land area of 1,119 ha and a population of 6,287 into Cao Bang provincial capital.
After the adjustment of its administrative boundaries, Cao Bang provincial capital shall have a land area of 5,523 ha and a population of 47,272, comprising 8 administrative units. Hoa An district shall have a land area of 65,648 ha and a population of 63,350, comprising 24 administrative units.- (Summary)
