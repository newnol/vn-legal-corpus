# Quyết định 25/2003/QĐ-BNV

DECISION No. 25/2003/QD-BNV of June 10, 2003 permitting the establishment of Vietnam Association of Laboratories
To permit the establishment of Vietnam Association of Laboratories, which is organized and operates under the Association's Charter approved by the Minister of the Interior, and is placed under the State management by the Ministry of Science and Technology.
The Association shall self-finance its operation and facilities.- (Summary)
