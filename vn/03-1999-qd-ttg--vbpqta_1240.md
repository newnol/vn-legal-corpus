# Decision 03/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 03/1999/QD-TTg of January 8, 1999 on the establishment of the Center for Quality Control of Measurement and Topographical Products under the General Land Administration
To establish the Center for Quality Control of Measurement and Topographical Products under the General Land Administration on the basis of the Board for Management of Measurement and Topographical Works under the Center for Land Administration Information and Archives, and rearrangement of the existing personnel of the General Land Administration.
The Center is a non-business unit having revenues, legal person status and its own seal and bank accounts.- (Summary)
