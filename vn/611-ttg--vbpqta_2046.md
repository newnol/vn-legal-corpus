# Quyết định 611-TTg

Decision No. 611-TTg of September 4, 1996 of the Prime Minister on the regimes of allowance for workers and employees in the following special branches in enterprises:
- Quantitative dietary
- Expenditures on fresh water for daily life
- The air safety reward regime
- Allowances for divers to the depth of 3 meters and more.- (Summary)
