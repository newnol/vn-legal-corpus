# Quyết định 55/2003/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 55/2003/QD-BNN of April 9, 2003 temporarily issuing norms of capital construction investment capital for works of the agriculture and rural development service
To temporarily issue norms on capital construction investment capital for works of the agriculture and rural development service in some regions as follows:
- Norm of investment capital spent in the capital construction of irrigation works in service of rice field irrigation.
- Norm of investment capital spent on the capital construction of forestry works.- (Summary)
