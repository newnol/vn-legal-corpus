# Thông tư 01/2004/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 01/2004/TT-BTC of January 7, 2004 guiding the allocation, use and settlement of funding for the election of deputies to the People's Councils of various levels, the 2004-2009 tenure
Under this Circular, funding for the election of deputies to the People's Councils of various levels, the 2004-2009 tenure, shall be covered by the central budget and must be strictly managed and efficiently used for the right purposes according to the prescribed regimes.
This Circular also specifies the spending contents as well as funding allocation, execution and settlement.
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and applies to the election of deputies to the People's Councils of various levels, the 2004-2009 tenure.- (Summary)
