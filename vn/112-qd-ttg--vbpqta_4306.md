# Decision 112/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Establishing Phu Yen University under the People’s Committee of Phu Yen province
Under this Decision, which takes effect on the date of its signing, Phu Yen University is established on the basis of Phu Yen Teachers’ Training College and Phu Yen Economic-Technical Secondary School.- (Summary)
