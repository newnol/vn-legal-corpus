# Decree 117/2006/NĐ-CP

DECREE
On the organization and operation of the Industrial Inspectorate
Industrial Inspectorate is a state inspectorate and functions to conduct administrative and specialized inspection within the scope of state management of industry according to the provisions of law.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
