# Joint circular 105/2005/TTLT/BNV-BTC

JOINT CIRCULAR
Guiding the application of the common minimum wage level to officials, public servants and employees in state agencies, non-business units of the State, political organizations and socio-political organizations
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."
The wages and allowances for the subjects defined in this Joint Circular apply as from October 1, 2005.- (Summary)
