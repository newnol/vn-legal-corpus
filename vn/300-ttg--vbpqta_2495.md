# Decision 300/TTg

DECISION No.300-TTg of May 8, 1997 on the appointment of members of the National Internet Coordinating Board.
To appoint Mr. Pham Gia Khiem, Minister of Science, Technology and Environment, as Chairman, and Mr. Mai Liem Truc, the General Director of the General Department of Post as Vice Chairman of the Board; six other members are the Minister of Education and Training, a Vice Minister of the Interior, a Vice Minister of Culture and Information, the Director of the National Center for Natural Sciences and Technologies; a Vice Minister of Science, Technology and Environment and the Vice Chairman of the Steering Committee for the National Program of Information Technology.- (Summary)
