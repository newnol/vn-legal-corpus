# Thông tư 58/1998/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No.58/1998/TT-BTC of May 11, 1998 guiding the amendments and supplements to Circular No.19-TC/TCT of March 16, 1995 guiding the implementation of Decree No.193-CP of December 29, 1994 of the Government on the registration fee
- To supplement Section I of Circular No.19-TC/TCT on the fee for registration of houses and/or land which are used as working offices of administrative and non-business agencies;
- To supplement Point 2a, Section III of Circular No.19-TC/TCT on the fee for registration of automobiles and/or motorbikes of the units attached to the Ministry of Defense;
- To amend and replace two set forms of registration fee declarations issued together with Circular No.19-TC/TCT.- (Summary)
