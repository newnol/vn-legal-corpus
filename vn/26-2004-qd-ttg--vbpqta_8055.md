# Quyết định 26/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 26/2004/QD-TTg of March 1, 2004 establishing the inter-branch working group for APEC 2006
To establish the inter-branch working group for APEC 2006 for coordination of activities of ministries and branches in research and proposal of plans and measures regarding contents, personnel and logistics in preparation for the hosting of informal summit of economic leaders and relevant conferences of APEC 2006 in Vietnam.
The group is composed of a leader of the Ministry for Foreign Affairs as its head, three departmental-level officials of the Government Office, the Ministry for Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Trade as its deputy-heads, and officials of departmental or equivalent level of ministries and branches being members of the National Committee for International Economic Cooperation, as its members.
The group starts its operation as from the effective date of this Decision and shall be dissolved when Vietnam National Committee for APEC 2006 is set up.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Sumamry)
