# Official dispatch 3399/VPCP-NN

OFFICIAL DISPATCH No. 3399/VPCP-NN OF JUNE 21, 2002 CORRECTING THE PRECIOUS AND RARE WILD PLANTS AND ANIMALS LIST PROMULGATED TOGETHER WITH THE GOVERNMENT�S DECREE No. 48/2002/ND-CP OF APRIL 22, 2002
The precious and rare wild plants and animals list promulgated together with the Government�s Decree No.48/2002/ND-CP of April 22, 2002 amending and supplementing the list of precious and rare forest plants and animals and the regime of management and protection thereof, issued together with Decree No.18/HDBT of January 17, 1992 of the Council of Ministers contains a number of errors due to the negligence in editing.
The Government Office, thereby, sends to the concerned agencies the already corrected list of precious and rare wild plants and animals in replacement of that already promulgated together with April 22, 2002 Decree No.48/2002/ND-CP.
The Government Office would like to apologize for the said errors.
For the Minister-Director of the Government Office Deputy Director NGUYEN CONG SU
