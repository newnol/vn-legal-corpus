# Chỉ thị 01/2004/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE No. 01/2004/CT-TTg of January 2, 2004 on reorganizing the inter-provincial passenger transportation by car
Under this Directive, the Prime Minister instructs some relevant ministries, provincial/municipal People's Committees as well as organizations and individuals engaged in inter-provincial passenger transportation by car to perform some specific tasks and coordinate their activities in order to speedily restore order and enhance the State management effectiveness in the domain of inter-provincial passenger transportation by car.- (Summary)
