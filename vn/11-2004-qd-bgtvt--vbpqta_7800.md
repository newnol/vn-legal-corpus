# Quyết định 11/2004/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION No. 11/2004/QD-BGTVT of July 14, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on management, administration and use of the information system in service of the management of inter-provincial passenger car transport activities
The information system in service of the management of inter-provincial passenger car transport activities shall be built as a web integrated in the website of Vietnam Road Administration, with the address of http://www.vra.gov.vn. Vietnam Road Administration and the provincial/municipal Transport Services and Services of Traffic and Public Works shall assign their technicians to update information to the system and take responsibility for such information's truthfulness.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
