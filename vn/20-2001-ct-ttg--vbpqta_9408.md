# Chỉ thị 20/2001/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE No. 20/2001/CT-TTg of September 11, 2001 on enhancing and raising the effect of the execution of civil judgments
In order to rectify and continue raising the effectiveness and effect of the execution of civil judgments, the Prime Minister gives the following instructions:
- The Ministry of Justice shall have to expeditionsly work out measures to consolidate and perfect the organizational system of civil judgment-executing agencies, complete their payroll and increase the quality of judgment executioners; enhance the direction, inspection and handling of violations, promptly guide the settlement of troubles arising in the execution of civil judgments; work out plans on assigning localities the responsibility to execute civil judgments;
- The presidents of the People�s Committees of various levels shall have to directly organize and instruct the execution of civil judgments in their respective localities;
- Agencies and organizations, including State agencies, shall have to voluntarily execute judgments, if they fail to do it, the judgment-executing agencies shall apply measures as prescribed by law;
The Prime Minister also directs the concerned ministries and branches to propagate and disseminate the legislation on civil judgment execution; and the creation of necessary conditions for execution of civil judgment.- (Summary)
