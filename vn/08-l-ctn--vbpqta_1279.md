# Order 08/L-CTN

Order No. 08/L-CTN of December 11, 1998 promulgating the Ordinance amending Article 21 of the Ordinance on preferential treatment of revolutionaries, fallen heroes and their families, war invalids, sick soldiers, resistance war activists and people with meritorious services to the revolution
This Ordinance was passed on December 1 st , 1998 by the X th National Assembly Standing Committee.- (Summary)
