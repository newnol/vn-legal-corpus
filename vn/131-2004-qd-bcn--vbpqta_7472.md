# Quyết định 131/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 131/2004/QD-BCN of November 12, 2004 transforming the Southern Agricultural Machinery Company into Southern Agricultural Machinery Limited Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Southern Agricultural Machinery Limited Company (Vikyno Co. Ltd.) is headquartered in Population Quarter 1, Binh Da ward, Bien Hoa city, Dong Nai province, and has a charter capital of VND 74,336,702,982.- (Summary)
