# Quyết định 32/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 32/2001/QD-TTg of March 12, 2001 on experimenting the reforms of procedures for the assignment of land and the granting of land-use-right certificates according to "one-door" mechanism in Dong Ha provincial town and Cam Lo district, Quang Tri province
The Prime Minister permits the experimental reform of administrative procedures according to "one-door" mechanism for the assignment of land and the granting of certificates of the right to use urban land to households and individuals in Dong Ha provincial town, and certificates of the right to use agricultural, forestry and rural land to households and individuals in Cam Lo district, Quang Tri province.- (Summary)
