# Decision 129/2003/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 129/2003/QD-TTg of June 26, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under Vietnam Television Station till 2005
To transform Vietnam Television Technology Development Investment Company (VTC), a business enterprise with 100% State capital under Vietnam Television Station, into an independent-accounting enterprise under the Ministry of Post and Telecommunications.
To transform Vietnam Television Film Corporation (VFC), a public-utility enterprise, into the Television Film Production Center, a non-business unit with revenues.- (Summary)
