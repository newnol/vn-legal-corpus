# Decision 05/2002/QD-BKHCN

DECISION No. 05/2002/QD-BKHCN OF OCTOBER 30, 2002 ANNULLING VIETNAMESE STANDARDS
THE MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY
Pursuant to the Government’s Decree No. 22/CP of May 22, 1993 on the tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment (now the Ministry of Science and Technology);
Pursuant to the Ordinance on Goods Quality of December 24, 1999;
At the proposal of the General Director of Standardization, Metrology and Quality Control,
DECIDES:
Article 1.- To annul the following 6 Vietnamese standards:
1. TCVN 2073 - 78 Light petroleum product – Determination of octane value by research method
2. TCVN 5837 – 1994 Pepper – Technical requirements
3. TCVN 5484 – 1991 Seasonings – Determination of acid-insoluble ash
4. TCVN 5486 – 1991 Seasonings – Determination of non-volatile ether extract
5. TCVN 5538 – 1991 Powder milk – Technical requirements
6. TCVN 5539 – 1991 Sweetened condensed milk-Technical requirements.
Article 2.- This Decision takes implementation effect 15 days after its signing.
For the Minister of Science and Technology Vice Minister BUI MANH HAI
