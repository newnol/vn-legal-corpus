# Nghị định 69/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 69/2003/ND-CP of June 13, 2003 detailing and guiding the implementation of Ordinance No. 01/2002/PL-UBTVQH11 of October 4, 2002 which amends Articles 22 and 23 of the Ordinance on preferential treatment of revolutionary activists, fallen heroes and their families, war invalids, diseased soldiers, resistance war activists and people with meritorious services to the Revolution
This Decree guides the subjects entitled to allowances and allowance regimes for people with meritorious services to the Revolution.- (Summary)
