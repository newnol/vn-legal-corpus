# Decree 29/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 29/2002/ND-CP of March 22, 2002 adjusting the administrative boundaries for the establishment of Nui Sam ward of Chau Doc provincial capital and Phu Hoa district township, and renaming Phu Hoa commune as Phu Thuan commune, Thoai Son district, An Giang province
To establish Nui Sam ward of Chau Doc provincial capital on the basis of a land area of 1,297 ha and a population of 21,241 of Vinh Te commune. After the adjustment of administrative boundaries, Vinh Te commune shall have a land area of 3,121 ha and a population of 5,172.
To establish Phu Hoa district township of Thoai Son district on the basis of a land area of 523 ha and a population of 9,033 of Phu Hoa commune; and a land area of 220 ha and a population of 2,056 of Vinh Trach commune. After the adjustment of administrative boundaries, Vinh Trach commune shall have a land area of 1,881 ha and a population of 16,725; Phu Hoa commune shall have a land area of 3,013 ha and a population of 9,816.
To rename Phu Hoa commune as Phu Thuan commune, Thoai Son district.- (Summary)
