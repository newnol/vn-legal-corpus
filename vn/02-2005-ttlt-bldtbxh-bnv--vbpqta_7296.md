# Thông tư liên tịch 02/2005/TTLT/BLĐTBXH-BNV

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 02/2005/TTLT-BLDTBXH-BNV of January 5, 2005 guiding the conversion from former salaries to new salaries for Managing Boards’ full-time members, Control Boards’ members and Managing Boards’ assistants in State companies under the Government’s Decree No. 205/2004/ND-CP of December 14, 2004
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, annulling Joint Circular No. 06/1998/TTLT-TCCP-BLDTBXH-BTC of October 20, 1998 of the Government Commission for Organization and Personnel (now the Ministry of Home Affairs), the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs and the Ministry of Finance.
The provisions of this Joint Circular apply as from October 1, 2004.- (Summary)
