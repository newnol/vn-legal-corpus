# Decree 91/CP

DECREE No.91-CP OF DECEMBER 18, 1995 OF THE GOVERNMENT PROMULGATING THE LIST OF COMMODITIES IN IMPLEMENTATION OF THE COMMON EFFECTIVE PREFERENTIAL TARIFFS (CEPT) OF THE ASEAN COUNTRIES FOR 1996
THE GOVERNMENT
Pursuant to the Law on Organization of the Government of September 30, 1992;
Pursuant to the Law on Import and Export Duties;
Proceeding from Resolution No.292- NQ/UBTVQH9 of November 8, 1995 of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly regarding Vietnam's import duty reduction program in furtherance of the Common Effective Preferential Tariffs (CEPT) of the ASEAN countries;
At the proposal of the Minister of Finance,
DECREES
Article 1.- To promulgate together with this Decree the List of commodities and their tax rates in implementation of the Common Effective Preferential Tariffs (CEPT) of ASEAN for 1996.
Article 2.- The List of commodities and their tax rates specified in Article 1 of this Decree shall be applicable only when these commodities are accompanied with certificates of origin issued by the exporting country(ies) as provided for by the CEPT.
Article 3.- This Decree takes effect from January 1st, 1996. The Ministers, the Heads of the ministerial-level agencies, the Heads of the agencies attached to the Government and the Presidents of the People's Committees of the provinces and cities directly under the Central Government shall have to implement this Decree.
On behalf of the Government
For the Prime Minister
Deputy Prime Minister
