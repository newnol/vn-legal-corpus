# Chỉ thị 28/1999/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE No. 28/1999/CT-TTg of September 24, 1999 reviewing the implementation of wage and social allowance policy
In order to correctly assess the real situation of the current wage and social allowance policy, thus creating basis for the further study of the reform of wage policy in the spirit of the Resolution of the 7 th plenum of the Party Central Committee, VIII th Congress, the Prime Minister requests the ministers, the heads of the ministerial-level agencies, the heads of the agencies attached to the Government and the presidents of the People�s Committees of the provinces and centrally-run cities to direct the implementation of the following:
- Reviewing and assessing the implementation of wage and social allowance policy from 1993 up to present.
- Realizing the regime of regularly reporting on wages and incomes: To study the adjustment and supplement of the wage and social allowance regime, the Prime Minister requests a number of agencies and units to biannually and annually report data on wages and incomes. In this regard, Hanoi capital, Ho Chi Minh City, Hai Phong and Da Nang cities and Dong Nai province shall each select 3 foreign-invested enterprises on a permanent basis, which have been operating in a stable manner and have per-capita incomes of under 700,000 VND/month, from 700,000 VND/month to under 1,500,000 VND/month and 1,500,000 VND/month and more.- (Summary)
