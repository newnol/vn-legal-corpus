# Quyết định 148/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 148/2004/QD-BCN of December 1, 2004 transforming Song Cong Diesel Company into Song Cong Diesel Limited Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Song Cong Diesel Limited Company (Disoco.Ltd.) is headquartered in Luong Chau ward, Song Cong town, Thai Nguyen province, and has a charter capital of VND 351,387 million.- (Summary)
