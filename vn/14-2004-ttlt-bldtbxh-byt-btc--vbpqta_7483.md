# Thông tư liên tịch 14/2004/TTLT/BLĐTBXH-BYT-BTC

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 14/2004/TTLT/BLDTBXH-BYT-BTC of November 8, 2004 guiding the implementation of the regime for resistance war activists and their offsprings affected with toxic chemicals used by the U.S. in the Vietnam war
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces Joint Circular No. 17/2000/TTLT/BLDTBXH-BTC of July 5, 2000 of the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs and the Ministry of Finance, and Circular No. 02/2001/TTLT-BYT-BLDTBXH of February 12, 2001 of the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs and the Ministry of Health.- (Summary)
