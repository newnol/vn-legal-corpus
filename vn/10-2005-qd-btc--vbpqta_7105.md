# Quyết định 10/2005/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 10/2005/QD-BTC of February 2, 2005 publicizing the list of issuance-underwriting members and issuing agents for Government bonds, Government-underwritten bonds and local administration bonds in 2005
The list issued together with this Decision includes 7 commercial banks and 9 securities companies.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
