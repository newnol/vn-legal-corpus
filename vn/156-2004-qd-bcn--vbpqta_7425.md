# Quyết định 156/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 156/2004/QD-BCN of December 3, 2004 on transforming Hung Yen Plastic Company into Hung Yen Plastic Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Hung Yen Plastic Joint-Stock Company, HPC, has a charter capital of VND 17,643,900,000, of which the State’s equities represent 80.69% and equities sold to other subjects, 19.31%.
Its head-office is located 115 Nguyen Thien Thuat street, Hung Yen provincial capital, Hung Yen province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
