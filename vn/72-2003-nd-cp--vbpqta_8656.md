# Nghị định 72/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 72/2003/ND-CP of June 20, 2003 splitting Hien rural district into Dong Giang and Tay Giang rural districts and Tra My rural district into Bac Tra My and Nam Tra My rural districts, Quang Nam province
To split Hien rural district into two rural districts of Dong Giang and Tay Giang.
Dong Giang rural district shall have a natural land area of 81,120 ha and a population of 20,798 with 11 dependent administrative units.
Tay Giang rural district shall have a natural land area of 90,120 ha and a population of 13,405 with 10 dependent administrative units.
To split Tra My rural district into Bac Tra My and Nam Tra My rural districts.
Bac Tra My rural district shall have a natural land area of 82,325 ha and a population of 36,386, with 12 dependent administrative units.
Nam Tra My rural district shall have a natural land area of 82,235 ha and a population of 19,876, with 10 dependent administrative units- (Summary)
