# Quyết định 162/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 162/2004/QD-BCN of December 8, 2004 transforming An Phu Footwear Enterprise of An Lac Footwear Company into An Phu Footwear Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, An Phu Footwear Joint-Stock Company is headquartered in Quarter 5, Tran Quang Dieu ward, Phu Tai Industrial Park, Quy Nhon city, Binh Dinh province.
Its charter capital is VND 4,215 million, 30% of which to be held by the State, 63.38% sold to its laborers and 6.62% to outsiders.- (Summary)
