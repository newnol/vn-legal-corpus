# Quyết định 38/2002/QĐ-BKHCNMT

DECISION No. 38/2002/QD-BKHCNMT of July 8, 2002 promulgating the provisional Regulation on the working modes of the Scientific and Technological Advisory Council for selection of organizations and individuals to take charge of State-level scientific and technological subjects in the 2001-2005 period
Issued together with this Decision was the provisional Regulation on working modes of Scientific and Technological Advisory Council for selection of organizations and individuals to take charge of State-level scientific and technological subjects in the 2001-2005 period.
This Regulation replaces the Regulation issued together with Decision No. 42/2001/QD-BKHCNMT of July 18, 2001 of the Minister of Science, Technology and Environment.- (Summary)
