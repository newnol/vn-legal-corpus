# Quyết định 62/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 62/2003/QD-TTg of April 21, 2003 establishing the State-level Scientific Council for perpetual maintenance and preservation of President Ho Chi Minh’s body
The State-level Scientific Council for perpetual maintenance and preservation of President Ho Chi Minh’s body shall be composed of Prof. Dr. Do Nguyen Phuong, member of the Party Central Committee and director of its Commission for Sciences and Education, as its chairman and 11 members being representatives of the concerned agencies.
The Scientific Council is tasked to provide the Prime Minister with scientific advice to perpetually and safely maintain and preserve President Ho Chi Minh’s body.- (Summary)
