# Decision 21/2000/QD-TTg

THE PRIME MINISTER
DECISION No.21/2000/QD-TTg of February 16, 2000 on the appointment of bidders for dike construction and consolidation
To ensure the fulfillment of the 2000 plan on dike construction and embankment before the rainy and flood reason, pending the promulgation of legal documents guiding the implementation of the Government�s Decree No.52/1999/ND-CP of July 8, 1999 and Decree No.88/1999/ND-CP of September 1 st , 1999, the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development and the People�s Committees of the provinces and cities having dikes shall appoint bidders for dike construction or consolidation as follows:
- To appoint bidders for the survey, design and/or construction consultancy with regard to regular dike consolidation works;
- To appoint bidders for dike construction or consolidation works with investment capital of under 500 million dong;
- To appoint bidders for dike construction or consolidation works, employing public labor.-
