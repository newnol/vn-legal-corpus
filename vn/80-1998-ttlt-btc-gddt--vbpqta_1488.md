# Thông tư liên tịch 80/1998/TTLT/BTC-GDĐT

THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE - THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION AND TRAINING
JOINT CIRCULAR No. 80/1998/TTLT/BTC-GDDT of June 13, 1998 providing additional guidance on Circular No. 36/TTLT of June 23, 1997 regarding the levels of expenses for the preparation of exam questions and the organization of exams for selection of brilliant pupils and graduate exams
This Circular provides additional guidance on the levels of expenses for inspection activities conducted before, during and after the exams.-
