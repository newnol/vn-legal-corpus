# Nghị định 20/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 20/2004/ND-CP of January 12, 2004 on the establishment of wards and communes of Hoi An provincial town and Dai Loc and Que Son rural districts, Quang Nam province
To establish Cam Chau ward of Hoi An provincial town on the basis of the whole land area of 570.30 ha and the population of 8,610 of Cam Chau commune.
To establish Cua Dai ward of Hoi An provincial town on the basis of a land area of 314.07 ha and a population of 4,875 of Cam An commune.
To establish Cam An ward of Hoi An provincial town on the basis of the remaining land area of 290.46 ha and the population of 4,765 of Cam An commune.
To establish Dai Hung commune of Dai Loc rural district on the basis of a land area of 8,869 ha and a population of 6,821 of Dai Lanh commune.
To split Que Xuan commune of Que Son rural district into Que Xuan 1 and Que Xuan 2 communes, which shall respectively have the land areas and the populations of 821 ha and 9,292 and 1,508 ha and 6,725.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
