# Quyết định 125/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 125/2001/QD-TTg of August 22, 2001 adjusting the Tay Ninh list of areas where investment is encouraged
To adjust the list of areas where investment is encouraged, issued together with the Government�s Decree No. 24/2000/ND-CP of July 31, 2000 detailing the implementation of the Law on Foreign Investment in Vietnam, as follows:
- To transfer all districts of Tay Ninh province from List B (List of areas meeting with difficult socio-economic conditions) to List A (List of areas meeting with especially difficult socio-economic conditions).
- To add Tay Ninh provincial capital to List B.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its signing.- (Summary)
