# Directive 05/2007/CT-BXD

DIRECTIVE
On further raising the quality of reorganization, renewal and development of state enterprises under the Construction Ministry in the 2007-2010 period
The Minister of Construction requests chairmen of managing boards and general directors of corporations or parent companies operating after the parent-subsidiary company model under the Ministry to perform several tasks in order to further raise the quality of reorganization, renewal and development of state enterprises under the Construction Ministry in the 2007-2010 period.- (Summary)
