# Quyết định 46/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 46/2004/QD-BCN of June 11, 2004 on renaming of Phuong Dong Joint Stock Company in Article 2 of the Industry Minister's Decision No. 226/2003/QD-BCN of December 19, 2003 on transformation of Phuong Dong company into Phuong Dong Joint Stock Company
Under this Decision, Phuong Dong Joint Stock Company is renamed Phuong Dong Production and Trade Joint Stock Company.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
