# Resolution 22/2007/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
On the 5-year (2006-2010) land use plan of Quang Ngai province
The People’s Committee of Quang Ngai province shall organize the implementation of the land use plan in association with socio-economic development objectives as well as defense, security and eco-environmental protection tasks.- (Summary)
