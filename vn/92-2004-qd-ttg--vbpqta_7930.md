# Quyết định 92/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 92/2004/QD-TTg of May 25, 2004 on the establishment of the Technology University under the Hanoi National University
To establish the Technology University on the basis of the Technology Faculty and the Center for Mechanics Training and Fostering Cooperation under Hanoi National University.
The Technology University is a tertiary training establishment under Hanoi National University, has the legal person status, its own seal and bank accounts.
It is tasked to train human resource of university and postgraduate degrees and foster talents in the fields of science and technology; research into, and apply sciences and technologies to meet the socio-economic development demand.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
