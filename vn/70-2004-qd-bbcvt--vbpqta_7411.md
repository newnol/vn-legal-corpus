# Decision 70/2004/QĐ-BBCVT

DECISION No. 70/2004/QD-BBCVT of December 9, 2004 on the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Office
The Office of the Ministry of Post and Telematics shall assist the Minister in performing the function of advising and coordinating with agencies and units of the Ministry in, implementing the Minister’s decisions; act as the main body in working with ministries, central branches and localities under the Minister’s direction; and conduct the Ministry’s administrative work.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces Decision No. 17/2003/QD-BBCVT of February 18, 2003.- (Summary)
