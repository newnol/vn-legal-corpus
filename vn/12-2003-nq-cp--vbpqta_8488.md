# Resolution 12/2003/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION No. 12/2003/NQ-CP of October 6, 2003 on the Government's September 2003 regular meeting
On September 29-30, 2003, the Government held its September regular meeting, where:
- It heard the Finance Minister's report on the performance of the 2003 budget tasks and the 2004 budget estimate allocation; and the Planning and Investment Minister's report on evaluating the 2003 development investment situation and the 2004 plans.
- It heard a draft decree on the planning work, presented by the Minister of Planning and Investment; and the report on issues related to the decree's contents with divergent opinions, presented by the Minister-Director of the Government Office.
- It heard the Justice Minister's report on the results of reviewing the World Trade Organization (WTO)'s agreements and comparing them with Vietnam's current law provisions.
- It heard a draft decree on organization and operation of youth volunteers as well as policies towards them, presented by the Minister of Home Affairs; and the report summing up the Government members' opinions on the draft decree, presented by the Minister-Director of the Government Office.
- It heard a draft ordinance on national reserves, presented by the Finance Minister; and the report summing up the Government members' opinions on the draft ordinance, presented by the Minister-Director of the Government Office.
- It considered the report on the performance of socio-economic development plans in September and the first 9 months of 2003, presented by the Ministry of Planning and Investment.- (Summary)
