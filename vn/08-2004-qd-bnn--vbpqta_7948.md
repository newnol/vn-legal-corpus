# Decision 08/2004/QD-BNN

DECISION No. 08/2004/QD-BNN-TY of March 30, 2004 on the application of Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) principles and standards of the Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN)
To deploy the application of Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) principles and standards of the Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN) to all veterinary drug-manufacturing establishments. Such application shall serve as one of basic criteria for consideration and granting of Marketing Authorization (MA).
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
