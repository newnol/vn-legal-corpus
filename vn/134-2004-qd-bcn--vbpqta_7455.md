# Quyết định 134/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 134/2004/QD-BCN of November 16, 2004 amending Clause 1 and Clause 3, Article 1 of the Industry Minister's Decision No. 82/2004/QD-BCN of August 24, 2004 on transforming Thanh Hoa Serpentine and Chemicals Company into Thanh Hoa Serpentine and Fertilizer Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, the joint-stock company shall have a charter capital of VND 3,811,300,000, of which equities sold to laborers of the company represent 93.83% and equities sold to other subjects, 6.17%.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
