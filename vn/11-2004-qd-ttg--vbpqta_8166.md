# Quyết định 11/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 11/2004/QD-TTg of January 16, 2004 assigning the task of doctorate training to the Law University of Ho Chi Minh City
This Decision assigns the task of doctorate training to the Law University of Ho Chi Minh City with specific specialized training disciplines to be considered and prescribed by the Ministry of Education and Training.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
