# Quyết định 12/2004/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 12/2004/QD-BTC of January 9, 2004 issuing the form of invoice for sale of assets confiscated for requisition into the State’s funds
Issued together with this Decision is the form No. 01/TSSQ-3L.04 of invoice for sale of assets confiscated for requisition into the State’s funds, which replaces the form No. 01/TSTT-3L of invoice for sale of assets confiscated for requisition into the State’s funds, issued together with the Finance Minister’s Decision No. 29/2000/QD-BTC of February 29, 2000.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
