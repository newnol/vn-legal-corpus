# Decision 32/2007/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
On the provision of loans to ethnic minority households meeting with exceptional difficulties for production development
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and annuls Decision No. 166/2001/QD-UBDT of September 5, 2001, of the Minister-Director of the Committee for Ethnicities and Mountainous Areas, and Joint Circular No. 912/2001/TTLT/UBDTMN/BTC/BKHDT of November 16, 2001, of the Committee for Ethnicities and Mountainous Areas, the Finance Ministry and the Planning and Investment Ministry.- (Summary)
