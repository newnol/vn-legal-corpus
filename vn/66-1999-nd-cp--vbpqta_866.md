# Nghị định 66/1999/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 66/1999/ND-CP of August 7, 1999 detailing the implementation of the Ordinance which amends Article 21 of the Ordinance on the preferential treatment of revolutionary activists, fallen combatants and their families, war invalids, war diseased, activists in the resistance wars and people with meritorious contributions to the revolution
The Decree stipulates in detail the allowance regime applicable to resistant war activists.- (Summary)
