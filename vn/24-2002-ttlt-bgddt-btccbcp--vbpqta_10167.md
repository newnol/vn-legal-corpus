# Thông tư liên tịch 24/2002/TTLT/BGDĐT-BTCCBCP

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 24/2002/TTLT-BGDDT-BTCCBCP of April 29, 2002 guiding the recruitment of general education and pre-school education teachers
Subjects of consideration for recruitment to work as general education and pre-school education teachers are Vietnamese citizens who wish to teach in public general education or pre-school education establishments and have graduated from pedagogy departments or schools at home or abroad.
This Circular guides the principles, scope and process of recruitment consideration.- (Summary)
