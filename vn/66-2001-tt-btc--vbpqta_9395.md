# Thông tư 66/2001/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 66/2001/TT-BTC of August 22, 2001 guiding the amendment of a number of points of Circular No. 85/1998/TT-BTC of June 25, 1998 of the Ministry of Finance guiding the Regulation on financial management for Vietnam Social Insurance
This Circular guides the amendment of the regulation on the regular expenses for the management of Vietnam Social Insurance system; the allocation and use of profits earned from the investment and growth of social insurance fund.- (Summary)
