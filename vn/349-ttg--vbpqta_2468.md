# Decision 349/TTg

Decision No.349-TTg of May 28, 1997 on the new membership of the Central Steering Committee for Flood and Storm Prevention and Fight, which is now composed of a Chairman, Mr. Nguyen Cong Tan, Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development; 5 Vice Chairmen and 15 members.
This Decision replaces Article 1 of Decision No.299-TTg of May 13, 1996 of the Prime Minister.- (Summary)
