# Decision 142/TTg

DECISION No.142-TTg OF MARCH 8, 1996 OF THE PRIME MINISTER assigning the task of post-graduate training to the Applied Mechanics Institute (under the National Center for Natural Science and Technology);
The subjects of study to be included in the post-graduate training program of the Applied Mechanics Institute shall be decided by the Ministry of Education and Training, and the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment.- (Summary)
