# Decision 1174/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 1174/QD-TTg of August 31, 2001 transforming Nha Trang Garment Materials Company into a joint-stock company
To transform Nha Trang Garment Material Company under the People?s Committee of Khanh Hoa province into Nha Trang Garment Accessories Joint-Stock Company (ISE.Co).
Its head office: 02 Nguyen Thien Thuat street, Nha Trang city, Khanh Hoa province.
Its charter capital is VND 18 billion. Form of equitization: Selling 100% of the enterprise?s net capital when being equitized; of which equities sold to laborers inside the enterprise represent 70% of the charter capital, equities sold to the outsiders, 30% in the following priority order: customers being agents that sell raw materials to and buy products from the company, workers and employees in Khanh Hoa province, and other organizations and individuals outside the company.
Nha Trang Garment Accessories Joint Stock Company is engaged in the following business lines:
- Producing and dealing in zippers of various kinds and garment accessories;
- Tailoring;
- Trade and service business;
- Other business lines as prescribed by law.- (Summary)
