# Decision 361/TTg

Decision No.361-TTg of May 30, 1997 on restructuring some organizations attached to the Ministry for Foreign Affairs.
- To merge the China Department and the Northeastern Asia Department into the Asia Department No.1
- To rename the Southeast Asia-South Pacific Department into the Asia Department No.2
- To rename the Eastern-Europe-Central Asia Department into the Europe Department No.1
- To rename the Northwestern Europe Department into the Europe Department No.2.- (Summary)
