# Nghị định 97/2001/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 97/2001/ND-CP of December 17, 2001 on the establishment of Nghia Chanh and Quang Phu wards of Quang Ngai provincial capital, Quang Ngai province
To establish Nghia Chanh ward on the basis of the whole natural land area of 404 hectares and the population of 11,385 of Nghia Chanh commune.
To establish Quang Phu ward on the basis of the whole natural land area of 727 hectares and the population of 16,220 of Quang Phu commune.- (Summary)
