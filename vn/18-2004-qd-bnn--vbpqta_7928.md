# Decision 18/2004/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 18/2004/QD-BNN of July 8, 2004 promulgating the law dissemination and education program of the agriculture and rural development sector for the 2004-2007 period
The law dissemination and education program of the agriculture and rural develop-ment sector for the 2004-2007 period, issued together with this Decision, dwells on the objectives, requirements, subjects, contents and forms of law dissemination and education, as well as organization of the implementation thereof.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
