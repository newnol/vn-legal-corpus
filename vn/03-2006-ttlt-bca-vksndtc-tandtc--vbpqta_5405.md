# Joint circular 03/2006/TTLT/BCA-VKSNDTC-TANDTC

JOINT CIRCULAR
Guiding the notification, sending and supply of offender-related information and documents
This Joint Circular guides the notification, sending and supply of offender-related information and documents among public security agencies, people’s procuracies and people’s courts of all levels.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
