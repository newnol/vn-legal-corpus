# Decree 100/CP

Decree No.100-CP of September 23, 1997 on the establishment of Thanh Loi commune of Thap Muoi district and Hoa Binh commune of Tam Nong district, Dong Thap province.
To establish Thanh Loi commune of Thap Muoi district with a land area of 4,268 hectares and a population of 3,310.
To establish Hoa Binh commune of Tam Nong district with a land area of 2,892.2 hectares and a population of 2,629.- (Summary)
