# Joint circular 4/1998/TTLT/TCCP-TC-TP

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 4/1998/TTLT-TCCP-TC-TP of July 23, 1998 guiding the implementation of Decision No. 57/1998/QD-TTg of March 5, 1998 of the Prime Minister on supplements to Decision No. 160-TTg of March 15, 1996 of the Prime Minister on the regime of allowances for juridical expert witnesses
This Circular specifically guides the regime of allowances for persons who directly assist the juridical expert witnesses and persons who are assigned by the legal proceeding agencies to be present in the juridical expert testimony period.-
