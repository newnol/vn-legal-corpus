# Quyết định 175/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 175/2004/QD-TTg of October 1, 2004 establishing Da Lat Yersin People-Founded University
Under this Decision, Da Lat Yersin People-Founded University is a tertiary educational establishment operating under the Regulation on People-Founded Universities, issued together with the Prime Minister's Decision No. 86/2000/QD-TTg of July 18, 2000.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
