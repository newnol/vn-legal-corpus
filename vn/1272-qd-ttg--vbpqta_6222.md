DECISION
On assignment of the 2006 socio-economic plan targets
With this Decision, which takes effect after its signing, the Prime Minister assigns the 2006 socio-economic development plan targets to ministries, ministerial-level agencies, government-attached agencies as well as other agencies and units.- (Summary)
