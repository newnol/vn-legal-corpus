# Quyết định 103/2005/QĐ-BNV

DECISION
Approving the Charter of Vietnam Anti-Counterfeiting and Intellectual Property Protection Association of Foreign-Invested Enterprises
Vietnam Anti-Counterfeiting and Intellectual Property Protection Association of Foreign-Invested Enterprises (VACIP) is a non-governmental, non-political and non-profit organization set up on the principle of voluntarism of foreign enterprises licensed to operate in Vietnam.
VACIP aims to protect legitimate rights and interests of its members, represent them in coordinating with Vietnamese organizations and state management agencies in anti-counterfeiting and intellectual property protection and raising of foreign investment efficiency of each enterprise in Vietnam.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
