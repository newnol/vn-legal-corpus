# Nghị định 36/2000/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 36/2000/ND-CP of August 18, 2000 on the adjustment of administrative boundaries of Bac Ha district to re-found Si Ma Cai district, Lao Cai province
To re-found Si Ma Cai district on the basis of a land area of 23,148 ha and a population of 22,394 of the existing Bac Ha district. Si Ma Cai district shall comprise 13 dependent administrative units: Thao Chu Phin, Ban Me, San Chai, Lung Sui, Man Than, Can Ho, Sin Cheng, Lu Than, Quan Than San, Can Cau, Nan Sin, Nan San and Si Ma Cai communes.- (Summary)
