# Quyết định 243/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 243/2003/QD-BCN of December 30, 2003 transforming Phuc Yen Printing and Cultural Product Company into Phuc Yen Printing Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Phuc Yen Printing and Cultural Product Company, an independent cost-accounting enterprise of Vietnam Paper Corporation, shall be transformed into a joint-stock company with its English transaction name as Phuc Yen Printing Joint-Stock Company, abbreviated to PPCO.
The joint-stock company shall have a charter capital of VND 7,000,000,000, with the State's equities representing 35%; equities sold to laborers of the company, 56.5%; and equities sold to other subjects, 8.5%.
It is headquartered at Tran Phu road, Phuc Yen township, Me Linh rural district, Vinh Phuc province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
