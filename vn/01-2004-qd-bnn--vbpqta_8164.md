# Quyết định 01/2004/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 01/2004/QD-BNN of January 12, 2004 on exceptional registration of some plant protection drugs into the list of those permitted for use in Vietnam
This Decision permits the exceptional registration of 4 insecticides (Sokupi 0.36 AS; Stop 5 DD; Dibonin 5 WP and Dibaroten 5 WP; 1 fungicide (Alyrice 5 SL); 1 yellow snail-killing drug (Molucide 80 WP); and 2 growth stimulators (Gibline 20T and 3G Gia gion gion - Bean sprout - 1.5 WP) into the list of those permitted for use in Vietnam.
The import and export of the plant protection drugs mentioned in this Decision shall comply with Circular No. 62/2001/TT-BNN of June 5, 2001 of the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
