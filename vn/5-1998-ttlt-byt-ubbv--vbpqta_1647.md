# Joint circular 5/1998/TTLT/BYT-UBBV&CSTEVN

JOINT CIRCULAR No.5/1998/TTLT-BYT-UBBV & CSTEVN of March 31, 1998 guiding the transfer of the objective of malnutrition prevention and combat.
This Circular guides the principles, contents, mode and time limit for the transfer of the objective of malnutrition prevention and combat from the Committees for Child Protection and Care of the provinces and cities directly under the Central Government to the provincial/municipal Health Services.- (Summary)
