# Joint circular 05/2003/TTLT/BYT-BQP

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 05/2003/TTLT-BYT-BQP of April 29, 2003 guiding the coordination of army and civilian health units in malaria prevention and combat
This Circular guides the coordination of army health units and civilian health units (State's health units, excluding army health units) in malaria prevention and combat throughout the country. This coordination aims to well bring into play the strengths of the civilian and army health units for the care of health of the people as well as army officers and soldiers in malaria prevention and combat.- (Summary)
