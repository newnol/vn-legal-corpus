# Circular 147/2002/TT-BQP

CIRCULAR No.147/2002/TT-BQP of October 14, 2002 additionally guiding Circular No. 2546/1999/TT-BQP of September 4, 1999 which guides the implementation of the Government�s Decree No. 23/1999/ND-CP of April 15, 1999 on the regime towards armymen and officials who fought on B, C and K battle fields during the anti-US resistance war and do not have to financially support any next of kin; and armymen and officials who were ordered by the Party to stay behind in the South after the signing of the 1954 Geneva Agreement
This Circular additionally guides the subjects and time as well as the organization of implementation of the regime towards armymen and officials who fought on B, C and K battle fields during the anti-US resistance war and do not have to financially support any next of kin; and armymen and officials who were ordered by the Party to stay behind in the South after the signing of the 1954 Geneva Agreement.- (Summary)
