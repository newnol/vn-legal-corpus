# Decision 394/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 394/QD-TTg of April 10, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Binh Duong province in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises where the State holds 100% of their charter capital and their legal person status remains unchanged: 4 public-utility enterprises and 8 business enterprises.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding at least 51% of shares when shares are sold for the first time: 2 in 2003 and 2 in 2004.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding under 50% of their charter capital or holding no shares: 1 in 2003 and 9 in 2004.
Enterprise to be merged: 1 in 2003.
Enterprises to be dissolved: 2 in 2003.- (Summary)
