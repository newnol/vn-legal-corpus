# Decision 53/2007/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION
Promulgating the Regulation on publicization of the commencement and suspension of operation of railway stations
The Regulation promulgated together with this Decision provides for the conditions, order, procedures and competence to decide on publicization of the commencement and suspension of operation of railway stations; and responsibilities of concerned organizations and individuals.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
