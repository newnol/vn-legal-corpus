# Chỉ thị 104/2006/CT-BNN

DIRECTIVE
On the anti-drought work and assurance of the 2006-2007 winter-spring crop production in northern provinces
The Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development requests concerned localities and units to perform several tasks in order to take initiative in the anti-drought work and the production of the 2006-2007 winter-spring crop.- (Summary)
