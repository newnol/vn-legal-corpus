# Decree 76/CP

Decree No.76-CP of June 16, 1997 on the establishment of Tu Ky township of Tu Ky district, Hai Duong province.
To establish Tu Ky township, the district town of Tu Ky district on the basis of the whole land area of 476 hectares and a population of 6,722 of An Nhan village, Dong Ky commune and La Tinh village, Tay Ky commune.-
