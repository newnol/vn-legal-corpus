# Decision 1567/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 1567/QD-TTg of December 14, 2001 on members of the Advisory Council for National Financial and Monetary Policies
The Advisory Council for National Financial and Monetary Policies shall be composed of Mr. Nguyen Tan Dung, Deputy Prime Minister, as its Chairman; Mr. Le Duc Thuy, Governor of Vietnam State Bank, as its standing member; seven representatives of the concerned ministries, branches and eight specialists in the field of finance and banking, as its members.- (Summary )
