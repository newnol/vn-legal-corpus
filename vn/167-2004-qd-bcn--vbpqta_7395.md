# Quyết định 167/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 167/2004/QD-BCN of December 9, 2004 approving the Charter on organization and operation of Sai Gon Beer-Alcohol-Beverage Corporation after the parent company-subsidiary company model
Under the 8-chapter and 48-article Charter approved together with this Decision, Sai Gon Beer-Alcohol-Beverage Corporation (SABECO) is headquartered at No. 187 Nguyen Chi Thanh street, District 5, Ho Chi Minh city.
The charter capital of the parent company (SABECO, set up under the Industry Minister’s Decision No. 74/2003/QD-BCN of May 6, 2003), by December 31, 2003 was VND 2,950.754 billion.
Subsidiary companies are the parent company’s member units where the parent company holds dominant shares or capital contributions.
Associated companies are those where the parent company does not hold dominant shares or capital contributions, operating under the Enterprise Law or the Law on Foreign Investment in Vietnam and relevant law provisions.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
