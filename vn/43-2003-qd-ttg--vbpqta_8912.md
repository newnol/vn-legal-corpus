# Quyết định 43/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 43/2003/QD-TTg of April 1, 2003 renaming two departments of the Committee for Physical Training and Sports
To change the names of the two Departments prescribed at Points 2 and 3, Clause a, Article 3 of the Government’s Decree No. 22/2003/ND-CP of March 11, 2003 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Committee for Physical Training and Sports as follows:
- To rename the High-Achievement Physical Training and Sports Department No. I as the High-Achievement Sports Department No. I;
- To rename the High-Achievement Physical Training and Sports Department No. II as the High-Achievement Sports Department No. II.- (Summary)
