# Quyết định 109/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 109/2004/QD-BCN of October 12, 2004 transforming Dap Cau Garment Company into Dap Cau Garment Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Dap Cau Garment Joint-Stock Company, abbreviated to DAGARCO, has a charter capital of VND 10,000,000,000; of which the State's equities represent 51% and equities sold to laborers in the company, 49%.
Its head office is located in Quarter 6 Thi Cau, Bac Ninh provincial capital, Bac Ninh province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
