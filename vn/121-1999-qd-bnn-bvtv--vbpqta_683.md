# Decision 121/1999/QD-BNN-BVTV

DECISION No. 121/1999/QD-BNN-BVTV OF AUGUST 25, 1999 ON THE OFFICIAL REGISTRATION AND ADDITIONAL REGISTRATION OF A NUMBER OF PLANT PROTECTION DRUGS PERMITTED TO BE USED IN VIETNAM
THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND RURAL DEVELOPMENT
Pursuant to the Government�s Decree No. 73-CP of November 1st, 1995 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development;
Pursuant to Article 26 of the Ordinance on Plants Protection and Quarantine promulgated on February 15, 1993;
Pursuant to the Regulation on the Management of Plants Protection Drugs issued together with the Government�s Decree No. 92-CP of November 27, 1993 guiding the implementation of the Ordinance on Plants Protection and Quarantine;
At the proposals of the Director of the Department for Plants Protection and the Director of the Department for Sciences, Technology and Product Quality,
DECIDES:
Article 1.- To allow the official registration of seven kinds of plant protection drugs in the list of plant protection drugs permitted to be used in Vietnam (their list is attached herewith), including: five kinds of insecticides, one herbicide and one rodenticide.
Article 2.- To allow the additional registration of 35 kinds of plant protection drugs in the list of plant protection drugs permitted for use in Vietnam (their list is attached herewith), including: 18 kinds of insecticides, 11 kinds of fungicides, four kinds of herbicides and two plant growth regulators.
Article 3.- This Decision takes effect 15 days after its signing.
The Department for Plants Protection shall have to provide detailed guidance for the safe and effective use of the above-named plant protection drugs.
Article 4.- The Director of the Ministry�s Office, the Director of the Department for Plants Protection, the Director of the Department for Sciences, Technology and Product Quality, heads of the units under the Ministry and domestic organizations and individuals and foreigners that manage, trade in, and/or use plant protection drugs in Vietnam shall have to implement this decision.-
For the Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development Vice-Minister NGO THE DAN
