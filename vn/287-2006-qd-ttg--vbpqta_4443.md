# Decision 287/2006/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
On seniority-based allowances for retired customs officers
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”
The recalculated pension of customs officers who retired between April 1, 1993, and December 31, 2006, applies as from January 1, 2007.- (Summary)
