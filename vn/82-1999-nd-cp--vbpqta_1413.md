# Nghị định 82/1999/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 82/1999/ND-CP of August 25, 1999 on the adjustment of administrative boundaries of communes in Bac Lieu provincial capital and Hong Dan, Gia Rai districts, Bac Lieu province
To establish Vinh Trach and Vinh Trach Dong communes of Bac Lieu provincial capital on the basis of the total land area and population of Thuan Hoa commune. Vinh Trach commune covers a land area of 4,229.81 ha and has a population of 12,804. Vinh Trach Dong commune shall comprise 4,656.97 ha of a land area and a population of 9,632.
To establish Ninh Quoi A commune of Hong Dan district on the basis of a land area of 3,691 ha and a population of 14,047 of Ninh Quoi commune.
To establish Phong Thanh Dong A commune of Gia Rai district on the basis of a land area of 2,691.54 ha and a population of 15,207 of Phong Thanh Dong commune.
To establish Long Dien Dong A commune of Gia Rai district on the basis of a land area of 5,947.93 ha and a population of 15,342 of Long Dien Dong commune.- (Summary)
