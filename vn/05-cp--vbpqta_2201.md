# Decree 05/CP

DECREE No.05-CP OF JANUARY 27, 1996 OF THE GOVERNMENT DIVIDING TU LOC, NINH THANH AND KIM THI DISTRICTS OF HAI HUNG PROVINCE
- To divide Tu Loc district into Tu Ky and Gia Loc districts
- To divide Ninh Thanh district into Ninh Giang and Thanh Mien districts
- To divide Kim Thi district into Kim Dong and An Thi districts.- (summary)
