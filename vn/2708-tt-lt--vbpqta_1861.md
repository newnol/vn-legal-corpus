# Thông tư liên tịch 2708/TT-LT

Joint Circular No.2708-TT/LT of October 14, 1997 guiding the implementation of Decree No.09-CP of February 12, 1996 of the Government on the regulation on the management and use of defense and security land.
This Circular provides detailed guidance on provisions on the planning and plans for the use of defense and security land; the land measurement; the granting of land use right certificates; the land recovery; the change of the land use purposes; the land registration and statistics and other provisions.- (Summary)
