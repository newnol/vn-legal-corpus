# Quyết định 171/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 171/2004/QD-BCN of December 16, 2004 approving the Charter on organization and operation of Vietnam Ceramic and Glass Limited Company
Under its Charter issued together with this Decision, Vietnam Ceramic and Glass Limited Company, VINACEGLASS, has a charter capital of VND 20,333,581,744.
Its head-office is located at No. 20-24, Nguyen Cong Tru street, Nguyen Thai Binh ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh city.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
