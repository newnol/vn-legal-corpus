# Decision 30/2007/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Promulgating the list of administrative units (communes, wards and townships) in difficulty-hit areas for use as a basis for implementation of specific regimes and policies for socio-economic development, security and defense maintenance, hunger elimination and poverty alleviation in order to improve material and spiritual lives of people and narrow the development gap compared to other areas in the country
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
