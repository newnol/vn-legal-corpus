# Thông tư 76/2005/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR
Amending and supplementing the Finance Ministry's Circular No. 79/2004/TT-BTC of August 10, 2004, which guides the implementation of the Prime Minister's Decision No. 78/2004/QD-TTg of May 7, 2004, adjusting and supplementing a number of mechanisms and policies to speed up the construction of population clusters and lines and dwelling houses in frequently-flooded provinces in the Mekong River delta
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
