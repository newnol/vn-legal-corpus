# Quyết định 188/2003/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 188/2003/QD-TTg of September 15, 2003 setting up the National Steering Committee for Preparation and Organization of ASEAN Trade Fair 2004
To set up the National Steering Committee for Preparation and Organization of ASEAN Trade Fair 2004 (called the National Steering Committee for ASEAN Trade Fair for short), which is composed of Deputy Prime Minister Vu Khoan as its head, Trade Minister Truong Dinh Tuyen as its standing deputy head and 13 representatives from concerned ministries and central agencies as its members.
The Steering Committee is also composed of 5 sub-committees in charge of trade fair contents, information, finance, guest reception and security.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
