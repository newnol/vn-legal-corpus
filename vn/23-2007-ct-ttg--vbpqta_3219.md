# Chỉ thị 23/2007/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On enhancing the application of solutions for controlling prices and stabilizing the market in the last months of 2007 in preparation for the 2008 Lunar New Year festival
The Prime Minister requests concerned ministries, branches and localities to perform several tasks in order to control prices and stabilize the market in the last months of 2007 in preparation for the 2008 Lunar New Year festival.- (Summary)
