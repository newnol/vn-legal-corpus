# Thông tư 26/2001/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 26/2001/TT-BTC of April 19, 2001 guiding a number of points on the allocation of funding for budget-funded social organizations and socio-professional organizations
In order to carry on administrative reform in the field of finance-budget, the Ministry of Finance guides a number of points on the allocation of funding for budget-funded social organizations and socio-professional organizations. Concretely, it guides the bases for allocating funding, the allocation and final settlement of funding.- (Summary)
