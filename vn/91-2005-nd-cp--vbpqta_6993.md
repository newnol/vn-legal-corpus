# Nghị định 91/2005/NĐ-CP

DECREE
Promulgating the Regulation on naming and renaming of roads, streets and public works
The 3-chapter and 17-article Regulation provides principles, competence and procedures for deciding on the naming and renaming of roads, streets and public works in urban centers of Vietnam.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
