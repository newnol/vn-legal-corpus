# Nghị định 73/1998/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 73/1998/ND-CP of September 15, 1998 on the establishment of Hung Nguyen township of Hung Nguyen district, Nghe An province
To establish Hung Nguyen district township of Hung Nguyen district, Nghe An province with the entire land area of 702.77 hectares and the entire population of 6,735 of Hung Thai commune and a land area of 8.73 hectares and a population of 564 of Hung Dao commune.- (Summary)
