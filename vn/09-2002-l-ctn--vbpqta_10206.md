# Lệnh 09/2002/L-CTN

ORDER No. 09/2002/L-CTN of April 12, 2002 promulgating the National Assembly’s Resolution
To promulgate the Resolution of the X th National Assembly, the 11 th session. This Resolution was adopted on April 2, 2002 by the X th National Assembly at its 11 th session, comprising two parts:
- The working program of the X th National Assembly, the National Assembly’s agencies, the State President, the Government, the Supreme People’s Court, the Supreme People’s Procuracy: The appraisal of operation of these agencies during the term.
- Major tasks from now till the end of the X th National Assembly, including tasks relating to the election of deputies to the XI th National Assembly; continuing to implement the Resolution on the 2002 tasks and the Resolution on the (2001-2005) 5-year plan; preparation for the first session of the XI th National Assembly and several other tasks.- (Summary)
