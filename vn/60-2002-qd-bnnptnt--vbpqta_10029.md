# Quyết định 60/2002/QĐ-BNNPTNT

DECISION No. 60/2002/QD-BNNPTNT of July 5, 2002 promulgating the branch�s standard
Issued together with this Decision was the branch�s standard: 14TCN 122-2002: The standard for flood prevention and combat in the Red river delta.
This standard prescribes the frequency of flood prevention and combat in the Red river delta region and the designed water levels for III- or higher-grade dykes of the Red river and Thai Binh river systems.
This standard shall apply to the elaboration and approval of projects on the planning of the systems of works for flood prevention and combat in the Red river delta, including: reservoirs, dykes, and floodwater-diverting, slowing and draining works.- (Summary)
