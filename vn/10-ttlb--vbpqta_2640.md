# Joint circular 10/TTLB

CIRCULAR No. 10/TTLB of December 11, 1996 providing guidance for the implementation of Decision No.556-TTg of September 12, 1995 of the Prime Minister on amendments to Decision No.327-CT of September 15, 1995
The Circular provides guidance on the targets, objects, modes and density of afforestation; the arrangement of projects under Program 327; investment policies and mechanism of State budget capital allocation and lending.- (Summary)
