# Quyết định 261/2006/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Approving the Agreement between the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the Government of the Federal Republic of Germany on cooperation in fighting dangerous and organized crime
With this Decision, the Prime Minister assigns the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to carry out foreign-relation procedures for approval of the Agreement and notify concerned agencies of the effective date of the Agreement.- (Summary)
