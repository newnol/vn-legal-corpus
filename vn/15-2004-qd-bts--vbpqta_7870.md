# Decision 15/2004/QD-BTS

DECISION No. 15/2004/QD-BTS of June 14, 2004 promulgating branch standards
To promulgate together with this Decision four branch standards:
1. 28TCN 204:2004: Freshwater fish - Broodstock of snakehead, spot snakehead, climbing perch and snakeskin gouramy - Technical requirements
2. 28TCN 205:2004: Freshwater fish - Larvae of snakehead, spot snakehead, climbing perch and snakeskin gouramy - Technical requirements
3. 28TCN 206:2004: Freshwater fish - Fry of snakehead, spot snakehead, climbing perch and snakeskin gouramy - Technical requirements
4. 28TCN 207: 2004: Freshwater fish - Fingerling of snakehead, spot snakehead, climbing perch and snakeskin gouramy - Technical requirements
The above standards are compulsorily applicable to establishments producing and trading in fishery breeds nationwide and take effect 15 days after their publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
