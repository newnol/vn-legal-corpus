# Nghị định 116/1997/NĐ-CP

DECREE No.116/1997/ND-CP of December 22, 1997 on the establishment of Bich Dong township of Viet Yen district and Voi township of Lang Giang district, Bac Giang province.
To establish Bich Dong township in Viet Yen district on the basis of 526.95 ha of natural land area and a population of 6,243 of Bich Son commune.
To establish Voi township in Lang Giang district on the basis of 335.87 ha of natural land area and a population of 4,560 of Yen My commune.- (Summary)
