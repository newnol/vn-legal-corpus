# Quyết định 15/2000/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 15/2000/QD-BCN of April 3, 2000 on transferring Cam Pha Region Coal Health Center which is an attached unit of Vietnam Coal Corporation’s Coal Industry Labor Health Center to the management of the People’s Committee of Quang Ninh province
To transfer in status quo Cam Pha Region Coal Health Center which is an attached unit of Vietnam Coal Corporation’s Coal Industry Labor Health Center to the management of the People’s Committee of Quang Ninh province.
This Decision takes effect as from May 1 st , 2000.- (Summary)
