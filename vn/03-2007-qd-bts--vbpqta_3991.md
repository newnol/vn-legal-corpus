# Decision 03/2007/QD-BTS 

DECISION
Promulgating the Regulation on circulation registration of aquatic animal veterinary drugs; and products for treating and rehabilitation of the aquaculture environment
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces the provisions on circulation registration of aquatic animal veterinary drugs in the Regulation on management of aquatic animal veterinary drugs, promulgated together with Decision No. 03/2002/QD-BTS of January 23, 2002.- (Summary)
