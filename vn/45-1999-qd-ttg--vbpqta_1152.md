Decision No.45/1999/QD-TTg of March 11, 1999 on the establishment of the Department for Multilateral Economic Cooperation under the Ministry for Foreign Affairs
To establish the Department for Multilateral Economic Cooperation under the Ministry for Foreign Affairs to assist the Minister for Foreign Affairs in performing the tasks of the Ministry for Foreign Affairs in the process of Vietnam�s joining to and participation in multilateral economic cooperation activities within such international organizations and forums as AFTA, APEC, WTO and other international and regional economic-commercial organizations.- (Summary)
