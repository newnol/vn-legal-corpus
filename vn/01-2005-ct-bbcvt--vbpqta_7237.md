# Chỉ thị 01/2005/CT-BBCVT

DIRECTIVE No. 01/2005/CT-BBCVT of January 6, 2005 on stepping up activities in service of the WTO accession negotiations and implementation of integration commitments on post, mail delivery, telecommunications and information technology
With this Directive, the Minister of Post and Telematics requests concerned units and agencies to carry out some tasks in order to take full use of development opportunities brought about by the WTO accession and integration commitments.- (Summary)
