# Quyết định 18/2003/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION No. 18/2003/QD-BGDDT of May 2, 2003 promulgating the form of master diplomas
Issued together with this Decision was the form of master diplomas.
The Post-Graduate Department and the Ministry’s Office shall have to print and allocate blank diplomas to post-graduate training establishments competent to grant master diplomas. This form shall be granted to students who are recognized by training establishments as having passed the exams.- (Summary)
