# Decision 41/2000/QD-TTg

Decision No.41/2000/QD-TTg of March 28, 2000 on the transformation of Concrete Company No.620 into a joint stock company
To transform Concrete Company No.620 - a State enterprise, into Chau Thoi Concrete Joint Stock Company No.620 (CJCO).
- Head office: Km 1877, National Highway 1A (old), Chau Thoi village, Binh An commune, Di An district, Binh Duong province.
- Charter capital: 58,800,000,000 VN dong, of which the State�s stake represents 50%, the shares to be sold to laborers in the company account for 25%, and those to be sold to outsiders make up 25%.
- Business lines and trades:
+ Manufacturing and assembling prefabricated concrete structural components;
+ Manufacturing construction materials of all kinds;
+ Manufacturing and repairing mechanical engineering tools, machinery and equipment;
+ Building traffic works (bridges, culverts, land-roads and ports); dredging canals, leveling ground, digging and filling foundations of construction works, applying blast-drilling method in construction activities;
+ Constructing industrial and civil projects;
+ Exporting and importing traffic and construction supplies and materials;
+ Providing services of transporting common concrete products, unhardened concrete, super-length and super-weight concrete structures.- (Summary)
