# Circular 06/GD-ĐT

CIRCULAR No.06-GD/DT of April 11, 1997 guiding the implementation of Decision No.1252-GD/DT of April 11, 1997 of the Ministry of Education and Training on the amendments and supplements to a number of provisions on the senior-high school (elementary and specialized) graduation exams.
This Circular provides guidances on the following contents: the time for doing the exams, the organization of the examination supervisory councils; the organization of marking exam papers, the organization of the second graduation exams for senior-high schools, elementary and specialized; complaints about marks given to examination papers and forms of discipline.- (Summary)
