# Decision 104/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.104/1999/QD-TTg of April 14, 1999 on a number of policies towards the youth volunteers who had already accomplished their tasks in the resistance war
Entitled to enjoy the policies under this Decision are youth volunteers who joined the resistance war in the period from July 15, 1950 to April 30, 1975, accomplished their tasks and returned to their families or died in action.- (Summary)
