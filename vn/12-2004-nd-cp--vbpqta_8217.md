# Nghị định 12/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 12/2004/ND-CP of January 8, 2004 on merging Te Tieu township and Dai Nghia commune for the establishment of Dai Nghia township of My Duc rural district, Ha Tay province
To establish Dai Nghia township of My Duc rural district on the basis of merging the whole land area and the population of Te Tieu township and Dai Nghia commune.
Dai Nghia township shall have a land area of 496.62 ha and a population of 6,913.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
