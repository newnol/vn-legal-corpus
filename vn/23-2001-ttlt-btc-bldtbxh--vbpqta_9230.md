# Thông tư liên tịch 23/2001/TTLT/BTC-BLĐTBXH

JOINT-CIRCULAR No. 23/2001/TTLT-BTC-BLDTBXH of April 6, 2001 guiding the implementation of the regime of school fee exemption and reduction for pupils and students being policy beneficiaries who are studying at non-public education and training institutions
The regime of school fee exemption and reduction shall apply to Heroes of the Armed Forces, Labor Heroes; war invalids, those who enjoy policies like war invalids; children of fallen heroes, children of war invalids, children of those who enjoy policies like war invalids, and children of diseased soldiers who are studying at non-public education and training institutions and can be verified as being subjects entitled to preferential treatment as provided for in the Government’s Decree No. 28/CP of April 29, 1995.
This Circular guides in detail measures for the implementation of the school fee exemption and reduction regime; the procedures for compilation of dossiers for enjoyment of the regime and funding for the implementation of this regime.- (Summary)
