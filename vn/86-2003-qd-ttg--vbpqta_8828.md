# Quyết định 86/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 86/2003/QD-TTg of May 7, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Hanoi city in the 2003-2005 period
Hanoi Housing Development and Investment Corporation shall have its legal person status retained and its member enterprises reorganized.
Enterprises whose 100% capital continues to be held by the State and whose legal person status remains unchanged: 6 public-utility enterprises and 57 business enterprises.
State enterprises to be merged into other enterprises: 24 in 2003, 15 in 2004 and 3 in 2005.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding more than 50% of their total shares: 3 in 2003, 7 in 2004 and 2 in 2005.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding a least 51% of their charter capital when shares are sold for the first time: 12 in 2003, 18 in 2004 and 10 in 2005.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding shares at low rates or holding no shares: 11 in 2003 and 6 in 2004.
Enterprises to be transferred to the Ministry of Communications and Transport for management: 3 in 2003.
Enterprises to be dissolved: 9 in 2003.
Enterprise to be transformed into non-business unit with revenues: 1 in 2003.- (Summary)
