# Circular 37/2006/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR
Guiding the regime of collection, remittance, management and use of charges for verification of papers and documents related to Vietnamese laborers working abroad, applicable to overseas labor management boards
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and annuls Official Letter No. 7585 TC/TCDN of July 23, 2003.- (Summary)
