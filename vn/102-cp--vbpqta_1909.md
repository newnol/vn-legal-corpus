# Decree 102/CP

DECREE No.102-CP of September 24, 1997 on the establishment of Khoai Chau township of Chau Giang district, Hung Yen province.
To establish Khoai Chau township, the capital of Chau Giang district, Hung Yen province, on the basis of the whole land area of 402.79 hectares and a population of 9,662 of Kim Nguu commune and a land area of 10.13 hectares of An Vi commune.- (Summary)
