# Directive 08/2008/CT-TTg 

DIRECTIVE
On the work of natural disaster, flood and storm prevention and combat and search and rescue in 2008
The Prime Minister requests ministries, branches, agencies and provincial/municipal People’s Committees to perform several tasks in order to well implement the work of natural disaster, flood and storm prevention and control and research and rescue in 2008.- (Summary)
