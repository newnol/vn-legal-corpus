# Decision 210/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 210/2004/QD-TTg of December 14, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on operation of the Steering Committee for the program of action against women- and children-trafficking crimes
The Steering Committee is tasked to assist the Government in directing the implementation of the action program; guide, urge and examine the prevention and combat of women- and children-trafficking crimes by ministries, branches and localities; organizing coordination among ministries, branches and mass organizations in this work; and directing international cooperation in the domains related to the work.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
