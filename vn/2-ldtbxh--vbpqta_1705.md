# Circular 2/LDTBXH

CIRCULAR No.2/LDTBXH of February 11, 1998 guiding the regime of wage increase to serve as basis for calculation of pensions for officials who once worked as experts in foreign countries.
This Circular guides the conditions, subject of application, basis for wage increase for calculation of pensions for officials who once worked as experts in foreign countries.- (Summary)
