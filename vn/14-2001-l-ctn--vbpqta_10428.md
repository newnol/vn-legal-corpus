# Lệnh 14/2001/L-CTN

ORDER No. 14/2001/L-CTN OF NOVEMBER 30, 2001 PROMULGATING ORDINANCE

Pursuant to Article 78 of the Law on the Organization of the National Assembly;
Pursuant to Article 51 of the Law on Promulgation of Legal Documents,

The Ordinance on Advertisement,
which was passed by the Standing Committee of the X th National Assembly of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam on November 16, 2001.
State President of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam TRAN DUC LUONG
