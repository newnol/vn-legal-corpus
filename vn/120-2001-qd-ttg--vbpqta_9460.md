# Quyết định 120/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 120/2001/QD-TTg of August 17, 2001 on the establishment of the Financial Institute
To establish the Financial Institute on the basis of merging Hanoi Finance-Accounting College, Financial Research Institute and Financial Officials� Fostering Center under the Ministry of Finance.
The Financial Institute shall be attached to the Ministry of Finance and subject to the State management by the Ministry of Education and Training as well as other ministries and branches according to their functions assigned by the Government.
The Financial Institute shall be tasked to:
- Train officials at tertiary and postgraduate levels in the field of finance-accounting;
- Provide fostering courses for various titles on professional skills as well as finance-accounting management technology;
- Conduct research into the finance-accounting management science and technology.- (Summary)
