# Quyết định 129/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 129/2004/QD-BCN of November 12, 2004 transforming Uong Bi Electric Mechanical Factory of Vang Danh Coal Company into Uong Bi Electric Mechanical Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Uong Bi Electric Mechanical Joint-Stock Company, UEMC, shall have a charter capital of VND 6,800,000,000, of which the State's equities represent 35.4% and equities sold to laborers of the factory, 64.6%.
Its head-office is located in Bac Son ward, Uong Bi township, Quang Ninh province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
