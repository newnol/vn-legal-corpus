# Nghị định 31/2000/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 31/2000/ND-CP of August 11, 2000 on the establishment of district township of Minh Hoa district, Quang Binh province
To establish Quy Dat township, the capital of Minh Hoa district, on the basis of a land area of 722 ha and a population of 4,763 of Quy Hoa commune; a land area of 15.8 ha and a population of 137 of Yen Hoa commune; a land area of 20.15 ha and a population of 226 of Xuan Hoa commune.-
