# Quyết định 52/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 52/2003/QD-TTg of April 15, 2003 approving the master plan for reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Ben Tre province till 2005
Enterprises with 100% of their charter capital being held by the State: 5.
Enterprises to be consolidated to become enterprises with 100% State capital: 2 in 2003 and 2 in 2004.
Enterprise to be transferred to Vietnam Tobacco Corporation: 1 in 2004.
Enterprise to be transformed into non-business unit with revenues: 1 in 2004.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding over 50% of their charter capital: 4 in 2003, 1 in 2004 and 1 in 2005.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding at least 51% of shares when shares are sold for the first time: 3 in 2003 and 1 in 2005.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding under 50% of their charter capital: 1 in 2003 and 1 in 2004.
Enterprise to be assigned or sold: 1 in 2003.
Enterprise to be dissolved: 1 in 2003.- (Summary)
