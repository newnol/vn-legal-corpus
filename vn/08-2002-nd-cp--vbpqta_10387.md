# Nghị định 08/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 08/2002/ND-CP of January 14, 2002 adjusting the administrative boundaries of Muong Te and Muong Lay rural districts for the establishment of Muong Nhe rural district and splitting Phong Tho rural district for the establishment of Tam Duong rural district, Lai Chau province
To establish Muong Nhe rural district on the basis of the natural land area of 170,286 hectares and the population of 12,153 of Muong Te rural district; and the natural land area of 80,504 hectares and the population of 13,364 of Muong Lay rural district. Muong Nhe rural district shall have a natural land area of 250,790 hectares and a population of 25,517, and comprise 6 communes.
To establish Tam Duong rural district on the basis of the natural land area of 82,843.7 hectares and the population of 52.567 of Phong Tho rural district. Tam Duong rural district shall comprise Phong Tho district capital and 14 communes.-
