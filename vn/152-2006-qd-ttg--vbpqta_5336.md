# Decision 152/2006/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Ratifying the Agreement between the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the Government of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka on the avoidance of double taxation and prevention of fiscal evasion for income taxes
Under this Decision, the Prime Minister ratifies the above-said Agreement, which was concluded on October 26, 2005, in Hanoi, Vietnam.- (Summary)
