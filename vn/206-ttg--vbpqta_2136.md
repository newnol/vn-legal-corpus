# Decision 206/TTg

Decision No. 206-TTg of April 11, 1996 of the Prime Minister to upgrade the Intermediate Teachers' Training School of Ha Tinh province into the Teachers' Training College
The Teachers' Training College of Ha Tinh province has the task of training and fostering general education school teachers of the collegial level in the province, to conduct educational research to meet the development requirements and to improve the quality of general education in the locality.- (Summary)
