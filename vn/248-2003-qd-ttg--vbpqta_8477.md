# Quyết định 248/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 248/2003/QD-TTg of November 20, 2003 renaming the Administration's Organization and Personnel Boards under the provincial/municipal People's Committees provincial/municipal Home Affairs Services
To rename the Administration's Organization and Personnel Boards under the provincial/municipal People's Committees provincial/municipal Home Affairs Services.
The functions, tasks, powers and organizational structures of the Home Affairs Services shall be specified by the provincial/municipal People's Committees.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
