# Circular 16/1998/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No.16/1998/TT-BTC of February 6, 1998 amending and supplementing Circular No.98/1997/TT-BTC of December 31, 1997 of the Ministry of Finance guiding the settlement of financial matters of non-business units attached to State corporations.
This Circular amends and supplements a number of points in Circular No.98/1997/TT-BTC to make them compatible with Decision No.1179/1997/QD-TTg of December 30, 1997 of the Prime Minister on a number of policies and measures for the management of the 1998 socio-economic development plan and State budget estimates.- (Summary)
