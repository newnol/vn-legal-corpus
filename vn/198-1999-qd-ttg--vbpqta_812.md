# Decision 198/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.198/1999/QD-TTg of September 30, 1999 on wages of officials and employees working at the investment and construction project management boards
Officials and employees working at the investment and construction project management boards attached to the ministries, branches or localities shall be salaried according to their professional skills and grades and enjoy position allowances as prescribed for those working in the administrative and non-business sector.
Officials and employees working at the investment and construction project management boards attached to enterprises shall be salaried according to their professional skills and grades, and enjoy position allowances as prescribed for employees working in enterprises.- (Summary)
