# Quyết định 925/1997/QĐ-TTg

Decision No.925/1997/QD-TTg of November 1 st , 1997 amending and supplementing Article 1 of Decision No.347-TTg of July 5, 1994 of the Prime Minister.
To set up the Central Steering Committee for residential house and land policies, with Deputy Prime Minister Ngo Xuan Loc as its Chairman; Minister of Construction Nguyen Manh Kiem as its Vice Chairman; Its members include a Vice Minister of Construction, a Vice Minister of Finance, a Vice Minister of Planning and Investment, a Vice Minister of Justice, a Vice Minister of Defense, a Deputy General Director of the General Land Administration, the Deputy Head of the Government Pricing Committee, the Director of the Department for House Management under the Ministry of Construction, a representative of the leading board of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front and a representative of the leading board of the Vietnam General Labor Confederation.- (Summary)
