# Chỉ thị 05/2003/CT-BTC

DIRECTIVE No. 05/2003/CT-BTC of April 9, 2003 on the implementation of, and response to, the campaign for purchase of the 2003 education bonds
The Finance Minister instructs the whole finance service to determine the implementation of the campaign for purchase of the 2003 education bonds as a central task of 2003.
The Minister also gives instructions on the service's agencies and units' works related to the bond issuance organization and bond purchase mobilization.- (Summary)
