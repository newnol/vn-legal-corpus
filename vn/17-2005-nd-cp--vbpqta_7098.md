# Nghị định 17/2005/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 17/2005/ND-CP of February 16, 2005 establishing new communes in Chu Se, Kong Chro, Dak Doa and Ia Grai districts, Gia Lai province
This Decree prescribes the establisment of Ia Hla, Bar Maih and Chu Pong communes in Chu Se district, Dak Pling commune in Kong Chro district, Dak Krong commune in Dak Doa district, and Ia Grang commune in Ia Grai district, Gia Lai province.
