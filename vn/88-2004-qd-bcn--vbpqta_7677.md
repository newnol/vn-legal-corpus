# Quyết định 88/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 88/2004/QD-BCN of September 8, 2004 amending a number of contents of the Industry Minister's Decision No. 74/2004/QD-BCN of August 6, 2004 transforming Nha Be Garment Company into Nha Be Garment Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Nha Be Garment Joint-Stock Company's charter capital is VND 64,000,000,000, of which the State's equities represent 51%, equities sold to laborers in the company, 47.71% and equities sold to other subjects, 1.29%.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
