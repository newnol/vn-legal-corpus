# Circular 06/2002/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 06/2002/TT-BTC of January 18, 2002 guiding the implementation of a number of points on the undertakings and measures to administer the 2002 State budget estimates
This Circular guides the provisions on the allocation and assignment of the State budget revenue and expenditure estimates; the organization, management and administration of the State budget.- (Summary)
