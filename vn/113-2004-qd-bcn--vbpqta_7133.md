# Quyết định 113/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 113/2004/QD-BCN of October 15, 2004 transforming the Electrical Equipment Company into Electrical Equipment Limited Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, the Electrical Equipment Limited Company (THIBIDI Co. Ltd.) is headquartered at Road No. 9, Bien Hoa I Industrial Park, Bien Hoa city, Dong Nai province.
Its charter capital is VND 55,437,326,019.- (Summary)
