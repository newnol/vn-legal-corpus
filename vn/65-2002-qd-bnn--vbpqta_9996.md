# Quyết định 65/2002/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 65/2002/QD-BNN of July 15, 2002 establishing the Finance-Accounting Bureau and the Bureau for Administrative Affairs and Organization under the Department for Flood and Storm Prevention and Combat and Dyke Management on the basis of separating the Bureau for Administrative Affairs, Organization and Finance
To establish the Finance-Accounting Bureau and the Bureau for Administrative Affairs and Organization under the Department for Flood and Storm Prevention and Combat and Dyke Management on the basis of separating the Bureau for Administrative Affairs, Organization and Finance.
The director of the above-said Department shall define the functions, tasks, powers and payrolls of these two bureaus.- (Summary)
