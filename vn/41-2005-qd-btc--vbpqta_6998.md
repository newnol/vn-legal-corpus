# Quyết định 41/2005/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
On the State’s development investment credit lending interest rate
Under this Decision, the interest rate of 7.8%/year shall apply to projects with credit contracts signed for the first time, as from the effective date of this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
