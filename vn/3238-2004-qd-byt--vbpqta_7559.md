# Quyết định 3238/2004/QĐ-BYT

DECISION No. 3238/2004/QD-BYT of September 16, 2004 approving the 2004-2007 program on dissemination and education of the health legislation
The program aims to widely disseminate health-related legal documents to the concerned subjects and the community, ensuring the effect of such documents and raise the efficiency of State management by law in the health sector.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
