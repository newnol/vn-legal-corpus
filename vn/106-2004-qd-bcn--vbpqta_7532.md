# Quyết định 106/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 106/2004/QD-BCN of October 11, 2004 transforming Hue Leather and Footwear Factory of Saigon Leather and Footwear Import-Export Company into Hue Leather and Footwear Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Hue Leather and Footwear Joint-Stock Company, abbreviated to HLF.,JSC, has a charter capital of VND 410,000,000; with 100% of its equities to be sold to laborers in the factory.
Its head office is located at 112 Dien Bien Phu, Hue city, Thua Thien-Hue province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
