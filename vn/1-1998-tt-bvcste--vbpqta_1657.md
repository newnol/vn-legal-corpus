# Circular 1/1998/TT-BVCSTE

CIRCULAR No.1/1998/TT-BVCSTE of March 7, 1998 guiding the activities of the Committees for Child Protection and Care of all levels to implement Directive No.6/1998/CT-TTg of January 23, 1998 of the Prime Minister on enhancing the child protection task, preventing and tackling the problem of street children and child labor abuse.
This Circular provide specific guidance for the Committees at each level: the central, provincial/municipal or grassroots level, in their activities including the mass aggitation and mobilization, elaboration of plans and experimentation of forms of activities in order to prevent and tackle the problem of street children and child labor abuse; the development of the Child Protection Fund,...- (Summary)
