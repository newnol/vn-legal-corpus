# Quyết định 50/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 50/2004/QD-BCN of June 17, 2004 amending Clauses 1 and 3, Article 1 of the Industry Minister's Decision No. 179/2003/QD-BCN of November 4, 2003 on transformation of Sai Gon Shoes Company into Sai Gon Shoes Joint-Stock Company
To amend Clauses 1 and 3, Article 1 of the Industry Minister's Decision No. 179/2003/QD-BCN of November 4, 2003 on transformation of Sai Gon Shoes Company into Sai Gon Shoes Joint-Stock Company regarding the charter capital structure (the State's share percentage: 51%; shares to be sold to the company's laborers: 49%) and the preferences for the company's laborers (preferential shares to be sold to the company's laborers amount to 70,744 valued at VND 2,122,320,000).
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary
