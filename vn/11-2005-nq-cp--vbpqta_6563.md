# Nghị quyết 11/2005/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
On the Government's September 2005 regular meeting
On September 28-29, the Government held its September regular meeting, where it heard:
- The Government Office's and the Agriculture and Rural Development Ministry's reports on damage caused by, and measures to remedy consequences of, Storm Damrey;
- The Planning and Investment Minister's report on the 2005 socio-economic plan performance and the 2006 development investment plan; and the Finance Minister's report on the 2005 state budget implementation, the 2006 state budget estimates and the 2006 central budget allocation;
- The Government's action program for implementation of Resolution No. 46-NQ/TW of February 23, 2005, on the protection of, care for and improvement of the people's health in the new situation, presented by the Health Minister;
- The Education and Training Minister's scheme on adjustment of tuition fees at educational institutions in the national education system; and the Health Minister's scheme on adjustment of hospital fees;
- The Natural Resources and Environment Minister's report on the procedures for promulgating Government's resolutions on approval and adjustment of land use plannings and plans of provinces, centrally-run cities, the Ministry of Defense, and the Ministry of Public Security;
- The Justice Minister's report on the finalization of the Real Estate Registration Bill; the Health Minister's report on the draft Ordinance on donation and transplantation of human tissues or organs; and the Government Office's report on gathering of the cabinet members' comments on this draft ordinance;
- The Finance Minister's report on the 2005 issuance of Government bonds to the international market.- (Summary)
