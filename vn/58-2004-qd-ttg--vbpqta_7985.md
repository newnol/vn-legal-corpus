# Quyết định 58/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 58/2004/QD-TTg of April 7, 2004 approving the scheme on experimental transformation of Hanoi Beer-Alcohol-Beverage Corporation into one organized and operating after the parent company-affiliate company model
The scheme aims to create conditions for boosting production and raising economic efficiency and competitiveness of Hanoi Beer-Alcohol-Beverage Corporation to suit the general planning on development of the beer, alcohol and beverage industry till 2010 already approved by the Prime Minister.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
