# Decision 120/2002/QD-BNN

DECISION No.120/2002/QD-BNN of November 21, 2002 promulgating the ranger certificates
The ranger certificates are certificates used exclusively for the ranger force, which certify that the certificate grantees are the ranger officials and public servants working in the ranger force, and which are used for the management of officials and public servants, who shall use them when performing their public duties in forest management and protection and implementation of forest legislation.
The ranger certificates shall have the use duration of 3 years and be re-granted upon expiry.
The ranger certificates’ forms are issued by the Ranger Department.- (Summary)
