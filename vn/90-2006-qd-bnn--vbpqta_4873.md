# Decision 90/2006/QD-BNN 

DECISION
Promulgating the lists of livestock feed and livestock-feed materials permitted to be imported into Vietnam
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces the Agriculture and Rural Development Minister’s Decision No. 01/2006/QD-BNN of January 6, 2006, on the lists of livestock feed and livestock-feed materials permitted to be imported into Vietnam.- (Summary)
