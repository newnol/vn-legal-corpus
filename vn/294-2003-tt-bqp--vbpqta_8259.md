# Thông tư 294/2003/TT-BQP

CIRCULAR No. 294/2003/TT-BQP of December 23, 2003 guiding the application of the specific allowance regimes to armymen, public servants and employees working in the military medical establishments
The specific allowance regimes guided in this Circular, including the 24/24 hour duty allowance, the epidemics-fighting allowance regime and surgery allowance regime, apply as from July 1, 2003.
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces the Defense Minister's Decision No. 166/2002/QD-BQP of November 18, 2002 on allowance regimes for army medical workers, who are on duty in the army medical establishments.- (Summary)
