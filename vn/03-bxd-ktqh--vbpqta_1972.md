# Circular 03/BXD-KTQH

THE MINISTRY OF CONSTRUCTION
CIRCULAR No.03-BXD/KTQH of June 4, 1997 guiding the elaboration and ratification of plannings on the construction of district towns and townships.
The Circular provides guidances on: the contents, order and method of the elaboration of plannings on the construction of district towns and townships; and the ratification thereof.
The elaboration and ratification of such plannings aim to establish the legal and scientific basis for the management and development of urban areas.- (Summary)
