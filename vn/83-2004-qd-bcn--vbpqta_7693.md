# Quyết định 83/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 83/2004/QD-BCN of August 24, 2004 amending Article 2 of the Industry Minister’s Decision No. 41/2004/QD-BCN of May 28, 2004 on the transformation of the Base Paper Enterprise of Ha Bac Nitrogenous Fertilizer and Chemicals Company into Song Thuong Joint Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Article 2 of above-said Decision No. 41/2004/QD-BCN is amended as follows:
To transform the Base Paper Enterprise of Ha Bac Nitrogenous Fertilizer and Chemicals Company into Hoa Binh Joint Stock Company (H.J.CO), which is headquartered in Quarter 1, Tho Xuong ward, Bac Giang provincial capital, Bac Giang province.- (Summary)
