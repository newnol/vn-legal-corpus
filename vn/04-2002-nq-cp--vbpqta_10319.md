# Resolution 04/2002/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION No. 04/2002/NQ-CP of April 9, 2002 on the Government�s March 2002 regular meeting
On April 4, 2002, the Government held its March 2002 regular meeting with the following principal contents:
- Discussing the draft decree on abolishing some permits and changing the mode of management over some others; the draft decree detailing the Marriage and Family Law involving foreign elements; and the draft ordinance on post and telecommunications.
- Considering the socio-economic situation in March and the first quarter of 2002.- (Summary)
