# Resolution 15/2000/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION No. 15/2000/NQ-CP of October 6, 2000 on a number of strategies and measures to continuously overcome flood consequences, stabilize the people�s life and promptly settle newly-arising issues, in order to ensure requirement for fast and sustainable socio-economic development in the Mekong River delta region
The Government issued this Resolution after its October 2, 2000 meeting, which discussed some strategies and measures for overcoming flood consequences, quickly stabilizing the people�s life and handling newly-arising problems, aiming at fast and sustainable socio-economic development in the Mekong River delta region.
This Resolution sets forth urgent tasks which require the direction by local administrations of various levels; points to the issues to be settled right after the flood water recedes; prescribes a number of strategies and policies for flood-stricken localities and people; and a number of long-term problems to be settled after this flood (on infrastructure and production arrangement).- (Summary)
