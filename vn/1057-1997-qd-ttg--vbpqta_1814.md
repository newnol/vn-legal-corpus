# Quyết định 1057/1997/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.1057/1997/QD-TTg of December 9, 1997 ratifying the general planning of the Da Nang University.
The Da Nang University is an inter-branch training and scientific research center with a system of schools, research institutes, experimental units..., which ensures a close combination between training and scientific research as well as technological transfer, in direct service of economic development in Central Vietnam and Central Highlands.
The University has an enrollment of 30,000 students.
The University shall be built in Ngu Hanh Son district of Da Nang city and Dien Ban district of Quang Nam province on an area of 300 hectares.- (Summary)
