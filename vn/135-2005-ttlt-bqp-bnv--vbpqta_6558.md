# Joint circular 135/2005/TTLT-BQP-BNV 

JOINT CIRCULAR
Guiding the implementation of the Government's Decree No. 119/2004/ND-CP on consolidation of organizational structures and assignment of officials, employees and army officers for defense work in ministries, branches and localities
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO," annulling Joint Circular No. 93/TT-LB of January 16, 1995, of the Defense Ministry and the Government's Commission for Organization and Personnel.- (Summary)
