# Quyết định 40/2005/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
Publicizing the list of legal documents issued by the Finance Ministry, which cease to be effective, are annulled or replaced by others
With this Decision, the Finance Minister publicizes a list of 233 legal documents, issued by the Finance Ministry by December 31, 2004, which cease to be effective.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
