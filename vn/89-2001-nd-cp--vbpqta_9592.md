# Decree 89/2001/ND-CP

DECREE No. 89/2001/ND-CP of November 22, 2001 establishing Phu Tai, Xuan An, Ham Tien and Phu Hai wards, renaming Ham Tien commune of Phan Thiet city, and establishing Da Mi commune of Ham Thuan Bac district, Binh Thuan province
To establish Phu Tai ward of Phan Thiet city with a land area of 285.43 ha and a population 6,676.
To establish Xuan An ward of Phan Thiet city with a land area of 203.97 ha and a population of 5,564.
To establish Ham Tien ward of Phan Thiet city with a land area of 1,039.15 ha and a population of 7,320.
To rename Ham Tien commune as Thien Nghiep commune with a land area of 7,404.05 ha and a population of 5,297.
To establish Phu Hai ward of Phan Thiet city with a land area of 1,213.2 ha and a population of 10,980.
To establish Da Mi commune of Ham Thuan Bac district with a land area of 14,537.69 ha and a population of 2,774.- (Summary)
