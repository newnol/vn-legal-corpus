# Nghị quyết 08/2005/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
On the Government’s June 2005 regular meeting
On June 30 and July 1, 2005, the Government held its June regular meeting where it heard:
- The Minister-Director of the Committee for Physical Training and Sports reporting on the program on raising physical strength and stature of Vietnamese people;
- The Minister of Post and Telematics presenting the Information Technology Bill;
- The Minister of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs presenting the Social Insurance Bill;
- The Minister of Planning and Investment presenting the “unified” Investment Bill and Enterprise Bill.
The Government also examined reports on production, import-export, investment and trade activities, Government’s direction and administration activities and State administrative reform in the first half of the year.- (Summary)
