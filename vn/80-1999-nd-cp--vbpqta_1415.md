# Nghị định 80/1999/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 80/1999/ND-CP of August 24, 1999 on the establishment of communes in Thot Not and Phung Hiep districts, Can Tho province
To establish Trung Kien commune of Thot Not district on the basis of a land area of 1,348.94 ha and a population of 23,979 of Trung Nhut commune; a land area of 115 ha and a population of 1,942 of Thuan Hung commune.
To establish Tan Long commune of Phung Hiep district on the basis of a land area of 2,172 ha and a population of 15,799 of Long Thanh commune.- (Summary)
