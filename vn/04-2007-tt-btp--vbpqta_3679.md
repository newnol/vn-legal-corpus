# Thông tư 04/2007/TT-BTP

CIRCULAR
Guiding the competence, order and procedures for registration of, and supply of information on, deferred-payment or installment-payment purchase contracts, asset leasing contracts, financial leasing contracts, and contracts on transfer of the right to claim debts
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces Circular No. 09/2005/TT-BTP of December 6, 2005, guiding the competence, order and procedures for registration of, and supply of information on, financial leasing contracts, and state management of registration of financial leasing contracts.- (Summary)
