# Nghị định 69/1999/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 69/1999/ND-CP of August 11, 1999 on the establishment of townships of Tra Linh, Thong Nong, Thach An and Quang Hoa districts, Cao Bang province
To establish Hung Quoc district township of Tra Linh district on the basis of the total land area of 1,540 ha and a population of 4,265 of Hung Quoc commune.
To establish Thong Nong district township of Thong Nong district on the basis of a land area of 935 ha and a population of 2,113 of Da Thong commune.
To establish Dong Khe district township of Thach An district on the basis of the total land area and population of Thuong Pha commune; a land area of 318.32 ha and a population of 1,112 of Le Lai commune. Dong Khe township, thereby, has a land area of 1,497.32 ha and a population of 3,701.
To establish Ta Lung township of Quang Hoa district on the basis of a land area of 636.2 ha and a population of 2,249 of Ta Lung commune.- (Summary)
