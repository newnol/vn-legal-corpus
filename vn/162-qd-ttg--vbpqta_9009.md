# Decision 162/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 162/QD-TTg of February 18, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Lao Cai province in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises where the State continues to hold 100% of their capital: 2 public-utility enterprises and 10 enterprises engaged in business activities.
Enterprise to be equitized with the State holding more than 50% of its charter capital: 1 in 2004.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding under 50% of their charter capital: 4 in 2003 and 2 in 2004.
Enterprises to be assigned or sold: 5 in 2003.
Enterprises to be merged into others: 1 in 2003 and 1 in 2004.- (Summary)
