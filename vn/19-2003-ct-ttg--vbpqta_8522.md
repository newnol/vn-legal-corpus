# Chỉ thị 19/2003/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE No. 19/2003/CT-TTg of September 11, 2003 on organizing the elaboration of urban construction plannings and managing construction according to plannings
The Prime Minister directs the ministries, branches, provincial/municipal People's Committees and urban administrations at all levels to perform the following tasks:
- Organizing the elaboration, consideration and approval of construction plannings;
- Managing construction according to the plannings;
- Mobilizing various sources of capital for urban development projects;
- Effecting the task assignment and responsibility decentralization and clearly determine the responsibilities of organizations and individuals in organizing the elaboration of construction plannings and managing construction according to these plannings.
The Ministry of Construction shall have to send periodical reports on the implementation of this Directive to the Prime Minister.- (Summary)
