# Nghị quyết 13/2004/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION No. 13/2004/NQ-CP of November 3, 2004 on the Government's October 2004 regular meeting
On November 1, 2004, the Government held its October regular meeting, where:
- It heard the Justice Minister presenting the National Assembly Standing Committee's draft resolution on dwelling house-related civil transactions involving foreign elements, which had been conducted before July 1, 1991; and the Minister-Director of the Government Office synthesizing the cabinet members' opinions on the draft resolution.
- It heard the Public Security Ministry presenting the draft ordinance on guards; and the Minister-Director of the Government Office synthesizing the cabinet members' opinions on the draft ordinance.
- It heard the Minister of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs presenting the draft ordinance on preferential treatment for people with meritorious contributions to the revolution; and the Minister-Director of the Government Office synthesizing the cabinet members' opinions on the draft ordinance.
- It discussed the Construction Minister's proposals on capital mechanisms for investment projects on cement production.
- It heard the Planning and Investment Ministry's reports on the export, import and investment situation in October and the socio-economic situation in the first 10 months of 2004; the Trade Ministry's report on the trade situation in October; the Home Affairs Ministry's report on one year's implementation of the "one-door" mechanism; and the Government Inspectorate's report on inspection and settlement of complaints and denunciations in the third quarter of 2004.- (Summary)
