# Nghị định 137/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 137/2003/ND-CP of November 14, 2003 on the establishment of district capitals of Nam Truc, Xuan Truong, My Loc and Giao Thuy districts, Nam Dinh province
To establish Nam Giang township, the capital of Nam Truc district, on the basis of the whole land area of 704.55 hectares and the population of 18,047 of Nam Giang commune.
To establish Xuan Truong township, the capital of Xuan Truong district, on the basis of the whole land area of 585.91 hectares and the population of 11,506 of Xuan Hung commune, and a land area of 30.40 hectares of Xuan Ngoc commune.
To establish My Loc township, the capital of My Loc district, on the basis of a land area of 221.71 hectares and a population of 2,256 of My Hung commune; a land area of 177.14 hectares and a population of 1,587 of My Thinh commune; and a land area of 70.32 and a population of 517 of My Thanh commune.
To establish Quat Lam township of Giao Thuy district on the basis of the whole land area of 759.41 hectares and the population of 9,726 of Giao Lam commune.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
