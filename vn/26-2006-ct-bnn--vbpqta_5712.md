# Directive 26/2006/CT-BNN

DIRECTIVE
On controlling rice grassy stunt and wilted stunt diseases for summer-fall crops in the Mekong river delta
With this Directive, the Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development requests concerned agencies and units to immediately perform several tasks in order to control rice grassy stunt and wilted stunt diseases for summer-fall crops in the Mekong River delta.- (Summary)
