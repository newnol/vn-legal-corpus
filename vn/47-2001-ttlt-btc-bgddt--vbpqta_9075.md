# Thông tư liên tịch 47/2001/TTLT/BTC-BGDĐT

Joint Circular No. 47/2001/TTLT-BTC-BGDDT of June 20, 2001 guiding the levels of allowances for compilation of questions for and organization of talented pupils’ examinations, international examinations and graduation examinations
This Circular guides the norms of expenditures for the work of compiling questions for and organizing talented pupils’ examinations, international examinations and graduation examinations. Scope and subjects of application are national examinations organized by the Ministry of Education and Training; national examinations organized by the provincial/municipal Education and Training Services; and international examinations.- (Summary)
