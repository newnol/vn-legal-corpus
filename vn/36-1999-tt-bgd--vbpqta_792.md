# Thông tư 36/1999/TT-BGD

CIRCULAR No. 36/1999/TT-BGDDT of September 27, 1999 guiding the implementation of the 40-hour-week working regime in education and training branch
The subjects of application are cadres, officials and laborers in educational administrative bodies at various levels and educational research institutes; cadres, teachers and laborers in educational establishments in the national educational system.
This Circular guides in detail the principles and conditions for implementation; contents of the implementation plan of the 40-hour-week working regime in education and training sector.- (Summary)
