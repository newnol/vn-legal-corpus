# Decision 03/1999/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No.03/1999/QD-BCN of January 19, 1999 on the transfer of the Consultancy, Designing and Construction Center from the Chemicals Construction and Installation Company to the Vietnam Industrial Construction Corporation
To transfer the Consultancy, Designing and Construction Center from the Chemicals Construction and Installation Company to the Vietnam Industrial Construction Corporation.
The Consultancy, Designing and Construction Center is a dependent cost-accounting unit of the Corporation, which is tasked to provide consultancy and services for the latter in a number of the following fields:
- Market probing and tapping
- Organizing the elaboration of construction projects for bidding
- Promoting, supporting member units of the Corporation in the field of marketing and bidding.
- Evaluating internal development investment projects
- Providing consultancy services and elaborating group-B investment projects with domestic and foreign-invested capital
- Designing group-B civil and industrial projects; drafting the project total estimates
- Providing domestic bidding consultancy services on: designing, procuring equipment, determining the construction and construction consultancy prices in service of contract signing by the investor and the bidders.
- Conducting experiments for the provision or inspection of technical parameters in service of the designing and quality evaluation of construction projects
- Evaluating the quality of construction projects
- Managing investment projects and building projects.- (Summary)
