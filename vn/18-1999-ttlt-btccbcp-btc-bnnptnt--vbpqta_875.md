# Joint circular 18/1999/TTLT/BTCCBCP-BTC-BNNPTNT

Joint Circular No. 18/1999/TTLT-BTCCBCP-BTC-BNNPTNT of June 28, 1999 guiding the implementation of the regime of allowances for dike controllers who personally take part in handling incidents in cases of flood and storm alarms
This Circular, jointly issued by the Government Commission for Organization and Personnel, the Ministry of Finance and the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development, guides the implementation of the Prime Minister’s Decision No.93/1999/QD-TTg of April 5, 1999 on eligible subjects, allowance levels, and working seniority for calculation of allowance levels, the funding sources for paying allowances to dike controllers who personally take part in handling incidents in cases of flood and storm alarms.- (Summary)
