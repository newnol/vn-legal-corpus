# Chỉ thị 09/2004/CT-BTS

DIRECTIVE No. 09/2004/CT-BTS of October 11, 2004 on accelerating the patriotic emulation movement within the fisheries branch and striving for the attainment of the 2004 State plan’s targets
The Minister of Fisheries issues this Directive requesting officials and employees of the entire branch to concentrate on overcoming difficulties and obstacles in production and business activities as to attain the 2004 State plan’s target, especially the fisheries export target.- (Summary)
