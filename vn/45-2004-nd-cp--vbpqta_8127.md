# Nghị định 45/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREES No. 45/2004/ND-CP of February 17, 2004 approving the numbers and list of election units, the number of deputies to be elected to the People’s Councils for the 2004-2009 term, and the number of deputies to be elected at each election unit of the Tra Vinh province
To approve 13 election units, 50 deputies to the provincial People’s Council of the 2004-2009 term and the number of deputies to be elected at each election unit in the province.
The Decree take effect 15 days after their publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
