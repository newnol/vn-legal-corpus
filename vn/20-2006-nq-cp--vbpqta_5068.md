# Resolution 20/2006/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
Adjusting the land-use planning up to 2010 and 2006-2010 land-use plan of Thai Nguyen province
The People’s Committee of Thai Nguyen province shall base itself on this Resolution to implement the land-use planning and plan in association with socio-economic development objectives and defense, security and eco-environment protection tasks.- (Summary)
