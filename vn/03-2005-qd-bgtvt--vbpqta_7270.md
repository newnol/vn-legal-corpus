# Decision 03/2005/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION No. 03/2005/QD-BGTVT of January 4, 2005 prescribing the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of Vietnam Civil Aviation Administration Inspectorate
Under this Decision, Vietnam Civil Aviation Inspectorate, CAI, constitutes part of the Transport Inspectorate system, and performs administrative and specialized inspection within the State management scope of Vietnam Civil Aviation Administration.
