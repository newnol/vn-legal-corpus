# Nghị định 29/1999/NĐ-CP

DECREE No.29/1999/ND-CP of April 24, 1999 on the establishment of Pleiku city of Gia Lai province
To establish Pleiku city of Gia Lai province on the basis of the whole land area of 22,569.6 hectares and the whole population of 160,192 of Pleiku provincial capital. Pleiku city shall consist of 14 grassroots administrative units, including the wards of Dien Hong, Yen Do, Hoa Lu, Hoi Thuong, Hoi Phu and Thong Nhat and the communes of Bien Ho, Tan Son, Tra Da, Chu A, An Phu, Tra Ba, Dien Phu and Gao.- (Summary)
