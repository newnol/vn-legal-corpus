# Decision 3526/2004/QĐ-BYT

DECISION No. 3526/2004/QD-BYT of October 6, 2004 approving the Program of action for health communication and education till 2010
The program of action, issued together with this Decision, aims to raise the awareness of the Party's organizations and administrations of all levels, socio-political organizations, communities and citizens on people's health protection and care, thus contributing to successfully implementing the strategy on people's health protection and care in the 2001-2010 period.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
