# Quyết định 25/2004/QĐ-BKHCN

DECISION No. 25/2004/QD-BKHCN of September 6, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on organization and operation of the Vietnam Codex Alimentarius Commission
The Vietnam Codex Alimentarius Commission, VCAC, is an inter-branch national organization chaired by the Ministry of Science and Technology, which has the functions of advising State management agencies on the food-related standardization, participating in international and regional organizations' activities related to food standards; and proposing policies on the management of food quality, safety and hygiene in Vietnam.
The General Department of Standardization, Metrology and Quality Control of the Ministry of Science and Technology shall act as the VCAC's standing body.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
