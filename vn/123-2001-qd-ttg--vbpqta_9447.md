# Quyết định 123/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 123/2001/QD-TTg of August 22, 2001 on the establishment of Phu Yen Regional Television Station under Vietnam Television Station
To establish Phu Yen Regional Television Station under Vietnam Television Station, which is tasked to produce and broadcast programs in service of the general programs of Vietnam Television Station, and to produce and broadcast programs in service of the region, which covers Binh Dinh, Phu Yen, Khanh Hoa, Binh Thuan, Ninh Thuan and Lam Dong provinces.
To dissolve Phu Yen Television Station and transfer the whole material base, technical equipment and payroll of Phu Yen Television Station to Phu Yen Regional Television Station.- (Summary)
