# Nghị định 31/1998/NĐ-CP

DECREE No.31/1998/ND-CP of May 16, 1998 on the establishment of a number of communes and wards of Son La provincial capital and Yen Chau, Moc Chau and Mai Son districts, Son La province.
To establish To Hieu ward of Son La provincial capital on the basis of a land area of 179 hectares and a population of 7,060 of Chieng Le ward.
To establish Quyet Tam ward of Son La provincial capital on the basis of a land area of 215 hectares and a population of 5,063 of Quyet Thang ward.
To establish Yen Son commune of Yen Chau district on the basis of a land area of 4,596.2 hectares and a population of 3,038 of Chieng On commune.
To establish Dong Sang commune of Moc Chau district on the basis of a land area of 4289 hectares and a population of 3,530 of Muong Sang commune.
To establish Ta Lai commune of Moc Chau district on the basis of a land area of 2,730 hectares and a population of 2,798 of Na Muong commune.
To establish Lien Hoa commune of Moc Chau district on the basis of a land area of 3,372 hectares and a population of 2,713 of Song Khua commune.
To establish Phieng Cam commune of Mai Son district on the basis of a land area of 13,825.1 hectares and a population of 3,786 of Chieng Noi commune.
To establish Na Ot commune of Mai Son district on the basis of a land area of 10,650 hectares and a population of 2,534 of Phieng Pan commune.- (Summary)
