# Nghị quyết 48/2005/QH11

RESOLUTION
On the 2006 central budget allocation
With this Resolution, the National Assembly requests the Government to assign budget collection and spending tasks to ministries, ministerial-level agencies, Government-attached agencies and other central agencies; and assign budget collection and spending tasks as well as additional central budget allocation levels to provincial/municipal budgets before November 20, 2005; and perform several other tasks.- (Summary)
