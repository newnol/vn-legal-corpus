# Decision 169/2002/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 169/QD-TTg of February 25, 2002 transforming Light Industry Construction Company No. 2 into Industry Construction Corporation
To transform Light Industry Construction Company No. 2, an independent-accounting member unit of Vietnam Industrial Construction Corporation under the Ministry of Industry, into Industry Construction Corporation.
Its head office: 146 Nguyen Cong Tru street, district 1, Ho Chi Minh City.
Its charter capital is VND 13.5 billion, of which the State equities represent 10%, equities sold to the laborers inside the enterprises, 70%, and equities sold to investors outside the enterprise, 20%.- (Summary)
