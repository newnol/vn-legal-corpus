# Decision 217/2004/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 217/2004/QD-TTg of December 31, 2004 approving the Scheme on experimental equitization of Vietnam Trading Engineering Construction Corporation
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Vietnam Trading Engineering Construction Corporation is transformed into Vietnam Trading Engineering Construction Joint-Stock Corporation (Vietracimex).
