# Quyết định 26/2003/QĐ-BNV

DECISION No. 26/2003/QD-BNV of June 10, 2003 approving the (amended) Charter of Vietnamese Pharmaceutical Association
To approve the (amended) Charter of Vietnamese Pharmaceutical Association, which was adopted by its IXth National Congress of Deputies on February 22, 2003.
Vietnamese Pharmaceutical Association, abbreviated to VPA, is a voluntary socio-professional organization of Vietnamese pharmacists as well as scientific and technical workers operating in Vietnam's pharmaceutical fields in order to enhance their unity, to learn and enrich their experiences and moralities and conduct research, aiming to bring into full play professional effects, and contribute to the pharmaceutical building and development.
The Association is organized on the principles of democracy and centralism, operates under the Vietnam Communist Party's leadership and the State's management. It is a member of Vietnam Union of Scientific and Technical Associations.
It operates nationwide, has the legal person status, and is headquartered in Hanoi.- (Summary)
