# Thông tư 05/2002/TT-BYT

CIRCULAR No. 05/2002/TT-BYT of May 30, 2002 guiding the sixth conferment of People’s Doctor and Meritorious Doctor titles
The Circular guides in detail the conferment subjects and criteria; the establishment of the Title Conferment Council; the title conferment consideration process; as well as title conferment consideration dossiers and implementation time.- (Summary)
