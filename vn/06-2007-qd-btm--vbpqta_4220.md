# Decision 06/2007/QĐ-BTM

DECISION
Amending and supplementing the Regulation on the grant of Vietnam’s certificates of origin, Form S, for goods for enjoyment of tariff preferences under the Agreement on Economic, Cultural, Scientific and Technical Cooperation between the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the Government of the People’s Democratic Republic of Laos, issued together with the Trade Minister’s Decision No. 0865/2004/QD-BTM of June 29, 2004
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces Decision No. 2368/2005/QD-BTM of September 16, 2005.- (Summary)
