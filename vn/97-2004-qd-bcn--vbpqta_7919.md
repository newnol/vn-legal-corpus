# Quyết định 97/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 97/2004/QD-BCN of September 23, 2004 transforming the Electro-Mechanics Materials Factory of the Electricity Company 1 into the Electro-Mechanics Materials Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, the Electro-Mechanics Materials Joint-Stock Company, abbreviated to EMC, has its charter capital of VND 20,000,000,000, of which the State's equities represent 51%; equities sold to laborers in the factory, 46.1%; and equities sold to other subjects, 2.9%.
Its head office is located at 508 Ha Huy Tap, Yen Vien township, Hanoi.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
