# Quyết định 57/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 57/2004/QD-TTg of April 6, 2004 establishing Ho Chi Minh City People-Founded Technology University
To establish Ho Chi Minh City People-Founded Technology University on the basis of Ho Chi Minh City People-Founded Technology College.
Ho Chi Minh City People-Founded Technology University is a tertiary education establishment, operating under the Regulation on People-Founded Universities, promulgated together with the Prime Minister's Decision No. 86/2000/QD-TTg of July 18, 2000.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
