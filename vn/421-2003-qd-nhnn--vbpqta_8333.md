# Quyết định 421/2003/QĐ-NHNN

DECISION No. 421/2003/QD-NHNN of May 5, 2003 amending the Regulation on investment in and procurement of equipment, supplies, raw materials, auxiliary materials and technical solutions in service of the process of money printing and minting by the State Bank, issued together with the State Bank Governor's Decision No. 580/2002/QD-NHNN of June 10, 2002
To annul Clause 3, Article 12 of the Regulation on investment in and procurement of equipment, supplies, raw materials, auxiliary materials and technical solutions in service of the process of money printing and minting by the State Bank.- (Summary)
