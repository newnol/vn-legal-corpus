# Decision 49/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.49/1998/QD-TTg of February 28, 1998 on a number of regimes applicable to sport athletes and trainers.
This Decision stipulates the pecuniary remunerations to non-salaried athletes, sick allowance, accident allowance or death allowance to athletes in national squads or selected teams of provinces and cities directly under the Central Government or of ministries and branches at the central level, who are not subject to social insurance under the Regulation on Social Insurance; and the levels of pecuniary rewards to athletes with high achievements in competitions and their trainers.- (Summary)
