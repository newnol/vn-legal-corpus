# Decision 229/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 229/QD-TTg of February 26, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under People’s Committee of Tra Vinh province till 2005
Enterprises whose legal person status remains unchanged and where the State continues to hold 100% of their charter capital: 3 public-utility enterprises and 5 enterprises engaged in business activities.
Enterprise to be equitized with the State holding more than 50% of its total shares: 1 in 2003.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding at least 51% of shares when their shares are sold for the first time: 3 in 2003 and 1 in 2005
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding shares at a low rate or holding no shares: 1 in 2004 and 1 in 2005.
Enterprise to be dissolved: 1 in 2004.
Enterprise section to be equitized: 1 in 2004.
Enterprises to be assigned to laborers’ collectives: 2 in 2003 and 1 in 2004.- (Summary)
