# Chỉ thị 02/2004/CT-BNN

DIRECTIVE No. 02/2004/CT-BNN of January 20, 2004 enhancing urgent measures to prevent and fight forest fires in the 2003-2004 dry season
Under this Directive, the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development requests the provincial/municipal People's Committees and Forest Fire Prevention and Fighting Commands as well as units in the branch and forest owners to immediately take some specific measures to actively prevent and fight forest fires and minimize damage caused thereby.- (Summary)
