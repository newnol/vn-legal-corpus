# Decree 63/CP

* DECREE NO.63-CP OF OCTOBER 7, 1995 OF THE GOVERNMENT ON DIVIDING VINH LAC DISTRICT AND THANH HOA DISTRICT OF VINH PHU PROVINCE:
To divide Vinh Lac district into two districts: Vinh Tuong and Yen Lac;
To divide Thanh Hoa district and 10 high-land communes of Song Thao district (Hien Luong, Quan Khe, Dong Lam, Lam Loi, Xuan Ang, Chue Luu, Bang Gia, Vo Tranh, Van Lang, Minh Coi) into two districts: Thanh Ba and Ha Hoa.- (SUMMARY)
