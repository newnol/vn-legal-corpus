# Nghị định 127/2005/NĐ-CP

DECREE
Guiding the implementation of the National Assembly's Resolution No. 23/2003/QH11 of November 26, 2003, and the National Assembly Standing Committee's Resolution No. 755/2005/NQ-UBTVQH11 of April 2, 2005, providing for the settlement of some specific cases involving houses and land in the course of implementation of house and land management policies and socialist transformation policies before July 1, 1991
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
