# Quyết định 15/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 15/2002/QD-TTg of January 17, 2002 on the establishment of the Department for Management of Construction Survey and Design under the Ministry of Construction
To establish the Department for Management of Construction Survey and Design under the Ministry of Construction on the basis of the Expertise Office, which shall assist the Minister of Construction in performing the function of State management over construction consultancy, survey and design, as well as the appraisal of technical design and total cost estimates of construction projects.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its signing.-
