# Quyết định 126/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 126/1999/QD-TTg of May 28, 1999 on the establishment of Hanoi Industrial College
To establish Hanoi Industrial College on the basis of Industrial Intermediate School No. 1.
Hanoi Industrial College is tasked to train and foster cadres with college or lower degrees; and conduct scientific research in service of socio-economic development.
Hanoi Industrial College is attached to the Ministry of Industry and subject to the State management over its education and training by the Ministry of Education and Training.- (Summary)
