# Directive 12/2003/CT-BGTVT

DIRECTIVE No. 12/2003/CT-BGTVT of May 22, 2003 reorganizing the work of inter-provincial passenger transportation by car
The Minister of Communications and Transport instructs Vietnam Land Road Administration to coordinate with the provincial/municipal Communications and Transport Services in:
- Reviewing the announced inter-provincial passenger transportation routes, abolishing those failing to meet the prescribed standards;
- Expeditiously completing the project on construction of car parks and car stops on national highways and submitting it to the Ministry of Communications and Transport for approval;
- Placing sign boards on car stops for embarking or disembarking passengers on all national highways managed by Vietnam Land Road Administration;
- Proposing the Minister to promulgate the Regulation on responsibilities of passenger-car drivers and measures to handle violations committed by passenger-car drivers and managing agencies.
The Directive also specifically states tasks under the responsibilities of the provincial/municipal Communications and Transport Services.- (Summary)
