# Decree 37/2001/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 37/2001/ND-CP of July 10, 2001 on the establishment of communes and township in Chau Thanh and Chau Thanh A districts, Can Tho province
To establish Phu Huu A commune in Chau Thanh district on the basis of a land area of 1,799 ha and a population of 10,593 of Phu Huu commune.
To establish Nga Sau township in Chau Thanh district on the basis of a land area of 1,100 ha and a population of 5,530 of Dong Phuoc commune.
To establish Dong Phuoc A commune in Chau Thanh dictrict on the basis of a land area of 2,258 ha and a population of 9,290 of Dong Phuoc commune.
To establish Nhon Nghia A commune in Chau Thanh A district on the basis of a land area of 1,840 ha and a population of 11,999 of Nhon Nghia commune.
To establish Truong Long A commune in Chau Thanh A district on the basis of a land area of 2,771 ha and a population of 10,619 of Truong Long Tay commune.- (Summary)
