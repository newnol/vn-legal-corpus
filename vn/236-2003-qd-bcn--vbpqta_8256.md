# Quyết định 236/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 236/2003/QD-BCN of December 29, 2003 approving the organization and operation charter of the Center for Industrial Information Technology
Under the charter issued together with this Decision, the Center for Industrial Information Technology, CIIT, is a non-business unit under the Ministry of Industry, which has the function of researching into, applying and developing informatic technology on State administrative management; organizing activities of information provision and investment consultancy; training and fostering officials and public employees according to law provisions.
The Center shall have the legal person status, its own seal and accounts.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
