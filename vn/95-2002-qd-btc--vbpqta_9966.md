# Quyết định 95/2002/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 95/2002/QD-BTC of August 15, 2002 on making notices on single payment of tax at a stable level for a given period of time by medium- and small-sized business households
The tax offices shall make notices on single payment of tax at a stable level for a given period of time by medium- and small-sized business households which have not yet implemented the accounting and voucher regime.- (Summary)
