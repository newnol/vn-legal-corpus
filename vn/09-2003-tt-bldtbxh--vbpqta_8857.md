# Thông tư 09/2003/TT-BLĐTBXH

CIRCULAR No. 09/2003/TT-BLDTBXH of April 15, 2003 guiding the implementation of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 187/2002/QD-TTg of December 30, 2002 on salary and allowance regime for officials and employees of the Social Policy Bank
Subject to the application of the salary and allowance regime under Decision No. 187/2002/QD-TTg are officials and employees working at the Social Policy Bank, including the Managing Board; the central executive body; transaction centers, transaction bureaus and branches of all levels.
This Circular guides the salary ranking, wage price unit and mid-shift meal regime for the above-said subjects.- (Summary)
