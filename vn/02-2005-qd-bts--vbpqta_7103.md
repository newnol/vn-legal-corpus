# Quyết định 02/2005/QĐ-BTS

DECISION No. 02/2005/QD-BTS of January 30, 2005 defining the functions, tasks and organizational structure of Southern Sub-Institute of Fisheries Planning
The Southern Sub-Institute of Fisheries Planning (SIFIP) is an economic non-business unit under the Fisheries Economic and Planning Institute, functioning to conduct research and elaborate fisheries development plannings.
It is headquartered at No. 30 Ham Nghi street, District 1, Ho Chi Minh city.
