# Directive 38/2006/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On a number of urgent measures for drought prevention and combat and protection of the 2006-2007 winter-spring crop
The Prime Minister requests ministries and localities to perform several tasks in order to cope with drought and protect the 2006-2007 winter-spring crop.- (Summary)
