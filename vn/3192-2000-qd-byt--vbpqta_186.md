# Decision 3192/2000/QD-BYT

THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH
Decision No. 3192/2000/QD-BYT of September 11, 2000 promulgating the Regulation on the functions and tasks of the medicinal professional sections under the provincial/municipal Health Services
The Regulation issued together with this Decision specifies the position, functions, tasks, organization, operation funding and working relations of the medicinal professional sections under the provincial/municipal Health Services.- ((Summary)
