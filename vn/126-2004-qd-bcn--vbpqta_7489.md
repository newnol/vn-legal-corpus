# Quyết định 126/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 126/2004/QD-BCN of November 8, 2004 transforming the Consulting Designing and Investment Service Company into Consulting Designing and Investment Service Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, the joint-stock company, abbreviated to INFISCO, shall have a charter capital of VND 4,000,000,000, of which equities to be sold to laborers of the company represent 95.27% and equities sold to other subjects, 4.73%.
Its head-office is located at No. 9 Nguyen Hong street, Ba Dinh district, Hanoi.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
