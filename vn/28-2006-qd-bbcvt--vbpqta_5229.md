# Quyết định 28/2006/QĐ-BBCVT

DECISION
Promulgating branch standards
Promulgated together with this Decision are two branch standards TCN 68-161: 2006 “Protection of telecommunication cables and radio stations against harmful effects from electric power lines - Technical requirements” and TCN 68-174: 2006 “Code of practice for lightning protection and earthing for telecommunication plants”.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
