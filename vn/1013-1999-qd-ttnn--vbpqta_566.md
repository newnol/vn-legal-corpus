# Decision 1013/1999/QĐ-TTNN

THE STATE INSPECTORATE
DECISION No. 1013/1999/QD-TTNN of November 25, 1999 promulgating the Regulation on examinations for grade-promoting inspectors to senior inspectors
The examination for grade-promotion of inspectors to senior inspectors for shall be organized by the State Inspectorate in coordination with the Government Commission for Organization and Personnel according to the Regulation on grade-promotion examinations.
The Regulation promulgated together with this Decision specifies the conditions for taking the examinations, the dossiers for examination registration, and the forms and contents of examinations for grade-promotion inspectors to senior inspectors.- (Summary)
