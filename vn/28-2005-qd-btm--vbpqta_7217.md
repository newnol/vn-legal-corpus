# Quyết định 28/2005/QĐ-BTM

DECISION OF THE MINISTER OF TRADE
Authorizing the approval of liquidation of machinery, equipment, transport means,
supplies and raw materials of foreign-invested enterprises
THE MINISTER OF TRADE
Pursuant to the Government's Decree No. 29/2004/ND-CP of January 16, 2004 on the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Ministry of Trade;
Pursuant to the Government's Decree No. 24/2000/ND-CP of July 31, 2000 detailing the implementation of the Law on Foreign Investment in Vietnam;
After consulting the People's Committees and the Industrial-Park and Export-Processing Zone Management Boards of the provinces and centrally-run cities;
At the proposal of the Director of Planning and Investment Department,
DECIDES:
Article 1.- To authorize the People's Committees and the Industrial-Park and Export-Processing Zone Management Boards of the provinces and centrally-run cities (hereinafter referred collectively to as authorized agencies) to approve the liquidation of machinery, equipment, transport means, supplies and raw materials of joint-venture enterprises, 100% foreign-capital enterprises, export-processing enterprises and parties to business cooperation contracts, which fall under the management scope of authorized agencies.
Article 2.- The liquidation approval shall comply with the provisions of the Trade Ministry's Circular No. 01/2005/TT-BTM of January 6, 2005 guiding the liquidation of machinery, equipment, transport means, supplies and raw materials of foreign-invested enterprises.
Article 3.- This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
Article 4.- The agencies stated in Article 1, the Director of the Ministry Office, the Director of the Planning and Investment Department, the heads of the Ministry's units and concerned organizations and individuals shall have to implement this Decision./.
