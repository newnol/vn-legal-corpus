# Quyết định 93/2006/QĐ-BNN

DECISION
Defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Inspectorate of the Animal Health Department
The Inspectorate of the Animal Health Department functions to conduct administrative and specialized inspection within the scope of state management by the Animal Health Department according to the provisions of law.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” annulling Decision No. 36 NN-TY/QD of January 25, 1994, of the Minister of Agriculture and Food Industry (now the Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development).- (Summary)
