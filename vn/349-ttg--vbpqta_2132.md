# Decision 349/TTg

Decision No.349-TTg of May 28, 1996 of the Prime Minister on the functions and tasks of the Plant Protection Department under the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development
The Plant Protection Department assists the Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development in performing the function of State management over plant protection and quarantine, and plant protection drugs throughout the country.- (Summary)
Decision No.350-TTg of May 28, 1996 of the Prime Minister on the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Forestry Development Department under the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development
The Forestry Development Department assists the Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development in performing the function of State management over the establishment, restoration and development of protection forests, special-use forests and planted timber forests and socializing the development of forestry.- (Summary)
