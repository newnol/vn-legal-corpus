# Quyết định 233/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 233/2003/QD-BCN of December 26, 2003 amending a number of contents of the Industry Minister's Decision No. 162/2003/QD-BCN of October 13, 2003 which transforms Vinh Thinh Chemicals Company into Vinh Thinh Chemicals Joint-Stock Company
This Decision amends Clauses 1 and 3 of Article 1 of the above-said Decision No. 162/2003/QD-BCN and takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
