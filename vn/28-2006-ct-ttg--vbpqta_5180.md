# Directive 28/2006/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
Enhancing the management of investment and construction funded with state capital in the second half of 2006
With this Directive, the Prime Minister instructs ministries and agencies drafting decrees guiding the implementation of the Investment Law, the Bidding Law, the Land Law and the Enterprise Law, and decrees on management and use of ODA and management of construction investment projects to expeditiously finalize such drafts and submit them to the Government for promulgation in August 2006.
He also requests concerned ministries, branches and localities to perform several tasks in order to raise investment and construction efficiency.- (Summary)
