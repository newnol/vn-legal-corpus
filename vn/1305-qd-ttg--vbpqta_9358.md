# Decision 1305/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 1305/QD-TTg of October 1, 2001 amending and supplementing a number of contents of the Prime Minister�s Decision No. 634/QD-TTg of July 20, 1998 which approves the investment project on building Bai Chay bridge on National Highway No. 18
- To amend Clause 3 of Article 1 on the project size and design standards.
- To amend Clause 4 of Article 1 on the total investment capital: The total investment capital amount is JPY 10,210 million and VND 836,898 million.
- To amend Clause 6 of Article 1 on the implementation tempo: The project will commence in June 2002 and be completed in December 2005.
- To supplement Clause 1 of Article 2: To discuss with the Defense Ministry in order to reach agreement on defense-related matters of the project.- (Summary)
