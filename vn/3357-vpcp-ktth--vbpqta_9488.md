#  3357/VPCP-KTTH

CORRIGENDUM No. 3357/VPCP-KTTH OF JULY 30, 2001 CORRECTING THE GOVERNMENT�S DECREE No. 26/2001/ND-CP OF JUNE 4, 2001 AMENDING AND SUPPLEMENTING A NUMBER OF ARTICLES OF THE GOVERNMENT�S DECREE No. 30/1998/ND-CP OF MAY 13, 1998 WHICH DETAILS THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THE LAW ON ENTERPRISE INCOME TAX
Due to negligence in the edition process, the Government Office hereby corrects an error in the Government�s Decree No. 26/2001/ND-CP of June 4, 2001 amending and supplementing a number articles of the Government�s Decree No. 30/1998/ND-CP of May 13, 1998 which details the implementation of the Law on Enterprise Income Tax, as follows:
The phrase in Clause 5 of Article 1 at page 3, line 3 counting from the bottom: "(including the refunded income tax amount and the amount of income tax gained due to capital transfer)"
Now shall be read as: "(including the refunded income tax amount and the income amount gained from capital transfer)".
For the Minister-Director of the Government Office Deputy Director NGUYEN QUOC HUY
