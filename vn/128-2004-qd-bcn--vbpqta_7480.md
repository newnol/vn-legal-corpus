# Quyết định 128/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 128/2004/QD-BCN of November 12, 2004 transforming Cam Pha Coal Processing and Trading Company into Cam Pha Coal Processing and Trading Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Cam Pha Coal Processing and Trading Joint-Stock Company (CPT) is headquartered at No. 170 Tran Phu road, Cam Tay ward, Cam Pha town, Quang Ninh province.
CPT's charter capital is VND 8,378 million, 62.65% of which to be held by the State, 36.17% sold to its laborers and 1.18% to outsiders.- (Summary)
