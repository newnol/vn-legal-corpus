# Thông tư liên tịch 04/1999/TTLT/BYT-BTC-BLĐTBXH

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 04/1999/TTLT/BYT-BTC-BLDTBXH of March 15, 1999 amending and supplementing Joint Circular No.15/1998/TTLT of December 5, 1998 guiding the implementation of the compulsory health insurance
This Circular was jointly issued by the Ministry of Health, the Ministry of Finance and the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs, amending and supplementing a number of points in Section 6, Part I of Joint Circular No.15/1998/TTLT of December 5, 1998 guiding the implementation of the compulsory health insurance.- (Summary)
