# Quyết định 141/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 141/2004/QD-BCN of November 22, 2004 on transforming Nam Dinh Silk Textile Company into Nam Dinh Silk Textile Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Nam Dinh Silk Textile Joint-Stock Company, NASILKMEX, has a charter capital of VND 23,000,000,000, of which the State's equities represent 51%, equities sold to laborers of the company, 39.09% and equities sold to other subjects, 9.91%.
Its head - office is located at No. 2 Ha Huy Tap street, Nam Dinh city, Nam Dinh province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
