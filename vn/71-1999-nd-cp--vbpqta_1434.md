# Nghị định 71/1999/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 71/1999/ND-CP of August 16, 1999 on the readjustment of administrative boundary for the establishment of new communes and wards in Hoi An town, Hien district and the renaming of Giang district, Quang Nam province
To establish Thanh Ha ward of Hoi An provincial town on the basis of a land area of 613 ha and a population of 8,779 of Cam Ha commune.
To establish Tan An ward of Hoi An provincial town on the basis of a land area of 73.5 ha and a population of 4,233 of Cam Pho ward; a land area of 74 ha and a population of 775 of Cam Ha commune.
To establish Ga Ri commune of Hien district on the basis of a land area of 4,703 ha and a population of 1,004 of Ch�Om district.
To establish A Nong commune of Hien district on the basis of a land area of 5,164 ha and a population of 853 of A Tieng commune.
To establish Jo Ngay commune of Hien district on the basis of a land area of 5,574 ha and a population of 1,740 of Song Kon commune.
To rename Giang district as Nam Giang district.- (Summary)
