# Quyết định 207/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 207/2003/QD-TTg of October 9, 2003 experimenting the organization and operation of the Transport and Ship-Chartering Company after the parent company-affiliate company model
This Decision of the Prime Minister approves the scheme on experimenting the organization and operation of the Transport and Ship-Chartering Company after the parent company-affiliate company, aiming to renew the organization and management thereof, to change the administrative linkage between the company and its dependent cost-accounting members into a financial investment mechanism, to create a close linkage with clear determination of rights and responsibilities regarding capital and economic interests of enterprises having the legal person status, to better provide information, marketing, consumption, supply, research and training services to member enterprises; and to create conditions for enterprises to expand their production capacities and raise their business efficiency and competitiveness.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
