# Decree 50/2000/NĐ-CP

THE GOVERNMENT
DECREE No. 50/2000/ND-CP of September 22, 2000 on the establishment of the district township of Phu Cu district, Hung Yen province
To establish Tran Cao district township, (the capital of Phu Cu district) Hung Yen province on the basis of the total land area of 472 ha and a population of 4,745 of Tran Cao commune.-
