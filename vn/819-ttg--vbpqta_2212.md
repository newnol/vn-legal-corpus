# Decision 819/TTg

DECISION No. 819-TTg OF DECEMBER 14, 1995 OF THE PRIME MINISTER SPECIFYING A NUMBER OF REGIMES OF SPECIAL ALLOWANCES FOR STATE OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES WORKING IN THE HYDROMETEOROLOGICAL BRANCH
- Flood and storm monitoring allowances applied to the monitors working on the days with floods or storms.
- Allowances for State officials and employees conducting storm monitoring or sea survey by aircraft and ships.
- Temporary monthly meal allowances for State officials and employees working at sea and at meteorological stations on islands.- (Summary)
