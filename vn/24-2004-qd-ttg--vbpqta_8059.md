# Quyết định 24/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 24/2004/QD-TTg of February 26, 2004 on the establishment of Nam Dinh Convalescence University
To establish Nam Dinh Convalescence University on the basis of Nam Dinh Medical College.
Nam Dinh Convalescence University is a tertiary training establishment under the Ministry of Health, subject to the State management over education by the Ministry of Education and Training, has the legal person status, its own seal and bank account. It shall operate under the University Regulation promulgated together with the Prime Minister's Decision No. 153/2003/QD-TTg of July 30, 2003.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
