# Circular 02/2008/TT-BNV

CIRCULAR
Amending Point b, Clause 1, Section I of the Home Affairs Ministry’s Circular No. 04/2007/TT-BNV of June 21, 2007, which guides the implementation of a number of articles of the Government’s Decree No. 116/2003/ND-CP of October 10, 2003, and Decree No. 121/2006/ND-CP of October 23, 2006, amending and supplementing a number of articles of Decree No. 116/2003/ND-CP on recruitment, employment and management of cadres and public employees in state non-business units
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” annulling Point b, Clause 1, Section I of Circular No. 04/2007/TT-BNV.- (Summary)
