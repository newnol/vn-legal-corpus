# Nghị định 112/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 112/2002/ND-CP of December 31, 2002 establishing communes of Lam Ha, Da Huoai, Da Teh and Cat Tien districts, Lam Dong province
To establish Nam Ha commune of Lam Ha district on the basis of a land area of 2,345 ha and a population of 4,103 of Nam Ban district township, which now has a land area of 2,089 ha and a population of 10,912.
To establish Phuoc Loc commune of Da Huoai district on the basis of a land area of 7,766 ha and a population of 3,008 of Ha Lam commune, which now has a land area of 4,414 ha and a population of 2,611.
To establish Da Pal commune of Da Teh district on the basis of a land area of 4,600 ha and a population of 2,834 of Trieu Hai commune, which is now narrowed to have a land area of 3,900 ha and a population of 2,956.
To establish Dong Nai Thuong commune of Cat Tien district on the basis of a land area of 9,395 ha and a population of 2,429 of Tien Hoang commune, which now has a land area of 5,022.91 ha and a population of 2,726.- (Summary)
