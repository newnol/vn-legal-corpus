# Joint circular 1699/2001/TTLT/BQP-BLDTBXH-BTC

Joint Circular No. 1699/2001/TTLT-BQP-BLDTBXH-BTC of June 19, 2001 guiding the implementation of the Government’s Decree No. 04/2001/ND-CP of January 16, 2001 which details the implementation of a number of articles of the 1999 Law on Vietnam People’s Army Officers regarding regimes and policies towards demobilized officers and officers who have been transferred to work as professional armymen or military employees
Subject to this Circular are professional army officers, who have retired, been transferred to civilian jobs or demobilized; officers who have been transferred to work as professional armymen or military employees; and professional armymen, who have been transferred to work as military employees.
The Circular guides in detail the regimes and policies towards the above-mentioned subjects.- (Summary)
