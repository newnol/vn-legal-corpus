# Decree 44/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 44/2002/ND-CP of April 11, 2002 establishing wards of Thanh Hoa provincial city and Bim Son provincial town, and Ben Sung district township of Nhu Thanh district, Thanh Hoa province
To establish Tan Son ward of Thanh Hoa provincial city with a land area of 78 ha and a population of 11,114.
To establish Dong Son ward of Bim Son provincial town with a land area of 1,931.1 ha and a population of 8,650.
To establish Ben Sung district township of Nhu Thanh district with a land area of 584.15 ha and a population of 3,955.- (Summary)
