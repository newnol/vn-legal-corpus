# Quyết định 88/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 88/2001/QD-TTg of June 5, 2001 on wage and allowance regimes for officials and employees working for Vietnam Deposit Insurance
The Prime Minister prescribes that the wage and allowance regimes stipulated in the Government�s Decree No. 26/CP of May 23, 1993 temporarily providing for the new wage regime in State enterprises shall apply to officials and employees working for Vietnam Deposit Insurance.
Vietnam Deposit Insurance shall be entitled to apply the minimum wage increase coefficient prescribed in the Government�s Decree No. 28/CP of March 28, 1997 on renewing the management of wages and incomes in State enterprises, provided that:
- The safety of capital allocated by the State is ensured;
- The average wage increase rate must be lower than the average labor productivity increase rate.- (Summary)
