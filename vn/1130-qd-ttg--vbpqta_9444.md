# Decision 1130/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 1130/QD-TTg of August 27, 2001 transforming the State-run Thong Nhat Match Company into a joint-stock company
To transform the State-run Thong Nhat Match Company, which is a member enterprise of Vietnam Paper Corporation, into Thong Nhat Match Joint-Stock Company (TMJCO).
Its head office: 670 Ngo Gia Tu street, Duc Giang district township, Gia Lam district, Hanoi city.
Its charter capital is VND 15,719,000,000, of which State equities represent 32%, equities sold to laborers inside the enterprise - 58% and equities sold to the outsiders - 10%.
Thong Nhat Match Joint-Stock Company is engaged in the following business lines:
- Producing and trading in match and paper;
- Conducting direct export and import;
- Engaged in other business lines as prescribed by law.- (Summary)
