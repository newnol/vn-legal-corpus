# Thông tư 47/1998/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No.47/1998/TT-BTC of April 9, 1998 amending and supplementing Circular No.9/1998/TT-BTC of January 20, 1998 guiding the management and use of money collected from the anti-smuggling activities.
This Circular amends and supplements the content of Item a, Point 3, Section III of Circular No.09/1998/TT-BTC on the distribution and use of money amounts deducted to reward the organizations and individuals with achievements in the combat against smuggling.- (Summary)
