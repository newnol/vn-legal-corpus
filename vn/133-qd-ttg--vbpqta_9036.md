# Decision 133/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 133/QD-TTg of January 30, 2003 approving the general plan on re-organization and renovation of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Soc Trang province in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises where the State continues to hold 100% of their charter capital and their legal person status remains unchanged: 1 public-utility enterprise and 5 enterprises engaged in business activities.
Enterprises to be equitized where the State holds more than 50% of their charter capital: 4 enterprises in 2003 and 1 enterprise in 2004.
Enterprises to be equitized where the State holds under 50% of their charter capital or does not hold shares: 2 enterprises in 2003, 1 enterprise in 2004, and 1 enterprise in 2005.
One enterprise shall be transformed into non-business unit with revenues in 2003.
Enterprises to be dissolved or declared bankrupt: 2 enterprises in 2003.- (Summary)
