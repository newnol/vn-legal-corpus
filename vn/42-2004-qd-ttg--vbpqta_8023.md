# Quyết định 42/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 42/2004/QD-TTg of March 23, 2004 assigning the task of doctoral training to Thai Nguyen university
This Decision assigns the task of doctoral training to Thai Nguyen university with specific training disciplines to be considered and prescribed by the Ministry of Education and Training. It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
