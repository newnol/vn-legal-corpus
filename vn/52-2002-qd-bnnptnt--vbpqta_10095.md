# Quyết định 52/2002/QĐ-BNNPTNT

DECISION No. 52/2002/QD-BNNPTNT of June 18, 2002 merging Song Be husbandry research and experimentation center and milch cow farming research and coaching center into a cattle husbandry research and training center under the Southern Agricultural Science and Technique Institute
The cattle husbandry research and training center under the Southern Agricultural Science and Technique Institute is a cattle-related research and experimentation, training, technological transfer unit.
The center shall be entitled to use its own seal and open its own accounts.- (Summary)
