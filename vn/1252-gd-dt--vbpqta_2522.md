# Decision 1252/GD-ĐT

THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION AND TRAINING
DECISION No.1252-GD/DT of April 11, 1997 amending and supplementing a number of provisions on the senior-high school (elementary and specialized) graduation exams and the ratification thereof.
This Decision amends and supplements the provisions on the examination supervising councils, the grant of graduation diplomas, the conditions for being recognized as graduates, the keeping of examination papers and the classification of graduates.- (Summary)
