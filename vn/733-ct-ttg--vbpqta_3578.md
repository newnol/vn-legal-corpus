# Directive 733/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On the elaboration of socio-economic development plans and state budget estimates for 2008
The Prime Minister requests ministries, branches, localities, economic groups and Corporations 91 to concentrate their efforts on the elaboration of socio-economic development plans and state budget estimates for 2008 according to the requirements specified in this Directive.- (Summary)
