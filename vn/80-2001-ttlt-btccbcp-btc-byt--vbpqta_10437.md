# Joint circular 80/2001/TTLT/BTCCBCP-BTC-BYT

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 80/2001/TTLT-BTCCBCP-BTC-BYT of December 5, 2001 guiding the implementation of the Prime Minister?s Decision No. 97/2001/QD-TTg of June 26, 2001 supplementing the allowance regimes for officials and employees of the heath service
