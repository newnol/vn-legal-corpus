# Decree 64/2000/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 64/2000/ND-CP of November 6, 2000 on adjustment of administrative boundary of Chau Thanh district for re-establishment of Chau Thanh A district, Can Tho province
To adjust the administrative boundary of Chau Thanh district for re-establishment of Chau Thanh A district, Can Tho province as follows:
To re-establish Chau Thanh A district on the basis of a natural land area of 22,139 ha and a population of 163,357 of Chau Thanh district. Chau Thanh A district has 8 dependent administrative units, comprising: Tan Thuan, Thanh Xuan, Tan Hoa, Truong Long, Nhon Ai, Nhon Nghia, Truong Long Tay and Tan Phu Thanh communes.-
DECREE No. 65/2000/ND-CP of November 6, 2000 on adjustment of administrative boundary of Ninh Son district for re-establishment of Bac Ai district, Ninh Thuan province
To adjust the administrative boundary of Ninh Son district for re-establishment of Bac Ai district, Ninh Thuan province as follows:
To re-establish Bac Ai district on the basis of a natural land area of 103,090.18 ha and a population of 29,835 of Ninh Son district. Bac Ai district has 9 dependent administrative units, comprising: Phuoc Binh, Phuoc Hoa, Phuoc Tan, Phuoc Dai, Phuoc Tien, Phuoc Thang, Phuoc Thanh, Phuoc Trung and Phuoc Chinh communes.- (Summary)
