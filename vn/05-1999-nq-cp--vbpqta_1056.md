# Nghị quyết 05/1999/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION No.05/1999/NQ-CP of May 5, 1999 on the Government April 1999 regular meeting
On April 28 and 29, 1999, the Government held its April 1999 regular meeting to discuss and resolve the following issues:
- The Government heard the Minister of Planning and Investment presenting the plan for "Bringing into play the internal resources, raising the investment capability, vigorously stimulating production and raising the rational consumption".
- It heard the State Auditor General presenting the draft Ordinance on the State Audit. The Government assigned the State Audit to coordinate with the concerned agencies in gathering comments from cabinet members for studying and finalizing the draft Ordinance, in which clarification must be made on such contents as the legal status, the organization and operation; powers and responsibilities of the State Audit; as well as the auditing objects, scope and process; so as to submit them to the Government for consideration at another meeting.
- It adopted the draft Regulation on bidding issued together with Decrees No.43/CP and No.93/CP (amended), presented by the Minister of Planning and Investment. It assigned the Ministry of Planning and Investment to coordinate with the concerned agencies in gathering comments from cabinet members, and finalizing the draft and submitting it to the Prime Minister for signing for promulgation in May 1999; and at the same time submitting to the Prime Minister for promulgation a directive on reorganization of the bidding and bid approving activities at the ministries, branches and localities with a view to overcoming negative acts and ensuring the truthfulness, objectivity and effectiveness of this activity.
- It adopted a draft decree detailing the implementation of the Law on Domestic Investment Promotion (amended), presented by the Minister of Planning and Investment. It assigned the Ministry of Planning and Investment to coordinate with the Government Office in finalizing such decree, then submitting it to the Prime Minister for signing for promulgation in May 1999.
- The Government also heard reports on "Result of revision of legal documents in the fields of export, import and customs"; "Situation and settlement of complaints and denunciations in the first quarter of 1999"; and "Socio-economic situation in April and the first four months of 1999".- (Summary)
