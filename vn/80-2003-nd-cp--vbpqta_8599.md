# Nghị định 80/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 80/2003/ND-CP of July 10, 2003 adjusting administrative boundaries of Ya Ly and Ya Xier communes, and establishing Ya Tang commune of Sa Thay rural district, Kon Tum province
To transfer a land area of 1,146 ha and a population of 659 of Ya Xier commune to Ya Ly commune for management.
To establish Ya Tang commune on the basis of a land area of 18,213 ha and a population of 3,162 of Ya Ly commune which is now left with a land area of 3,823 ha and a population of 2,074.
Ya Xier shall be left with a land area of 4,074 ha and a population of 2,910.- (Summary)
