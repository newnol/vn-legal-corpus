# Quyết định 30/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 30/2001/QD-TTg of March 12, 2001 assigning the task of doctorate training to the Folklore Research Institute
To assign the task of doctorate training to the Folklore Research Institute, which is under the National Center of Social Sciences and Humanity.
The Ministry of Education and Training shall assume the prime responsibility and coordinate with the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment, and the National Center of Social Science and Humanity in prescribing specialties of doctorate training for the Folklore Research Institute.- (Summary)
