# Quyết định 235/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.235/1999/QD-TTg of December 23, 1999 on setting up the Steering Board for the campaign "the entire people unite to build the cultural life"
To set up the Steering Board for the campaign "the entire people unite to build the cultural life" to step up the propaganda, education and performance of cultural tasks in the new period, education of patriotism and socialism in association with the patriotic emulation movement and the cause of industrialization and modernization.
The Steering Board is composed of the Minister of Culture and Information as its Head, the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front as its Deputy Head, and representatives of the leaderships of the Party Central Committee�s Commission for Culture and Ideology and Commission for Mass Mobilization, the State Institute of Emulation and Commendation, the Central Committee of the Vietnam Peasants� Association, the Central Committee of the Vietnam War Veterans� Association, the Central Committee of the Vietnam Women Union, the Central Committee of the Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth Union, the Vietnam Labor Confederation, the National Committee of the Vietnam Union of Literature and Art Societies, the National Center for Social Sciences and Humanities, the Ministry of Defense, the Ministry of Education and Training, the Ministry of Public Security, the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs, the Commission for Ethnic Minorities and Mountainous Areas, the Commission for Physical Training and Sports, the Vietnam Television Station and the Radio Voice of Vietnam, as its members.- (Summary)
