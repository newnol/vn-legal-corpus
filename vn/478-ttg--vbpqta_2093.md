# Decision 478/TTg

Decision No.478-TTg of July 24, 1996 of the Prime Minister to upgrade the Light Industry Eco-Technical School into the Light Industry Eco-Technical College.
The Light Industry Economic-Technical College is placed directly under the Ministry of Industry and subject to the branch management of the Ministry of Education and Training.- (Summary)
