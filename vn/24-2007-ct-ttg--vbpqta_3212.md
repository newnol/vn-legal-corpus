# Directive 24/2007/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
Further enhancing the implementation of legal provisions on charges and fees and policies on mobilization and use of people’s contributions
The Prime Minister requests ministries, branches and localities to strictly abide by legal provisions on charges and fees and policies on mobilization and use of people’s contributions.- (Summary)
