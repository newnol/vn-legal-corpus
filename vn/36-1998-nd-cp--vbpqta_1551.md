# Nghị định 36/1998/NĐ-CP

THE GOVERNMENT
DECREE No.36/1998/ND-CP of June 9, 1998 on dividing Tam Dao district of Vinh Phuc province into Tam Duong and Binh Xuyen districts.
The Tam Duong district shall have a land area of 20,988 ha and a population of 135,171, including 18 communal-level administrative units.
The Binh Xuyen district shall have a land area of 21,401 ha and a population of 115,546, including 14 communal-level administrative units.- (Summary)
