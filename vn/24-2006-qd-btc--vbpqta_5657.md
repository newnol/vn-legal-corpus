# Decision 24/2006/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
Promulgating the regime of state budget accounting and state treasury operations
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces the contents on general provisions, system of accounting documents, system of accounts and system of accounting books, issued together with Decision No. 130/2003/QD-BTC of August 18, 2003.
The regime of financial statements and administration accounting reports shall still comply with Decision No. 130/2003/QD-BTC.- (Summary)
