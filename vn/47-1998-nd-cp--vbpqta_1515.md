# Nghị định 47/1998/NĐ-CP

DECREE No.47/1998/ND-CP of July 8, 1998 on the establishment of Ha Tien provincial capital, Kien Giang province, and its wards.
To establish Ha Tien provincial capital with a land area of 8,573.39 hectares and a population of 34,541; it comprises 4 wards (Binh Son, Dong Ho, To Chau and Phao Dai) and 3 communes (My Duc, Thuan Yen and Tien Hai).- (Summary)
