# Quyết định 132/1998/QĐ-TTg

THE PRIME MINISTER
DECISION No. 132/1998/QD-TTg of July 21, 1998 assigning the task of executing the program for building of models in scientific and technological application in service of socio-economic development of rural and mountainous areas in the 1998-2002 period
To assign the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment to organize the execution of the program for building of models in scientific and technological application in service of socio-economic development of rural and mountainous areas in the 1998-2002 period.
The Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment shall have to set up the steering board of the program; coordinate with the People's Committees of the provinces and cities directly under the Central Government in integrating this program with the national target programs and other socio-economic programs in order to ensure the efficiency of the execution of various programs in the same locality.-
