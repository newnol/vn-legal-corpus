# Decree 62/2001/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 62/2001/ND-CP of September 12, 2001 promulgating the Regulation on the organization of funerals for deceased State officials and employees
Issued together with this Decree is the Regulation on the organization of funerals for deceased State officials and employees, which prescribes the organization of funerals for high-ranking officials who are incumbent or have been relieved from their posts, as well as incumbent or retired State officials and employees.- (Summary)
