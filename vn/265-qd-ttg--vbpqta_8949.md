# Quyết định 265/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 265/QD-TTg of March 11, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Ha Tinh province till 2005
Enterprises which continue to have 100% of their charter capital held by the State and be legal entities: 10 public-utility enterprises and 7 business enterprises.
To be equitized, with the State holding over 50% of charter capital: In 2003: 3 enterprises; in 2004: 5 enterprises and in 2005: 2 enterprises.
To be equitized, with the State holding under 50% of charter capital or holding no shares: In 2003: 5 enterprises; in 2004: 3 enterprises and in 2005: 2 enterprises.
To be assigned or sold in 2003: 4 enterprises.
To be relocated to other localities in 2003: 2 enterprises.
To be merged in 2003: 2 enterprises.
Enterprises undertaking contractual business assignment, which shall be equitized, with the State holding under 50% of charter capital or holding no share in 2005: 2 enterprises.- (Summary)
