# Quyết định 107/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 107/2004/QD-BCN of October 12, 2004 transforming Van Diem Paper Company into Van Diem Paper Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Van Diem Paper Joint-Stock Company, abbreviated to VPC, has a charter capital of VND 10,500,000,000; of which the State's equities represent 30%; equities sold to laborers in the company, 66.47%; and equities sold other subjects, 3.53%.
Its head office is located in Phu Minh township, Phu Xuyen rural district, Ha Tay province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
