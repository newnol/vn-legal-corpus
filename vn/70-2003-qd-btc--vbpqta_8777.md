# Quyết định 70/2003/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 70/2003/QD-BTC of May 15, 2003 prescribing the time limit for terminating the handling of entries of investment capital support budget revenues and expenditures for State enterprises
As from May 31, 2003, to terminate the handling of entries of investment capital support revenues and expenditures for enterprises from debt amounts payable to the budget, including taxes and budget remittable amounts, as from December 31, 1999 backwards, which had been appropriated by State enterprises for investment into fixed assets under the approved projects and which could still not have been paid so far.
For investment capital support dossiers sent by enterprises to the Finance Ministry before January 17, 2003, if all procedures prescribed by the Finance Ministry are met, the handling of entries of investment capital support revenues and expenditures shall continue till the end of May 30, 2003.- (Summary)
