# Quyết định 238/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 238/2003/QD-TTg of November 13, 2003 establishing Nam Dinh Industrial Parks Management Board
To establish Nam Dinh province Industrial Parks Management Board under the People's Committee of Nam Dinh province for the management of the industrial parks in Nam Dinh province.
The Management Board shall have to perform the tasks and exercise the powers regarding the management of industrial parks according to the provisions of Article 27 of the Regulation on Industrial Parks, Export-Processing Zones and Hi-Tech Parks, issued together with the Government's Decree No. 36/CP of April 24, 1997.
It has the legal person status, its accounts and seal bearing the national emblem. Its payroll and operation funding is included in the payrolls and budget allocated by the State to Nam Dinh province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
