# Chỉ thị 03/2002/CT-BTC

DIRECTIVE No. 03/2002/CT-BTC of September 6, 2002 enhancing the work of building, training and fostering the contingent of financial officials for communes, wards and townships
The Minister of Finance requested the heads of the units under and attached to the Ministry as well as the directors of the Finance-Pricing Services of the provinces and centrally-run cities to organize the performance of the following tasks:
- To investigate and evaluate the actual situation of the contingent of financial officials in communes and wards;
- To draw up plans on transferring officials from the financial service to those communes and wards which currently lack financial officials;
- To draw up plans on building, training and fostering the contingent of financial officials for communes and wards;
- To make local budget funding estimates for the work of training and fostering financial officials for communes and wards;
- To compile a set of standard programs on training and fostering financial officials for communes and wards.- (Summary)
