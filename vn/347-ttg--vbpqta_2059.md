# Decision 347/TTg

Decision No.347-TTg of May 28, 1996 of the Prime Minister on the tasks and powers of the Ranger Department under the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development
The Ranger Department assists the Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development in performing the function of State management over forest management and protection, professional direction and law enforcement, fighting against violations of law on forest management and protection throughout the country.- (Summary)
