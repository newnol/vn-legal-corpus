# Thông tư 01/2005/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 01/2005/TT-BTC of January 4, 2005 guiding the regime of collection, remittance, management and use of licensing fees, fees for change of license contents, fees for registration of operation of arbitration centers and branches thereof and court fees related to arbitration
Under this Circular, organizations and individuals filing applications for the grant of establishment licenses, change of license contents or registration of operation of arbitration centers and branches thereof or requesting courts to appoint or remove arbitrators; to review arbitration councils’ decisions on arbitration agreements or arbitration councils’ competence to handle disputes; to apply, alter or cancel interim urgent measures; to cancel arbitral awards, or appeal against courts’ rulings shall have to pay fees at the following levels:
- Fee for granting a license for establishment of an arbitration center: VND 500,000;
- Fee for change of an establishment license’s contents: VND 100,000;
- Fee for registration of operation of an arbitration center: VND 200,000;
- Fee for registration of operation of an arbitration center’s branch: VND 100,000;
- Fee for requesting the court to appoint or replace an arbitrator: VND 100,000;
- Fee for requesting the court to review an arbitration council’s decisions on arbitration agreement or an arbitration council’s competence to handle the disputes: VND 300,000;
- Fee for requesting the court to apply, alter or cancel provisional urgent measures: VND 500,000;
- Fee for requesting the court to cancel an arbitral award: VND 500,000;
- Fee for appeal against a court ruling: VND 300,000.- (Summary)
