# Quyết định 1130/2004/QĐ-NHNN

DECISION No. 1130/2004/QD-NHNN of September 9, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on organization and operation of the Department for Banks and Non-Bank Credit Institutions
The Department for Banks and Non-Bank Credit Institutions is a unit under the State Bank, functioning to assist the Governor in performing the State management over the setting-up, operation and development of credit institutions (excluding cooperative credit institutions), as well as banking activities of other organizations, in order to contribute to ensuring the system safety.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
