# Chỉ thị 02/2005/CT-BGTVT

DIRECTIVE No. 02/2005/CT-BGTVT of January 4, 2005 on enhancing the anti-smoking work within the transport service
The Transport Minister issued this Directive requesting all agencies, organizations and individuals in the transport service to immediately perform some tasks to enhance the anti-smoking work.- (Summary)
