# Quyết định 01/2005/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 01/2005/QD-BCN of January 10, 2005 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of Ho Chi Minh City University of Industry
Under this Decision, Ho Chi Minh City University of Industry, HUI, is a unit attached to the Industry Ministry, functions to train and foster human resources of tertiary or lower levels of various industrial econo-technical disciplines. It also functions as a scientific and technological research institute in service of management, production and business activities of the industry sector and socio-economic development.
Its head-office is located at No. 12, Nguyen Van Bao street, Ward 4, Go Vap district, Ho Chi Minh city.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
