# Directive 13/2006/CT-BXD

DIRECTIVE
Enhancing the management of the quality of construction works of private investors
The Minister of Construction requests provincial/municipal People’s Committees, works’ investors and use managers, and contractors engaged in construction activities to perform several tasks in order to raise the quality of construction works of private investors.- (Summary)
