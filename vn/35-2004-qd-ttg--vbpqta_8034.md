# Quyết định 35/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 35/2004/QD-TTg of March 17, 2004 establishing Ninh Binh province's Industrial Parks Management Board
To establish Ninh Binh province's Industrial Parks Management Board under the People's Committee of Ninh Binh province to manage the industrial parks in Ninh Binh province.
Ninh Binh province's Industrial Parks Management Board shall have to perform the tasks and powers in managing industrial parks according to the provisions of Article 27 of the Regulation on Industrial Parks, Export-Processing Zones and Hi-Tech Parks, promulgated together with the Government's Decree No. 36/CP of April 24, 1997.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
