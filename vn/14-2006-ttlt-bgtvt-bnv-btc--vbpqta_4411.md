# Joint circular 14/2006/TTLT-BGTVT-BNV-BTC 

JOINT CIRCULAR
Guiding the implementation of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 70/2006/QD-TTg of March 29, 2006, on the specific sea allowance regime for cadres, civil servants, workers and employees working on marine search and rescue ships, and Decision No. 179/2006/QD-TTg of August 7, 2006, on the job-based preferential allowance regime for the marine search and rescue force
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
