# Quyết định 07/2002/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION No. 07/2002/QD-BGDDT of March 19, 2002 promulgating the Regulation on organizing the form of supervised self-study for complementary senior secondary education
Issued together with this Decision is the Regulation on organizing the form of supervised self-study for complementary senior secondary schools.
Supervised self-study is a study form of informal education, according to which the learners must study learning documents and textbooks by themselves in order to gather knowledge and practice skills by themselves under teachers� guidance.
The form of supervised self-study applies to learners aged 18 years or older, who voluntarily register their study at continued education centers or complementary education schools.
The form of supervised self-study applies only to obligatory study subjects in the complementary junior secondary education program and complementary senior secondary education program.- (Summary)
