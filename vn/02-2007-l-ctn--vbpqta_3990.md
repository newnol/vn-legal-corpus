# Order 02/2007/L-CTN

ORDER
On law promulgation
The State President promulgates Law No. 84/2007/QH11 Amending and Supplementing Article 73 of the Labor Code, giving laborers one more public holiday on Hung Kings’s anniversary (March 10 of the lunar year).- (Summary)
