# Thông tư liên tịch 297/1998/TTLT/KTNN-BTC

JOINT CIRCULAR No.297/1998/TTLT-KTNN-BTC of July 16, 1998 guiding the implementation of the regulations on the uniform for officials of the State auditing system.
The officials who are on the State Audit's payroll shall be supplied with uniform to be worn while on duty. The design and colors of the uniform shall be stipulated by the State Auditor General.
All the audit officials shall have to wear uniform as prescribed when working at the office or at other agencies.- (Summary)
