# Chỉ thị 07/2005/CT-BXD

DIRECTIVE
On the implementation of the Politburo's Decision No. 41/NQ-TW and the Prime Minister's Decision No. 34/2005/QD-TTg promulgating the Government's action program for implementation of Resolution No. 41/NQ-TW
With this Directive, the Minister of Construction requests heads of units in the construction sector to perform several tasks in order to implement the Politburo's Resolution No. 41/NQ-TW of November 15, 2004, on environmental protection in the national industrialization and modernization period; the Prime Minister's Decision No. 34/2005/QD-TTg of February 22, 2005, promulgating the Government's action program for implementation of Resolution No. 41/NQ-TW, and Directive No. 23/2005/CT-TTg of June 21, 2005, on accelerating the management of solid wastes in urban centers and industrial parks.- (Summary)
