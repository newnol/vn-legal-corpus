# Quyết định 107/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 107/1999/QD-TTg of April 23, 1999 on the establishment of the Management Board of Nghe An industrial parks
To establish the Management Board of Nghe An industrial parks to manage industrial parks in Nghe An province.
The Management Board of Nghe An industrial parks shall perform the tasks and exercise powers of management of industrial parks according to Article 27 of the Regulation on Industrial Parks, Export Processing Zones and Hi-Tech Parks, issued together with the Government�s Decree No.36/CP of April 24, 1997.- (Summary)
