# Nghị định 104/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 104/2002/ND-CP of December 18, 2002 separating Ayun Pa rural district of Gia Lai province into Ia Pa and Ayun Pa rural districts
To establish Ia Pa rural district on the basis of a natural land area of 87,088 ha and a population of 41,484 of Ayun Pa rural district. This rural district shall comprise 9 dependent administrative units being Po To, Chu Rang, Kim Tan, Ia Tul, Ia Broai, Ia Kdam, Chu Mo, Ia Trok and Ia Mron communes.
After the adjustment of its administrative boundaries, Ayun Pa rural district shall have a natural land area of 78,891.5 ha and a population of 87,648, comprising 12 dependent administrative units being Chu A Thai, Ia Ke, Ia Sol, Ia Piar, Ia Peng, Ia Hiao, Ia Rbol, Ia Yeng, Ia Rto and Ia Sao communes and Ayun Pa and Phu Thien district townships.- (Summary)
