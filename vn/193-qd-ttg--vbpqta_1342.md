# Decision 193/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 193/QD-TTg of October 1st, 1998 annulling 9 decisions regarding the establishment of inter-branch organizations
To annul the 9 following decisions:
1. Decision No.205/CT of May 28, 1984 of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers on the establishment of the Sub-Committee for study of Vietnam's continental shelf.
2. Decision No.05/CT of January 12, 1989 of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers on the establishment of the steering group for the drafting of new tax laws.
3. Decision No.50/CT of March 4, 1989 of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers on the establishment of the Board for studying the exploitation of Ca Mau peninsula.
4. Decision No.42/CT of February 8, 1990 of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers on the establishment of the State Council for evaluation of the general plan for development of Vietnam's electric power industry in the 1991-1995 period.
5. Decision No.62/CT of March 1st, 1990 of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers on the establishment of the Steering Committee for the planning and building of Hanoi capital.
6. Decision No.113/CT of April 6, 1992 of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers on the establishment of the State Council for acceptance inspection upon completion of the North-South 500 KV electric power transmission lines.
7. Decision No.109/TTg of March 22, 1993 of the Prime Minister on the establishment of the Group for study of the plan for handling foreign debts and foreign assets in Vietnam.
8. Decision No.183-TTg of April 16, 1994 of the Prime Minister on the establishment of the National Bid Evaluation Council.
9. Decision No.425-TTg of August 24, 1993 of the Prime Minister on the establishment of the State Council for evaluation and ratification of the technological plan for exploitation and building of Bach Ho oil field.- (Summary)
