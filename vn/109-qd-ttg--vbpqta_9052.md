# Decision 109/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 109/QD-TTg of January 24, 2003 approving the master plan for reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Hung Yen province in the 2002-2005 period
Nine public-utility enterprises and 3 business enterprises shall continue to have 100% of their charter capital held by the State by 2005.
In 2002-2003:
- To be equitized, with the State holding under 50% of their charter capital or holding no equities: 12 enterprises.
- To be equitized, with the State holding under 50% of charter capital or holding no equities: 1 unit.
- To be dissolved: 1 enterprise.
In 2004:
- To be equitized, with the State holding over 50% of its charter capital: 1 enterprise.
- To be equitized , with the State holding under 50% of their charter capital or holding no equities: 9 enterprises.- (Summary)
