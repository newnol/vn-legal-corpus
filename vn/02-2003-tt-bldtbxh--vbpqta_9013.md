# Thông tư 02/2003/TT-BLĐTBXH

CIRCULAR No. 02/2003/TT-BLDTBXH of February 7, 2003 guiding the implementation of the Government’s Decree No. 102/2002/ND-CP of December 11, 2002 detailing the allowance regime for diseased soldiers or workers and employees leaving their jobs and enjoying the working capacity-loss regime, who are also war invalids or persons enjoying policies like war invalids
This Circular guides the subjects and conditions for enjoying allowances, the allowance regime, the dossiers and procedures for the implementation of the allowance regime for diseased soldiers or workers and employees leaving their jobs and enjoying the working capacity-loss regime, who are also war invalids or persons enjoying policies like war invalids.- (Summary)
