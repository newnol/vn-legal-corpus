# Nghị định 65-CP

Decree No.65-Cp of October 28, 1996 of the Government to establish An Luu township of Kim Mon district, Hai Hung province comprising the whole area and population of An Luu commune.-
Decree No.66-CP of October 28, 1996 of the Government to rename Ha Long ward of Ha Long city and readjust the administrative boundaries of Hai Ninh and Co To districts, Quang Ninh province:
- To rename Ha Long ward of Ha Long city into Hong Gai ward.
- To assign the management of Chan island from Hai Ninh district to Co To district.- (Summary)
