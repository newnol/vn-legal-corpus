# Decision 521/2002/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 521/QD-TTg of July 1, 2002 adding capital for the 2002 natural disaster prevention and fight
The Finance Ministry shall deduct VND 207 billion from the 2002 State budget reserve source for investment in the construction of urgent works as well as natural disaster prevention and fight works.- (Summary)
