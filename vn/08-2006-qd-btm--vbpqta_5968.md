# Decision 08/2006/QĐ-BTM

DECISION
Promulgating the Regulation on border markets, border-gate markets and markets in economic zones in land border gates between Vietnam and neighboring countries
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces the Trade Minister’s Decision No. 0774/1998/QD-BTM of July 4, 1998, Decision No. 0807/1998/QD-BTM of July 15, 1998, and Decision No. 0724/1999/QD-BTM of June 8, 1999.- (Summary)
