# Circular 16/GDĐT

THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION OF TRAINING
Circular No.16-GDDT of August 14, 1997 guiding the implementation of the Regulation on organization and operation of the general education boarding schools for ethnic minority pupils.
This Circular guides in details the following contents: the name of the schools, the purpose of the establishment of the schools and the training objectives, the position, characteristics and tasks of the schools, the organizational structures and scales of the schools, the conditions for establishing the schools, the enrollment of students, the school organization and management, the implementation of training programs.- ( Summary)
