# Quyết định 151/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 151/2004/QD-BCN of December 2, 2004 transforming Vinh Son-Song Hinh Hydro Power Plant into Vinh Son-Song Hinh Hydro Power Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Vinh Son-Song Hinh Hydro Power Joint-Stock Company (VSHPC) is headquartered at No. 21 Nguyen Hue street, Quy Nhon city, Binh Dinh province.
Its charter capital is VND 1,250 billion, 60% of which to be held by the State, 5% sold to its laborers and 35% to outsiders.- (Summary)
