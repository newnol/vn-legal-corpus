# Quyết định 18/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.18/1998/QD-TTg of January 24, 1998 on the establishment of the Office of the National Committee for Drug Prevention and Combat.To establish the Office of the National Committee for Drug Prevention and Combat to assist the Committee in organizing the performance of its tasks and plans for drug prevention and combat. The tasks and powers of the Committee Office shall be defined by the Chairman of the National Committee for Drug Prevention and Combat.- (Summary)
