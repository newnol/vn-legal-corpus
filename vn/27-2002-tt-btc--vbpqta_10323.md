# Circular 27/2002/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 27/2002/TT-BTC of March 22, 2002 guiding the financial regime applicable to debt-managing and asset-exploiting companies under commercial banks
Subject to the application of this Circular are debt-managing and asset-exploiting companies under commercial banks set up under the Prime Minister�s Decision No. 150/2001/QD-TTg of October 5, 2001.
This Circular guides the management and use of capital and assets; revenue and expenditure management; profit distribution and deduction for setting up funds; the accounting, inspecting, auditing and reporting regimes as well as financial publicity of the above-said subjects.- (Summary)
