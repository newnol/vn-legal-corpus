# Circular 99/2006/TT-UBTDTT

CIRCULAR
Guiding the implementation of the allowance regime for leaders in physical training and sport non-business organizations
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces Circular No. 408/TT/TC-DT of May 16, 1994, of the General Department of Physical Training and Sports.
The allowance regime provided for in this Circular applies as from October 1, 2004.- (Summary)
