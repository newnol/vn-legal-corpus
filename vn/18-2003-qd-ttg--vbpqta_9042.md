# Quyết định 18/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 18/2003/QD-TTg of January 28, 2003 changing the organizational form and name of Ton Duc Thang People-Founded Technological University
To transform Ton Duc Thang People-Founded Technological University into a semi-public university and rename it Ton Duc Thang Semi-Public University, which is under the People’s Committee of Ho Chi Minh City.- (Summary)
