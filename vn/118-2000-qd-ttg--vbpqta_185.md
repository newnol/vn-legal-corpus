# Decision 118/2000/QD-TTg

THE PRIME MINISTER
DECISION No. 118/2000/QD-TTg of October 10, 2000 on reorganizing Ho Chi Minh City National University
To reorganize Ho Chi Minh City National University as follows:
To separate the Agro-Forestrial, Economics, Laws, Architecture and Technical Pedagogics universities from Ho Chi Minh City National University and organize them into:
- Ho Chi Minh City Agro-Forestrial University,
- Ho Chi Minh City Economics University,
- Ho Chi Minh City Laws University,
- Ho Chi Minh City Architecture University,
- Ho Chi Minh City Technical Pedagogics University.
The above-said universities shall be attached to the Ministry of Education and Training and have their own legal person status, seals and accounts.- (Summary)
