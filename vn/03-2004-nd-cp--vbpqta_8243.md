# Nghị định 03/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 03/2004/ND-CP of January 2, 2004 on the adjustment of administrative boundaries of Krong No and Cu Jut rural districts, Dak Nong province
After the transfer of Nam Ka and Ea R'Bin communes of Krong No rural district to Lak rural district, Dak Lak province, Krong No rural district shall be left with 81,680 ha and 51,086 people with 10 dependent administrative units, including 9 communes and Dak Mam township.
After the transfer of Hoa Phu, Hoa Khanh and Hoa Xuan communes of Cu Jut rural district to Dak Lak province, Cu Jut rural district shall be left with 71,889 ha and 77,048 people with 8 dependent administrative units, including 7 communes and Ea Tling township.
Dak Nong province shall have 651,438 ha and 363,118 people with 6 dependent rural districts.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, annulling all previous regulations contrary hereto.- (Summary)
