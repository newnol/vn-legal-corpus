# Thông tư 02/2004/TT-BXD

CIRCULAR No. 02/2004/TT-BXD of April 22, 2004 guiding the additional calculation of project construction expenditures because of unexpected surge of construction steel prices
This Circular guides the principles and methods of additional calculation of project construction expenditures, applicable as from January 1, 2004. It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
