# Quyết định 154/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 154/2004/QD-BCN of December 3, 2004 transforming Hanoi Leather and Shoes Company into Hanoi Leather and Shoes Import-Export Investment Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Hanoi Leather and Shoes Import-Export Investment Joint-Stock Company (HANSHOES.JSC) is headquartered at No. 409 Tam Trinh road, Hoang Mai district, Hanoi.
Its charter capital is VND 50 billion, 51% of which to be held by the State, 37.41% sold to its laborers and 11.59% to outsiders.- (Summary)
