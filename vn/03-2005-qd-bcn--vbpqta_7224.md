# Quyết định 03/2005/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 03/2005/QD-BCN of January 12, 2005 amending Clause 1 and Clause 3, Article 1 of the Industry Minister’s Decision No. 127/2004/QD-BCN of November 10, 2004 on transforming Dien Quang Light Bulb Company into Dien Quang Light Bulb Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, the charter capital of the joint-stock company is VND 23,500,000,000, of which the State’s equities represent 51%, equities sold to laborers of the company, 38.96%, and equities sold to other subjects, 10.04%.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
