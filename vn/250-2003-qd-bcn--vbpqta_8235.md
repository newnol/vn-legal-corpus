# Quyết định 250/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 250/2003/QD-BCN of December 31, 2003 transforming the Forwarding and Transportation Enterprise under Power Company 1 into Power Service and Transport Joint-Stock Company
To transform the Forwarding and Transportation Enterprise under Power Company 1 (an independent-accounting member of Vietnam Electricity Corporation) into Power Service and Transport Joint-Stock Company (POTRACO).
Its head-office: 231 Ngo Quyen road, May Chai ward, Ngo Quyen district, Hai Phong city.
Its charter capital is VND 13.9 billion, with 51% of which being held by the State, 46.39% being sold to its laborers, and 2.61% being sold to outsiders.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
