# Quyết định 192/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.192/1998/QD-TTg of September 29, 1998 on the establishment of the Vinh Phuc Teachers' Training College
To establish the Vinh Phuc Teachers' Training College on the basis of the Vinh Phuc Teachers' Training Intermediate School.
The Vinh Phuc Teachers' Training College is attached to the People's Committee of Vinh Phuc province, subject to the State management by the Ministry of Education and Training and tasked to train and foster teachers of college and lower levels.-
