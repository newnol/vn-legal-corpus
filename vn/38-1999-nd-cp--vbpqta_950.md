# Nghị định 38/1999/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 38/1999/ND-CP of June 18, 1999 the readjustment of administrative boundaries and establishment of communes of Duc Trong, Lam Ha and Di Linh districts and Bao Loc provincial capital, Lam Dong province
To establish Hiep An commune of Duc Trong district on the basis of a land area of 5,400 hectares and a population of 6,100 of Hiep Thanh commune.
To establish Lien Ha commune of Lam Ha district on the basis of a land area of 4,853 hectares and a population of 5,086 of Tan Ha commune.
To establish Tan Nghia commune of Di Linh district on the basis of a land area of 3,470 hectares and a population of 5,923 of Dinh Lac commune.
To establish Dai Lao commune of Bao Loc provincial capital on the basis of a land area of 6,220 hectares and a population of 16,457 of Loc Chau commune.- (Summary)
