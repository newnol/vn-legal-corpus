# Decision 09/2005/QD-BNN

DECISION No. 09/2005/QD-BNN of February 2, 2005 on the exceptional registration of a number of plant protection drugs into the list of those permitted for use in Vietnam
Under this Decision, 9 plant protection drugs are exceptionally registered into the list of those permitted for use in Vietnam.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
