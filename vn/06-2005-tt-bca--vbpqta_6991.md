# Circular 06/2005/TT-BCA

CIRCULAR
Guiding the registration and management of road traffic motorized means in the Lao Bao special trade-economic zone (Quang Tri province)
Under this Circular, each Vietnamese or foreign organization or individual investing or doing business and based in the zone may register only one car or motorcycle.
