# Quyết định 144/2002/QĐ-BQP

DECISION No. 144/2002/QD-BQP of October 10, 2002 transforming the plastic and bags factory under Military Zone 4�s Economic Cooperation Company into Vinh Plastic & Bags Joint-Stock Company
To transform the plastic and bags factory under Military Zone 4�s Economic Cooperation Company into Vinh Plastic & Bags Joint-Stock Company.
Its head office: Block 8, Ben Thuy ward, Vinh city of Nghe An province.
Its charter capital is VND 14 billion, of which the State equities represent 49%, equities sold to the laborers inside the factory, 12.86%, and equities sold the outsiders, 38.14%.
Vinh Plastic & Bags Joint-Stock Company is engaged in the following production and business lines:
- Producing cement bags and plastic products;
- Printed bags;
- Conducting import and export;
- Other production and business lines under law provisions.- (Summary)
