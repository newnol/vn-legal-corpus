# Decision 134/203/QD-CTN

DECISION No. 134/203/QD-CTN of March 19, 2003 permitting the relinquishment of Vietnamese nationality
To permit the relinquishment of Vietnamese nationality for:
509 citizens residing in the Federal Republic of Germany
116 citizens residing in Denmark
06 citizens residing in Finland
02 citizens residing in Sweden
17 citizens residing in Slovakia
07 citizens residing in the Czech Republic
09 citizens residing in the Republic of Austria
02 citizens residing in Poland
06 citizens residing in the United States of America
12 citizens residing in Japan
01 citizen residing in the Republic of Korea
25 citizens residing in the People's Democratic Republic of Laos
118 citizens residing in China (Taiwan).- (Summary)
