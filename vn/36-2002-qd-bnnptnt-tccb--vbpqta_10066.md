# Quyết định 36/2002/QĐ-BNNPTNT-TCCB

DECISION No. 36/2002/QD-BNNPTNT-TCCB of May 14, 2002 transferring in status quo the Tan Thanh Agricultural Irrigation Experiment - Environment station under the Southern Irrigation Science Institute to the Mekong Delta Rice Institute
To assign the director of the Southern Irrigation Science Institute and the director of the Mekong Delta Rice Institute to complete the hand-over and reception of all relevant material and technical bases, land as well as dossiers and documents, labor and payroll of the Tan Thanh Agricultural Irrigation Experiment - Environment station before May 31, 2002.- (Summary)
