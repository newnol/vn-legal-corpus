# Thông tư liên tịch 11/2002/TTLT/BGDĐT-BTCCBCP-BTC-BLĐTBXH

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 11/2002/TTLT-BGDDT-BTCCBCP-BTC-BLDTBXH of March 27, 2002 guiding the implementation of the Government's Decree No. 35/2001/ND-CP of July 9, 2001 on the policies towards teachers and educational administrators working in specific schools and areas meeting with exceptionally difficult socio-economic conditions
This Circular guides the regime of subsidies and allowances for teachers and educational administrators working in specific schools and areas meeting with exceptionally difficult socio-economic conditions.- (Summary)
