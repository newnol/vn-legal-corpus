# Circular 6/1998/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No.6/1998/TT-BTC of January 10, 1998 guiding the implementation of the financial management regime applicable to the lottery companies.
This Circular provides detailed guidance on the turnovers of the lottery companies, the expenses of the lottery companies, the turnover tax applicable to lottery companies, the distribution of net profits and setting up of funds and the financial reporting regime.- (Summary)
