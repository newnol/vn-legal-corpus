# Decree 04/1998/NĐ-CP

DECREE No.04/1998/ND-CP of January 9, 1998 on the establishment of a number of district townships and communes of Cu M'gar, Ea H'leo and Krong Bong districts, Dac Lak province.
To establish Quang Phu district township and Quang Tien commune of Cu M'gar district on the basis of the whole area and population of Quang Phu commune. Quang Phu township has a land area of 1,007 hectares and a population of 8,568. Quang Tien commune has a land area of 2,666 hectares and a population of 5,281.
To establish Ea Drang township, the district township of Ea H'leo district on the basis of a land area of 1,688 hectares and a population of 12,275 of Ea Khal.
To establish Krong Kmar township, the district township of Krong Bong district on the basis of a natural land area of 530 hectares and a population of 6,000 of Khue Ngoc Dien commune.- (Summary)
