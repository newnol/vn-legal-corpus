# Quyết định 241/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 241/2003/QD-BCN of December 30, 2003 transforming Construction and Steel Structure Company into Construction and Steel Structure Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, the Construction and Steel Structure Company, an independent cost-accounting enterprise of Vietnam Industrial Construction Corporation, shall be transformed into a joint-stock company with its English transaction name as Construction and Steel Structure Joint-Stock Company, abbreviated to COMESS.
The joint-stock company shall have a charter capital of VND 20,000,000,000, with the State’s equities representing 52%; equities sold to laborers of the company, 45.6%; and equities sold to other subjects, 2.4%.
It is headquartered at 275 Nguyen Trai, Thanh Xuan ward, Thanh Xuan district, Hanoi.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
