# Circular 141/1999/TT-BTC-HCSN

CIRCULAR No. 141/1999/TT-BTC-HCSN of December 7, 1999 guiding the payment of convalescence allowance levels to people with meritorious services to the revolution
This Circular specifies the subjects eligible for convalescence, convalescence duration and allowance levels, convalescence funding sources and organization of implementation.- (Summary)
