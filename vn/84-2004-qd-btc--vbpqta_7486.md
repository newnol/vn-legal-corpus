# Quyết định 84/2004/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 84/2004/QD-BTC of November 15, 2004 on the temporary non-collection of road tolls for National Highway 20 at Dinh Quan toll-booth - km 51+940 (Dong Nai province) and Duc Trong toll-booth - km 203+500 (Lam Dong province)
This Decision applies to two-wheeled motorcycles, three-wheeled motorcycles, motorized two wheelers, motorized three wheelers and the like.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
