# Decree 19/2007/NĐ-CP

DECREE
Establishing Ninh Binh city of Ninh Binh province
Under this Decree, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” Ninh Binh city is established on the basis of the whole land area, population and dependent administrative units of Ninh Binh provincial capital.- (Summary)
