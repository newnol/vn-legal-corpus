# Nghị định 37/1999/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 37/1999/ND-CP of June 15, 1999 on the establishment of district townships of Ham Thuan Bac, Ham Thuan Nam, Duc Linh and Tanh Linh districts, Binh Thuan province
To establish Ma Lam district township of Ham Thuan Bac district on the basis of the total land area of 1,550 ha and a population of 11,890 of Ma Lam commune.
To establish Thuan Nam district township of Ham Thuan Nam district on the basis of a land area of 2,870.3 ha and a population of 10,936 of Tan Lap commune.
To establish Vo Xu district township of Duc Linh district on the basis of the total land area of 2,765 ha and a population of 15,833 of Vo Xu commune.
To establish Lac Tanh district township of Tanh Linh district on the basis of the total land area of 3,816 ha and a population of 13,272 of Lac Tanh commune.- (Summary)
