# Quyết định 18/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 18/1999/QD-TTg of February 11, 1999 on the establishment of a Teacher Training College in Tuyen Quang province
To establish Tuyen Quang Teacher Training College on the basis of the Tuyen Quang Teacher Training High School.
The Tuyen Quang Teacher Training College shall be attached to the People�s Committee of Tuyen Quang province and subject to the State management by the Ministry of Education and Training. The college is tasked to train and foster teachers of college or lower level.- (Summary)
