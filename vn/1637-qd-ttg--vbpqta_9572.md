# Decision 1637/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 1637/QD-TTg of December 31, 2001 on the provision of a number of newspapers and gazettes to areas inhabited by ethnic minority people and mountainous areas
The State shall provide a number of newspapers and gazettes in order to enhance the information work in service of the cause of political and socio-economic development in areas inhabited by ethnic minority people and mountainous areas.
The Decision clearly states the types of newspapers and gazettes, scope and subjects of distribution; the financial mechanism and assignment of responsibilities of relevant ministries and branches in providing newspapers and gazettes for the above-said areas.- (Summary)
