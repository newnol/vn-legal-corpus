# Nghị định 23/2005/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 23/2005/ND-CP of March 2, 2003 adjusting the administrative boundaries for expansion of Chuc Son township, Chuong My district, Ha Tay province
This Decree prescribes the adjustment of administrative boundaries of Ngoc Son, Phung Chau and Tien Phuong communes to expand Chuc Son township, Chuong My district, Ha Tay province.
