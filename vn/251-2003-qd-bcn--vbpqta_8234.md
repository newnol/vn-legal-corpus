# Quyết định 251/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 251/2003/QD-BCN of December 31, 2003 transforming Thai Binh Domestic-Use and Fine-Art Porcelain and Glass Workshop of the Insulation Porcelain and Glass Enterprise under Power Company 1 into Porcelain and Glass Joint-Stock Company
To transform Thai Binh Domestic-Use and Fine-Art Porcelain and Glass Workshop of the Insulation Porcelain and Glass Enterprise under Power Company 1 (an independent-accounting member of Vietnam Electricity Corporation) into Porcelain and Glass Joint-Stock Company (PGC).
Its head-office: Road 39B, Tien Hai district township, Thai Binh province.
Its charter capital is VND 2 billion, with 100% of which being sold to its laborers.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
