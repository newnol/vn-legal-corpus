# Quyết định 266/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 266/2003/QD-TTg of December 17, 2003 approving the scheme on development of the goods export market in the 2004-2005 period
This Decision approves the scheme on development of the goods export market in the 2004-2005 period with specific targets, development orientations and market development solutions; and assigns specific tasks of carrying out such solutions to the concerned ministries, including the Trade Ministry, the Finance Ministry, the Justice Ministry and the Planning and Investment Ministry.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
