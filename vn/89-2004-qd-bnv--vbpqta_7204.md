# Quyết định 89/2004/QĐ-BNV

DECISION No. 89/2004/QD-BNV of December 27, 2004 approving the Charter of Vietnam Private Universities Association
This Decision approves the 6-chapter and 23-article Charter of Vietnam Private Universities Association (VIPUA), which was adopted at its first congress on September 9, 2004.
VIPUA is a voluntary socio-professional organization of Vietnam’s private universities and colleges, subject to State management over associations by the Ministry of Home Affairs and over its operation by the Ministry of Education and Training.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
