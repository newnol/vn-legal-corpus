# Directive 22/2006/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On ensuring safety for fishing activities, especially offshore fishing, in sea areas
With this Directive, the Prime Minister instructs People’s Committees of coastal provinces and cities and concerned ministries and branches to perform several tasks in order to ensure safety for fishing people, ships and boats, especially for offshore fishing activities.- (Summary)
