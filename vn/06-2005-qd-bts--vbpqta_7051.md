# Quyết định 06/2005/QĐ-BTS

DECISION No. 06/2005/QD-BTS of February 17, 2005 promulgating branch standards
Under this Decision, the following two branch standards are promulgated:
- 28 TCN 92: 2005: Marine shrimp hatchery - Technical and sanitary requirements;
- 28 TCN 220: 2005: Giant freshwater prawn hatchery - Technical and sanitary requirements.
The above-said standards take effect 15 days after their publication in the Official Gazette, for shrimp breed-producing establishments which are newly built or have been built but have not yet operated; or as from July 1, 2005, for operating shrimp breed-producing establishments.- (Summary)
