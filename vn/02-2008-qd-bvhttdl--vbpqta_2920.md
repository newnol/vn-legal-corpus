# Decision 02/2008/QD-BVHTTDL 

DECISION
On ranking national relics
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” the historical, artistic and architectural relics of Ha, Trung and Thuong (lower, middle and upper) temples in Minh Quang and Ba Vi communes, Ba Vi district, Ha Tay province, are ranked as national relics.- (Summary)
