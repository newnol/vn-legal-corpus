# Decision 132/2006/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
On the regime of job-based preferential allowance for cadres and civil servants in forest and plant protection, animal health and dike control sectors
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces Decision No. 66/2002/QD-TTg of May 27, 2002.
For rangers who have enjoyed the allowance regime provided for in Decision No. 66/2002/QD-TTg, the preferential job-based allowance regime specified in this Decision applies as from October 1, 2004.- (Summary)
