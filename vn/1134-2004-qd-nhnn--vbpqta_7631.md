# Quyết định 1134/2004/QĐ-NHNN

DECISION No. 1134/2004/QD-NHNN of September 9, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on organization and operation of the Office
The Office is a unit under the State Bank, functioning to assist the Governor in synthesizing and coordinating activities according to work programs and plans; and performing the State management over press, clerical and emulation activities of the banking sector; and perform the administrative work for activities at the State Bank's headquarter.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
