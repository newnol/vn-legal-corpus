# Decision 68/1999/QĐ-BTC

Decree No. 90/1999/ND-CP of September 1 st , 1999 on the establishment of Dong Xoai provincial capital as well as its wards and communes, Binh Phuoc province
To establish Dong Xoai town, the provincial capital of Binh Phuoc province, with 7 administrative units, a land area of 16,957 ha and a population of 50,758.
To establish wards and communes of Dong Xoai provincial capital:
To establish Tan Dong ward with a land area of 745 ha and a population of 4,823.
To establish Tan Xuan ward with a land area of 1,469 ha and a population of 12,311.
To establish Tan Binh ward with a land area of 552 ha and a population of 7,884.
To establish Tan Phu ward with a land area of 843 ha and a population of 7,169.
To establish Tien Thanh commune with a land area of 2,420 ha and a population of 2,820.
To establish Tan Thanh commune with a land area of 5,728 ha and a population of 8,390.
To establish Tien Hung commune with a land area of 5,200 ha and a population of 7,361.- (Summary)
