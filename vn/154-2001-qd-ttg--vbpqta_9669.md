# Quyết định 154/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 154/2001/QD-TTg of October 10, 2001 approving the adjustment of the General Planning on Ho Chi Minh City National University
The Prime Minister approves the adjustment of the General Planning on Ho Chi Minh City National University with the contents on its scope and boundary, used land acreage, training scale up to 2020 and structure of land use planning.- (Summary)
