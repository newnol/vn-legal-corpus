# Decree 49/2001/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 49/2001/ND-CP of August 15, 2001 on the establishment of communes in Cu Jut district and Buon Don district, Dac Lac province
To establish Cu Knia commune of Cu Jut district on the basis of a land area of 2,987 ha and a population of 3,278 of Truc Son commune.
To establish Dac Wil commune of Cu Jut district on the basis of a land area of 42,140 ha and a population of 5,159 of Ea Po commune.
To adjust administrative boundaries of Ea Bar commune and Cuor Knia commune of Buon Don district: To merge a land area of 930 ha and a population of 4,006 of Ea Bar commune into Cuor Knia commune. After the adjustment of administrative boundaries, Ea Bar commune shall have a land area of 2,600 ha and a population of 14,407; Cuor Knia commune shall have a land area of 7,728 ha and a population of 16,215.
To establish Tan Hoa commune of Buon Don district on the basis of a land area of 5,698 ha and a population of 8,621 of Cuor Knia commune. After the adjustment of administrative boundaries, Cuor Knia commune shall have a land area of 2,030 ha and a population of 7,594.- (Summary)
