# Quyết định 118/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 118/2002/QD-TTg of September 17, 2002 amending and supplementing a number of articles of the Prime Minister�s Decisions No. 422/QD-TTg of May 19, 1998 and No. 89/1999/QD-TTg of April 3, 1999 on setting up anti-smuggling and trade frauds joint control stations
The amended and supplemented contents shall be as follows: The joint control stations shall consist of officials of police, customs, border-guard, market control and tax forces. The heads, organization and payroll of joint control stations shall be decided by the presidents of the People�s Committees of the provinces where such stations are set up. The Steering Committee for smuggling and trade frauds combat shall issue the regulation on organization and operation of anti-smuggling and trade frauds joint control stations.- (Summary)
