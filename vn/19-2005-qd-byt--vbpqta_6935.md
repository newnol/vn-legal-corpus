# Quyết định 19/2005/QĐ-BYT

DECISION
Adjusting the plan on application of "Good Manufacturing Practice" principles and standards, issued together with the Health Minister's Decision No. 3886/2004/QD-BYT of November 3, 2004, on application of "Good Manufacturing Practice" principles and standards recommended by the World Health Organization, and "Good Storage Practice" principles, issued together with the Health Minister's Decision No. 2701/2001/QD-BYT of June 29, 2001, on application of "Good Storage Practice" principles
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
