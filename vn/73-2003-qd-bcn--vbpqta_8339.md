# Quyết định 73/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 73/2003/QD-BCN of May 6, 2003 transforming Binh Dien II fertilizer enterprise into Binh Dien fertilizer company under Vietnam Chemicals Corporation
To transform Binh Dien II fertilizer enterprise under the Southern fertilizer company into Binh Dien fertilizer company under Vietnam Chemicals Corporation.
Binh Dien fertilizer company is a State enterprise, an independent economic-accounting member of Vietnam Chemicals Corporation.
Its head-office: C12/21, National Highway No. 1, Tan Kien, Binh Chanh, Ho Chi Minh City.
Charter capital at the time of its establishment: VND 35 billion.
It is engaged in the following principal production and business lines:
- Producing and trading in assorted inorganic, organic, microbiological and modicum fertilizers as well as plant and animal growth stimulants.
- Importing and exporting fertilizer products, fertilizer-manufacturing equipment and technologies, agricultural tool machines, plant varieties and assorted farm produce. Researching and producing fertilizer products.
- Dealing in other production, business and service lines under the provisions of law.- (Summary)
