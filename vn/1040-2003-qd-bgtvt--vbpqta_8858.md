# Decision 1040/2003/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION No. 1040/2003/QD-BGTVT of April 15, 2003 supplementing the Regulation on management of tests for granting land-road motorized vehicle driving licenses, issued together with the Communications and Transport Minister’s Decision No. 4352/2001/QD-BGTVT of December 18, 2002
This Decision supplements Clause 5 of Article 5 and Clause 2 of Article 13, of the Regulation on management of tests for granting land-road motorized vehicle driving licenses.
Under the amended contents, if driving licenses are marked with two violations, the drivers must participate in Land-Road Traffic Law tests for re-granting of driving licenses; if 3 violations are marked, only 12 months after the third hole is punched can the violators participate in such tests.- (Summary)
