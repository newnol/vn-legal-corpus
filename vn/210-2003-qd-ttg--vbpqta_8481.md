# Quyết định 210/2003/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 210/2003/QD-TTg of October 13, 2003 on the functions, tasks, organization and operation regulation of the Prime Minister's Research Commission
The Prime Minister's Research Commission, established under his Decision No. 473/QD-TTg of May 30, 1998, is a body researching into, and advising the Prime Minister on, renewal of economic, social and State administrative mechanisms and policies.
This Decision prescribes the specific tasks, rights, organizational structure and working regulation of the Research Commission, takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, and replaces Articles 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 and 7 of the Prime Minister's Decision No. 473/QD-TTg of May 30, 1998 and the operation regulation of the Research Commission promulgated together with Decision No. 473/QD-TTg.- (Summary)
