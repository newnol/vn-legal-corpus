# Quyết định 14/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 14/2001/QD-TTg of February 12, 2001 on the reorganization of Ha Noi National University
To reorganize Ha Noi National University on the basis of re-arranging its existing universities, scientific and technological research institutes, faculties, centers and attached units.
The organizational structure of Ha Noi National University includes:
- The member universities:
+ The University of Natural Sciences;
+ The University of Social Sciences and Humanities;
+ The Foreign Language College.
- The Information Technology Institute;
- The faculties and affiliated scientific and technological research centers.
- The Office and a number of functional sections assisting the director of Ha Noi National University.
- The attached units to serve the training, scientific research and application activities.
Basing himself on the training and scientific and technological research requirements in each period, the Prime Minister shall decide the establishment, addition or readjustment of the number of member universities and scientific and technological research institutes of Ha Noi National University.
Ha Noi National University has the legal person status, is organized and operates under the Government�s Decree on the National Universities and the Regulation on organization and operation of the National Universities promulgated by the Prime Minister.- (Summary)
