# Quyết định 25/2000/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 25/2000/QD-BCN of April 6, 2000 on the transfer of Electrical Engineering School No. 2, Ho Chi Minh city, under Power Company No. 2, to the Vietnam Electricity Corporation
Electrical Engineering School No. 2 (abbreviated as EES2) is an establishment which trains and fosters cadres of electrical economic-technical intermediate degree or lower under the educational system of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam. The school is subject ot the State management by the Ministry of Industry, the Ministry of Education and Training and the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs.
Head office: Huong Lo 12, Thanh Loc ward, District 12, Ho Chi Minh city.
The school’s training and fostering scale: from 1,800 to 4,000 students.- (Summary)
