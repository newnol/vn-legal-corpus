# Chỉ thị 75/2005/CT-BNN

DIRECTIVE
On enhancing the work of forest fire prevention and fight
With this Directive, the Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development requests provincial/municipal People’s Committees to direct various levels to strictly implement the Prime Minister’s Directive No. 21/2002/CT-TTg of December 12, 2002, on enhancing forest fire prevention and fight; provincial/municipal Ranger Sub-Departments and Agriculture and Rural Development Services; the Ranger Department and relevant units to well perform the work of forest fire prevention and fight.- (Summary)
