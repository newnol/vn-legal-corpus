# Quyết định 151/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 151/2002/QD-TTg of November 7, 2002 approving the master plan for reorganization and renewal of State enterprises of Vinh Long province in the 2002-2005 period
The master plan for reorganization and renewal of State enterprises of Vinh Long province in the 2002-2005 period was approved by the Prime Minister in the above-said Decision. Issued together with this Decision were the list of enterprises, where the State continues to hold 100% of charter capital and maintains its legal person status, and the roadmap for reorganization of enterprises in each year.- (Summary)
