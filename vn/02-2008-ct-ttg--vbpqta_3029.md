# Chỉ thị 02/2008/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
Promoting the movement of study promotion, talent promotion and building of a learning society
The Prime Minister instructs levels, branches, localities and units to perform several tasks in order to promote the movement of study promotion, talent promotion and building of a learning society, step by step building and developing an open education model - learning society model, in the spirit of the X th Party Congress’ Resolution.- (Summary)
