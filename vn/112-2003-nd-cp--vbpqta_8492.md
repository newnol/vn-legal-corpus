# Nghị định 112/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 112/2003/ND-CP of October 3, 2003 the establishment of new townships of Cam Xuyen and Vu Quang rural districts, Ha Tinh province, and renaming Vu Quang commune of Vu Quang rural district as Huong Quang commune
To establish Thien Cam township of Cam Xuyen rural district, Ha Tinh province, on the basis of the whole land area of 1,401.17 ha and the population of 7,510 of Cam Long commune.
To establish Vu Quang township, the capital of Vu Quang rural district, on the basis of the whole land area and population of Huong Dai commune as well as the land areas of 103 and 159 ha and the population of 373 and 282 of Huong Minh and Duc Bong communes, respectively. Vu Quang district capital shall have a land area of 3,786 ha and a population of 2,449.
To rename Vu Quang commune of Vu Quang rural district as Huong Quang commune.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
