# Thông tư 51/2004/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 51/2004/TT-BTC of June 9, 2004 guiding the implementation of the Government's Decree No. 123/2004/ND-CP of May 18, 2004 on a number of specific budget financial mechanisms applicable to Hanoi capital
This Circular guides the regulations on specific budget financial mechanisms applicable to Hanoi capital, including budget management mechanism and mechanism for mobilization of financial sources for Hanoi capital development investment.
Apart from specific budget financial mechanisms prescribed in above-said Decree No. 123/2004/ND-CP and this Circular's guidance, Hanoi city must also abide by the general regulations on budget financial management prescribed in the State Budget Law, the Government's Decree No. 60/2003/ND-CP of June 6, 2003, the Finance Ministry's Circular No. 59/2003/TT-BTC of June 23, 2003 and other legal documents on budget finance.
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
