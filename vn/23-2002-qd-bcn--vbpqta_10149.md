# Quyết định 23/2002/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 23/2002/QD-BCN of June 7, 2002 transforming Phu Tho Hoa Electronics Company into Viettronics and Mechanics Phu Tho Hoa Joint-Stock Company
To transform Phu Tho Hoa Electronics Company into Viettronics and Mechanics Phu Tho Hoa Company.
Its head office: 1026B Luy Ban Bich street, ward 19, Tan Binh district, Ho Chi Minh city.
Its charter capital: VND 3.12 billion (three billion, one hundred and twenty million Vietnam dong), of which the State equities represent 30%, equities sold to the laborers inside the company, 40%, and equities sold to the outsiders, 30%.
Viettronics and Mechanics Phu Tho Hoa Company is engaged in the following production and business lines (at the time of transformation into a joint-stock company):
- Production, assembly and trading of electric and electronic products;
- Production and trading of plastic and civil mechanical engineering products;
- Direct import and export;
- Dealing in other production and business lines as prescribed by law.- (Summary)
