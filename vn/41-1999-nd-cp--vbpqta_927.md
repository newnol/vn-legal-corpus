# Nghị định 41/1999/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 41/1999/ND-CP of June 25, 1999 on the establishment of Phu Khuong ward, Ben Tre provincial capital, Ben Tre province
To establish Phu Khuong ward, Ben Tre provincial town of Ben Tre province on the basis of the total land area of 597 ha and a population of 15,414 of Phu Khuong commune.- (Summary)
