# Decree 27/2000/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 27/2000/ND-CP of August 4, 2000 on the establishment of Vu Quang district in Ha Tinh province
To readjust administrative boundaries of Duc Tho, Huong Khe and Huong Son districts in order to establish Vu Quang district, Ha Tinh province, on the basis of Duc Lien, Duc Huong, Duc Bong, Duc Linh, Duc Giang and An Phu (Duc Tho district) communes; Huong Tho, Huong Minh, Huong Dai, Huong Dien and Vu Quang (Huong Khe district) communes and Son Tho commune (Huong Son district).
Vu Quang district shall have a land area of 62,284 ha and a population of 35,877, comprising 12 administrative units of the above-mentioned communes.- (Summary)
