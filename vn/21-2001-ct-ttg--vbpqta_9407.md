# Chỉ thị 21/2001/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE No. 21/2001/CT-TTg of September 11, 2001 of the Prime Minister on using the State budget to procure assets, supplies, equipment and facilities for the construction of working offices and administrative management activities
In order to actively contribute to the practice of thrift and the fight against wastefulness, creating conditions to boost the development of domestic production, the Prime Minister requests: As from October 1, 2001, all agencies and units, when using State budget allocations to procure assets, supplies, equipment and facilities for the construction of their working offices and administrative management activities, shall have to buy home-made products, except for assets, supplies, equipment and facilities of types which cannot be produced at home yet.- (Summary)
