# Quyết định 250/2005/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
On organization and operation of the agency in charge of the website of the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam
Under this Decision, the agency in charge of the website of the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam is a non-business unit located in the Government Office; has the function to directly manage and administer the operation of the Government's website; and is organized and operates after the model of an online newspaper; has its own seal and account.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
