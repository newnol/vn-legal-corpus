# Directive 03/2006/CT-BTS

DIRECTIVE
On enhancing the management of exploitation of aquatic resources in Vietnam’s sea areas
With this Directive, the Minster of Fisheries requests concerned departments and units to well perform several tasks in order to enhance the management of exploitation of aquatic resources in Vietnam’s sea areas.- (Summary)
