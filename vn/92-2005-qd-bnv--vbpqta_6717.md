# Quyết định 92/2005/QĐ-BNV

DECISION
Approving the Charter of Vietnam Marine Geology Association
Vietnam Marine Geology Association (VIMGA), a member of the Vietnam General Association of Geology, is a voluntary socio-professional organization of those persons engaged in marine geology, aiming to help one another to bring into play their talent and intellect, raise their professional qualifications and ethics and dedicate themselves to the development of the mineral raw-material industry, in service of national industrialization and modernization.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
