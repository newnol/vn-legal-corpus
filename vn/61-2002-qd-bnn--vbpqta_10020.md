# Quyết định 61/2002/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 61/2002/QD-BNN of July 8, 2002 promulgating the list of goods being domestic animal breeds subject to quality standard announcement
To issue together with this Decision the list of goods being the breeds of domestic animals subject to quality standard announcement, including pig, poultry, bovine, rabbit, goat, horse, buffalo, bee, and silkworm.- (Summary)
