# Decision 236/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 236/2003/QD-TTg of November 13, 2003 on the list of State secrets classified as strictly confidential and top secret in the diplomatic service
This Decision promulgates the list of State secrets classified as strictly confidential and top secret in the diplomatic service.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces the Prime Minister's Decisions No. 730/TTg of December 1, 1994 promulgating the list of State secrets in the foreign affairs sector and No. 324/TTg of May 27, 1995 promulgating the list of State secrets of the Government's Committee for Frontier Issues.- (Summary)
