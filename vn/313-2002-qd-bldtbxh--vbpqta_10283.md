# Quyết định 313/2002/QĐ-BLĐTBXH

DECISION No. 313/2002/QD-BLDTBXH of March 13, 2002 setting up the Steering Committee for the 2002 labor-employment survey
The Central Steering Committee for the labor-employment survey shall be headed by Mr. Le Duy Dong, Vice Minister of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs. The Central Steering Committee shall have to organize and direct the deployment of the whole 2002 labor-employment survey according to the plan already approved.
The provincial-level Steering Committees for the labor-employment survey shall be set up by the presidents of the provincial/municipal People�s Committees. The provincial/municipal Steering Committees shall have to direct and organize the deployment of the survey in their respective localities according to the plans prescribed by the Central Steering Committee.- (Summary)
