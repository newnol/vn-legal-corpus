# Circular 32/2006/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR
Guiding accounting operations regarding taxes and other revenues from imports and exports
This Circular takes effect as from October 1, 2006, and replaces the regime on accounting of import tax and export tax, issued together with Decision No. 559/QD-KTTT of November 15, 2000, of the General Director of Customs.- (Summary)
