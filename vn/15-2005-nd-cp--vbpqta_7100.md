# Nghị định 15/2005/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 15/2005/ND-CP of February 7, 2005 adjusting administrative boundaries of, and establishing new communes in, Tan Hiep, Kien Luong, Hon Dat and Chau Thanh districts, Kien Giang province
This Decree provides for the adjustment of administrative boundaries of Thanh Dong and Thanh Dong B communes of Tan Hiep district and the establishment of Phu Loi and Vinh Phu communes of Kien Luong district, Linh Huynh and My Thai communes of Hon Dat district, Vinh Hoa Phu commune of Chau Thanh district.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
