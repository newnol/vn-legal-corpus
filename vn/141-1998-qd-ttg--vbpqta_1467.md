# Quyết định 141/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 141/1998/QD-TTg of August 1st, 1998 reorganizing and adding new functions and tasks to the Labor Protection Science and Technique Research Institute
To add the Labor Protection Science and Technique Research Institute to the List of Institutes stipulated in Section 1, Article 1 of Decision No.782-TTg of October 24, 1996 of the Prime Minister on the reorganization of the scientific and technological research and development agencies.
The Labor Protection Science and Technique Research Institute shall be subject to the management by the Vietnam Labor Confederation and the direction by the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment over its scientific and technological activities.- (Summary)
