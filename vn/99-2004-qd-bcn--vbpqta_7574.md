# Quyết định 99/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 99/2004/QD-BCN of September 24, 2004 transforming Van Don Plastic Factory of Vietnam Plastic Company into Van Don Plastic Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Van Don Plastic Joint-Stock Company (SIMIPLAST) is headquartered at No. 320 Van Don Wharf, Ward 2, District 4, Ho Chi Minh city.
Its charter capital is VND 4,370 million, 50.98% of which to be held by the State, 43.99% sold to its laborers, and 5.03% to outsiders.- (Summary)
