# Quyết định 468/2003/QĐ-BTM

DECISION No. 468/2003/QD-BTM of April 23, 2003 adding Appendix No. 3 to the Regulation on granting of Vietnam's certificates of ASEAN goods origins - Form D for enjoyment of preferences under the Agreement on Common Effective Preferential Tariff
To add Appendix No. 3 to the Regulation on granting of Vietnam's certificates of ASEAN goods origins - Form D for enjoyment of preferences under the Agreement on Common Effective Preferential Tariff (CEPT), issued together with the Trade Ministry's Decision No. 416/TM-DB of May 13, 1996 and Decision No. 78/1998/QD-BTM of July 30, 1998 on the supplementation thereof as follows:
To add the code of Quang Ninh Industrial Parks' Managing Board as follows:
Code No. 29 - Quang Ninh Industrial Parks' Managing Board.- (Summary)
