# Quyết định 24/2000/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 24/2000/QD-BCN of April 6, 2000 on the consolidation of the Electrical Engineering Intermediate School No. 1 and the In-service Fostering School under Power Company No. 1, into the Electrical Engineering School No. 1 under the Vietnam Electricity Corporation
Electrical Engineering School No.1 (abbreviated as EES1) is an establishment which trains and fosters cadres of electrical economic-technical intermediate degree under the educational system of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam. The school is subject to the State management by the Ministry of Industry and the Ministry of Education and Training.
Head office: Hoang Quoc Viet street, Tu Liem district, Hanoi.
The school’s training and fostering scale: from 1,800 to 3,500 students.- (Summary)
