# Thông tư liên tịch 07/2003/TTLT/BTC-BNV-BYT

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 07/2003/TTLT-BTC-BNV-BYT of January 15, 2003 guiding the amendments to Point 2, Section I of Circular No. 150/LB-TT of April 16, 1996 of the Government’s Commission for Organization and Personnel, the Ministry of Finance and the Ministry of Health, which guides the implementation of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 794/TTg of December 5, 1995 prescribing a number of specific allowance regimes for health officials and employees
This Circular guides the 24/24 on-duty allowance regime and the overtime work regime for health officials and employees.- (Summary)
