# Joint circular 72/TTLT

JOINT Circular No.72-TTLT of October 14, 1997 guiding the contents and levels of expenditure of the national medical program.
The national medical program aims to achieve a number of objectives set by the State for each period of operation. The fund for the program shall come from the State budget, foreign aid and other sources prescribed by the State. Such fundings shall be managed in accordance with the Law on the State Budget. Besides, the People’s Committees of the provinces and cities directly under the Central Government may take initiative in mobilizing other funds from contributions of individuals and social organizations supporting the operation of the program.
This Circular also provides detailed guidance on the content of expenditures and expenditure level for each objective of the program.- (Summary)
