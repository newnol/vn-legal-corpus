# Circular 260/1999/TT-BKHCNMT

Circular No.260/1999/TT-BKHCNMT of March 1st, 1999 guiding the implementation of Directive No.43/1998/CT-TTg of December 31, 1998 of the Prime Minister on the prevention, handling and overcoming of the impacts of "Y2K" in the fields where information technology is used
This Circular guides the prevention, handling and overcoming of the impacts of "Y2K" in the fields where information technology is used in the ministries, the agencies attached to the Government, the provinces and centrally-run cities, and the corporations and major State enterprises.- (Summary)
