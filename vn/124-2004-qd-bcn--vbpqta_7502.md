# Quyết định 124/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 124/2004/QD-BCN of November 4, 2004 transforming Da Nang Handbag Factory under Saigon Leather and Footwear Import-Export Company into Da Nang Leather Goods Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Da Nang Leather Goods Joint-Stock Company have a charter capital of VND 1,054,000,000, of which equities sold to laborers of the factory represent 93.86% and equities sold to other subjects, 6.14%.
The joint-stock company's trademark is VINABAGS.
Its head-office is located at 291/9 Tran Cao Van street, Da Nang city.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
