# Decision 53/2006/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
Amending and supplementing the State Budget Index system
This Decision amends and supplements Items and Sub-items of the State Budget Index system promulgated together with Decisions No. 280 TC/QD/NSNN of April 15, 1997, No. 148/2002/QD-BTC of December 6, 2002, No. 156/2002/QD-BTC of December 20, 2002, No. 26/2004/QD-BTC of March 18, 2004, No. 23/2005/QD-BTC of April 15, 2005, No. 70/2005/QD-BTC of October 19, 2005, and No. 01/2006/QD-BTC of January 5, 2006; and Circulars No. 156/1998/TT-BTC of December 12, 1998, No, 60/1999/TT-BTC of June 1, 1999, No. 67/2000/TT-BTC of July 13, 2000, and No. 67/2001/TT-BTC of August 22, 2001.
It takes effect on January 1, 2007.- (Summary)
