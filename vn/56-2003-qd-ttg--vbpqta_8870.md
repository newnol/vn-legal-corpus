# Quyết định 56/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 56/2003/QD-TTg of April 15, 2003 approving the master plan for reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Lai Chau province till 2005
Enterprises with 100% of their charter capital being held by the State: 10 public-utility enterprises and 28 business enterprises to be kept intact; 4 State-run forestry farms to be temporarily kept intact pending reorganization under the Government’s stipulations.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding at least 51% of their charter capital when shares are sold for the first time: 3 in 2003.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding under 50% of charter capital or holding no shares: 1 in 2003 and 3 in 2004.
Enterprise to be assigned: 1.
Enterprises to be dissolved: 2.- (Summary)
