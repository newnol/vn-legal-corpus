# Decree 22/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 22/2002/ND-CP of March 15, 2002 adjusting the administrative boundary of Vinh Hai ward for the establishment of Vinh Hoa ward of Nha Trang city, Khanh Hoa province
To establish Vinh Hoa ward on the basis of a natural land area of 1,156.44 hectares and a population of 9,369 of Vinh Hai ward.- (Summary)
