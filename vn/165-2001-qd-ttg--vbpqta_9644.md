# Quyết định 165/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 165/2001/QD-TTg of October 24, 2001 setting up the Customs Department of Binh Phuoc province
To set up the Customs Department of Binh Phuoc province under the General Department of Customs.
The Customs Department of Binh Phuoc province shall have its own seal and account, and its office headquartered in Binh Phuoc province.- (Summary)
