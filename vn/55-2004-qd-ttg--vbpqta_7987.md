# Quyết định 55/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 55/2004/QD-TTg of April 6, 2004 approving the special-class State enterprise
This Decision approves the classification of the Company for Purchase and Sale of Enterprises' Outstanding Debts and Assets under the Finance Ministry as a special-class State enterprise. It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
The Minister of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs and the Finance Minister shall have to guide the transfer of salaries and position allowances of managerial officials of special-class State enterprises for the Company for Purchase and Sale of Enterprises' Outstanding Debts and Assets.- (Summary)
