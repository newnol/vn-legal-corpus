# Decision 184/1999/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 184/1999/QD-TTg of September 10, 1999 on the establishment of the Military Technical, Scientific and Technological Center under the Ministry of Defense
To establish the Military Technical, Scientific and Technological Center under the Ministry of Defense on the basis of re-arranging and re-organizing the Military Technical Institute and other technical institutes of the Ministry of Defense.
The Military Technical, Scientific and Technological Center is tasked to conduct scientific, technical and technological research and the application of advanced technique and technology in service of army building and defense consolidation, contributing to the cause of national industrialization and modernization.- (Summary)
