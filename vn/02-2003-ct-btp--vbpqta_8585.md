# Chỉ thị 02/2003/CT-BTP

DIRECTIVE No. 02/2003/CT-BTP of July 14, 2003 continuing the marriage registration for cases where men and women had lived together as husbands and wives between January 3, 1987 and January 1, 2001
This Directive assigns the provincial/municipal People's Committees, local judicial agencies and relevant units under the Ministry of Justice to complete before August 1, 2004 the marriage registration for cases where men and women had lived together as husbands and wives between January 3, 1987 and January 1, 2001.- (Summary)
