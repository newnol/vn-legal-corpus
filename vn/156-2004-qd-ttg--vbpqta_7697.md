# Quyết định 156/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 156/2004/QD-TTg of August 25, 2004 on the lists of strictly confidential- and top secret-degree State secrets of the National Politics-Truth Publishing House
This Decision prescribes information and documents classified as strictly confidential- and top secret-degree State secrets of the National Politics-Truth Publishing House.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication of the Official Gazette .- (Summary)
