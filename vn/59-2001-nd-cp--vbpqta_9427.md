# Nghị định 59/2001/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 59/2001/ND-CP of August 29, 2001 dividing Quang Ha district, Quang Ninh province into Hai Ha and Dam Ha districts
To divide Quang Ha district of Quang Ninh province into Hai Ha and Dam Ha districts:
Hai Ha district shall have a land area of 69,013.1 ha and a population of 46,995.
Dam Ha district shall have a land area of 41,436.4 ha and a population of 29,938.- (Summary)
