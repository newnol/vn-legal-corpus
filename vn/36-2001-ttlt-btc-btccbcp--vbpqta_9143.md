# Thông tư liên tịch 36/2001/TTLT/BTC-BTCCBCP

CIRCULAR No. 36/2001/TTLT-BTC-BTCCBCP of May 25, 2001 guiding the implementation of the serving allowance regime
This Circular guides in detail application subjects and levels of serving allowance; the implementation organization and funding for payment of serving allowances according to the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 144/2000/QD-TTg of December 14, 2000 on serving allowance regime.- (Summary)
