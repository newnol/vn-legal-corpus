# Quyết định 1049/2004/QĐ-NHNN

DECISION No. 1049/2004/QD-NHNN of August 23, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on organization and operation of the Banking Times
The Banking Times is a non-business unit of the State Bank, acting as a mouthpiece, a social forum and a tool for propagating and disseminating the Party's guidelines and undertakings, the State's policies and laws and the banking sector's activities according to the Press Law and the State Bank Governor's regulations.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces Decision No. 122/1999/QD-NHNN9 of April 8, 1999.- (Summary)
