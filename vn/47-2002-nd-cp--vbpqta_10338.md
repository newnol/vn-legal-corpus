# Decree 47/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 47/2002/ND-CP of April 19, 2002 on the establishment of communes of O Mon, Phung Hiep and Thot Not rural districts, Can Tho province
To establish Truong Xuan A commune of O Mon rural district with a natural land area of 3,679.07 ha and a population of 13,094.
To establish Binh Thanh commune of Phung Hiep rural district with a natural land area of 2,392.97 ha and a population of 9,497.
To establish Trung Thanh commune of Thot Not rural district with a natural land area of 2,194.8 ha and a population of 15,864.
To establish Thanh My commune of Thot Not rural district with a natural land area of 2,481.65 ha and a population of 11,448.- (Summary)
