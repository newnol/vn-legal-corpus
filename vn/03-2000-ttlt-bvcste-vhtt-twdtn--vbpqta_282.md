# Joint circular 03/2000/TTLT/BVCSTE-VHTT-TWDTN

JOINT-CIRCULAR No. 03/2000/TTLT-BVCSTE-VHTT-TWDTN of July 5, 2000 guiding the implementation of the Prime Minister’s Directive No. 03/2000/CT-TTg of January 24, 2000 on stepping up cultural and entertainment activities for children
The Vietnam Committee for Child Protection and Care, the Ministry of Culture and Information and the Central Union of Ho Chi Minh Communist Youth agree the plan for the implementation of the above-said Directive with the following activities:
- Propagation and education activities;
- Construction of cultural and entertainment institutions for children;
- Development of the production of cultural and artistic products for children;
- Cultural and personnel training policies;
- Inspection, supervision.- (Summary)
