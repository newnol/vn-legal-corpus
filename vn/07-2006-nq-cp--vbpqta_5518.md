# Resolution 07/2006/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
Adjusting the land use planning up to 2010 and the five-year (2006-2010) land use plan of Binh Thuan province
The Governments approves the adjustments to land use planning up to 2010 and the five-year (2006-2010) land use plan of Binh Thuan province and assigns the president of the People’s Committee of Binh Thuan province, the Minister of Natural Resources and Environment and heads of concerned agencies to implement this Resolution.- (Summary)
