# Quyết định 64/2003/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 64/2003/QD-BNN of June 2, 2003 promulgating the norms for production of clay powder used in dike consolidation by augering and grouting
To issue together with this Decision the norms for production of clay powder for used in dike consolidation by augering and grouting.
These norms shall serve as basis for calculation of the unit price of clay powder used in dike consolidation by augering and grouting.- (Summary)
