# Quyết định 21/2004/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 21/2004/QD-BNN of July 30, 2004 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational apparatus of Region-I Plant Quarantine Sub-Department
Region-I Plant Quarantine Sub-Department is an unit under the Plant Protection Department, functioning to perform the State management over plant quarantine, having the legal person status, its own seal, operation funding and bank accounts.
The Sub-Department is headquartered at 2 Tran Quang Khai street, Hai Phong city, and operates in Hai Phong city and Thai Binh, Hung Yen, Hai Duong and Quang Ninh provinces.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
