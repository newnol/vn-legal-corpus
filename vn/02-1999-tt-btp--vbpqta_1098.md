# Thông tư 02/1999/TT-BTP

CIRCULAR No.02/1999/TT-BTP of January 11, 1999 guiding the mission-trip allowance regime applicable to State officials and employees of the justice service, who make working trips inside the country
The Circular provides detailed guidance on the contents of the mission-trip allowance, the management and use thereof with regard to State officials and employees of the justice service, who make working trips inside the country and are allocated with such allowance by the Ministry.- (Summary)
