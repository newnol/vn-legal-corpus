# Thông tư 73/2004/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 73/2004/TT-BTC of July 16, 2004 amending Circular No. 117/2002/TT-BTC of December 27, 2002 guiding the implementation of the financial management regime applicable to construction lottery companies
This Circular amends Points 2.2 and 2.8, Section 2, Part II of Circular No. 117/2002/TT-BTC of December 27, 2002.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
