# Decision 271/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 271/QD-TTg of March 12, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Thai Binh province in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises which continue to have 100% of their charter capital held by the State and be legal entities by 2005: 11 public-utility enterprises and 4 business enterprises.
To be equitized, with the State holding over 50% of charter capital: In 2003: 5 enterprises and in 2004: 8 enterprises..
To be equitized, with the State holding under 50% of charter capital or holding no share: In 2003: 17 enterprises; in 2004: 5 enterprises; and in 2005: 1 enterprise.
To be equitized, with the State holding at least 51% of charter capital when shares are sold for the first time in 2004: 1 enterprise.
To be transformed from State enterprises into non-business units with revenues in 2003: 2 enterprises.
To be merged in 2003: 1 enterprise.
To be dissolved or bankrupted in 2003: 20 enterprises.- (Summary)
