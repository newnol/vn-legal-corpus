# Quyết định 173/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 173/2003/QD-TTg of August 19, 2003 on adjusting the boundary of Cat Tien National Park
To adjust the boundary of Cat Tien National Park on the basis of its practical situation, suitable to the current socio-economic-natural conditions, aiming to effectively protect the forest resources, ecological systems and diversity of the National Park.
After the adjustment its boundary, Cat Tien National Park shall cover an area of 70,548.36 ha; of which 26,969.80 ha belong to Lam Dong province; 39,108.80 ha to Dong Nai province; and 4,469.76 ha to Binh Phuoc province.- (Summary)
