# Quyết định 98/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 98/2004/QD-BCN of September 24, 2004 transforming Thang Long Plastic Factory of Vietnam Plastic Company into Thang Long Plastics Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Thang Long Plastic Joint-Stock Company, abbreviated to THALOPLAST, has its charter capital is VND 3,500,000,000, of which the State equities represent 50.69%; equities sold to laborers in the factory, 37.91%; and equities sold to other subjects, 11.4%.
Its head office is located at 360 Giai Phong Avenue, Thanh Xuan district, Hanoi.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
