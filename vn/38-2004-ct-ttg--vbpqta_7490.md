# Chỉ thị 38/2004/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE No. 38/2004/CT-TTg of November 9, 2004 on enhancing the ethnic minority language training for officials and employees working in ethnic minority and mountainous areas
Under this Directive, the ethnic minority language training for officials and employees working in ethnic minority and mountainous areas is a compulsory task in service of the management, socio-economic development and maintenance of security and national defense in these areas.- (Summary)
