# Decision 71/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.71/1998/QD-TTg of March 31, 1998 on the establishment of the school for training and fostering of construction cadres.
To establish the school for training and fostering of construction cadres under the Ministry of Construction, on the basis of the center for training and fostering of construction cadres.
The school is tasked to provide professional training and skill fostering for personnel of the construction industry.- (Summary)
