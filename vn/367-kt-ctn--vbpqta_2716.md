# Decision 367-KT/CTN

DECISION No. 367-KT/CTN ON THE 15TH OF NOVEMBER 1994, OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE STATE ON AWARDING THE TITLE OF PEOPLE'S TEACHER
THE PRESIDENT OF THE SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF VIETNAM
Proceeding from Article 103 of the Constitution of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam;
At the proposal of the Prime Minister,
DECIDES:
To award the title of People's Teacher to:
1. Mr. Duong Trong Bai, senior specialist, the Vietnam Institute of Educational Sciences;
2 Mr. Tran dinh Dan, former teacher, the Phan Dinh Phung Secondary General Education School, Ha Tinh province;
3. Mr. Phan Huy Le, professor of history, the Hanoi University;
4. Mr. To Nhu Khue, specialist, the Military Medical Institute, the Ministry of Defense;
5. Mr. Tran Xuan Truong, director, the Military Institute of Politics, the Ministry of Defense;
6. Mr. Mai Huu Khue, professor, the National Institute of Administration;
7. Mr. Dao Trong Tuyen, professor, the National Institute of Administration;
8. Mr. Tran Dinh Gian, former dean of faculty, the Hanoi Teachers' Training College No 1;
In recognition of their outstanding contributions to the building and development of education of the nation.
President of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam
LE DUC ANH
