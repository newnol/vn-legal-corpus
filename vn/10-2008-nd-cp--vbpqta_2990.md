# Decree 10/2008/ND-CP 

DECREE
Establishing Hoi An city of Quang Nam province
Under this Decree, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” Hoi An city is established on the basis of the whole land area, population and dependent administrative units of Hoi An provincial capital.- (Summary)
