# Quyết định 224/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 224/2003/QD-TTg of November 4, 2003 on the establishment of the provincial- and district-level Defense Education Councils
Under this Decision of the Prime Minister, Defense Education Councils shall be set up in provinces and centrally-run cities as well as rural districts, urban districts, provincial capitals and cities in order to assist the presidents of the provincial- or district-level People's Committees in considering and deciding on the orientations, programs and plans on defense education activities in their localities on the basis of the working plans and guidance of the Central Defense Education Council as well as the defense tasks and requirements in their respective localities.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Sumamry)
