# Quyết định 117/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 117/2003/QD-TTg of June 11, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under the Ministry of Health in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises where the State continues to hold 100% of their charter capital and their legal person status remains unchanged till 2005: 11.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding over 50% of their total shares: 2 in 2003 and 2 in 2004.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding at least 51% of their charter capital when shares are sold for the first time: 2 in 2003 and 2 in 2005.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding under 50% of their charter capital or holding no shares: 3 in 2003, 1 in 2004 and 1 in 2005.
To dissolve Vietnam Medical Equipment Corporation after the transfer of ownership of its member enterprises is completed.
The Ministry of Health shall direct the stepping up of materialization of the pharmaceutical development strategy till 2010; reorganize the distributing system, enhance the State management over medicine prices and quality in order to meet the people’s requirements on disease prevention and treatment; and at the same time expeditiously elaborate the scheme on production of antibiotics and essential medicines and submit it to the Prime Minister for consideration and decision.- (Summary)
