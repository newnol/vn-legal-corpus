# Directive 96/2006/CT-BNN

DIRECTIVE
Prevention and control of brown hoppers, rice grassy stunt disease and wilted stunt disease in southern provinces
The Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development requests concerned localities and units to perform several tasks in order to prevent and control brown hoppers, rice grassy stunt disease and wilted stunt disease in southern provinces.- (Summary)
