# Decision 466/TTg

Decision No.466-TTg of July 2, 1997 on the general inventory and re-assessment of the State's fixed assets in the administrative and non-business sector.
In order to grasp the existing situation of the State assets in administrative and non-business agencies which shall serve as basis for setting standards, norms, regime and policies for annual and long-term management of assets, ensure the strict observance of the Law on the State Budget and the Ordinance on the practice of thrift and combat against wastefulness in the procurenment and use of the State assets in administrative and non-business agencies, the Prime Minister decides to conduct a general inventory and re-assessment of the State's fixed assets in administrative and non-business units from the central to the grass-roots levels, the State agencies in foreign countries, the organizations of the Party, the defense and security agencies and socio-political organizations using the State budget funds.-
