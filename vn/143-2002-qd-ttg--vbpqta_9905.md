# Quyết định 143/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 143/2002/QD-TTg of October 21, 2002 establishing the Steering Committee for Construction of (new) National Assembly Building and Ba Dinh Hall
The Steering Committee for Construction of (new) National Assembly Building and Ba Dinh Hall shall be composed of its head being Deputy Prime Minister Nguyen Tan Dung, 3 deputy heads and 10 members being representatives of the concerned ministries and branches.
The Steering Committee shall have the following functions and powers:
- To direct the implementation of the investment project on construction of (new) National Assembly Building and Ba Dinh Hall at Ba Dinh political center zone, Hanoi, according to the project�s feasible study report approved by the Prime Minister;
- To direct the proposition of specific mechanisms and solutions in the course of preparation and implementation of the project, then submit them to the Prime Minister for consideration and decision;
- To inspect and urge the project implementation by concerned ministries and branches and the People�s Committee of Hanoi city; to decide on measures to handle problems arising in the course of preparation and implementation of the project;
- To direct the cooperation with domestic and foreign organizations and experts in the project preparation and implementation.- (Summary)
