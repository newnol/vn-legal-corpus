# Decision 123/1999/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 123/1999/QD-TTg of May 11, 1999 on the establishment of the Management Board of the Techno-Economic Program for Information Technology
To establish the Management Board of the Techno-Economic Program for Information Technology to assist the Prime Minister in organizing the implementation of the Techno-Economic Program for Information Technology.
The Management Board of the Techno-Economic Program for Information Technology shall have the following tasks and powers:
- To elaborate, on the basis of the socio-economic development strategy and scientific-technological development strategy, the overall plan, five-year plans and annual plans for the implementation of the Techno-Economic Program for Information Technology, then submit them to the Prime Minister for approval;
- To organize and direct the implementation of the Techno-Economic Program for Information Technology according to Decision No. 54/1998/QD-TTg of March 3, 1998 of the Prime Minister promulgating the Regulation on management and execution of the techno-economic programs; to coordinate with the functional agencies in evaluating, approving and allocating funds for the Program�s projects according to the current regulations, and at the same time to monitor, guide and inspect the execution of projects by the ministries, branches and localities; to propose to the Prime Minister for consideration and handling problems arising beyond its competence in the course of implementation of the Program; and to be entitled to request the ministries, branches and localities to organize the execution of the Program�s projects;
- To coordinate with the concerned agencies in formulating the policies, regimes and measures to implement the Techno-Economic Program for Information Technology, then submit them to the Prime Minister for promulgation; and to promulgate documents guiding and inspecting the implementation of regulations on management and execution of the Techno-Economic Program for Information Technology;
- To periodically report as prescribed on the implementation of the Program and coordinate with the concerned agencies in making pre-acceptance evaluation and inspection of the execution results of the Program�s projects.
The Management Board of the Techno-Economic Program for Information Technology shall be headed by the Minister of Science, Technology and Environment and have its standing body situated at the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment.- (Summary)
