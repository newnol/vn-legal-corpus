# Quyết định 220/2005/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
On the establishment of the Vietnamese Academy of Agricultural Sciences
Under this Decision, the Vietnamese Academy of Agricultural Sciences, VAAS, is an organization attached to the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development and tasked to conduct scientific research, technology transfer and postgraduate training in the agricultural domain in service of state management requirements and agricultural development.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
