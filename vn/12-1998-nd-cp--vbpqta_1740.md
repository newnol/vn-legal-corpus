# Decree 12/1998/NĐ-CP

DECREE No.12/1998/ND-CP of February 9, 1998 on the establishment of Song Cau commune of Khanh Vinh district, Khanh Hoa province.
To establish Song Cau commune of Khanh Vinh district, Khanh Hoa province on the basis of a land area of 2,513 hectares and a population of 1,214 of Khanh Phu commune.- (Summary)
