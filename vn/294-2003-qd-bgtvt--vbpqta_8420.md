# Quyết định 294/2003/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION No. 294/2003/QD-BGTVT of January 28, 2003 promulgating the Regulation on the responsibilities and handling of organizations and individuals having violated the regulations on management and administration of operation of land-road motorized vehicles' tonnage-inspecting stations
This Regulation defines the responsibilities and forms of handling of violators of the regulations on managing and administering the operation of land-road motorized vehicles' tonnage-inspecting stations.
This Regulation shall apply to organizations and individuals that manage and personally perform tasks at vehicles' tonnage-inspecting stations under the communications and transport service.- (Summary)
