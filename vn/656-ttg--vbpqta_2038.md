# Quyết định 656-TTg

Decision No.656-TTg of September 13, 1996 of the Prime Minister on the socio-economic development of the Central Highlands in the periods of 1996-2000 and 1996-2010.
The Prime Minister�s Decision points out: The Central Highland has a position of strategic importance for the national economy, politics, security and defense.
The Decision lays down the main targets for the socio-economic development of the Central Highlands in the periods of 1996-2000 and 1996-2010, including:
- Development of agriculture and forestry.
- Development of industry.
- Education and health care, cultural and social affairs.
- The sedentarization, population movement and development of new economic zones.
- Hunger elimination and poverty alleviation.
The Prime Minister�s Decision also touches upon basic solutions regarding transport; protection and exploitation of water resources; electricity; urban development; development of human resources, science and technology; land and protection of forest resources; capital and investment; renewal of State enterprises and development of the various forms of the cooperative economy.- (Summary)
