# Quyết định 04/2004/QĐ-BTS

DECISION No. 04/2004/QD-BTS of April 1, 2004 promulgating a branch standard
This Decision promulgates the branch standard "Procedure for diagnosis of white spot disease in Penaeid shrimp by Polymerase Chain Reaction", which is encouraged for application by fishery disease-testing establishments nationwide. It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
