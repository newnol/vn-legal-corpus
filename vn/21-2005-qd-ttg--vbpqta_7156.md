# Quyết định 21/2005/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 21/2005/QD-TTg of January 25, 2005 on transforming Can Tho Thermal Power Plant into Can Tho Thermal Power Company Limited
Under this Decision, Can Tho Thermal Power Company Limited, CTTP Co, Ltd., has a charter capital of VND 180,000,000,000.
Its head-office is located at No. 01, Le Hong Phong street, Tra Noc ward, Binh Thuy district, Can Tho city.
