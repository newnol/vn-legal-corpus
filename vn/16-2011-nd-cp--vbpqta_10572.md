# Decree 16/2011/NĐ-CP

DECREE
ON AMENDING AND SUPPLEMENTING THE GOVERNMENT’S DECREE NO 105/2004/ND-CP OF MARCH 30, 2004, OF INDEPENDENT AUDITOR
GOVERNMENT
Pursuant to the Law on Government Organization of December 25, 2001; Pursuant to the Accounting Law of June 17, 2003; Pursuant to the Enterprises Law of November 29, 2005; At the proposal of the Minister of Finance,
DECREES:
Article 1: T o amend and supplement Point b, Clause 1, Article 13 of the Government's Decree No.105/2004/ND-CP, of March 30, 2004 on independent auditors as follows:
“b) Having bachelor or higher degrees in Economic-Finance-Bank or Accounting-Auditing and having financial or accounting actual working time for at least 5 years or longer or actual time of working as auditor assistants in auditing enterprises for at least 4 years or longer, counted from the year of bachelor degree issuing to the year of exam registration
Article 2 : This Decree takes effect from April 20, 2011.
Ministers, heads of ministry-equivalent agencies, heads of agencies of Government, President's People Committee of centrally-run provinces and cities shall be responsible for implement this Decree.
