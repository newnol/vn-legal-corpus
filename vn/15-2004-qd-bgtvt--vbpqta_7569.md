# Quyết định 15/2004/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION No. 15/2004/QD-BGTVT of September 16, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on responsibilities and forms of handling of organizations and individuals involved in the railway infrastructure management and maintenance
The Regulation prescribes violation acts and handling forms as well as violation-handling competence in the management and maintenance of national railway infrastructure.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
