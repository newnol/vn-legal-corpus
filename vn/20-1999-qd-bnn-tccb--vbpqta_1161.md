# Decision 20/1999/QD-BNN-TCCB

DECISION No.20/1999/QD-BNN-TCCB of January 26, 1999 ratifying the Statute on Organization and Operation of the Irrigation Construction and Technology Transfer Company attached to the Irrigation Science Institute
The Irrigation Construction and Technology Transfer Company is a State enterprise attached to the Irrigation Science Institute.
The Statute of the Irrigation Construction and Technology Transfer Company, issued together with this Decision concretely specifies the powers, responsibilities and obligations of the Company; its managerial, production and business organizations; its finance; and its re-organization, dissolution and bankruptcy.- (Summary)
