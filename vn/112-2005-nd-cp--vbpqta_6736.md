# Nghị định 112/2005/NĐ-CP

DECREE
Establishing Quang Ngai city of Quang Ngai province
Under this Decree, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO," Quang Ngai city is established on the basis of the whole 3,712 hectares and 133,843 people with 10 dependent administrative units (8 wards and 2 communes) of Quang Ngai provincial capital.- (Summary)
