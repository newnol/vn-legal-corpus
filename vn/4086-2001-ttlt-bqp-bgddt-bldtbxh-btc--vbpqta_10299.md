# Thông tư liên tịch 4086/2001/TTLT/BQP-BGDĐT-BLĐTBXH-BTC

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 4086/2001/TTLT-BQP-BGDDT-BLDTBXH-BTC of December 24, 2001 guiding the implementation of the Government?s Decree No. 15/2001/ND-CP of May 1, 2001 on defense education
This Circular guides the regulations on the subjects, contents, time, organization and methods of defense education; the scope of responsibilities of the ministries, the ministerial-level agencies, the agencies attached to the Government and the provinces and centrally-run cities in the defense education work; and fundings spent on defense education.- (Summary)
