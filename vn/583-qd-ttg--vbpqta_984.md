# Decision 583/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 583/QD-TTg of June 1 st , 1999 on financial support for the creation of literary, art and press works and projects in two years 1999-2000
The works and projects to be considered for financial support shall cover the following subjects:
- The national history, revolution, resistance wars and achievements in the fields of culture, arts and press.
- The renewal process, active factors in the society and typical characters of the era.
- The youth and youngsters
The Decision also stipulates in detail conditions for and objects of consideration for financial support; the contents of financial support and the assignment of responsibilities for the performance of the task of giving financial support.- (Summary)
