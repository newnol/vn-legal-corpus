# Quyết định 25/2003/QĐ-BTC

Decision No. 25/2003/QD-BTC of March 1, 2003 annulling the ceiling sale prices of construction steel
As from March 1, 2003, the ceiling sale prices of construction steel, prescribed in Decision No. 20/1999/QD-BVGCP of April 10, 1999 and Document No. 23/BVGCP-TLSX of January 9, 2003 of the Government Pricing Committee on the sale price of construction steel, are annulled.- (Summary)
