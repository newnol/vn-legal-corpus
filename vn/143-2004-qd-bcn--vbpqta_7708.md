# Quyết định 143/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 143/2004/QD-BCN of November 23, 2004 on transforming Nam Dinh Textile - Garment Engineering Company into Nam Dinh Textile - Garment Engineering Joint - Stock Company
Under this Decision, Nam Dinh Textile - Garment Engineering Joint-Stock Company, NATEXENCO, has a charter capital of VND 4,000,000,000, of which the State's equities represent 15% and equities sold to laborers of the company, 85%.
Its head - office is located at No. 1 Giai Phong avenue, Nam Dinh city, Nam Dinh province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
