# Decision 131/2006/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Annulling a decision
This Decision annuls the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 639/TTg of August 12, 1997, on the organization, functions, tasks and powers of seaport authorities. It takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
