# Quyết định 07/2001/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION No. 07/2001/QD-BGDDT of March 30, 2001 amending and supplementing a number of articles of the Regulation on senior-secondary, specialized secondary and junior-secondary education graduation examinations
This Decision amends and supplements a number of articles of the Regulation on senior-secondary, specialized secondary and junior-secondary education graduation exams, issued together with Decision No. 15/1998/QD-BGDDT of April 1, 1998, Decision No. 07/1999/QD-BGDDT of February 27, 1999 and Decision No. 03/2000/QD-BGDDT of February 28, 2000 of the Minister of Education and Training.- (Summary)
