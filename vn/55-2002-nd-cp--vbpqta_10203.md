# Nghị định 55/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 55/2002/ND-CP of May 13, 2002 establishing a ward and communes of Bac Lieu provincial capital and Vinh Loi district, Bac Lieu province
To establish ward 1 of Bac Lieu provincial capital with a land area of 582.6 ha and a population of 17,568.
To establish Chau Hung A commune of Vinh Loi district with a land area of 2,959.21 ha and a population of 9,680 of Chau Hung commune.
To establish Vinh Hung A commune of Vinh Loi district with a land area of 2,080.93 ha and a population of 9,380 of Vinh Hung commune.- (Summary)
