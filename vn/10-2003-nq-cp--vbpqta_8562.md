# Nghị quyết 10/2003/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION No. 10/2003/NQ-CP of August 4, 2003 of the Government's July 2003 regular meeting
The Government's July 2003 regular meeting issued the resolution, stating that it had considered the Government's program and plan for the implementation of the Politburo's Resolution on further reorganizing, renovating and developing the State-run agricultural and forestry farms; the (amended) draft ordinance on execution of civil judgments and the draft ordinances on plant varieties and animal breeds; and the implementation of the socio-economic plans in July and the first 7 months of the year.- (Summary)
