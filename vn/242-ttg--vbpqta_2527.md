# Decision 242-TTg

DECISION No.242-TTg of April 17, 1997 on the regime of allowances for officials and employees directly involved in the forced execution of court judgments.
This Decision provides for the allowance levels, financial sources for allowances for judgment execution officials including procurators, policemen, members of self-defense or militia forces, representatives of local administration, representatives of social organizations directly involved in the forced execution of court judgments.- (Summary)
