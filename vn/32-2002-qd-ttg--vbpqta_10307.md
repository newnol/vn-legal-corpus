# Quyết định 32/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 32/2002/QD-TTg of February 8, 2002 establishing the Vietnam Sub-Committee in the Vietnam-Tunisia Inter-Governmental Committee for Economic, Trade, Cultural and Scientific-Technical Cooperation
The Vietnam Sub-Committee under the Vietnam-Tunisia Inter-Governmental Committee for Economic, Trade, Cultural and Scientific-Technical Cooperation shall include a Vice-Minister of Trade as its chairman and representatives of the Ministry for Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Planning and Investment, the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development, the Ministry of Industry, the Ministry of Aquatic Resources, and the Ministry of Culture and Information as its standing members.- (Summary)
