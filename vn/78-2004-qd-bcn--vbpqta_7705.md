# Quyết định 78/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 78/2004/QD-BCN of August 10, 2004 transforming Hai Phong Metal Company into Hai Phong Metal Joint-Stock Company
To transform Hai Phong Metal Company, an independent cost-accounting member enterprise of Vietnam Steel Corporation, into Hai Phong Metal Joint-Stock Company (HPC).
Its head-office is located at 32 Tran Khanh Du road, Ngo Quyen district, Hai Phong city.
Its charter capital is VND 15 billion, with 84.26% of which to be sold to its laborers and 15.74% to outsiders.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
