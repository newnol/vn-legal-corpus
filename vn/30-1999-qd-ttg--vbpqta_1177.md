# Quyết định 30/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.30/1999/QD-TTg of March 1st, 1999 on assigning the task of doctorate training to the Naval Academy
To assign the task of doctorate training to the Naval Academy. The Ministry of Education and Training shall assume the prime responsibility and coordinate with the Ministry of Defense and the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment to define the areas with doctorate training for the Naval Academy.- (Summary)
