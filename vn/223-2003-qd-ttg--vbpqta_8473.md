# Quyết định 223/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 223/2003/QD-TTg of November 3, 2003 approving the investment project on development of raw-material zone for Thanh Hoa paper and paper pulp plant
Under the plan approved by the Prime Minister in this Decision, the investment project on development of raw-material zone for Thanh Hoa paper and paper pulp plant shall be implemented in the 2002-2011 period, with a total investment capital of around VND 997,454.8 million, including State budget capital, preferential credit loans and people's owned capital.
In the first phase, the project aims to exploit natural and planted bamboo forests for the annual supply of 250,000 tons of raw materials for Thanh Hoa paper and paper pulp plant, which has the capacity of 50,000 tons of pulp/year.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
