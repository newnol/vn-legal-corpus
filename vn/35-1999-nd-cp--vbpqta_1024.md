# Nghị định 35/1999/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 35/1999/ND-CP of May 14, 1999 on the establishment of Van Giang district township of Chau Giang district, Hung Yen province
To establish Van Giang district township of Chau Giang district, Hung Yen province on the basis of the whole land area of 677.91 hectares and the whole population of 8,590 of Van Phuc commune.- (Summary)
