# Quyết định 268/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 268/2003/QD-TTg of December 22, 2003 amending and supplementing a number of articles of the Prime Minister's Decision No. 246/1998/QD-TTg of December 22, 1998 on some special regimes for military pilots, crew members and subjects directly servicing the flights at military airfields under the Ministry of Defense
This Decision amends Point a, Clause 2, and Clause 3 of Article 1 and amends and supplements Article 4 of the Prime Minister's Decision No. 246/1998/QD-TTg of December 22, 1998.
This Decision takes effect as from January 1, 2004.- (Summary)
