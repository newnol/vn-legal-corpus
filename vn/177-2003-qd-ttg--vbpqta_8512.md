# Quyết định 177/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 177/2003/QD-TTg of September 1, 2003 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the State Archives Administration
The State Archives Administration is an agency under the Ministry of Home Affairs, which performs the function of State management over records and archives and management of national archival documents under the Vietnamese State's Archives according to law provisions.
The State Archives Administration has the seal bearing the national emblem.- (Summary)
