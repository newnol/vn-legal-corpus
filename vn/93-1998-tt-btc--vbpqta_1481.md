# Thông tư 93/1998/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 93/1998/TT-BTC of June 30, 1998 stipulating the regime of spendings on conferences
This Circular stipulates the regime of spendings on meetings in the State management agencies, the non-business units, the Party's agencies, the socio-political organizations, the social organizations and the socio-occupational organizations that use funds allocated from the State budget.-
CIRCULAR No. 94/1998/TT-BTC of June 30, 1998 stipulating the regime of working travel allowances for State officials and employees who are sent on working missions inside the country.
This Circular stipulates the specific amounts to be paid for working travel allowances, as well as the management of payment for working travel allowances.-
