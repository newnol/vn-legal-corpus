# Thông tư 11/2002/TT-BYT

CIRCULAR No. 11/2002/TT-BYT of July 8, 2002 guiding the implementation of post allowance regime for heads and deputy heads of sections and departments in prophylactic medicine units of the provincial/municipal level
Subjects governed by this Circular are officials and employees who are appointed to the posts of head and deputy head of sections and departments in medical public-service units of the provincial level.
This Circular prescribes the allowance levels, conditions to enjoy and methods to calculate allowances; and sources for allowance payment.- (Summary)
