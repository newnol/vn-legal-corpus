# Quyết định 120/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 120/2004/QD-BCN of November 2, 2004 transforming the Mechanical Product Export-Import Company into the Mechanical Product Export-Import Limited Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, the Mechanical Product Export-Import Limited Company (MECANIMEX Co. Ltd.) is headquartered at No. 37, Trang Thi street, Hoan Kiem district, Hanoi city, and has a charter capital of VND 34,897 million.- (Summary)
