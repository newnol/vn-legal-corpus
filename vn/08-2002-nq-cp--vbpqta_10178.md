# Nghị quyết 08/2002/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION No. 08/2002/NQ-CP of July 4, 2002 on the Government�s June 2002 regular meeting
On June 28 and 29, 2002, the Government held its June 2002 regular meeting, considering the following issues:
- It considered the supplementary report on the implementation of the 2001 plan and budget as well as plan and budget tasks in the first 6 months of 2002; the supplementary report on the results of implementation of the 2001 State budget, the State budget situation in the first 6 months of 2002 as well as major measures to successfully perform the 2002 financial-State budget task. The Government assigned the Ministry of Planning and Investment to assume the prime responsibility and coordinate with the Ministry of Finance, the Government�s Office and the General Department of Statistics in standardizing data, finalizing reports and submitting them to the Prime Minister for supplementary report before the National Assembly.
- It considered the report on a number of issues regarding the Government�s direction and administration work in the first 6 months of 2002.
- It gave comments on the Bill Amending and Supplementing a Number of Articles of the Law on Complaints and Denunciations, and the draft Ordinance on Industrial Mobilization.- (Summary)
