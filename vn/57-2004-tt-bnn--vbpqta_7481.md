# Thông tư 57/2004/TT-BNN

CIRCULAR No. 57/2004/TT-BNN of November 1, 2004 guiding the implementation of the policy in support of population relocation and settlement in Vietnam-China border communes under the Prime Minister’s Decision No.120/2003/QD-TTg of June 11, 2003
This Circular applies in support of population relocation and resettlement for execution of the project on population arrangement and settlement in Vietnam-China border communes in the provinces of Lai Chau, Dien Bien, Lao Cai, Ha Giang, Cao Bang, Lang Son and Quang Ninh.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
