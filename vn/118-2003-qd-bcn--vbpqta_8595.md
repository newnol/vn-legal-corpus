# Quyết định 118/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 118/2003/QD-BCN of July 7, 2003 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Industrial Review of Vietnam
The Industrial Review of Vietnam is an agency providing theoretical information on industrial science, technology and economy, which is attached to the Ministry of Industry.
The Review is a non-business unit with revenues and has the legal person status.- (Summary)
