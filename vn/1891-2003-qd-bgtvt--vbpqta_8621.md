# Quyết định 1891/2003/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION No. 1891/2003/QD-BGTVT of July 1, 2003 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of Vietnam Railway Administration
Vietnam Railway Administration is attached to the Ministry of Communications and Transport, performs the function of specialized State management over railway communications and transport nationwide.
Vietnam Railway Administration has the legal person status and its owns seal; is entitled to enjoy allocations from the State budget.
It is headquartered in Hanoi.- (Summary)
