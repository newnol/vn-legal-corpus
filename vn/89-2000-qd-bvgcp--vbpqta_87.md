# Decision 89/2000/QĐ-BVGCP

DECISION No. 89/2000/QD-BVGCP of November 13, 2000 on freight of goods transportation by cars
To issue together with this Decision the Table of freight of goods transportation by cars and the Guidelines for calculating freight of goods transportation by cars, applicable to:
- Determination of goods transport freight which shall be paid from the State budget sources. In cases where the bidding mechanism is applied to cargo freight, the bid-winning freight shall apply.
- Determination of unit price for price and freight subsidies of each goods item in each locality, which shall serve as basis for calculation of annual estimation of price and freight subsidies in the provinces which implement policies for mountainous areas according to the Government�s Decree No. 20/1998/ND-CP of March 31, 1998 on trade development in mountainous areas, off-shore islands and ethnic minority regions.
- To be used as basis for reference by various units during the process of negotiating goods transport contracts other than the above-mentioned cases.- (Summary)
