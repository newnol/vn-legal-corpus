# Joint circular 01/1999/TTLT/BKH-BTC-BLĐTBXH

JOINT CIRCULAR No.01/1999/TTLT/BKH-BTC-BLDTBXH of March 15, 1999 guiding the planning of the combination of programs and/or projects for hunger elimination and poverty alleviation
Programs and/or projects with objectives, tasks and sources which the poor and poor communes directly benefit from shall be called programs and/or projects for hunger elimination and poverty alleviation. There are 21 domestic and international cooperation programs and projects for hunger elimination and poverty alleviation in the 1998-2000 period. The planning of programs and projects for hunger elimination and poverty alleviation is aimed to direct resources of each program and project to the common objective of hunger elimination and poverty alleviation. The combination of resources of programs and projects for hunger elimination and poverty alleviation shall be effected right at the stage of capital distribution and allocation of the planned norms.- (Summary)
