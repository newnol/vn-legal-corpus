# Order 02/2002/L-CTN

ORDER No. 02/2002/L-CTN ON THE PROMULGATION OF LAW
THE PRESIDENT OF THE SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF VIETNAM
Pursuant to Articles 103 and 106 of the 1992 Constitution of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam;
Pursuant to Article 78 of the Law on Organization of the National Assembly;
Pursuant to Article 50 of the Law on Promulgation of Legal Documents,
HEREBY PROMULGATES:
The Law on Organization of the National Assembly,
which was passed by the X th National Assembly of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam at its 10 th session on December 25, 2001.
President of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam TRAN DUC LUONG
