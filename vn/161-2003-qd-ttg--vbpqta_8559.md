# Quyết định 161/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 161/2003/QD-TTg of August 4, 2003 promulgating the Regulation on training and fostering of State officials and employees
The Regulation on training and fostering of State officials and employees, issued together with this Decision, applies to the following subjects:
- Administrative employees and reserve employees working in State agencies at the central, provincial and district levels;
- Officials and employees in communes, wards and townships;
- Deputies to the People's Councils of all levels.
Training and fostering aim to provide trainees with political theoretical knowledge, State management knowledge and skills, professional qualifications, foreign language and informatics knowledge as well as other support knowledge in order to build up a contingent of State officials and employees who are professionally proficient, meeting the requirements of raising the efficiency of the State apparatus, have good moralities and are loyal to the State.- (Summary)
