# Quyết định 50/2006/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
Providing for levels of the fee for grant of ABTCs to APEC business people, and the regime of fee collection, remittance, management and use
This Decision shall take effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”
Fee-collecting agencies shall register, declare, collect, remit, manage and use collected fee amounts according to the provisions of Circular No. 63/2002/TT-BTC of July 24, 2002, and Circular No. 45/2006/TT-BTC of May 25, 2006, amending Circular No. 63/2002/TT-BTC.- (Summary)
