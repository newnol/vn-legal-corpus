# Quyết định 138/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 138/2003/QD-TTg of July 11, 2003 transforming the South Basic Chemicals Company into a one-member limited liability company
To transform the South Basic Chemicals Company, an independent-accounting member enterprise of Vietnam Chemicals Corporation, into a one-member limited liability company.
The South Basic Chemicals Limited Company has a charter capital of VND 61 billion.
Its production and business lines: raw materials, chemical products, and relevant research and testing activities as well as technical services.
Its head office: 22 Ly Tu Trong street, Ben Nghe ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh city.- (Summary)
