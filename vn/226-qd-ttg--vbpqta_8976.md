# Decision 226/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 226/QD-TTg of February 26, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under People's Committee of Tien Giang province in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises where the State holds 100% of their charter capital and their legal person status remains unchanged till 2005: 4 public-utility enterprises and 9 enterprises engaged in business activities.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding more than 50% of their charter capital: 1 in 2003 and 3 in 2005.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding at least 51% of shares when their shares are sold for the first time: 1 in 2003 and 3 in 2004.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding under 50% of their charter capital or holding no shares: 3 in 2003 and 1 in 2004.
Enterprise to be dissolved: 1 in 2003.
Enterprise to be declared bankrupt: 1 in 2003.
Enterprises to be transformed into non-business units with revenues: 3 in 2003.- (Summary)
