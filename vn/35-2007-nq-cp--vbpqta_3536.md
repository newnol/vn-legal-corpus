# Resolution 35/2007/NQ-CP 

RESOLUTION
On the Government’s June 2007 regular meeting
On July 2 and 3, 2007, the Government held its June regular meeting where it heard concerned ministries’ reports on the performance of tasks in the first half of the year and implementation solutions for the second half of the year; agreed to approve a draft decree guiding the implementation of the Youth Law and assigned the Ministry of Home Affairs to finalize and submit it to the Prime Minister for promulgation in July; discussed a draft resolution on policies to sell state-owned dwelling houses under Decree No. 61/CP of July 5, 1994; and heard the bill on regulations for acquisition of assets of individuals or organizations.- (Summary)
