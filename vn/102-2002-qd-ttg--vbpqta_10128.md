# Quyết định 102/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 102/2002/QD-TTg of July 30, 2002 upgrading Vu Quang Nature Conservation Zone into Vu Quang National Park, Ha Tinh province
To upgrade Vu Quang Nature Conservation Zone into a national park belonging to the system of Vietnam�s special-use forests.
Objectives and tasks of Vu Quang National Park are:
- To conserve the model ecological system of Northern Truong Son forests, to preserve the typical bio-diversity of the natural forests in Southwestern area of Zone IV belonging to Truong Son mountain range contiguous to the Vietnam-Laos border.
- To contribute to maintaining the ecological balance and expanding the forest coverage, thus ensuring environmental security and eco-natural sustainable development of Zone IV�s provinces, and at the same time bringing into play the values of the forest ecological system in service of scientific research, sightseeing and ecological tourism.
The People�s Committee of Ha Tinh province shall directly manage Vu Quang National Park.- (Summary)
