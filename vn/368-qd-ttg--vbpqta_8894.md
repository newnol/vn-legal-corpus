# Decision 368/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 368/QD-TTg of April 3, 2003 approving the master plan for reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under Vietnam Coal Corporation in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises which continue to have 100% of their charter capital owned by the State: 5 non-business units, 24 independent cost-accounting member enterprises and 5 dependent cost-accounting member enterprises to be kept intact; and 3 independent cost-accounting member enterprises to be transformed into one-member limited liability companies.
To be equitized, with the State holding at least 51% of shares when shares are sold for the first time: 12 units.
To merge Cam Pha Mechanical Engineering Company into Cam Pha Dynamic Engineering Company in 2003.
To split 3 geological factories under Geological and Mineral Exploitation Company to establish Geology Company, a dependent cost-accounting enterprise of the Corporation.- (Summary)
