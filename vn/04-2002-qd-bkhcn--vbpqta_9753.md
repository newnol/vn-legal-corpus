# Decision 04/2002/QD-BKHCN

DECISION No. 04/2002/QD-BKHCN OF OCTOBER 30, 2002 ANNULLING VIETNAMESE STANDARDS
THE MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY
Pursuant to the Government’s Decree No. 22/CP of May 22, 1993 on the tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment (now the Ministry of Science and Technology);
Pursuant to the Ordinance on Goods Quality of December 24, 1999;
At the proposal of the General Director of Standardization, Metrology and Quality Control,
DECIDES :
Article 1.- To annul the following 7 Vietnamese standards:
1. TCVN 4536 - 88 Textile materials – Methods of determining color fastness – General provisions
2. TCVN 4537 - 88 Textile materials – Methods of determining color fastness against soap-washing
3. TCVN 5232 – 90 Textile materials – Methods of determining color fastness against dry-bleaching
4. TCVN 5074 – 90 Textile materials – Methods of determining color fastness against water
5. TCVN 5235 – 90 Textile materials – Methods of determining color fastness against sweat
6. TCVN 4538 – 94 Textile materials – Methods of determining color fastness – Color fastness against friction
7. TCVN 5437 : 1991 Enamelled ceramic tiles for walling
Article 2.- This Decision takes implementation effect after its signing.
For the Minister of Science and Technology Vice Minister BUI MANH HAI
