# Quyết định 06/2003/QĐ-BTS

DECISION No. 06/2003/QD-BTS of May 30, 2003 promulgating the branch-level standards
Issued together with this Decision are the following 5 branch-level standards:
1. 28 TCN 182:2003: Sulfite in fishery products - Method for quantitative analysis
2. 28 TCN 183:2003: Boric acid and borates in fishery products - Method for qualitative analysis
3. 28 TCN 184:2003: Urea in fishery products - Method for qualitative analysis
4. 28 TCN 185:2003: Polyphosphate in fishery products - Method for quantitative analysis by ion chromatography
5. 28 TCN 186:2003: Chloramphenicol in fishery products - Method for quantitative analysis by gas chromatography.- (Summary)
