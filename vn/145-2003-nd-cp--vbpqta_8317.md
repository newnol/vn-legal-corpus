# Decree 145/2003/ND-CP

DECREE No. 145/2003/ND-CP of December 1, 2003 establishing Tay Tra rural district; expanding townships, and establishing and renaming communes of Binh Son and Ly Son rural districts, Quang Ngai province
To establish Tay Tra rural district on the basis of a land area of 33,680 ha and a population of 16,750 of Tra Bong rural district. Tay Tra rural district shall consist of nine dependent administrative units.
After the adjustment of its administrative boundary, Tra Bong rural district shall be left with a land area of 41,875 ha, a population of 28,336, and have 10 dependent administrative units.
To transfer a land area of 52.37 ha and a population of 1,041 of Binh Trung commune and a land area of 20.43 ha and a population of 502 of Binh Thoi commune, Binh Son rural district, to Chau O township. Chau O township shall have a land area of 235.80 ha and a population of 8,732.
To establish An Binh commune of Ly Son rural district on the basis of a land area of 69 ha and a population of 398 of Ly Vinh commune.
To rename Ly Vinh commune An Vinh commune.
To rename Ly Hai commune of Ly Son rural district An Hai commune.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
