# Circular 45/1999/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 45/1999/TT-BTC of May 4, 1999 stipulating working trip allowances regime for State officials and employees who travel abroad on short working missions
This Circular applies to officials and employees in administrative and non-business agencies and units, agencies and units of Party and mass organizations and armed forces, who go abroad on short working mission with State budget-allocated allowances.- (Summary)
