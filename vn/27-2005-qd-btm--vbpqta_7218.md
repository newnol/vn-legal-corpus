# Quyết định 27/2005/QĐ-BTM

DECISION OF THE MINISTER OF TRADE
Authorizing the approval of plans on import of finished products
for combination with export products of foreign-invested enterprise s
THE MINISTER OF TRADE
Pursuant to the Government's Decree No. 29/2004/ND-CP of January 16, 2004 on the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Ministry of Trade;
Pursuant to the Government's Decree No. 24/2000/ND-CP of July 31, 2000 detailing the implementation of the Law on Foreign Investment in Vietnam;
After consulting the People's Committees and the Industrial-Park and Export-Processing Zone Management Boards of the provinces and centrally-run cities;
At the proposal of the Director of the Planning and Investment Department,
DECIDES:
Article 1.- To authorize the People's Committees and the Industrial-Park and Export-Processing Zone Management Boards of the provinces and centrally-run cities (hereinafter referred collectively to as authorized agencies) to approve plans on the import of finished products for combination with export products of joint-venture enterprises, 100% foreign-capital enterprises, export-processing enterprises or parties to business cooperation contracts (hereinafter referred collectively to as foreign-invested enterprises), which fall under the management scope of authorized agencies.
Article 2.- The approval of plans on the import of finished products for combination with export products of foreign-invested enterprises shall comply with the following regulations:
1. Export products of foreign-invested enterprises must be compatible with production and/or business lines prescribed in the investment licenses.
2. The quantity of finished products to be imported (including loss percentage) must be compatible with the quantity of export products of foreign-invested enterprises.
3. The value of each finished product to be imported (calculated according to CIF price) must account for less than 40% of the value of an export product (calculated according to FOB price).
Article 3.- Authorized agencies shall permit the import of finished products for combination with export products of foreign-invested enterprises only after the enterprises have obtained goods orders or contracts on product export.
Article 4.- The Planning and Investment Department shall regularly inspect the authorization prescribed in this Decision.
Article 5.- This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
Article 6.- The agencies stated in Article 1, the Director of the Ministry Office, the Director of the Planning and Investment Department, the heads of units of the Ministry and the concerned organizations and individuals shall have to implement this Decision./.
