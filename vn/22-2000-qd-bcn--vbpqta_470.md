# Quyết định 22/2000/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 22/2000/QD-BCN of April 5, 2000 on the transfer of the Miners Transport Enterprise, a unit of the Vietnam Coal Corporation, to the Supplies Transport and Handling Company
To transfer the Miners Transport Enterprise, a dependent accounting member enterprise of the Vietnam Coal Corporation, to the Supplies Transport and Handling Company, an independent accounting member enterprise of the Corporation.
The enterprise is headquartered in Cam Dong ward, Cam Pha town, Quang Ninh province.
Its business lines:
- Transporting officials and employees to and from the enterprise
- Transporting coal, earth and rock and other commodities
- Repairing automobiles and mechanical engineering processing
- Exploiting and processing coal.- (Summary)
