# Quyết định 229/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 229/2003/QD-BCN of December 24, 2003 transforming Bien Hoa Electronics Company into Vietronics Bien Hoa Joint-Stock Company
To transform Bien Hoa Electronics Company, an independent-accounting member of Vietnam Electronics and Informatics Corporation, into Vietronics Bien Hoa Joint-Stock Company (BELCO).
Its head-office: 52-54 Nguyen Hue street, District 1, Ho Chi Minh city.
Its charter capital is VND 60 billion, with 51% of which being held by the State, 35.67% being sold to its laborers, and 13.33% being sold to outsiders.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
