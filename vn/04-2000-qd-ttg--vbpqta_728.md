# Decision 04/2000/QD-TTg

Decision No. 04/2000/QD-TTg of January 5, 2000 on the establishment of Cuu Long People-Founded University
To establish Cuu Long People-Founded University, which is a tertiary training establishment subject to the State management by the Ministry of Education and Training. It has the legal person status, its own seal and bank accounts. It is headquartered in Vinh Long province.
The system of diplomas and certificates issued by Cuu Long People-Founded University belongs to the national system of diplomas and certificates.
Cuu Long People-Founded University shall operate under the law, the Regulation on people-founded universities promulgated by the Minister of Education and Training, and its statute approved by the Minister of Education and Training.- (Summary)
