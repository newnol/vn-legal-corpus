# Quyết định 59/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 59/2003/QD-TTg of April 17, 2003 approving the master plan for reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Yen Bai province in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises with 100% of their charter capital being held by the State: 7 public-utility enterprises and 10 business enterprises to be kept intact.
Enterprise to be equitized, with the State holding at least 51% of their charter capital when shares are sold for the first time: 1 in 2003.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding shares at low levels or holding no shares: 7 in 2003, 2 in 2004 and 3 in 2005.
Enterprise to be assigned or sold: 1 in 2003.
Enterprise to be dissolved: 1 in 2003.- (Summary)
