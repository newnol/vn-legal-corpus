# Circular 299/2003/TT-BQP

CIRCULAR No. 299/2003/TT-BQP of December 24, 2003 guiding the application of the convalescence leave and health restoration regimes to armymen prescribed in the Government's Decree No. 89/2003/ND-CP of August 5, 2003
Subjects entitled to convalescence leave and health restoration regimes prescribed in Clause 3, Article 1 of the Government's Decree No. 89/2003/ND-CP include officers, professional armymen as well as non-commissioned officers and soldiers.
This Circular also prescribes the conditions, duration, and spending levels, for enjoyment of the above-said regimes.
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, annulling those regulations on convalescence leave and health restoration regimes contrary to this Circular.- (Summary)
