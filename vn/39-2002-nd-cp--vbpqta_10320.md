# Decree 39/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 39/2002/ND-CP of April 8, 2002 establishing wards of Viet Tri city and renaming Song Thao district Cam Khe district, Phu Tho province
To establish Ben Got ward of Viet Tri provincial city with a land area of 256.10 ha and a population of 5,732.
To establish Duu Lau ward with a land area of 631 ha and a population of 7,994.
To rename Song Thao district Cam Khe district.- (Summary)
