# Nghị định 84/2001/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 84/2001/ND-CP of November 14, 2001 on the establishment of communes and wards in Go Quao, An Minh, Tan Hiep and Giong Rieng rural districts and Rach Gia provincial capital, Kien Giang province
To establish Vinh Thang commune of Go Quao rural district with a natural land area of 3,383 hectares and a population of 10,325.
To establish Van Khanh Dong commune of An Minh rural district with a natural land area of 4,001 hectares and a population of 7,169.
To establish Van Khanh Tay commune of An Minh rural district with a natural land area of 4,641 hectares and a population of 6,339.
To establish Tan Thanh commune of Tan Hiep rural district with a natural land area of 3,386.31 hectares and a population of 10,443.
To establish Ngoc Thanh commune of Giong Rieng rural district with a natural land area of 2,397 hectares and a population of 8,039.
To establish Ngoc Thuan commune of Giong Rieng rural district with a natural land area of 3,739 hectares and a population of 8,540.
To establish Ban Thach commune of Giong Rieng rural district with a natural land area of 2,106.4 hectares and a population of 9,663.
To establish Vinh Quang ward of Rach Gia provincial capital with a natural land area of 1,064.8 hectares and a population of 18,449.
To establish Vinh Loi ward of Rach Gia provincial capital with a natural land area of 398.4 hectares and a population of 7,659.- (Summary)
