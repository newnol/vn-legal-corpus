# Quyết định 08/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 08/2003/QD-TTg of January 10, 2003 establishing Dong Thap Teachers’ Training University
Dong Thap Teachers’ Training University is set up on the basis of Dong Thap Teachers’ Training College. It is attached to the Ministry of Education and Training, has legal person status, seal and accounts. Its office is headquartered in Cao Lanh provincial capital, Dong Thap province.
It is tasked to:
- Train pre-school education teachers and general-education teachers of all educational levels for Dong Thap province and the Mekong river delta region;
- Provide professional fostering and re-training for the contingent of teachers and education administrators;
- Conduct scientific research and apply scientific advances in service of socio-economic development.- (Summary)
