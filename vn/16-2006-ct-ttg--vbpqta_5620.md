# Directive 16/2006/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On urgent measures to prevent and control cattle foot-and-mouth disease
The Prime Minister requests ministries, branches and localities to immediately perform several tasks in order to prevent and combat cattle foot-and-mouth disease.- (Summary)
