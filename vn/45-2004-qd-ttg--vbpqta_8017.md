# Quyết định 45/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 45/2004/QD-TTg of March 25, 2004 assigning the doctoral training task to the Development Strategy Institute
The Prime Minister issued this Decision to assign the doctoral training task to the Develop-ment Strategy Institute. The Education and Training Ministry shall consider and prescribe its specific training majors.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
