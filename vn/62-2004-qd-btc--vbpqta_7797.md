# Quyết định 62/2004/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 62/2004/QD-BTC of July 16, 2004 amending and supplementing a number of articles of the Regulation on issuance of traditional-type national construction lotteries, promulgated together with the Finance Minister's Decision No. 143/2003/QD-BTC of September 9, 2003
This Decision amends and supplements Articles 7, 8, 11 and 16 of the Regulation on issuance of traditional-type national construction lotteries, promulgated together with the Finance Minister's Decision No. 143/2003/QD-BTC of September 9, 2003; and takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
