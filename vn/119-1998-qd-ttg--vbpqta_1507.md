# Quyết định 119/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.119/1998/QD-TTg of July 9, 1998 on the establishment of the Dong Nai Decorative Fine Arts College.
To establish the Dong Nai Decorative Fine Arts College on the basis of the Decorative Fine Arts Intermediate School in Dong Nai province.
The Dong Nai Decorative Fine Arts College is placed under the Ministry of Culture and Information and subject to the State management by the Ministry of Education and Training over its educational and training activities. The college is tasked to train and foster professional personnel and managerial officials of college and lower levels for various decorative fine arts organizations.- (Summary)
