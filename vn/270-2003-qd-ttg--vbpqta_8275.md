# Decision 270/2003/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 270/2003/QD-TTg of December 24, 2003 prescribing the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the National Reserve Department under the Ministry of Finance
Under this Decision, the National Reserve Department is an organization under the Ministry of Finance, which shall perform the State management over national reserves and directly manages a number of national reserve commodities according to law provisions.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
