# Decree 98/1998/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 98/1998/ND-CP of November 19, 1998 on the establishment of Phuoc Long ward of Nha Trang city, and Ninh Son commune of Ninh Hoa district, Khanh Hoa province
To establish Phuoc Long ward of Nha Trang city on the basis of a land area of 427 ha and a population of 14,391 of Phuoc Hai ward.
To establish Ninh Son commune of Ninh Hoa district on the basis of a land area of 17,175 ha and a population of 6,267 of Ninh An commune.- (Summary)
