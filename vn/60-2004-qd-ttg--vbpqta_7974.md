# Quyết định 60/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 60/2004/QD-TTg of April 9, 2004 renaming Hai Phong Pedagogic University Hai Phong University
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Hai Phong Pedagogic University is renamed Hai Phong University.
Hai Phong University is a tertiary training establishment under the People's Committee of Hai Phong city and subject to the State management over education and training by the Ministry of Education and Training. The University has the legal person status, its own seal and bank accounts.
It is tasked to train human resources of tertiary or lower levels and conduct scientific research in service of socio-economic development.- (Summary)
