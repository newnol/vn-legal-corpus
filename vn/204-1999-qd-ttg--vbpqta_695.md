# Quyết định 204/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 204/1999/QD-TTg of October 18, 1999 on the setting up of the Steering Board for the construction of Chu Lai open economic zone
The Steering Board for the construction of Chu Lai open economic zone shall be headed by Deputy Prime Minister Nguyen Tan Dung. The Board shall also include the Minister of Planning and Investment, the Minister of Finance, the Minister-Head of the Government Commission for Organization and Personnel, the Minister of Justice, the Minister of Construction, the General Director of Customs, the Head of the Management Board of Vietnamese Industrial Parks, the President of the People�s Committee of Quang Nam province, the Secretary of the Party Committee of Quang Nam province and a Deputy Head of the Party Central Committee�s Economic Commission, as its members.
The Steering Board for the construction of Chu Lai open economic zone shall have the following tasks and powers:
- To direct the elaboration of the feasibility study for the construction of Chu Lai open economic zone.
- On the basis of the approved study, to direct the elaboration of overall planning and detailed planning; to assign tasks to and urge the concerned ministries, branches and localities in formulating administrative-economic institutions as well as investment promotion mechanisms and policies to be applicable to Chu Lai open economic zone, then submitting them to the competent agencies for consideration and decision; to elaborate the management regulation for Chu Lai open economic zone, then submit it to the Prime Minister for promulgation signing.
- To direct, urge and inspect the performance of the functions, tasks and powers of the Board in preparation for the execution of Chu Lai open economic zone project.
- The Steering Board shall have to regularly report on the tempo of work performance and propose to the Prime Minister for timely solution matters arising beyond the competence of the concerned ministries and branches and the Steering Board.
- To study the construction of special economic zones suitable to Vietnam�s socio-economic development conditions.- (Summary)
