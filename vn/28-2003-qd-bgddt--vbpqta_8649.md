# Quyết định 28/2003/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION No. 28/2003/QD-BGDDT of June 23, 2003 promulgating the Rules for the VIth national Phu Dong Tournament - 2004
Phu Dong Tournament is a physical training and sports meet of general-education pupils at all educational levels and grades.
Issued together with this Decision are the Rules for 15 competition events at the VIth national Phu Dong Tournament - 2004, including track and field, gymnastics, badminton, shuttle cock-kicking, chess, table tennis, football, volleyball, basketball, swimming, all-in wrestling, Karatedo, Taekwondo, Judo and crossbow archery.- (Summary)
