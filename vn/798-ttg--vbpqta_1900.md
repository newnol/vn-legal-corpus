# Decision 798/TTg

DECISION No.798-TTg of September 24, 1997 on the establishment of Ho Chi Minh City people-funded technical college.
Ho Chi Minh City people-funded technical college is a college training institution subject to the State management by the Ministry of Education an Training. Its system of academic diplomas and certificates belongs to the national system of academic diplomas and certificates.- (Summary)
