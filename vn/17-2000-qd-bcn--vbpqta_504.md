# Quyết định 17/2000/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 17/2000/QD-BCN of April 3, 2000 on transferring Noi Dia Region Coal Prophylactic Medicine Center (including Dong Anh-based hospital) which is an attached unit of Vietnam Coal Corporation’s Coal Industry Labor Health Center to the management of the People’s Committee of Hanoi City
To transfer in status quo Noi Dia Region Coal Prophylactic Medicine Center, including the hospital located in Dong Anh district, Hanoi, which is an attached unit of Vietnam Coal Corporation’s Coal Industry Labor Health Center to the management of the People’s Committee of Hanoi City.
This Decision takes effect as from May 1 st , 2000.- (Summary)
