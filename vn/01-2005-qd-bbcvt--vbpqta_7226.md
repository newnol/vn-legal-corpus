# Quyết định 01/2005/QĐ-BBCVT

DECISION No. 01/2005/QD-BBCVT of January 11, 2005 annulling the compulsory-application validity of branch standards
This Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, annuls the compulsory-application validity of 17 branch standards listed therein and branch standards, procedures and processes which are issued by the General Department of Post (now the Ministry of Post and Telematics) before 1994 but remain valid.- (Summary)
