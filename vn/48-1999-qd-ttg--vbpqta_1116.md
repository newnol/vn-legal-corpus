# Decision 48/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.48/1999/QD-TTg of March 20, 1999 on the establishment of Industrial College IV
To establish Industrial College IV on the basis of Industrial Techniques Intermediate School IV.
The Industrial College IV shall be placed under the Ministry of Industry and subject to State management by the Ministry of Education and Training for its education and training. The college is tasked to train and foster technicians with college and lower levels.- (Summary)
