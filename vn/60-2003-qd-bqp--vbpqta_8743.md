# Quyết định 60/2003/QĐ-BQP

DECISION No. 60/2003/QD-BQP of May 23, 2003 on seconding army officers to the Education and Training Services of key provinces and centrally-run cities
To second army officers to the Education and Training Services of key provinces and centrally-run cities in order to assist the directors of these Services in carrying out the work of defense and defense education in their localities.- (Summary)
