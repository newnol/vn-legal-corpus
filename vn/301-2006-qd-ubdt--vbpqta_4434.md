# Decision 301/2006/QĐ-UBDT

DECISION
Recognizing three ethnic minority and mountainous regions according to their development levels
The three ethnic minority and mountainous regions recognized under this Decision shall serve as a basis for realization of socio-economic development investment policies suitable to the development level of each region.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
