# Nghị quyết 62/2007/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
On the Government’s December 2007 regular meeting
On December 23-24, 2007, the Government held its December regular meeting where it heard a report reviewing the Government’s direction and administration work in 2007 and the work program for 2008; a report on the socio-economic situation in December and the whole year 2007 and solutions for the first quarter of 2008; a report on the domestic market situation in December and the whole year 2007; a report on the price administration work in 2007 and orientations for 2008; a report on the administrative reforms in 2007 and tasks for 2008; and a report on the anti-corruption work in 2007.
The Government also heard a scheme on measures to reduce traffic congestions in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City; the Bill on Public Duties and the Bill on Health Insurance.- (Summary)
