# Decision 77/1998/QD-TTg

DECISION No.77/1998/QD-TTg of April 6, 1998 on the admission of the Ca Mau Agricultural Product and Foodstuff Export and Import Company as a member of the Southern Food Corporation.
To admit the Ca Mau Agricultural Product and Foodstuff Export and Import Company as a member of the Southern Food Corporation. The president of the People's Committee of Ca Mau province and the Managing Board and the General Director of the Southern Food Corporation shall have fulfill their respective tasks of hand-over and take-over of the Ca Mau Agricultural Product and Foodstuff Export and Import Company.- (Summary)
