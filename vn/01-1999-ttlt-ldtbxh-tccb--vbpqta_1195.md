# Joint circular 01/1999/TTLT/LDTBXH-TCCB

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 01/1999/TTLT-LDTBXH-TCCB of January 6, 1999 providing guidance on the job training management organizations at the central and local levels
This Circular provides detailed guidance on functions, tasks and staff of organizations engaged in the job training at the ministries, ministerial-level agencies, agencies attached to the Government, provinces and centrally-run cities, as well as rural and urban districts, provincial capitals and towns.- (Summary)
