# Nghị định 33/1998/NĐ-CP

DECREE No.33/1998/ND-CP of May 23, 1998 on the establishment of the General Department for Vocational Training directly under the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs.
To set up the General Department for Vocational Training under the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs to assist the Minister in performing the function of State management over the vocational training in the whole country.
The General Department's head office shall be located in Hanoi.- (Summary)
