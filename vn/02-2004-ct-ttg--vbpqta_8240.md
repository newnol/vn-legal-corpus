# Chỉ thị 02/2004/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE No. 02/2004/CT-TTg of January 2, 2004 on directing the organization of the election of deputies to the People's Councils of all levels for the 2004-2009 tenure
The Prime Minister instructs relevant ministries, ministerial-level agencies and Government-attached agencies as well as People's Committees of all levels to expeditiously draw up plans for the performance of some tasks in order to successfully organize the election of deputies to the People's Councils of all levels for the 2004-2009 tenure.- (Summary)
