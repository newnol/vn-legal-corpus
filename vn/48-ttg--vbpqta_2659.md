# Decision 48/TTg

Decision No.48-TTg of January 24, 1997 of the Prime Minister to upgrade the Central External Economics School into the External Economics College attached to the Ministry of Trade and subject to the branch management by the Ministry of Education and Training.
This Decision takes effect from February 7, 1997.- (Summary)
