# Decree 82/2007/NĐ-CP

DECREE
Providing for the supply of information in service of formulation and administration of national monetary policies
This Decree defines organizations and individuals that supply information to the State Bank of Vietnam for the latter to make monetary balances of financial institutions, assess and forecast economic development as well as financial and monetary market trends in service of formulation and administration of national monetary policies.
It does not govern the supply of information by credit institutions to the State Bank.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
