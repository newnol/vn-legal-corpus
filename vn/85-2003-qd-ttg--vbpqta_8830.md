# Quyết định 85/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 85/2003/QD-TTg of May 6, 2003 on application of financial regime to the Radio Voice of Vietnam
This Decision prescribes the financial regime for activities of the Radio Voice of Vietnam in 2003.
The Radio Voice of Vietnam’s financial sources in 2003 include: the State budget allocations for its regular activities with VND 168,440 million, the retained revenues from non-business activities and financial supports from domestic and foreign organizations and individuals.
This Decision also specifies the spending contents.- (Summary)
