# Quyết định 19/2004/QĐ-BTS

DECISION No. 19/2004/QD-BTS of August 25, 2004 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Tonkin Gulf Aquatic Resource Exploitation and Protection Sub-Department
The Tonkin Gulf Aquatic Resource Exploitation and Protection Sub-Department, headquartered in Hai Phong city, is an agency performing the specialized State management over aquatic resource exploitation and protection falling within the competence of the Aquatic Resource Exploitation and Protection Department in sea areas of the coastal provinces from Quang Ninh to Thua Thien - Hue according to law provisions.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
