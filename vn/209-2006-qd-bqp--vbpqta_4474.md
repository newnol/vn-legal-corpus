# Decision 209/2006/QĐ-BQP

DECISION
Promulgating the Operation Regulation of Military Commands of agencies and organizations
This Decision defines the operation principles; functions, tasks and powers; working regime and relations of Military Commands of state agencies, non-business units, economic organizations, political organizations and socio-political organizations.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
