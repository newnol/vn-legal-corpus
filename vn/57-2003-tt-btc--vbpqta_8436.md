# Thông tư 57/2003/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 57/2003/TT-BTC of June 13, 2003 guiding the mechanism for payment of goods and services as debt repayment to the Russian Federation in replacement of the Finance Ministry's Circular No. 10/2001/TT-BTC of January 30, 2001
Based on the debt-repayment schedule, annually, the Russian Federation shall organize selection drives and notify the Vietnamese party of the names of the Russian organizations authorized to import goods or services from Vietnam in such year.
Within the framework of debt-repayment turnover already notified by the Russian Federation, the organizations authorized by the Russian Federation may freely select goods items and services as well as partners being Vietnamese enterprises (of all economic sectors) for signing debt-repayment goods or services export contracts in accordance with international trade practices as well as laws of Vietnam and the Russian Federation.
The authorized Russian organizations may re-export or authorize Vietnamese partners to re-export the purchased goods to the third country.
All Vietnamese enterprises of various economic sectors may participate in exporting goods or providing services like common trade transactions in order to repay debts to the Russian Federation. Such enterprises shall be entitled to export incentive regime as for goods or services exported by common trade mode.- (Summary)
