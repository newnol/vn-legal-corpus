# Quyết định 33/2005/QĐ-BXD

DECISION
Promulgating the "Norm on cost estimates for work construction ' Installation part"
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO." This "Norm on cost estimates for work construction ' Installation part" shall apply nationwide and be used to calculate construction unit costs, which shall be served as a basis for the formulation of cost estimates and total cost estimates of construction works and the management of construction investment expenses.- (Summary)
