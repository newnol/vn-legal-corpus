# Decision 274/TTg

Decision No.274-TTg of April 28, 1997 on the establishment of the State Steering Committee for the Off-Shore Fishing Program.
The State Steering Committee for the Off-Shore Fishing Program shall be placed under the direction of Deputy Prime Minister Tran Duc Luong. It shall be composed of the Minister of Aquaproducts as its Chairman, a Vice-Minister of Planning and Investment as its Vice-Chairman, and a Vice Minister of Finance, a Vice Minister of Communication and Transport and the Deputy Governor of the State Bank of Vietnam as its members. The Ministry of Aquaproducts shall act as its standing body
The Committee shall have to assist the Prime Minister in directing and making decisions on issues that are beyond the powers of the Ministries; direct and supervise the coordination among branches and localities in organizing the implementation of the off-shore fishing program.- (Summary)
