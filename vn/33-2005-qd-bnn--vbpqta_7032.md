# Quyết định 33/2005/QĐ-BNN

DECISION
Promulgating the list of veterinary drugs and raw materials for production of veterinary drugs permitted for circulation till December 31, 2005
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO" and replaces Decision No. 17/2004/QD-BNN of May 14, 2004, of the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development, promulgating the 2004 list of veterinary drugs.- (Summary)
