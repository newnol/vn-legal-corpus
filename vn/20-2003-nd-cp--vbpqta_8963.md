# Nghị định 20/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 20/2003/ND-CP of March 10, 2003 on the adjustment of administrative boundary between Ba Lien commune of Ba To rural district and Pho Phong commune of Duc Pho rural district, Quang Ngai province
To transfer a part of the natural area of Pho Phong commune, Duc Pho rural district, to Ba Lien commune, Ba To rural district.
After the adjustment of administrative boundary, Ba To rural district shall have a natural land area of 113,254 ha and a population of 46.449 and its Ba Lien commune, a natural land area of 4,112 ha and a population of 935; Duc Pho rural district shall have a natural land area of 37,167 ha and a population of 147,427 and its Pho Phong commune, a natural land area of 5,407 ha and a population of 9,177.- (Summary)
