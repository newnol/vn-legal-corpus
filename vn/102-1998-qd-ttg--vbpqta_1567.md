# Quyết định 102/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.102/1998/QD-TTg of June 2, 1998 on the setting up of the Management Board of the Techno-Economic Program for Materials Technology.
To set up the Management Board of the Techno-Economic Program for Materials Technology to assist the Prime Minister in implementing the Resolution No.88-CP of December 31, 1996 of the Government on the Development of Materials Science and Technology in Vietnam till the Year 2010.
The Minister of Industry shall act as the Chairman of the Management Board of the Techno-Economic Program for Materials Technology.
The Management Board shall have the following tasks and powers:
- To draw up the strategy for the development of the materials science and technology, the phased long-term plan and annual plans for the implementation of the Techno-Economic Program for Materials Technology and submit them to the Prime Minister for approval;
- To organize and direct the implementation of the Techno-Economic Program for Materials Technology; to coordinate with functional agencies in evaluating, approving and allocating operating funds for the projects under the Program in accordance with the current regulations, and at the same time, to monitor, guide and inspect the execution of such projects;
- To coordinate with the concerned agencies in working out policies, regimes and measures for the implementation of the Techno-Economic Program for Materials Technology, then submit them to the Prime Minister for promulgation; to promulgate documents guiding and inspecting the implementation of the Government's regulations;
- To make periodical reports on the implementation of the Program and coordinate with the concerned agencies in evaluating and inspecting the execution of the projects under the Program.
This Decision replaces Decision No.443-TTg of June 20, 1997 of the Prime Minister on the setting up of the Management Board of the National Program for Materials Technology.- (Summary)
