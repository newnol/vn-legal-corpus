# Directive 22/2007/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On the development of private enterprises
The Prime Minister instructs ministers, heads of ministerial-level and government-attached agencies and provincial/municipal People’s Committee presidents to perform several tasks in order boost the development of private enterprises.- (Summary)
