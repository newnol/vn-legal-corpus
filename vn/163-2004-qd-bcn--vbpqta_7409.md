# Quyết định 163/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 163/2004/QD-BCN of December 8, 2004 transforming An Lac Footwear Company into An Lac Footwear Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, An Lac Footwear Joint-Stock Company (ALSIMEX) is headquartered at No. 92/4 National Road 1, An Lac township, Binh Chanh district, Ho Chi Minh city.
Its charter capital is VND 77.5 billion, 51% of which to be held by the State, 36.5% sold to its laborers and 12.5% to outsiders.- (Summary)
