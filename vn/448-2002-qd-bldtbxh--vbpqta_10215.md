# Decision 448/2002/QĐ-BLĐTBXH

DECISION No. 448/2002/QD-BLDTBXH of April 9, 2002 promulgating the Regulation on examination, test and graduation recognition for long-term full-time job-trainees
To issue together with this Decision the Regulation on examination, test and graduation recognition for long-term full-time job-trainees at job-training schools, universities, colleges and intermediate vocational schools, which have been granted certificates of registration for long-term job training.
The Regulation prescribes the regimes of regular and periodical tests; the organization of examinations and tests at semester-ends; the classification of trainees; the organization of graduation examination and consideration for graduation recognition.- (Summary)
