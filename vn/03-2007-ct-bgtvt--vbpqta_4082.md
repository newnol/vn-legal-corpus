# Directive 03/2007/CT-BGTVT

DIRECTIVE
Stepping up the reorganization, renewal, development, and raising the operation efficiency of, transport enterprises in 2007
The Minister of Transport instructs concerned departments and units to perform several tasks in order to speed up the reorganization, renewal, development, and raising the operation efficiency of, transport enterprises through equitization, sale or bankruptcy of small-sized enterprises suffering from losses.- (Summary)
