# Quyết định 111/2001/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 111/2001/QD-BNN of November 23, 2001 establishing the Steering Committee for Forest Fire Prevention and Fighting and promulgating the Regulation on operation of the Steering Committee
To establish the Steering Committee for Forest Fire Prevention and Fighting under the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development with a Vice Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development, who is in charge of forestry affairs, as its head.
Issued together with this Decision is the Regulation on operation of the above-said Steering Committee.- (Summary)
