# Quyết định 133/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 133/2004/QD-BCN of November 16, 2004 on transforming Vietnam Ceramic and Glass Company into Vietnam Ceramic and Glass Company Limited
Under this Decision, Vietnam Ceramic and Glass Company Limited, VINACEGLASS, shall have a charter capital of VND 20,333,581,744.
Its head-office is located at No. 20-24 Nguyen Cong Tru, Nguyen Thai Binh ward, district 1, Ho Chi Minh City.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
