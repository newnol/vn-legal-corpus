DECREE No. 12/2001/ND-CP OF MARCH 27, 2001 REORGANIZING A NUMBER OF SPECIALIZED AGENCIES UNDER THE PEOPLEï؟½S COMMITTEES OF THE PROVINCES AND CENTRALLY-RUN CITIES AND THE PEOPLEï؟½S COMMITTEES OF THE DISTRICTS, PROVINCIAL CAPITALS AND TOWNS

DECREES:
Article 1.-
1. To establish the Committees for Population, Family and Children under the Peopleï؟½s Committees of the provinces and centrally-run cities on the basis of merging the Committees for Population and Family Planning and the Committees for Child Protection and Care.
2. To rename the Offices of the provincial/municipal Peopleï؟½s Committees, established under Decree No. 156/HDBT of December 17, 1981, the Offices of the Peopleï؟½s Councils and Peopleï؟½s Committees. 4. To transfer the task of State management over the public health in localities from the Health Centers of the districts, provincial capital and towns to the Peopleï؟½s Committees of the same level.
Article 2.-
1. This Decision takes effect after its signing.
2. The Minister-Director of Government Commission for Organization and Personnel shall assume the prime responsibility and coordinate with the concerned agencies in guiding the implementation of this Decree.
Article 3.- The ministers, the heads of the ministerial-level agencies and the heads of the agencies attached to the Government and the presidents of the Peopleï؟½s Committees of the provinces and centrally-run cities shall have to implement this Decree.
