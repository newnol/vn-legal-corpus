# Quyết định 78/2005/QĐ-BVHTT

DECISION
On the additional classification and renaming of a national relic
Under this Decision, the historical relic of Great Poet Nguyen Du memorial park in Tien Dien commune, Nghi Xuan district, Ha Tinh province, is classified as a national relic.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces the Culture Minister’s Decision No. 313/VH-QD of April 28, 1962.- (Summary)
