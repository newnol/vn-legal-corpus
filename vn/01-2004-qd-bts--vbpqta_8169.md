# Decision 01/2004/QD-BTS

DECISION No. 01/2004/QD-BTS of January 14, 2004 promulgating the branch-level standards
Promulgated together with this Decision are the following 4 branch-level standards:
- 28 TCN 102: 2004: Compound pellet feed for tiger shrimp (Penaeus monodon);
- 28 TCN 187: 2004: Compound pellet feed for giant freshwater prawn (Macrobrachium rosenbergii);
- 28 TCN 188: 2004: Compound pellet feed for Pangasianodon hypophthalmus and Pangasius bocourti;
- 28 TCN 189: 2004: Compound pellet feed for Oreochromiss sp.
The above-said standards must be applied by establishments producing and/or trading in feed for shrimp and fish, and take effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
