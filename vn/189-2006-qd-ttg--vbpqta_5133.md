# Decision 189/2006/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Approving the Agreement on Economic, Commercial, Cultural, Scientific and Technological Cooperation between the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the Government of the Federal Republic of Nigeria
The Prime Minister approves the Agreement, which was concluded on November 24, 2005, in Hanoi, and assigns the Ministry of Foreign Affair to carry out diplomatic procedures for the approval of the Agreement and publicize the Agreement according to current regulations.- (Summary)
