# Quyết định 88/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 88/2003/QD-BCN of May 14, 2003 approving the Charter on organization and operation of Vietnam Leather and Footwear Corporation
This Decision approves the Charter on organization and operation of Vietnam Leather and Footwear Corporation.
Vietnam Leather and Footwear Corporation, abbreviated to LEAPRODEXIM VIETNAM, is an independent cost-accounting State enterprise under the Industry Ministry, which is set up, invested and managed by the State in the capacity as its owner.
The company’s headquarter: 25 Ly Thuong Kiet street, Hoan Kiem district, Hanoi.
The company is engaged in the following production and business lines:
- Producing and trading in assorted leather articles, footwear, products processed from leather or leatherette as well as other raw materials and auxiliary materials;
- Exporting and importing assorted raw materials, auxiliary materials, supplies, chemicals, machinery, equipment, accessories, transport means and other commodities in service of domestic production, business and consumption;
- Conducting international cooperation in labor export, dealing in fairs and exhibitions, information and advertisement, and office lease.
- Acting as product purchase, sale and display agents for domestic or foreign economic organizations.
- Dealing in other commodities and services according to law provisions.- (Summary)
