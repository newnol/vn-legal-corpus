# Thông tư 25/2001/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 25/2001/TT-BTC of April 16, 2001 guiding the financial management applicable to the implementation of the regime of reward for outstanding achievements in performing socio-economic and defense tasks
Subjects of application of the reward regime prescribed in this Circular are all individuals and collectives that have been rewarded by the State President, Government, Prime Minister, ministries, ministerial-level agencies, agencies attached to the Government or People�s Committees of various levels for their outstanding achievements in performing socio-economic and defense tasks.
The Circular guides in detail on the reward levels; the deduction for establishment, use and final settlement of annual reward sources.- (Summary)
