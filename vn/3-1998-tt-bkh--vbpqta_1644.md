# Circular 3/1998/TT-BKH

CIRCULAR No.3/1998/TT-BKH of March 27, 1998 guiding the implementation of Decision No.52/1998/QD-TTg of March 3, 1998 of the Prime Minister on the implementation of the 1998 investment credit plan.
This Circular provides specific guidance for the provisions on the subjects to benefit from the 1998 investment credit plan; the loan interest rates; the debt payment deadlines; the mortgage for borrowing capital; the investment procedures; the assignment of plans and execution thereof.- (Summary)
