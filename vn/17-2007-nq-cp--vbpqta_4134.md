# Resolution 17/2007/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
On the Government’s February regular meeting
On March 2, 2007, the Government held its February regular meeting where it heard:
- The Minister-Director of the Government Office presenting a draft working report of the Government and the Prime Minister in the 2002-2007 term; the Planning and Investment Minister’s additional report on the performance of the 2006 socio-economic tasks and implementation of the 2007 socio-economic development plan; and the Finance Minister’s additional report on materialization of the 2006 state budget and implementation of the 2007 state budget tasks;
- The Minister of Natural Resources and Environment presenting a Prime Minister’s draft directive on redressing shortcomings and weaknesses in land use plannings and land use under investment projects;
- The Planning and Investment Minister’s report on socio-economic situation in February and the first two months of 2007; the Trade Minister’s report on trade situation in February; the Home Affairs Minister’s report on administrative reform results in February; and the Government Inspector General’s anti-corruption report.- (Summary)
