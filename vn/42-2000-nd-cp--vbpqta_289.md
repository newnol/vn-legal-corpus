# Decree 42/2000/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 42/2000/ND-CP of August 30, 2000 on the establishment of a township of Ninh Son district, Ninh Thuan province and renaming Tan Son commune as Luong Son commune
To establish Tan Son township (the capital of Ninh Son district) on the basis of land area of 1,764 ha and a population of 10,242 of Tan Son commune.
To rename Tan son commune as Luong Son commune.- (Summary)
