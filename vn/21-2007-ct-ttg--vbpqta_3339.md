# Chỉ thị 21/2007/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
Providing preferential loans for students of universities, colleges and job training schools
The Prime Minister instructs concerned ministries, branches and agencies to perform several tasks in order to create conditions for students meeting with difficulties to learn until graduation from universities, colleges and job training schools.- (Summary)
