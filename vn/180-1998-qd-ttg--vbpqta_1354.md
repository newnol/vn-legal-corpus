# Quyết định 180/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 180/1998/QD-TTg of September 21, 1998 on assigning the task of tertiary training to the officers' training schools of the army
To assign the task of tertiary training to the following officers' training schools under the Ministry of Defense:
- Land force officers' training school 1;
- Land force officers' training school 2;
- The army engineering officers' training school;
- The artillery officers' training school;
- The information technique commanding officers' training school;
- The tank-armor officers' training school;
- The chemical defense officers' training school;
- The special force officers' training school.
The academic certificates and diplomas of these schools belong to the national system of certificates and diplomas. The tertiary training targets, contents and programs of these schools shall be decided by the Minister of Defense after consulting the Minister of Education and Training.- (Summary)
