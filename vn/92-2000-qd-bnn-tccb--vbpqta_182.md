Decision No. 92/2000/QD-BNN-TCCB of August 23, 2000 approving the organization and operation Charter of the Consultant and Technology Transfer Company of Hanoi Water Resources University
To approve "the organization and operation Charter of the Consultant and Technology Transfer Company of Hanoi Water Resources University - CTC", including 8 chapters with 35 articles.
