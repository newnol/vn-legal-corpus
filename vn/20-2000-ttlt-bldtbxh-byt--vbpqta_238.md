# Joint circular 20/2000/TTLT/BLĐTBXH-BYT

THE MINISTRY OF LABOR, WAR INVALIDS AND SOCIAL AFFAIRS - THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH
JOINT-CIRCULAR No. 20/,2000/TTLT-BLDTBXH-BYT of September 20, 2000 guiding the re-evaluation of injuries for injured people and re-evaluation of working capacity for diseased soldiers
This Circular specifies subjects and dossiers for re-evaluation of injuries and illness for injured people (including war invalids, war invalid-allowance beneficiaries and those who are not war invalids) and diseased soldiers; the responsibility assignment and authorization for re-evaluation of injuries and illness to medical examination councils of the provinces and centrally-run cities; funding for the re-evaluation of injuries and illness.- (Summary)
