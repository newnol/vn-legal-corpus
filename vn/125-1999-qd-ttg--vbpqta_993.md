# Decision 125/1999/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 125/1999/QD-TTg of May 28, 1999 on the establishment of Agro-Forestrial College
To establish the Agro-Forestrial College on the basis of the Central Agro-Technical Intermediate School.
The Agro-Forestrial College is tasked to train and foster cadres with college or lower degrees; and conduct scientific research in service of socio-economic development.
The Agro-Forestrial College is attached to the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development and subject to the State management over its education and training by the Ministry of Education and Training.- (Summary)
