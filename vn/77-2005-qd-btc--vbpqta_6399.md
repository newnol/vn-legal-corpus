# Quyết định 77/2005/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
Promulgating 03 Vietnamese standards on price evaluation (drive 2)
Issued together with this Decision are three Vietnamese standards on price evaluation:
- TDGVN 02 – Non-market values served as a basis for evaluation of asset prices;
- TDGVN 05 – Procedures for evaluation of asset prices;
- TDGVN 06 – Economic principles controlling the evaluation of asset prices.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
