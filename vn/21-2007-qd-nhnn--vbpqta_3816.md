# Decision 21/2007/QĐ-NHNN

DECISION
Annulling the State Bank Governor’s Decision No. 987 / 2004/QD-NHNN of August 5, 2004, which promulgates the Financial Regulation applicable to the State Bank’s non-business units with revenues
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”
The State Bank’s non-business units with revenues shall apply the financial mechanism specified in the Government’s Decree No. 07/2006/ND-CP of January 10, 2006.- (Summary)
