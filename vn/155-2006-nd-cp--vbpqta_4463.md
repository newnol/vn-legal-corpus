# Decree 155/2006/NĐ-CP

DECREE
On the establishment of Ha Dong city of Ha Tay province
To establish Ha Dong city of Ha Tay province on the basis of the whole land area, population and independent administrative units of Ha Dong town.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
