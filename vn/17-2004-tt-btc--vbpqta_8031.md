# Thông tư 17/2004/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 17/2004/TT-BTC of March 12, 2004 amending and supplementing Circular No. 97/2000/TT-BTC of October 12, 2000 which guides the application of financial regimes to grassroots people's credit funds
This Circular amends Section 1 of Chapter I; Section 2, Part II of Chapter II; and Section 2, Part II of Chapter II. It annuls the stipulation: "A 5% deduction for setting up the job severance reserve fund for employees working in the fund. The balance of this fund shall not exceed 6 months' salary actually paid in the year of the grassroots people's credit fund"; and replaces the phrase "people's credit funds" in the above-said Circular No. 97/2000/TT-BTC with the phrase "grassroots people's credit funds".
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
