# Joint circular 272/TTLB

Inter-ministerial Circular No.272/TTLB of February 15, 1997 guiding the implementation of Decree No.71-CP of July 13, 1994 of the Government on national defense education in the system of political or administrative schools and the mass organizations
This Circular provides detailed guidance on the role of this education, the subject-matters; the aims, requirements, objects and period of study in defense education; the responsibility of the ministries, departments, branches and schools in guiding this education.- (Summary)
