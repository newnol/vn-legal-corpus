# Nghị định 16/1998/NĐ-CP

DECREE No.16/1998/ND-CP of March 18, 1998 on the establishment of a number of communes of Loc Ninh, Binh Long and Phuoc Long districts, Binh Phuoc province.
To establish Loc Thien, Loc Dien and Loc Thuan communes of Loc Ninh district.
To divide Loi Hung commune of Binh Long district into two communes of Tan Hung and Tan Loi.
To establish Phu Trung and Long Binh communes of Phuoc Long district.- (Summary)
