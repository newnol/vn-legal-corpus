# Quyết định 103/2004/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 103/2004/QD-BTC of December 28, 2004 prescribing the regime of collection, remittance, management and use of charges for testing medicines, medicine samples, medicine materials and cosmetics
This Decision applies to Vietnamese and foreign organizations and individuals that produce and/or trade in medicine materials, medicine samples, human-use preventive and curative medicines and/or cosmetics.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces Joint Circular No. 17-TT/LB of March 7, 1995 and Joint Circular No. 32/1999/TTLT/BTC-BYT of March 25, 1999 of the Finance Ministry and the Health Ministry.- (Summary)
