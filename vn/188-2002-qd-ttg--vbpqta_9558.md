# Decision 188/2002/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 188/QD-TTg of March 6, 2002 on setting up the Steering Committee for Ground Clearance for Hoa Lac Hi-Tech Park, Hanoi National University and Cultural and Tourist Village of Vietnamese Nationalities
To set up the Steering Committee for Ground Clearance for Hoa Lac Hi-Tech Park, Hanoi National University and Cultural and Tourist Village of Vietnamese Nationalities to assist the Prime Minister in directing all activities relating to the compensation, relocation and re-settlement of local people and ground clearance of the land area planned for the construction of Hoa Lac Hi-Tech Park, Hanoi National University and Cultural and Tourist Village of Vietnamese Nationalities.
The Steering Committee shall be composed of the president and vice president of the People�s Committee of Ha Tay province as its head and deputy head, and representatives of concerned ministries and branches as its members.- (Summary)
