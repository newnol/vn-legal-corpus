# Quyết định 117/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 117/2004/QD-BCN of November 1, 2004 transforming the Cotton and Fibered Plant Research Institute of Vietnam Cotton Company into Institute for Cotton Research and Development, a non-business unit of Vietnam Textile and Garment Corporation
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, the Institute for Cotton Research and Development (INCORD) is headquartered at km 14, National Road 27, Nha Ho hamlet, Nhon Son commune, Ninh Son district, Ninh Thuan province.- (Summary)
