# Quyết định 48/2003/QĐ-BVHTT

DECISION No. 48/2003/QD-BVHTT of August 21, 2003 promulgating the Regulation on management of personnel dossiers and internal affair dossiers in agencies and units under the Ministry of Culture and Information
The Regulation promulgated together with this Decision prescribes the management and use of:
- Personnel dossiers of officials, employees and laborers in administrative agencies, non-business units and State enterprises under the Ministry of Culture and Information.
- Internal affair dossiers of administrative agencies, non-business units and State enterprises under the Ministry of Culture and Information.- (Summary)
