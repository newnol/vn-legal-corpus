# Decision 883/2002/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 883/QD-TTg of October 3, 2002 appointing vice-presidents of the National Committee for AIDS prevention and combat and drug and prostitution prevention and combat
To appoint the persons named below as vice-presidents of the National Committee for AIDS prevention and combat and drug and prostitution prevention and combat:
- Mr. Le Hong Anh, Minister of Public Security (in replacement of Mr. Le Minh Huong, former Minister of Public Security);
- Mrs. Tran Thi Trung Chien, Minister of Health (in replacement of Mr. Do Nguyen Phuong, former Minister of Health).- (Summary)
