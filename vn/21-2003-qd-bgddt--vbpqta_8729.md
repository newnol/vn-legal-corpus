# Quyết định 21/2003/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION No. 21/2003/QD-BGDDT of May 28, 2003 amending and supplementing the Regulation on test, examination, class promotion consideration and graduation recognition for formal intermediate vocational education, issued together with the Education and Training Minister’s Decision No. 29/2002/QD-BGDDT of May 14, 2002
This Decision amends and supplements the provisions on intermediate vocational graduation examination prescribed in Clause 1, Article 37 of the Regulation on test, examination, class promotion consideration and graduation recognition for formal intermediate vocational education.- (Summary)
