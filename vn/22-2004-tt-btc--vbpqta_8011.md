# Thông tư 22/2004/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 22/2004/TT-BTC of March 24, 2004 guiding the management and use of the funding for training and fostering of cooperatives' cadres in the 2004-2005 period
Under this Circular, cooperative cadres to be trained are divided into two groups:
- Group 1: Key cooperative cadres being managers, chief accountants and chief controllers, who have not yet been provided with basic knowledge on management and administration of economic activities of cooperatives.
- Group 2: Professional cadres of non-agricultural cooperatives.
The maximum duration of each short-term training course shall be 15 days.
The budget expenditure level for training of key cooperative cadres shall be VND 1,500,000/trainee, while the budget's support level for training of professional cadres of non-agricultural cooperatives shall be VND 350,000/trainee/15-day course.
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
