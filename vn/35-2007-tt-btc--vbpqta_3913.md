# Circular 35/2007/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR
Guiding the implementation of the Government’s Decree No. 137/2006/ND-CP of November 14, 2006, providing for the decentralization of the state management of state properties at administrative agencies and public non-business units and properties over which the state ownership has been established
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and annuls the Finance Ministry’s Decision No. 20/1999/QD-BTC of February 25, 1999, and Decision No. 55/2000/QD-BTC of April 19, 2000.- (Summary)
