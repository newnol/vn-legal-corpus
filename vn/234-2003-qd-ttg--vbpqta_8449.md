# Decision 234/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 234/2003/QD-TTg of November 13, 2003 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the National Institute of Public Administration
The National Institute of Public Administration is a non-business organization under the Ministry of Home Affairs and subject to the State management by the Minister of Home Affairs. It is a national center, enjoys autonomy and takes self-responsibility for its organizational apparatus, payroll and finance under law provisions; functions to train and foster the contingent of State officials and employees, administrative public-employee titles of all levels, grassroots officials and public employees, reserve public employees, specialized administrative and State management lecturers and researchers; conduct administrative scientific research and advise the Government on the administrative and State management domain.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
