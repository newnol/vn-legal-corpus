# Nghị định 74-CP

Decree No.74-CP of November 22, 1996 of the Government on the establishment of Thanh Xuan and Cau Giay inner districts and the establishment and renaming of a number of wards of Hanoi City:
- To establish Thanh Xuan district, including 11 attached administrative units viz Thanh Xuan, Thuong Dinh, Kim Giang, Phuong Liet, Thanh Xuan Nam, Thanh Xuan Bac, Khuong Mai, Khuong Trung, Khuong Dinh, Ha Dinh and Nhan Chinh wards
- To establish Cau Giay district, including 7 attached administrative units viz. Quan Hoa, Nghia Do, Nghia Tan, Mai Dich, Dich Vong, Yen Hoa and Trung Hoa wards
- To rename Cau Giay ward of Ba Dinh district into Ngoc Khanh ward
- To rename Nguyen Trai ward of Dong Da district into Nga Tu So ward
- To rename Thanh Xuan ward of Thanh Xuan district into Thanh Xuan Trung.- (Summary)
