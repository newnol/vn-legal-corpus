# Decision 50/2002/QD-BNN

DECISION No. 50/2002/QD-BNN of June 12, 2002 promulgating pharmaceutical standards used in veterinary
To promulgate the following standards:
- 10 TCN 535-2002: Technical standard of Sodium Bicarbonate;
- 10 TCN 536-2002: Technical standard of Nitroxynil 25% injection medicine;
- 10 TCN 537-2002: Technical standard of Chloramine B;
- 10 TCN 538-2002: Technical standard of Chloramine T;
- 10 TCN 539-2002: Technical standard of Flumequine;
- 10 TCN 540-2002: Technical standard of Flumequine 20% oral solution;
- 10 TCN 541-2002: Technical standard of Amprolium chloral hydrate 10% and 20% powder medicine;
- 10 TCN 542-2002: Technical standard of Oxalat Iron Tablet 0.05 g;
- 10 TCN 543-2002: Technical standard of Oxalat Iron;
- 10 TCN 544-2002: Technical standard of Amprolium chloral hydrate.- (Summary)
