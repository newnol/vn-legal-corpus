# Joint circular 06/2000/TTLT/BLDTBXH-TWDTNCSHCM

Joint Circular No. 06/2000/TTLT-BLDTBXH-TWDTNCSHCM of March 21, 2000 supplementing Joint Circular No. 16/1999/TTLT-BLDTBXH-TWDTNCSHCM of July 6, 1999 guiding the examination of invalidity of youth volunteers, who joined and got wounded during the resistance war and are managed by the communication and transport branch
This Joint Circular additionally guides and amends Point 2.1, Section 2, Part II of Joint Circular No.16/1999/TTLT of July 6, 1999.- (Summary)
