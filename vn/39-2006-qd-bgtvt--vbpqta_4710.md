# Decision 39/2006/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION
Supplementing the program for training crewmembers and operators of means of inland waterway transport
This Decision promulgates the program for training crewmembers and operators of means of inland waterway transport to supplement the program promulgated together with Decision No. 37/2004/QD-BGTVT of December 24, 2004.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
