# Quyết định 25/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 25/2003/QD-TTg of February 17, 2003 adjusting the selling prices of petrol and oil nationwide
The maximum selling prices (value added tax included) of some types of petrol and oil shall be as follows:
- Retail price of non-lead petrol RON 92: VND 5,600/liter.
- Retail price of non-lead petrol RON 90: VND 5,400/liter.
- Retail price of non-lead petrol RON 83: VND 5,200/liter.
- Retail price of diesel oil 0.5%S: VND 4,400/liter.
- Retail price of kerosene: VND 4,300/liter.
- Retail price of fuel oil: VND 3,200/liter.
This Decision takes effect as from February 18, 2003.- (Summary)
