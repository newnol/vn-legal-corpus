This Decree�s provisions on arm badges, rank badges, insignia and formal dress apply to officers, career military personnel, non-commissioned officers and soldiers on active service, mobilized reserve force as well as demobilized officers and career military personnel, who are entitled to wear them according to the Defense Ministry�s regulations.
