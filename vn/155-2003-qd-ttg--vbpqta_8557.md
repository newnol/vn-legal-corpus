# Decision 155/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 155/2003/QD-TTg of July 30, 2003 amending and supplementing a number of specific allowance regimes for officials and employees of the health service
This Decision amends and supplements a number of specific allowance regimes for officials and employees of the health service: 24/24 hour-duty allowance; anti-epidemics allowance; and surgery and operation allowance.- (Summary)
