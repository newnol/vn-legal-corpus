# Quyết định 73/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 73/2004/QD-BCN of August 6, 2004 setting up Thanh Hoa Paper Raw Material Company
Thanh Hoa Paper Raw Material Company, an independent cost-accounting company under Vietnam Paper Corporation, is a State enterprise, having the legal person status, own seal and account and operating according to its charter, the Enterprise Law and relevant law provisions.
Its charter capital is VND 18,629,942,491.
Its headquarter is located in Ngoc Lac district township, Ngoc Lac rural district, Thanh Hoa province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
