# Resolution 08/2007/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
Approving the adjusted land use planning up to 2010 and the five-year (2006-2010) land use plan of Long An province
Based on the approved criteria, the People’s Committee of Long An province shall organize the implementation of the land use planning and plan in association with socio-economic development objectives as well as defense, security and eco-environmental protection tasks.- (Summary)
