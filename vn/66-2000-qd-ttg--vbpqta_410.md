# Quyết định 66/2000/QĐ-TTg

THE PRIME MINISTER
DECISION No. 66/2000/QD-TTg of June 13, 2000 on a number of policies and financial mechanism for the implementation of the program on canal solidification
The State encourages localities to mobilize all resources and create favorable mechanism for accelerating the implementation tempo of irrigation canal solidification. Capital shall be used with priority given to the implementation of new constructions or investment projects in areas with water shortage, areas irrigated by electric pumping stations, reservoirs, spillways, embanked water canals which strongly absorb water, canals running across bad soil prone to slide or fill-ups; canals running by urban areas, villages or hamlets.
The decision spells out the procedures for the elaboration and approval of the investment projects for canal solidification; the investment capital and credit capital for the implementation of canal solidification program.- (Summary)
