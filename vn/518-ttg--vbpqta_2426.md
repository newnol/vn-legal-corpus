# Decision 518/TTg

Decision No.518-TTg of July 11, 1997 on the establishment of Hong Bang people-funded university.
Hong Bang university is a tertiary education institution and subject to the State management by the Ministry of Education and Training. It is located in Ho Chi Minh City.
The diploma system of the university pertains to the national diploma system.-
