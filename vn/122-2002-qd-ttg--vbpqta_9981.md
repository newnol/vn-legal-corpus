# Quyết định 122/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No 122/2002/QD-TTg of September 19, 2002 transferring the Government Pricing Committee to the Ministry of Finance
To transfer the Government Pricing Committee to the Ministry of Finance. To assign the Minister of Finance to manage this Committee.
This Decision takes effect after its signing.- (Summary)
