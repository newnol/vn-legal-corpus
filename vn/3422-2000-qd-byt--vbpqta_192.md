# Decision 3422/2000/QD-BYT

DECISION No. 3422/2000/QD-BYT of October 2, 2000 promulgating the Regulation on consideration of the conferment of Ton That Tung Scientific Award
Ton That Tung Scientific Award is a form of commendation of the Health service in acknowledgement of the outstanding achievements made by individuals or collectives in the activities of science, technology and environment research and development, which contribute to the cause of surgical development. Ton That Tung Scientific Award shall be considered every year and conferred on the death anniversary of Professor Ton That Tung (May 7 th every year).
This Regulation also specifies the subjects, criteria, procedures and dossier for award conferment consideration as well as the interests of individuals and collectives to be conferred this award.-
