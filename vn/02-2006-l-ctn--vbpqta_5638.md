# Order 02/2006/L-CTN

ORDER
Promulgating the National Assembly Standing Committee’s Resolution on termination of effect of the Ordinance on Public-Utility Labor Obligations
According to the Resolution promulgated under this Order, the Ordinance on Public-Utility Labor Obligations shall cease to be effective as from January 1, 2007.- (Summary)
