# Nghị định 17/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 17/2004/ND-CP of January 9, 2004 on the establishment of wards of Nam Dinh city, Nam Dinh province
To establish Loc Vuong ward on the basis of a land area of 420.07 ha and a population of 7,962 of Loc Vuong commune.
To establish Loc Ha ward on the basis of a land area of 349.50 ha and a population of 6,931 of Loc Ha commune.
To establish Thong Nhat ward on the basis of the remaining land area of 36.53 ha and the population of 1,200 of Loc Vuong commune; the remaining land area of 0.60 ha and the population of 221 of Loc Ha commune; a land area of 13.70 ha and a population of 1,464 of Quang Trung ward; and a land area of 18 ha and a population of 4,193 of Vi Hoang ward.
Thong Nhat ward shall have a land area of 68.83 ha and a population of 7,078.
To establish Cua Nam ward on the basis of a land area of 127.60 ha and a population of 4,828 of Nam Phong commune; and a land area of 50 ha and a population of 1,300 of Nam Van commune.
Cua Nam ward shall have a land area of 177.60 ha and a population of 6,128.
To establish Tran Quang Khai ward on the basis of a land area of 90.60 ha and a population of 8.489 of Nang Tinh ward.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
