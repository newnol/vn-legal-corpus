# Decision 164/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 164/QD-TTg of February 18, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under Vietnam Paper Corporation till 2005
Enterprises with the State holding 100% of their capital: 8 units.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding more than 50% of their charter capital: 2 in 2004 and 1 in 2005.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding under 50% of their charter capital: 3 in 2003 and 1 in 2005.
Enterprise to be merged into others: 1 in 2005.- (Summary)
