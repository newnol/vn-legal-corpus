# Joint circular 94/2006/TTLT-BVHTT-BNV-BTC 

JOINT CIRCULAR
Guiding the implementation of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 180/2006/QD-TTg of August 9, 2006, on occupation-based allowances for art performers of the culture and information service
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and annuls Circular No. 52/1999/TTLB-BTCCBCP-BTC-BVHTT of November 22, 1999, of the Government Commission for Organization and Personnel (now the Ministry of Home Affairs), the Ministry of Finance and the Ministry of Culture and Information.- (Summary)
