# Quyết định 26/2000/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 26/2000/QD-BCN of April 6, 2000 on the transfer of Electrical Engineering School No. 3 under Power Company No. 3 to the Vietnam Electricity Corporation
Electrical Engineering School No. 3 (abbreviated as EES3) is an establishment which trains and fosters cadres of electrical economic-technical intermediate degree or lower under the educational system of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam. The school is subject to the State management by the Ministry of Industry, the Ministry of Education and Training and the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs.
Head office: 42 Le Hong Phong street, Hoi An town, Quang Nam province.
The school’s training and fostering scale: from 1,800 to 3,500 students.- (Summary)
