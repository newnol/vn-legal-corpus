# Decree 76/1999/ND-CP

Decree No. 76/1999/ND-CP of August 28, 1999 on the establishment of Ben Cau district township, Tay Ninh province
To establish Ben Cau township,- the capital of Ben Cau district, Tay Ninh province, on the basis of a land area of 638 ha and a population of 5,555 of Loi Thuan commune.- (Summary)
