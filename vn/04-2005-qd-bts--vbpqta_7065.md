# Quyết định 04/2005/QĐ-BTS

DECISION No. 04/2005/QD-BTS of February 1, 2005 defining the functions, tasks and organizational structure of the Research Institute for Marine Fisheries
Under this Decision, the Research Institute for Marine Fisheries, RIMF, is a non-business organization of the Fisheries Ministry, functions to make surveys on marine resources, sea environment, biological diversity and sea conservation, to research into exploitation technologies and forecast fishing grounds and marine resources; to research into post-harvest technologies; to transfer technologies, carry out experimental production and participate in scientific and technological researches on the sea; and to conduct post-graduate training.
