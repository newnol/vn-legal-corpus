# Thông tư liên tịch 12/2002/TTLT/BLĐTBXH-BTC

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 12/2002/TTLT-BLDTBXH-BTC of July 12, 2002 guiding the implementation of a number of regimes and policies towards officials working at medical establishments set up under the July 6, 1995 Ordinance on Handling of Administrative Violations
This Circular prescribes the occupational allowance regime and the specialized health service allowance regime as well as the funding sources and forms of payment for these regimes.- (Summary)
