# Quyết định 3902/QĐ-BNV

DECISION
Correcting Joint Circular No. 81/2005/TTLT/BNV-BTC of August 10, 2005, of the Home Affairs Ministry and the Finance Ministry
This Decision corrects Joint Circular No. 81/2005/TTLT/BNV-BTC of August 10, 2005, of the Home Affairs Ministry and the Finance Ministry, guiding the conversion of old salaries into new ones for several official and employee ranks in the water resources, education and training, culture and information, health, and market management services.- (Summary)
