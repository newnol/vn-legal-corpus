# Thông tư liên tịch 04/2003/TTLT/BCN-BTC

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 04/2003/TTLT-BCN-BTC of December 30, 2003 guiding the management and use of fines for breaches of electricity sale and purchase contracts
Subject to the application of this Joint Circular are parties involved in electricity sale and purchase; the Councils for handling of violations on electricity supply and use under the provincial/municipal Industry Services and concerned organizations and individuals.
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
