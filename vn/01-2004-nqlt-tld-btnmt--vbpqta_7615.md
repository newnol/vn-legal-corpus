# Joint Resolution 01/2004/NQLT-TLD-BTNMT

JOINT RESOLUTION No. 01/2004/NQLT-TLD-BTNMT of November 15, 2004 on coordination of environmental protection activities for sustainable development
This Joint Resolution aims to raise laborers' awareness of environmental protection tasks; mobilize laborers to participate in environmental protection activities; and enhance the coordination between Vietnam Labor Confederation and the Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment as well as the participation of trade unions of various levels in environmental protection activities.
This Joint Resolution takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
