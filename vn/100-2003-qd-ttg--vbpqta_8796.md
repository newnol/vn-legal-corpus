# Quyết định 100/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 100/2003/QD-TTg of May 16, 2003 approving the master plan for reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under the National Center of Natural Sciences and Technologies in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises with 100% of their charter capital being held by the State: 8.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding shares at low rates or holding no shares: 1 in 2004 and 2 in 2005.
Enterprises to be assigned: 1 in 2003, 1 in 2004 and 1 in 2005.
Enterprise to be dissolved: 1 in 2003.
Enterprise to be sold: 1 in 2004.- (Summary)
