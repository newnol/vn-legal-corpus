# Decree 67/2000/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 67/2000/ND-CP of November 9, 2000 on the adjustment of administrative boundaries for the establishment of communes and wards in Chu Se district and Pleiku city, Gia Lai province
To establish Ia Phang commune in Chu Se district on the basis of a land area of 12,710.5 ha and a population of 5,214 of Nhon Hoa commune.
To establish Ia Dreng commune in Chu Se district on the basis of a land area of 2,351.7 ha and a population of 2,486 of Ia Hru commune.
To establish Yen The ward in Pleiku city on the basis of land area of 1,187 ha and a population of 12,916 of Bien Ho commune.
To establish Tra Ba ward in Pleiku city on the basis of a land area of 758.67 ha and a population of 13,990 of Tra Ba commune.
To rename Tra Ba commune in Pleiku city as Chu HDrong commune.- (Summary)
