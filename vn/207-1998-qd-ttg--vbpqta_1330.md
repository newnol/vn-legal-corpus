# Quyết định 207/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 207/1998/QD-TTg of October 23, 1998 on the establishment of the Binh Dinh Teachers' Training College
To establish the Binh Dinh Teachers' Training College on the basis of Binh Dinh Teachers' Training School.
The Binh Dinh Teachers' Training College is attached to the People's Committee of Binh Dinh province and subject to the State management by the Ministry of Education and Training.
The Binh Dinh Teachers' Training College is tasked to train and foster teachers of college and lower levels.- (Summary)
