# Quyết định 265/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 265/2003/QD-TTg of December 16, 2003 on the regime applicable to persons exposed to HIV or infected with HIV/AIDS due to occupational accidents or risks
This Decision provides for the entitlements enjoyed by persons who are exposed to HIV or infected with HIV/AIDS due to various occupational accidents and risks, including free-of-charge diagnosis, treatment with anti-HIV/AIDS medicines, paid leaves while being medically treated, etc. Social insurance participants who are HIV/AIDS infected and quit their jobs shall also be given various allowances by the social insurance fund.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
