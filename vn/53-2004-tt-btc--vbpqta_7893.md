# Thông tư 53/2004/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 53/2004/TT-BTC of June 9, 2004 guiding the implementation of the Prime Miniser's Decision No. 54/2004/QD-TTg of April 5, 2004 on the preferential financial and budgetary mechanisms applicable to Hai Phong city
This Circular guides the preferential financial and budgetary mechanisms applicable to Hai Phong city, including the preferential mechanism of budget management and the preferential mechanism for mobilization of capital sources for development.
Besides the preferential financial and budgetary mechanisms prescribed in the Prime Minister's Decision No. 54/2004/QD-TTg of April 5, 2004 and this Circular's guidance, the city shall also comply with the general provisions on financial and budgetary management in the State Budget Law, the Government's Decree No. 60/2003/ND-CP of June 6, 2003, the Finance Ministry's Circular No. 59/2003/TT-BTC of June 23, 2003 and other relevant legal documents.
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
