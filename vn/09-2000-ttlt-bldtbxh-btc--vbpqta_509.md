# Joint circular 09/2000/TTLT/BLĐTBXH-BTC

THE MINISTRY OF LABOR, WAR INVALIDS AND SOCIAL AFFAIRS - THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE
JOINT CIRCULAR No. 09/2000/TTLT-BLDTBXH-BTC of March 29, 2000 guiding the amendments to Item II of Joint-Circular No. 03/2000/TTLT-BLDTBXH-BTC of the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs and the Ministry of Finance guiding the adjustment of subsidy and allowance levels for revolutionary activists, fallen heroes’ families, war invalids, diseased soldiers and people with meritorious services to the Revolution
This Circular guides the amendment to the burial allowance level specified in Item II, Joint-Circular No. 03/2000/TTLT/BLDTBXH-BTC.- (Summary)
