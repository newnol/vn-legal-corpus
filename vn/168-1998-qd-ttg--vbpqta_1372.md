# Quyết định 168/1998/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 168/1998/QD-TTg of Sep-tember 8, 1998 to add new members to the Vietnam Committee for the Elderly's International Year
To add representatives leading of officials of the Radio Voice of Vietnam and of Vietnam Television Station to the membership of the Vietnam Committee for the Elderly's International Year.-
Decision No. 169/1998/QD-TTg of September 8, 1998 on the establishment of the Management Board for the National Target Program on Employment
To set up the Management Board of the National Target Program on Employment with Mrs. Nguyen Thi Hang, Minister of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs as its Chairman; the Board is composed of 3 Vice Chairmen and 3 members being representatives of the Ministry of Planning and Investment, the Ministry of Finance, the Ministry of Industry, the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development and the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs.
The Management Board for the National Target Program on Employment is tasked to implement the Prime Minister's stipulations in Decision No. 126/1998/QD-TTg of July 11, 1998 on ratifying the National Target Program on Employment till the year 2000, Decision No. 531-TTg of August 8, 1996 on the management of the national programs and Decision No. 05/1998/QD-TTg of January 14, 1998 on the management of the national target programs.- (Summary)
