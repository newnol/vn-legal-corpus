# Thông tư liên tịch 01/2000/TTLT/BLĐTBXH-BTC

JOINT CIRCULAR No.01/2000/TTLT-BLDTBXH-BTC of January 17, 2000 guiding the adjustment of pension and social insurance allowance levels under the Government’s Decree No.175/1999/ND-CP of December 15, 1999
This Circular guides the method of re-calculation of pension and social insurance allowance levels of the following types: pension, allowance for laborers having lost their working capacity, allowance for rubber workers, sickness and maternity allowances, labor accident and occupational disease allowances, funeral allowance, and allowance for commune/ward officials who have retired from their work and are entitled thereto under the Government’s Decree No.09/1998/ND-CP of January 23, 1998.- (Summary)
