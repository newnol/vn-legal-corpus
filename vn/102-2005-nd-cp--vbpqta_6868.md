# Decree 102/2005/ND-CP

DECREE
On the establishment of wards and communes of Thanh Khe urban district and Hoa Vang rural district, and the establishment of Cam Le urban district, Da Nang city
This Decree provides for the establishment of Hoa Khe, Thanh Khe Dong and Thanh Khe Tay wards of Thanh Khe urban district, and Hoa An, Hoa Tho Dong and Hoa Tho Tay communes of Hoa Vang rural district.
It also stipulates the establishment of Cam Le urban district on the basis of the whole land areas and population of Hoa Tho Dong, Hoa Tho Tay, Hoa Phat, Hoa An and Hoa Xuan communes of Hoa Vang rural district and Khue Trung ward of Hai Chau urban district.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
