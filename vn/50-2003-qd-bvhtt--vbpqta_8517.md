# Quyết định 50/2003/QĐ-BVHTT

DECISION No. 50/2003/QD-BVHTT of August 22, 2003 prescribing the levels of fundings for the purchase of theoretical and political books of the system of libraries
The "Regulation on the levels of fundings for the purchase of theoretical and political books of the system of libraries", promulgated together with this Decision, prescribes the minimum levels of annual fundings for the purchase of theoretical and political books of the system of libraries and information-library centers allocated operation fundings by the State.- (Summary)
