# Quyết định 249/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 249/2003/QD-BCN of December 31, 2003 transforming Mineral Development Company No. 4 into Mineral Development Joint-Stock Company No. 4
To transform Mineral Development Company No. 4 (an independent-accounting member of Vietnam Mineral Corporation) into Mineral Development Joint-Stock Company No. 4 (MIDECO4).
Its head-office: Le Viet Thuat road, Vinh city, Nghe An province.
Its charter capital is VND 6 billion, with 51% of which being held by the State and 49% being sold to its laborers.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
