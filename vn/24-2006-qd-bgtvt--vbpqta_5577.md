# Decision 24/2006/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION
Promulgating branch standard 22 TCN 307-06 “Road motor vehicles - Automobiles - General safety requirements”
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.” For motorized vehicles which had been granted quality certificates before the effective date of this Decision, their manufacture and assembly at home may be extended for up to 12 months.- (Summary)
