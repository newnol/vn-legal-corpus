# Thông tư 06/2003/TT-BLĐTBXH

CIRCULAR No. 06/2003/TT-BLDTBXH of February 19, 2003 guiding the adjustment of pension and social insurance subsidy levels under the Government’s Decree No. 03/2003/ND-CP of January 15, 2003
Subject to the adjustment of pension and social insurance subsidy levels are:
- Pensioners;
- Working capacity loss subsidy beneficiaries;
- Rubber worker subsidy beneficiaries;
- Illness, maternity, labor accident, occupational disease and death allowance subsidy beneficiaries;
- Commune and ward officials who stop working and enjoy subsidies.
This Circular guides the adjustment of pension and social insurance subsidy levels for the above-said subjects.- (Summary)
