# Nghị định 66/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 66/2004/ND-CP of February 19, 2004 on transferring the State Securities Commission to the Finance Ministry
To transfer the State Securities Commission to the Finance Ministry and assign the Finance Minister to perform the management of the State Securities Commission.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
