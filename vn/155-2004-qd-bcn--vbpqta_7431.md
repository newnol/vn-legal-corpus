# Quyết định 155/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 155/2004/QD-BCN of December 3, 2004 transforming Phu Lam Footwear Company into Phu Lam Footwear Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Phu Lam Footwear Joint-Stock Company (FULAM) is headquartered at No. 71C Ly Chieu Hoang road, Ward 10, District 6, Ho Chi Minh city.
Its charter capital is VND 6.5 billion, 100% of which to be sold to its laborers.- (Summary)
