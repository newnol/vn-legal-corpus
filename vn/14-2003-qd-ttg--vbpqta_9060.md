# Quyết định 14/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 14/2003/QD-TTg of January 20, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under Vietnam Tobacco Corporation in the 2003-2005 period
Under the master plan, 10 business enterprises and 1 non-business unit shall continue to have 100% of their capital held by the State; 3 enterprises shall be equitized, and with the State holding over 50% of their charter capital (one enterprise shall be equitized each year) and 1 enterprise shall be merged into other enterprise (in 2003).- (Summary)
