# Decision 93/2000/QD-BNN-TCCB

Decision No. 93/2000/QD-BNN-TCCB of August 23, 2000 approving the organization and operation Charter of Bac Nam Ha Irrigation and Drainage Management Company
To approve "the organization and operation Charter of Bac Nam Ha Irrigation and Drainage Management Company - BNH IDMC", including 10 chapters with 35 articles.
Bac Nam Ha Irrigation and Drainage Management Company is a public-utility State enterprise, tasked to manage, exploit and protect Bac Nam Ha irrigation system as assigned in order to meet the water demands for agricultural production, industry and daily life of Ha Nam and Nam Dinh provinces.- (Summary)
