# Quyết định 11/2001/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION No. 11/2001/QD-BGDDT of April 16, 2001 promulgating the Regulation on the organization and operation of the Study Subjects Council
The Study Subjects Council is an organization providing professional consultancy, having the function of contributing opinions, suggestion and proposal to the Minister of Education and Training on matters relating to the contents and methods of teaching assorted subjects at various grades and levels of general education.
The Regulation on the organization and operation of the Study Subjects Council, prescribes the functions, tasks and powers; organization; working style; working conditions of the Study Subjects Council; and its relationship with functional units.- (Summary)
