# Quyết định 36/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 36/2002/QD-TTg of March 13, 2002 supplementing Decision No. 167/2001/QD-TTg of October 26, 2001 on a number of measures and policies to develop milch cow farming in Vietnam in the 2001-2010 period
The provinces not yet mentioned in Article 2, Clause 1, of Decision No. 167/2001/QD-TTg of October 26, 2001, Decision No. 181/2001/QD-TTg of November 19, 2001 and Decision No. 26/2002/QD-TTg of February 5, 2002 of the Prime Minister, but meeting all condition for efficient milch cow farming, shall be entitled to enjoy the policies prescribed in Decision No. 167/2001/QD-TTg.- (Summary)
