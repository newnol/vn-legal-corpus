# Quyết định 27/2005/QĐ-BXD

DECISION No. 27/2005/QD-BXD of August 8, 2005, promulgating 9 Vietnamese construction standards
Under this Decision, the following 9 Vietnamese construction standards, which are transformed from ISO international standards, are promulgated:
- TCXDVN 339: 2005 (ISO 9836: 1992): “Performance standards in building - Definition and calculation of area and space indicators;”
- TCXDVN 340: 2005 (ISO 10209-1: 1992): “Technical product documentation - Vocabulary - Part 1 - Terms relating to technical drawings - General and types of drawings;”
- TCXDVN 342: 2005 (ISO 834-1): “Fire-resistance tests - Elements of building construction - Part 1 - General requirements;”
- TCXDVN 343: 2005 (ISO 834-3: 1999) “Fire-resistance tests - Elements of building construction - Part 3 - Commentary on test method and test data application;”
- TCXDVN 344: 2005 (ISO 834-4): “Fire-resistance tests - Elements of building construction - Part 4 - Specific requirements for loadbearing vertical separating elements;”
- TCXDVN 345: 2005 (ISO 834-5): “Fire-resistance tests - Elements of building construction - Part 5 - Specific requirements for loadbearing horizontal separating elements;”
- TCXDVN 346: 2005 (ISO 834-6): “Fire-resistance tests - Elements of building construction - Part 6 - Specific requirements for beams;”
- TCXDVN 347: 2005 (ISO 834-7): “Fire-resistance tests - Elements of building construction - Part 7 - Specific requirements for columns;”
- TCXDVN 348: 2005 (ISO 834-8): “Fire-resistance tests - Elements of building construction - Part 8 - Specific requirements for non-loadbearing vertical separating elements.”
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
