# Quyết định 174/1999/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 174/1999/QD-TTg of August 23, 1999 on the regime on professional allowance and bonus for laborers engaged in art performances of the culture-information service.
This Decision specified the allowance level and the funding source for allowance paid to art performers in the culture-information service; level and fund for bonus paid to art performers in the culture-information service.- (Summary)
