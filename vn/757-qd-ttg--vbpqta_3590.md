# Decision 757/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Consolidating the Government’s Steering Committee for Administrative Reform
This Decision takes effect on the date of its signing and replaces the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 102/2004/QD-TTg of June 8, 2004.
It annuls Decision No. 01/QD-BCDCCHC of February 22, 2002, of the Prime Minister-Head of the Government’s Steering Committee for Administrative Reform, and Decision No. 1306/QD-TTg of December 9, 2004, of the Prime Minister.- (Summary)
