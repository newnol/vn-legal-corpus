# Quyết định 20/2004/QĐ-BTS

DECISION No. 20/2004/QD-BTS of August 30, 2004 establishing the Aquatic Resource Exploitation and Protection Technical Service Center under the Aquatic Resource Exploitation and Protection Department
The Aquatic Resource Exploitation and Protection Technical Service Center is headquartered at 10 Nguyen Cong Hoan street, Ba Dinh district, Hanoi.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
