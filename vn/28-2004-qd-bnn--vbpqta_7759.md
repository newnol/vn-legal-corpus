# Quyết định 28/2004/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 28/2004/QD-BNN of July 30, 2004 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational apparatus of Region-VIII Plant Quarantine Sub-Department
Region-VIII Plant Quarantine Sub-Department is an unit under the Plant Protection Department, functioning to perform the State management over plant quarantine, having the legal person status, its own seal, operation funding and bank accounts.
The Sub-Department is headquartered Lao Cai provincial capital, and operates in Lao Cai, Ha Giang, Tuyen Quang, Yen Bai and Lai Chau provinces.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
