# Decision 16/2006/QĐ-BCN

DECISION
Promulgating industrial glass-porcelain branch standards
Promulgated together with this Decision are two industrial glass-porcelain branch standards: 24 TCN 04: 2006 “Raw material for producing hard porcelain tableware - Quartz” and 24 TCN 05: 2006 “Raw material for producing hard porcelain tableware - Clay.”
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
