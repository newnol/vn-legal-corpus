# Quyết định 73/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 73/2001/QD-TTg of May 7, 2001 assigning the task of doctorate training to Ho Chi Minh City Pasteur Institute
To assign the task of doctorate training to Ho Chi Minh City Pasteur Institute.
The Ministry of Education and Training shall assume the prime responsibility and coordinate with the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment and the Ministry of Health in prescribing majors of doctorate training for Ho Chi Minh city Pasteur Institute.- (Summary)
