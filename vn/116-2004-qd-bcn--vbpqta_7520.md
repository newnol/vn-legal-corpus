# Quyết định 116/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 116/2004/QD-BCN of October 29, 2004 transforming Chien Thang Garment Company into Chien Thang Garment Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Chien Thang Garment Joint-Stock Company (CHIGAMEX) is headquartered at No. 22 Thanh Cong street, Ba Dinh district, Hanoi.
CHIGAMEX's charter capital is VND 12 billion, 51% of which to be held by the State and 49% sold to its laborers.- (Summary)
