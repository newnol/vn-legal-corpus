# Decision 15/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 15/QD-TTg of January 7, 2002 transforming District I Production, Service Provision and Import-Export Company (FIMEXCO) into District I Production, Service Provision and Import-Export Joint-Stock Company
To transform I District Production, Service Provision and Import-Export Company (FIMEXCO) into District I Production, Service Provision and Import-Export Joint-Stock Company.
Headquarter of the joint-stock company: No. 71C Dong Khoi Street, District I, Ho Chi Minh City.
Charter capital of the joint-stock company is VND 13,800,000,000, of which the equities held by the Finance-Administration Commission of Ho Chi Minh City Party Committee is 10%, equities sold to officials and employees of the company, 65%, and equities sold to other shareholders, 25%.- (Summary)
