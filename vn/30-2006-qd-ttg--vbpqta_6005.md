# Quyết định 30/2006/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Promulgating the Government's action program on enforcement of the anti-corruption Law
THE PRIME MINISTER
Pursuant to the December 25, 2001 Law on Organization of the Government;
Pursuant to the November 29, 2005 Anti-Corruption Law;
Pursuant to the Government's Resolution No. 17/2005/ND-CP of December 29, 2005;
At the proposal of the Inspector General,
DECIDES:
Article 1.- To promulgate together with this Decision the Government's action program on enforcement of the Anti-Corruption Law.
Article 2.- This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."
Article 3.- Ministers, heads of ministerial-level agencies, heads of Government-attached agencies, and presidents of provincial/municipal People's Committees shall have to implement this Decision.
