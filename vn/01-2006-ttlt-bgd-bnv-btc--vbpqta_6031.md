# Thông tư liên tịch 01/2006/TTLT-BGD-BNV-BTC

JOINT CIRCULAR
Guiding the implementation of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 244/2005/QD-TTg of October 6, 2005, on the preferential allowance regime for teachers directly engaged in teaching activities in public educational institutions
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and annuls Joint Circular No. 147/1998/TTLT-TCCP-TC-LDTBXH-GDDT of March 5, 1998, and Point 4.9, Clause 4 of Joint Circular No. 42/2003/TTLT/BGDDT-BTC of August 29, 2003, of the Ministry of Education and Training and the Ministry of Finance.- (Summary)
