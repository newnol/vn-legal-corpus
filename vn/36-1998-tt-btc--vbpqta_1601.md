# Circular 36/1998/TT-BTC

Circular No.36/1998/TT-BTC of March 21, 1998 guiding the financial regime applicable to the Vietnam Air Traffic Management Center.
This Circular specifically guides the regulations on revenues and expenditures, management of financial revenues and expenditures; settlement of financial results, financial plans; accounting inspection, financial statements and financial publicity of the Vietnam Air Traffic Management Center.- (Summary)
DECISION No.369/1998/QD-BTC of March 28, 1998 on the simplification of administrative procedures in the allocation, payment and lending of the State's investment capital for capital construction via the system of the General Department of Investment and Development.
Issued together with this Decision was the Regulation on simplification of administrative procedures in the allocation, payment and lending of the State's investment capital for capital construction via the system of the General Department of Investment and Development.
The Regulation clearly stipulates the implementation of the detailed 1998 plan for each project, which shall serve as basis for the capital allocation; dossiers and procedures for opening of accounts and receiving capital; the role of the investment and development agency as transaction venue; the responsibility of the investment and development departments for receiving dossiers and announcing results of the allocation or lending of capital to the investors.. - (Summary)
