# Quyết định 73/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 73/2003/QD-TTg of April 29, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Nghe An province till 2005
Enterprises whose 100% charter capital continues to be held by the State and whose legal person status remains unchanged: 11 public-utility enterprises and 24 business enterprises.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding more than 50% of their charter capital: 3 in 2003, 8 in 2004 and 2 in 2005.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding at least 51% of their shares when shares are sold for the first time: 2 in 2003 and 2 in 2004.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding shares at low rates or holding no shares: 5 in 2003, 7 in 2004 and 4 in 2005.
Enterprise to be merged: 1 in 2003.
Enterprise to be relocated to other place: 1 in 2003.
Enterprise to be sold: 1 in 2003.
Enterprise to be transformed into non-business unit with revenues: 1 in 2004.
Enterprises to be kept for consolidation then having their ownership transferred or being dissolved or declared bankrupt: 3 in 2003, 5 in 2004 and 5 in 2005.- (Summary)
