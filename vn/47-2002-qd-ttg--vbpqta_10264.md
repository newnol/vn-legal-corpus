# Decision 47/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 47/2002/QD-TTg of April 11, 2002 on regimes towards armymen and defense workers and employees, who participated in the resistance war against the French colonialists and had been demobilized (released from active service or abandoned their jobs) as from December 31, 1960 backwards
The Decision prescribed the lump-sum allowance regime for armymen and defense workers and employees, who had participated in the resistance war against the French colonialists as armymen or defense workers before July 20, 1954 and had been demobilized as from December 31, 1960 backwards, and who do not enjoy salaries, monthly allowance for working capacity loss or regime towards diseased soldiers.- (Summary)
