# Decision 173/2002/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 173/2002/QD-TTg of December 2, 2002 reorganizing the National Steering Committee for overcoming the consequences of toxic chemicals used by the U.S. during the Vietnam war
The National Steering Committee for overcoming the consequences of toxic chemicals used by the U.S. during the Vietnam war shall be composed of the Minister of Natural Resources and Environment as its head and a Vice Minister of Natural Resources and Environment as its deputy head.
The Steering Committee�s assisting office is located at the Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment.
The Steering Committee shall be entitled to use the seal and accounts of the Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment.- (Summary)
