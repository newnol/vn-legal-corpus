# Joint circular 44/2007/TTLT-BTC-BKHCN

JOINT CIRCULAR
Guiding norms for elaboration and allocation of cost estimates for scientific and technological subjects and projects funded with the state budget
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces Joint Circular No. 45/2001/TTLT/BTC-BKHCNMT of June 18, 2001, of the Ministry of Finance and the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment (now the Ministry of Science and Technology).- (Summary)
