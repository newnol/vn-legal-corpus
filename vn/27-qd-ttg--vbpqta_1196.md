# Decision 27/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 27/QD-TTg of February 23, 1999 on setting up the Steering Committee for formulation of "Vision 2020"
To set up the Steering Committee for formulation of "Vision 2020" which shall be headed by the Minister of Science, Technology and Environment.
To assign the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment to act as a standing body for organizing the formulation of "Vision 2020".- (Summary)
