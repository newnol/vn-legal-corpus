# Chỉ thị 39/2004/CT-BNN

DIRECTIVE No. 39/2004/CT-BNN of August 17, 2004 on expeditiously performing the work of personnel planning
Under this Directive, the Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development requests the heads of the agencies and units under the Ministry to expeditiously perform the personnel planning work, which should be regarded as their central task in the last 6 months of 2004.- (Summary)
