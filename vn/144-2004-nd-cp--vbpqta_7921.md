# Decree 144/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 144/2004/ND-CP of July 14, 2004 on the organization, tasks and powers of the physical training and sports inspectorate
The physical training and sports inspectorate performs the function of examining and inspecting the observance of policies and laws on physical training and sports.
Subject to the examination and inspection of the physical training and sports inspectorate are Vietnamese organizations and individuals at home and abroad as well as foreign organizations and individuals engaged in physical training and sports activities on Vietnamese territory and in foreign countries under the management of the Physical Training and Sports Committee.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
