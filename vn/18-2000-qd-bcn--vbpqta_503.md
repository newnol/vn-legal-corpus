# Quyết định 18/2000/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 18/2000/QD-BCN of April 5, 2000 on transferring the Mining and Construction Vocational School which is an attached unit of Vietnam Coal Corporation to Noi Dia Coal Company
To transfer in status quo the Mining and Construction Vocational School, which is an attached unit of Vietnam Coal Corporation to Noi Dia Coal Company, member of Vietnam Coal Corporation.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its signing.- (Summary)
