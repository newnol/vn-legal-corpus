# Quyết định 34/2002/QĐ-BKHCNMT

DECISION No. 34/2002/QD-BKHCNMT of May 31, 2002 promulgating the Provisional Regulation on working procedures of the Scientific and Technological Council for determination of the State-level subjects, the 2001-2005 period
Issued together with this Decision is the Provisional Regulation on working procedures of the Scientific and Technological Council for determination of the State-level subjects, the 2001-2005 period.
This Regulation replaces the Regulation issued together with Decision No. 07/2001/QD-BKHCNMT of April 11, 2001 of the Minister of Science, Technology and Environment.- (Summary)
