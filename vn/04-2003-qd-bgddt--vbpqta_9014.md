# Quyết định 04/2003/QĐ-BGDĐT

This Decision amends Articles 1, 2, 5, 6, 7, 10, 11, 13, 15, 16, 18, 19 and 20 of the Regulation on compilation of questions for examination to select talented 12 graders of senior secondary education.- (Summary)
