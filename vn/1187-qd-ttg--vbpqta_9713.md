# Decision 1187/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 1187/QD-TTg of December 16, 2002 amending and supplementing the Prime Minister?s Decision No. 1253/QD-TTg of December 29, 1999 on setting up the working group for the implementation of the Enterprise Law
This Decision amends and supplements Articles 2 and 3 of Decision No. 1253/QD-TTg.
Under the amended contents, the working group for the implementation of the Enterprise Law shall be headed by the Minister of Planning and Investment.
The working group is tasked to:
- Urge and monitor the implementation of the Enterprise Law by the ministries, branches, localities and enterprises; sum up good models and experiences; detect violations and acts against the provisions of the Enterprise Law and decrees guiding the implementation thereof, and report them to the Prime Minister for handling.
- Revise and evaluate the regulations guiding the implementation of the Enterprise Law and other relevant regulations; report and suggest to the Prime Minister amendments and supplements to contents which are no longer suitable.
- Disseminate and propagate the Enterprise Law and its guiding documents to enterprise owners and managers as well as concerned officials who perform the State management thereof; propagate and popularize good models as well as achievements and shortcomings in the implementation of the Enterprise Law with a view to mobilizing public initiatives and opinions for the further implementation of the Enterprise Law in a consistent manner.- (Summary)
