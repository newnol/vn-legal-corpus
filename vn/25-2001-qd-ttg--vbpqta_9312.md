# Quyết định 25/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 25/2001/QD-TTg of March 2, 2001 transforming Bien Hoa Building Materials Production and Construction Company, a State enterprise, into a joint-stock company
To transform Bien Hoa Building Materials Production and Construction Company, a State enterprise, into Bien Hoa Building Materials Production and Construction Joint-Stock Corporation. (B.B.C.C.)
Headquarters of the joint-stock company: K4/79C Tan Ban quarter, Buu Hoa ward, Bien Hoa city, Dong Nai province.
The company�s charter capital: VND 35,000,000,000, in which the State�s equities represent 30%; equities sold to laborers of the enterprise, 40%; and equities sold to Vietnamese shareholders outside the enterprise, 30%.
B.B.C.C. shall be engaged in the following business lines:
- Exploitation of clay, construction sand and stones
- Production of bricks, tiles and trading in building materials
- Production of ready-made concrete, foreign-made building materials sale agencies
- Construction of traffic and civil works, industrial works, infrastructure investment and construction.- (Summary)
