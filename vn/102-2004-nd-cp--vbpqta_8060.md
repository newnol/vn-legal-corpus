# Nghị định 102/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 102/2004/ND-CP of February 27, 2004 detailing the implementation of a number of articles of the Cipher Ordinance on entitlements and policies for persons engaged in cipher work upon their retirement, transfer to other branches, resignation from jobs or transfer to other jobs in cipher organizations
This Decree details the implementation of a number of articles of the December 25, 2001 Cipher Ordinance on entitlements and policies for persons engaged in cipher work and currently enjoying salaries in the salary table of the armed forces, who are not army men or men of the people's police, upon their retirement, transfer to other branches, resignation from jobs or transfer to other jobs in cipher organizations
This Decree provides for specific entitlements and policies for the above-said persons, methods for calculating pensions and allowances, and converting durations for calculating other entitlements.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
