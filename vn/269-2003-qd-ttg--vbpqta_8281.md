# Decision 269/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 269/2003/QD-TTg of December 22, 2003 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of Vietnam Maritime Administration
Vietnam Maritime Administration (VINAMARINE) is an organization under the Ministry of Communications and Transport, performing the specialized maritime State management function nationwide according to law provisions.
It has the legal person status and seal bearing the national emblem, enjoys State budget allocations, has its accounts at the State Treasury, and is headquartered in Hanoi.
VINAMARINE is composed of 14 organizations assisting the Director, 3 Maritime Sub-Departments, provincial/municipal Port Authorities, and 4 dependent non-business units.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
