# Decision 1026/QD-TTg

Decision No. 1026/QD-TTg of August 10, 2001 approving the project on coordination of army and civil medicines to better care for people�s health and serve national security and defense in the 2001-2010 period
This project aims to build and consolidate the joint army-civil medical stations and centers with a view to meeting the requirements of protecting and caring for the health of people and armymen; contributing to social stability and security and defense consolidation in border and island areas.
The Ministry of Health shall act as the project owner.- (Summary)
