# Quyết định 71/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 71/2003/QD-BCN of May 6, 2003 transferring Minerals Company I Hospital of Vietnam Minerals Corporation to the People's Committee of Cao Bang province
To transfer Minerals Company I Hospital under Vietnam Minerals Corporation to the People's Committee of Cao Bang province for management.
Vietnam Minerals Corporation shall have to direct the transfer of material and technical bases, assets, organizational apparatus and other relevant matters of Minerals Company I Hospital in status quo to the People's Committee of Cao Bang province.- (Summary)
