# Quyết định 125/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 125/2004/QD-BCN of November 5, 2004 transforming Mao Khe Mechanical Factory of Mao Khe Coal Company into Mao Khe Mechanical Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Mao Khe Mechanical Joint-Stock Company, abbreviated to CKMK, shall have a charter capital of VND 8,155,000,000, of which the State's equities represent 36.39% and equities sold to laborers of the factory, 63.61%.
Its head-office is located in Quang Trung quarter, Mao Khe township, Dong Trieu district, Quang Ninh province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
