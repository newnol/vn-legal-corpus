# Decision 39/2001/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 39/2001/QD-TTg of March 23, 2001 on the establishment of Northwest University
To establish Northwest University on the basis of Northwest Teachers� College. The headquarter of the University shall be located in Son La province.
Northwest University is tasked to:
- Train personnel of university and college levels
- Provide professional fostering for officials and employees
- Conduct scientific researches, technology transfer and provide technical services for socio-economic development of the northwestern region.
Northwest University shall be placed under the management of the Ministry of Education and Training.- (Summary)
