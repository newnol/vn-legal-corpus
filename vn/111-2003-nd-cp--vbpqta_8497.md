# Nghị định 111/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 111/2003/ND-CP of October 1, 2003 establishing wards and commune of Ha Long city and Mong Cai provincial town, Quang Ninh province
To establish Tuan Chau ward of Ha Long city on the basis of the whole land area of 405.4 ha and the population of 4,002 of Tuan Chau commune.
To establish Hung Thang ward of Ha Long city on the basis of the whole land area of 389 ha and the population of 4,877 of Hung Thang commune.
To establish Bac Son commune of Mong Cai provincial town on the basis of a land area of 5,043.67 ha and a population of 1,216 of Hai Son commune, which shall be left with a land area of 9,881.33 ha and a population of 842.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, annulling all previous regulations contrary thereto.- (Summary)
