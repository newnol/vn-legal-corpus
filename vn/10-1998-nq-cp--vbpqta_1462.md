# Nghị quyết 10/1998/NQ-CP

THE GOVERNMENT
RESOLUTION No. 10/1998/NQ-CP of August 6, 1998 on the Government's July 1998 regular meeting
The Government discussed and resolved the following issues:
1. Regarding the draft Land Law (amended): The Government agreed on major contents to be amended and/or supplemented, including: objects of land allotment and land lease; rights and obligations of organizations, family households and individuals with land allotted or leased by the State; per-capita land-use quota and land hoarding; land-allotment and land-lease term; competence to assign or lease land and the granting of land-use right certificates; matters relating to land prices and public land fund; the question of order and discipline in the State management of land. The Government assigns the General Land Administration to coordinate with the concerned agencies in collecting comments to the draft Land Law, soon finalize it and proceed with other necessary jobs so as to submit such law (amended) to the National Assembly for adoption in its October 1998 session.
2. To adopt the draft Law on Amendments and Supplements to a Number of Articles of the Law on the Election of Deputies to the People's Councils.
3. To adopt the basic contents of the Government's plan of action for the materialization of the Resolution of the fifth plenum of the Vietnam Communist Party's Central Committee (VIIIth Congress) on "building and developing Vietnam's advanced culture bearing national identity".
4. Regarding the plan on labor export in the 1998-2010 period: The Government assigned the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs to collect comments made at the meeting and complete the draft plan to submit it to the Prime Minister for approval and signing for promulgation a Decree on sending Vietnamese laborers to work for given terms abroad (to replace Decree No. 7-CP of January 20, 1995 of the Government).
5. The Government also heard reports on major issues concerning the socio-economic situation in July and in the first seven months of the year. The Government requested the ministries, branches and localities to seriously implement Resolution No. 8/1998/NQ-CP of July 16, 1998 of the Government on solutions to control the socio-economic development plan in the second half of the year, striving to achieve the readjusted socio-economic targets and preparing necessary conditions for drafting and implementing the 1999 plan.
6. The Government heard a report on the examination, inspection and settlement of citizens' complaints and denunciations in the second quarter of 1998. It affirmed that it is necessary to enhance the effectiveness and fruitfulness of the examination and inspection work while perfecting the post-inspection handling mechanism.-
