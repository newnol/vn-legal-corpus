# Quyết định 22/2001/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 22/2001/QD-BCN of April 16, 2001 on the establishment of Can Tho Dairy Plant under the Vietnam Milk Corporation
To establish Can Tho Dairy Plant, under the Vietnam Milk Corporation. Can Tho Dairy Plant is a dependent accounting unit of the Vietnam Milk Corporation.
The plant is headquartered at Tra Noc I industrial park, Tra Noc ward, Can Tho city, Can Tho province.
Can Tho Dairy Plant shall turn out the following products:
- Pasteurized fresh milk, yaourt, creams and biscuits;
- Other products as assigned by the Vietnam Milk Corporation according to law provisions.- (Summary)
