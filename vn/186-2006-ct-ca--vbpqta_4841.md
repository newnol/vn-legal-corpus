# Chỉ thị 186/2006/CT-CA

DIRECTIVE
On the enforcement of criminal judgments
The Chief Judge of the Supreme People’s Court requests heads of units under the Supreme People’s Court, chief judges of local people’s courts and military courts at all levels to perform several tasks in order to raise the effectiveness and efficiency of the enforcement of criminal judgments.- (Summary)
