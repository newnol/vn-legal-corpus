# Decision 839/2001/QĐ-BTS

DECISION No. 839/2001/QD-BTS of October 10, 2001 promulgating the Working Regulation of the Ministry of Aquatic Resources
Issued together with this Decision is the Working Regulation of the Ministry of Aquatic Resources.
This Regulation prescribes the working style of the Ministry of Aquatic Resources, applicable to the Ministry itself, in order to strengthen and raise the working efficiency, create positive changes and order and discipline in the Ministry�s activities, raise the effectiveness and efficiency of the Ministry�s State administrative management, thus meeting current State administrative reform requirements.- (Summary)
