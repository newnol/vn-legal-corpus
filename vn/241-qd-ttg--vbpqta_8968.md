# Decision 241/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 241/QD-TTg of March 4, 2003 approving the plan on reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under Vietnam Cement Corporation till 2005
Enterprises which continue to have 100% of their charter capital held by the State and be legal entities: 10 business enterprises and 2 non-business units.
To be equitized with the State holding over 50% of charter capital: In 2003: 2 enterprises, in 2004: 2 enterprises.
The Corporation shall elaborate and submit to the Prime Minister a separate scheme on the establishment of several new enterprises: In 2003: 3 enterprises, in 2004: 2 enterprises.
To be reorganized during the elaboration of the scheme on experimental establishment of Vietnam Cement Conglomerate: 5 enterprises.- (Summary)
