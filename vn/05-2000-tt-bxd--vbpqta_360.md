# Circular 05/2000/TT-BXD

CIRCULAR No. 05/2000/TT-BXD of June 27, 2000 guiding the implementation of supports for people who had joined revolutionary activities before the August 1945 Revolution to improve their housing conditions according to the Prime Minister�s Decision No. 20/2000/QD-TTg of February 3, 2000
This Circular reaffirms a number of principles and requirements of supports for people who had joined revolutionary activities before the August 1945 Revolution to improve their housing conditions suited to the socio-economic situation and meeting their aspirations.
The contents of the Prime Minister�s Decision No. 20/2000/QD-TTg of February 3, 2000 guided and concretized in this Circular include: subjects liable for housing support; conditions and criteria therefor; area of dwelling house and land to be given as support (level of support); cases where spouses or children of deceased eligible subjects shall enjoy or be considered for supports,...
The provincial/municipal Services of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs shall coordinate with the Boards for Organization and Personnel of the provincial/municipal Party Committees and administrations in examining and listing eligible subjects in strict accordance with Decision No. 20 and the guidance in this Circular.- (Summary)
