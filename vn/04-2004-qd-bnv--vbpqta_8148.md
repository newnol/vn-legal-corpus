# Decision 04/2004/QĐ-BNV

DECISION No. 04/2004/QD-BNV of January 16, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on specific criteria of the commune, ward and district township officials and public employees
This Decision promulgates the Regulation on specific political, ethical, educational and professional criteria of the commune, ward and district township officials and public employees and takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
