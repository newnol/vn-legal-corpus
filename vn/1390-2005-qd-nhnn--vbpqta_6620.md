# Quyết định 1390/2005/QĐ-NHNN

DECISION
Promulgating the Regulation on organization and operation of the Vault Safety Service Center
Under this Decision, the Vault Safety Service Center is a non-business unit under the State Bank and has the function of providing public services on vault safety.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
