# Quyết định 612/2002/QĐ-CTN

DECISION No. 612/2002/QD-CTN of September 24, 2002 permitting the relinquishment of Vietnamese nationality
To permit the relinquishment of Vietnamese nationality for 254 citizens residing in the Federal Republic of Germany, 26 citizens residing in the Republic of Austria, 29 citizens residing in the Czech Republic, 2 citizens residing in Vanuatu, 1 citizen residing in the United States of America, 1 citizen residing in Sweden, 9 citizens residing in Finland, 11 citizens residing in Denmark, 60 citizens residing in the Netherlands, 3 citizens residing in Poland, 1 citizen residing in the People’s Republic of China (the exclusive administrative zone of Hong Kong), 26 citizens residing in Japan, 21 citizens residing in the Lao People’s Democratic Republic, 1 citizen residing in Belarus, and 145 citizens residing in Taiwan.- (Summary)
