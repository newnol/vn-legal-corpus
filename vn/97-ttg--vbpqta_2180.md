# Decision 97/TTg

DECISION No.97-TTg OF FEBRUARY 9, 1996 OF THE PRIME MINISTER ON THE INVESTMENT IN THE PROJECT TO BUILD THE TAM DIEP CEMENT FACTORY IN NINH BINH PROVINCE
Name of the Project: The Tam Diep - Ninh Binh Cement Factory
Location: Quang Son commune, Tam Diep town, Ninh Binh province
Investor: The Ninh Binh Cement Company.
Design capacity: 4,000 tons of clinker/day, equivalent to 1.4 million tons of cement/year
Main product: PC30, PC40, PC50 (TCVN/2682-92)
The total investment capital is estimated at a maximum of 252 million USD at current exchange rate.- (Summary)
