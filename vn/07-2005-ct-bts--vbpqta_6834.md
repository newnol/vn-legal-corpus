# Chỉ thị 07/2005/CT-BTS

DIRECTIVE
On raising the efficiency of, and combating waste and loss in, construction investment funded by state capital, within the Fisheries Ministry
With this Directive, the Fisheries Minister instructs the Finance-Planning Department, the Science and Technology Department, the Organization and Personnel Department, the Inspectorate, heads of units assigned to act as project investors, investors, and project management units to perform several tasks in investment and construction management.- (Sumamry)
