# Decision 267/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 267/2003/QD-TTg of December 19, 2003 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Civil Aviation Administration of Vietnam (CAAV)
This Decision prescribes the position, functions, tasks, powers, organizational structure and leadership of the CAAV and takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
