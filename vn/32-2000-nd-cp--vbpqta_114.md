# Nghị định 32/2000/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 32/2000/ND-CP of August 11, 2000 on the adjustment of administrative boundaries of Van Luong and Van Hung communes and Van Gia township of Van Ninh district, Khanh Hoa province
To adjust the administrative boundaries of communes and Van Gia township of Van Ninh district, Khanh Hoa province as follows:
1. To merge into Van Gia township a land area of 82 ha and a population of 4,150 of Luong Hai village of Van Luong commune.
2. To merge into Van Luong commune a land area of 272 ha and a population of 5,031 of Tan Duc village of Van Hung commune.
After the adjustment of administrative boundaries:
- Van Gia township shall have a land area of 285 ha and a population of 19,985.
- Van Luong commune shall have a land area of 6,115 ha and a population of 9,602.
- Van Hung commune shall have a land area of 4,823 ha and a population of 8,894.- (Summary)
