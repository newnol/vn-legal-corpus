# Quyết định 08/2003/QĐ-BKHCN

DECISION No. 08/2003/QD-BKHCN of April 3, 2003 promulgating the Regulation on working modes of the Scientific and Technological Council for consultancy and determination of the State-level scientific and technological subjects as well as experimental production projects
Issued together with this Decision is the Regulation on working modes of the Scientific and Technological Council for consultancy and determination of the State-level scientific and technological subjects as well as experimental production projects.
This Decision replaces the Science, Technology and Environment Minister's Decision No. 34/2002/QD-BKHCNMT of May 28, 2002 promulgating the Provisional Regulation on working modes of the Scientific and Technological Council for determination of the State-level subjects in the 2001-2005 period.- (Summary)
