# Quyết định 136/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 136/2004/QD-TTg of August 2, 2004 setting up the Economics and Business Administration College under Thai Nguyen University
The Economics and Business Administration College under Thai Nguyen University is set up on the basis of consolidating the Agricultural Economics Faculty of the Forestry and Agriculture College and the Industrial Economics Faculty of the Industrial Technique College, Thai Nguyen University.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
