# Quyết định 127/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 127/2003/QD-TTg of June 25, 2003 approving the master plan for reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under the Ministry of Natural Resources and Environment in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises where the State continues to hold 100% of their charter capital and they remain to be legal entities: 2 public-utility enterprises and 1 business enterprise.
Enterprises to be merged: 5 in 2003.
Enterprise to be equitized with the State holding under 50% of its charter capital or holding no shares: 1 in 2004.- (Summary)
