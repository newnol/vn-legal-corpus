# Decision 25/2006/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION
Promulgating the Regulation on university and college formal training
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces the Education and Training Minister’s Decision No. 04/1999/QD-BGDDT of February 11, 1999, promulgating the Regulation on organization of training, exams, and graduation exams and recognition of formal university and college graduation.- (Summary)
