# Decree 22/2007/ND-CP 

DECREE
Establishing Soc Trang city of Soc Trang province
Under this Decree, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” Soc Trang city is established on the basis of the whole land area, population and dependent administrative units of Soc Trang provincial capital.- (Summary)
