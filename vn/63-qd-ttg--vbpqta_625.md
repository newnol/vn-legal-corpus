# Decision 63/QD-TTg

DECISION No.63/QD-TTg of January 18, 2000 on terminating the operation of the Steering Committee for implementation of Noi Bai T1 terminal project
To dissolve the Steering Committee for implementation of Noi Bai T1 terminal project, which was established under Decision No.1137/QD-TTg of December 21, 1998.
The remaining work of the project shall be controlled by the director of Vietnam Civil Aviation Department. For matters beyond his competence, the director of Vietnam Civil Aviation Department shall report them to the Prime Minister for consideration and decision.
The Decision takes effect after its signing.- (Summary)
