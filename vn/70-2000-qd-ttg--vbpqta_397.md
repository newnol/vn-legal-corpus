# Quyết định 70/2000/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 70/2000/QD-TTg of June 20, 2000 on the conversion of the Fuel Company under Sai Gon Commercial Corporation into a joint-stock company
To convert the State-run Fuel Company under Sai Gon Commercial Corporation, Ho Chi Minh city, into Sai Gon Fuel Joint-Stock Company (SFC).
Head office: 01 A, Pham Ngoc Thach street, Ben Nghe ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh city.
Charter capital: VN dong 17,000,000,000, of which the State equities represent 20%, equities sold to laborers inside the enterprise: 65% and those sold to subjects outside the enterprise: 15%.
Business lines:
- Trading in:
+ Petroleum, lubricants, grease, fuel gas, gas stoves; car washing and keeping services of various kinds.
+ Supplies, machinery, equipment and accessories in service of petroleum trading.
- Purchasing raw materials and materials as well as producing and processing wood products.
- Purchasing and processing agricultural and aquatic products.
- Importing material wood, supplies and equipment used for production and processing of wood products; supplies, machinery, equipment, accessories for petroleum trading, gas stoves and assorted accessories thereof.
- Exporting refined wood products.- (Summary)
