# Decision 480/TTg

Decision No.480-TTg of July 24, 1996 of the Prime Minister to upgrade the Intermediate School of Communications and Transport of Region I into the College of Communications and Transport.
The College of Communications and Transport is attached to the Ministry of Communications and Transport and subject to the branch management of the Ministry of Education and Training.- (Summary)
