# Thông tư 130/2005/TT-BNV

CIRCULAR
Guiding the implementation of a number of articles of the Government’s Decree No. 54/2005/ND-CP on the regimes of job severance and training cost compensation, applicable to public employees
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces Circular No. 28/1999/TT-BTCCBCP of July 31, 1999, of the Government’s Commission for Organization and Personnel (now the Ministry of Home Affairs).- (Summary)
