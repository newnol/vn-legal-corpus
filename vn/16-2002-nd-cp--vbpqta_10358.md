# Nghị định 16/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 16/2002/ND-CP of January 31, 2002 merging Lao Cai and Cam Duong towns into Lao Cai provincial capital of Lao Cai province
To merge the whole land area and population of Lao Cai and Cam Duong towns into Lao Cai provincial capital. Lao Cai provincial capital shall have a land area of 23,095.8 ha and a population of 77,167, including 9 wards and 7 communes.- (Summary)
