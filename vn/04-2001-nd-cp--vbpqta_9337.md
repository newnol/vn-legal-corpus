# Decree 04/2001/ND-CP

Decree No. 04/2001/ND-CP of January 16, 2001 detailing the implementation of a number of articles of the 1999 Law on Vietnam People�s Army Officers regarding regimes and policies towards demobilized officers and active officers who have been transferred to work as professional armymen or military employees
