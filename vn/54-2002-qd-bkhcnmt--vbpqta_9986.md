# Quyết định 54/2002/QĐ-BKHCNMT

DECISION No. 54/2002/QD-BKHCNMT of July 17, 2002 supplementing Article 2 of Decision No. 37/2002/QD-BKHCMNT of July 8, 2002 of the Minister of Science, Technology and Environment promulgating the provisional Regulation on selection of organizations and individuals to take charge of State-level scientific and technological subjects in the 2001-2005 period and supplementing Article 2 of Decision No. 38/2002/QD-BKHCNMT of July 8, 2002 of the Minister of Science, Technology and Environment promulgating the provisional Regulation on working modes of the Advisory Council for Selection
To add the provisions on the consideration and assessment of dossiers of registration for selection of organizations and individuals to take charge of State-level scientific and technological subjects included in the 2002 plan, which had been submitted by July 1, 2002.- (Summary)
