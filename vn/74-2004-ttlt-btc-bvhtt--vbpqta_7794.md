# Thông tư liên tịch 74/2004/TTLT-BTC-BVHTT

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 74/2004/TTLT/BTC-BVHTT of July 19, 2004 guiding the funding support for literary and artistic works creation activities in 2004
In 2004, the State shall provide funding support for orientated literary and artistic works creation activities to localities through the local literature and art associations and the National Committee of Vietnam's Union of Literary and Artistic Associations.
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
