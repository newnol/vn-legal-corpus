# Nghị quyết 32/2006/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
Approving the adjusted land use planning up to 2010 and the five year (2006-2010) land use plan of Vinh Long province
The People’s Committee of Vinh Long province shall base itself on the criteria approved under this Resolution to organize the implementation of the land use planning and plan in association with socio-economic development objectives as well as defense, security and eco-environmental protection tasks.- (Summary)
