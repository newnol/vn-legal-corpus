# Decision 69/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.69/1998/QD-TTg of March 31, 1998 on the distribution of the "Cong Bao" (Official Gazette) of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam to communes, wards and district townships.
As from May 1st, 1998, the "Cong Bao" (Official Gazette) of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam shall be distributed to the People's Committees of the communes, wards and district townships throughout the country.
Each commune, ward or district township shall build its own "legal book case" in order to provide legal materials in service of study and use by local officials and people as well.- (Summary)
