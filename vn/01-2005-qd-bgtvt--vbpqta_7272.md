# Decision 01/2005/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION No. 01/2005/QD-BGTVT of January 4, 2005 prescribing the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of Vietnam Railway Administration Inspectorate
Under this Decision, Vietnam Railway Administration Inspectorate, RAAI, constitutes part of the Transport Inspectorate system, and performs administrative and specialized inspection within the State management scope of Vietnam Railway Administration.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
