# Quyết định 29/2003/QĐ-BNV

DECISION No. 29/2003/QD-BNV of June 11, 2003 permitting the establishment of Vietnam Securities-Trading Association
To permit the establishment of Vietnam Securities-Trading Association, which is organized and operates according to its Charter approved by the Minister of the Interior and is placed under the State Securities Commission's State management over securities activities.
The Association shall self-finance its operation and facilities.- (Summary)
