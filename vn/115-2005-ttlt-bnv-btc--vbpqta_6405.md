# Joint circular 115/2005/TTLT-BNV-BTC 

JOINT CIRCULAR
Guiding the implementation of the Government’s Decree No. 119/2005/ND-CP of September 27, 2005, on the adjustment of allowances for commune cadres who had retired under the Government Council’s Decision No. 130-CP of June 20, 1975, and the Ministers Council’s Decision No. 111-HDBT of October 13, 1981
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
