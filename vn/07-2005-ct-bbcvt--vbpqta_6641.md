# Chỉ thị 07/2005/CT-BBCVT

DIRECTIVE
On public telecommunications network connection and services
With this Directive, the Minister of Post and Telematics instructs units under the Ministry to perform several tasks in order to well observe state regulations on telecommunications and the Internet, and the Prime Minister's Directive No. 08/2003/CT-TTg of April 4, 2003, on raising the efficiency and competitiveness of enterprises.- (Summary)
