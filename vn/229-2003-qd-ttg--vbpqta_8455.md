# Quyết định 229/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 229/2003/QD-TTg of November 7, 2003 establishing the Website of the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam on the Internet
The Website of the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam on the Internet (or the Government's Website), based at the Government's Office, is a governmental information agency, which provides people in the country and foreigners with official information on the Party's undertakings and policies as well as the State's laws; the Government's and the Prime Minister's direction and administration; socio-economic development situation and other domains nationwide on the Internet.
The Government's Website functions to coordinate via-Internet information-supplying activities of administrative agencies of all levels along the direction of serving the information-related public services and the establishment of on-line relationships between the Government and the people.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
