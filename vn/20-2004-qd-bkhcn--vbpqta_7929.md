# Quyết định 20/2004/QĐ-BKHCN

DECISION No. 20/2004/QD-BKHCN of July 22, 2004 promulgating the Charter on organization and operation of the Science and Development newspaper
The Charter on organization and operation of the Science and Development newspaper, issued together with this Decision, prescribes the tasks, powers, organizational apparatus and working relations of, as well as financial management applicable to, the Science and Development newspaper, the mouthpiece of the Ministry of Science and Technology.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
