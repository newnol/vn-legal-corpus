# Circular 118/2000/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 118/2000/TT-BTC of December 22, 2000 prescribing the management of commune budgets and other financial activities in communes, wards and district townships
Financial activities of communes, wards and district townships (collectively called communes) include commune budgets and other financial activities arising in their respective localities.
The commune People�s Committees shall organize the uniform management of commune budgets and other financial activities in communes.
This Circular specifies the revenue sources, spending tasks and the process of managing commune budgets; the scope and organization of management of other financial activities in communes.- (Summary)
