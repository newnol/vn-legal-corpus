# Circular 54/2006/TT-BVHTT

CIRCULAR
Guiding the planning of karaoke bars and dance halls
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”
The order of formulating, evaluating and approving the planning shall comply with the guidance in the Planning and Investment Ministry’s Circular No. 05/2003/TT-BKH of July 22, 2003.- (Summary)
