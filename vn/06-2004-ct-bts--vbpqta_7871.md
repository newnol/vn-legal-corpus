# Chỉ thị 06/2004/CT-BTS

DIRECTIVE No. 06/2004/CT-BTS of June 7, 2004 on reorganizing the financial and accounting management at administrative and non-business units under the Fisheries Ministry
The Fisheries Minister issued this Directive to reorganize the financial and accounting management, strict observance of the Budget Law, the Accounting Law and financial policies and regimes, and raise the responsibilities of organizations and individuals in the management and use of the State's budget and assets at their units, instructing the budget beneficiary units, departments, the Office of the Fisheries Ministry, the Department for Aquatic Product Quality, Safety, Hygiene and Veterinary Drugs Management, the Department for Aquatic Resources Exploitation and Protection Center, the National Fishery Promotion, the fisheries intermediate schools, the research institutes and centers, the Science and Technology Department, the Organization and Personnel Department, the International Cooperation Department, the Planning and Finance Department and the Fisheries Ministry's Inspectorate to act according to their functions and tasks and coordinate with one another in making their financial and accounting management activities more effective and compliant with laws and regulations.- (Summary)
