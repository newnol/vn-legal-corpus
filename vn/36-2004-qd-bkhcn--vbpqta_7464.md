# Quyết định 36/2004/QĐ-BKHCN

DECISION No. 36/2004/QD-BKHCN of October 29, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on organization and operation of the Office of the Ministry of Science and Technology
The Science and Technology Ministry's Office functions to advise the Minister on directing and administering the Ministry's activities; assist the Minister in regulating and coordinating activities of the Ministry's units; build, manage and implement the Ministry's working programs and plans, and performs several other tasks.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces Decision No. 550/QD-TCCBKH of May 12, 1997 of the Minister of Science, Technology and Environment (now the Minister of Science and Technology).- (Summary)
