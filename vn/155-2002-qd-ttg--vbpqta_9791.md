# Decision 155/2002/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 155/2002/QD-TTg of November 12, 2002 adjusting the boundaries of Tam Dao National Park
To transfer 1,888 ha of Tam Dao National Park to the People�s Committee of Tuyen Quang province for use management according to the approved planning. This acreage belongs to Son Duong district with 201 ha in Hop Thanh commune, 1, 255 ha in Khang Nhat commune, 185 ha in Hop Hoa commune, 152 ha in Thien Ke commune, and 95 ha in Ninh Lai commune.- (Summary)
