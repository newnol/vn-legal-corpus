# Joint circular 128/2005/TTLT-BNV-BTC-BLDTBXH

JOINT CIRCULAR
Guiding the implementation of a number of articles of the Government’s Decree No. 211/2004/ND-CP of December 22, 2004, detailing the implementation of a number of articles of the Cipher Ordinance regarding uniforms, leave regimes and fulfillment of annual public-labor obligations of persons engaged in cipher work
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
