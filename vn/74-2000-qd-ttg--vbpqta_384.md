# Quyết định 74/2000/QĐ-TTg

THE PRIME MINISTER
DECISION No. 74/2000/QD-TTg of June 27, 2000 on transforming the Hydraulic Construction Company 2 into a joint-stock company
To transform the Hydraulic Construction Company 2, which belongs to Hydraulic Construction Corporation I of The Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development, into the Hydraulic Construction Joint-Stock Company 2 - HSCO2.
Headquarter: Tan Minh commune, Soc Son district, Hanoi city.
Charter capital: 15,500,000,000 VN dong, in which the State�s equities represents 26%, equities sold to laborers of the enterprise: 66%, equities sold to objects outside the enterprise: 8%.
Business lines:
- Construction of hydraulic works: dykes, dams, embankments, reservoirs, water supply and drainage systems, water conduits.
- Construction of hydro-technical works of hydroelectric power stations, construction of electricity transmission lines and transformer stations of up to 35KV.
- Construction of civil works and coverings of industrial works of group B.
- Construction of land roads of up to grade III as well as bridges and sluices thereon.
- Construction and handling of constructions� foundation.
- Repair and upgrading of traffic and hydraulic works.
- Exploitation and transportation of construction materials.
- Importation of and trading in specialized supplies and equipment for hydraulics sector.
- Dealing in hotels and tourism.- (Summary)
