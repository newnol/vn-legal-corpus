# Decree 102/2002/ND-CP

DECREE No. 102/2002/ND-CP of December 11, 2002 prescribing the allowance regime for diseased soldiers or workers and employees leaving their jobs and enjoying the working-capacity loss regime, who are also war invalids or persons enjoying policies like war invalids
The allowance regime prescribed in this Decree includes monthly injury allowance for war invalids, allowance for diseased soldiers and allowance for working capacity loss.- (Summary)
