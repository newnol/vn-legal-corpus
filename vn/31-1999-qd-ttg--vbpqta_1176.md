# Quyết định 31/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.31/1999/QD-TTg of March 1st, 1999 on assigning the task of doctorate training to the Psychology Institute of the National Center for Social Sciences and Humanities
To assign the task of doctorate training to the Psychology Institute of the National Center for Social Sciences and Humanities. The Ministry of Education and Training shall assume the prime responsibility and coordinate with the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment to define the specialties of psychological doctorate training for the Psychology Institute.- (Summary)
