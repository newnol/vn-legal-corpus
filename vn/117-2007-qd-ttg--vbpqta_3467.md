# Quyết định 117/2007/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Amending and supplementing a number of articles of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 118/TTg of February 27, 1996, on supporting people with meritorious services to the revolution to improve their housing conditions, and Article 3 of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 20/2000/QD-TTg of February 3, 2000, supporting persons who joined revolutionary activities before the August 1945 Revolution to improve their housing conditions
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
