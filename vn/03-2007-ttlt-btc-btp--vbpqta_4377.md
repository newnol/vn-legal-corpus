# Thông tư liên tịch 03/2007/TTLT-BTC-BTP

JOINT CIRCULAR
Guiding the regime of collection, remittance, management and use of the fee for registration of, and the charge for supply of information on, security transactions
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces Joint Circular No. 33/2002/TTLT-BTC-BTP of April 12, 2002.
Other relevant matters not mentioned in this Joint Circular shall comply with the guidance in the Finance Ministry’s Circular No. 63/2002/TT-BTC of July 24, 2002, and Circular No. 45/2006/TT-BTC of May 25, 2006.- (Summary)
