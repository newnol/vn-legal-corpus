# Decision 357/2000/QD-UBTDTT

Decision No. 357/2000/QD-UBTDTT of March 17, 2000 on the issuance of the Regulation on examinations for grade promotion from instructors to trainers, from trainers to chief-trainers
The agencies competent to organize the examinations for grade promotion from instructors to trainers are the ministries, the ministerial-level agencies, the agencies attached to the Government and the provincial/municipal People’s Committees. The agencies competent to organize the examinations for grade promotion from trainers to chief-trainers are the ministries, the ministerial-level agencies and the agencies attached to the Government.
The conditions and dossiers for examination sitting, the examination contents and form are specified in the Regulation issued together with this Decision.- (Summary)
