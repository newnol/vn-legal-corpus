# Thông tư 20/2004/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 20/2004/TT-BTC of March 19, 2004 guiding the handling and reorgani-zation of State-owned houses and land in Can Tho city and Hau Giang province
This Circular guides the principles for handling and reorganizing State-owned houses and land in Can Tho city and Hau Giang province; as well as the management and use of proceeds from the sale of houses and transfer of land-use right (or land-lease right transfer). It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
