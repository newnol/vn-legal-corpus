# Decree 28/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 28/2002/ND-CP of March 22, 2002 establishing Luong Bang district township, Kim Dong district, Hung Yen province
To establish Luong Bang district township of Kim Dong district, Hung Yen province on the basis of the whole land area of 755 ha and a population of 9,173 of Luong Bang commune.- (Summary)
