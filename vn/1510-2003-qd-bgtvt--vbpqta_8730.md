# Quyết định 1510/2003/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION No. 1510/2003/QD-BGTVT of May 27, 2003 promulgating the Regulation on responsibilities of, and forms of handling, organizations and individuals committing violations in the management and maintenance of land-road traffic infrastructure
Issued together with this Decision was the Regulation on responsibilities of, and forms of handling, organizations and individuals committing violations in the management and maintenance of land-road traffic infrastructure.
This Regulation determines responsibilities of, and forms of handling, organizations and individuals involved in the management and maintenance of land-road traffic infrastructure belonging to the State-invested system of national highways.
The presidents of the provincial People’s Committees and investors shall base themselves on this Regulation to specify the determination of responsibilities of, and form of handling, organizations and individuals involved in the management and maintenance of systems of local roads, systems of special-use roads and roads built by BOT mode to suit the management characteristics of each locality or organization where special-use roads or BOT roads exist.- (Summary)
