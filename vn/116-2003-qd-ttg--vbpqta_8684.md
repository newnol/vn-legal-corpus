# Quyết định 116/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 116/2003/QD-TTg of June 10, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under the Committee for Nationalities till 2005
The Nationalities and Mountainous Areas Development Construction Company shall be transformed into an independent-accounting member enterprise of the Trade and Construction Corporation under the Ministry of Communications and Transport in 2003.
The Nationalities and Mountainous Areas Development Support Company shall be dissolved in 2003.
The establishment of the Company for Labor Cooperation with Foreign Countries shall comply with the current regulations.- (Summary)
