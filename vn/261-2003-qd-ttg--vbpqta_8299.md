# Quyết định 261/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 261/2003/QD-TTg of December 10, 2003 on the establishment of Bac Giang province's Industrial Parks Management Board
This Decision provides for the establishment of Bac Giang province's Industrial Parks Management Board under the People's Committee of Bac Giang province, which shall manage industrial parks in the locality.
Bac Giang province's Industrial Parks Mana-gement Board shall have to perform tasks and exercise powers related to the management of industrial parks defined in Article 27 of the Regula-tion on Industrial Parks, Export-Processing Zones and Hi-Tech Parks, issued together with the Government's Decree No. 36/CP of April 24, 1997.
Bac Giang province's Industrial Parks Management Board shall have the legal person status, its own seal and accounts.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
