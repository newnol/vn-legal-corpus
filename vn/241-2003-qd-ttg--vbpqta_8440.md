# Quyết định 241/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 241/2003/QD-TTg of November 17, 2003 on the establishment of the Physical Training and Sport Service of Phu Tho province and the renaming of its Culture, Information and Sport Service the Culture and Information Service of Phu Tho province
To establish the Physical Training and Sport Service under the People's Committee of Phu Tho province and rename its Culture, Information and Sport Service the Culture and Information Service of Phu Tho province.
The president of the People's Committee of Phu Tho province shall specify the functions, tasks and organizational structures of the Physical Training and Sport Service and the Culture and Information Service of Phu Tho province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
