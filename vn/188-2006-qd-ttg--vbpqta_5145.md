# Decision 188/2006/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Ratifying the Agreement on the ASEAN Harmonized Electrical and Electronic Equipment (EEE) Regulatory Regime
With this Decision, the Prime Minister assigns the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to notify the ASEAN Secretariat of the date of ratifying, and duplicate and publicize, the above-said Agreement according to regulations. He also requests the Ministry of Science and Technology and concerned ministries and agencies to make necessary preparations for implementation of the Agreement when it comes into force.- (Summary)
