# Quyết định 02/2005/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 02/2005/QD-BNN of January 5, 2005 promulgating the Regulation on management of caged bears
Under the Regulation issued together with this Decision, the deadline for registration of caged bears is February 28, 2005. From March 1, 2005 on, households rearing bears without registration shall be handled according to law provisions.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
