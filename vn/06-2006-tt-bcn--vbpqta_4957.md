# Circular 06/2006/TT-BCN

CIRCULAR
Guiding the implementation of a number of provisions of the Government’s Decree No. 106/2005/ND-CP of August 17, 2005, detailing the guiding the implementation of a number of articles of the Electricity Law regarding the protection of safety of high-voltage power grids
This Circular shall take effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replace the Industry Ministry’s Circular No. 07/2001/TT-BCN of September 11, 2001.- (Summary)
