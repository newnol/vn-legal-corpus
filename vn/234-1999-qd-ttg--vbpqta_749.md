# Quyết định 234/1999/QĐ-TTg

THE PRIME MINISTER
DECISION No.234/1999/QD-TTg of December 22, 1999 on raising pensions for people who had retired before September 1st, 1985
To add 25,000 VNdong (twenty five thousand dong) to the monthly pension of cadres, workers, State employees, armymen and policemen who had retired before September 1st, 1985.- (Summary)
