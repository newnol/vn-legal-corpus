# Quyết định 98/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 98/2003/QD-TTg of May 14, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under the State Bank of Vietnam in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises whose 100% charter capital continues to be held by the State and whose legal person status remains unchanged till 2005: 1 public-utility enterprise and 5 business enterprises.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding at least 51% of their charter capital when shares are sold for the first time: 1 in 2003 and 3 in 2004.
Vietnam Gold, Silver and Gemstones Corporation shall be merged into Vietnam Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development and Mekong River Delta Housing Development Bank in 2003.- (Summary)
