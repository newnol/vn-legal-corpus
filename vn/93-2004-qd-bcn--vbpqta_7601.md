# Decision 93/2004/QD-BCN

DECISION No. 93/2004/QD-BCN of September 17, 2004 transforming Bach Dang Plastic Company into Bach Dang Plastic Joint-Stock Company
Bach Dang Plastic Joint-Stock Company (BADAPLAST) is headquartered at No. 9 Hoang Dieu street, Hong Bang district, Hai Phong city.
Its charter capital is VND 10,030.5 million, 51% of which to be held by the State, 44.94% sold to its laborers, and 4.06% to outsiders.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
