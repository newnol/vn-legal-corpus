# Circular 29/1998/TT-BTC

Circular No.29/1998/TT-BTC of March 11, 1998 guiding the financial mana-gement regime so as to implement the Regulation on the National Reserve Management issued together with Decree No.10-CP of February 24, 1996 of the Government.
This Circular guides the provisions on the drafting of budget estimates, the allocation of the approved budget estimates for the national reserve activities; the sale and purchase of national reserve goods; the management of fees for sale, purchase and preservation of national reserve goods; the allocation of capital construction investment funds; the import and/or export of reserve goods.- (Summary)
