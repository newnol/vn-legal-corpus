# Quyết định 23/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 23/2002/QD-TTg of January 31, 2002 on organization of firework display on the occasion of the Lunar New Year and a number of national anniversaries
The Prime Minister prescribes the organization of firework display on the occasion of the Lunar New Year and a number of national anniversaries; stipulates the State management over the organization of firework production, storage, transportation and display.- (Summary)
