# Nghị định 132/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 132/2003/ND-CP of November 6, 2003 adjusting the administrative boundaries for the establishment of Long Bien and Hoang Mai urban districts, Hanoi city, and establishing their wards
To establish Long Bien urban district on the basis of the whole land areas and populations of Thuong Thanh, Giang Bien, Ngoc Thuy, Viet Hung, Hoi Xa, Gia Thuy, Bo De, Long Bien, Thach Ban and Cu Khoi communes as well as Gia Lam, Duc Giang and Sai Dong townships of Gia Lam rural district.
After the adjustment of administrative boundaries, Long Bien urban district shall have 6,038.24 ha, 170,706 people and 14 dependent administrative units, including Gia Thuy, Ngoc Lam, Bo De, Phuc Dong, Phuc Loi, Thuong Thanh, Giang Bien, Ngoc Thuy, Viet Hung, Long Bien, Thach Ban, Cu Khoi, Duc Giang and Sai Dong wards.
To establish Hoang Mai urban district on the basis of the whole land areas and populations of Dinh Cong, Dai Kim, Hoang Liet, Thinh Liet, Thanh Tri, Vinh Tuy, Linh Nam, Tran Phu and Yen So communes and 55 ha of Tu Hiep commune of Thanh Tri rural district; and the whole land areas and populations of Mai Dong, Tuong Mai, Tan Mai, Giap Bat and Hoang Van Thu wards of Hai Ba Trung urban district.
After the adjustment of administrative boundaries, Hoang Mai urban district shall have 4,104.10 ha, 187,332 people and 14 dependent administrative units, including Hoang Liet, Yen So, Vinh Hung, Dinh Cong, Dai Kim, Thinh Liet, Thanh Tri, Linh Nam, Tran Phu, Mai Dong, Tuong Mai, Tan Mai, Giap Bat and Hoang Van Thu wards.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, annulling all previous regulations contrary thereto.- (Summary)
