# Decree 157/2006/NĐ-CP

DECREE
On the organization and operation of the Foreign Affairs Inspectorates
Foreign Affairs Inspectorates, including the Foreign Affairs Inspectorate under the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Foreign Affairs Inspectorates under provincial/municipal Foreign Services, shall conduct administrative and specialized inspection of external-relation activities within state management scope in accordance with law.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
