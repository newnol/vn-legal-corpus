# Circular 07/2001/TT-BLDTBXH

CIRCULAR No. 07/2001/TT-BLDTBXH of March 13, 2001 guiding the amendment to the division of responsibility for the settlement of regimes for injured people after the reevaluation of their injuries
This Circular guides the amendment to the division of responsibility for the settlement of regimes for injured people after the reevaluation of their injuries prescribed in Item a, Point 4, Section B, Part I of Joint-Circular No. 20/2000/TTLT-BLDTBXH-BYT of September 20, 2000.- (Summary)
