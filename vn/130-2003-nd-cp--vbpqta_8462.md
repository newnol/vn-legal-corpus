# Decree 130/2003/ND-CP

DECREE No. 130/2003/ND-CP of November 5, 2003 establishing Binh Tan and Tan Phu urban districts and their wards; adjusting the administrative boundaries of wards in Tan Binh urban district; and establishing communes and townships in Binh Chanh, Can Gio and Hoc Mon rural districts, Ho Chi Minh city
To establish Binh Tan urban district on the basis of the whole land areas and populations of Binh Hung Hoa, Binh Tri Dong and Tan Tao communes and An Lac township of Binh Chanh rural district.
After the adjustment of administrative boundaries, Binh Tan urban district shall have 5,188.67 ha, 254,635 people and 10 dependent administrative units, including Binh Hung Hoa, Binh Hung Hoa A, Binh Hung Hoa B, Binh Tri Dong, Binh Tri Dong A, Binh Tri Dong B, Tan Tao, Tan Tao A, An Lac and An Lac A wards.
To establish Tan Tuc township of Binh Chanh rural district on the basis of the whole land area of 856.41 ha and the population of 10,939 of Tan Tuc commune.
To establish Tan Phu urban district on the basis of the whole land areas and populations of Wards 16, 17, 18, 19 and 20; 110.23 ha and 23,590 people of Ward 14; and 356.73 ha and 26,414 people of Ward 15 of Tan Binh urban district.
After the adjustment of administrative boundaries, Tan Phu urban district shall have 1,606.98 ha, 310,876 people and 11 dependent administrative units, including Tan Son Nhi, Tay Thanh, Son Ky, Tan Quy, Tan Thanh, Phu Tho Hoa, Phu Thanh, Phu Trung, Hoa Thanh, Hiep Tan and Tan Thoi Hoa wards.
To adjust the administrative boundaries of wards in Tan Binh urban district as follows:
- To transfer 3.22 ha and 758 people of Ward 13 to Ward 11 for management;
- To transfer 1.49 ha and 1,425 people of Ward 13 to Ward 12 for management;
- To transfer 79.75 ha and 26,019 people of Ward 13 to Ward 14 for management;
- To transfer 9.26 ha and 3,201 people of Ward 14 to Ward 13 for management.
To establish Can Thanh township of Can Gio rural district on the basis of the whole land area of 2,408.93 ha and the population of 9,834 of Can Thanh commune.
To establish Trung Chanh commune of Hoc Mon rural district on the basis of a land area of 174.30 ha and a population of 19,715 of Tan Xuan commune.
To establish Xuan Thoi Dong commune of Hoc Mon rural district on the basis of a land area of 308.90 ha and a population of 15,877 of Tan Xuan commune.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, annulling all previous regulations contrary thereto.- (Summary)
