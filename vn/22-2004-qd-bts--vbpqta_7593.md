# Quyết định 22/2004/QĐ-BTS

DECISION No. 22/2004/QD-BTS of September 15, 2004 promulgating branch standards
Under this Decision, the following four branch standards are promulgated:
- 28 TCN 211: 2004: The procedure for seed production of Basa catfish;
- 28 TCN 212: 2004: The procedure for seed production of Basa bocourti;
- 28 TCN 213: 2004: The procedure for intensive grow-out of Basa catfish;
- 28 TCN 214: 2004: The procedure for intensive grow-out of Basa bocourti.
These standards take effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
