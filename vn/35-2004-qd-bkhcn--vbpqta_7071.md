# Quyết định 35/2004/QĐ-BKHCN

DECISION No. 35/2004/QD-BKHCN of October 29, 2004 promulgating Vietnamese standard
This Decision promulgates 01 Vietnamese standard TCVN 7342: 2004: Carbon steel scrap used as charge material for ordinary carbon steel making - Classification and technical requirements.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
