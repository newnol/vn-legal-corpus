# Circular 74/2005/TT-BNV 

CIRCULAR
Guiding a number of articles of the Government's Decrees No. 115/2003/ND-CP, No. 116/2003/ND-CP and No. 117/2003/ND-CP of October 10, 2003, on the regime of reserve employees; the recruitment, employment and management of officials and employees in the State's non-business units; and the recruitment, employment and management of officials and employees in state agencies
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
