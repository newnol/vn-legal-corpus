# Quyết định 104/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 104/2004/QD-BCN of September 30, 2004 transforming the Tourism and Trading Company into Vina Coal Tourism and Trading Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Vina Coal Tourism and Trading Joint-Stock Company, abbreviated to VTTC, has a charter capital of VND 10,465,000,000; of which the State's equities represent 69.16%; equities sold to laborers in the company, 29.89%; and equities sold to other subjects, 0.95%.
Its head office is located at 226 Le Duan Avenue, Dong Da district, Hanoi.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
