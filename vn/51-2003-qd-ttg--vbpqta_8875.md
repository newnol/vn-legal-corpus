# Quyết định 51/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 51/2003/QD-TTg of April 15, 2003 approving the master plan for reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Hoa Binh province in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises with 100% of their charter capital being held by the State: 15.
Enterprise to be equitized with the State holding at least 51% of shares when shares are sold for the first time: 1 in 2003.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding under 50% of shares or holding no shares: 10 in 2003, 5 in 2004 and 4 in 2005.
Enterprises to be assigned or sold: 5 in 2003 and 1 in 2005.
Enterprises to be transferred to Vietnam Forestry Corporation: 2 in 2003.
Enterprises to be dissolved: 3 in 2003.
Enterprise to be bankrupted: 1 in 2003.- (Summary)
