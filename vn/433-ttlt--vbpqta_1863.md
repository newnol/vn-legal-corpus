# Thông tư liên tịch 433-TTLT

Joint Circular No.433-TT/LT of September 25, 1997 guiding the application of the regime of allowances for officials and employees directly engaged in the forcible execution of judgments.
This Circular provides guidance on the objects and allowance levels as well as funding sources for the payment of allowances for the forcible execution of judgments.- (Summary)
