# Directive 40/2007/CT-BNN

DIRECTIVE
Enhancing flood and storm prevention and combat, search and rescue, and mitigation of damage caused by natural disasters
The Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development requests presidents of provincial/municipal People’s Committees to perform several tasks in order to implement the Prime Minister’s Directive No. 08/2007/CT-TTg of April 3, 2007, enhancing flood and storm prevention and combat, search and rescue, and mitigation of damage caused by natural disasters in 2007.- (Summary)
