# Thông tư liên tịch 72/2000/TTLT/BTCCBCP-BTC

JOINT CIRCULAR No.72/2000/TTLT-BTCCBCP-BTC of December 26, 2000 guiding the readjustment of the minimum wage, subsidy and cost-of-living allowance levels for subjects enjoying wages, allowances, subsidies and cost-of-living allowances from the State budget’s funding source
This Circular guides the subjects and scope of application; the method of recalculating wage, allowance, subsidy and cost-of-living allowance levels; funding source, allocation and payment; reports on extra wage, subsidy, allowance and cost-of-living allowance funds in 2001.- (Summary)
