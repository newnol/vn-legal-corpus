# Decree 88/CP

DECREE No.88-CP of August 6, 1997 on the establishment of Hai Duong city in Hai Duong province.
To establish Hai Duong city in Hai Duong province with the total land area of 3,626.8 ha and a population of 143,895 of the provincial capital of Hai Duong.- (Summary)
