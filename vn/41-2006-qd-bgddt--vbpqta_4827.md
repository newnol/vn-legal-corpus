# Decision 41/2006/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION
Promulgating the Regulation on organization of inspections of exams
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces the Education and Training Minister’s Decision No. 20/1998/QD-BGDDT of May 4, 1998, promulgating the Regulation on organization of inspection of exams under the Education and Training Ministry’s regulations.- (Summary)
