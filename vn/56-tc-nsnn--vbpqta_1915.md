# Circular 56/TC-NSNN

THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE
Circular No.56-TC/NSNN of August 21, 1997 guiding the implementation of Decision No.100-QD/TW of June 3, 1995 of the Secretariat of the Party Central Committee on the operating funds for district political fostering centers.
This Circular guides the expenditure, drafting of estimates, the management, allocation and final settlement of the funds.- ( Summary)
