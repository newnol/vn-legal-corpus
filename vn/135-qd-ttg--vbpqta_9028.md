# Decision 135/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 135/QD-TTg of January 30, 2003 approving the general plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Ninh Thuan province in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises where the State continues to hold 100% of their charter capital and their legal person status remains unchanged: 5 public-utility enterprises and 4 enterprises engaged in business activities.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding more than 50% of their charter capital: 2 enterprises in 2003, 1 in 2004 and 3 in 2005.
Enterprises and sections of enterprises to be equitized with the State holding under 50% of their charter capital or holding no shares: 1 enterprise in 2003, 2 in 2004 and 1 in 2005.
Business-contracting enterprises to be assigned or sold: 1 enterprise in 2003 and 1 in 2005.
Enterprise to be transformed into non-business unit with revenues: 1 in 2003.- (Summary)
