# Decision 162/2006/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Approving the Agreement between Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the Government of the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela on visa exemption for holders of diplomatic and official passports
The Prime Minister approves the Agreement, which was signed on May 24, 2006, and assigns the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to carry out related procedures and notify concerned agencies of the effective date of this Agreement.- (Summary)
