# Decision 1290/QD-TTg 

DECISION
Promulgating the national List of projects calling for foreign investment during 2006 – 2010
THE PRIME MINISTER
Pursuant to the December 25, 2001 Law on Organization of the Government;
Pursuant to the Prime Minister's Directive No. 15/2007/CT-TTg of June 22, 2007, on a number of major measures to promote foreign investment in Vietnam;
At the proposal of the Minister of Planning and Investment,
DECIDES:
Article 1.- To promulgate the national list of projects calling for foreign investment during 2006 -2010 (below referred to as the list).
The list may be adjusted in each period to meet development requirements.
Article 2.- Listed projects are important projects in which investment has been approved
Ministries, branches and localities shall coordinate with and assist foreign investors in project formulation preparation and project execution.
Depending on the size and characteristics of each project, the Prime Minister may decide to set up working groups composed of authorized representatives of concerned ministries, branches and localities to promptly and effectively deal with specific issues arising in relation to projects.
Article 3.- Based on this list, concerned ministries, branches and localities shall coordinate with the Ministry of Planning and Investment in drafting project introduction documents to serve as a basis for seeking investment.
Article 4.- The Ministry of Planning and Investment shall act as the general coordinator to review the situation of granting investment certificates to the listed projects; assume the prime responsibility for, and coordinate with the Ministry of Finance and concerned ministries, branches and localities in, studying and proposing mechanisms and policies on investment incentives and support for special projects to the Prime Minister for consideration and decision.
Article 5.- This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO" and replaces the national list of projects calling for foreign direct investment during 2001-2005 promulgated together with the Prime Minister's Decision No. 62/2002/QD-TTg of May 17, 2002.
Article 6.- Ministers, heads of ministerial-level agencies, heads of government-attached agencies, presidents of provincial/municipal People's Committees, management boards of industrial parks, economic zones and hi-tech parks shall implement this Decision.
