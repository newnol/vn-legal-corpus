# Decision 232/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 232/2003/QD-TTg of November 13, 2003 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Institute for Development Strategies
The Institute for Development Strategies is a national institute under the Ministry of Planning and Investment, functioning to research into and propose socio-economic development strategies and plannings of the whole country and territorial regions; organize scientific research, training and fostering of specialized officials and organize activities of consultancy on strategies and plannings according to law provisions.
The Institute is a scientific non-business unit, having the legal person status, its own seal and accounts, and autonomously operating under law provisions.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, annulling the Planning and Investment Minister's Decision No. 18 BKH/TCCB of November 29, 1995 on the functions, tasks, powers and organizational apparatus of the Institute for Development Strategies.- (Summary)
