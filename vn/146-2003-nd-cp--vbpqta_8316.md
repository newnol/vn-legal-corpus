# Nghị định 146/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 146/2003/ND-CP of December 1, 2003 establishing communes of Bac Quang rural district and establishing Quang Binh rural district, Ha Giang province
To establish Tan Bac commune of Bac Quang rural district on the basis of a land area of 5,690 ha and a population of 3,644 of Tan Trinh commune.
To establish Dong Thanh commune of Bac Quang rural district on the basis of a land area of 3,891.10 ha and a population of 2,293 of Dong Yen commune and a land area of 1,478 ha and a population of 443 of Vinh Hao commune.
To establish Quang Binh rural district on the basis of a land area of 52,767 ha and a population of 42,947 of Bac Quang rural district; a land area of 17,013 ha and a population of 5,457 of Hoang Su Phi rural district; and a land area of 7,683 ha and a population of 2,482 of Xin Man rural district.
Quang Binh rural district shall have a land area of 77,463 ha and a population of 50,886 with 15 dependent administrative units.
After the adjustment of its administrative boundaries, Bac Quang rural district shall be left with a land area of 108,359, a population of 102,293, and have 23 dependent administrative units, while these figures for Hoang Su Phi and Xin Man rural districts shall be 62,942, 53,447, and 25; 58,192, 50,748, and 19, respectively.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
