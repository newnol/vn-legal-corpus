# Resolution 01/2007/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
On the Government’s December 2006 regular meeting
On December 26 and 27, 2006, the Government held its regular meeting where it adopted a report on the Government’s direction and administration work in 2006 and its 2007 working program, presented by the Minister-Director of the Government Office; a report on socio-economic situation in December and the whole year of 2006, presented by the Planning and Investment Minister; a report on commercial activities in December and the whole year of 2006, presented by the Trade Minister; a report on the issuance of, and investment with the proceeds from, government bonds, presented by the Finance Minister; a report on the administrative reform in 2006 and key tasks for 2007, presented by the Home Affairs Minister; and a report on the implementation of the Government’s program of action for the enforcement of the Law Against Corruption, presented by the Government Inspector General. The Government also gave its comments on the Bill on Chemical Industry, presented by the Ministry of Industry.
The Government agreed to establish Steering Anti-Corruption Committees in provinces and centrally run cities and assigned the Ministry of Defense to make investment in projects on building border patrol roads under specific mechanisms.- (Summary)
