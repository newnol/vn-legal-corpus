# Thông tư 07/2005/TT-BTP

CIRCULAR
Guiding the implementation of a number of articles of the Government's Decree No. 122/2004/ND-CP of May 18, 2004, defining the functions, tasks, powers and organization of legal sections of ministries, ministerial-level agencies, Government-attached agencies, professional agencies under provincial/municipal People's Committees, and state enterprises
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
