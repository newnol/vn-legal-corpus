# Decision 158/QD-TTg

DECISION No.158/QD-TTg of March 2, 1999 to authorize the Minister-Head of the Government Commission for Organization and Personnel to permit the establishment of societies
To authorize the Minister-Head of the Government Commission for Organization and Personnel to permit the establishment and ratify statutes, of societies and associations of economic organizations operating throughout the country or within a number of provinces and/or centrally-run cities as well as the societies for friendship with the peoples of other countries.
The Minister-Head of the Government Commission for Organization and Personnel shall be answerable to the Prime Minister for permitting the establishment and ratifying the statutes, of the above-said societies and associations in strict compliance with the provisions of law.- (Summary)
