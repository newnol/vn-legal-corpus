# Quyết định 1500/2005/QĐ-CTN

DECISION No. 1500/2005/QD-CTN of December 19, 2005, permitting the relinquishment of Vietnamese nationality
Under this Decision, which takes effect after its signing, 8 citizens residing in the Czech Republic, 19 in the Republic of Singapore, 4 in the Republic of Poland, 17 in the Kingdom of Denmark, 1 in the Federal Republic of Germany, 4 in the Republic of Austria, 2 in Japan, 32 in Slovakia, 38 in China (Hong Kong), 7 in the US, and 1 in the Kingdom of Sweden, are permitted by the State President to relinquish the Vietnamese nationality.- (Summary)
