# Chỉ thị 03/2004/CT-BTS

DIRECTIVE No. 03/2004/CT-BTS of April 12, 2004 enhancing the 2004 national defense work
In order to raise the quality and efficiency of the national defense work of the units, the Minister of Fisheries instructs the units under the Ministry to well perform a number of tasks regarding the national defense work such as to create clear changes in awareness and responsibilities in the tasks of national defense, protection of political security as well as order and safety in the units in the new situation; to raise the quality of the self-defense and mobilized reserve forces; etc.- (Sumamry)
