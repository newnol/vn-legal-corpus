# Nghị định 02/2005/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 02/2005/ND-CP of January 5, 2005 adjusting administrative boundaries and establishing wards of Ba Dinh and Cau Giay urban districts, and establishing Trau Quy township, Gia Lam rural district, Hanoi city
In Ba Dinh urban district:
To transfer 1 ha of land and 200 people of Cong Vi ward to Ngoc Khanh ward for management.
To transfer 5.58 ha of land and 805 people of Ngoc Khanh ward to Cong Vi ward for management.
To establish Lieu Giai ward on the basis of 52.88 ha of land and 13,415 people of Cong Vi ward, and 19.9 ha of land and 5,226 people of Ngoc Ha ward.
To establish Vinh Phuc ward on the basis of 73.72 ha of land and 15,743 people of Cong Vi ward.
In Cau Giay urban district:
To transfer 12.44 ha of land and 1,370 people of Quan Hoa ward to Dich Vong ward for management.
To transfer 48.1 ha of land and 9,898 people of Dich Vong ward to Quan Hoa ward for management.
To establish Dich Vong Hau ward on the basis of 52.88 ha of land and 8,684 people of Quan Hoa ward, and 94.84 ha of land and 11,281 people of Dich Vong ward.
In Gia Lam rural district:
To establish Trau Quy township on the basis of the whole 734.57 ha of land and 21,772 people of Trau Quy commune.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
