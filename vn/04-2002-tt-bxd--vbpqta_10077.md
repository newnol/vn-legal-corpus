# Thông tư 04/2002/TT-BXD

CIRCULAR No. 04/2002/TT-BXD of June 27, 2002 guiding the adjustment of cost estimates of capital construction works
This Circular guides in detail the adjustment of the value of construction and installation cost estimates (adjustment of labor expenditure and adjustment of construction machine expenditure), and the adjustment of other expenditures in the total cost estimates of construction works.- (Summary)
