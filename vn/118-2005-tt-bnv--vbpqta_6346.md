# Thông tư 118/2005/TT-BNV

CIRCULAR
Guiding the calculation of severance allowances for people who leave their jobs due to payroll streamlining under the Government’s Resolutions No. 16/2000/NQ-CP of October 18, 2000, and No. 09/2003/NQ-CP of July 28, 2003
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.” People who left their jobs from October 1, 2005, shall enjoy severance allowances under the guidance in this Circular.- (Summary)
