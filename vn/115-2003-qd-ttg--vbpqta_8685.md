# Quyết định 115/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 115/2003/QD-TTg of June 10, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Ha Giang province in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises where the State continues to hold 100% of their charter capital and their legal person status remains unchanged: 5 public-utility enterprises and 5 business enterprises.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding over 50% of their charter capital: 1 in 2003, 1 in 2004 and 1 in 2005.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding under 50% of their charter capital or holding no shares: 3 in 2003, 1 in 2004 and 6 in 2005.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding at least 51% of their charter capital when shares are sold for the first time: 2 in 2004.
Enterprises to be assigned or sold: 2 in 2003 and 2 in 2004.
Enterprise to be dissolved: 1 in 2003.- (Summary)
