# Quyết định 1834/2003/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION No. 1834/2003/QD-BGTVT of June 24, 2003 temporarily announcing inland waterway passenger transportation routes
This Decision temporarily announces the list of inland waterway passenger transportation routes.
Vietnam Riverway Administration shall certify the registration of operation routes for passenger transportation business for the following subjects:
- Foreign-invested enterprises.
- Across-border transportation.
The provincial/municipal Communications and Transport Services or Communications and Public Works Services shall certify the registration of operation routes for passenger transportation business for organizations and individuals other than the above-mentioned subjects.
Organizations and individuals engaged in the business of inland waterway passenger transportation along fixed routes shall be allowed to register for operation on routes announced by competent agencies. If organizations and individuals wish to operate on routes not yet announced, they must request the provincial/municipal Communications and Transport Services or Communications and Public Works Services to submit the cases to competent agencies for consideration and decision on additional announcement.
Organizations and individuals which are engaged in passenger transportation on announced routes shall be prioritized in the certification of operation routes of their means.
This Decision takes effect as from September 1, 2003.
To annul Section II, Part A of Circular No. 06/2002/TT-BGTVT of April 25, 2002 of the Ministry of Communications and Transport.- (Summary)
