# Quyết định 27/2000/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 27/2000/QD-BCN of April 6, 2000 on the consolidation between Electricians Training School under Power Company No. 1 and Mechanic-Electrician and Power Workers Training School under Electrical Equipment Producing Company into Electricians Training School under the Vietnam Electricity Corporation
Electricians’ Training School (abbreviated as ETS) is an electricians’ training establishment belonging to the State vocational training schools. The school is subject to the State management by the Ministry of Industry, the Ministry of Education and Training and the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs.
Head office: Tan Dan commune, Soc Son district, Hanoi city.
The school’s training scale: from 1,200 to 4,000 students.-
