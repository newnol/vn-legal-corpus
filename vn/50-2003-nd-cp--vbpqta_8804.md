# Nghị định 50/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 50/2003/ND-CP of May 15, 2003 establishing communes of Tan Hung, Thanh Hoa, Duc Hue and Thu Thua districts, Long An province
To establish Vinh Buu commune of Tan Hung district on the basis of a land area of 3,995 ha and a population of 4,155 of Vinh Dai commune, which is now left with a land area of 3,964 ha and a population of 4,240.
To establish Thanh An commune of Thanh Hoa district on the basis of a land area of 6,489 ha and a population of 3,065 of Thuy Tay commune, which is now left with a land area of 3,177 ha and a population of 3,540.
To establish My Binh commune of Duc Hue district on the basis of part of the land areas and population of Binh Thanh and Binh Hoa Hung communes. My Binh commune shall have a land area of 4,169 ha and a population of 3,509. Binh Thanh commune shall be reduced to a land area of 2,589.5 ha and a population of 2,205. Binh Hoa Hung commune shall be narrowed to a land area of 3,090.1 ha and a population of 2,132.
To establish Long Thanh commune of Thu Thua district on the basis of a land area of 4,310.5 ha and a population of 2,871 of Long Thanh commune, which is now left with a land area of 3,307.5 ha and a population of 3,825.
To establish Tan Lap commune of Thu Thua district on the basis of a land area 3,870 ha and a population of 2,696 of Tan Thanh commune, which is reduced to a land area of 3,966 ha and a population of 3,399.- (Summary)
