# Thông tư 55/2004/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 55/2004/TT-BTC of June 10, 2004 guiding the drafting of the 2005 State budget estimates
The Finance Ministry issued this Circular to guide the appraisal of the implementation of the 2004 State budget revenue and expenditure tasks, which shall serve as basis for the drafting of the 2005 State budget revenues and expenditure estimates; the objectives, requirements, principal contents of the drafting.
The ministries and State agencies shall, according to their assigned functions and tasks, have to elaborate socio-economic targets in domains under their management and promptly notify them to the Finance Ministry, other ministries, central agencies and localities before July 1, 2004 to serve as basis for the drafting of the 2005 State budget estimates.
This Circular also guides the drafting of 2005 local budget estimates on the basis of the revenue and expenditure tasks stably assigned to all local administrations.
This Circular applies to the making of the 2005 State budget estimates report according to the provisions of the State Budget Law.- (Summary)
