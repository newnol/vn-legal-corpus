# Quyết định 126/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 126/2004/QD-TTg of July 13, 2004 establishing the Foreign Language College under Hue University
To establish the Foreign Language College, which is a tertiary education establishment under Hue University and has the legal person status, its own seal and bank accounts.
It is tasked to train foreign-language bachelors; teach foreign languages for member colleges of Hue University; and conduct linguistics research to meet socio-economic development requirements.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
