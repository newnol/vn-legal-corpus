# Circular 108/1999/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 108/1999/TT-BTC of September 4, 1999 providing additional guidance on a number of points on working-trip allowances regime for State officials and employees who travel abroad on short working missions
This Circular provides additional guidance on a number of points of Circular No. 45/1999/TT-BTC of May 4, 1999 of the Ministry of Finance stipulating the regime of working-trip allowances for State officials and employees who travel abroad on short working missions.- (Summary)
