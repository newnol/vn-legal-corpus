# Quyết định 65/2004/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 65/2004/QD-BTC of August 10, 2004 annulling legal documents issued by the Ministry of Finance
Under this Decision, the following documents are annulled:
- Circular No. 48/2000/TT-BTC of May 29, 2000 guiding the reallocation, management and use of enterprise income tax amounts actually remitted into the State budget for investment in the printed press.
- Official Dispatch No. 10784 TC/DT of November 9, 2001 guiding the mutual ceasing of revenues and expenditures of Tam Diep cement project.
- Official Dispatch No. 1323 TC/TCT of February 14, 2003 guiding the mutual ceasing of revenues and expenditures of import tax on raw materials imported for investment in Phu My fertilizer plant project.
- Official Dispatch No. 2362 TC/TCT of June 30, 1998 guiding the reallocation of income tax to the Radio Voice of Vietnam.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
