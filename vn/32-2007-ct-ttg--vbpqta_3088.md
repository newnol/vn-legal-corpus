# Chỉ thị 32/2007/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
Reviewing the implementation of the Government’s Decree No. 12/2002/ND-CP of January 22, 2002, on topographical and cartographical activities
The Prime Minister instructs concerned ministries, branches and localities to perform several tasks in order to address weaknesses and problems in the implementation of Decree No. 12/2002/ND-CP for the elaboration of a Bill on Topography and Cartography.- (Summary)
