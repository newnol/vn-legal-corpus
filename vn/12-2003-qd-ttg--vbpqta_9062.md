# Quyết định 12/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 12/2003/QD-TTg of January 17, 2003 on the functions, tasks and composition of the Council for promotion of small- and medium-sized enterprise development
The Council for promotion of small- and medium-sized enterprise development functions to advise the Prime Minister on mechanisms and policies to encourage the development of small- and medium-sized enterprises nationwide, concretely:
- The orientations, strategies and plans for the development of small-and medium sized enterprises in line with the national orientations and strategies on socio-economic development.
- Proposing amendments and supplements and perfecting the mechanisms and policies for the development of small- and medium-sized enterprises.
- The measures, solutions and programs to assist small- and medium-sized enterprises in raising their capability and competitiveness.
- Other tasks related to the development of small- and medium-sized enterprises, assigned by the Prime Minister.
The Council shall be headed by Mr. Vo Hong Phuc, Minister of Planning and Investment.
The Council’s standing body is the Department for Small- and Medium-sized Enterprise Development.- (Summary)
