# Decision 79/2006/QĐ-BVHTT

DECISION
On ranking a national relic
Under this Decision, which shall take effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” the historical relic of House of worship for the founders of plated-paper making in Nghia Do ward, Cau Giay district, Hanoi city, is ranked as a national relic.- (Summary)
