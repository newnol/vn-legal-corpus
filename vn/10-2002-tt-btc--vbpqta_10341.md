# Circular 10/2002/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 10/2002/TT-BTC of January 30, 2002 guiding the allocation, use and final settlement of funding for the election of deputies to the XI th National Assembly
The funding for the election of deputies to the XI th National Assembly shall be allocated by the central budget.
The Circular guides the spending contents, the distribution, allocation and final settlement of the above-said funding source.- (Summary)
