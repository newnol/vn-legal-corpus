# Quyết định 148/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 148/2002/QD-TTg of October 30, 2002 establishing the Institute of Technology and Environment under the National Center for Natural Sciences and Technologies
To establish the Institute of Technology and Environment under the National Center for Natural Sciences and Technologies. The Institute has its own seal and accounts, and its office headquartered in Hanoi.
The director of the National Center for Natural Sciences and Technologies shall define the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of the Institute.- (Summary)
