# Quyết định 660/2003/QĐ-BTM

DECISION No. 660/2003/QD-BTM of June 4, 2003 prohibiting the circulation and trading of puffer and products thereof on the market
To strictly prohibit acts of circulating, processing or trading puffer and products thereof in any form on the market.
The violating organizations and individuals shall be administratively sanctioned for trading or transporting goods banned from circulation, or examined for penal liability. Material evidences used in violations must be confiscated and/or destroyed.
This Decision takes effect as from June 20, 2003.- (Summary)
