# Quyết định 03/2004/QĐ-BTS

Decision No. 03/2004/QD-BTS of March 1, 2004 promulgating branch standards
Promulgated together with this Decision are the following eight branch standards:
1. 28 TCN194:2004: Residues of metabolites of nitrofurans group in fishery products Ố Method for quantitative analysis by LC/MS/MS
2. 28 TCN195:2004: Organophosphorus pesticide residues in fish and fishery products Ố Method for quantitative analysis by Gas Chromatography
3. 28 TCN196:2004: Sulfonamit in fishery products Ố Method for quantitative analysis by High Performance Liquid Chromatography
4. 28 TCN197:2004: Penicillin in fishery products Ố Method for quantitative analysis by High Performance Liquid Chromatography
5. 28 TCN198:2004: Histamin in fishery products Ố Method for quantitative analysis by High Performance Liquid Chromatography
6. 28 TCN199:2004: Salmonella in fishery products Ố Method for quantitative analysis by Polymerase Chain Reaction
7. 28 TCN200:2004: Vibrio cholerae in fishery products Ố Method for quantitative analysis
8. 28 TCN201:2004: Frozen fishery products Ố Scallop meat
These standards come into force 15 days after their publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
