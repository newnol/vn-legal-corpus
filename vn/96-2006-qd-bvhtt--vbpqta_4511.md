# Decision 96/2006/QĐ-BVHTT

DECISION
On ranking a national relic
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” the archaeological-architectural-artistic relic of Bai Ong archaeological site, Bai Lang archaeological site, Tien Hien mausoleum, Xom Cam well, first bird-nest hunters temple, Hai Tang pagoda and Ong Ngu (whale) tomb in Tan Hiep commune, Hoi An provincial town, Quang Nam province, is ranked as a national relic.- (Summary)
