# Nghị định 87/1999/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 87/1999/ND-CP of August 31, 1999 on the dissolution of Song Boi farm township in Lac Thuy district, Hoa Binh province
To dissolve Song Boi farm township of Lac Thuy district (Hoa Binh province) whose inhabitants currently living in Dong Tam, Lac Long, Co Nghia, Lien Hoa, Phu Lao, Phu Thanh and Hung Thi communes are now subject to the management by such communes.- (Summary)
