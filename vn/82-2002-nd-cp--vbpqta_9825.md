# Nghị định 82/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 82/2002/ND-CP of October 17, 2002 establishing Lang Son city of Lang Son province
To establish Lang Son city of Lang Son province on the basis of the whole land area and population of Lang Son provincial capital.
Lang Son city shall have a land area of 7,769.35 ha and a population of 106,110, comprising 8 administrative units being 5 wards and 3 communes.- (Summary)
