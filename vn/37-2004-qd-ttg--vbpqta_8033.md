# Quyết định 37/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 37/2004/QD-TTg of March 17, 2004 establishing the Service for External Affairs of Cao Bang province
To establish the Service for External Affairs under the People's Committee of Cao Bang province on the basis of the Section for External and Frontier Issues of the Office of the People's Council and People's Committee of Cao Bang province.
The president of the People's Committee of Cao Bang province shall define the Service's functions, tasks and organizational structure.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
