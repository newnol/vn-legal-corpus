# Nghị định 19/1998/NĐ-CP

DECREE No.19/1998/ND-CP of March 26, 1998 on the establishment of Giao Hung commune of Giao Thuy district, Nam Dinh province.
To establish Giao Hung commune of Giao Thuy district, Nam Dinh province on the basis of a land area of 751.7 hectares and a population of 1.445 of the Con Ngan alluvial deposit.- (Summary)
