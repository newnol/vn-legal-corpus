# Quyết định 01/2001/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION No. 01/2001/QD-BGDDT of January 29, 2001 on university and college entrance examinations, applicable to form of on-the-job training by informal education mode
This Decision stipulates the university and college entrance examinations, applicable to the form of on-the-sport training (the former in-service training system) by the informal education mode. The enrollment applicable to forms of correspondence and self-study with guidance is prescribed in other documents of the Ministry of Education and Training.
This Decision specifies the entrance examinations; the conditions for sitting at examinations; subjects entitled to the consideration for direct enrollment in the on-the-job training form; dossiers and procedures for registration for sitting at examinations, sending and receipt of notices on sitting at examinations.- (Summary)
