# Quyết định 45/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 45/2004/QD-BCN of June 10, 2004 amending a number of contents in the Industry Minister's Decision No. 219/2003/QD-BCN of December 16, 2003 on transformation of Vietnam Computer Company 1 into Vietnam Computer Joint Stock Company 1
Under this Decision, Vietnam Computer Joint Stock Company 1 is renamed Vietnam Computer and Communication Joint Stock Company (VIETCOM).
This Decision amends Clause 1, Article 1 on charter capital structure and Clause 3, Article 1 on preferential shares to be sold to laborers in the company.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
