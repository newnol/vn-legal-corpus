# Quyết định 25/2004/QĐ-BTNMT

DECISION No. 25/2004/QD-BTNMT of November 1, 2004 promulgating the plan on implementation of the Land Law
The plan issued together with this Decision covers the grant of land-use right certificates; land use planning; review of the land-use situation and recovery of unused land and land used in contravention of regulations; and examination, inspection and reporting regimes.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
