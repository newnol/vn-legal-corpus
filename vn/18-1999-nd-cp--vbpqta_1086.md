# Nghị định 18/1999/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 18/1999/ND-CP of April 10, 1999 on the readjustment of administrative boundaries and the establishment of communes and wards of the districts of Dai Tu and Pho Yen and Song Cong provincial town, Thai Nguyen province
To establish Tan Linh commune of Dai Tu district on the basis of a land area of 2,344 ha and a population of 4,876 of Phuc Linh commune.
To establish Pho Co ward of Song Cong provincial town on the basis of a land area of 465 ha and a population of 4,898 of Cai Dan commune.
To establish Vinh Son commune of Song Cong provincial town on the basis of a land area of 410 ha and a population of 904 of Ba Xuyen commune together with a land area of 832 ha and a population of 1,119 of Cai Dan commune.
To establish Cai Dan ward of Song Cong provincial town on the basis of a land area of 533 ha and a population of 4,336 of Cai Dan commune.
To transfer Binh Son commune of Pho Yen district to Song Cong provincial town for the latter�s management.- (Summary)
