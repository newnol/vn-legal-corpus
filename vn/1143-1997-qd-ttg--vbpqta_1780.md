# Quyết định 1143/1997/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.1143/1997/QD-TTg of December 29, 1997 to upgrade Financial-Accounting Intermediate School No.III into the Financial-Accounting College.
The Financial- Accounting College is attached to the Ministry of Finance and subject to the State management over education and training by the Ministry of Education and Training. The college has the task of training and fostering personnel of college level; and conducting scientific research to meet the socio-economic development requirements.- (Summary)
