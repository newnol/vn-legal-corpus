# Quyết định 150/1999/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 150/1999/QD-TTg of July 8, 1999 on the general asset inventory and re-determination of asset value of the State enterprises
The Prime Ministers decides:
- To organize a general inventory of assets being managed and used by the State enterprises, including fixed assets, current assets and other kinds of asset; to determine capital sources available at zero hour of January 1st, 2000.
- To re-determine the value of a number of fixed assets belonging to a number of branches and enterprises, of which the book-accounting prices vary with the actual prices.- (Summary)
