# Nghị định 75/1999/NĐ-CP

Decree No. 75/1999/ND-CP of August 20, 1999 on the establishment of Thuan An district township in Phu Vang district, Thua Thien Hue province
To establish Thuan An district township of Phu Vang district, Thua Thien Hue province, on the basis of the whole land area of 1,650 ha and a population of 17,516 of two communes of Thuan An and Phu Yen.- (Summary)
