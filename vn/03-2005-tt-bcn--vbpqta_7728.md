# Circular 03/2005/TT-BCN

CIRCULAR
Guiding the implementation of a number of contents of the Government's Decree No. 134/2004/ND-CP of June 9, 2004, on encouraging the development of rural industry
This Circular guides the performance of industrial promotion activities, a number of contents of industrial promotion policies, and planning work in industrial promotion activities.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
