# Quyết định 110/2001/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 110/2001/QD-BNN of November 23, 2001 promulgating norms of allowances for officials and field allowances for the provincial-level officials under the Project on protection and development of coastal submerged land areas in Southern Vietnam
This Decision prescribes the norms of allowances for officials and field allowances from the DANIDA’s non-refundable aid source for the provincial-level officials under the Project on protection and development of coastal submerged land areas in Southern Vietnam.- (Summary)
