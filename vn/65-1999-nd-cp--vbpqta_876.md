# Decree 65/1999/ND-CP

Decree No. 65/1999/ND-CP of August 5, 1999 on the administrative boundary readjustment for establishment of new communes and district townships in Quan Son, Muong Lat, Nhu Xuan and Tho Xuan districts of Thanh Hoa province
To establish Na Meo commune of Quan Son district on the basis of a land area of 12,195 hectares and a population of 2,605 of Son Thuy commune.
To establish Muong Min commune of Quan Son district on the basis of a land area of 8,190 hectares and a population of 2,115 of Son Dien commune.
To establish Muong Ly commune of Muong Lat district on the basis of a land area of 12,009 hectares and a population of 3,874 of Trung Ly commune.
To establish Thanh Hoa commune of Nhu Xuan district on the basis of a land area of 9,187 hectares and a population of 1,813 of Thanh Phong commune.
To dissolve Sao Vang agricultural farm township of Tho Xuan district and establish Sao Vang township on the basis of a population of 4,950 currently under the management of Sao Vang agricultural farm township, with a land area of 102.5 hectares of Tho Lam commune and 186.5 hectares of Xuan Thang commune.- (Summary)
