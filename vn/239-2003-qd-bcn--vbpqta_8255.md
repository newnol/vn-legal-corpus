# Quyết định 239/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 239/2003/QD-BCN of December 29, 2003 transforming Chemical Construction and Installation Mechanical Factory under Chemical Installation Company into Chemical Construction and Installation Mechanical Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, the Chemical Construction and Installation Mechanical Factory under Chemical Installation Company (an independent cost-accounting enterprise of Vietnam Industrial Construction Corporation) shall be transformed into a joint-stock company with its English transaction name as Chemical Construction and Installation Mechanical Joint-Stock Company, abbreviated to CCIM.
The joint-stock company shall have a charter capital of VND 3,500,000,000; with the State’s equities representing 51% and equities sold to laborers of the company, 49%.
It is headquartered at Km 5, Lam San zone, So Dau ward, Hong Bang urban district, Hai Phong city.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
