# Quyết định 13/2005/QĐ-BKHCN

DECISION
Promulgating the Organizational and Operational Charter of the Goods Quality Management Department under the General Department of Standardization, Metrology and Quality Control
According to the Charter, issued together with this Decision, the Goods Quality Management Department is an agency attached to the General Department of Standardization, Metrology and Quality Control and tasked to assist the General Director in performing the management of goods and service quality as well as goods labeling.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
