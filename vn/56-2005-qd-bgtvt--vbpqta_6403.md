# Quyết định 56/2005/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION
On the organization and operation of the Vietnam Maritime Search and Rescue Coordination Center
Under this Decision, the Vietnam Maritime Search and Rescue Coordination Center, Vietnam MRCC, is a non-business unit under the Vietnam Maritime Administration and functions to search and rescue people and vessels at sea.
This Decision takes effect as from January 1, 2006, and replaces the Transport Minister’s Decision No. 2628/QD-TCCB-LD of October 2, 1996.- (Summary)
