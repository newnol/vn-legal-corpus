# Circular 3/1999/TT-TCHQ

Circular No. 3/1999/TT-TCHQ of June 16, 1999 providing professional guidance on the legal work of the General Department of Customs
This Circular provides detailed guidance on a number of points on the legal work of the customs service, concretely on the legislation; the review and systemization of legal documents; the law popularization and education as well as the inspection of law enforcement; the guidance and inspection of the handling of law violations as well as the settlement of complaints about decisions on sanctioning administrative violations in the customs domain.- (Summary)
