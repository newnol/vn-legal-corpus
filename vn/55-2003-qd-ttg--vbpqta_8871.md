# Quyết định 55/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 55/2003/QD-TTg of April 15, 2003 approving the master plan for reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under the People’s Committee of Quang Ninh province in the 2003-2005 period
Enterprises with 100% of their charter capital being held by the State: 9 public-utility enterprises and 6 business enterprises to be kept intact.
To reorganize 10 forestry farms according to the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 187/1999/QD-TTg of September 16, 1999 on renewing the organization and management mechanisms of State-run forestry farms.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding dominant shares: 10 in 2003, 2 in 2004 and 3 in 2005.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding at least 51% of their charter capital when shares are sold for the first time: 1 in 2003 and 4 in 2004.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding shares at low levels or holding no shares: 10 in 2003, 4 in 2004 and 2 in 2005.
Enterprises to be assigned or sold: 2.
Enterprise to be transferred to other unit: 1.
Enterprise to be dissolved: 1.- (Summary)
