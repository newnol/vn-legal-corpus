# Decision 134/2002/QD-BTC

DECISION No. 134/2002/QD-BTC of October 30, 2002 on temporarily ceasing the collection of Chuong Duong bridge toll for motorized two-wheelers, motorized three-wheelers, motorcycles and motor tricycles
As from October 30, 2002, to temporarily cease the collection of bridge toll for motorized two-wheelers, motorized three-wheelers, motorcycles and motor tricycles passing the Chuong Duong bridge toll booth until a new decision is issued.- (Summary)
