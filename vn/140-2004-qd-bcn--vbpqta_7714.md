# Quyết định 140/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 140/2004/QD-BCN of November 22, 2004 on transforming the Electrical Equipment Production Company into Electrical Equipment Manufacturing Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, the Electrical Equipment Manufacturing Joint-Stock Company, EEMC, has a charter capital of VND 50,000,000,000, of which the State's equities represent 51%, equities sold to laborers of the company, 45.29% and equities sold to other subjects, 3.71%.
Its head - office is located at Block 3A, Dong Anh township, Dong Anh district, Hanoi.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
