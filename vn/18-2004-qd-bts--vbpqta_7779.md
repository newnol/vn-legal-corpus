# Quyết định 18/2004/QĐ-BTS

DECISION No. 18/2004/QD-BTS of July 30, 2004 promulgating branch's standards
With this Decision, the Minister of Fisheries promulgated 3 branch's standards:
28TCN 208:2004: Material for fishing net - Used qualitative norms of netting yarns
28TCN 209:2004: Material for fishing net - Used qualitative norms of synthetic fiber ropes
28TCN 210:2004: Material for fishing net - Used qualitative norms of netting.
Establishments engaged in the production and/or trading of materials for fishing net shall have to apply the above-stated standards as from July 1, 2005.- (Summary)
