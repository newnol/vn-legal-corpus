# Quyết định 82/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 82/2004/QD-BCN of August 24, 2004 transforming Thanh Hoa Secpentin and Chemicals Company into Thanh Hoa Secpentin and Fertilizer Joint Stock Company
To transform Thanh Hoa Secpentin and Chemicals Company, an independent cost-accounting member enterprise of Vietnam Chemicals Corporation, into Thanh Hoa Secpentin and Fertilizer Joint Stock Company (SEPCO).
Its head office is located in Hoang Giang commune, Nong Cong district, Thanh Hoa province.
Its charter capital is VND 3,811.3 million, to be wholly sold to its laborers.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
