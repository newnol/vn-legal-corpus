# Directive 10/2007/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On implementation of the Law on Gender Equality
The Prime Minister requests ministries, ministerial-level agencies, government-attached agencies and provincial/municipal People’s Committees to perform several tasks in order to make cadres, civil servants and people be properly aware of the Law on Gender Equality and prepare conditions for implementation of the Law.
This Directive takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
