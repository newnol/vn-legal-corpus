# Nghị quyết 09/1999/NQ-CP

Resolution No. 09/1999/NQ-CP of August 2, 1999 on its July 1999 regular meeting
On July 28, 1999, the Government held its July 1999 regular meeting to discuss and resolve the following issues:
- The Minister of Construction proposed the plan "Viewpoint on and solutions to unsolved matters related to houses and land managed and allocated by the State for use during the former process of implementing the socialist transformation policies". The Government assigned the Ministry of Construction to base itself on the cabinet members� comments to improve such plan before the Government submits it to the National Assembly Standing Committee for consideration and resolution thereon.
- The Government adopted the report on the socio-economic situation in July as well as in the first seven months of 1999, presented by the Minister of Planning and Investment. It requested the leading officials of the ministries, branches and localities to closely grasp the actual situation and effectively implement the Government�s Resolution No.08/1999/NQ-CP of July 9, 1999 on the measures to administer the performance of the tasks in the last six months of 1999, with special attention being paid to the compilation of documents detailing this Resolution and the organization of implementation.
- The Minister of Public Security, who is Vice Chairman of the National Committee for Drug Prevention and Combat, presented a report reviewing the drug prevention and combat activities in the period from November 30, 1998 to July 20, 1999. The Government also adopted anti-drug measures proposed by the National Committee for Drug Prevention and Combat. It assigned the National Committee for Drug Prevention and Combat to direct and urge the ministries, branches and localities to keep on applying strong, comprehensive and resolute measures with a view to mobilizing the entire population to take part in the drug prevention and combat, considering this a mass revolutionary movement; and launch a campaign against this social vice in the last six months of 1999 and several subsequent years.
- The meeting heard the Minister of Trade reporting the result of the Vietnam-US trade agreement negotiations.
- It heard the State Inspectorate reporting the work of inspection and settlement of complaints and denunciations in the second quarter of 1999.- (Summary)
