# Decision 15/2006/QĐ-BCN

DECISION
Promulgating leather-footwear branch standards
Promulgated together with this Decision are three leather-footwear branch standards: 24 TCN 01: 2006 “Soft cowhide for producing handbags and wallets - Technical requirements and testing methods”; 24 TCN 02: 2006 “Labor standards applicable to Vietnam’s leather-footwear industry”; and 24 TCN 03: 2006 “Shoe cap leather - Classification based on use area”.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
