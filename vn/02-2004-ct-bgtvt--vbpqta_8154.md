# Directive 02/2004/CT-BGTVT

DIRECTIVE No. 02/2004/CT-BGTVT of February 3, 2004 on enhancing the work of examination and inspection in the communications and transport service
The Minister of Communications and Transport issued this Directive requesting the heads of agencies and units under the Ministry to carry out some specific tasks in order to overcome existing problems in the work of examination and inspection in the communications and transport service.- (Summary)
