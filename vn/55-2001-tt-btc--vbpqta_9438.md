# Circular 55/2001/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 55/2001/TT-BTC of July 6, 2001 supplementing and amending Circular No. 110/2000/TT-BTC of November 14, 2000 which guides the management and use of State enterprises� capital for investment and construction
To amend and supplement the fourth dash (-), Point 3.2, Item 3 - the competence to decide investment and realize investment for projects using State enterprises� development investment capital, Part II, more concretely:
"- For Group-B investment projects of the public-utility State enterprises, which have been established under the decisions of ministries, branches or provincial/municipal People�s Committees, the agencies deciding their establishment shall decide the investment. For the public-utility State enterprises, which have been established under the Prime Minister�s decisions, the immediate superior (or specialized) State management bodies shall decide the investment".
To add a number of points to Point 3.2, Item 3 of Part II, more concretely:
"The Group-C investment projects shall be decided by enterprises".- (Summary)
