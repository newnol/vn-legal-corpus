# Quyết định 25/2004/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 25/2004/QD-BNN of July 30, 2004 defining the functions, tasks, powers and organizational apparatus of Region-V Plant Quarantine Sub-Department
Region-V Plant Quarantine Sub-Department is an unit under the Plant Protection Department, functioning to perform the State management over plant quarantine, having the legal person status, its own seal, operation funding and bank accounts.
The Sub-Department is headquartered at 149 Ho Dac Di street, Dong Da district, Ha noi city, and operates in Hanoi city and the provinces of Bac Ninh, Bac Giang, Vinh Phuc, Phu Tho, Ha Tay, Ninh Binh, Nam Dinh, Ha Nam, Hoa Binh and Son La.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
