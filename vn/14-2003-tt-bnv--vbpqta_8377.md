# Circular 14/2003/TT-BNV

CIRCULAR No. 14/2003/TT-BNV of April 16, 2003 guiding the application of minimum salary level in the assignment of payroll package as well as administrative management fundings, administrative streamlining policies and financial regimes applicable to non-business units with revenues
This Circular guides the adjustment of minimum salaries for:
- Expansion of the experimental assignment of payroll package and administrative management funding under the Prime Minister's Decision No. 192/2001/QD-TTg of December 17, 2001.
- Performance of administrative streamlining policies in administrative agencies and non-business units under the Government's Resolution No. 16/2000/NQ-CP of October 18, 2000.
- Application of financial regimes to non-business units with revenues under the Government's Decree No. 10/2002/ND-CP of January 16, 2002.- (Summary)
