# Quyết định 130/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 130/2004/QD-BCN of November 12, 2004 transforming Thai Nguyen Non-Ferrous Metal Company into Thai Nguyen Non-Ferrous Metal Limited Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Thai Nguyen Non-Ferrous Metal Limited Company (TMC. Co.Ltd.) is headquartered in Phu Xa ward, Thai Nguyen city, Thai Nguyen province, and has a charter capital of VND 43,376,037,135 at the time of transformation.- (Summary)
