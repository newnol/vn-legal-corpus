# Thông tư 18/2003/TT-BLĐTBXH

CIRCULAR No. 18/2003/TT-BLDTBXH of July 15, 2003 guiding the allowance regime for people with meritorious services to the revolution
This Circular guides subjects and conditions for enjoying allowances; the allowance levels; the procedures for the elaboration and management of dossiers of certification of the allowance regimes for people with meritorious services to the revolution.- (Summary)
