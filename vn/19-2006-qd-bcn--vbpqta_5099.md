# Decision 19/2006/QĐ-BCN

DECISION
Promulgating electric equipment regulations
This Decision promulgates electric equipment regulations, including general provisions, coded 11 TCN-18-2006 (Part I); system of electric conducive lines, coded 11 TCN-19-2006 (Part II); distribution equipment and transformer stations, coded 11 TCN-20-2006 (Part III); and protection and automation, coded 11 TCN-21-2006 (Part IV).
It shall take effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replace Decision No. 507/DL/KT of December 22, 1984, of the Ministry of Electricity (now the Ministry of Industry).- (Summary)
