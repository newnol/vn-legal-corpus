# Decision 58/2003/QD-BNN

DECISION No. 58/2003/QD-BNN of May 5, 2003 promulgating the Regulation on reception and processing of information and documents; elaboration, comment contribution, verification, evaluation, submission for signing, distribution, management and archival of the Agriculture and Rural Development Ministry's documents
Issued together with this Decision is the Regulation on reception and processing of information and documents; elaboration, comment contribution, verification, evaluation, submission for signing, distribution, management and archival of the Agriculture and Rural Development Ministry's documents.
This Regulation prescribes the process and responsibilities for the reception and processing of information and inbound documents; the elaboration of documents, comment contribution, verification, evaluation, submission for signing, distribution, management and archival of the Ministry's documents.
This Regulation applies to the Ministry's departments, Office and Inspectorate.- (Summary)
