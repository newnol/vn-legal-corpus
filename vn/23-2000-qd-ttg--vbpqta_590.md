# Decision 23/2000/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 23/2000/QD-TTg of February 18, 2000 on the conversion of Housing Investment and Trading Company under the Saigon Real Estate Corporation into a joint stock company
To convert the State-run "Housing Investment and Trading Company" into Investment and Trading of Real Estate Joint Stock Company � INTRESCO.
- Head office: 18 Nguyen Binh Khiem, District 1, Ho Chi Minh city
- Charter capital: Vietnam dong 25,000,000,000; of which the State shares represent 30%, shares sold to the company�s officials and employees account for 20% and those sold to shareholders outside the company make up 50%.
- Business lines:
+ Investing and trading in real estates
+ Providing legal services regarding land and housing; consultancy in elaboration of investment projects and planning designs
+ Surveying and designing small and medium construction projects
+ Producing and trading in construction materials, making interior decoration
+ Constructing civil, industrial and interior decorating works
+ Constructing urban technical infrastructure works, industrial parks; ground leveling, building bridges and roads, and water drainage system
+ Directly exporting and importing construction supplies, machinery and equipment.- (Summary)
