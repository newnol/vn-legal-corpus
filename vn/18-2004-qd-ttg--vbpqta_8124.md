# Quyết định 18/2004/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 18/2004/QD-TTg of February 12, 2004 on the list of State secrets classified as top secret in the domains falling under the Justice Ministry’s State management
This Decision promulgates the list of State secrets classified as top secret in the domains falling under the Justice Ministry’s State management, replacing the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 850/TTg of December 27, 1995 on the list of State secrets in the justice service. It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
