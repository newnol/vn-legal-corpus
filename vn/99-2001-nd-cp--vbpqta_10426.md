# Nghị định 99/2001/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 99/2001/ND-CP of December 25, 2001 establishing wards and adjusting the administrative boundaries of wards of Phan Rang-Thap Cham provincial capital, Ninh Thuan province
To establish Dong Hai, My Dong and Dai Son wards.
To adjust the administrative boundaries of Bao An, Phuoc My, Phu Ha, Thanh Son, Tan Tai and Kinh Dinh wards.
After the establishment and boundary adjustment of their wards and communes, Phan Rang-Thap Cham provincial capital shall have 15 administrative units, comprising the wards of Do Vinh, Bao An, Phuong My, Phu Ha, Thanh Son, My Huong, Dao Long, Tan Tai, Kinh Dinh, My Dong, Dai Son and Dong Hai, and communes of Thanh Hai, Van Hai and My Van.- (Summary)
