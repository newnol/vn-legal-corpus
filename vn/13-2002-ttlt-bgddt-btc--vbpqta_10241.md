# Joint circular 13/2002/TTLT/BGDDT-BTC

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 13/2002/TTLT-BGDDT-BTC of March 28, 2002 guiding the implementation of the Prime Minister's Decision No. 194/2001/QD-TTg of December 21, 2001 on the adjustment of policy scholarship and social allowances for ethnic minority pupils and students studying at public training schools prescribed in Decision No. 1121/1997/QD-TTg of December 23, 1997 of the Prime Minister
This Circular guides subjects entitled to the adjustment of policy scholarship and social allowances; the adjustment level and implementation funding.- (Summary)
