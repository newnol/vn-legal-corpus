# Nghị định 54/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 54/2002/ND-CP of May 13, 2002 establishing communes of Chu Prong and Ayun Pa districts, and transferring Ia Kenh commune of Ia Grai district to Pleiku provincial city, Gia Lai province
To establish Ia Ga commune of Chu Prong district with a land area of 12,277 ha and a population of 2,100.
To establish Ia Pior commune of Chu Prong district with a land area of 9,629 ha and a population of 3,908.
To establish Ia Drang commune of Chu Prong district with a land area of 4,105 ha and a population of 5,581.
To establish Ia Peng of Ayun Pa district with a land area of 1,800 ha and a population of 2,982.
To establish Ia Ke commune of Ayun Pa district with a land area of 5,928 ha and a population of 8,579.
To transfer Ia Kenh commune of Ia Grai district to Pleiku provincial city for management.- (Summary)
