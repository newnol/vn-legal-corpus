# Nghị định 155/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 155/2003/ND-CP of December 9, 2003 on the establishment of An Khe provincial town, Dak Po rural district and Dak Po commune of Dak Po rural district, Gia Lai province
To establish An Khe provincial town on the basis of the whole land area and population of Tu An, Cuu An, Song An and Thanh An commune and An Khe township of An Khe rural district.
To establish An Binh, Tay Son, An Phu and An Tan wards of An Khe provincial town. An Khe provincial town shall have a land area of 19,912.10 ha and a population of 62,600, and 8 dependent administrative units.
To establish Dak Po rural district on the basis of the whole land area and population of Phu An, Tan An, Cu An, Ya Hoi, Yang Bac, An Thanh and Ha Tam of An Khe rural district. Dak Po rural district shall have a land area of 49,961.50 ha and a population of 34,563 and 8 dependent administrative units.
To establish Dak Po commune of Dak Po rural district on the basis of a land area of 1,963 ha and a population of 3,092 of An Thanh commune.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
