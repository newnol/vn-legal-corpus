# Circular 04/2006/TT-BBCVT

CIRCULAR
Amending and supplementing a number of points of Circular No. 04/2001/TT-TCBD of November 20, 2001, of the General Department of Post (now the Ministry of Post and Telematics), guiding the implementation of the Government’s Decree No. 55/2001/ND-CP of August 23, 2001, on management, provision and use of Internet services, Internet exchange services and online services in the post and telecommunications sector
This Circular shall take effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.” The provisions of above-said Circular No. 04/2001/TT-TCBD which are not amended or supplemented in this Circular remain effective.- (Summary)
