# Decision 50/TTg

Decision No.50-TTg of January 24, 1997 of the Prime Minister to upgrade the Labor and Social Affairs Cadres School into the Labor and Social Affairs College attached to the Ministry of Labor, War Invalid and Social Affairs and subject to the branch management by the Ministry of Education and Training.
This Decision takes effect from February 7, 1997.- (Summary)
