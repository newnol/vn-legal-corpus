# Decree 41/2000/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 41/2000/ND-CP of August 29, 2000 on splitting or establishment of communes in Ngoc Hien, Dam Doi and Thoi Binh districts, Ca Mau province
To establish Tam Giang Tay commune in Ngoc Hien district on the basis of a land area of 9,235 ha and a population of 6,672 of Tam Giang commune.
To establish Tam Giang Dong commune in Ngoc Hien district on the basis of a land area of 9,530.79 ha and a population of 5,468 of Tam Giang commune.
To establish Ta An Khuong Nam commune in Dam Doi district on the basis of a land area of 3,124.81 ha and a population of 8,657 of Ta An Khuong commune.
To establish Ta An Khuong Dong commune in Dam Doi district on the basis of a land area of 3,208.45 ha and a population of 8,186 of Ta An Khuong commune.
To establish Tan Loc Bac commune in Thoi Binh district on the basis of a land area of 3,709 ha and a population of 10,156 of Tan Loc commune.
To establish Tan Loc Dong commune in Thoi Binh district on the basis of a land area of 4,064 ha and a population of 4,478 of Tan Loc commune.- (Summary)
