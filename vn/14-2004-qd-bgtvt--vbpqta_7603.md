# Quyết định 14/2004/QĐ-BGTVT

DECISION No. 14/2004/QD-BGTVT of September 15, 2004 decentralizing the competence to approve the final investment capital settlements of completed projects of the transport branch
With this Decision, the Minister of Transport authorizes the directors of the Vietnam Road Administration; the Vietnam Maritime Bureau; the Vietnam Aviation Administration and chairmen of Corporations 90 and 91 to approve the final investment capital settlements of completed Group C-projects which are funded with the State capital, credit capital guaranteed by the State and the State's development investment credit capital.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
