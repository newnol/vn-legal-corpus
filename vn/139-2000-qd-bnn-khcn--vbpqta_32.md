# Decision 139/2000/QD-BNN-KHCN

Decision No. 139/2000/QD-BNN-KHCN of December 19, 2000 promulgating the branch standard
To promulgate the branch standard titled "The testing procedures of tobacco varieties".
These procedures stipulate general principles, major contents and methods for national test of tobacco varieties which have been domestically selected and created or imported.
Organizations and individuals having tobacco varieties tested and the testing bodies shall have to abide by the Government�s Decree No. 07/CP of February 5, 1996 on management of plant varieties and the Agriculture and Rural Development Ministry�s Circular No. 02/NN/KNKL/TT of March 1, 1997 guiding the implementation of the said Decree.- (Summary)
