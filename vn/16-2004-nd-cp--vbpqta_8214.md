# Nghị định 16/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 16/2004/ND-CP of January 9, 2004 on the adjustment of the administrative boundaries and expansion of Ninh Binh provincial capital, Ninh Binh province
To transfer the whole land areas and populations of Ninh Khanh, Ninh Nhat, Ninh Tien, Ninh Phong, Ninh Son and Ninh Phuc communes of Hoa Lu rural district to Ninh Binh provincial capital.
Ninh Binh provincial capital shall have a land area of 4,674.80 ha, a population of 102,539 and 14 dependent administrative units; while these figures for Hoa Lu rural district shall be 10,343.20, 66.356 and 11, respectively.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
