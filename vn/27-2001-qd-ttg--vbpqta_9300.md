# Quyết định 27/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 27/2001/QD-TTg of March 8, 2001 on the annual labor-employment survey (the 2001-2005 period)
To conduct the annual sample labor-employment survey (the 2001-2005 period) in both urban and rural regions throughout the country.
The survey covers the following contents:
- Basic characteristics of the population, educational level, technical and professional qualifications of the labor force.
- Employment situation and employment structure of the work force.
- Unemployment situation and structure of unemployed labor.
- Underemployment situation and structure of underemployed labor.
- Impacts of labor-employment policies and solutions.
The Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs shall assume the prime responsibility and coordinate with the General Department of Statistics in working out plans for, organizing the survey and announcing the survey results in the fourth quarter every year.- (Summary)
