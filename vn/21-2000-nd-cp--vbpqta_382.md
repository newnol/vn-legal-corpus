# Decree 21/2000/ND-CP

THE GOVERNMENT
DECREE No. 21/2000/ND-CP of July 7, 2000 on the establishment of Cam Ranh town, Khanh Hoa province and its wards
To establish Cam Ranh town of Khanh Hoa province on the basis of a land area of 68,435 ha and a population of 191,066 people of Cam Ranh district, with 9 wards and 18 communes.
The administrative boundary of Cam Ranh town: It borders on the East Sea to the east, on Khanh Son district to the west, on Ninh Thuan province to the south and on Nha Trang city and Dien Khanh district to the north.
To establish 9 wards in Cam Ranh town, including: Ba Ngoi, Cam Loc, Cam Loi, Cam Linh, Cam Thuan, Cam Phu, Cam Phuc Nam, Cam Phuc Bac and Cam Nghia.- (Summary)
