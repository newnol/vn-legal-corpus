# Directive 30/2006/CT-BNN

DIRECTIVE
Enhancing and renewing the statistical work
With this Directive, the Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development requests concerned departments, enterprises, agencies and units under the Ministry and provincial/municipal Services of Agriculture and Rural Development to perform several tasks in order to consolidate, enhance and renew the statistical work to meet the branch management requirements in the new situation.- (Summary)
