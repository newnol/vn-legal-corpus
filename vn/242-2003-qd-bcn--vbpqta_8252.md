# Quyết định 242/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 242/2003/QD-BCN of December 30, 2003 transforming Chuong Duong Beverages Company into Chuong Duong Beverages Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Chuong Duong Beverages Company, an independent cost-accounting enterprise of Sai Gon Beer, Liquor and Beverages Corporation, shall be transformed into a joint-stock company with its English transaction name as Chuong Duong Beverages Joint-Stock Company, abbreviated to CDBECO.
The joint-stock company shall have a charter capital of VND 85,000,000,000, with the State's equities representing 51%; equities sold to laborers of the company, 37.36%; and equities sold to other subjects, 11.64%.
It is headquartered at 379 Ben Chuong Duong, Cau Kho ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh city.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
