# Nghị quyết 15/2004/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION No. 15/2004/NQ-CP of December 30, 2004 on the Government’s December 2004 regular meeting
On December 27 and 28, 2004, the Govern-ment held its December regular meeting, where:
- The Minister-Director of the Government Office presented a report reviewing the Government’s direction and administration work in 2004 and its 2005 working program; the Minister of Planning and Investment reported on the socio-economic situation and production and investment results in 2004; and the Minister of Trade reported on the situation of domestic market and export in 2004.
- The Minister of Post and Telematics presented the overall plan on building an e-Government in Vietnam till 2010; the Minister of Finance presented the Bill Amending and Supplementing a Number of Articles of the Customs Law and the Minister of Health presented the draft Ordinance on HIV/AIDS Prevention and Combat.- (Summary)
