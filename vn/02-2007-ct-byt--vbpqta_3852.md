# Directive 02/2007/CT-BYT

DIRECTIVE
On the work of flood prevention and combat and search and rescue in 2007
The Minister of Health requests directors of provincial/municipal Health Services and heads of attached units to perform several tasks in order to enhance the work of flood prevention and combat and search and rescue in 2007.- (Summary)
