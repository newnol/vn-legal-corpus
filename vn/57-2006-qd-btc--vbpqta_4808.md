# Quyết định 57/2006/QĐ-BTC

DECISION
Adjusting the levels of charge and fee for management of food quality, hygiene and safety specified in the Finance Minister’s Decision No. 80/2005/QD-BTC of November 17, 2005
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”
Other charge and fee levels and the management of collection, remittance, management and use for management of food quality, hygiene and safety which are not specified in this Decision still comply with Decision No. 80/2005/QD-BTC.- (Summary)
