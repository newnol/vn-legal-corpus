# Thông tư 105/1998/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No.105/1998/TT-BTC of July 23, 1998 guiding the implementation of regimes applicable to the Party's lecturers
This Circular guides the regulations on the regime of paying remuneration to lecturers; material facilities in service of their activities; funds for organizing information symposia and lecturers' conferences.- (Summary)
