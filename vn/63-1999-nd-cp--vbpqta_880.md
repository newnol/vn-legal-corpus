# Decree 63/1999/ND-CP

Decree No. 63/1999/ND-CP of August 2, 1999 on the readjustment of administrative boundaries of the expanded district townships of Huong Son and Can Loc districts, Ha Tinh province
To annex the whole land area and population of Son Pho commune to Pho Chau township - district township of Huong Son district. After having its administrative boundaries readjusted, Pho Chau township shall have a land area of 422 hectares and a population of 8,290.
To annex the whole land area and population of Dai Loc commune to Can Loc township - district township of Can Loc district and rename it Nghen township. After having its administrative boundaries readjusted, Nghen township shall have a land area of 1,136 hectares and a population of 11,824.- (Summary)
