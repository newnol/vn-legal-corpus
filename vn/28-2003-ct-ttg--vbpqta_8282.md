# Chỉ thị 28/2003/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE No. 28/2003/CT-TTg of December 19, 2003 on organizing the enforcement of the Law on National Borders
The Prime Minister instructs the concerned ministries, provincial/municipal People's Committees, agencies and branches to organize the enforcement of the Law on National Borders, which was passed by the XIth National Assembly on June 17, 2003 at its 3rd session and takes effect as from January 1, 2004.- (Summary)
