# Quyết định 63/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 63/2003/QD-TTg of April 21, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under the Ministry of Communications and Transport till 2005
- State corporations: 11 corporations have their legal person status unchanged and 5 corporations shall be transformed to experimentally operate after the parent company-affiliate company model.
- State enterprises under the Ministry:
State enterprises whose legal person status remains unchanged: 62 public-utility enterprises and 3 business enterprises; 3 enterprises shall be transformed to experimentally operate after the parent company-affiliate company model; and 3 enterprises being schools or institutes.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding more than 50% of their total shares: 2.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding at least 51% of shares when shares are sold for the first time: 11.
To be established: 15 public-utility enterprises.
- This Document also states the reorganization of State enterprises being members of the corporations set up under the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 90/TTg.
Reorganization roadmap: 34 enterprises shall be equitized and 2 enterprises shall be merged in 2003; 31 and 40 enterprises shall be equitized in 2004 and 2005 respectively.- (Summary)
