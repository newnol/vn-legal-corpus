# Thông tư 34/2001/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 34/2001/TT-BTC of May 25, 2001 guiding the regime of collection and use of fees for enrolment to public schools and educational institutions
Enrolment fee payers prescribed in this Circular are examinees who register for sitting at and actually take part in entrance examinations to State-run public schools and educational and training institutions.
The Circular specifies the collection levels; the management of the collection, payment and use of fee amounts.- (Summary)
