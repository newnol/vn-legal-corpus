# Decree 01/CP

Decree No.01-CP of January 2, 1997 of the Government on the readjustment of the boundary of Nam Dinh town of Nam Ha province
To transfer Nam Phong and Nam Van communes from Nam Ninh district to Nam Dinh town. After readjustment of its boundary, Nam Dinh town shall have a land area of 6,760 ha, a population of 263,600 and 25 communes and wards; it borders Thai Binh province to the East, Binh Luc district to the West, Nam Ninh district to the South and Ly Nhan district to the North.- (Summary).-
