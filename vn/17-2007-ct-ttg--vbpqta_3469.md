# Chỉ thị 17/2007/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
Enhancing the management of assets of project management units using state capital
The Prime Minister instructs ministers, heads of ministerial-level, government-attached and other central agencies, provincial-level People’s Committee presidents, chairmen of boards of directors and general directors of economic groups and state corporations set up under the Prime Minister’s decisions to inspect the management and use of assets of project management units using state capital and perform other tasks in order to contribute to practicing thrift and combating waste and corruption.- (Summary)
