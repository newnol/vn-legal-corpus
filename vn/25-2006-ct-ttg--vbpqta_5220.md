# Directive 25/2006/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On upper secondary education streaming
The Prime Minister asks the Ministry of Education and Training, concerned ministries and localities to concentrate on performing several tasks in order to obtain good results from upper secondary education streaming.- (Summary)
