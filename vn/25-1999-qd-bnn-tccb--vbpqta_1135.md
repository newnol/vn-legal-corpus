# Decision 25/1999/QD-BNN-TCCB

DECISION No.25/1999/QD/BNN-TCCB of February 1 st , 1999 on the establishment of the Gia Lai Water Resources and Agro-Forestry Research Center under the Tay Nguyen (Central Highlands) Agro-Forestry Science and Technique Institute
To establish the Gia Lai Water Resources and Agro-Forestry Research Center under the Tay Nguyen Agro-Forestry Science and Technique Institute. The Center�s head office is located in Pleiku provincial capital, Gia Lai province.
The Gia Lai Water Resources and Agro-Forestry Experimental Research Center is a non-business organization for agricultural and forestry water resources science and technology having the same tasks as those of the Tay Nguyen Agro-Forestry Science and Technique Institute.- (Summary)
