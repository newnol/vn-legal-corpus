# Decision 107/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 107/QD-TTg of January 24, 2003 changing the use purposes of forestry land for execution of the project on building Dai Ninh hydro-electric power plant in Binh Thuan province
To change the use purposes of 1,144.31 ha of forestry land under production forests and protection forests for building various works of Dai Ninh hydro-electric power plant in Binh Thuan province, in which:
- Forestry land to have their use purposes changed to other ones: 178.71 ha.
- Forestry land to have their use purposes temporarily changed for use as sites for building auxiliary works and make-shift works of the plant: 965.6 ha.
The provincial People’s Committee shall direct its attached agencies to evaluate and submit to competent authorities the to-be-compensated damage caused to forests and land-affixed assets for recovery and assignment of land for building various works of the hydro-electric power plant.
Upon the expiry of the use duration, the Project Management Board shall have to restore forests in temporarily-used areas and hand over such areas to local administrations for re-assignment to their former owners for use for forestry purposes.- (Summary)
