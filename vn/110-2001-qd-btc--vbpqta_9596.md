# Decision 110/2001/QD-BTC

DECISION No. 110/2001/QD-BTC of October 31, 2001 amending and supplementing names and tax rates of commodities under Heading No. 2710 of the Preferential Import Tariff
This Decision amends the preferential import tax rates of a number of commodities under Heading No. 2710 prescribed in Decision No. 107/2001/QD-BTC of October 18, 2001 of the Minister of Finance.- (Summary)
