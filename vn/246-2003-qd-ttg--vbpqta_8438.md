# Quyết định 246/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 246/2003/QD-TTg of November 18, 2003 on the approval of the master plan for reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under Vietnam Oil and Gas Corporation till 2005
To approve the master plan for reorganization and renewal of State enterprises under Vietnam Oil and Gas Corporation till 2005.
According to the master plan, the State shall continue holding 100% of capital of eight State enterprises, while seven other enterprises shall be equitized (three in 2004 and four in 2005) with over 50% of their capital shall continue to be held by the State.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
