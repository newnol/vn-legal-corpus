# Decision 44/2004/QD-BBCVT

DECISION No. 44/2004/QD-BBCVT of October 15, 2004 on the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structure of Vietnam Post newspaper
Vietnam Post newspaper is a unit attached to the Ministry of Post and Telematics, functioning as a mouthpiece of the Ministry and a social forum for the cause of post, telecommunication and information technology development.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces Decision No. 64/2003/QD-BBCVT of March 28, 2003.- (Summary)
