# Quyết định 121/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 121/2002/QD-TTg of September 19, 2002 transferring Vietnam Civil Aviation Administration to the Ministry of Communications and Transport
To transfer Vietnam Civil Aviation Administration to the Ministry of Communications and Transport. To assign the Minister of Communications and Transport to manage Vietnam Civil Aviation Administration.
This Decision take effect after its signing.- (Summary)
