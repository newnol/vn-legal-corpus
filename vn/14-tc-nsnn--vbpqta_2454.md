# Circular 14/TC-NSNN

THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE
Circular No.14-TC/NSNN of March 28, 1997 guiding the management of budget revenues and expenditures of communes, townships and wards.
Communal/township/ward budgets are part of the State budget set up and managed by the communal/township/ward People�s Committees and subject to decision and supervision by the communal/township/ward People�s Councils.
In this Circular, the Ministry of Finance provides detailed guidances on the contents of revenues and expenditures of the communal/township/ward budgets; the establishment, remittance and account settlement of the communal/township/ward budgets.- (Summary)
