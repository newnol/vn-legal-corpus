# Quyết định 20/2001/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 20/2001/QD-BCN of April 4, 2001 amending Clause 1, Article 1 and Article 2 of the Industry Minister�s Decision No. 73/2000/QD-BCN of December 22, 2000 to transform Binh Dinh Vegetable Oil Company into Binh Dinh Vegetable Oil Joint-Stock Company
The amended contents are as follows:
To transform Binh Dinh Vegetable Oil Company into Binh Dinh Vegetable Oil Stock Company - BIVOCO, as from April 1, 2001.
The stock company is headquartered at Section 4, Quang Trung ward, Quy Nhon city, Binh Dinh province.
Charter capital of the stock company: VND 4,000,000,000, in which equities sold to the laborers of the company represent 68.6% and equities sold to other subjects outside the company, 31.4%.- (Summary)
