# Circular 38/1999/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 38/1999/TT-BTC of April 12, 1999 guiding the financial management mechanism applicable to the gratitude fund
This Circular guides in detail the provisions on the levels of contributions to be mobilized for setting up the gratitude fund; assignment of the responsibility for collection and organization of collection for the gratitude fund; and spending contents of the gratitude fund.- (Summary)
