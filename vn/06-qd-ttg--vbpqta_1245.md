# Decision 06/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 06/QD-TTg of January 5, 1999 ratifying the socio-economic development project for Bo Y- Ngoc Hoi border-gate area of Kon Tum province
To ratify the socio-economic development project for Bo Y- Ngoc Hoi border-gate area of Kon Tum province, including Sa Loong, Bo Y, Dak Su, Dak Nong, Dak Duc communes and Plei Kan township, with the total ground area of 400 ha.
According to the planning orientations, the space of the border-gate area shall be divided into functional sections, including:
- The trade and tourist center;
- The export, import and cross-border services section;
- The border market;
- The section for sub-contracted production, processing and assembly of products;
- The special-use warehouse and bonded warehouse;
- Car parks and terminals for other means of transport;
- The management center; the border-gate checkpoint;
- To develop agriculture and forestry in combination with the planning of the tree planting and reservoir building, so as to ensure that the border-gate area be green, clean and beautiful;
- To build the border-gate area into an urban area according to the planning and develop population centers along the direction of modernity combined with the unique national architecture;
- To step by step build the area�s infrastructure along the line of modernization, on a par with the position of an international border gate.-(Summary)
