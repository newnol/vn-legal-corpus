# Resolution 69/2006/QH11

RESOLUTION
On the allocation of the 2007 central budget
Under this Resolution, the total central budget revenue in 2007 will be VND 216,015 billion, accounting for 71.8%, while the total local budget revenue will be VND 84,885 billion, representing 28.2%, of the total state budget revenue.
The total central budget expenditure in 2007 will be VND 206,857 billion, making up 57.9%, while the total local budget expenditure will be VND 150,543 billion, accounting for 42.1% of the total state budget expenditure.- (Summary)
