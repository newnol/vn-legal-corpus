# Nghị định 08/2004/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 08/2004/ND-CP of January 2, 2004 on the establishment of wards and townships of Dakrong and Huong Hoa rural districts, Quang Tri province
To establish Krong Klang township, the district capital of Dakrong rural district, on the basis of a land area of 384.70 ha and a population of 1,616 of Mo O commune and a land area of 1,436.30 ha and a population of 1,010 of Huong Hiep commune.
Krong Klang township shall have a land area of 1,821 ha and a population of 2,626.
To establish Huong Viet commune of Huong Hoa rural district on the basis of a land area of 6,520 ha and a population of 1,208 of Huong Lap commune.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
