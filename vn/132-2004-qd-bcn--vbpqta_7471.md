# Quyết định 132/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 132/2004/QD-BCN of November 12, 2004 transforming Tran Hung Hao Mechanical Company into Tran Hung Hao Mechanical Limited Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Tran Hung Hao Mechanical Limited Company (TMC Co.Ltd.) is headquartered at No. 114, Mai Hac De street, Hai Ba Trung district, Hanoi city, and has a charter capital of VND 90,141,846,000.- (Summary)
