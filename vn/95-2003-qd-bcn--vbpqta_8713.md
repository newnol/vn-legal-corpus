# Quyết định 95/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 95/2003/QD-BCN of June 5, 2003 transforming the Power Construction and Supplies Enterprise into Power Construction Company 3.2, which is one-member limited liability company under the Power Construction Company No. 3
To transform the Power Construction and Supplies Enterprise into one-member limited liability Power Construction Company 3.2 under the Power Construction Company No. 3.
Power Construction Company 3.2 is an affiliate company.
Its charter capital at the time of establishment: VND 6,055,171,282.
Its production and business lines:
- Building and installing electric, industrial, civil, communications and irrigation works;
- Producing and trading in assorted construction materials, Block bricks, Terrazzo bricks and Daramic bricks; exploiting stones, turning out concrete products;
- Processing and manufacturing supplies and spare parts in service of power construction and installation; producing electricity-conducting aluminum cables; dealing in electric supplies and equipment as well as construction materials;
- Experimenting and correcting electric equipment and spare parts in the course of building electric works;
- Providing investment and construction consultancy, including: elaboration and evaluation of investment projects, survey, planning design, work design and project management consultancy;
- Dealing in housing and urban technical infrastructure development; dealing in interior and exterior decoration goods items; providing land and housing services;
- Dealing in other production and business lines permitted by law in conformity with the company’s capability.- (Summary)
