# Decision 222/2005/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Approving the master plan on e-commerce development in the 2006-2010 period
Under this plan, by 2010, about 60% of large enterprises will conduct business-to-business e-commerce, approximately 80% of small- and medium-sized enterprises will be aware of the interests brought about by e-commerce and conduct business-to-business or business-to-consumer e-transactions, and around 10% of households will conduct business-to-consumer or consumer-to-consumer e-transactions.
The plan also sets forth viewpoints and major policies and solutions for e-commerce development.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication "CONG BAO."- (Summary)
