# Quyết định 93/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 93/2003/QD-TTg of May 12, 2003 promulgating the Regulation on operation of the Central Steering Committee for Forest Fire Prevention and Fighting
The Central Steering Committee for Forest Fire Prevention and Fighting is an advisory organization which assists the Prime Minister in directing the work of forest fire prevention and fighting throughout the country.
The Regulation on operation of the Central Steering Committee for Forest Fire Prevention and Fighting, issued together with this Decision, prescribes the organization and operation of the Steering Committee and its members in performing the common tasks of the Steering Committee.- (Summary)
