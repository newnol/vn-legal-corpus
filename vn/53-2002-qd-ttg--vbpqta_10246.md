# Decision 53/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 53/2002/QD-TTg of April 23, 2002 on the establishment of the Vietnamese Section of the Joint-Commission for the development of economic and commercial relations between the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the Government of the United States
Chairman of the Section: A Vice Minister of Trade.
Its standing members: Representatives of leaderships of the Ministry of Trade, the Ministry for Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Planning and Investment, the Ministry of Finance and the Government Office.
The Vietnamese Section shall be tasked to coordinate with the US Section in performing the functions and tasks of the Joint-Commission for development of economic and commercial relations according to the provisions of the Vietnam-US Bilateral Trade Agreement, in accordance with the Prime Minister�s Decision No. 258/TTg of April 29, 1995 on the organization and operation of the Vietnamese Sections in the inter-governmental Commissions on economic, commercial, scientific, technological and cultural cooperation with foreign countries.- (Summary)
