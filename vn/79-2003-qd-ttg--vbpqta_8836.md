# Quyết định 79/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 79/2003/QD-TTg of April 29, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises of Vietnam Coffee Corporation till 2005
Enterprises whose legal person status remains unchanged and whose 100% charter capital continues to be held by the State: 24.
Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding more than 50% of their total shares: 1 in 2003 and 2 in 2004.
Enterprises’ sections to be equitized with the State holding shares at low rates or holding no shares: 2 units in 2003 and 2 in 2004.
Enterprises to be sold or assigned to laborers’ collectives: 2 in 2003 and 1 in 2004.
Enterprises to be merged: 15 in 2003 and 19 in 2004.
Enterprises to be dissolved: 2 in 2003.- (Summary)
