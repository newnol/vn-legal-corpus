# Quyết định 94/2004/QĐ-BTC

DECISION No. 94/2004/QD-BTC of December 7, 2004 prescribing the regime of collection, remittance, management and use of the fee for issuance of judicial record cards
The fee for issuance of judicial record cards is VND 100,000/issuance/person, applicable to both Vietnamese citizens and foreigners.
Particularly for Vietnamese citizens residing in mountainous, deep-lying and remote communes meeting with exceptional difficulties under Program 135, this fee is VND 50,000.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces Decision No. 146/2000/QD-BTC of September 16, 2000.- (Summary)
