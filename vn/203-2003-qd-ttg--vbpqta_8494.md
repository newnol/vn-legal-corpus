# Quyết định 203/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 203/2003/QD-TTg of September 30, 2003 establishing the Propagation-Culture Sub-Committee in service of ASEM 5
To establish the Propagation-Culture Sub-Committee under the National Committee for Preparation and Organization of the 5th summit of the Asian-European Cooperation Meeting (ASEM 5), with Vice Minister of Culture and Information Tran Chien Thang as its head and 17 other persons as its members.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
