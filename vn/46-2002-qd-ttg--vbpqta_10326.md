# Quyết định 46/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 46/2002/QD-TTg of April 3, 2002 establishing the Committee for organizing the 22 nd SEA Games in Vietnam in 2003
The Committee for organizing the 22 nd SEA Games in Vietnam in 2003 shall be composed of the Minister-Director of the Sports and Physical Training Committee as its Chairman; and representatives of the concerned ministries, branches and localities as its 3 vice chairmen and 17 members.
The Committee for organizing the 22 nd SEA Games shall operate under the direction of the National Steering Committee for preparation for the 22 nd SEA Games.
The Committee for organizing the 22 nd SEA Games shall prepare for and administer the organization of the 22 nd SEA Games.
The Committee for organizing the 22 nd SEA Games is tasked to:
- Draw up the overall plan for organizing the 22 nd SEA Games.
- Examine and urge the construction, upgrading and improvement of works, provide equipment in service of the exercises and competitions at the Sea Games.
- Work out and organize the implementation of the plans in the following domains:
+ Training, coaching athletes in preparation for competitions;
+ Information, traffic and communication;
+ Ceremonies, protocol, accommodation and public services;
+ Order, security and safety;
+ Finance and financing;
+ Technical services.
- Organize the making of common regulations and rules of the Games, the competition rules of each game in compatibility with the Regulation of the Southeast Asian Sport Council as well as the competition rules of international sport federations;
- Coordinate activities of the concerned ministries, branches and localities in order to organize the Games;
- Organize the recruitment, fostering, training and employment of officials who shall administer sport competitions, service officials and volunteers.- (Summary)
