# Quyết định 457/2003/QĐ-NHNN

DECISION No. 457/2003/QD-NHNN of May 12, 2003 promulgating the technical process of inter-bank electronic clearing operations
The technical process of inter-bank electronic clearing operations, issued together with this Decision, prescribes the procedures and order for the handling, control, payment and book-keeping accounting of money transfer payments between banks and other organizations licensed to provide payment services which participate in electronic clearing organized and directed by the State Bank.
This process replaces the process issued together with Decision No. 212/2002/QD-NHNN of March 20, 2002 of the State Bank Governor.- (Summary)
