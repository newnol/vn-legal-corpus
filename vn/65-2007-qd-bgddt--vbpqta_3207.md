# Quyết định 65/2007/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION
Promulgating the Regulation on criteria for evaluation of university education quality
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces Chapter II of the Provisional Regulation on evaluation of university quality, promulgated together with the Education and Training Minister’s Decision No. 38/2004/QD-BGD&DT of December 2, 2004.- (Summary)
