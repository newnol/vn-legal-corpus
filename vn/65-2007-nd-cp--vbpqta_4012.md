# Decree 65/2007/ND-CP 

DECREE
Adjusting the administrative boundaries of Cam Ranh town and Dien Khanh district for the establishment of Cam Lam district; adjusting the administrative boundaries for the establishment of communes and townships of Cam Lam and Truong Sa districts and the expansion of Cam Nghia ward of Cam Ranh town, Khanh Hoa province
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
