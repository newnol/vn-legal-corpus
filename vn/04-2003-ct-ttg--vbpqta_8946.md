# Chỉ thị 04/2003/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE No. 04/2003/CT-TTg of March 12, 2003 on the implementation of Directive No. 22/CT-TW of February 24, 2003 of the Party Central Committee’s Secretariat on enhancing the Party’s leadership over the work of ensuring traffic order and safety and continuing to implement the Government’s Resolution No. 13/2002/NQ-CP of November 19, 2002 on solutions to check the rise in, then proceed to reduce traffic accidents and congestion
The Prime Minister requests the provincial/municipal People’s Committees to perform the following tasks:
- To organize preliminary reviews in order to assess and draw experiences in the organization of implementation of Resolution No. 13/2002/NQ-CP.
- To consolidate the Traffic Safety Committees of all levels.
- In Hanoi capital, Ho Chi Minh city and other cities as well as towns, it is necessary to boost the development of mass transit, first of all buses; and to take initiative in working out measures to restrict the use of personal transport means.
- To put a definite end to the situation of encroaching roadbeds, pavements and traffic work protection corridors.
- To bring into full play the coordination among mass associations and organizations in maintaining traffic order and safety.
The Prime Minister also directs tasks relating to the Ministry of Communications and Transport, the Ministry of Finance, the Ministry of Education and Training, mass media, the National Committee for Traffic Safety, State officials and employees and members of socio-political associations and societies.- (Summary)
