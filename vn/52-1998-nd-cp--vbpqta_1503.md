# Nghị định 52/1998/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 52/1998/ND-CP of July 20, 1998 on the establishment of Mong Cai provincial capital of Quang Ninh province and its wards as well as communes.
To establish Mong Cai provincial capital on the basis of a land area of 52,000 ha and a population of 57,838 of Hai Ninh district. Mong Cai provincial capital is composed of 5 wards: Ka Long, Tran Phu, Hoa Lac, Ninh Duong and Tra Co, and 11 communes: Hai Son, Hai Yen, Hai Xuan, Hai Hoa, Binh Ngoc, Quang Nghia, Hai Tien, Van Ninh, Hai Dong, Vinh Thuc and Vinh Trung.- (Summary)
