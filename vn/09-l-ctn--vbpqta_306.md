# Lệnh 09/L-CTN

ORDER No. 09/L-CTN OF AUGUST 1 st , 2000 ON THE PROMULGATION OF ORDINANCE
THE PRESIDENT OF THE STATE OF THE SOCIALIST REPUBLIC OF VIETNAM
Pursuant to Article 103 and Article 106 of the 1992 Constitution of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam;
Pursuant to Article 78 of the Law on Organization of the National Assembly;
Pursuant to Article 51 of the Law on Promulgation of Legal Documents,
PROMULGATES:
The Ordinance amending Article 18 of the Ordinance on Organization of Military Courts,
which was passed on July 25, 2000 by the Standing Committee of the X th National Assembly of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam.
President of the State of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam TRAN DUC LUONG
