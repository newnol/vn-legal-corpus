# Decision 37/1999/QD-BGDD

DECISION No. 37/1999/QD-BGDDT of September 30, 1999 promulgating the Regulation on recruitment, training and consideration for enrolment in colleges and universities applicable to pre-enrolment students
The Regulation, issued together with this Decision, prescribes the objects, conditions and process of pre-enrolment recruitment; the organization of study, examination and testing in pre-enrolment courses; and the consideration for enrolment in colleges and universities after finishing such courses.- (Summary)
