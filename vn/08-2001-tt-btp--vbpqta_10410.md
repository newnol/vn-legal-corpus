# Thông tư 08/2001/TT-BTP

CIRCULAR No. 08/2001/TT-BTP of December 18, 2001 guiding the financial management in implementing the emulation and commendation regime in the justice service
This Circular guides the expenses for the emulation and commendation work and the reward levels for individuals and collectives, that are commended and/or rewarded for their achievements recorded in a year, extraordinarily commended and/or rewarded, or commended and/or rewarded for their achievements recorded in many consecutive years.- (Summary)
