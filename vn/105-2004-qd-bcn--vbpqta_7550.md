# Quyết định 105/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 105/2004/QD-BCN of October 5, 2004 transforming Garment 10 Company into Garment 10 Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Garment 10 Joint-Stock Company, abbreviated to GARCO 10, has a charter capital of VND 54,000,000,000; of which the State's equities represent 51% and equities sold to laborers in the company, 49%.
Its head office is located in Sai Dong ward, Long Bien district, Hanoi.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
