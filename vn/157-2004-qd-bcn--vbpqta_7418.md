# Quyết định 157/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 157/2004/QD-BCN of December 6, 2004 on transforming Rang Dong Plastic Company into Rang Dong Plastic Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Rang Dong Plastic Joint-Stock Company, Rang Dong Plastic, has a charter capital of VND 90,000,000,000, of which the State’s equities represent 51%, equities sold to laborers of the company, 37.40% and equities sold to other subjects, 11.60%.
Its head-office is located at No. 190 Lac Long Quan street, ward 3, district 11, Ho Chi Minh city.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
