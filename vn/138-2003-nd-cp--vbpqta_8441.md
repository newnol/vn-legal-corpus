# Nghị định 138/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 138/2003/ND-CP of November 17, 2003 on the establishment of Nam Can and Phu Tan districts, Ca Mau province
To establish Phu Tan district on the basis of a land area of 44,595 hectares and a population of 109,642 of Cai Nuoc district.
To establish Nam Can district on the basis of a land area of 53,291.40 hectares and a population of 70,745 of Ngoc Hien district.
This Decree takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
