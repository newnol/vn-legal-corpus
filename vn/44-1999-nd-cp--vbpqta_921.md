# Nghị định 44/1999/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 44/1999/ND-CP of July 1st, 1999 on the establishment of a number of communes of Yen Thanh district, Nghe An province
To establish Tay Thanh commune on the basis of a land area of 2,200 ha and a population of 5,866 of Quang Thanh commune.
To establish Dai Thanh commune on the basis of a land area of 906 ha and a population of 4,720 of Minh Thanh commune.
To establish Kim Thanh commune on the basis of a land area of 2,300 ha and a population of 4,150 of Dong Thanh commune.- (Summary)
