# Directive 45/2007/CT-BNN

DIRECTIVE
On expeditious reorganization of local forest protection bodies
The Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development requests directors of provincial/municipal Agriculture and Rural Development Services and heads of provincial/municipal Forest Protection Sub-Departments to perform several tasks in order to expeditiously reorganize local forest protection bodies.- (Summary)
