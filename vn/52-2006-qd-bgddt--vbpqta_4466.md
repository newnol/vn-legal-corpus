# Decision 52/2006/QĐ-BGDĐT

DECISION
Promulgating the Regulation on examinations for selection of excellent pupils
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and annuls the Education and Training Minister’s Decision No. 65/1998/QD-BGDDT of December 18, 1998, Decision No. 41/1999/QD-BGDDT of December 10, 1999, Decision No. 44/2001/QD-BGDDT of November 12, 2001, Decision No. 05/2004/QD-BGDDT of March 1, 2004, Decision No. 05/2005/QD-BGDDT of February 17, 2005, Decision No. 04/2006/QD-BGDDT of January 26, 2006, and Decision No. 18/2006/QD-BGDDT of May 11, 2006.- (Summary)
