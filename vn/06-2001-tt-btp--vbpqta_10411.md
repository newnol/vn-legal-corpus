# Thông tư 06/2001/TT-BTP

CIRCULAR No. 06/2001/TT-BTP of November 8, 2001 guiding the emulation and commendation work in the justice service
The Circular guides the commendation and reward of individuals working at units of the Justice Ministry, the judgment-executing bodies, the local people�s courts, and collectives in the justice service. More concretely, it guides the regulations on commendation and reward principles; commendation and reward forms and criteria, procedures and contents of dossiers of application for commendation and reward; emulation and commendation funding, handling of violations and settlement of complaints and denunciations in the emulation and commendation work.- (Summary)
