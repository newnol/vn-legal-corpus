# Decree 14/CP

DECREE No.14-CP of February 18, 1997 of the Government on the establishment of the district townships of Tan Yen and Luc Nam districts, Bac Giang province.
To establish Cao Thuong district township of Tan Yen district with a land area of 269.14 hectares and a population of 4,663.
To establish Doi Ngo district township of Luc Nam district with a land area of 472 hectares and a population of 5,139.- (Summary)
