# Quyết định 205/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 205/2003/QD-TTg of October 2, 2003 approving the scheme on "organizing the management of, and job training and creation for, post-detoxication persons" in Ho Chi Minh city
The scheme on "organizing the management of, and job training and creation for, post-detoxication persons" in Ho Chi Minh city, approved together with this Decision, aims to create necessary conditions for post-detoxication persons to re-integrate themselves into the community.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
