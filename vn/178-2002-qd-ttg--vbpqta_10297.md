# Decision 178/2002/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 178/QD-TTg of February 28, 2002 transforming the State-run Company for Financing and Promoting Technology (FPT) into a joint-stock company
To transform the State-run Company for Financing and Promoting Technology under the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment into the Corporation for Financing and Promoting Technology.
Its head office: 89 Lang Ha street, Lang Ha ward, Dong Da district, Hanoi.
Its charter capital is VND 20 billion, of which the State equities represent 51% and equities sold to the laborers inside the enterprise, 49%.- (Summary)
