# Quyết định 32/2002/QĐ-BTCCBCP

DECISION No. 32/2002/QD-BTCCBCP of July 1, 2002 issuing commemorative medals and the Regulation on consideration for the conferment of commemorative medals "For the cause of the State organizational branch"
To issue commemorative medals "For the cause of the State organizational branch" for acknowledgement of individuals’ meritorious contributions to the cause of construction and development of the State organizational branch.
The consideration for conferment of commemorative medals shall comply with the Regulation issued together with this Decision.- (Summary)
