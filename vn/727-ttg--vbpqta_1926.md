# Decision 727/TTg

Decision No.727-TTg of September 4, 1997 on the establishment of the Department for War Invalids, Martyrs and People with Meritorious Services to the Country under the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs.
To establish the Department for War Invalids, Martyrs and People with Meritorious Services to the Country under the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs on the basis of the Department of policies towards war invalids and martyrs in order to assist the Minister of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs in managing the activities related to war invalids, martyrs and people with meritorious services to the country.- (Summary)
