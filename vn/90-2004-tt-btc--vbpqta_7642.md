# Thông tư 90/2004/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 90/2004/TT-BTC of September 7, 2004 guiding the regime of collection, remittance, management and use of road tolls
Under this Circular, liable to road tolls are land-road motorized transport means and special-use vehicles participating in road traffic.
This Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
