# Quyết định 142/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 142/2004/QD-BCN of November 23, 2004 on transforming Da Nang Textile and Garment Manufacturing Import - Export Company into Da Nang Textile and Garment Manufacturing Import - Export Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Da Nang Textile and Garment Manufacturing Import - Export Joint - Stock Company, VINATEX DANANG, has a charter capital of VND 10,000,000,000, of which the State's equities represent 51% and equities sold to laborers of the company, 49%.
Its head - office is located at 25 Tran Quy Cap street, Da Nang city.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
