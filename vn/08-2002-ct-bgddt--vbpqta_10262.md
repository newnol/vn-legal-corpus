# Chỉ thị 08/2002/CT-BGDĐT

DIRECTIVE No. 08/2002/CT-BGDDT of March 20, 2002 on training defense education teachers for senior secondary and intermediate vocational training schools
The Minister of Education and Training set the requirements on the number of defense education teachers; the task of training defense education teachers; regime towards teachers sent to defense education teacher-training courses and funds for such training.- (Summary)
