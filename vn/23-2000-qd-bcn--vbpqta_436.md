# Quyết định 23/2000/QĐ-BCN

THE MINISTRY OF INDUSTRY
DECISION No. 23/2000/QD-BCN of April 5, 2000 on the establishment of Industrial and Mine Investment Consulting Company under the Vietnam Coal Corporation
To establish the Industrial and Mine Investment Consulting Company (abbreviated as IMCC), which is an independent cost-accounting member enterprise of the Vietnam Coal Corporation, on the basis of merger between the Electricity-Coal Investment Consulting Company and Industrial and Mine Construction Consulting Company.
Head office: 565 Nguyen Trai street, Thanh Xuan Nam ward, Thanh Xuan district, Hanoi city.
Business lines:
- Planning, consulting in investment and construction of mine and power (thermo-power plants, transmission lines, transformer stations of up to 110KV, public lighting,...), industrial, civil, communications and transport, post and telecommunications projects;
- Topographical, hydrographic-geological, geological, biological environment and environmental survey in service of elaborating investment projects and works designing;
- Evaluating investment projects, technical designing, making total cost estimates for the projects not implemented by the company;
- Providing consultancy for the export and import of equipment and accessories in service of the mining and other industrial branches;
- Providing investment consultancy and construction services in the above-mentioned fields for other domestic and foreign units;
- Building medium- and small-scale industrial, civil, communications and transport works, power lines and transformer stations of 35KV or under;
- Conducting research in application of sciences and technologies, technological transfer in service of the mining industry and the units having such demand;
- Cooperating in production, business and consumption of coal, construction materials and other services.- (Summary)
