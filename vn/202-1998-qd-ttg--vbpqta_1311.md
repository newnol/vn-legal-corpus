# Quyết định 202/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.202/1998/QD-TTg of October 16, 1998 on adding the Institute for Physical Training and Sport Science to the list of institutes stipulated in Decision No.782-TTg of October 24, 1996 of the Prime Minister
To add the Institute for Physical Training and Sport Science under the Physical Training and Sport Commission to the list of institutes specified in Section 1, Article 1 of Decision No.782-TTg of October 24, 1996 of the Prime Minister on the organizational arrangement of the scientific and technological research and development agencies.- (Summary)
