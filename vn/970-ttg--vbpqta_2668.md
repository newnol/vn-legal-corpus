# Decision 970/TTg

DECISION No.970-TTg of December 28, 1996 of the Prime Minister on the establishment of the national preserve around Ke Go lake encompassing three districts of Cam Xuyen, Ky Anh and Huong Khe, Ha Tinh province
The management, protection and construction of the Ke Go national preserve shall comply with the regulation on the management and use of special-purpose forests and the ratified feasibility study.- (Summary)
