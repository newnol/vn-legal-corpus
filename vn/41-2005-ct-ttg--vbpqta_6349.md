# Chỉ thị 41/2005/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE No. 41/2005/CT-TTg of December 29, 2005, enhancing drought prevention and combat and protection of the 2005-2006 winter-spring crop in northern delta and midland provinces
With this Directive, the Prime Minister requests ministries, branches and localities to take initiative in preventing, combating and remedying consequences caused by droughts in order to protect the 2005-2006 winter-spring crop.- (Summary)
