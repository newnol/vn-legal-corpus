# Nghị định 53/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 53/2003/ND-CP of May 19, 2003 adjusting administrative boundaries for the establishment of communes, wards and townships of Thoai Son, Phu Tan and Tan Chau districts as well as Chau Doc provincial capital, An Giang province
To establish Vinh My ward of Chau Doc provincial capital on the basis of a land area of 779.6 ha and a population of 14,870 of Vinh My commune, which is renamed Vinh Chau commune with a land area of 2,168.4 ha and a population of 3,357.
To establish Oc Eo township of Thoai Son district on the basis of a land area 989.9 ha and a population of 11,819 of Vong The commune.
To establish Binh Thanh commune of Thoai Son district on the basis of parts of land areas and population of Vong The and Thoai Giang communes. Binh Thanh commune shall have a land area of 2,948.3 ha and a population of 10,987. Vong The commune shall be left with a land area of 2,862.8 ha and a population of 6,060. Thoai Giang commune shall be left with a land area of 2,616 ha and a population of 11,279.
To establish Long Hoa commune of Phu Tan district on the basis of a land area of 780 ha and a population of 8,757 of Long Son commune which shall be left with a land area of 1,299 ha and a population of 13,006.
To transfer a land area of 165 ha and a population of 3,484 of Long An commune, Tan Chau district, to Tan Chau township for management. Tan Chau township shall have a land area of 751 ha and a population of 36,478. Long An commune shall be left with a land area of 1,197 ha and a population of 13,918.- (Summary)
