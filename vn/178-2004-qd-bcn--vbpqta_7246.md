# Quyết định 178/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 178/2004/QD-BCN of December 31, 2004 approving the Charter on organization and operation of Hanoi Beer-Alcohol-Beverage Corporation after the parent company-subsidiary company model
Under the 8-chapter and 48-article Charter, the parent company (Hanoi Beer-Alcohol-Beverage Corporation - HABECO) is headquartered at No. 183 Hoang Hoa Tham street, Ba Dinh district, Hanoi, and has a charter capital of VND 969,583 million by December 31, 2003.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
