# Circular 13/1998/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No.13/1998/TT-BTC of February 5, 1998 guiding the collection and remittance of the import petrol and oil surcharge into the State budget.
To collect and remit into the State budget the surcharge levied on import petrol and oils of various kinds from February 1st, 1998 at the following rates (calculated on CIF import prices):
- Petrol of various kinds (except for aircraft petrol): 15%,
- Naphtha, Refomate and other preparations for mixing petrol: 15%,
- Diesel, kerosene and aircraft fuels: 18%,
- Fuel oil: 10%.- (Summary)
