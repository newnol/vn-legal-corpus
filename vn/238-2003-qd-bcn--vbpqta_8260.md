# Decision 238/2003/QD-BCN

DECISION No. 238/2003/QD-BCN of December 29, 2003 transforming Hong Nam Mechanical Factory of Vietnam Industrial Construction Corporation into Hong Nam Mechanical Joint-Stock Company
To transform Hong Nam Mechanical Factory of Vietnam Industrial Construction Corporation into Hong Nam Mechanical Joint-Stock Company (HOMECO).
Its head-office: Linh Nam commune, Thanh Tri rural district, Hanoi.
Its charter capital is VND 3.5 billion, with 51% of which being held by the State and 49% being sold to its laborers.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
