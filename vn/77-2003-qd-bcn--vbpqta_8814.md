# Quyết định 77/2003/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 77/2003/QD-BCN of May 6, 2003 promulgating the Regulation on preservation, reception, delivery and exchange of national reserve cotton seeds
Issued together with this Decision was the Regulation on preservation, reception, delivery and exchange of national reserve cotton seeds.
National reserve cotton seeds are the State's properties which are assigned to enterprises for preservation. National reserve cotton seeds shall be used only for providing support to cotton growers in cases where the cotton already planted are damaged due to natural disasters, therefore seeds are needed for re-growing in order to ensure raw materials for the State and stabilize production and life of cotton growers.
National reserve cotton seeds must be preserved, received and delivered strictly according to regulations. Enterprises conducting the preservation of national reserve cotton seeds must open monitoring books right at the beginning, clearly inscribing the quantity of each bale and each strain, the preservation methods, as well as quantitative and qualitative developments of cotton seeds.- (Summary)
