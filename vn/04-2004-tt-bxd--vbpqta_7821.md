# Thông tư 04/2004/TT-BXD

CIRCULAR No. 04/2004/TT-BXD of July 7, 2004 guiding the implementation of the Prime Minister's Decision No. 78/2004/QD-TTg of May 7, 2004 which adjusts and supplements a number of mechanisms and policies in order to speed up the construction progress of population clusters and lines as well as dwelling houses in frequently flooded provinces in the Mekong River delta
This Circular guides in detail a number of contents of above-said Decision No. 78/2004/QD-TTg and takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
