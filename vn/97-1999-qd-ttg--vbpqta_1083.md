# Decision 97/1999/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.97/1999/QD-TTg of April 7, 1999 on the merger of the Cultural House Vocational Guidance Center into the grassroots Culture and Information Department
To merge the Cultural House Vocational Guidance Center into the grassroots Culture and Information Department.
To annul Point 3, Item e, Article 2 of Decision No.123/TTg of March 1st, 1995 of the Prime Minister on the rearrangement and re-organization of units attached to the Ministry of Culture and Information.- (Summary)
