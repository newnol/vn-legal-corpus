# Quyết định 02/2005/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 02/2005/QD-BCN of January 10, 2005 approving the Charter on organization and operation of the Industrial Technical Secondary School
Under its Charter issued together with this Decision, the Industrial Technical Secondary School, ITSS, is a vocational establishment attached to the Industry Ministry and functions to train and foster human resources of intermediate level and technical workers of various disciplines. It also functions as a scientific and technological research institute in service of management, production and business activities of the industry sector and socio-economic development.
Its head-office is located at No. 202, Tran Nguyen Han street, Bac Giang provincial capital, Bac Giang province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
