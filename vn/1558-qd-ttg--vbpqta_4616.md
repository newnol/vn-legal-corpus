# Quyết định 1558/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Establishing Bac Lieu University under the People’s Committee of Bac Lieu province
Under this Decision, which takes effect on the date of its signing, Bac Lieu University is established on the basis of Bac Lieu Teachers Training College and the Continuing Education Center of Bac Lieu province, and operates under the University Regulation promulgated together with the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 153/2003/QD-TTg of July 30, 2003.- (Summary)
