# Decision 1140/2004/QD-NHNN

DECISION No. 1140/2004/QD-NHNN of September 10, 2004 canceling bank codes; amending and supplementing codes of provinces and cities; and amending payment transaction names of joint-venture banks and foreign banks' branches in Appendix No. 1 and at Points 4 and 5 of Appendix No. 3 to the State Bank's Governor's Decision No. 1247/2003/QD-NHNN of October 20, 2003 promulgating the Regulation on the system of bank codes used in payment transactions via the State Bank
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
