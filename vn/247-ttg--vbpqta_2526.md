# Decision 247/TTg

DECISION No.247-TTg of April 19, 1997 appointing additional members of the Ad-hoc Committee for settling matters related to the graves of Vietnamese army volunteers who died in Laos.
Mr. Le Van Han, Deputy Chief of the Political General Department of the Ministry of National Defense, is appointed as its Standing Deputy Head;
Mr. Le Duy Dong, Vice Minister of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs is appointed as its Deputy Head.- (Summary)
