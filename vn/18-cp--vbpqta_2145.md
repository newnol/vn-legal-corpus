# Decree 18/CP

DECREE No.18-CP OF APRIL 4, 1996 OF THE GOVERNMENT ISSUING THE LIST OF COMMODITIES AND THEIR IMPORT TARIFFS IN IMPLEMENTING THE PROGRAM OF REDUCING TARIFFS ON GOODS IMPORTED FROM EUROPEAN COMMUNITY (EU) COUNTRIES IN THE YEARS 1996-1997
THE GOVERNMENT
Pursuant to the Law on Organization of the Government of September 30, 1992;
Pursuant to the Law on Export and Import Taxes of December 26,1991 and the Law on Amendments and Complements to the Law on Export and Import Taxes of July 5, 1993;
Pursuant to the Agreement on Textiles and Garments Trade between the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the European Community initialed on December 15, 1992 and the letters of exchange signed to complement this Agreement;
At the proposal of the Minister of Finance,
DECREES:
Article 1.- To issue along with this Decree the List of Commodities and their Import Tariffs to be collected in the years 1996 and 1997 pursuant to the Agreement on Textiles and Garments Trade between the Socialist Republic of Vietnam and the European Community initialed on December 15, 1992 and the letters of exchange signed to complement this Agreement.
Article 2.- The List of Commodities and the Import Tariffs of each commodity mentioned in Article 1 of this Decree shall apply only when this commodity has been certified as to its origin in the export country as stipulated in the above-said Agreement.
Article 3.- This Decree takes effect on the 1st of January 1996.
Article 4.- The Ministers, the Heads of ministerial-level agencies, the Heads of the agencies attached to the Government and the Presidents of the People's Committees in the provinces and cities directly under the Central Government shall have to implement this Decree.
On behalf of the Government
For the Prime Minister
Deputy Prime Minister
PHAN VAN KHAI
