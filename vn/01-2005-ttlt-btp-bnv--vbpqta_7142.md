# Thông tư liên tịch 01/2005/TTLT/BTP-BNV

JOINT CIRCULAR No. 01/2005/TTLT/BTP-BNV of January 24, 2005 guiding the implementation of a number of articles of the Government’s Decree No. 122/2004/ND-CP of May 18, 2004 on the functions, tasks, powers and organizational structures of the Legal Departments of ministries, ministerial-level agencies, Government-attached agencies, professional agencies under provincial/municipal People’s Committees and of State enterprises
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
