# Resolution 13/2001/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION No. 13/2001/NQ-CP of December 4, 2001 on the Government�s November 2001 regular meeting
The Government held its November 2001 regular meeting on November 29 and 30, 2001, discussing and resolving the following issues:
- It gave its opinions on the scheme on agricultural restructuring, the processing of agricultural and forestry products and rural production and business lines (the 2001-2005 plan). The Government stressed that the agricultural and rural economic restructuring must meet the requirements of promptness, efficiency and sustainability; efforts must be concentrated on implementing a number of breakthrough solutions in the process of agricultural and rural economic restructuring such as the creation of conditions for commodity-producing farmers to sign product-consumption contracts with enterprises, breeds and varieties, sciences and technologies in service of agriculture, and mechanism to ensure that farmers can borrow adequate capital for development of commodity production� The economic restructuring must ensure the synchronous settlement of agricultural, rural and farmer-related issues.
- It gave opinions on the Bill Amending the Law on Organization of the National Assembly; the Bill Amending and Supplementing a Number of Articles of the Law on Election of Deputies to the National Assembly; the Accounting Bill; the Draft Ordinance on Prostitution Prevention and Combat; and the Draft Ordinance on Bidding.
- It considered the socio-economic situation in November and the first 11 months of 2001.- (Summary)
