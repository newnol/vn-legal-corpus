# Quyết định 43/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 43/2004/QD-BCN of June 8, 2004 amending Clause 3, Article 1 of the Industry Minister's Decision No. 20/2004/QD-BCN of March 26, 2004 transforming Binh An Glue Enterprise of An Lac Shoe Company into Binh An Glue Joint-Stock Company
To amend Clause 3, Article 1 of above-said Decision No. 20/2004/QD-BCN on the preferences given to the enterprise's laborers: a total of 2,050 preferential shares valued at VND 61.5 million shall be sold to 34 laborers in the enterprise.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
