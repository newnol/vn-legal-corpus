# Quyết định 71/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 71/2004/QD-BCN of August 5, 2004 amending Clause 1, Article 1 of the Industry Minister's Decision No. 170/2003/QD-BCN of October 23, 2003 transforming the Grinding Wheels Company into Hai Duong Grinding Wheels Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, Hai Duong Grinding Wheels Joint-Stock Company shall have the charter capital of VND 7,490,500,000; with 61.8% of which to be held by the State and 38.2% to be sold to its laborers.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
