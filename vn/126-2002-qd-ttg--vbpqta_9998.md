# Quyết định 126/2002/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 126/2002/QD-TTg of September 27, 2002 establishing the Economics College under Hue University
To establish the Economics College under Hue University on the basis of the Economics Faculty under Hue University.
The Economics College under Hue University is tasked to train and foster university-degree officials in the fields of economics, conduct scientific research and provide services for training and research activities.- (Summary)
