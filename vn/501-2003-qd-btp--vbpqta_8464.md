# Quyết định 501/2003/QĐ-BTP

DECISION No. 501/2003/QD-BTP of October 29, 2003 promulgating the Regulation on organization and operation of the Emulation and Commendation Council of the judicial sector
The Emulation and Commendation Council of the judicial sector is set up under the Justice Minister's decision and functions to assist the Minister in the work of emulation and commendation in the judicial sector.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
