# Circular 28/2002/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 28/2002/TT-BTC of March 26, 2002 guiding the management and use of funding for the free-of-charge provision of several types of newspapers and journals to regions inhabited by ethnic minority peoples and mountainous areas
The Circular guides in detail the newspaper - and journal- receiving subjects as well as the distribution quantity; and funding allocation and management.- (Summary)
