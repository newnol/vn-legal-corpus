# Thông tư 05/1999/TT-BTM

CIRCULAR No. 05/1999/TT-BTM OF MARCH 18, 1999 AMENDING AND SUPPLEMENTING CLAUSE 5, SECTION II OF CIRCULAR No. 03/1999/TT-BTM OF JANUARY 15, 1999 OF THE MINISTRY OF TRADE
Pursuant to the provisions of the Prime Minister�s Decision No. 254/1998/QD-TTg of December 30, 1998 on the management of goods import and export in 1999;
The Ministry of Trade hereby readjusts Clause 5, Section II of Circular 03/1999/TT-BTM of January 15, 1999 of the Ministry of Trade guiding the implementation of the Prime Minister�s Decision No. 254/1998/QD-TTg of December 30, 1999 as follows:
Domestic enterprises investing the manufacture and assembly of motorcycle in IKD form shall be entitled to import IKD components, according to their production capacity in accordance with the certification of full qualifications for the assembly of motorized two-wheelers in IKD form by the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment after its examination and consultation with the Ministry of Industry and the Ministry of Communications and Transport; with the list of import details, the list of localized details certified by the Ministry of Science, Technology and Environment and with other provisions of Circular No. 04/1998/TT-BTM of March 12, 1998 of the Trade Ministry.
This provision shall also replace Point 2.3, Clause 2, Section II of Circular 04/1998/TT-BTM of March 12, 1998 of the Ministry of Trade guiding the import of components for motorcycles in IKD form. The other provisions of the Trade Ministry�s Circular No. 04/1998/TT-BTM of March 12, 1998 remain effective.
This Circular takes effect after its signing and is an inseparable part of Circular No. 03/1999/TT-BTM of January 15,1999 of the Trade Ministry.
For the Minister of Trade
Vice Minister
MAI VAN DAU
