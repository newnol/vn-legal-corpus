# Quyết định 04/2003/QĐ-BKHCN

DECISION No. 04/2003/QD-BKHCN of February 18, 2003 promulgating the Program of action for accelerating the propagation and dissemination of legislation on science and technology (the 2003-2007 period)
To issue together with this Decision the Program of action for accelerating the propagation and dissemination of legislation on science and technology (the 2003-2007 period).
The contents of legislation to be propagated and disseminated cover all legal documents in the fields under the State management scope of the Ministry of Science and Technology, including:
- Legislation on science and technology
- Legislation on industrial property.
- Legislation on technology transfer.
- Legislation on standardization, measurement and quality control.
- Legislation on radiation safety and control.
The propagation and dissemination forms:
- Organizing direct introduction of legal documents.
- Organizing training courses on legislation.
- Publishing and widely distributing books on science and technology legislation.
- Loading legal documents onto Internet.
- Propagating legislation on the mass media.- (Summary)
