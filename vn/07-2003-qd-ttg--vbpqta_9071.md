# Quyết định 07/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 07/2003/QD-TTg of January 9, 2003 approving the planning on the system of student defense education centers in the 2001-2010 period
The 2001-2010 planning on the system of student defense education centers was approved by the Prime Minister with the following contents:
To build the system of student defense education centers throughout the country, ensuring the defense education for 70% of the number of students enrolled every year as well as part of intermediate vocational and senior-high school students in localities where the centers are headquartered.
Defense education centers are organized in the following forms:
- Defense education centers under the ministries and central-level agencies;
- Defense education centers under the provincial/municipal People’s Committees.
Under the planning, 16 defense education centers shall have been formed throughout the country by 2010.- (Summary)
