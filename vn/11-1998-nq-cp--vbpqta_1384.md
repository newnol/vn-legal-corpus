# Nghị quyết 11/1998/NQ-CP

THE GOVERNMENT
RESOLUTION No. 11/1998/NQ-CP of September 1st, 1998 on the Government's August 1998 regular meeting
The Government held its monthly regular meeting on August 26-27, 1998 to discuss:
- The project on "agricultural and rural development in the industrialization and modernization period"; and
- The Bill on Organization of the People's Councils and People's Committees (amended).
The Government gave its comments on the draft legislative program of the Xth National Assembly. It assigned the Ministry of Justice to assume the prime responsibility and coordinate with the Government Office in summing up opinions and reporting them to the Prime Minister so that the latter may propose the National Assembly Standing Committee to add to the specific 1999-2000 programs draft laws and ordinances of great urgency for socio-economic development as well as for national defense and security. The Government requested the ministries and agencies to expeditiously study and elaborate bills and/or ordinances already assigned to them, ensure their quality, then submit them to the Government, the National Assembly Standing Committee and the National Assembly for adoption in accordance with the prescribed time-limit; at the same time, to draft documents guiding the implementation thereof, which shall be submitted to the Government for adoption so that the laws and ordinances can be enforced right after they take effect.
The Government requested the ministries, branches and localities to continue restructuring their organizational apparatuses in a rational and efficient manner; step up the renovation of working style, overcome authoritarianism, trouble-making, delay and impediment in work; at the same time, to raise the capability of directing and organizing the implementation so as to create a new change, contributing to the successful performance of the socio-economic tasks in the remaining months of the year and in the whole 1998.- (Summary)
