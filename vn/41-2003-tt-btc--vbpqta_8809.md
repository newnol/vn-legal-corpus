# Thông tư 41/2003/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 41/2003/TT-BTC of May 6, 2003 guiding the implementation of Article 5 of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 132/2002/QD-TTg of October 8, 2002 on allotment of production and residential land to local ethnic minority people in the Central Highlands
The central budget shall provide support at the level of VND 4 million/ha for the area of land actually reclaimed under the schemes approved by competent State agencies.
The maximum support level from the central budget as compensation for land-reclamation remuneration, including yield, when land areas under annual trees are recovered from households and/or individuals, shall be VND 4 million/ha.
The specific support levels for each locality shall be determined on the basis of decisions on land recovery (recovery of gardens) and decisions on land allotment. Based on the central budget support levels and the practical situation in the localities, the provincial/municipal Finance-Pricing Services shall elaborate and submit support levels to the provincial-level People’s Committees for approval.
This Circular guides the reception and assignment of recovered gardens to households.- (Summary)
