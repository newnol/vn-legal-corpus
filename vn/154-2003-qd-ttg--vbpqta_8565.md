# Quyết định 154/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.154/2003/QD-TTg of July 30, 2003 establishing the People's Security University
To establish the People's Security University on the basis of the People's Security Academy's branch in Ho Chi Minh City. The University is attached to the Ministry of Public Security and subject to the State management over education by the Ministry of Education and Training.- (Summary)
