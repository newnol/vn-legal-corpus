# Nghị định 18/1998/NĐ-CP

DECREE No.18/1998/ND-CP of March 24, 1998 on the establishment of a number of communes and district townships in Dak Nong, Ea Sup and Dak Mil districts, Dak Lak province.
To establish Dak Som commune of Dak Nong district on the basis of a land area of 7,500 hectares and a population of 1,315 of Dak Plao commune.
To establish Cu Kbang commune of Ea Sup district on the basis of a land area of 8,060 hectares and a population of 2,435 of Ea Le commune.
To annex a land area of 4,000 hectares and a population of 568 of Ea Sup commune to Ea Le commune.
To establish Ea Sup township and the district township of Ea Sup district on the basis of a land area of 1,350 hectares and a population of 5882 of Ea Sup commune.
To rename Ea Sup commune as Cu M'Lan commune.
To establish Ia Lop commune of Ea Sup district on the basis of a land area of 46,573 hectares and a population of 2,256 of Ea Rok commune.
To establish Dak Song commune of Dak Mil district on the basis of a land area of 11,745 hectares and a population of 2,936 of Thuan Hanh commune.
To establish Dak R'la commune of Dak Mil district on the basis of a land area of 10,204 hectares and a population of 4,143 of Dak Gan commune.- (Summary)
