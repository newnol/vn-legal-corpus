# Directive 05/2007/CT-BBCVT

DIRECTIVE
Enhancing the management of radio frequency transmission equipment installed in fishing vessels to secure the safety for radio information on the sea
The Ministry of Post and Telematics requires concerned localities and agencies to perform several tasks in order to secure the safety of radio information on the sea.- (Summary)
