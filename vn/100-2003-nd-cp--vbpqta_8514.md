# Nghị định 100/2003/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 100/2003/ND-CP of August 29, 2003 adjusting the administrative boundaries and establishing communes in Ea H'Leo, Krong Nang, Krong Ana, Krong No and Dak R'Lap districts, Dak Lak province
To adjust the administrative boundaries of Ea Hiao commune of Ea H'Leo district and Dlie Ya commune of Krong Nang district.
To establish Cu Klong and Ea Tan communes of Krong Nang district.
To establish Dray Sap commune of Krong Ana district.
To establish Nam N'Dir commune of Krong No district.
To rename Nam Nung commune of Krong No district Nam Nung commune.
To establish Dak Ru commune of Dak R'Lap district.- (Summary)
