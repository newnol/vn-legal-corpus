# Thông tư liên tịch 01/2006/TTLT-BTP-BNV-BTC

JOINT CIRCULAR
Guiding the implementation of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 233/2005/QD-TTg of September 26, 2005, on job-based responsibility allowance regime for executors and notaries public
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” annulling Joint Circular No. 34A/2001/TTLT/BTCCBCP-BTC-BTP of June 21, 2001, of the Government’s Commission for Organization and Personnel (now the Ministry of Home Affairs), the Ministry of Finance and the Ministry of Justice.
The job-based responsibility allowance regime for district- and provincial-level executors applies as from October 1, 2004, while that for notaries public applies as from October 23, 2005 (the effective date of Decision No. 233/2005/QD-TTg).- (Summary)
