# Quyết định 47/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 47/2004/QD-BCN of June 15, 2004 amending Clause 1 and Clause 3, Article 1 of the Industry Minister's Decision No. 21/2004/QD-BCN of March 30, 2004 on transformation of Rang Dong Light Bulb and Vacuum Flask Company into Rang Dong Light Bulb and Vacuum Flask Joint Stock Company
To amend Clause 1 and Clause 3, Article 1 of the Industry Minister's Decision No. 21/2004/QD-BCN of March 30, 2004 on transformation of Rang Dong Light Bulb and Vacuum Flask Company into Rang Dong Light Bulb and Vacuum Flask Joint Stock Company regarding the charter capital structure (the State's share percentage: 51%; shares to be sold to the company's laborers: 40.91%; and shares to be sold to outsiders: 8.09%) and the preferences for the company's laborers (preferential shares to be sold to the company's laborers is 174,333 valued at VND 5,229,990,000).
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
