# Quyết định 68/2006/QĐ-TTg

DECISION
Establishing the State Council for Pre-Acceptance Test of Construction Works
The State Council for Pre-Acceptance Test of Construction Works shall assist the Prime Minister in controlling the quality of important national construction works through its inspection and pre-acceptance test activities.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and replaces Decision No. 211/2003/QD-TTg of October 14, 2003.- (Summary)
