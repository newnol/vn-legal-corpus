# Quyết định 23/2005/QĐ-BVHTT

DECISION
On ranking a national relic
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO,” the historical relic of headquarters of the Party Central Committee’s Board for Ideological Education (1949-1953) in Tan Trao commune, Son Duong district, Tuyen Quang province, is now a national relic.- (Summary)
