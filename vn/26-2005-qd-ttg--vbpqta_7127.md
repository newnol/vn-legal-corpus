# Quyết định 26/2005/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 26/2005/QD-TTg of January 31, 2005 on the establishment of the Labor and Social Affairs University
With this Decision, the Prime Minister decides to set up the Labor and Social Affairs University, a tertiary training establishment of the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs, on the basis of the Labor and Social Affairs College.
