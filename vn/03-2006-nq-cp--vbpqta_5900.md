# Resolution 03/2006/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
On the Government’s February 2006 regular meeting
On February 27 and 28, the Government held its February regular meeting, where it discussed:
- A scheme on export promotion in the 2006-2010 period, presented by the Minister of Trade;
- The Bill on Sending Laborers Abroad, presented by the Minister of Labor, War Invalids and Social Affairs;
- The Bill on Notarization, presented by the Minister of Justice;
- The Bill on Dikes, presented by the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development;
- The Bill on Residence, presented by the Ministry of Public Security.
- A report on socio-economic situation in the first two months of 2006 and results of production, business, service and investment activities in February and a report on trade activities in February, presented by the Trade Minister.
The Government also heard the Minister of Trade reporting on some matters of concern in the WTO negotiation process.- (Summary)
