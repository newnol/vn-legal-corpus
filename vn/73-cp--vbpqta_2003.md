# Decree 73-CP

Decree No.73-CP of November 22, 1996 of the Government on the establishment of the communes and townships of Kon Plong, Dak Glei and Ngoc Hoi and Dak Ha district and Kon Tum provincial town in Kon Tum province:
- To establsih Po E commune and Kon Plong township in Kon Plong district
- To establish Dak Man commune and Dak Glei township in Dak Glei district
- To divide Duc Nong commune of Ngoc Hoi district into Dak Duc and Dak Nong communes
- To establish Ngok Wang commune of Dak Ha district
- To establish Dak Ro Va commune of Kon Tum provincial town.- (Summary)
