# Decision 11/2005/QD-BGTVT

DECISION No. 11/2005/QD-BGTVT of January 17, 2005 amending and supplementing a branch standard
This Decision amends and supplements the branch standard 22 TCN 269-2000 “Signaling rules for Vietnam’s inland waterway” issued together with Decision No. 4099/2000/QD-BGTVT of December 28, 2000 of the Minister of Transport.
This Decision takes effect as from June 15, 2005.- (Summary)
