# Quyết định 100/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 100/2004/QD-BCN of September 24, 2004 transforming Tan Phu Plastic Factory of Vietnam Plastic Company into Tan Phu Plastic Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, which takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette, Tan Phu Plastic Joint-Stock Company (TAPLAST) is headquartered at No. 323 Luy Ban Bich road, Hoa Thanh ward, Tan Phu district, Ho Chi Minh city.
Its charter capital is VND 8.7 billion, 51% of which to be held by the State, 39.68% sold to its laborers, and 9.32% to outsiders.- (Summary)
