# Quyết định 49/2001/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 49/2001/QD-TTg of April 10, 2001 transforming SAVIMEX Economic Cooperation and Import-Export Company into SAVIMEX Economic Cooperation and Import-Export Corporation
To transform SAVIMEX Economic Cooperation and Import-Export Company (Sai Gon Trading Corporation) which is under the People�s Committee of Ho Chi Minh City into SAVIMEX Economic Cooperation and Import-Export Corporation - SAVIMEX.
Headquarter of the Corporation: 194 Nguyen Cong Tru, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City.
Charter capital of the Corporation: VND 45,000,000,000, in which the State�s equities represent 25%, equities sold to laborers of the Corporation, 50%, and equities sold to shareholders outside the Corporation, 25%.
SAVIMEX shall be engaged in the following business lines:
- Domestically producing and trading well as exporting processed timbers and wood products;
- Export and import of supplies, machinery and equipment for the processing of timbers and wood products;
- Civil, industrial, agricultural, forestry and fishery construction;
- Office leasing and dealing in dwelling houses;
- Other production and business lines as prescribed by law.- (Summary)
