# Quyết định 121/2004/QĐ-BCN

DECISION No. 121/2004/QD-BCN of November 2, 2004 transforming the Construction and Machine Erection Engineering Factory of the Mining Construction Company into Construction and Machine Erection Joint-Stock Company
Under this Decision, the Construction and Machine Erection Joint-Stock Company, abbreviated to COMEC, shall have a charter capital of VND 2,375,000,000; of which the State's equities represent 45%, equities sold to laborers of the factory, 51.3% and equities sold to other subjects, 3.7%.
Its head-office is located in Cam Tu ward, Cam Pha town, Quang Ninh province.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
