# Decision 183/1999/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 183/1999/QD-TTg of September 9, 1999 permitting the establishment of Vietnam Study Promotion Fund
To establish Vietnam Study Promotion Fund under the Vietnam Study Promotion Society. The Vietnam Study Promotion Fund shall operate for non-profit purposes on the principle of voluntary contributions by domestic and foreign organizations and/or individuals with a view to financing the training and education activities, promoting good teaching and learning; and it shall comply with the Statute of the Vietnam Study Promotion Fund.
The Ministry of Finance shall approve the Statute of the Vietnam Study Promotion Fund and inspect the fund�s activities.- (Summary)
