# Directive 03/2006/CT-TTg

DIRECTIVE
On a number of measures to continue handling development investment credit loans for building or reformation of offshore fishing or fishing service ships
With this Directive, the Prime Minister instructs concerned ministries and provincial/municipal People’s Committees to perform several tasks in order to accelerate the handling of the State’s development investment credit loans for building or reformation of offshore fishing or fishing service ships.- (Summary)
