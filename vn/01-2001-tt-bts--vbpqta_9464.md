# Circular 01/2001/TT-BTS

Circular No. 01/2001/TT-BTS of June 8, 2001 amending Point a, Item 6 of Joint Circular No. 04/1998/TTLT-TS-KHDT-TC-NHNNVN of December 17, 1998 of the Ministry of Aquatic Resources, the Ministry of Planning and Investment, the Ministry of Finance and the State Bank of Vietnam which guides the management and use of investment credit capital under the State�s plan for projects on the building and modification of off-shore fishing ships and fishing service ships
In order to create conditions for localities to well invest in production development with a view to making the off-shore fishing program efficient, the Ministry of Aquatic Resources issued this Circular, amending Point a, Clause 6 of Joint Circular No. 04/1998/TTLT-TS-KHDT-TC-NHNNVN.- (Summary)
