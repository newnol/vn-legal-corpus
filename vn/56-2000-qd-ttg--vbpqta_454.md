# Decision 56/2000/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 56/2000/QD-TTg of May 22, 2000 on transforming the General Handicraft and Art Product Export Company under the Industry Service of Ho Chi Minh City into a joint stock company
To transform the General Handicraft and Art Product Export Company, which is a State enterprise under the Industry Service of Ho Chi Minh City, into General Handicraft and Art Product Export Joint Stock Company, abbreviated names HARTEXCO.
Headquarter: 139 Hai Ba Trung Street, District 3, Ho Chi Minh City
Charter capital: 14,000,000,000 VND, in which the State�s equity represents 30%, equity sold to laborers of the company as well as subjects outside the company represents 70%.
Business lines:
- Production of and trading in lacquer, ceramics and wood products of various kinds.
- Trading in agricultural, foodstuff products, industrial foodstuffs, aquatic and marine products, glassware and inox products, shampoo and cleansing and bleaching substances.
- Exportation of handicraft and fine art articles, products made of rush, jute, coconut fibers, rattan, bamboo, leaves, wood products, carpets, lacquer, ceramics and porcelains, products made of silk, stone, plaster, cement, mica, bone, tortoise shells, coral, mother-of-pearl, curving materials, leathers, imitation leathers, multi-purpose hardware, as well as fine art jewelry.
- Importation of raw materials, supplies, machinery and equipment in service of the production of handicraft articles, interior decoration items, electric and mechanical products as well as office equipment.- (Summary)
