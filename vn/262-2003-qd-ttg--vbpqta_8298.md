# Quyết định 262/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 262/2003/QD-TTg of December 10, 2003 on setting up the External Affair and Frontier Issue Service of Ha Giang province
This Decision prescribes the setting up of the External Affair and Frontier Issue Service under the People's Committee of Ha Giang province on the basis of reorganizing the External Relation Board of the provincial People's Committee.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
