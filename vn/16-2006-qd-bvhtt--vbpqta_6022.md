# Quyết định 16/2006/QĐ-BVHTT

DECISION
On the ranking of a national relic
Under this Decision, the architectural-artistic relic of Phu Cuu communal house in Van Giang commune, Ninh Giang district, Hai Duong province, is ranked as a national relic.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
