# Decision 58/2000/QĐ-TTg

Decision No. 58/2000/QD-TTg of May 24, 2000 on transforming the Import-Export Trading and Service Company of District 10, Ho Chi Minh City, into a joint-stock company
To transform the Import-Export Trading and Service Company of District 10, Ho Chi Minh City, which is a State enterprise, into Thien Nam Trading Inport-Export Joint Stock Company, abbreviated name TENIMEX.
Headquarter: No. 355 - 365 Ngo Gia Tu Street, Ward 3, District 10, Ho Chi Minh City.
Charter capital: 13,000,000,000 dong, in which the State�s equity represents 35%, equity sold to laborers of the company and other subjects outside the company represents 65%.
Business lines:
- Direct importation and exportation of agricultural, aquatic and marine products, food, foodstuff, electronic products, motorized vehicles, raw materials and fuel, supplies machinery and equipment, fertilizer, transport means, construction and interior decoration materials, cosmetics.
- Provision of services on entrusted import and/or export of commodities which are allowed by the State
- Provision of pawning, tourism, hotel, restaurant, karaoke services, representative office leasing, gold and silver trading, auto saloon, car repair, color-photograph developing, office machines, photocopy and termite-killing...
- Sale, purchase, agent and bailment of industrial products; food, foodstuff, materials and equipment, transport means, home-made alcohol and cigarette, agricultural, aquatic and marine products, mechanical, electric and electronic products, construction and interior-decoration materials, fertilizer, insecticide, supplies, machinery and equipment, cosmetics, books and cultural products.- (Summary)
