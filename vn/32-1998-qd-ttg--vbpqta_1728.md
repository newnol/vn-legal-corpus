# Decision 32/1998/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No.32/1998/QD-TTg of February 10, 1998 on the establishment of organizations attached to the Vietnam Television Station.
To establish the Financial and Accounting Department and the Planning Department attached to the Vietnam Television Station on the basis of re-organizing the Planning and Financial Department.-
DECISION No.34/1998/QD-TTg of February 11, 1998 on the establishment of the judicial training school.
To establish of the judicial training school under the Ministry of Justice. The school is tasked to train judges and other judicial titles through full-time and in-service courses.- (Summary)
