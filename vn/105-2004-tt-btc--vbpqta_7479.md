# Thông tư 105/2004/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 105/2004/TT-BTC of November 9, 2004 guiding the regime of debt recovery expenditures for liquidation of people's credit funds which have their operation permits withdrawn or are carrying out liquidation or dissolution procedures
This Circular shall not apply to people's credit funds which have their operation permits withdrawn due to merger, division, separation or consolidation.
It takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
