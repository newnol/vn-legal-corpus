# Decision 1274/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 1274/QD-TTg of September 26, 2001 transforming the State-run Housing Development Company of Ba Ria-Vung Tau province into a joint-stock company
To transform the State-run House Development Company under the Construction Service of Ba Ria-Vung Tau province into Baria-Vungtau House Development Joint-Stock Company (HODE-CO).
Its head office: 02 Truong Cong Dinh street, ward 2, Vung Tau city, Ba Ria-Vung Tau province.
Its charter capital is VND 18,102,300,000, of which State equities represent 40%, and equities sold to the subjects inside and outside the enterprise make up 60%.
Baria-Vungtau House Development Joint-Stock Company is engaged in the following business lines:
- Trading in houses and construction materials;
- Building and developing houses in urban population quarters and industrial parks; building and installing civil and industrial works.- (Summary)
