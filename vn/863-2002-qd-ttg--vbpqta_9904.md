# Decision 863/2002/QD-TTg

DECISION No. 863/QD-TTg of September 30, 2002 appointing Mr. Le Van Bang to be the director of the Working Committee for Foreign Non-Governmental Organizations
Mr. Chu Tuan Cap shall be relieved from the post of the director of the Working Committee for Foreign Non-Governmental Organizations. To appoint Mr. Le Van Bang, Vice-Minister for Foreign Affairs, to this post.- (Summary)
