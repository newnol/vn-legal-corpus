# Quyết định 1995/2004/QĐ-BYT

DECISION No. 1995/2004/QD-BYT of June 4, 2004 promulgating the Regulation on granting, management and use of certificates of voluntary blood donation and the form thereof
Certificates of voluntary blood donation, which are uniformly used in the public medical establishments having the function of collecting blood, shall be directly granted to voluntary blood donors right after they donate blood at voluntary donation places according to Joint Circular No. 12/TTLB-BYT-BTC of February 25, 2004 of the Health Ministry and the Finance Ministry. For each time of blood donation, a blood donor shall be granted one certificate.
These certificates shall be valid for free-of-charge blood transfusion when their holders need blood transfusion at the public medical establishments throughout the country.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
