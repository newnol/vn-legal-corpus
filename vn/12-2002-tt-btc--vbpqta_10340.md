# Circular 12/2002/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 12/2002/TT-BTC of February 4, 2002 guiding the use of funds allocated by the central budget to ensure traffic order and safety
This Circular guides the use of funds allocated by the central budget to the ministries and the central agencies which directly participate in the work of ensuring traffic order and safety.- (Summary)
