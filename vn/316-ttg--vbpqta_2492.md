# Decision 316/TTg

DECISION No.316-TTg of May 15, 1997 on the dismissal of the Vice Chairman of the Steering Committee for Irrigation Development and Rural Construction in the Mekong Delta Region.
Mr. Phan Si Ky, Vice Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development shall no longer hold the post of Vice Chairman of the Steering Committee for Irrigation Development and Rural Construction in the Mekong Delta Region.- (Summary)
