# Resolution 17/2006/NQ-CP

RESOLUTION
Adjusting the land use planning up to 2010 and the five-year (2006-2010) land use plan of Ben Tre province
The People’s Committee of Ben Tre province shall base itself on this Resolution to organize the implementation of the land use planning and plan in combination with the fulfillment of socio-economic development objectives as well as defense, security and eco-environmental protection tasks.- (Summary)
