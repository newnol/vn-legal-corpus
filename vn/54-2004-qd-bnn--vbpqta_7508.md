# Quyết định 54/2004/QĐ-BNN

DECISION No. 54/2004/QD-BNN of October 28, 2004 promulgating the Working Regulation of the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development
The Regulation prescribes the working principles, regimes, responsibilities and style of, and order of settling matters by, the Ministry's leadership as well as heads, officials and employees of the Ministry's agencies and units.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette and replaces Decision No. 40/1998/QD-BNN of March 2, 1998.- (Summary)
