# Quyết định 198/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 198/2003/QD-TTg of September 24, 2003 transforming the Transportation Service Company II into one being experimentally organized and operating after the parent company-affiliate company model
To approve the scheme on transforming the Transportation Service Company II under the Ministry of Communications and Transport into one being experimentally organized and operating after the parent company-affiliate company model.
To rename the Transportation Service Company II the Multi-Mode Transportation Company, which shall operate after the parent company-affiliate company as follows:
- The parent company (the Multi-Mode Transportation Company) is an independent-accounting State enterprise with legal person status, directly engaged in production/business activities, has capital invested in affiliate companies, and takes responsibility for preservation and development of State capital at the company and capital invested in affiliate companies.
- Affiliate companies are joint-stock companies and two-plus member limited liability companies with the parent company holding dominant shares or dominant contributed capital (over 50% of the charter capital).
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
