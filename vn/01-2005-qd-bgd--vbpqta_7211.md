# Quyết định 01/2005/QĐ-BGD

DECISION No. 01/2005/QD-BGDDT of January 12, 2005 promulgating the framework tertiary education programs for humanities disciplines of university degree
Promulgated together with this Decision are 8 framework tertiary education programs for use in faculties, institutes and universities tasked to train the above-said disciplines.
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in the Official Gazette.- (Summary)
