# Quyết định 65/2003/QĐ-TTg

DECISION No. 65/2003/QD-TTg of April 22, 2003 approving the master plan on reorganization and renovation of State enterprises under the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development till 2005
Enterprises under the Ministry, where the State continues to hold 100% of their charter capital till 2005: 3 public-utility enterprises and 3 business enterprises.
Corporations which remain to be State corporations: 9.
The roadmap for reorganization of enterprises under the Ministry is as follows:
- Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding more than 50% of their charter capital: 6 in 2002 and 2003, and 4 in 2004.
- Enterprises to be equitized with the State holding under 50% of their charter capital or holding no shares: 4 in 2002 and 2003, and 4 in 2004.
- Enterprises to be financially restructured by the State for ownership transfer (to be equitized, assigned, sold, dissolved or declared bankrupt): 2 from 2002 till the end of 2004.
The master plan also sets the roadmap for reorganization of enterprises under corporations.- (Summary)
