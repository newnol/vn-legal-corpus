# Circular 03/2002/TT-BLĐTBXH

CIRCULAR No. 03/2002/TT-BLDTBXH of January 9, 2002 guiding the implementation of the Government�s Decrees No. 28/CP of March 28, 1997 and No. 03/2001/ND-CP of January 11, 2001 on renewing the management of wages and incomes in State-run construction enterprises
Subject to this Circular are State enterprises engaged in the construction and installation of projects or project items, funded with State budget capital, State development investment credit capital, credit capital guaranteed by the State and development investment capital of State enterprises.
This Circular provides guidance on wage expenses for the elaboration of the construction projects� cost estimates and wage payment mechanism of the enterprises after winning bids or being appointed bidders.- (Summary)
