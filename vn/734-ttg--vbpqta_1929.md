# Decision 734/TTg

Decision No.734-TTg of September 6, 1997 on the establishment of organizations providing legal assistance for the poor and the social policy beneficiaries.
To establish the Department of Legal Assistance under the Ministry of Justice to assist the Minister of Justice in performing the State management over free-of-charge legal assistance for the poor and the social policy beneficiaries.
The provinces and cities directly under the Central Government shall be entitled to establish organizations to provide free legal assistance for the poor and the social policy beneficiaries. Such organizations shall be attached to the provincial/municipal Justice Services and established by decisions of the Presidents of the provincial/municipal People�s Committees.- (Summary)
