# Decree 46/2002/NĐ-CP

DECREE No. 46/2002/ND-CP of April 19, 2002 on the adjustment of administrative boundaries for the establishment of a number of communes and townships of Phu My and Van Canh rural districts, Binh Dinh province
To establish Binh Duong township of Phu My rural district with a natural land area of 392 ha and a population of 5,420.
To establish My Chanh Tay commune of Phu My rural district with a natural land area of 2,640 ha and a population of 5,297.
To establish Van Canh township, the capital of Van Canh rural district, with a natural land area of 1,995.87 ha and a population of 5,195.- (Summary)
