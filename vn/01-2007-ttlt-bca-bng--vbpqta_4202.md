# Joint circular 01/2007/TTLT-BCA-BNG 

JOINT CIRCULAR
Revising the provisions of Point b, Section 1, Part III of Joint Circular No. 04/2002/TTLT/BCA-BNG of January 29, 2002, guiding the implementation of the Government’s Decree No. 21/2001/ND-CP of May 28, 2001, detailing the implementation of the Ordinance on Foreigners’ Entry into, Exit from, and Residence in Vietnam
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO.”- (Summary)
