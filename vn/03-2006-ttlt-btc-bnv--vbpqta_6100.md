# Thông tư liên tịch 03/2006/TTLT-BTC-BNV

JOINT CIRCULAR
Guiding the implemen-tation of the Government’s Decree No. 130/2005/ND-CP of October 17, 2005, providing for the regime of autonomy and self-responsibility for use of administrative management payrolls and funds by state agencies
This Joint Circular takes effect 15 days after its publication in “CONG BAO” and annuls Joint Circular No. 17/2002/TTLT-BTC-BTCCBCP of February 8, 2002, of the Ministry of Finance and the Government Commission for Organization and Personnel (now the Ministry of Home Affairs).- (Summary)
