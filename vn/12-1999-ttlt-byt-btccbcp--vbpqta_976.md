# Thông tư liên tịch 12/1999/TTLT/BYT-BTCCBCP

Joint Circular No. 12/1999/TTLT/BYT-BTCCBCP of May 22, 1999 on amendments and supplements to Joint Circular No.02/1998/TTLT-BYT-BTCCBCP
This Circular amends and supplements Point 5, Section II; Sub-Point 1.1, Point 1, Section III and Point 1, Section IV of Joint Circular No. 02/1998/TTLT/BYT-BTCCBCP of June 27, 1998 of the Ministry of Health and the Government Commission for Organization and Personnel guiding the implementation of the Government’s Decree No. 01/1998/ND-CP of January 3, 1998 on the system of local health organizations.- (Summary)
