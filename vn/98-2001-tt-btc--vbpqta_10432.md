# Thông tư 98/2001/TT-BTC

CIRCULAR No. 98/2001/TT-BTC of December 5, 2001 guiding petrol charge accounting
This Circular shall apply to all production and/or business establishments of all economic sectors, which are engaged in the activities of producing and/or trading in petrol and oil, or buy petrol and oil for use.- (Summary)
