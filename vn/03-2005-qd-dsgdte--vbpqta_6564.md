# Decision 03/2005/QĐ-DSGĐTE

DECISION
Promulgating the Regulation on initial records and statistical reports on population, family and children
This Decision takes effect 15 days after its publication in "CONG BAO" and replaces the National Committee for Population and Family Planning's Decision No. 01/2001/QD-UB of February 9, 2001, promulgating the Regulation on initial records and statistical reports on population and the Vietnam Committee for Child Protection and Care's Decision No. 112/1998/QD-BT of July 5, 1998, on the community-based system for collection of information on children.- (Summary)
